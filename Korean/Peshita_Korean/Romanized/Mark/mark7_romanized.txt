1	waheunasu rwaseheu peurise usapre desau men uritrem
2	waheujau nasin men talmidaui daheurin raheuma kat ra meusyagan idaihon wesadaru
3	kulhon gel ihudaiye waprise en hu dapttirait ra meusigin idaihon ra rasin mettul dahidin matreumanusa deukasise
4	umen syuka era amdin ra rasin usagiiyasa heuranyasa it airen deukaberu deunetron mamudyasa deuhase watkettte watmanai neuhasya wadalsasa
5	usyaru sapre waprise reumana talmidaik ra meuhalhin aiheu matreumanusa deukasise era kat ra meusyagan idaihon aheurin raheuma
6	hu den emal reuhon syapil etnabi raikon esyaya neubiiya natbai bape aiheu daheusip dama hana beusepwaseheu u myakal ri rephon den sagi rahik meni
7	watrikait daheurin ri kat malpin yulpane deupukdane dapnai nasya
8	seubakton gel pukdana daraha wahidin ton matreumanusa dapnai nasya mamudyasa deuhase watkettte usagiiyasa airen dalharen damyan
9	amal reuhon syapil ttalmin ton pukdana daraha datkimon matreumanuthon
10	muse gel emal dyakal rabuheu uremaheu uman damssahe raba urema mauta neumut
11	aton den amrin ton en nimal gapra rabu au remeheu kulbani medem deumeni tisal
12	ura syapkin ton reheu deunebet medem rabu au remeheu
13	umatren ton melsa daraha mettul matreumanusa datremton watdamyan reuharen sagiiyasa apdin ton
14	wakra yesyu reuhensya kureheu wemal reuhon syumuni kulhon wettaru
15	rait medem dalbal men balnasya hu uael reheu deumetkaheu meusaiyep reheu era medem deunapek meneheu hau u meusaiyep reubal nasya
16	man diit reheu etne deunetma netma
17	kat den al yesyu reubaita men kensya syaru talmidaui al matra hau
18	amal reuhon hahana ap aton atrin ton ra yadin ton deuhul medem deumen reubal ael reubal nasya ra metkaheu meusaiyep reheu
19	deura wa reurebeheu ael era reuhalseheu umetteude beusatkisa damdakya kuraheu mehulta
20	medem den deunapek men balnasya hau u meusaiyep reubal nasya
21	men reugau gel men reba dapnai nasya napkan maheuseubasa bisyasa gaura janyusa ganabusa ketra
22	arubusa bisyusa neheura ssaheunusa aina bitta gudapa syapheuranusa syattyusa
23	haren kulhen bisyasa men reugau u napkan wamsaiban reheu reubalnasya
24	men taman kam yesyu wesa rathuma deussul watssaidan ual reubaita hat ura ssabe wa deunat neda beheu ura etkaheu deunetse
25	meheuda gel semat atsa heuda metturaseheu diit wat reubalsaheu ruha ttanpeusa wesat neprat keudam rekraui
26	hi den atsa iseheu wat hanpeusa men puniki deusuriiya ubaya wat meneheu deunapek sida men balsaheu
27	wemal raheu yesyu seubuki rukdam deunetbon beunaiya ra wat gel syapira deunesap raheuma dapnaiya unalme reuhalbe
28	hi den nat wamra reheu in mari ap kalbe men teuhet pasure aheurin paltuse dapnaiya
29	amal raheu yesyu jeri mettul hade melsa neupak reheu sida men balsehi
30	wejat reubaitaheu wetkeuhat balsaheu kat ramya balsa unapik menaheu sidaheu
31	tup neupak yesyu men teuhuma deussul watssaidan wesa ryama dakrira bathuma detrat-meudinasa
32	waitiu reheu halsya hat pika ubae wau meneheu dansim raui ida
33	unakdeheu men kensya balhudaui walmi ssebaseheu betnaui urak wakrep reuresyaneheu
34	uhal batmaiya wettanaheu wemal reheu etpaheu
35	ubaheu beusyasa etpataheu etnaui wettari esara deuresyaneheu umarel peusikait
36	ujahal enon dalnat ra nimron waheuma deuhu meujahal wa reuhon henon yatil maheureujin wau
37	uyatirait metdamrin wau wamrin deuhul medem syapil abet reuhalse abet deunetmon watra meumalrin danmalron
