1	uhat eprat syapsa maryam makdeurata umaryam dyakup usyarum jeuban herume deunisyan nemseuhana
2	beusyapra den beuhat beusyaba esai reubet keubura kat deunaheu semsya
3	wamran wai beunapseuhen man den agel ran kipa men tara deubet keubura
4	uhal heujai damakra ha kipa raba wat gel ttap
5	uaren reubet keubura waheujai raima dyasep men yamina wattip ettteura hewalta watmaheu
6	hu den emal reuhen ra tetheuran ryesyu natraiya bayan ten hau detdeukep kam reheu ra wa teunan ha duksa aika deusim wa
7	era jeren emaren reusalmidaui walhipa deuha kadem reuhon rakrira taman teheujuna aikana demal reuhon
8	uhat seuma rak wanpak men kapra ahit wa reuhen gel teheura walsisa walnat medem ra emaren dahiran wai gel
9	beusyapra den beuhat beusyaba kam wetheuji rukdam reumaryam makdeurata ha deusyaba sidin apek wa menaheu
10	uhi ejat saprat reuhanon dameheu wau dabirin hwau ubahen
11	uhenon kat seumau damran deuhai wetheuji reuhen ra haimenu enen
12	basal haren etheuji ratren menhon batmusa heurisa kat meuhalhin wajin rakrisa
13	uhenon ejaru emaru reusyalka ap ra reuhanon haimenu
14	heuraiyat den etheuji raheudasal kat seumihin uhaset reuhasirut haimanuthon walkasyut rephon dalhanon daheujau deukam ra haimenu
15	wemal reuhon jeru ralma kureheu waheureju seubalsi beuhuraheu beurisa
16	aina damhaimen uamet haiye waina deura meuhaimen methaiyap
17	aswasa den rairen damhaimnin haren nekpan beusemi side napkon wapresyane hase neumalron
18	wahwawasa netkeuron wen sama deumauta netton ra nahal enon wiidaihon neusimon al keurihe unethalmon
19	yesyu den maran men basal deumarel amhon ratmaiya seurek wisep men yamina daraha
20	henon den neupaku waheureju beuhul duka umaran madal wa reuhon umasyal meraihon baswasa dapdin wau
