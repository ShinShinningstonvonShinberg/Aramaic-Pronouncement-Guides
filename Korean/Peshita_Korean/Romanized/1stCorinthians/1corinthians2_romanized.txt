2	ura danet napsi bainathon aiheu deumedem yada na era en ryesyu meusiha ap reheu kat jeukip
3	wena beudeheureusa sagisa ubalsisa hwit rwathon
4	umerasi uharujusi ra hwat bampisanusa deumere deuheheumeusa era beusahwisa deuruha wathaira
5	deura tehwe haimanuthon beuheheumeusa dapnainasya era beuhaira daraha
6	heheumeusa den meumalrinan bakmire heheumeusa ra deuhana alma apra deusyarittanaui dalma hana deumetbatrin
7	era meumalrinan heheumeusa daraha beurat ha damhasya wat ukadem wa palsyaheu araha men keudam alme reusyupha diran
8	ha deuhat men syarittane dalma hana ra yadaheu eru gel yaduheu rau reumaraheu deusetbuheuta jakpin wau
9	era aiheu daheusip daina ra heujat wetna ra semat ual reba deubalnasya ra seurek medem deuttaiyep araha rairen deuraheumin reheu
10	ran den gara araha beuruheheu ruha gel kulmedem bassya ap umkaui daraha
11	aina hu gel balnasya dyada dapbalnasya era en ruha deubal nasya deubeheu hahana ap deubaraha nat ra yada era en ruheheu daraha
12	heunan den ra wa ruha dalma neusapneu era ruha deumen araha deuneda mauheubasa deumen araha esihep ran
13	airen dap meumalrinan ra wa byulpana deumere deuheheumeusa dapnainasya era byulpana deuruha walruhane ruhanyasa meupaheuminan
14	balnasya gel deubanpet u ra meukabel ruhanyasa syattyusa enen gel reheu ura metkaheu deuneda dapruheu metdin
15	ruhana den kul medem daen uhu men nat ra metdin
