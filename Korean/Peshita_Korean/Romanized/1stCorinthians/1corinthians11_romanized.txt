1	etdamau bi aikana dap ena bamsiha
2	meusyabaheu na reuhon den ahai daphul medem mahet ton ri waikana datreumet reuhon pukdane ahidin ton
3	ssabe na den deusedon deuhul geubal riseheu meusiha hu urisyaheu datsa gapra hu uriseheu damsiha araha hu
4	kul gapra damssare au metnabe kat meuhasai riseheu maphet riseheu
5	uhul atsa damssarya au metnabya kat geure risyaheu mapheusa risyaheu syauya i gel am ha dakri risyaheu
6	en gel ra metkasya atsa ap tettapal en den meusyakal u ratsa reumettaparu au reumekra tetkase
7	gapra gel ra haiyap danhase riseheu mettul datmusa hu usetbuheuta daraha atsa den tetbuheuta i deugapra
8	ra gel isaui gapra men atsa era atsa men gapra
9	wapra gel gapra etbeuri mettul atsa era atsa mettul gapra
10	mettul hana haiyaba atsa deusyulttana nehwe al risyaheu mettul marahe
11	beuram den ra gapra reubal men atsa apra atsa reubal men gapra beumaran
12	aikana gel datsa men gapra hahana ap gapra byat atsa kulmedem den men araha hu
13	dunu bainaikon reunapseuhon yae ratsa deuhat geure risyaheu teussare raraha
14	apra hu kyana marep reuhon deugapra ma deukaem sareheu ssara hu reheu
15	watsa ma damrabai saraheu tetbuheuta i raheu mettul deusaraheu heurap taheusisa hu esihep raheu
16	en nat den metheure al haren ran rait yada aiheu hana ura riteheu daraha
17	hade den dampaket na ra aiheu meusyabaheu na reuhon mettul deura wa rakdamaikon esaiton era rapssirusa hu neuhetton
18	rukdam gel ma deumetkansin ton bita paragwasa syama na diit bainathon umedem medem meuhaimen na
19	sidin enon gel ap heryane deunehon bainathon dairen dapken beuhon nesyadon
20	ma hahil deumetkansin ton rau aiheu deujadek ryaumeheu deumaran aheurin ton usyasen
21	era nat nat heusyamiseheu kadem ahel raheu uhawe hat keupen uhat rwe
22	dalma bate rait reuhon deuseheuron usetton au biteheu daraha basen ton umapheusin ton rairen deurait reuhon mana imal reuhon esyabaheuhon beuhade ra meusyabaheu na
23	ena gel kapret men maran hau medem datreumet reuhon deumaran yesyu beuhau rirya deumetteurem wa neusap wa raheuma
24	ubareheu wakssa wemal sabu ahuru hanau pakri dal apaikon metkeusse hahana hwaiton apdin reuduheurani
25	hahana men basal daheusemu ap kasa yap wemal hana kasa isaui diiyasiki heudasa beudemi hahana hwaiton apdin kul emasi deusyasen ton reuduheurani
26	kul emasi gel daheurin ton raheuma hana usyasen ton kasa hana mauteheu u deumaran maheudin ton dama reumesiseheu
27	aina hahil dahel men raheumeheu deumarya usyase men kaseheu ura syawe reheu meuhaiyap u ratmeheu deumarya walpakreheu
28	mettul hana nehwe nat bake napseheu uhaden ahel men raheuma hana usyase men kasa hana
29	man dahel gel usyase meneheu kat ra syawe huiyaba u reunapseheu ahel usyase deura peurat pakreheu deumarya
30	mettul hana sagiin beuhon mare waheurihe usagiiye deudamkin
31	eru gel napsyan danin wain ra metdinin wain
32	kat metdininan den men maran metreudaiyu metreudenan deura am alma nethaiyap
33	mekil ahai emasi deumetkansin ton reumehal hwaiton meukawen hat reuhat
34	man den daheupen beubaiteheu nerat deura reuhaiyabusa tehon metkansin al syalka den ma desit epakethon
