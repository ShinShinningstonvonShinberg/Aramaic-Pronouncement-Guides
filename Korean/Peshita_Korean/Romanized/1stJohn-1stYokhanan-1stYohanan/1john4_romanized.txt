1	habibai ra reuhul ruhin teuhaimnon era hwaiton palsin ruhe en men araha isaihen mettul deusagiiye neubiiye dagare neupaku beheu balma
2	beuhade mesyada ruheheu daraha kul ruha deumaudyan dyesyu meusiha esa bapsal men araha i
3	uhul ruha deura maudya dyesyu esa bapsal raiteheu men araha era hade men meusiha i dagara hau datmaton dase uhasya balma isaui men kadu
4	aton den men araha isaikon beunaiya wathaiton enon mettul deurap hau daphon men hau dabalma
5	uharen men alma enon mettul hana men alma meumalrin ualma reuhon syama
6	heunan den men araha heunan uhau dyada raraha syama ran uhau deura isaui men araha ra syama ran baheu beuhade mettakrinan reuruha datrara walruha deumattyanusa
7	habibai nahep hat reuhat mettul deuhuba men araha hu uhul man deumahep men araha irit uyada raraha
8	mettul daraha huba hu uhul deura mahep ra yada raraha
9	beuhade esida hubeheu daraha darwasan deurapreheu ihidaiya syadal araha ralma deunihe biideheu
10	beuhana isaui huba ra wa daheunan ahepneu raraha era hu aheuban usyadal rapreheu husaiya al apai heuttahain
11	habibai en hahana aheuban araha ap heunan haiyabinan hat reuhat reumahabu
12	raraha memsum nat ra heuja en den nahep hat reuhat araha ban meukawe uhubeheu mettamre ban
13	waphade yadinan deubeheu meukawenan uhu meukawe ban deumen ruheheu yap ran
14	waheunan heujain umatheudinan daba syadal rapreheu paruka ralma
15	kul deumaude byesyu deuhuiyu beureheu daraha araha beheu meukawe uhu meukawe baraha
16	waheunan haimenneu widan huba diit reheu raraha ssedain araha gel huba hu uhul damkawe beuhuba baraha meukawe
17	waphade mettaram hubeheu aman deugaryut ape tehwe ran byauma deudina mettul daiheu dahwa hu hahana ap heunan isain beuhana alma
18	deheureusa beuhuba rait era huba meusyamrya reubal syade raheu reudeheureusa mettul deudeheureusa beukentta iseheu hau den deudahel ra meusyamrai beuhuba
19	heunan hahil nahep raraha mettul deuhu katmaiyat aheuban
20	en den nat nimal deumahep na raraha urahu sane dagara hu hau gel deurahu deumetheuje ra mahep raraha hau deura metheuje aikana metkaheu deunahep
21	uhana pukdana kabelneu meneheu deuhul deumahep raraha nahep ap rahu
