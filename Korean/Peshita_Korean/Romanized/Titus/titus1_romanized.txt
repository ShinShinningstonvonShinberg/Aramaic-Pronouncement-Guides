1	pauraut apdeheu daraha watriheheu dyesyu meusiha beuhaimanusa dakbaiya daraha widasa datrara dapdeheurat araha
2	al sapra deuhaiye dararam damraheu araha syarira keudam japnaui dalma
3	wakra melseheu beujapnaheu byat karujusan ha dethaimnet ena beupukdana daraha mahyanan
4	reuttittaut beura syarira beuhaimanusa deugawa ttaibusa watrama men araha aba umen maran yesyu meusiha mahyanan
5	mettulhana u seubaktaheu wit bakritti dairen deuhasiran tatken watkim kasise bamdina meudina aikana deupakettaheu
6	aina datra resyan u wahwa bara daheuda atsa wiit reheu beunaiya meuhaimne deura meussahen ura isaihon deura syubat basuttusa
7	haiyap u gel kasisya deunehwe deura resyan aiheu rabaita daraha ura nehwe metdabal beureyan napseheu ura nehwe hemsan ura nehwe abal al hamra ura tehwe raheutta ideheu reumemha ura nehwe rahem yutrane ttanpe
8	era nehwe rahem aheuseunaiye unehwe rahem ttabasa unehwe neuhep unehwe kiin unehwe hasya walbiheu napseheu men reugigasa
9	wapttil reheu al yulpana deumelsa deuhaimanusa deunetkaheu ap rambaiyau byulpaneheu heurima walmakasu rairen deumetheuren
10	it gel sagiiye deura mettapdin watrikan meraihon umatten reyane dapnainasya yatirait airen deumen geujulta enon
11	hanon dware ramsakaru pumhon bate gel sagiiye meuhaprin umalpin medem deura ware mettul yutrane ttanpe
12	emal nat menhon neubiiya dilhon dapnai keuritti beuhuljeuban dagarin enon haiwasa bisyasa uhalsasa battirasa
13	usyarirait iseheu saheudusa hade mettul hana kasyait hwait maket reuhon deunehon heurimin beuhaimanusa
14	ura netreumon reusyuyasa dihudaiye walpukade dapnainasya deusanen seurara
15	kulmedem gel deuhe u rathaiya rairen den damsaibin ura meuhaimnin medem dathe rait reuhon era meusaiyap u reyanhon usilteuhon
16	umauden dyadin raraha babadaihon den kaprin beheu wiisaihon seuniiye watra pyat umatraiya reuhul bat ttap
