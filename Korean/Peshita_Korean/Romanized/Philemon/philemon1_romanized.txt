1	pauraut asireheu dyesyu meusiha uttimaseaut aha reupirimaun habiba upalha daman
2	urapya habiptan uralhipaut palha daman warita dapbaitaheu
3	ttaibusa amhon watrama men araha abon umen maran yesyu meusiha
4	maude na rarahi beuhuljeuban umetdeuhal na raheu batrawasi
5	ha men deusemet haimanusaheu uhuba diit raheu rwat maran yesyu warwat kulhon kadise
6	deusehwe syautapusa deuhaimanusaheu yaheuba pire babade ubidasa deuhul ttaban diit reuhon byesyu meusiha
7	hadusa gel sagisa it ran ubuiyaa dabyat hubaheu etteunihu raheume deukadise
8	mettul hade paresiiya sagisa it ri bamsiha depkut raheu airen deujatkan
9	mettul huba den meba u bae na menaheu ena pauraut diisai saba aiheu dyada at hasya den ap asira dyesyu meusiha
10	ubae na menaheu al beri aina dyeldet basurai anisimaut
11	hau deubatban rait wa raheu beheu hathu hasya den ap raheu ap ri ttap hasyaheu
12	usyadalteheu raheu at den aiheu daryalda diri hahana kabera
13	ssabe wit gel darwasi eheudiui deunehwe meusyamet ri heurapaik basure dewangeriiyaun
14	berat melkaheu den ra sseubit medem reumebat deura aiheu deubakttira tehwe ttapsaheu era beussebyanaheu
15	keubal den ap mettul hade syani deusyasa dararam teheudiui
16	ra mekil aiheu apda era yatil men apda aha habiba diri hat keuma diraheu ubapsal wapmaran
17	en hahil isaik ri syautapa kabera aiheu deuri
18	wen medem hatraheu au haiyap hade rai heusyup
19	ena pauraut ketbet biidai ena para na deura imal raheu dap napsyaheu haiyap at ri
20	in ahi ena etteuniheu baheu beumaran aniheu raheumai bamsiha
21	mettul dathil na deusyama at ri ketbet raheu uyada na dyatil men ma damal na abet at
22	baheuda den ap ttaiyep ri bet matrya meusabal na gel deubatrawathon mesihep na reuhon
23	syael batramaheu epapra syabya dami byesyu meusiha
24	umalkaut waritttalhaut udima uruka matranai
25	ttaibuseheu deumaran yesyu meusiha am ruheuhon amin
