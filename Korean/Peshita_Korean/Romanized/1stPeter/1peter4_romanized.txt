2	deura mekil ralgigasa dapnainasya nihe keuma japna diisaui beupakra era reussebyana daraha
3	sapek gel japna hau dabal dapraheuton beheu ssebyana deuhanpe basuttusa waprawaiyusa wapssaheunusa ubatmara wappulhana deuside
4	uha hasya metdamrin wamgatpin raikon batra mettalhiton amhon beuha asuttusa katmata
5	hanon dyaheubin petgama raraha hau dasit ramdan mise uhaiye
6	mettul hana gel ettabal ap reumise deunetdinon aiheu beunainasya bapsal unihon baraha beuruheu
7	mattyat raheu den halsa deuhul mettul hana etnakapu wettiru ratrusa
8	wakdam kul medem huba haripa rwat heudade nehwe reuhon huba gel meuhape suga daheuttahe
9	wahwaiton raheumin aheuseunaiye deura retna
10	uhulnat menhon mauhapta deukabel men araha neusyamet baheu reuhapraui aiheu rabai bate ttabe deuttaibusa meuparatta daraha
11	kulman dammarel aiheu melsa daraha neumarel uhulman damsyamet aiheu deumen haira hau daraha yahep reheu daphul dapdin ton nettabaheu araha byat yesyu meusiha hau deudireheu i tetbuheuta wiikara raram almin amin
12	habibai ra tetdamron beunesyune deuhawen reuhon aiheu deumedem nuheura gadet reuhon mettul dalbuheuranhon u hawen
13	era heudau deumettautpin ton reuhasyaui damsiha deuhahana ap beugeryana deusetbuheuteheu teheudon userujon
14	wen methatdin ton al apai seumeheu damsiha ttubaikon deuruha meusyabaheuta daraha metteuniha raikon
15	balhut ra nat menhon aiheu kattura au aiheu ganaba au aiheu abet bisyasa nehwe haet
16	en den haet aiheu keuritttyana ra nephat era neusyabaheu raraha beheu beuhana seuma
17	mettul deujapna u dansyare dina men baiteheu daraha en den menan meusyare aida i halsa dairen deura mettteupisin ratbalseheu daraha
18	wen jadika reumaheusen haiye rasia uhattaiya aika metteuhaheu
19	mettul hana airen deuhasin aiheu ssebyaneheu daraha nakron reheu napsyathon babade syapire aiheu dalbaruiya meuhaimna
