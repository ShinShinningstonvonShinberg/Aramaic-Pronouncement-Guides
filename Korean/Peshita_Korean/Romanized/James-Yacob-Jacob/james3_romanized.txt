1	ra sagiiye malpane nehon beuhon ahai era hwaiton yadin deudina yatira haiyabinan
2	sagiiyasa gel mettarinan kuran kul dapmelsa ra syara hana isaui gapra geumira deumetkaheu neusyabet ap kureheu pakreheu
3	ha gel peugude beupuma deuraheusya ramenan aiheu deunettapdon ran uhureheu gutmeuhon maheupeuhinan
4	ap elpe asinasa kat deubiran reuhen ruhe kasyasa men kaisa jura metnatpan rasal deuhaal ssebyaneheu deuhau damdabal
5	hahana ap resyana hadama u jura umettare ap nura julta abe sagiiye maukda
6	uresyana nura u ualma daheuttisa aiheu aba u uhu resyana kat isaui beuhadamain meuhatem reheu reuhureheu pakran umauket yubare deusyalbasan deuraheuttin aiheu gikre uyaket ap hu beunura
7	kulhon gel kyane deuhaiwasa watparaheusa uraheusya dyama wadyapsya mettapdin rahyana deunasyusa
8	resyana den nat ra etkaheu deuneheubeusiui bitta hade deura mettaheusa meure u sama deumauta
9	beheu meubalhinan reumarya waba ubeheu rattinan rapnainasya deubatmusa daraha bidin
10	umeneheu men puma napkan bulkasa urauttasa ra ware ahai deuharen hahana nettaran
11	dalma metkeuha deumen hat mabua nepkon maiya heuraiya umarire
12	au dalma metkeuha tita ahai deujaite tebet au geupeta tine hahana ap ra maiya marihe metkeuhin deunesapdon heuraiya
13	manu menhon deuhakim walde neuhawe badaui beuhupahe syapire beuheheumeusa makiheuta
14	en den heusama marira it beuhon au heryana beurebaikon ra tethatron al kutta watdakron
15	mettul deuhade heheumeusa men rel ra neheutat era iseheu aranata men husyabe deunapsya umen side
16	aika gel diit heusama uheryana taman ap deuruhya uhulmedem deubit
17	heheumeusa den deumen rel dahya i umarya seurama umakiha umettamanya umarya raheume upire ttabe watra palgusa i ubape ra natba
18	pire den deujadikusa beusyaina metdarin rairen dapdin seurama
