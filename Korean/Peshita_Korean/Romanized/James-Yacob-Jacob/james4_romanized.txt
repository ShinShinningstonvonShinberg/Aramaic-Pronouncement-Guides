1	men aika it beuhon keurabe umasswasa ra wa men reugigasa deumakreuban beuhadamaikon
2	metrakreugin ton urait reuhon ukatrin ton uttanin ton ura asya biidaikon unassen ton wakrabe apdin ton urait reuhon mettul deura syarin ton
3	syarin ton ura natbin ton mettul deubisyait syarin ton aiheu datsalson reugigathon
4	gaiyare ra yadin ton deureheumeuseheu dalma hana beldeubabusa i daraha aina hahil deussabe deunehwe raheuma dalma hana beldeubaba hawe raraha
5	au dalma seurikait saprin ton demal keusaba deubatnana raga ruha damra ban
6	ttaibusa den yatilta yap ran maran mettul hade emal daraha meumakeheu reurame walmakihe yahep ttaibusa
7	ettabadu hahil raraha ukumu rukbal sattana uarek menhon
8	wakrubu rwat araha unetkarap reuhon dakau idaikon hattaiye kadesu rebawathon peurigai napsya
9	etmakahu wetaru uguheukeuhon repra netheupeheu uhadwathon raksa
10	etmakahu keudam marya wanramremhon
11	ra tehon meumalrin al heudade ahai hau gel dammarel al ahu au daen rahu meumarel al namusa udaen reunamusa wen reunamusa daen at ra hwait abudeheu deunamusa era daiyaneheu
12	hat u saem namusa udaiyana deuhu metkaheu deunahe unaubet at den man at deudaen at reheu reukaribaheu
13	mana den nimal ap al airen damrin dyaumana au meuhal ajinan ramdita aida deuhi uapdinan taman syata heuda umettakrinan uyatrinan
14	ura yadin mana hawe meuhal mana enon gel haiyain era en raheuga deukaril metheuje uttarek umaupe
15	heurap deunimron den marya netbe unihe apdinan hade au ha
16	mettapheurin baheusiruthon kul syuphara daiheu hana bisya u
17	waina dyada ttapsa ura abet raheu heuttaha hawe reheu
