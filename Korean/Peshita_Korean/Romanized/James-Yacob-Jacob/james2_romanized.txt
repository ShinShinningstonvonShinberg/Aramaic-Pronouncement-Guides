1	ahai ra beumasap bape tehon ahidin reuhaimanusa deusetbuheuteheu deumaran yesyu meusiha
2	en gel neul raheunutteuhon nat detkaseheu deudaheuba au deumanaui syapire uneul metkina beumane ssae
3	wathuron beuhau dalbit mane syapire usimron reheu at halka tep syapil walmetkina timron reheu at kum reuhal au tep raheu halka keudam kupsya deurekrain
4	ra ha etparakton reuhon beunapseuhon wahwaiton meupalsyane deumaheuseubasa bisyasa
5	seumau ahai habibe ra wa reumetkine dalma atire den beuhaimanusa geuba araha deunehon yalte beumalkusa ha damraheu araha rairen deuraheumin reheu
6	aton den syattuna reumetkina ra ha atire mettaren raikon uhenon nakdin reuhon reubet dina
7	ra ha henon meugatpin al seuma ttaba detkeuri raikon
8	wen namusa daraha beuhade meusyalmiton aiheu daheusip deuselham reukaribaheu aiheu napsyaheu syapil apdin ton
9	en den bape natbin ton heuttisa apdin ton umetkaunin ton men namusa aiheu aprai al namusa
10	aina gel deuhureheu namusa nattal ubaheuda syara reuhureheu namusa ethaiyap
11	hau gel demal deura teugul hu emal deura tekttul en den ra gaal at era kattel at hwait raheu abal al namusa
12	hahana hwaiton meumalrin uhahana hwaiton sarin aiheu nasya dapnamusa deuhirusa sidin ton reumetdanu
13	dina gel hawe deura raheume al hau deura abet raheume mettaren ton beuraheume al dina
14	mana henyana ahai en nat amal diit ri haimanusa wabade rait reheu dalma metkeuha haimanuseheu deusaheui
15	wen aha au hasa nehon altterain uhasirin saibalta dyauma
16	unimal reuhon nat menhon jeru batrama seuhanu watbau ura tetron reuhon seunikuseheu deupakra mana henyana
17	hahana ap haimanusa deura bade misa i balhudeheu
18	amal gel nat raheu it raheu haimanusa uri it ri bade hawani haimanusaheu deura bade wena meuhawe na raheu haimanusi men badai
19	meuhaimen at deuhat u araha syapil abet at ap side meuhaimnin urarin
20	ssabe at den deuseda au balnasya harasya deuhaimanusa deura bade misa i
21	abon apraham ra wa men bade etdadak dasek riithak beureheu al matbeuha
22	haje at deuhaimanuseheu saiat rabadaui umen bade haimanuseheu etgamrat
23	watrem keusaba demal deuhaimen apraham baraha wethatbat reheu reujadiku uraheuma daraha etkeuri
24	haje at deumen bade metdadak balnasya ura wa men haimanusa balhut
25	hahana ap rahap janisa ra wa men bade etdatkat deukaprat reugasyuse ubuulha heurisa apkat enon
26	aikana deupakra deura ruha mit u hahana ap haimanusa deura bade misa i
