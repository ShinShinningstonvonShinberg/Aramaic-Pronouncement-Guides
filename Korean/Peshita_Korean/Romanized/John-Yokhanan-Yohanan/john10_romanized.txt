1	amin amin amal na reuhon deuman deura ael men tara rattyara dana era sarek men duka heuranya hau ganaba hu ugaiyasa
2	hau den dael men tara raya hu dana
3	walhana nattal tara pasaheu reheu tara uana syama kareheu uelbaui kare batmahaihon umapek reuhon
4	uma dapek aneheu keudameheu ajel uelbaui direheu ajin batreheu mettul dyadin kareheu
5	basal nuheuraiya den ra aja ana era alka meneheu deura yada kareheu deunuheuraiya
6	hade peresa emal reuhon yesyu henon den ra idau mana marel amhon
7	amal reuhon den tup yesyu amin amin amal na reuhon dena na tareheu dana
8	uhulhon airen desau ganabe enon ugaiyase era ra semat enon ana
9	ena na tara ubi en nat neul nihe uneul unepuk ureya netkaheu
10	ganaba ra ase era deuneknup watnekttul watnaubet ena esit deuhaiye nehon reuhon umedem dyatil nehwe reuhon
11	ena na raya ttaba raya ttaba napseheu saem heurap aneheu
12	agira den deura hwa raya urau direheu enon elbe ma daheuja diba dase syabek ana uarek wase diba hattep wambadal raheu rana
13	agira den arek mettul dagira hu ura beuttil reheu al ana
14	ena na raya ttaba uyada na reudiri umesida na men diri
15	aikana dyada ri abi wena yada na rabi unapsi saem na heurap ana
16	it ri den ap elbe heurane airen deura hwau men ttyara hana wap reuhon ware ri reumaitaiyu enon unetmon kari usehwe ana kuraheu heuda uhat raya
17	mettul hana abi rahem ri dena saem na napsi deusup etbiheu
18	ra wa nat syakel raheu meni era ena saem na raheu men ssebyani syarit na gel dasimiheu usyarit na deusup etbiheu deuhana pukdana kapret men abi
19	wahwat tup palgusa bainai ihudaiye mettul mere haren
20	wamrin wau sagiiye menhon deudaiwa it reheu umetna seuna mana syamin ton reheu
21	heurane den amrin wau haren mere ra wai deudaiwana reuma daiwa metkaheu aine deusamya rampatahu
22	hwa den ida deuhudasa buuritrem usaswa wa
23	wamhareheu wa yesyu beuhaikra betttwa datremon
24	uhatru ihudaiye wamrin reheu dama remasi nasep at napsyan en at u meusiha emal ran garyait
25	na yesyu wemal reuhon emret reuhon ura meuhaimnin ton wabade dena abet na batmeheu dabi henon saheudin rai
26	era aton ra meuhaimnin ton mettul deura hwaiton men elbai aikana demret reuhon
27	elbe diri kari syamin wena yada na reuhon uhenon asen basari
28	wena yahep na reuhon haiye dararam ura nipdon raram ura nat neheuttup enon men idai
29	abi gel dyap ri men kul rap u ura nat metkaheu deumen ideheu dabi neheuttup
30	ena wabi hat heunan
31	watkaru tup ihudaiye kipe reumelgeumeheu
32	amal reuhon yesyu sagiiye bade syapire men rwat abi hawithon mettul aina bada menhon rakmin ton ri
33	amrin reheu ihudaiye ra wa mettul bade syapire rakmin nan raheu era mettul damgadep at uhat isaik bal nasya abet at napsyaheu araha
34	amal reuhon yesyu ra wa hahana keusip beunamuthon dena emret darahe aton
35	en reuhanon emal arahe mettul darwathon hwat melsa daraha ura metkaheu keusaba deunetteure
36	raina daba katseheu usyatreheu ralma aton amrin ton damgadep at al demret reuhon dapreheu na daraha
37	era abet na bade dabi ra teuhaimnunani
38	en den abet na apen ri ra meuhaimnin ton reuhon rabade haimenu deusedon wathaimnon dabi bi wena babi
39	wabau wau tup deuneheuduna wanpak reheu men bet idaihon
40	wejal reheu repra dyuldeunan reuduksa aika diisaui wa yuhanan men keudim kat mamet wa wahwa taman
41	wesau nasya sagiiye rwaseheu wamrin wau dyuhanan ap ra heuda asa bat kulmedem den demal yuhanan al gapra hana syaril u
42	usagiiye haimenu beheu
