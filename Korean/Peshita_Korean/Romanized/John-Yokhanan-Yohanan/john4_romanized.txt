1	ida den yesyu datmau peurise deusalmide sagiiye bat umamet yatil men yuhanan
2	kat ra wa hu yesyu mamet wa era talmidaui
3	usyapkaheu rihut wejal reheu tup rakrira
4	mapda wat reheu den deunise nebal al bet syamraiye
5	wesa ramdita deusyamraiye deumetkarya syahal al gep keurisa dyap wa yakup ryausep beureheu
6	wiit wa taman mina deumaiya dyakup hu den yesyu re wa men amra duulha wisep wa reheu al mina wiit wai set syain
7	wesat atsa men syamrin deusemre maiya wemal raheu yesyu hap ri maiya ette
8	talmidaui gel aru wau ramdita deunetbeunon reuhon saibalta
9	amra reheu ha atsa syamrata aikana at ihudaiya at umeni syael at reumetta diisai atsa syamrata ra gel methathin ihudaiye am syamraiye
10	na yesyu wemal raheu eru yada waiti mauhapta daraha umanu hana demal rehi hap ri ette ati syara waiti reheu uyahep wa rehi maiya haiye
11	amra reheu atsa ha mari ra daura it raheu ubira amika aimeka raheu maiya haiye
12	reuma at rap at men abon yakup hau deuhu yap ran bira hade uhu menaheu etti wapnaui uaneheu
13	na yesyu wemal raheu kul deunette men haren maiya tup nethe
14	kul den deunette men maiya dena etel reheu ra nethe raram era maiya hanon dyahep na reheu nehon beheu mina deumaiya deunabin reuhaiye dararam
15	amra reheu atsa ha mari hap ri men haren maiya deura tup ethe ura hwit asya darya men halka
16	amal raheu yesyu jeri keura reubarehi usa reuhalka
17	amra reheu rait ri bara amal raheu yesyu syapil emalti deurait ri bara
18	hamsya gel barin hwau rehi uhana diit rehi hasya ra hwa barehi hade syarilta emalti
19	amra reheu ha atsa mari hajya na danbiiya at
20	abahain beuhana ttura seugedu waton amrin ton deubuuritrem u asal dware reumetgat
21	amal raheu yesyu atsa haimenini dasya syasa deura beuhana ttura wap ra buuritrem tetgeudon raba
22	aton sakdin ton reumedem deura yadin ton heunan den sakdin nan reuma dyadin nan deuhaiye men ihudaiye enon
23	era asya syasa uhasya iseheu emasi deusagude syarire netgeudon raba beuruha ubatrara ap aba gel daiheu haren u sagude bae
24	ruha hu gel araha wairen deusakdin reheu beuruha ubatrara ware deunetgeudon
25	amra reheu atsa ha yada na damsiha ase uma desa hu marep ran kulmedem
26	amal raheu yesyu ena na dammarel na amehi
27	uhat meumarel esau talmidaui umetdamrin wau dam atsa meumarel ra den nat emal mana bae at au mana meumarel at amaheu
28	usepkat kulsaheu atsa wejat ramdita wamra reunasya
29	tau heujau gapra demal ri kul medem depdet reuma huiyu meusiha
30	wanpaku nasya men meudita wasen wau rwaseheu
31	ubainat haren baen wau meneheu talmidaui wamrin reheu raban rat
32	hu den emal reuhon it ri mehulta dehul aida daton ra yadin ton
33	amrin talmide bainaihon reuma nat aiti reheu medem reumehal
34	amal reuhon yesyu mehulti diri iseheu debet ssebyaneheu deuman deusyatrani wesyalmiui rabadeheu
35	ra aton amrin deubasal alba yalhin ase heussada ha amal na reuhon darimu ainaikon waheujau arasa dahwal umatti raheussada men kadu
36	waina deuhasset akra nasep uhanet pire reuhaiye dararam ujarua uhassuda aheuheuda neheudon
37	beuhade gel iseheu melsa datrara daheurin u jara waheurin hasset
38	ena syadalteuhon reumeheussat medem deura wa aton riton beheu heurane gel riu waton alton al amreuhon deuhanon
39	men ha meudita den sagiiye haimenu beheu syamraiye mettul melsaheu datsa ha deumatheuda wat demal ri kulmedem depdet
40	uhat esau rwaseheu hanon syamraiye bau meneheu deunehwe rwathon wahwa rwathon teuren yaumin
41	usagiiye haimenu beheu mettul melseheu
42	wamrin wau ratsa ha deumekil ra wa mettul melsehi meuhaimnin nan beheu heunan gel seuman widan deuhanau syarirait meusiha mahyaneheu dalma
43	ubasal teuren yaumin neupak yesyu men taman wejal rakrira
44	hu gel yesyu seuhet danbiiya bamditeheu ra mesyakal
45	kat den esa rakrira kapru geuriraiye daheujau aswasa kul dabat buuritrem badida esau wau gel wap henon radida
46	esa den tup yesyu reukatne dakrira aika dabat maiya hamra wiit wa baheupalnahum bet malka hat dapreheu keuriheu wa
47	hana seuma desa yesyu men ihut rakrira wejal rwaseheu ubae wa meneheu deunehut unase rapreheu karip wa gel rammat
48	amal reheu yesyu en aswasa usetmeurasa ra teheujon ra teuhaimnon
49	amal reheu hau bet malka mari hut atra maet reheu ttarya
50	amal reheu yesyu jel beuraheu hai u uhaimen hau gapra beumelsa demal reheu yesyu wejal
51	kat den neuhet wa aru apdaui usapru wamrin reheu beuraheu hya
52	usyael enon baina edana etheurem amrin reheu etmari batba syain seubakseheu esyasa
53	wida abu dapha syasa deubaheu emal reheu yesyu dapraheu hya uhaimen hu ubaiteheu kureheu
54	hade tup asa deusalten bat yesyu kat esa men ihut rakrira
