1	beuresit isaui wa melsa uhu melsa isaui wa rwat araha waraha isaui wa hu melsa
2	hana isaui wa beuresit rwat araha
3	kul biideheu hwa uberadaui apra heuda hwat medem dahwa
4	beheu haiye hwa uhaiye isaihon nuheura dapnainasya
5	uhu nuheura beuhesyuha manhal uhesyuha ra atreuheheu
6	hwa balnasya dettadal men araha seumeheu yuhanan
7	hana esa reusaheudusa deunethat al nuheura deuhulnat neuhaimen biideheu
8	ra hu hwa nuheura era deunethat al nuheura
9	isaui wa gel nuheura datrara deumanhal reuhulnat dase ralma
10	balma wa ualma biideheu hwa ualma ra yadeheu
11	reudireheu esa udireheu ra kapru
12	airen den deukapru yap reuhon syulttana dapnaiya daraha nehon rairen damhaimnin batmeheu
13	airen deurau men deuma ura men ssebyana deubetra ura men ssebyana deugapra era men araha esiredu
14	umelsa betra hwa wagen ban waheujain syupheheu syupha aiheu dihidaiya deumen aba damre ttaibusa ukutta
15	yuhanan seuhet raui waka wemal hanau hau demret deubasari ase wahwa reheu keudamai mettul deukatma u meni
16	umen maryuseheu heunan kuran neusapneu uttaibusa heurap ttaibusa
17	mettul deunamusa byat muse esihep seurara den uttaibusa byat yesyu meusiha hwa
18	araha ra heuja nat memsum ihidaiya araha hau diisaui buba dabu hu ettai
19	uhade hi saheuduseheu dyuhanan kat syadaru rwaseheu ihudaiye men uritrem kaheune urewaiye dansyaruna at man at
20	waudi ura keupal waudi deurau ena na meusiha
21	usyaru tup mana hahil iriiya at wemal ra isai neubiiya at wemal ra
22	wemaru reheu umanu at deunetel petgama rairen deusyatron mana amal at al napsyaheu
23	emal ena kara deukare beumatbeura daswau ulheheu deumarya aikana demal esyaya neubiiya
24	hanon den dettadaru men peurise wau
25	usyaru wemaru reheu mana hahil mamet at en at ra isaik meusiha ura iriiya ura neubiiya
26	na yuhanan wemal reuhon ena mamet na beumaiya bainathon den kaem hau daton ra yadin ton reheu
27	hanau hau deubasari ase wahwa reheu keudamai hau dena ra syawe na detre alke damsanaui
28	haren beubet-anya hwai bepra dyuldeunan aika deumamet wa yuhanan
29	waryauma deubatreheu heuja yuhanan ryesyu dase rwaseheu wemal ha emreheu daraha hau deusyakel heuttiseheu dalma
30	hanau dena emret raui deubasari ase gapra wahwa reheu keudamai mettul deukatma u meni
31	wena ra yada wit reheu era deunesida riitraiyel mettul hana esit ena dapmaiya amet
32	wathet yuhanan wemal daheujit reuruha deunaheuta men seumaiya aiheu yauna ukauyat raui
33	wena ra yada wit reheu era man deusyatrani damet beumaiya hu emal ri daina deuhaje at deunaheuta ruha wamkauya raui hanau mamet beuruha deukutsya
34	wena heujit watheudet deuhanau beureheu daraha
35	waryauma heurina kaem wa yuhanan watren men talmidaui
36	uhal byesyu kat meuhareheu wemal ha emreheu daraha
37	watmau teuraihon talmidaui kat emal wejaru reuhon batreheu dyesyu
38	wetpeuni yesyu waheuja enon dasen batreheu wemal reuhon mana baen ton amrin reheu raban aika hawe at
39	amal reuhon tau useheujon henon den esau waheujau aika deuhawe warwaseheu hwau yauma hau wiit wai aiheu syae sal
40	hat den men hanon datmau men yuhanan wejaru batreheu dyesyu isaui wa andeurewat ahu deusemon
41	hana heuja rukdam reusemon ahu wemal reheu etkaheuna ramsiha
42	waityeheu rwat yesyu uhal beheu yesyu wemal at u semon beureheu-dyauna at tetkeure kipa
43	waryauma heurina sseuba yesyu reumepak rakrira wetkaheu reupiripaut wemal reheu ta basari
44	hu den piripaut isaui wa men bet-ssaiyada men meuditeheu dandeurewat watsemon
45	upiripaut etkaheu reunasanaiil wemal reheu hau daheusap raui muse beunamusa ubanbiiye etkaheuna dyesyu u bal yausep men natrat
46	amal reheu nasanaiil men natrat metkaheu medem deuttap nehwe amal reheu piripaut ta useheuje
47	waheuja yesyu reunasanaiil kat ase rwaseheu wemal raui ha syarirait bal itraiyel deuneheura rait beheu
48	amal reheu nasanaiil men aimeka yada at ri amal reheu yesyu atra nekreheu piripaut kat teuhet tita at heujisaheu
49	na nasanaiil wemal reheu rabi at u beureheu daraha at u malkeheu diitraiyel
50	amal reheu yesyu al demret raheu daheujisaheu teuhet tita meuhaimen at deuraulban men haren teheuje
51	amal reheu amin amin amal na reuhon deumen hasya teheujon seumaiya dapsihin umarahaui daraha kat salkin unaheutin rwat beureheu deunasya
