1	waryauma datrasa hwat mettusa beukatne meudita dakrira wemeheu dyesyu taman wat
2	wap hu yesyu usalmidaui etkeuriu raheu reumettusa
3	waheusal wa hamra wamra reheu emeheu ryesyu hamra rait reuhon
4	amal raheu yesyu ma ri urehi atsa ra dakil esat syaasi
5	amra emeheu ramsyamsyane medem damal reuhon bedu
6	it wai den taman agane deuhipa set deusiman reusatkisa dihudaiye daheudan teuren teuren rebin au teurasa
7	amal reuhon yesyu meurau enen maiya ragane wamrau enen dama rel
8	amal reuhon jeuruu mekil waitau reurit seumaha waitiu
9	uhat ttem hau rit seumaha maiya hanon dahwau hamra ura yada wa men aimeka wa meusyamsyane den yadin wau deuhenon meurau enon reumaiya keura rit seumaha reuhatna
10	wemal reheu kul nat rukdam hamra ttaba maite uma darwiu haden aina dapssil at den neuttalta reuhamra ttaba dama reuhasya
11	hade i asa katmata dabat yesyu beukatne dakrira wauda syupheheu uhaimenu beheu talmidaui
12	basal hade neuhet raheupalnahum hu wemeheu wahaui usalmidaui usaman wau karil yaumasa
13	ukarip wa petha dihudaiye watrek ruuritrem yesyu
14	wetkaheu beuhaikra reuharen damjapnin taure uelbe uyaune uramalpane dyatbin
15	wabat reheu peuragera men hapra walhulhon apek men haikra warelbe walsaure uramalpane wesyat ulpanhon upasuraihon heupaheu
16	walhanon damjapnin yaune emal seukuru haren meka ura tepduneheu reubaiteheu dabi bet tegulta
17	wetdeuharu talmidaui daheusip datnaneheu deubaitaheu aheurani
18	nau den ihudaiye wemaru reheu mana asa meuhawe at ran deuharen abet at
19	na yesyu wemal reuhon seusuru haikra hana uratrasa yaumin ena meukim na reheu
20	amrin reheu ihudaiye ralbin uset seunin etbeuni haikra hana wat ratrasa yaumin meukim at reheu
21	hu den emal wa al haikra deupakreheu
22	kat kam den men bet mise etdeuharu talmidaui deuhade emal wa uhaimenu raheusabe walmelsa demal yesyu
23	kat isaui wa den yesyu buuritrem beupetha badida sagiiye haimenu beheu daheujau aswasa dabet
24	hu den yesyu ra meuhaimen wa reuhon napseheu mettul deuhu yada wa reuhulnat
25	ura seunik wa deunat nethat reheu al kul balnat hu gel yada wa mana it beubalnasya
