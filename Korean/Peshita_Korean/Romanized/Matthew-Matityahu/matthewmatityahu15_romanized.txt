1	haden keurebu rwat yesyu peurise usapre deumen uritrem wamrin
2	reumana talmidaik aprin al matreumanusa deukasise ura meusigin idaihon ma daheurin raheuma
3	na yesyu wemal reuhon reumana ap aton aprin ton al pukdana daraha mettul matreumanuthon
4	araha gel emal yakal rabuheu uremaheu uman damssahe rabu uremeheu meumat neumut
5	aton den amrin ton kul man deunimal raba au rema kulbani medem deusetheune meni ura nyakal rabu au remeheu
6	ubattelton melsa daraha mettul matreumanusa dilhon
7	natbai bape syapil etnabi raikon esyaya wemal
8	ama hana beusepwaseheu u myakal ri rephon den sagi rahik meni
9	watrikait daheurin ri kat malpin yulpane deupukdane dapnainasya
10	wakra reuhense wemal reuhon seumau wettaru
11	ra wa medem dael reupuma meusaiyep reubalnasya era medem deunapek men puma hau u meusaiyep reubalnasya
12	haden keurebu talmidaui wamrin reheu yada at daprise datmau melsa hade etkeuseru
13	hu den na wemal reuhon kul netbeusa aida deura natbaheu abi deubatmaiya tetkal
14	seubuku reuhon seumaiya enon nagude datmaiya samya den reusamya en netbal teuraihon beugumassa naprin
15	wana semon kipa wemal reheu mari pasek ran matra hana
16	hu den emal reuhon dama reuhasya ap aton ra mettakrin ton
17	ra yadin ton deumedem dael reupuma reuhalsa hu ajel umen taman beusatkisa metteude reubal
18	medem den deumen puma napek men reba napek uhuiyu meusaiyep reheu reubalnasya
19	men reba hu gel napkan maheuseubasa bisyasa gaura ketra janyusa ganabusa saheudut syukra gudapa
20	haren enen damsaiban reubalnasya en nat den nerat kat ra meusyagan idaui ra mettaiyap
21	wanpak men taman yesyu wesa rathuma deussul watssaidan
22	uha atsa keunanata men teuhume hanon nepkat kat kaya wamra etraham rai mari beureheu deudawit beurasi bisyait metdapra men sida
23	hu den ra panyaheu petgama wakrebu talmidaui bau meneheu wamrin seuriheu deukaya batran
24	hu den na wemal reuhon ra ettatret era rwat elbe dattau men bet itraiyel
25	hi den esat sekdat reheu wemrat mari adaraini
26	emal raheu ra syapil reumesap raheuma dapnaiya walmalmaiyu reuhalbe
27	hi den emrat in mari ap kalbe aheurin men paltuse deunaprin men pasure deumaraihon uhaen
28	haden emal raheu yesyu au atsa raba i haimanusehi nehwe rehi aiheu deussabya ati wesasyat balsaheu men ha syasa
29	usyani men taman yesyu wesa al gep yama dakrira watrek reuttura wisep taman
30	wakrebu rwaseheu kense sagiiye diit wau amhon heugire watmaiya uhalse wapsige waheurane sagiiye walmiu enon rwat rekraui dyesyu wasi enon
31	aiheu deunetdamron kense hanon deuhajen halse dammalrin wapsige deumethalmin waheugire damhalhin watmaiya deuhajen usyabahu raraha ditraiyel
32	hu den yesyu keura reusalmidaui wemal reuhon metraham na al kensya hana deuha teurasa yaumin kawiu rwasi urait reuhon ma deuneheuron udetre enon kat ssamin ra ssabe na dalma nupon buulha
33	amrin reheu talmidaui aimeka ran beuhulba raheuma dansaba kensya hana kureheu
34	amal reuhon yesyu keuma raheumin it reuhon amrin reheu syaba ukaril nune dakdeuke
35	wapkat reuhense deunettamkon al ara
36	watkal reuharen syaba raheumin walnune usyabaheu wakssa uyap reusalmidaui usalmide yabu reuhense
37	weharu kulhon watbau watkaru tautare dakssaiye meure syaba etpeuridin
38	hanon den deharu hawen wau alba alpin gapre seuttal men nese watraiye
39	uhat seura reuhense seurek relpa wesa rathuma deumakdu
