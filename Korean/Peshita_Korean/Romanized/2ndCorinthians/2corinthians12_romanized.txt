1	reumettapharu ware era ra pakaheu ase na ri gel reuhejwane walgeryanaui deumaran
2	yadana gapra bamsiha men keudam albaetre seunin en bapgal den wen deura peugal ra yada na araha hu yada detheuttep hu hana dama seumaiya datrasa
3	uyada na reheu reuhana balnasya en bapgal den wen deura peugal ra yada na araha hu yada
4	detheuttep reupaldaisa watma mere deura metmalran airen deura syarit reubalnasya rammararu enen
5	al hana ettaphal al napsi den ra ettaphal era en beuhulhane
6	en etbe gel dettaphal ra hawe na syattya seurara hu gel amal na haetna den dalma nat netrae rai yatil men ma deuhaje ri watsyama meni
7	watra etrim byatirusa deugeryane etteurem ri seupaiya reubetri maraheheu deusattana deunehwe meukapaheu ri deura etrim
8	ual hana teurat japnin bit men mari deunaprek meni
9	wemal ri sapka raheu ttaibusi hairi gel baheurihusa hu metgeumal hadyait hahil ettaphal beuhulhanai deunagen rai haireheu damsiha
10	mettul hana ssabe na beuhulhane beussara buulssane baldupya baheubusya dal apai meusiha emasi daheuriheu na gel haden u hailsan na
11	ha wit hasil reyana beusyuphari daton erattunani haiyabin waiton gel daton tatheudon rai mettul deura batret medem men seurihe airen deuttap myatrin wapen ra wit medem
12	aswasa datrihe epdet bainathon beuhul meusaipranu ubakbarwasa wapsetmeurasa waphaire
13	beumana gel etbassalton men idasa heuranyasa era en beuhade dena ra yekret raikon seubuku ri hade saheurusa
14	ha hade datrat i japnin damttaiyap na dise rwathon ura ikal raikon mettul deura bae na dilhon era reuhon ra gel haiyabin beunaiya ramsam simasa rabahe era abahe rapnaihon
15	ena den hadyait unapkasa apek wap keunumi etel al apai napsyathon apen kat yatirait mahep na reuhon aton beussirait maheubin ton ri
16	waheubal ena ra yekret raikon era aiheu gapra hara beuneheura geunapteuhon
17	reuma byat nat heurin deusyatret rwathon esyanet raikon
18	men ttittaut bit usyatret ameheu rahe reuma medem esyaan raikon ttittaut rau baheuda ruha hareheuneu waphen bekbasa
19	reuma tup saprin ton dalhon napkinan ruha keudam araha bamsiha meumalrinan uhulhen habibai mettul benyana hu dilhon
20	dahel na gel dalma ise rwathon ura etkaheuhon aiheu deussabe na wetteuhaheu reuhon ap ena aiheu ma deura ssabeton dalma heryana waheusama uhemsa uessyana umehal-kalssa uretna waheusirusa watgusya
21	dalma kat ise rwathon neumakhani arahi wesebel al sagiiye airen daheuttau ura tabu men ttanpusa umen janyusa umen paheujusa dabadu
