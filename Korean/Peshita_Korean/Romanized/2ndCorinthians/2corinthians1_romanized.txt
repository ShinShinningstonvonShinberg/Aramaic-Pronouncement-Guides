1	pauraut seuriha dyesyu meusiha beussebyaneheu daraha uttimaseaut aha rita daraha diit beukaurinsaut walhulhon kadise diit bahaiiya kuraheu
2	ttaibusa amhon watrama men araha abon umen maran yesyu meusiha
3	meubaraheu u araha abu deumaran yesyu meusiha aba deuraheume waraha deuhul buiya
4	hau dambaiya ran beuhulhon ulssanain dap heunan netkaheu neubaiya rairen daphul ulssanain enon beuhau buiyaa daheunan metbaiinan men araha
5	aikana gel deumesyatrin ban hasyaui damsiha hahana byat meusiha mesyatal ap buiyaan
6	apen den mesalssinan al apai buiyahon u ual apai haiyaikon mesalssinan wen metbaiinan mettul daton tetbaiun usehwe beuhon heupittusa datsaipron enon reuhase hanon dap heunan hasinan reuhon
7	usapran daraikon syaril u yadinan gel den syautapin ton beuhase syautapin ton ap beubuiyaa
8	ssabenan den deusedon ahain al ulssana dahwa ran basiiya deuraulbait eseretneu yatil men hairan dama deukaribin wau haiyain reumetttaraku
9	ual napsyan peusakneu mauta deura nehwe ran tuheurana al napsyan era al araha hau damkim mise
10	hau deumen maute hasine palkan usup meusaprinan deuparek ran
11	bamatranusa deubauthon dal apain deusehwe mauhapseheu darwasan ttaibusa dabida bapai sagiiye usagiiye naudon reheu al apain
12	syupharan gel hanau saheudusa deureyanan deubapsittusa wapdahyusa wapttaibusa daraha ethapaheuneu balma ura beuheheumeusa deupakra uyatirait rwathon dilhon
13	ra wa heuranyan katbinan reuhon era airen dyadin ton ap mettaudin ton teuhil na den dadama raheurata tettaudon
14	aikana dap ettaudaton karil men sagi deusyuphalhon heunan aiheu dap aton diran byaumeheu deumaran yesyu meusiha
15	waphana tuheurana ssabe wit men keudim dise rwathon daipait teukapron ttaibusa
16	webal raikon reumakedaunaiye usup men makedauniiya rwathon ise waton teuraunani rihut
17	hade hahil detrait reuma aiheu meusalheuba etrait au dalma dapsal enen airen deumetrae na mettul dware wa deunehwe beuhen in in ura ra
18	meuhaiman u araha deura hwat melsan darwathon in ura
19	beureheu gel daraha yesyu meusiha hau deubiidan etkeuret reuhon bi wapsirwanaut wapttimaseaut ra wa in ura era in hwa beheu
20	kulhon gel mulkanaui daraha beheu hu bamsiha in hwau mettul hana biideheu yaheubinan amin reusetbuheuteheu daraha
21	araha hu den meusyaral ran amhon bamsiha hau deuhu mathan
22	uhatman uyap raheubuna deuruheheu beurebawasan
23	ena den raraha mathet na al napsi deumettul deuhaet na raikon ra esit reukaurinsaut
24	ra wa mettul deumarai haimanuthon heunan era matrane heunan deuhadwathon beuhaimanusa hu gel kamin ton
