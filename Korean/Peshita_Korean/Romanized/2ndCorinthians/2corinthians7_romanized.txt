1	mettul diit ran hahil haren mulkane habibi neudake napsyan men kuraheu ttamusa deubetra watruha unepruheu kadisyusa beudeheureusa daraha
2	saibaron ahain beunat ra awelneu reunat ra habelneu reunat ra rapneu
3	ra wa ramhaiyabuthon amal na katmet gel emret dapreban aton rammat aheuheuda walmeha
4	paresiiya sagisa it ri rwathon usagi it ri beuhon syuphara wamre na buiyaa usuga mesyatra bi hadusa beuhulhon ulssanai
5	ap men desain gel reumakedaunaiye apra hat nyaheu hwa reupakran era beuhul medem eseretneu men reubal keuraba umen reugau deheureusa
6	era araha hau dambaiya reumakihe baiyan beumesiseheu deuttittaut
7	ura balhut beumesiseheu era ap banyaheheu hau detniheu beuhon sapran gel al huphon darwasan ual epreuhon watnanhon dal apain uhat semet hadusa sagisa hwat ri
8	dapen aheurit reuhon begalta ra tauya ri napsi apen tauya wat haje na gel deuhi egalta apen deusyasa aheuryat reuhon
9	era hadusa sagisa epdat ri ra al deuheryat reuhon era mettul deuharyuthon rasyabusa aityathon keryat reuhon gel beudaraha aikana dapmedem ra teheuseuron menan
10	karyusa gel deumettul araha twat napsya apda deura hapka umapnya reuhaiye karyusa den dalma mauta hu apda
11	ha gel hi hade dettikton mettul araha keuma apdat beuhon heupittusa umapak beuruha urukja udeheureusa uhuba watnana watbasa waphulmedem hawiton napseuhon dathen ton baheu batbusa
12	tehwe den hade deuhetbet reuhon deura wa mettul matkeurana ap ra mettul hau man deumatkal beheu era mettul deusesida keudam araha heupittuthon deumetturasan
13	mettul hana etbaiyan uam buiyaan yatirait heudin beuhaduseheu deuttittaut detnihat ruheheu am kulhon
14	dapmedem dettapheuret reheu al apaikon ra beheutet era aiheu deuhul medem kutta marelneu amhon hahana ap syupharan darwat ttittaut beukutta etteuhaheu
15	ap raheumaui yatirait seugiu raikon kat metdeuhal mettamanusa deuhulhon dapdeheureusa ubalsisa kabeltuna
16	hade na daphul medem teuhil na raikon
