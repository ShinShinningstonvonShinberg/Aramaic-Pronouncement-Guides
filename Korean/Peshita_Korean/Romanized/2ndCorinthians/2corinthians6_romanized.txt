3	reuma beumedem tetron reunat elsa deusukalsa deura nehwe muma beusetmettan
4	era beuhul medem neuhawe napsyan damsyamsyane heunan daraha bamsaipranusa sagisa buulssane banankat baheubusye
5	beunekde basure batgusye beureusa beusyaheura beussauma
6	beudahyusa bidasa beunagirut ruha beubasimusa beuruha deukutsya beuhuba deura neheura
7	beumelsa deukutta beuhaira daraha beujaina deujadikusa dabyamina wapsemara
8	beusyupha wapssara beukurasa wapgunaiya aiheu mattyane usyarire
9	aiheu ra idie widiinan aiheu misinan uha hain nan aiheu deumetreudenan ura matinan
10	aiheu deuharya ran waphuljeuban hadenan aiheu metkine walsagiiye matrinan aiheu deumedem rait ran uhul medem ahidinan
11	puman peusiheu u rwathon kaurinsaiye ureban rwiheu

14	ura tehon beunai jauga rairen deura meuhaimnin aida gel syautapusa it reujadikusa am aura au aina hulttana it reunahira am hesyuha
15	au aida syalmusa it ramsiha am sattana au aida meunasa it reudamhaimen am deura meuhaimen
16	aida den auyusa it reuhaikreheu daraha am deuside aton den haikreheu aton daraha haiya aikana damil demal beuhon wehareheu beuhon wehwe araheuhon uhenon nehon ri ama
