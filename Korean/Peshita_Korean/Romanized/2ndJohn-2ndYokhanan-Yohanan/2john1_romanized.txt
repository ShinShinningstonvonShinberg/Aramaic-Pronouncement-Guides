1	kasisya rakbisa kuriiya urapneheu airen dena mahep na batrara ra wa den ena balhudai era kulhon airen dyadu ratrara
2	mettul seurara aina damkawe ban uaman isaui raram
3	tehwe aman ttaibusa uraheume watrama men araha aba umen maran yesyu meusiha beureheu daba batrara waphuba
4	heudit sagi detkeuhet men beunaiki damhalhin batrara aikana deupukdana neusapnan men aba
5	uhasya meupit na rehi kuriiya ra wa aiheu pukdana hasa kasep na rehi era hau diit wa ran men syuraiya deunahep hat reuhat
6	uhana isaui huba danhareheu aiheu pukdanaui hana pukdana isaui aiheu ma datmaton men syuraiya deubeheu hwaiton meuhalhin
7	mettul deusagiiye mattyane neupaku beheu balma airen deura mauden dyesyu meusiha esa bapsal hana isaui mattyana wanttiheuritttaut
8	etdaru beunapseuhon deura taupdon medem dapraheuton era akra meusyalmana tetparon
9	kul aina dabal ura meukawe byulpaneheu damsiha araha rait beheu hau damkawe byulpaneheu hana uraba urapra it reheu
10	en nat ase rwathon uhana yulpana ra maite ra teukapruna beubaita waheudi raheu ra timron reheu
11	hau gel damal reheu heudi raheu meusyautap u rabadaui bise
12	kat sagiiyasa it wa ri reumeheutap reuhon ra bit dabyat kalttisa wadyusa era meusabal na dise rwathon upuma rwat puma neumarel deuhadusa diran tehwe meusyamrya
13	syarin seuramehi beunaiya deuhasehi geubisa amin
