1	waheujit heurina maraha danhet men seumaiya wamattap nana uketta datmaiya al riseheu uhejweheu aiheu semsya urekraui aiheu amude deunura
2	wiit reheu biideheu keusabuna peusiha usam rekreheu dyamina al yama deusemara den al ara
3	waka beukara rama aiheu arya deugasal uhat ka mareru syaba ramin beukaraihon
4	uhat mareru syaba ramin meuttaiyep wit reumeheutap usemet kara men seumaiya deusyaba damal heusum hau ma deumareru syaba ramin ura teheuteubiui
5	umaraha hau daheujit deukaem al yama ual yapsya darim ideheu ratmaiya
6	wima beuhau deuhai raram almin hau dapraheu ratmaiya watbaheu urara watbaheu deusup japna ra nehwe
7	era byaumasa deumaraha deusyaba ma dasit reumejak wettaram rajeheu daraha hau deusabal rapdaui neubiiye
8	ukara semet men seumaiya tup dammarel ami wamal jel sap raheusabuna deubiideheu deumaraha deukaem al ara ual yama
9	wejet rwat maraha kat amal na reheu reumetal ri raheusabuna wemal ri sap wahura unamal raheu kalsaheu era beupumaheu nehwe aiheu depsya
10	unetbet raheusabuna men ideheu deumaraha wehalteheu wiit wa beupumi aiheu depsya harya uhat ehalteheu merat kalsi
11	wemal ri ihip raheu tup japna reumetnabaiyu al amme wemwasa uresyane umalke sagiiye
