1	waheujit heurina maraha danhet men seumaiya diit raui keurida dathuma usisyalta rapsa biideheu
2	urapkeheu reusanina heuya katmaiya hau diisaui ahelkalssa usattana watreheu arep seunin
3	walmyeheu bathuma wehat watba rel meneheu deura tup natte reuhulhon amme basal haren ihip reumetryeheu karil japna
4	waheujit mautbe wisebu raihon udina esihep reuhon unapsyasa haren detpeusek mettul saheudusa dyesyu umettul melsa daraha udairen deura seugedu reuhaiyusa ura reussalmaheu ura neusabu rutma al bet ainaihon au al idaihon dahyau wamrehu am meusiha arep seunin
5	uhade i kyamta katmata
6	ttubana u ukadisya man diit reheu meunasa bakyamta katmata ual haren rait syulttana reumauta tenyana era nehon kaheune daraha udamsiha unamreuhon ameheu arep seunin
7	uma dettaram arep seunin netteure sattana men heubusyeheu
8	unepuk reumattaiyu reuhulhon amme balba jauyasaheu dara reuguk walmaguk uramhanasyu enon rakraba airen deumenyanhon aiheu hara dyama
9	watreku al peusaiyaheu dara uhatruheu ramdita deumatrisa deukadise uramdita habipta uneheutat nura men seumaiya men araha weheurat enon
10	wahelkalssa mattyanhon etreumi byamsa deunura uheprisa aika deuhaiyusa wanbiiya dagara unettankon imama urirya raram almin
11	waheujit kulsya raba hewara uradyasep rel meneheu hau deumen keudam apaui elkat ara watmaiya wasal ra etteuhaheu reuhon
12	waheujit reumise raulbe wajure deukamu keudam kulsya usepre etpeusahu waheurina sepra etpeusaheu diisaui deudina wetdinu mise men airen daheusiban beusepra aiheu badaihon
13	uyap yama mise deubeheu umauta wasyul yabu mise deusseidaihon wetdin hat hat menhon aiheu badaihon
14	umauta wasyul etreumiu byamsa deunura hana diisaui mauta tenyana
15	waina deura etteuhaheu dalsim baheusaba deuhaiye etreumi byamsa deunura
