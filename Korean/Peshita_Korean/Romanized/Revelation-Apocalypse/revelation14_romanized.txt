1	waheujit uha emra kaem al ttura deussehyon uameheu ma walbin walba alpin diit raihon seumeheu watma dabu keusip al bet ainaihon
2	usemet kara men seumaiya aiheu kara deumaiya sagiiye waiheu kara deurama raba kara aina deusemet aiheu kisaruda deunaket beukisaraui
3	wamsyaphin aiheu tetbuheuta heudasa keudam kulsya wakdam alba haiwan wakdam kasise ura nat etmeussi reumilpaheu reusetbuheuta era en ma walbin walba alpin jeubinai men ara
4	haren enon airen dam nese ra etttawasu beusure gel isaihon haren deunakpu remra kul kal deunijal haren etdeubenu men nasya risisa raraha uremra
5	dappumhon ra ettaheuhat dagarusa deura mum gel enon
6	waheujit heurina maraha deuparaheu messat seumaiya wiit reheu raui seubalsa dararam ramsabaru al yatbai ara ual kul am wemwan usyalban uresyan
7	reumimal beukara raba deuharu men araha uhabu reheu tetbuheuta mettul desat syasa deudineheu watgudu reudabat seumaiya wara uyama uainasa deumaiya
8	waheurina datren neukip wa reheu wamal neprat neprat babel rapsa aida deumen hemta deujanyusaheu atkyat reuhulhon amme
9	waheurina maraha datrasa neukep reuhon reumimal beukara raba aina datget reuhaiyusa walssalmaheu watkal rutmaheu bet ainaui
10	ap hu nette men hamra deuhemseheu deumarya damjik deura heltta beuhasa deurukjeheu unettanak beunura uheprisa keudam marahe kadise wakdam emra
11	watnana deusatnikhon raram almin nesak urait reuhon neupisya imama urirya airen deusakdin reuhaiyusa walssalmaheu walman datkal rutma datmaheu
12	halka iseheu meusaipranusa deukadise airen danttaru pukdanaui daraha uhaimanuseheu dyesyu
13	usemet kara men seumaiya damal keusup ttubaihon reumise airen danadu beumaran men hasya in amal ruha mettul deunetteunihon men amraihon
14	uha nana hewalta ual nana yasep deumusa deubalnasya wiit reheu al riseheu keurira deudaheuba ual ideheu magalsa haripta
15	waheurina maraha neupak men haikra waka beukara raba radyasep al nana syadal magalsaheu waheussut mettul desat syasa reumeheussat
16	walmi hau dyasep al nana magalseheu al ara wethatdat ara
17	waheurina maraha neupak men haikra deubatmaiya waraui it magalsa haripta
18	waheurina maraha neupak men matbeuha diit reheu syulttana al nura waka beukara raba reudiit reheu magalsa haripta syadal at magalsaheu haripta wakttup ratgure deuhalmaheu dara mettul dalbai enbaui
19	walmi maraha magalseheu al ara wakttap reuhalmaheu dara walmi beumassalta rapsa deuhemseheu daraha
20	wetteudisyat massalta reubal men meudita wanpak deuma men massalta dama rapgude deuraheusya al arep umasen ettteudawan
