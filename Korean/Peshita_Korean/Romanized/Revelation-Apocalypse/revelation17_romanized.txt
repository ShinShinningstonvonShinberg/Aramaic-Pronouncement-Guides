1	wesa hat men syaba marahe diit raihon seuba jaburin umarel ami reumimal ta basari ehaweheu dina deujanisa dyatba al maiya sagiiye
2	damaheu janiu malkeheu dara warwiu kulhon amureheu dara men hamra deujanyusaheu
3	wapkani reuhulba beuruheu waheujit atsa dyatba al haiyusa sumakta deumarya seumahe deugudapa diit raheu rise syaba kalnasa den sal
4	watsa it wa damatpa algaune wathurisa deumatheuban beudaheuba uhipe ttabasa umalganyasa wiit raheu kasa deudaheuba al idaheu wamre ttamusa usuiyaba deujanyusaheu
5	ual bet aineheu keusip raja babel rapsa ema deujanyasa watsuiyabeheu dara
6	waheujit atsa deurauya men deuma deukadise umen deuma deusaheudaui dyesyu wetdamret dumara raba kat heujisaheu
7	wemal ri maraha reumana etdamalteu ena amal na raheu raja datsa wathaiyusa dattina raheu diit raheu syaba risin wasal kalnan
8	haiyusa daheujait iseheu wat uraiteheu sida deusesak men yama urapdana aja unetdamron amrai al ara hanon deura keusibin seumahaihon beusepra deuhaiye men talmyaseheu dalma deuhajen haiyusa diiseheu wat uraiteheu ukelbat
9	halka hauna reudiit reheu heheumeusa syaba risin syaba enon tturin aika dyatba atsa raihon
10	umalke syaba enon hamsya neuparu uhat isaui hau heurina ra dakil esa uma desa karil ihip reheu ramhataru
11	usanina uhaiyusa ha diiseheu uraiteheu uha datmanya umen syaba i urapdana aja
12	wasal kalnan daheujait etra malkin enon airen deumalkusa ra dakil neusabu era syulttana aiheu malke heuda syasa syakrin am haiyusa
13	haren hat ssebyana it reuhon uhaira usyulttana dilhon reuhaiyusa yaheubin
14	haren am emra nakreubon wemra netke enon mettul deumare hu deumarawasa wamreheu malke wadameheu keuraiya wakbaiya wamhaimne
15	wemal ri maiya daheujait daraihon yatba janisa amme uhense wemwasa uresyane isaihon
16	wasal kalnasa daheujait reuhaiyusa haren netnyan reujanisa uharapta ualtterata nepdunaheu ubetraheu neheuron unaukdunaheu beunura
17	araha gel yahep beurebawathon deunepdon ssebyaneheu unepdon ssebyanhon hat unetron malkuthon reuhaiyusa ha dama deunettamryan meraui daraha
18	watsa aida daheujait meudita rapsa aida diit raheu malkusa al malkeheu dara
