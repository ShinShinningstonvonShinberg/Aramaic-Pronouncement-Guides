1	wathamsya jak waheujit kaukba danpal men seumaiya al ara wesihep reheu keurida deubiraui dathuma
2	watrek teunana men bire aiheu teunana datuna raba deumetteugal waheuseheu semsya waal men teunana deubire
3	umen teunana neupaku kamsse al ara wesihep reuhon syulttana diit rekalbe dara
4	wesemal reuhon deura naheuron retbaheu dara walhul yurak apra riirane era en rapnainasya airen deurait reuhon hatma daraha bet ainaihon
5	wesihep reuhon deura nektteuron enon era nettankon yalhe hamsya usatnikhon aiheu tatnika dekalba ma deunapra al nat
6	wabyaumasa hanon nebon beunainasya reumauta ura netkeuhuna unetrakreugon rammat uneruk mauta menhon
7	watmusa deukamsse aiheu deumusa deuraheusya damttaibin rakraba ual risyaihon aiheu keurira datmusa deudaheuba wapaihon aiheu ape deunasya
8	usara it reuhon aiheu sara deunese usenaihon aiheu daryawasa
9	wiit wa reuhon seryana aiheu seryana deupaljeura ukara deugepaihon aiheu kara deumalkeubasa deuraheusya sagiiye deuraheuttin rakraba
10	wiit reuhon dunbyasa aiheu deumusa dekalba uukse den beudunbyathon usyulttanhon reumaharu rapnainasya yalhe hamsya
11	wiit raihon malka maraheheu dathuma datmeheu eprait abadu walmait seuma reheu it syare
12	wa hat ejal ha tup asen teuren wa
13	basal haren maraha detta jak usemet kara hat men alba kalnaseheu deumatbeuha deudaheuba dakdam araha
14	damal reumaraha seusisaiya diit reheu sipura seuri ralba marahe dasirin al naheura raba peurat
15	wetteuriu alba marahin airen damttaibin reusyasa waryauma waryalha walsyata deunektteuron tulsa dapnainasya
16	umenyana deuhairawasa deuparase talten rebu rebwan semet menyanhon
17	uhahana heujit raheusya beuhejwa uradyatbin raihon it seryana deunura ukalhetna deuheprisa ukalkeupasa deuraheuseuhon aiheu kalkeupasa daryawasa umen pumhon napka nura uheprisa watnana
18	umen haren teurat mahwan etkattaru tulsa dapnainasya umen nura umen keprisa umen teunana deunapek men pumhon
19	mettul deusyulttana deuraheusya beupumhon wap beudunbyathon
20	usyalka dapnainasya deura etkeuttaru beumahwasa haren ura tabu men bat idaihon deura netgeudon reudaiwe urapsaheure deudaheuba watsima udanhasya watkaisa wathipa airen deura reumeheuja ura reumetma meussen au ramharahu
21	ura tabu men ketraihon umen halsyaihon umen janyuthon
