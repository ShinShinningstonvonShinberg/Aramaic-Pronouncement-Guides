1	waheujit kat peusaheu emra hat men syaba ttabin usemet raheuda men alba haiwan damra aiheu kara deurame ta waheuji
2	usemet waheujit uha susya hewara wadyasep raui it reheu ketta wesihep reheu keurira wanpak jaka ujahe watnetke
3	uhat peusaheu ttaba datren semet reuhaiyusa deusalten damra ta
4	wanpak susya sumaka uradyasep raui esihep reheu reumesap seurama men ara deuraheudade neunakson wesyaheubat reheu halba rapsa
5	uhat etpeusaheu ttaba datrasa semet reuhaiyusa datrat damra ta uha susya ukama wadyasep raui it masasa biideheu
6	usemet kara men bet haiwasa damal kaba deuhette beudinara watrasa kabin dasara beudinara walhamra walmetha ra tahal
7	uhat peusaheu ttaba dalba semet kara deuhaiyusa damra ta
8	waheujit susya yuraka watmeheu deuhau dyasep raui mauta wasyul neukipa reheu wesihep reheu syulttana al rubaheu dara dankattel beuhalba waphapna wapmauta waphaiyusa dara
9	uhat peusaheu reuttaba deuhamsya heujit reusaheuteu men matbeuha reunapsyasa detkattal mettul melsa daraha umettul saheudusa dyesyu ha diit wa reuhon
10	wakau beukara raba wamrin dama remasi marya kadisya usyarira ra danat usabat deuman men amureheu dara
11	wesyaheubat reuhulhat hat menhon ettteura hewalta wesemal deunetteunihon at edan jeuban jul dama deumettamren ap keunawathon wahaihon airen dasidin reumetkattaru aiheu dap hanon
12	waheujit kat peusaheu ttaba detta unauda raba wa usemsya aiheu saka deusara ukam hwa usaheura kureheu hwa reheu aiheu deuma
13	uhaukbe datmaiya neuparu al ara aiheu tita deusyadya pakueheu men ruha asinta ma deumetteujia
14	watmaiya etpeuret waiheu keusabe etkeurehu uhul ttul uhul gajalta men dukathon etteujiu
15	umalke dara uraulbane urisyai alpe uatire uhairawasa uhul apde wapnai hire ttasiu napseuhon bamare wapsyue deutture
16	wamrin reutture usyue deuperu rain uttasyau ran men keudam apaui demra
17	mettul desa yauma raba deurukjeuhon umanu metkaheu ramkam
