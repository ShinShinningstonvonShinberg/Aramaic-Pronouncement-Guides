1	wesihep ri kanya deumusa deusyaptta ukaem wa maraha wamal kum wamsyuheu reuhaikra daraha walmatbeuha urairen deusakdin beheu
2	waldalta dalgau men haikra apek men reubal ura temseuhiheu mettul desyaheubat ramme uramdita kaditta neudusyon yalhe albin watren
3	wetel ratren saheudai reumetnabaiyu yaumin arep umasen wettin kat ttipin sake
4	haren enon teuren jaitin usalten meunaran dakdam mara deuhuraheu ara kamin
5	uman deubae deunahal enon napka nura men pumhon waheura rabeldeubabaihon uraina deussabe deunahal enon hahan ihip reuhon reumetkeuttaru
6	uharen it reuhon syulttana deuneheudon ratmaiya deura nehut metra byaumasa danbiiyuthon wiit reuhon syulttana deunaheupeuhon maiya ratma watnemhon rara beuhul mahwan keuma deunetbon
7	uma deusyamriu saheuduthon haiyusa deusalka men yama tebet amhon keuraba usetke enon usekttul enon
8	watradaihon al syuke damdita rapsa aida deumetkarya ruhanait seudum umetren aika deumalhon ettteurep
9	uhajen men emwasa usyalbasa uresyane uamme ratradaihon teurasa yaumin upelgeheu uratradaihon ra netbeukon reumetteusamu beukapre
10	uamureheu dara neheudon raihon unetpathon umauheubasa neusyatron raheudade mettul teuren neubiin deusyaneku ramureheu dara
11	umen basal teurasa yaumin upelgeheu ruha haisa men araha erat beuhon ukamu al rekraihon uruha deuhaiye neprat raihon udeheureusa rapsa hwat al airen deuhajen reuhon
12	watmau kara raba men seumaiya damal reuhon saku reuha watreku ratmaiya banana umatdin beuhon beldeubabaihon
13	wapsyasa ha hwa jaua raba uhat men etra damdita neuparu wetkattaru beujaua seumahe deugapre alpe syaba watsyalka hwau beudeheureusa uyabon tetbuheuta raraha deubatmaiya
14	ha teuren wa ejaru uha wa datrasa ase meheuda
15	umaraha deusyaba jak wahwau kare raulbe batmaiya damrin hwat malkuseheu dalma darahan udamsiheheu wamreheu raram almin
16	uetrin walba kasise airen dakdam araha yatbin al kulsawathon neuparu al apaihon watgedu raraha
17	reumimal maudenan raheu marya araha ahit kul diisaui wiisaui wa dansapteu beuhairaheu raba wamreheuteu
18	uamme reugeju wesa rukjaheu ujapna deumise deunetdinon usetel akra rapdaik neubiiye walkadise waldaheurai seumaheu rajure am raulbe wathabel rairen deuhaberu rara
19	wetpeusaheu haikra batmaiya wethajyat kibusa deudiiyasiki direheu beuhaikra wahwau balke urame ukare unauda ubalda raba
