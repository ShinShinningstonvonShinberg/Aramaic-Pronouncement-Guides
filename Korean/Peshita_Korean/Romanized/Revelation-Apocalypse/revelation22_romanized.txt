1	uhauyani naheura deumaiya haiye dahya ap nahira aiheu geurida unapek men kulsyeheu daraha udemra
2	umessat syukeheu meka umeka al naheura kaisa deuhaiye dabet pire teuresal waphul iraheu yahep piraui uttalpaui rasyusa damme
3	uhul helma ra nehwe taman uhulsyeheu daraha udemra baheu nehwe uapdaui neusyamsyuna
4	uneheujon apaui watmeheu al bet ainaihon
5	urirya ra nehwe taman ura netbe reuhon nuheura watraga unuheureheu deusemsya mettul deumarya araha manhal reuhon umalkeuhon raram almin
6	wemal ri haren mere meuhaimnan usyariran umarya araha deuruhasa danbiiye kadise syadal reumaraheheu ramhawaiyu rapdaui ma dihip reumehwa bagal
7	uha ase na bagal ttubaui reuman deunattal mere danbiiyusa daheusaba hana
8	ena yuhanan daheuja watma haren uhat heujit usemet nepret reumetgat keudam rekraui deumaraha damhawe ri haren
9	wemal ri heuji ra keunasaheu isai udahaik neubiiye udairen deunatrin haren mere daheusaba hana raraha seugut
10	wemal ri ra teheutum mere danbiiyusa daheusaba hana japna gel keurep
11	watmawel tup nawel watssa tup netttassa ujadika tup nebet jadikusa ukadisya tup netkadat
12	ha ase na meheuda wakri ami wetel reuhulnat aiheu badeheu
13	ena arap wena tau katmaiya waheuraiya usyuraiya usyurama
14	ttubaihon radapdin pukdanaui nehwe syulttanhon al kaisa deuhaiye wapsara neron ramdita
15	ujanaiye ukatture upalhai peusaheure reubal uttame uharase uhul hajaiyai uapdai dagarusa
16	ena yesyu syatret reumarahi dansahet beuhon haren keudam idasa ena na ekara usyalbeuseheu deudawit uameheu uhaukap ssapra nahira
17	uruha uhalsa amrin ta watsyama nimal ta udathe nise unesap maiya haiye magan
18	meusahet na reuhul deusyama melsa danbiiyusa daheusaba hana daina dansim raihen neusim raui araha mahwasa daheusiban baheusaba hana
19	waina dambassal men mere daheusaba danbiiyusa hade neubassal araha meunaseheu men kaisa deuhaiye umen meudita kaditta airen daheusiban baheusaba hana
20	amal kat meusahet haren in ase na bagal ta marya yesyu
21	ttaibuseheu deumaran yesyu meusiha am kulhon kadisyaui amin
