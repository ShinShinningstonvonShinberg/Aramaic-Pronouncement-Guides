1	usemet kara raba men haikra damal reusyaba marahin jeru wasyudu seuba jaburin deuhemseheu daraha al ara
2	wejal katmaiya wesyat jabureheu al ara wahwa syuheuna bisya uhibana al nasya diit reuhon rutma deuhaiyusa wairen deusakdin reussalmaheu
3	umaraha datren esyat jabureheu byama wahwa yama aiheu misa uhul napsya haisa misat byama
4	umaraha datrasa esyat jabureheu beunaheurawasa wabainasa deumaiya wahwau deuma
5	usemet reumaraha deumaiya damal jadik at hau diisaui wiisaui wa uhasya deuharen danteu
6	mettul datma danbiiye watkadise esyadu watma yapteu reuhon reumetta syawen enon
7	usemet reumatbeuha damal in marya araha ahit kul syaririn ujadikin dinaik
8	umaraha dalba esyat jabureheu al semsya wesihep reheu deunahem rapnainasya beunura
9	wetheumemu beunainasya beuhuma raba ugadepu ratma daraha diit reheu syulttana al mahwasa haren ura tabu reumetal reheu tetbuheuta
10	umaraha deuhamsya esyat jabureheu al kulsyaheu deuhaiyusa wahwat malkusaheu hesyuheuta wamrasin wau resyanaihon men kiba
11	ugadepu ratma daraha datmaiya men kibaihon umen syuheunaihon ura tabu men badaihon
12	umaraha detta esyat jabureheu al naheura raba peurat wibesu maui deusetttaiyap ulha deumalke men matneuhai semsya
13	waheujit men pumeheu deusanina umen pumaheu deuhaiyusa umen pumeheu danbiiya dagara ruhe teurat ra dahyasa aiheu ulde
14	isaihen gel ruhe deuside airen dapdan aswasa dajan al malke deusibel ramhanasyu enon rakraba dyauma hau raba daraha ahit kul
15	ha ase aiheu ganaba ttubaui reuhau dil unattal manaui deura alttel neuhareheu uneheujon beheuteuseheu
16	wanhanet enon ratra deumetkeure eprait makdu
17	umaraha deusyaba esyat jabureheu baal wanpak kara raba men haikra men keudam kulsya damal hwa
18	wahwau balke urame unauda hwa raba dahwaseheu ra hwa men dahwau beunainasya al ara daiheu hana jaua hahana rap wa
19	wahwat meudita rapsa ratrat meunawan wamdinasa damme neupari ubabel rapsa etdaheurat keudam araha reumetal raheu kasa deuhamra deuhemseheu watrukjeheu
20	uhul gajalta elkat utture ra etteuhahu
