1	karip wa den adida deupattire deumetkeure petha
2	ubaen wau rabai kaheune usapre daikana nektteuruna daheurin wau gel men ama
3	al wa den sattana bihuda deumetkeure seuharyutta diisaui wa men menyana datresal
4	wejal marel am rabai kaheune usapre urabai haire deuhaikra aiheu deunatreumiui reuhon
5	waheudiu wakimu deunetron reheu ketpa
6	wettaudi reuhon ubae wa pera deunatreumiui reuhon berat men kensya
7	umatti yauma deupattire deubeheu it wa yada deunetneuhet petha
8	usyadal yesyu reuhipa waryuhanan wemal reuhon jeru ttaiyebu ran petha deunerat
9	henon den emaru reheu aika ssabe at danttaiyep
10	amal reuhon ha ma darin ton ramdita paga beuhon gapra datkil geuraba deumaiya jeru batreheu
11	waika dael emaru reumareheu deubaita raban amal ainau bet matrya aika dehul petha am talmidai
12	uha hu meuhawe reuhon erisa heuda rapsa damsyauya taman ttaiyebu
13	wejaru etkahu aiheu demal reuhon uttaiyebu petha
14	uhat hwa edana esa yesyu etteumeheu watresal seurihe ameheu
15	wemal reuhon reksa raktani deuhana petha ehul amhon keudam dehat
16	amal na reuhon gel deumekil ra eheuriui dama deunetram beumalkuseheu daraha
17	wansap kasa waudi wemal sabu hana uparegu bainathon
18	amal na gel reuhon deura ette men yalda dakpeta dama deusise malkusa daraha
19	wansap raheuma waudi wakssa uyap reuhon wemal hanau pakri dal apaikon mesihep hade hwaiton apdin reuduheurani
20	uhahwat ap al kasa men basal daheusemu emal hana kasa diiyasiki heudasa beudemi daheurapaikon meseset
21	beuram ha ideheu deumatreumani al pasura
22	wapreheu deunasya ajel aikana detpeuret beuram wa reuhau gapra deubiideheu metteurem
23	usyariu danakbon bainathon deumanu kai menhon hau deuhade sit reumesal
24	hwa den ap heryana bainathon deuman it beuhon deurap
25	hu den yesyu emal reuhon malkaihon damme maraihon enon watsyarittin raihon apdai ttabasa metkeuren
26	aton den ra hahana era aina deurap beuhon nehwe aiheu jura waina deurisya u aiheu meusyamsyana
27	manu gel rap hau datmiheu au hau damsyamet ra wa hau datmiheu ena den isai bainathon aiheu hau damsyamet
28	aton enon den deuhatalton rwasi beunesyunai
29	wena mettaude na reuhon aiheu dettaudi ri abi malkusa
30	deuseheuron usetton al pasura deumalkusa diri usetbon al kulsawasa watdunon teuresal syaptte diitraiyel
31	wemal yesyu reusemon semon ha sattana syael deuneruphon aiheu dalhette
32	wena bit raik deura teheusal haimanusaheu ap at batban etpani usyaral ahaik
33	semon den emal reheu mari amaheu meuttaiyap na walbet asire walmauta
34	amal reheu yesyu amal na raheu semon deura nekre talnakra yaumana dama datrat japnin teheupul deura yada at ri
35	wemal reuhon kat syadalteuhon deura kise watra talmare wamsane reuma heusal reuhon medem amrin reheu ura medem
36	amal reuhon men hasya man diit reheu kisa nesap uhahana ap talmara uman deurait reheu saipa neujaben naheuteheu unetben reheu saipa
37	amal na reuhon gel dap hade daheusiba ware deusetmare bi dam aware etmeune kulhen gel darai ettaram
38	uhenon amrin reheu maran ha halka it teuren saipin amal reuhon sapkin
39	wanpak wejal aiheu damat wa reuttura deubet jaite wejaru batreheu ap talmidaui
40	uhat matti reuduksa emal reuhon ssarau deura teron reunesyuna
41	uhu peurak menhon aiheu matde kipa usam bulkaui wamssare wa
42	wamal aba en ssabe at neprani kasa hana beuram ra ssebyani era diraheu nehwe
43	wetheuji reheu maraha men seumaiya damhaiyel reheu
44	uhat hwa beudeheureusa teuhibait meussare wa wahwat duteheu aiheu selse datma wanpal al ara
45	ukam men sseuruseheu wesa rwat talmidaui wetkaheu enon kat damkin men aksa
46	wemal reuhon mana damkin ton kumu ssarau deura teron reunesyuna
47	uat hu meumarel ha kensya uhau deumetkeure ihuda hat men teuresal ase keudamaihon wakrep rwat yesyu unatkeheu hade gel asa yap wa reuhon daina deunasek na huiyu
48	amal reheu yesyu ihuda beunusyakta matrem at reheu rapreheu deunasya
49	kat heujau den airen dameheu medem dahwa amrin reheu maran nemhe enon beusaipe
50	wamha hat menhon rapdeheu deurap kaheune unatbaheu etneheu dyamina
51	na den yesyu wemal kadu dama reuhade wakrep retneheu deuhau dapra wasyaheu
52	wemal yesyu reuhanon desau raui rabai kaheune ukasise urabai haire deuhaikra aiheu dal retttaiya neupakton rai beusaipe waphutre deuseheudunani
53	kuryum amhon wit beuhaikra ura ausetton rai idaiya era hade i syaathon usyulttana deuhesyuha
54	wehadu aityu reubaiteheu deurap kaheune usemon ase wa batreheu men ruheuka
55	auhedu den nura messat dalta uyatbin wau heudareheu uyasep wa ap hu semon bainathon
56	waheujaseheu raimsa heuda dyasep rwat nura uharat beheu wamra ap hana ameheu hwa
57	hu den keupal wemal atsa ra yada na reheu
58	ubasal karil heuja heurina wemal reheu ap at menhon at kipa den emal ra hwit
59	ubasal syaa heuda heurina metheure wa wamal syarirait ap hana ameheu wa ap geuriraiya u gel
60	amal kipa gapra ra yada na mana amal at umeheuda kat hu meumarel keura talnakra
61	wetpeuni yesyu uhal beuhipa wetdeuhal semon melseheu deumaran demal wa reheu dakdam deunekre talnakra teheupul bi teurat japnin
62	wanpak reubal semon beuha marirait
63	ugapre dahidin hwau ryesyu meubathin wau beheu wamhapen wau reheu
64	umahen wau reheu al apaui wamrin etnaba manu meuhaheu
65	waheuranyasa sagiiyasa meugatpin wau wamrin raui
66	uhat nekhat etkanasu kasise urabai kaheune usapre watku reubet keunutteuhon
67	wamrin reheu en at u meusiha emal ran amal reuhon en imal reuhon ra teuhaimnunani
68	wen esyaelhon ra meupanen ton ri petgama au syaren ton ri
69	men hasya nehwe beureheu deunasya yasep men yamina deuhaira daraha
70	amrin den kulhon at u hahil beureheu daraha amal reuhon yesyu aton amrin ton dena na
71	amrin mana tup metben ran saheude heunan gel seuman men pumeheu
