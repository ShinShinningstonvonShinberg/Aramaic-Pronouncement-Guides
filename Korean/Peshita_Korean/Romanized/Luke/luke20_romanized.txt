1	wahwa beuhat men yaumasa kat marep beuhaikra rama wamsabal kamu raui rabai kaheune usapre am kasise
2	wamrin reheu emal ran baina syulttana haren abet at umanu hau dyap raheu syulttana hana
3	na yesyu wemal reuhon esyaelhon ap ena melsa wemaru ri
4	mamudiseheu dyuhanan men seumaiya wat au men beunai nasya
5	henon den methatbin wau beunapseuhon wamrin den nimal men seumaiya amal ran umettul mana ra haimentuna
6	en den nimal men beunai nasya ragem ran ama kureheu meupasin gel dyuhanan neubiiya hu
7	wemaru reheu deura yadinan men aimeka i
8	amal reuhon yesyu ura ena amal na reuhon baina syulttana haren abet na
9	usyari deunimal rama matra hana gapra hat neussap kalma wauheudeheu reuparahe wabet japna sagiiya
10	wapjapna syadal apdeheu rwat parahe deunetron reheu men pire deuhalma parahe den meuhau usyatru kat seurik
11	wausep usyadal rapdeheu heurina henon den ap reuhau meuhau ussaru usyatru kat seurik
12	wausep usyadal datrasa henon den wap reuhau ssalpu wapku
13	amal mare kalma mana ebet esyadal beri habiba keubal neheujuna unetkaheudon
14	kat heujau den parahe methatbin wau beunapseuhon wamrin hanau yalta tau nektteuriui usehwe yaltusa diran
15	wapku reubal men kalma ukatru mana hahil nebet reuhon mare kalma
16	nise unaubet reuparahe hanon unetel kalma raheurane kat seumau den emaru ra tehwe hade
17	hu den hal beuhon wemal umana i ha daheusiba deuhipa datriu banaiye hi hwat reurit kalna deujawisa
18	uhul deunepel al ha kipa netra uhul man deuhi tepel raui tetreui
19	bau wau den rabai kaheune usapre deunalmon raui idaiya beuha syasa watheru men ama idau gel daraihon emal matra hana
20	usyadaru rwaseheu gasyuse deumetdamen beujadike deuneheuduna beumelsa unatreumuna reudaiyana walsyulttaneheu deuhikmuna
21	usyaru wamrin reheu malpana yadinan datrissait meumarel at umarep ura nasep at bape era beukutta ulha daraha marep at
22	syarit ran deunetel keusep risya reukesal au ra
23	hu den ettakal haruthon wemal mana meunasen ton ri
24	hawauni dinara deuman it beheu ssalma waheusibasa henon den emaru deukesal
25	amal reuhon yesyu habu hahil deukesal reukesal udaraha raraha
26	ura etkahu reumehat meneheu melsa keudam ama wetdamaru al petgameheu watseku
27	keurebu den nasin men jadukaiye hanon damrin dakyamta rait usyaru
28	wamrin reheu malpana muse keusap ran den nat neumut ahu diit reheu atsa deura beunaiya nesap ahu atseheu wankim jara rahu
29	syaba den ahin it wau katmaiya neusap atsa umit deura beunaiya
30	unatbaheu datren ratseheu uhana mit deura beunaiya
31	udatrasa tup natbaheu uhahwat wap syabasaihon umisu ura seubaku beunaiya
32	umisat beuhalsa ap atsa
33	bakyamta hahil daina menhon tehwe atsa syabasaihon gel natbuheu
34	amal reuhon yesyu beunaui dalma hana natbin nese unese hauyan reugapre
35	hanon den dalhau alma swau urakyamta deumen bet mise ra natbin nese wap ra nese hauyan reugapre
36	apra gel tup rammat metkeuhin aiheu marahe enon gel wapnaiya isaihon daraha mettul dahwau beunaiya dakyamta
37	deukamin den mise ap muse badek atkal gel beusanya kat amal marya araheheu dapraham waraheheu diithak waraheheu dyakup
38	araha den ra wa deumise era deuhaiye kulhon gel hain enon reheu
39	wanau nasin men sapre wamrin reheu malpana syapil amal at
40	ura tup amrahu ramsyaaruseheu al medem
41	wemal wa reuhon aikana amrin sapre al meusiha dapreheu u deudawit
42	uhu dawit emal baheusaba deumatmure demal marya reumari tep raheu men yamini
43	dama desim beldeubabaik teuhet rekraik
44	en hahil dawit mari kare reheu aikana beureheu u
45	uhat kureheu ama syama wa emal reusalmidaui
46	etdaru men sapre deussaben ramharahu bettteure uraheumin seurama beusyuke urit mautbe baheunusyasa urit seumahe baheusyamyasa
47	hanon daheurin bate dalmeurasa belsa deumaulhin sseurawathon henon neukapron dina yatira
