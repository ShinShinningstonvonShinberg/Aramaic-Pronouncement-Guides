2	usapre waprise ratnin wau wamrin hana reuhattaiye meukabel wahel amhon
3	wemal reuhon yesyu matra hana
4	manu menhon gapra diit reheu ma elbin wen nibat hat menhon ra syabek tesin usesa beudapra wajel bae reuhau debat dama deunetkeuhiui
5	uma detkeuheheu hade usyakel reheu al katpaseheu
6	wase reubaiteheu ukare reuraheumaui uratbabaui wamal reuhon heudau ami detkeuhet elbi dabit hwa
7	amal na reuhon deuhahana tehwe hadusa batmaiya al hat hattaiya deusaep au al tesin usesa jadikin deura metbaya reuhon tyabusa
8	au aida i atsa diit raheu etra jujin usaubet hat menhon ura manheura seuraga uhama baita ubaya reheu beuttirait dama deusetkeuhiui
9	uma detkaheuseheu karya reuraheumasaheu uratbabasaheu wamra reuhen heudaiyen ami detkeuhet juji dabit wa
10	amal na reuhon deuhahana tehwe hadusa keudam marahaui daraha al hat hattaiya deusaep
11	wemal wa reuhon tup yesyu reugapra hat it wa reheu beunaiya teuren
12	wemal reheu beureheu jura abi hap ri palgusa deumattya ri men baitaheu uparek reuhon kenyaneheu
13	umen basal yaumasa karil kanet hau beureheu jura kul medem damtta wejal ratra rahika usaman badal kenyaneheu kat haiye parahait
14	uhat gamal kul medem diit wa reheu hwa kapna raba batra hau usyari hasal reheu
15	wejal neukep reheu reuhat men beunai meudita datra hau uhu syatreheu rakrisa reumera heujire
16	umetrakrak wa reumemra kalseheu men harube hanon daheurin wau heujire ura nat yahep wa reheu
17	uhat esa rwat napseheu emal keuma hasya agire it bet abi dyatil reuhon raheuma wena halka reuhapni abet na
18	ekum ijal rwat abi wimal reheu abi heuttit batmaiya wakdamaik
19	ura mekil syawe na dapraheu etkeure bedaini aiheu hat men agiraik
20	ukam esa rwat abu uat hu rahik heuja abu wetraham raui walhet neupal al ssaureheu unatkeheu
21	wemal reheu beureheu abi heuttit batmaiya wakdamaik ura syawe na dapraheu etkeure
22	emal den abu rapdaui apeku ettteura risyata albesyu usimu ejeksa biideheu wasenu meusane
23	waitau keutturu taura deupetma unehul unetbasam
24	deuhana beri misa hwa wahya wabida hwa wetteuhaheu usyariu reumetbasamu
25	hau den beureheu kasisya bakrisa wa uhat esa wakrep rwat baita seuma kal jeumara deusagiiye
26	wakra reuhat men tteuraiye usyareheu manau hana
27	amal reheu ahuheu esa wakttal abuheu taura deupetma deuhat heurim akbeureheu
28	walget ura ssabe wa reumeal wanpak abu ba meneheu
29	hu den emal rabu ha keuma seunin paraheu na raheu apdusa ura memsum epret pukdanaheu umen meusum gadya ra yapteu ri detbasam am raheumai
30	reuhana den beuraheu kat paraheu kenyanaheu am janyasa wesa neuhatteu reheu taura deupetma
