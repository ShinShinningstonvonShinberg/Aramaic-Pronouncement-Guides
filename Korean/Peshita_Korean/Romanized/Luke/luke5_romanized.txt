1	hwa den kat keunat raui kensya reumetma melsa daraha uhu kaem wa al yat yamsa deugenesal
2	heuja seupine talten deukaman al gep yamsa ussaiyade datreku menhen wamsigin meussidathon
3	waheuda menhen deusemon hwat kipa watrek yesyu isep baheu wemal deunetbeurunaheu karil men yapsya reumaiya uyasep wa umarep men seupita reuhensya
4	uhat seusek men mamreureheu emal reusemon deubaru rumka walmau meussitteuhon reussaida
5	na semon wemal reheu rabi rirya kureheu riin umedem ra ehatneu al melsaheu den rame na meussitta
6	uhat hade badu heubasu nune sagiiye deuttap umetttarya wat meussitteuhon
7	walmaju reuhapraihon deubatpita heurisa deunison natron enon uhat esau meurau enen seupine taltaihen aiheu deukariban wai reumetba
8	kat heuja den semon kipa neupal keudam rekraui dyesyu wemal reheu bae na menaheu mari peuruk raheu meni deugapra na hattaiya
9	temha gel aheudeheu wa walhulhon dameheu al ssaida hau deunune deussadu
10	hahwat den ap ryakup waryuhanan beunai japdai diisaihon wau syautape deusemon emal den yesyu reusemon ra tethal men hasya beunai nasya tehwe ssaet reuhaiye
11	ukarebu enen seupine rara watbaku kul medem wesau batreheu
12	uhat hwa yesyu baheuda men meudinasa esa gapra damre kureheu galba heuja ryesyu wanpal al apaui ubae wa meneheu wemal reheu mari en ssabe at metkaheu at ramdakaiyusi
13	wapsyat ideheu yesyu keurep reheu wemal reheu ssabe na etdaka ubal syaseheu ejal meneheu galbeheu wetdaki
14	upakdeheu dalnat ra timal era jel hawa napsyaheu reuhaheune ukarep heurap tatkisaheu aikana deupaket muse reusaheuduthon
15	wanpak raui tteba yatirait umetkanat wa ama sagiiya reumetma meneheu walmesasaiyu men kulhanaihon
16	hu den meusyane wa reudapra wamssare
17	wahwa beuhat men yaumasa kat marep wa yesyu yatbin wau peurise umalpai namusa desau wau men kul kurya dakrira udihut uduuritrem uhaira deumarya isaui wa reumasaiyuthon
18	unasya aitiu balsa gapra hat meusyarya ubaen wau deunaron neusimuna keudamaui
19	uhat ra etkahu daikana naruna mettul suga dama seureku reuhon regara usyabu am alseheu men tatrira ramssasa keudamaui dyesyu
20	kat heuja den yesyu haimanuthon emal reuhau meusyarya gapra seubikin raheu heuttahaik
21	usyariu sapre waprise methatbin wamrin manu hana dammarel gudapa manu metkaheu reumetbak heuttahe era en araha balhut
22	yesyu den ida maheuseubathon wana wemal reuhon mana methatbin ton beurephon
23	aida peusika reumimal datbikin raheu heuttahaik au reumimal kum hareheu
24	deusedon den deusyarit u beureheu deunasya bara deunetbuk heuttahe amal ramsyarya raheu amal na kum seukul alsaheu ujel reubaitaheu
25	umeheuda kam rinaihon watkal alseheu wejal reubaiteheu kat meusyabaheu raraha
26	usemha ehat reuhulnat wamsyaphin wau raraha wetmeuriu deheureusa wamrin daheujain yaumana tetmeurasa
27	basal haren neupak yesyu waheuja maheusa datmeheu rewi dyasep bet maheuse wemal reheu ta basari
28	watbak kul medem ukam ejal batreheu
29	wabat reheu rewi beubaiteheu kubara raba wiit wa kensya sagiiya deumaheuse udaheurane datmihin hwau amhon
30	uratnin wau sapre waprise wamrin reusalmidaui reumana am maheuse uhattaiye aheurin ton usyasen
31	wana yesyu wemal reuhon ra metbe asya raheurime era rairen deubit bit bidin
32	ra esit dekre reujadike era reuhattaiye rasyabusa
33	henon den amrin reheu reumana talmidaui dyuhanan ssamin aminait wamssaren ap daprise diraheu den aheurin usyasen
34	hu den emal reuhon ra metkeuhin ton rapnaui daknuna keuma deuhatna amhon deusepdon danssumon
35	nison den yaumasa kat netteurim hatna menhon haden neussumon beuhanon yaumasa
36	wemal reuhon matra deura nat kaet ulkasa men mana hasa urame al mana beuraiya deura reuhasa kaet urapraiya ra syalma ulkasa deumen hasa
37	ura nat rame hamra hasa beujeke beuraiyasa wen den ra meubaja hamra hasa reujeke uhu hamra meseset ujeke apdan
38	era hamra hasa beujeke hatasa ramen watraihon metnatrin
39	ura nat syase hamra atika umeheuda bae hasa amal gel atika basim
