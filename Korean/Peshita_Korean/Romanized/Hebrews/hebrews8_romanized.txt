1	risya den deuhulhen it ran rap kumre aina disep men yamina deuhulsya deurabusa batmaiya
2	wahwa meusyamsyana deubet kutsya watmatkeuna datrara hau dakba araha ura balnasya
3	kul rap kumre gel deukaem dankarep kulbane udephe mettul hana jatka wat ap reuhana deunehwe it reheu medem dankarep
4	weru bara wa apra kumra hawe wa mettul diisaihon wau kumre damkalbin wau kulbane aiheu dapnamusa
5	hanon damsyamsin ratmusa waltteranyasa deuharen deubatmaiya aiheu desemal reumuse kat abet wa matkeuna daheuji wabet kulmedem batmusa ha dethajyat raheu beuttura
6	hasya den tetmetta damyatra men ha kabel yesyu meusiha aheuma damyatra ap diiyasiki ha dabit baheu messaiya wapsyudaiye damyatrin men deuha esyaheubat
7	eru gel iseheu wat katmata deura resyan rait wa atra reuhade deusalten
8	rase reuhon gel wemal deuha yaumasa asen amal marya wekmul al baita deubet itraiyel ual baita deubet ihuda diiyasiki heudasa
9	ra aiheu ha diiyasiki dyeheubet rabahaihon byauma deheudet biithon wapket enon men ara deumetren mettul deuhenon ra kawiu beudiiyasiki diri ap ena beusit beuhon emal marya
10	hade den diiyasiki detel reubaita deubet itraiyel basal yaumasa hanon amal marya etriui reunamusi beumadaihon ual rebawathon eheuteubiui wehwe reuhon ena araha uhenon nehon ri ama
11	ura narep nat reubal meuditeheu apra rahu unimal deuda reumarya mettul deuhulhon nedunani men julhon wadama reukasithon
12	wehase enon men aulhon waheuttahaihon tup ra etdeuhal reuhon
13	beuha demal heudasa reukatmata atkaheu waina dasek wasep karip u raheubara
