1	mekil ahai habibe walhime uhadwasi waheuriri hahana kumu beumaran habibai
2	men ewaheudiiya bae na umen sunttiha deuhat reyana nehwe reuhen beumaran
3	ap menaheu bae na bal jaugi syarira deusehwe madal reuhen deuhenen ri ami bewangeriiyaun am keurimit uam syalka damatranai hanon datmahaihon keusibin baheusaba deuhaiye
4	heudau beumaran beuhuljeuban usup amal na heudau
5	umakihuthon tesida rwat kulnat maran karip u
6	deumedem ra titpon era beuhuljeuban batrusa wapbausa wapsaudisa serathon nesyadan keudam araha
7	watrameheu daraha hau deurap men kul mada neunattal rebawathon umadaikon byesyu meusiha
8	mekil ahai airen deusyariran wairen deunaheupan wairen deuhinan wairen deudahyan wairen dalhiman wairen datbihan wairen bade deusyupha watkurasa haren etraau
9	haren direpton ukabelton watmaton waheujaiton bi haren suru waraha datrama nehwe amhon
10	raulbait den heudit beumaran dakepton reumissap diri aikana dap yatpin waiton era ra seupikin waiton
11	ra wa den mettul daheusal ri amal na ena gel yelpet deunehwe sapek ri hau medem diit ri
12	yada na detmakaheu yada na u ap desyatal beuhul waphul medem meudarat na ap beusaba ap beuhapna bamsyalteuhusa waphasirusa
13	deuhul medem meusse na haira bamsiha damhaiyel ri
14	beuram syapil batton dettautapton ruulssanai
15	yadin ton den ap aton piripisaiye dapsyuraiya datbalsa kat nepket men makedauniiya apra heuda men idasa ettautap ri beuhutban matba umatra era aton balhut
16	dap reusesarauniki heuda jeuban usalten heusyaheusi syadalton ri
17	rau deumauhapsa bae na era bae na deupire netgon reuhon
18	kulmedem kapret uyatil ri wamre na unetbet kul deusyadalton ri byat epapraudittat riha basima udepha meukapra deusyapal raraha
19	warahi neumare kuraheu seunikuthon aiheu utreheu beusyupha dyesyu meusiha
20	raraha den abon syupha wiikara raram almin amin
21	syaru batrama deuhulhon kadise dabyesyu meusiha syarin batramhon ahe dami
22	syarin batramhon kulhon kadise yatirait haren deumen baiteheu deukesal
23	ttaibuseheu deumaran yesyu meusiha am kulhon amin
