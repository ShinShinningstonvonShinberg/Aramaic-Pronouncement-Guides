1	beukasisya ra tegul era apisa aiheu deuraba urairen datren aiheu deurahaik
2	walkasisyasa aiheu emhasa urairen deuttaryan aiheu deurahwasaheu beuhuraheu dahyusa
3	ralmeurasa yakal rairen dalmeurasa enen batrara
4	wen it almalta diit raheu beunaiya au beunai beunaiya nilpon rukdam deubapnai baithon netdatkon unepron hupre rabahaihon hade gel meukapra keudam araha
5	aida den deusyarirait almalta i wamsyauhatta hade sapraheu al araha u wamina batrawasa wapbawasa beurirya ubiimama
6	aida den deupalha ettteuraniiya misa i kat haiya
7	haren hwait meupaket reuhen datra resyan nehuyan
8	en nat gel dairen deudireheu enon uyatirait dairen diisaihon beunai baita deuhaimanusa ra yassep hana keupal reheu beuhaimanusa ubit u men airen deura meuhaimnin
9	hwait hahil gabe almalta aida deura beussira men seutin seunin aida dalhat u gapra hwat
10	wiit raheu saheudusa dabade syapire en rabyat beunaiya en kaprat aheuseunaiye en asigat rekraihon deukadise en aruhat rarisse en halhat beuhul bat ttap
11	men almeurasa den airen deuttaryan ettal metttaryan reuhen gel al meusiha ubayan reumehwa reugapre
12	udinhen kaiyam u datram haimanuthen katmata
13	yalpan den ap heubananusa kat metkalkan bet bate ura balhut heubananusa era ap deunatgyan mamreura wanpalkan seurikasa wanmalran medem deura ware
14	ssabe na hahil dairen deuttaryan netdaugan unildan beunaiya wandapran bataihen ura netran rabeldeubaba apra heuda elsa deussuhisa
15	al deuhasya gel syariu nat nat reumettta basal sattana
16	en nat meuhaimna au meuhaimanta it reuhon almeurasa neusalson enen ura nikran al ita deurairen dalmeurasa enen datrara tetpak
17	kasise airen deusyapil metdaprin riikara pipa nesyon yatirait airen deuraen beumelsa wabyulpana
18	emal gel keusaba deura teprum taura batraheuta usyawe u para akreheu
19	al kasisya keuttikranusa ra teukabel era al pum teuren watrasa saheudin
20	rairen deuhatten keudam kulnat kawen dap syalka deunasya netheuron
21	meusahet na raheu keudam araha umaran yesyu meusiha umarahaui geubaiya deuharen tettal ura netkadam reyanaheu reumedem ura tesul medem beumasap bape
22	ida bagal al nat ra teusim ura tettautap baheuttahe nuheuraiye napsyaheu ttal beudahyusa
23	umekil maiya ra tette era hamra karil wait syase mettul etttumhaheu umettul kulhanaik amine
24	it beunainasya daheuttahaihon idiin enon wamkatmin reuhon reubet dina wiit deubasalhon ajin
25	hahana ap bade ttabe idiin enon wairen daheuranyait enon reumetsya ra metkeuhin
