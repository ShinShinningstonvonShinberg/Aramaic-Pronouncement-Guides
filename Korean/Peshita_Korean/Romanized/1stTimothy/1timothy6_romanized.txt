1	airen diisaihon teuhet nira dapdusa reumaraihon beuhul ikal neheudon deura nehwe seumeheu daraha uyulpaneheu metgadap
2	airen den diit reuhon maraiya meuhaimne ra nepson beuhon al dahaihon enon era yatirait neusyamsyon enon al damhaimne enon uhabibe haren deumetteunihin beusetmetteuhon haren arep wabi menhon
3	en den it nat deumarep yulpana heurina ura metkarap reumere heurimasa deumaran yesyu meusiha waryulpana deudeheurat araha
4	hana metrim kat medem ra yada era keuriheu batrasya ubabasa deumere deumenhen hawe heusama uheryana ugudapa wamsam beureyana bisya
5	wathaka dapnainasya airen damhabal reyanhon wakrijin men kutta usaprin deusegulta i deheurat araha at den etrak men haren
6	tegultan gel diran raba i diiseheu deheurat araha baheusyaheusa deumetsan
7	medem gel ra aelneu ralma widia dapra deunapek meneheu metkeuhinan
8	mettul hana sapka ran mehulta usaheusisa
9	airen den deussaben reumetal naprin beunesyune wappahe ubalgigasa sagiiyasa deusaheuran wamsakpan wamttaban rapnainasya baheubara ubapdana
10	ekara den deuhulhen bisyasa iseheu reheumat ketpa wiit nasya detrakragu reheu umen haimanusa ttau unapseuhon aeru reudawane sagiiye
11	at den au balnasya daraha men haren ruk uhat basal jadikusa ubasal kinusa ubasal haimanusa ubasal huba ubasal meusaipranusa ubasal makihusa
12	wetkatat baguna ttaba deuhaimanusa watreheu haiye dararam dalhon etkeurit waudit taudisa ttapsa keudam saheude sagiiye
13	meusahet na raheu keudam araha hau deumahe kul uyesyu meusiha hau dathet keudam panttiiyaut-pirattaut saheudusa syapilta
14	deusetriui pukdana deura ttulsya watra muma dama reugeryaneheu deumaran yesyu meusiha
15	hau dasit beujapneheu neuhaweui araha meubalha uhailsana balhudaui malka deumalke umara deumarawasa
16	hau deuhu balhudaui ra methabal uamal beunuheura deunat ra metkaheu deunetkarap reheu unat men beunainasya ra heuja wapra meusse reumeheujyeheu hau deureheu ikara usyulttana raram almin amin
17	ratire dalma hana paket deura netrimon beureyanaihon ura nettaheuron al utra deurait raui tuheurana era al araha haiya hau dyap ran kul atirait ranyahan
18	unepdon bade ttabe unetron beusurane syapire unehon deuririn reumetal walmettautapu
19	wansimon reunapseuhon sesetta ttapsa reumedem dasit deunatreuhon haiye syarira
20	au ttimase etdal beumedem detgel raheu waruk men beunat kare seurikasa umen heupahasa didasa dagalsa
21	airen gel deumettauden raheu ttau reuhon men haimanusa ttaibusa amaheu amin
