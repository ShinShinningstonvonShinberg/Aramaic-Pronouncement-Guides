1	wemal akripaut reupauraut mapat raheu rammararu al apai napsyaheu haden pauraut peusyat ideheu unapek wa ruha wamal
2	al kul ma deumetreuse na men ihudaiye malka akripe sabal na al napsi deuttubana na dakdamaik yaumana napek na ruha
3	yatirait dyada na dampat at beuhulhon jittime unamuse dihudaiye mettul hana bae na menaheu dapmakrat ruha tetmani
4	yadin enon gel ap henon ihudaiye en ssaben deunetheudon dubarai deumen ttaryusi dahwau ri men syuraiya bami ubuuritrem
5	mettul deumen nukra meupasin bi uyadin dabyulpana risyaiya daprise hit
6	uhasya al sapra deusyudaiya dahwa wa rabahasan men araha kaem na umetdin na
7	dal hana sapra taltaetre syalban meusapran danmanan batrawasa heupittasa diimama watrirya waraui al hana sapra metreuse na men idai ihudaiye malka akripe
8	mana danin ton ra ware danhaimen damkim araha mise
9	ena gel men keudim samet beureyani deusakupre sagiiye esul rukbal seumeheu dyesyu natraiya
10	hade depdet ap buuritrem ukadise sagiiye almit bet asire beusyulttana deukapret men rabai kaheune uhat metkatrin wau menhon ettautpet rairen deuhaiyebu enon
11	waphul keunusya mettanat wit beuhon kat aret wit deunehon meugatpin batmeheu dyesyu waprukja sagiiya damre wit raihon ap ramdinasa heuranyasa napek wit reumeldap enon
12	uhat ajel wit mettul hade reudalmeusuk beusyulttana wapmapsanusa deurabai kaheune
13	beupelgeheu dyauma buulha heujit men seumaiya au malka datrek rai ual kul dami wau nuheura damyatal men deusemsya
14	wanpalneu kuran al ara usemet kara kat amal ri eprait syawal syawal mana radep at ri keuse u raheu rambaattu rukse
15	wena emret man at mari umaran emal ri ena hu yesyu natraiya dat radep at
16	wemal ri kum al rekraik mettul hade gel etheujit raheu dakimaheu meusyamsyana usaheuda deumedem daheujaitani udasit at deuseheujeni
17	wepasseheu men ama dihudaiye umen amme heurane darwathon meusyadal na raheu
18	datpataheu ainaihon aiheu deuneheupeuhon men hesyuha reunahira umen syulttaneheu deusattana rwat araha wankapron syupkan heuttahe upesa am kadise beuhaimanusa deubi
19	mettul hade malka akripe ra kamet beuheryana rukbal hejwa seumaiyana
20	era aheureujet men reukutmin reuhanon dapdalmeusuk walhanon deubuuritrem udaphulhen kurya dihut wap ramme aheureujet dansubon unetpeunon rwat araha unepdon bade deusyawen rasyabusa
21	ual apai haren aheuduni ihudaiye beuhaikra ussaben wau reumektteurani
22	atrani den araha dama ryauma hana uha kaem na wamsahet na rajura walraba kat medem reubal men muse wanbiiye ra amal na era airen deuhenon emaru dasidan deunehuyan
23	deunehat meusiha unehwe risisa dakyamta deumen bet mise udasit deunaheuret nuheura rama waramme
24	uhat hahana napek wa pauraut ruha ka pitttaut beukara rama seunait raheu paure sepra sagiiya apdaheu deusetne
25	amal pauraut ra seunit nassiha pitttaut era merai seurara usaknusa meumarel na
26	wap malka akripaut yatirait yada raihen deuharen umettul hana in bakre meumarel na keudamaui mettul daheuda men haren mere ra sabal na deuttayan reheu deura wa beuttusya siran
27	meuhaimen at malka akripe ranbiiye yada na damhaimen at
28	amal reheu akripaut beukaril medem meupit at ri dehwe keuritttyana
29	upauraut emal bae wit men araha beukaril wapsagi ra wa raheu balhut era ap reuhulhon airen deusyamin ri yaumana deunehon ahwasi reubal men asure haren
30	ukam reheu malka uhikmauna ubelniki wairen dyatbin wau amhon
31	uhat peuraku men taman meumalrin wau hat am hat wamrin deumedem deusyawe reumauta au rasure ra bat gapra hana
32	wemal akripaut reupitttaut metkaheu wa gapra hana deunetteure eru beugen kesal ra keura
