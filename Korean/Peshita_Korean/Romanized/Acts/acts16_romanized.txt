1	umatti wa reudelbi meudita walruttteura it wa den taman talmida hat datmeheu ttimaseaut beuraheu dihudata heuda meuhaimanta wabu almaiya wa
2	uhulhon talmide deumen ruttteura umen ikaniwan matheudin wau raui
3	reuhana sseuba wa pauraut deunetbeuriui ameheu wansap gatreheu mettul ihudaiye diit wau batra yadin wau gel kulhon rabu dalmaiya wa
4	uhat ajin wau bamdinasa maheureujin wau umalpin reuhon deunehon natrin pukdane airen daheusabu seurihe ukasise deubuuritrem
5	beuram idasa metkaiman wai beuhaimanusa usagyan wai beumenyana kuryum
6	harehu den baprugiiya wapgarattiiya atrawasa waheurat enon ruha deukutsya deura neumalron melsa daraha basiiya
7	uhat esau reumusiiya atra ssaben wau deunijun men taman reubisuniiya ura apsat reuhon ruheheu dyesyu
8	uhat neupaku men musiiya neuhesu reuhon ratrawat atra
9	waphejwa deurirya etheuji reupauraut aiheu gapra hat makedauniiya deukaem ubae meneheu kat amal deusa reumakedauniiya uadaraini
10	kat den heuja pauraut hana hejwa meheuda sseubain reumepak reumakedauniiya mettul dettakalneu deumaran keuran dansabal enon
11	waldain men tteurawat watratneu reusamatraki umen taman ryauma heurina esain reuneapaurit meudita
12	umen taman reupiripaut deuhi i risya deumakedauniiya wiiseheu kaurauniiya hwain den baheu beuhade meudita yaumasa idie
13	wanpakneu byauma deusyapsa reubal men tara damdita al yat naheura mettul deusaman metheuje wa bet sseurusa uhat isepneu meumalrin wain am nese daheunisyan wai taman
14	watsa heuda meujapnat algwana deudaheura wat men araha seumaheu hwa rudiiya men tewattira meudita deuhade peusaheu rebaheu maran usyama wat medem damal pauraut
15	uemdat wat hi wapnai baitaheu ubaya wat menan wamra den hu deusyarirait teuhirin ton deuhaimnet beumaran tau seurau reuhon beubaiti usagi eratsan
16	wahwa deuhat ajinan reubet sseurusa pegat ban raimsa heuda diit wa raheu ruha deuketme uapda wat reumareheu tegulta sagisa beuketma deukatma wat
17	wasya wat basal pauraut ubatreuneu ukaya wat wamra haren nasya apdaui enon daraha meuraima wamsaprin reuhon ulha deuhaiye
18	uhahana apda wat yaumasa sagiiye wetteupil pauraut wemal raheu reuruha ha paket na rehi batmeheu dyesyu meusiha deusepkin menaheu ubaheu beusyasa nepkat
19	uhat heujau mareheu danpak reheu menaheu sapra deusegulteuhon aheudu reupauraut walsira wangadu aitiu enon reusyuka
20	ukarebu enon rettteurattige walrise damdita wamrin wau deuharen nasya meudalhin raheu ramditan mettul dihudaiye enon
21	umaheureujin ran yade airen deura mapat ran ramkabaru walmebat mettul deurumaiye nan
22	uhensya sagiiya etkanasu raihon haden ettteurattige sadeku naheutaihon wapkadu dannakdon enon
23	uhat nagedu enon sagi almiu enon bet asire upakedu reunattal bet asire deunettal enon jeuhirait
24	hu den kat kabel hana pukdana ael heubat enon beubaita gawaiya deubet asire wesal rekraihon beusada
25	wappelgeheu deurirya pauraut usira meussaren wau wamsyaphin wau raraha usyamin wau reuhon asire
26	umen serya jaua raba hwa wetteuji sesese deubet asire wetpeusahu meheuda tare kulhon wasuraihon deuhulhon etteuriu
27	uhat ettil nattal bet asire waheuja dapsihin tare deubet asire neusap sapsira waba deunekttul napseheu mettul deusabal wa daraku reuhon asire
28	wakra pauraut beukara rama wemal reheu ra tebet reunapsyaheu medem deubit mettul deuhuran halka heunan
29	wanhal reheu seuraga waswal ual kat raet wanpal al rekraihon deupauraut watsira
30	wapek enon reubal wemal wa reuhon marai mana ware ri reumebat aiheu dihe
31	uhenon amrin wau reheu haimen beumaran yesyu meusiha usihe at ubaitaheu
32	umareru ameheu melsa deumarya uam kulhon beunai baiteheu
33	ubaheu beusyasa beheu beurirya deubal athi enon men nekdeuhon ubal syaseheu mat hu wapnai baiteheu kulhon
34	watbal asek enon reubaiteheu usam reuhon pasura urawet wa hu wapnai baiteheu beuhaimanusa daraha
35	uhat hwa ssapra syadaru ettteurattige reusyakrai syaptte deunimron reurap asire seuri reuharen nasya
36	uhat seuma rap bet asire al emal reheu hi hade melsa reupauraut deusyadaru ettteurattige aiheu deusetteuron uhasya puku jeru batrama
37	amal reheu pauraut deura saheuru nakdon rin alma kureheu reunasya rumaiye walmyon bet asire uhasya matsyait mapkin ran ra gel era henon nison napkunan
38	wejaru syakrai syaptte wemaru rettteurattige mere haren desemal reuhon uhat seumau deurumaiye enon deuheru
39	wesau rwathon wabau menhon deunepkon wansyanon reuhon men meudita
40	uhat neupaku men bet asire aru rwat rudiiya waheujau taman rahe ubaiyau enon wanpaku
