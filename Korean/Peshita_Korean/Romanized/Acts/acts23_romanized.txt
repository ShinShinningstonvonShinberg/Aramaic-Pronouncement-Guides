18	udapreheu kentteuruna raraima wareheu rwat kiriiyalha wemal pauraut asira keurani ubae meni daite hana raima rwasaheu diit reheu medem deunimal raheu

20	wemal reheu raima ihudaiye ethasyabu deunebon menaheu deusahet reupauraut meuhal reuhenseuhon aiheu ssaben medem yatil deunilpon meneheu
