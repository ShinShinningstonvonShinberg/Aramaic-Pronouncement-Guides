1	wapkat raui pitttat deunettadal rwat kesal riittariiya watreumeheu reupauraut urasire heurane ameheu reugapra hat kentteuruna men etpil sebattti datmeheu hwa yuriiyaut
2	uhat hwat deunelde neuhetneu relpa diiseheu wat men atreumeunuttaut meudita waja wat ratra dasiiya ual wa aman relpa aritttalhaut makedauniiya deumen sesarauniki meudita
3	waryauma heurina mattin reussaidan wethasyaheu kentteuruna bamraheumanusa rwat pauraut wapet reheu deunijal rwat raheumaui unetteuniheu
4	umen taman reudain umettul deuruhe sakupran wai etkeureheuneu al kupraut
5	wabalneu yama deukirikiiya watpampuriiya umattin reumura meudita deurukiiya
6	wetkaheu taman kentteuruna elpa men areksandeuriiya daja wat riittariiya wautban baheu
7	umettul dyakirait radya wat ryaumasa sagiiye reumaheusen mattin rukbal keunidaut gajalta umettul deura syapka wat ruha deunijal teurissait etkeureheuneu al keuritti rukbal salmauni meudita
8	walmaheusen kat radenan heudareheu mattin reuduksa deumetkarya reumine syapire ukariba wat raheu meudita datmaheu rasea
9	wahwain taman japna sagiiya dama dabal ap yauma deussauma dihudaiye wahwa reheu kentta deunelde nat byama umareheu wa reuhon pauraut
10	wamal gapre haje na deubuulssana waphutrana sagiiya hauya maldisan rau balhut deumaupraheu delpan era ap deunapsyasan diran
11	kentteuruna den reukubelnitti walmaraheu delpa syama wa yatil men meraui deupauraut
12	umettul deura ahen wa hau reumina reumattaiyu beheu saswa sagiiye menan ssaben wau deunelde men taman wenhu deumetkeuhin danmanon unatton balmina hat diisaui wa bakritti umetkeure wa punikseu uhaal wa reusaimna
13	uhat netbat ruha deusaimna watbaru dammatten aiheu ssebyanhon raden wau heudarai keuritti
14	umen basal karil neupak rain matba darara deumetkeure ttupanikaut eurakridaun
15	wethatpat elpa ura etkeuhat datkum rukbal ruha uyapneu raida deuhi
16	uhat balneu gajalta heuda deumetkarya kuda reumaheusen etkaheuneu ehatneu reukalkura
17	uhat seukalnaheu meuhaissin wain umatkeunin raheu relpa umettul deudaheurin wain deudalma nepel beumahatteheu dyama ahetna ralmenaun uhahwat raden wain
18	uhat kam reheu rain kimuna kasya ryauma heurina seudain manin byama
19	waryauma datrasa mane diraheu delpa biidain seudain
20	uhat ehat reheu saswa yaumasa yatire ura semsya metheuje wa ura saheura ura kaukbe sapra deuhaiyain kul kureheu etpeusek wa reheu
21	uhat nat medem ra mettaibal wa haden kam pauraut bainathon wemal eru ettteupitton ri gapre ra raden wain men keuritti umethatkin wain men hutrana umen ulssana hana
22	uhasya mareheu na deusehon deura aka napsya gel menhon heuda ra apda era en elpa
23	etheuji ri gel beurirya hana maraheheu daraha hau deudireheu na ureheu paraheu na
24	wemal ri ra tethal paure sit u raheu ramkam keudam kesal uha yap raheu araha mauhapta reuhul deuraden amaheu
25	mettul hana etrababu gapre meuhaimen na gel baraha deuhahana hawe aiheu ma detmaral ami
26	beuram reugajalta heuda it ran deunetteude
27	umen basal albeusasal yaumin dattain wetttarapneu beuhatriiyaut yama beupelgeheu deurirya seubaru marahe deurara metkalbin wau
28	walmiu eukinat wetkahu kaumin etrin usup karil reudau wetkahu kaumin hamsyaetre
29	uhat dahirin wain dalma netteuhaheu ran beudukyasa diit beuhen syue almiu men halsaheu delpa eukinat alba wamssaren wau deunehwe yauma
30	marahe den bau reumerak menaheu men elpa wahesu menaheu reukalkura ryama belsa deunijun baheu unetrunaheu relpa bara
31	uhat heuja pauraut emal reukentteuruna urettteurattiiyautte den haren belpa ra meuhatrin aton ra metkeuhin ton deusihon
32	haden peusaku ettteurattiiyautte reuhapreheu deukalkura men elpa usyapkuheu ttaya
33	hu den pauraut dama dahwa ssapra meupit wa reuhulhon dankapron saibalta kat amal reuhon yaumana ha albeusasal yaumin men kentta medem ra ttim reuhon
34	mettul hade bae na menhon datkapron mehulta reukuiyama deuhaiyaikon mensa gel men risya deuhat menhon ra apda
35	uhat haren emal neusap raheuma usyabaheu raraha keudam kulhon wakssa wakep reumehal
36	wetbaiyau kulhon ukaberu tulsaiya
37	isain wain den belpa masen usyabin uset napsyan
38	uhat seubau mehulta akeru men elpa watkaru hette watdau byama
39	uhat hwa yauma sapane aida i ara ra ettaudau era haru al gep yapsya kenpa heuda dyama aika deuranen wau den metkeuha nethunaheu relpa
40	wapsaku eukinat men elpa walpiu enen byama watrau raheube deusaukane watrau almenaun jura reuruha deunatba uraden wau rapai yapsya
41	ugesyat elpa beuduksa deurama bainat teuren umkin dyama wetharyat baheu ukam reheu gabaheu katmaiya ura metteuji wa gabaheu den heuraiya etteuri men keuttira deugalre
42	watbau wau ettteurattiiyautte dankatron enon rasire deura nalmon sahwa unelkon reuhon menhon
43	ukentteuruna keura enon men hade mettul deussabe wa deunahe reupauraut urairen deumetkeuhin wau reumalmaiyu sahwa peukat reuhon dapkatmaiye nethon unepron rara
44	walsyalka al dape ual kaise heurane delpa abaru enon uhahana kulhon ettaujabu rara
