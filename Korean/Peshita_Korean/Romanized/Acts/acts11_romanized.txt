1	wettamat wat ratrihe urahe deubihut dap amme kaberu melsa daraha
2	uhat seurek semon ruuritrem danin wau ameheu hanon deumen geujulta
3	kat amrin darwat nasya ulre al waret amhon
4	wakep wa semon basal basal reumimal reuhon
5	deuhat meussare wit byaupi heujit beuhejwa danhet wa mana hat aina deudame wa reuhetana wasil wa balba kalnaseheu usyaep wa men seumaiya wesa dama rwasi
6	uharet beheu uhaje wit diit beheu haiwasa dalbat rekraihen uraheusya dara wap paraheusa datmaiya
7	usemet wit kara damal wa ri semon kum kut wahul
8	wemret hat mari deumemsum ra al reupumi datma udamsaiyap
9	usup kara emal ri men seumaiya deumedem daraha daki at ra teusaiyep
10	hade hwat teurat japnin wettarak reheu kulmedem ratmaiya
11	ubaheu beusyasa teurasa gaprin dettadaru rwasi men kaulniriiyaut men kesariiya esau ukamu al tara deudalta datre wit baheu
12	wemal ri ruha deujel amhon deura puraga wesau ami ap haren seuta ahin ualneu reubaiteheu deugapra
13	wettai ran aikana heuja beubaiteheu maraha deukam wemal reheu deusyadal ryaupi meudita waita reusemon deumetkeure kipa
14	uhu neumarel amaheu mere daphen tihe at uhureheu baitaheu
15	uhat akpet wit taman rammararu aknat ruha deukutsya raihon aiheu ma darain men keudim
16	wetdaheuret melseheu deumaran demal wa dyuhanan amet beumaiya aton den temdon beuruha deukutsya
17	en hahil araha syauyait yaheubaheu mauhapta ramme airen deuhaimenu beumaran yesyu meusiha aiheu dap ran ena man wit detpak wit deheure raraha
18	uhat haren mere seumau seuriu reuhon usyabahu raraha wamrin wau daheubal ap ramme araha yap tyabusa reuhaiye
19	hanon den detbadaru wau men ulssana dahwa wa al etttepanaut mattiu wau dama reupuniki wap ratra deukupraut uranttiiyahiiya kat am nat ra meumalrin wau melsa era balhut am ihudaiye
20	it wau den nasya menhon men kupraut umen kurini haren aru wau ranttiiyauhi wammalrin wau am yaunaiye wamsaprin wau al maran yesyu
21	wiit wat amhon ideheu deumarya usagiiye haimenu wetpeuniu rwat marya
22	wettamat wat hi hade retnaihon dapnai ita deubuuritrem usyadaru reubalnaba ranttiiyauhi
23	uhat esa reusaman waheuja ttaibuseheu daraha heudi ubae wa menhon daphureheu rephon nehon nakipin reumaran
24	mettul deugapra wa ttaba wamsyamrai wa beuruha deukutsya waphaimanusa wettausap wa ama sagiiya reumaran
25	uhu neupak wa reuttalsaut reumeba reusyawal
26	uhat etkeuheheu aityeheu ameheu ranttiiyahiiya usyata kuraheu aheuheuda keunisin wau bita warepu ama sagiiya men haden katmaiyat etkeuriu banttiiyauhi talmide keuritttyane
27	wabyaumasa hanon esau men uritrem reusaman neubiiye
28	ukam hat menhon datmeheu hwa agabaut wauda enon beuruheu deuhapna raba hawe beuhuraheu ara wahwa kapna hana byaumai keuraudiiyaut kesal
29	beuram den talmide aiheu ma diit wa reunat nat menhon peurasu dansyatron reusetmetta dahe airen damrin bihut
30	usyadaru byat balnaba usyawal reukasise deusaman
