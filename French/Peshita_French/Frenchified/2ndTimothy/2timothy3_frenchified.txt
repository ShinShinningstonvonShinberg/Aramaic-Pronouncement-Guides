1	hadhè dén hwayt yadha davyawmatha hrayyè ni'thon zavnè qchayya
2	wnèhon bnaynacha rahmay nafchhon wrahmay kèspa chavhranè ramè mghadfanè dalnachayhon la mèttpissin kafray btayboutha rachiè
3	akhlay qarsè mchabdhay lrègtha barirayyè sanyay tavatha
4	machlmanè msarhvè hthirè rahmay rghighatha tav mèn houbèh d'alaha
5	d'iyth lhon èskima ddhèhlath alaha wmèn haylèh rahiqin aylén dhakhana ènon dhouq ènon mènakh
6	mènhon ènon guér halén damhaldhin béth batè wchavén nèchè datmiran bahtahè wmètdavran larghighatha mchahlfatha
7	davkhoulzvan yalpan wmèmthoum lidhatha dachrara lmè'tha la mèchkhin
8	aykana dén dyanis wyambris qamw louqval mouchè hakhana af hènon qamin louqval chrara nacha damhabal rèyanhon waslén mèn haymanoutha
9	èla la ni'thon laqdhamayhon chatyouthhon guér mèthyadha y lkhoulnach aykana d'af dhanon èthyadhath
10	at dén èthayt bathar youlpany wvathar doubaray wvathar sèvyany wvathar haymanouthy wvathar naguirouth rouhy wvathar houby wvathar msaybranouthy
11	wvathar rdhifouthy wvathar hachay wyadha at aylén saybrèth b'antiyyakhiyya wvi'yqanawn wavloustra aydha rdhifoutha saybrèth wmèn koulhén pasyany mary
12	wkhoulhon dén aylén dsavén bdhèhlath alaha dnihon byèchou mchiha mèthradhpin
13	bnaynacha dén bichè wmatyanè nawsfon al bichouthhon kadh taén wmatén
14	at dén qawa b'aylén dilèft wè'chtarart yadha at guér mèn manou ilèft
15	wadhmèn talyouthakh sèfrè qadichè yalif at dmèchkhin danhakmounakh lhayyè bhaymanoutha dyèchou mchiha
16	koul kthav davrouha èthkthèv mawtrana hou lyoulpana walkhouwana walthourassa walmardoutha davkhi'noutha
17	dnèhwè gmir barnacha d'alaha walkhoul vadh tav mchalman
