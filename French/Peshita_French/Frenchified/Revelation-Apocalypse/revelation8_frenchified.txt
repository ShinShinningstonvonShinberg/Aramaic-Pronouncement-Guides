1	wkhadh pthah tava dchava hwa chèthqa bachmayya aykh pèlgouth chaa
2	wahzith lchava mala'khin aylén daqdham alaha qamin waw d'èthihèvw lhon chava chifourin
3	wahrina mala'kha ètha wqam al madhbha wi'yth lèh pirma ddhahva wè'thihèv lèh bèsmè saguiyyè' dnètèl baslawatha dkhoulhon qadichè al madhbha daqdham koursya
4	waslèq ètra dvèsmè baslawatha dqadichè mèn yadh mala'kha qdham alaha
5	wansav mala'kha lpirma wamla mèn noura dal madhbha wa'rmi al ara wahwaw ramè wqalè wvarqè wnawda
6	wchava mala'khin dalayhon chava chifourin tayyèvw nafchhon lmazaqou
7	whaw qadhmayya azèq wahwa bardha wnoura dafthikhin bmayya wè'thrmiw al ara wthoulthah d'ara iqèdh wthoultha d'iylanè iqèdh wkhoul èsba d'ara iqèdh
8	wdhathrén zaq wahwa aykh toura raba dyaqèdh nfal byama wahwa toulthèh dyama dma
9	wmith toultha dkhoul bèryatha davyama d'iyth bèh nafcha wthoultha d'èlfa èthhabal
10	wdhathlatha zaq wanfal mèn chmayya kawkva raba dyaqèdh aykh chalhèvitha wanfal al toultha dnahrawatha wal aynatha dmayya
11	wachmèh dkhawkva mèthè'mar apsithna wahwa toulthhon dmayya aykh apsinthin wsougha' davnaynacha mithw mètoul d'èthmarmarw mayya
12	wdha'rba zaq wavla toulthèh dchèmcha wthoulthèh dsahra wthoultha dkhawkvè wahchèkhw toulthhon wyawma la hawi toulthèh wlilya hakhwath
13	wchèmèth lnèchra hadh dfarah bachmayya d'amar wa wa wa lamouréh d'ara mèn qala dchifourè dathlatha mala'khin dathidhin lmazaqou
