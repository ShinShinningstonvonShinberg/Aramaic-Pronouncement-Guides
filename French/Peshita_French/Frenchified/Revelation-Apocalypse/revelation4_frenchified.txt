1	mèn bathar halén hzith wha tara pthiha bachmayya wqala haw dchèmèth aykh chifoura malèl amy lmi'mar saq lharka wè'hawékh ma dihiv lmèhwa bathar halén
2	wmèhdha hwith brouh wha koursya sim bachmayya wal koursya yathèv
3	wadhyathèv aykh dmoutha dhèzwa dkhi'fa dyachpèh wadhsardhawn wqèchta danana dahdharawy dkhoursya dmouth hèzwa dazmaraghdhè
4	wahdhar koursya koursawatha èsrin wa'rba walayhon dén dkhoursawatha èsrin wa'rba qachichin dyathbin datifin ma'nè hèwarè wal qarqfathhon klilè ddhahva
5	wmèn koursawatha nafqin ramè wvarqè wqalè wchava nahirè dyaqdin qdhamawy dkhoursya aylén d'iythayhon chva rouhin d'alaha
6	waqdham koursya yama dazghoughitha aykh doumya daghlidha wavmèsath koursya wahdharawy dkhoursya arba haywan dmalyan aynè mèn qdhamayhén wmèn bèstarhén
7	hayyoutha qadhmata damya la'rya whayyoutha dthartén dmoutha dèghla whayyoutha dathlath ith lah apè aykh dvarnacha whayyoutha d'arba dmoutha dnèchra dfarah
8	arbatayhén haywatha koulhdha mènhén qama wi'yth lah mèn tèfréh walèl chta guèpin houdhrana'ith wmèn lghaw malyan aynè wchèlya layt lhén imama wlilya lmi'mar qadich qadich qadich marya alaha ahidh koul haw d'iythawy wa wi'ythawy wa'thè
9	wma dyav arbatayhén haywatha tèchbouhta wi'yqara wqoubal tayboutha ladhyathèv al koursya wladhhay lalam almin amin
10	nèplon èsrin wa'rba qachichin qdham man dyathèv al koursya wnèsgdhon lalam almin amin lman dhay wnarmon klilayhon qdham koursya kadh amrin
11	dchawét ou maran wa'lahan lmèssav tèchbouhta wi'yqara whayla mètoul d'at brayt koul wavyadh sèvyanakh hway wè'thbri
