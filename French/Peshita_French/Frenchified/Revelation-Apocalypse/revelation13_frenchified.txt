1	wqamèth al hala dyama wahzith dsalqa hayyoutha mèn yama d'iyth lah sar qarnan wachva qarqfan wal qarnathah èsra ta'ghin wal qarqfathah chma dghoudafa
2	whayyoutha ha dahzith dmoutha wath dnèmra wrèghléh aykh ddhèba wfoumah aykh d'aryawatha wyav lah tanina haylèh wkhoursyèh wchoultana raba
3	wahdha mèn qarqfathah aykh pita lmawta wamhoutha dmawtah ètha'syath wè'tdamrath koulah ara bathar hayyoutha
4	wasghèdhw lthanina dyav choultana lhayyoutha wasghèdhw lhayyoutha lmi'mar manou ddhamè lhayyoutha hadhè wmanou mèchkah lmaqravou amah
5	wè'thihèv lah pouma dammalèl rawrvatha wghoudafa wè'thihèv lah choultana lmèbadh yarhè arbin wathrén
6	wfèthhath poumah lamghadafou qdham alaha dathghadèfy bachma wavmachrya d'aylén dachrén bachmayya
7	wè'thihèv lah lmèbadh qrava am qadichè walmèzka ènon wè'thihèv lah choultana al koulhén charbatha wè'mwatha wlèchanè wammè
8	wnèsgdhon lah koulhon amouréh d'ara hanon aylén dla kthivin bakhthava dhayyè haw d'èmra qtila qdham tarmyathèh dalma
9	man d'iyth lèh èdhnè nèchma
10	man davchèvya mawbèl bchèvya azèl wa'yna davharba qatèl bharba nèthqtèl harka y haymanoutha wamsaybranoutha dqadichè
11	wahzith hayyoutha hritha dsalqa mèn ara wi'yth wa lah tartén qarnan wdhamya lè'mra wammalla wath aykh tanina
12	wchoultana dhayyoutha qadhmata koulèh tèbèdh qdhamawy wthèbèdh la'ra wladhamrin bah wnèsgdhon lhayyoutha qadhmata ha d'èthhalmath mhoutha dmawtah
13	wthèbèdh athwatha rawrvatha aykana dnoura tèbèdh lmèhath mèn chmayya al ara qdham bnaynacha
14	wthatè ladhamrin al ara byadh athwatha d'èthihèv lah lmèbadh qdham hayyoutha lmi'mar ladhamrin al ara lmèbadh salma lhayyoutha aydha d'iyth lah mhoutha dharba wahyath
15	wè'thihèv lah dthètèl rouha lsalma dhayyoutha wthèbèdh dkhoul dla nèsgdhon lèh lsalma dhayyoutha nèthqatlon
16	wthèbèdh lkhoulhon zourè wrawrvè atirè wmèskinè marayya wavdè dnèthihèv lhon rouchma al idhayhon dyamina aw al béth aynayhon
17	dla nach nèzbèn aw nzabèn touv èla ayna d'iyth lawy rouchma dachma dhayyoutha aw mènyana dachmah
18	harka ithéh hèkhmtha wdhi'yth bèh hawna nèhchviwy lmènyana dhayyoutha mènyana w guér dvarnacha chèthma' wèchtin wchèth
