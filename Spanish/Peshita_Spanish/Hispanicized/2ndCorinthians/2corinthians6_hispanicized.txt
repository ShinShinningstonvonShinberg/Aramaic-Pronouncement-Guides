1	wa'yj madrane baenan menjon dla testarak bjon taybuza d'alaha dkabelton
2	emar guer davsavna mkabla nizaj wavyawma djayye adartaj ha hasha savna mkabla wha hasha yawma djayye
3	lma bmedem tetlon lnash elza dzukalza dla nehwe muma bzeshmeshtan
4	ela bjul medem njawe nafshan damshamshane jnan d'alaha bamsaybranuza sagui'za b'uwlsane b'anankas bajvushye
5	bnegde b'asure bashgushye ble'uza bshahra bsawma
6	bdajyuza bidaza bnaguiruz ruja bvasimuza bruja dkudsha bjuba dla nejla
7	bmelza dkushta bjayla d'alaha bsayna dsadikuza davyamina wavsemala
8	bshuvja wavsara bkulasa wavgunayya ayj matyane wsharire
9	ayj la idie widiinan ayj mizinan wha jain nan ayj dmezrdenan wla matinan
10	ayj djarya lan wavjulsvan jadenan ayj meskine walsaguiyye' matrinan ayj dmedem layt lan wjul medem ajidinan
11	puman pzij u lwazjon kawrinzayye wleban rwij
12	la alisiton ban alisiton den brajmaykon
13	ayj dlavnayya den amar na puruny juvly dalwazjon wrawajw jubjon lwazy
14	wla tehon bnay sawga la'ylen dla mhaymnin ayda guer shawtafuza iz lsadikuza am awla aw ayna jultana iz lnahira am jeshuja
15	aw ayda shalmuza iz lamshija am satana aw ayda mnaza iz ldamhaymen am dla mhaymen
16	ayda den awyuza iz lhaykleh d'alaha am dshi'de aton den haykleh aton d'alaha jayya aykana d'amir d'emar bhon we'halej bhon we'hwe alahhon whenon nehon li ama
17	metul hana pukw men baynazhon we'zpashw menhon amar marya waltame' la tezkarvon we'na ekabeljon
18	we'hwe ljon la'va wa'ton tehon li lavnayya wlavnaza amar marya djul ajid
