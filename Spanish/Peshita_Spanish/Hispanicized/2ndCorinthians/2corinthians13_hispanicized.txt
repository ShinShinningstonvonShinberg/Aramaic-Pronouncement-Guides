1	hade dazlaz i savnin d'ize na lwazjon dal pum tren wazlaza sahdin tkum kul mela
2	men kdim emrez wiz ljon wzuv mkadem na amar na ljon ayj d'af tarten savnin dahwiz lwazjon emrez ljon wa'f hasha kad rajik na kazevna lhalen dajtaw walsharka dajrane d'en ize tuv la ejus
3	metul dvukya baeton damshija dammalel bi haw dla hwa mjil bjon ela jaylzan u bjon
4	afen esdkef guer bamjiluza ela jay u bjayla d'alaha wa'f jnan mjilinan ameh ela jain nan ameh bjayla d'alaha davjon
5	nafshjon bkaw en bah bhaymanuza kamin ton nafshjon asaw aw la meshtawditon dyeshu mshija bjon u we'n la maslayya ton
6	msabar na den dzedon dajnan la wayn maslen
7	bae na den men alaha dla nehwe bjon medem dvish ayj dvukyan dilan nezjse ela d'aton tehon sarin tavaza wajnan nehwe ayj maslayya
8	la guer meshkjinan dnebed medem lukval kushta ela jlaf kushta
9	jadenan den ma dajnan krihin wa'ton jaylzanin hade guer af msalenan d'aton tetgamron
10	metul hana kad rajik na halen kazev na dla ma d'eziz kashya'iz esur ayj shultana dyav li mary lvenyanjon wla lsujafjon
11	mekil ajay jdaw we'zgarw we'zbayya'w wa'wyuza wshayna nehwe bjon wa'laha djuba wadshalmuza nehwe amjon
12	sha'lw bashlama jad djad bnushakta kadishta
13	sha'lin bashlamjon kulhon kadishe
14	shlameh dmaran yeshu mshija wjubeh d'alaha wshawtafuza druja dkudsha am kuljon amin
