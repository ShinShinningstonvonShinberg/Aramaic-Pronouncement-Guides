1	yadinan guer d'en baytan dva'ra hana dfagra neshtre ela iz lan benyana dmen alaha bayta dla bavad idayya bashmayya lalam
2	af al hade guer mettanjinan waswenan dnelbash baytan dmen shmayya
3	ela af ma dalveshn neshtjaj lan artel
4	kad izayn guer hasha bhana bayta mettanjinan men yukreh wla savenan lmeshljeh ela dnelbash lel meneh dzezbla mizuzeh bjayye
5	wdamated lan lah lhade alaha hu haw dyav lan rahvuna drujeh
6	metul dyadinan hajil wampasinan dajma davfagra shrenan anidinan men maran
7	bhaymanuza hu guer mhaljinan wla bajsayya
8	metul hana tjilinan wamsawjinan dnenad men pagra wnehwe lwaz maran
9	wmezjaptinan d'en anude jnan we'n amure leh hwayn shafrin
10	kulan guer zidinan lamkam kdam bim damshija dnezpra nash nash bfagreh medem david leh en dtav we'n dvish
11	metul dyadinan hajil dejlzeh dmaran lavnaynasha hu mpisinan la'laha den glenan savar na den d'af lmadaykon diljon glenan
12	la wa tuv nafshan mshabjinan ljon ela elza hu yahbinan ljon dahwayton meshtavhrin ban lwaz hanon dva'pe w meshtavhrin wla bleba
13	en guer shatenan la'laha we'n takninan ljon
14	jubeh guer damshija ales lan dranenan hade djad jlaf kulnash miz maden kulnash miz leh
15	wajlaf kulnash hu miz d'aylen djain la lnafshhon nijon ela lhaw ayna dal apayhon miz wkam
16	wmekil jnan lnash bafgar la yadinan we'n idan bafgar lamshija ela men hasha la yadinan
17	kul man dvamshija hu hajil briza hu jdaza atikaza var lhen
18	wjul medem hwa jaza men alaha haw drayan leh bamshija wyav lan teshmeshta dzaruza
19	alaha wa guer bamshija haw drai lalma am rabuzeh wla jshav lhon jtahayhon wsam ban dilan melza dzaruza
20	isgade jnan hajil jlaf mshija wa'yj haw d'alaha bae menjon b'iydan jlaf mshija hajil baenan ezraaw la'laha
21	haw guer dla yada wa jtiza metulazjon jtiza avdeh dajnan nehwe beh sadikuza d'alaha
