1	mawdinan ljon den ajayn taybuzeh d'alaha d'ezyahbaz bidaza dmakedawnayye
2	davvukya saguiyya' d'uwlsanhon yatiruza hwaz ljadwazhon wumka dmeskinuzhon ezyatar buzra dafshituzhon
3	sahed na guer d'ayj jaylhon wyatir men jaylhon bsevyan nafshhon
4	baw menan bvauza sagui'za dneshtawtfon btaybuza dzeshmeshta dkadishe
5	wla ayj ma dsavrin wayn ela nafshhon ashlemw lukdam lmaran wa'f lan bsevyaneh d'alaha
6	dajnan neve men titaws d'ayj dshari hajana nshalem bjon af taybuza hade
7	ela aykana davjulmedem mezyatrin ton bhaymanuza wavmelza wvidaza wavjul jfitw wavjuban dalwazjon hajana af bhade taybuza tezyatron
8	la wa mefkad paked na ljon ela bajfituza djavraykon shrara djubjon mnase na
9	yadin ton guer taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija dmetulazjon ezmaskan kad izawy atira d'aton bmeskinuzeh tetron
10	memlaj den malej na ljon hade damadra ljon metul dmen eshtkady shariton law lmesba baljud ela af lmebad
11	hasha den shalemw bavada haw medem dasvayton d'aykana dahwa ljon suwaja lmesba hajana bavada tshalmon men ma d'iyz ljon
12	en guer sevyana izawy ayj ma d'iyz leh hajwaz mezkabal la wa ayj ma dlayt leh
13	la wa guer dlajrane tehwe rwajta waljon ulsana
14	ela bshawyuza hwaw bsavna hana dyatiruzjon diljon tehwe ljasiruzhon dhalen d'af yatiruzhon dhanon tehwe ljasiruzjon diljon dzehwe shawyuza
15	ayj dajziv dhaw d'asgui shkal la ezyatar leh whaw dkalil shkal la ezbasar leh
16	taybu den la'laha dyahbah jfituza hade jlafaykon blebeh dtitaws
17	bauzan guer kabel wmetul dtav btil leh men sevyaneh nfak se'daykon
18	shadarn den ameh la'jon ayna dzeshbujteh b'ewangueliyyawn bjulhen idaza
19	hajana d'af megba gve men idaza dnepuk aman btaybuza hade dmeshtamsha menan lshuvjeh dileh d'alaha wallubavan dilan
20	kanitinan den bhade dla nash nsim ban muma bhade rabuza dmeshtamsha menan
21	yasifinan guer dshapiraza la kdam alaha baljud ela af kdam bnaynasha
22	shadarn den amhon af la'jon ayna dlan bke bjulsvan bsaguiyya'za dajfita hu hasha den yatira'iz jfit bzujlana saguiyya' dalaykon
23	en hajil titaws shawtafa hu dily wamadrana bjon we'n ajayn jrane shlije enon didaza dshuvjeh damshija
24	mekil tajwiza djubjon wshuvharan davjon bhon jawaw bparsup idaza kulhen
