1	pawlaws shlija dyeshu mshija bsevyaneh d'alaha wtimaze'aws aja lita d'alaha d'iyz bkawrinzaws waljulhon kadishe d'iyz b'aja'iyya kulah
2	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
3	mvaraj u alaha avu dmaran yeshu mshija ava drajme wa'laha djul buyya
4	haw damvayya lan bjulhon ulsanayn d'af jnan neshkaj nvayya la'ylen davjul ulsanayn enon bhaw buyya'a dajnan mezbay'inan men alaha
5	aykana guer dmezyatrin ban jashawy damshija hajana byad mshija mezyatar af buyya'an
6	afen den meza'lsinan al apay buyya'jon u wal apay jayyaykon meza'lsinan we'n mezbay'inan metul d'aton tezbay'un wzehwe bjon jfituza dazsaybron enon ljashe hanon d'af jnan jashinan lhon
7	wsavran dalaykon sharir u yadinan guer d'en shawtafin ton bjashe shawtafin ton af bvuyya'a
8	savenan den dzedon ajayn al ulsana dahwa lan b'asiyya drawrva'iz eze'lesn yatir men jaylan dama dkarivin waw jayyayn lmettalaku
9	wal nafshan psakn mawta dla nehwe lan tujlana al nafshan ela al alaha haw damkim mize
10	haw dmen mawte jasine parkan wzuv msabrinan dfarek lan
11	bamadranuza dvauzjon dal apayn dzehwe mawhavzeh dalwazan taybuza davida b'apay saguiyye' wsaguiyye' nawdon leh al apayn
12	shuvharan guer hanaw sahduza dreyanan dvafshituza wavdajyuza wavtaybuza d'alaha ezhapajn balma wla bjejmza dfagra wyatira'iz lwazjon diljon
13	la wa jranyan kazbinan ljon ela aylen dyadin ton af meshtawdin ton tjil na den dadama lajrata teshtawdon
14	aykana d'af eshtawdaton kalil men sagui dshuvharjon jnan ayj d'af aton dilan byawmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
15	wavhana tujlana save wiz men kdim d'ize lwazjon d'aifa'iz tkablon taybuza
16	we'bar laykon lmakedawnayye wzuv men makedawniyya lwazjon ize wa'ton tlaunany lihud
17	hade hajil d'ezraiz lma ayj msarhva ezraiz aw dalma davsar enen aylen dmezrae na metul dwale wa dnehwe bhen in in wla la
18	mhayman u alaha dla hwaz melzan dalwazjon in wla
19	breh guer d'alaha yeshu mshija haw dvi'ydan ezkres ljon bi wavsilwanaws wavtimaze'aws la wa in wla ela in hwa beh
20	kulhon guer mulkanawy d'alaha beh hu bamshija in hwaw metul hana b'iydeh yahbinan amin lzeshbujteh d'alaha
21	alaha hu den msharar lan amjon bamshija haw dhu mashjan
22	wjatman wyav rahvuna drujeh blebawazan
23	ena den la'laha mashed na al nafshy dmetul dja'es na laykon la eziz lkawrinzaws
24	la wa metul dmaray haymanuzjon jnan ela madrane jnan djadwazjon bhaymanuza hu guer kamin ton
