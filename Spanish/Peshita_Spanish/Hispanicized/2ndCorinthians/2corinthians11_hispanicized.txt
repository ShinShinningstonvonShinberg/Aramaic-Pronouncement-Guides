1	eshtuf den msaybrin wayton li kalil d'emalel pakiha'iz ela af msaybrin ton u li
2	ta'en na guer bjon batnana d'alaha mjartjon guer lgavra jad bzulta djiza d'ekarev lamshija
3	dajel na den dalma ayj d'ati jewya ljawa banjiluzeh hajana nezjablon reyanaykon men pshituza dalwaz mshija
4	en guer haw d'eza lwazjon jrina yeshu ajres ljon ayna dajnan la ajresn aw ruja jriza nsavton ayda dla nsavton aw svarza jriza ayda dla kabelton shapir mettpisin wayton
5	rane na guer dmedem la basrez men shlije aylen dtav myatrin
6	afen guer bura na bmelazy ela la bidazy ela bjulmedem ezglin lwazjon
7	aw dalma maskalu asklez dmakjez nafshy d'aton tetrimon wmagan ajrsez ljon svarzeh d'alaha
8	widaza jranyaza jalsez wnesbez nafkaza lzeshmeshta diljon
9	wjad eziz se'daykon wajsar li la yekrez al nash menjon snikuzy guer maliw aje d'ezaw men makedawnayye wavjul medem netrez nafshy wnatar na dla ikar laykon
10	izawy bi shrareh damshija dhana shuvhara la nezbatal bi b'azrawaza d'aja'iyya
11	lmana metul dla majev na ljon alaha hu yada
12	ela hade daved na af ebed d'efsuk u elazhon d'aylen dvaen elza davhaw medem dmeshtavhrin neshtajjon ajwazan
13	izayhon guer halen shlije dagale wfale njile wamdamen nafshhon lashlije damshija
14	wlayt lmetdamaru bhade en hu guer dsatana metdame bmala'ja dnuhra
15	la hwaz raba svuza en af mshamshanawy metdamen bamshamshane dsadikuza hanon djarzhon tehwe ayj vadayhon
16	tuv den amar na lma nash nasbar lay ayj dsajla na we'n la afen ayj sajla kabluny d'af ena kalil eshtavhar
17	medem dammalel na la wa bmaran mmalel na ela ayj davfakihuza bhade dukza dshuvhara
18	metul dsaguiyye' meshtavhrin bvesra af ena eshtavhar
19	mnajin ton u guer lmeshtmau ljasiray reyana kad aton jakime ton
20	wmetdnen ton lman damshabed ljon walman d'ajel ljon walman dnasev menjon walman dmetrim laykon walman dmaje ljon al apaykon
21	ayj davsara amar na ayj haw dajnan mjilinan u bjasiruz reyana amar na davjulmedem dmamraj nash af ena mamraj na
22	en evrayye enon af ena en isralayye enon af ena en sareh enon d'avraham af ena
23	en mshamshane enon damshija bjasiruz reyana amar na dyatir menhon ena ble'uza yatir menhon bmajwaza yatir menhon b'asure yatir menhon bmawte savnin saguiyya'n
24	men ihudayye jamesh savnin arbin arbin jasir jda belez
25	tlaz savnin bshavte eznagdez jda svan ezragmez tlaz savnin hwiz nuwaga imama wlilya dla sfita byama hwiz
26	b'uwrjaza saguiyya'za bkundinaws dnahrawaza bkundinaws dgayyase bkundinaws dmen tuhmy bkundinaws dmen amme bkundinaws hwiz bamdinaza bkundinaws hwiz bjurba bkundinaws byama bkundinaws dmen aje dagale
27	bamla wavle'uza bshahra saguiyya' bjafna wavsahya bsawma saguiyya' barya wavartelayyuza
28	star men yatiraza wajnushya dalay djulyum wsefzy dal apay kulhen idaza
29	manu mezkrah wla ena mezkrah na manu mezkshel wla ena yaked na
30	en lmeshtavharu wale bjurhanay eshtavhar
31	yada u alaha avu dmaran yeshu mshija mvarja dalalam almin dla mjadev na
32	bdarmsuk rab jayla d'aretaws malka natar wa mdita ddarmuskayye lme'jdany
33	wmen kawza basrigta shabuny men shura we'zpaltez men idawy
