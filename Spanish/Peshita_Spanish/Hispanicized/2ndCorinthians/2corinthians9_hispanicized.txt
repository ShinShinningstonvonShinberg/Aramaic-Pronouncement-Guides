1	al teshmeshta den dkadishe yatirta aved na en kazev na ljon
2	yada na guer tuyyaveh dreyanjon wmetul hana eshtavhrez bjon lmakedawnayye d'aja'iyya zida y men eshtkady watnana diljon lsaguiyye' gareg
3	shadrez den la'je dla nestarak shuvharan d'eshtavharn bjon al hade svuza d'aykana d'emrez tehon mtayvin
4	dalma ni'zon amy makedawnayye wneshkjunajon kad la zidin ton wnevhaz jnan dla ni'mar d'aton tevhzon bshuvhara haw d'eshtavharn
5	metul hana ezbtel li d'eve men ajay halen dni'zon kdamay lwazjon wanatdon burkza ha dmen kdim eshtmaton dzehwe mtayva hajana ayj burkza la wa ayj yanuza
6	hade den man dsara bjawsana af bjawsana jased wman dsara bvurkza bvurkza nejsud
7	kul nash ayj d'iyz breyaneh la ayj dmen karyuza aw ayj dmen ktira lyahuva hu guer jadayya rajem alaha
8	matya y den b'iyday alaha kul taybu danyatar bjon davjulsvan bjulmedem haw ma dsafek ljon nehwe ljon wzezyatron bjul vad tav
9	ayj dajziv dvadar wyav lmeskine wsadikuzeh kayyama lalam
10	haw den dyahev sara lsarua wlajma lme'julta hu netel wnasgue sarjon wanrabe pi're dsadikuzjon
11	davjul medem tetron bjulah pshituza dhi gamra b'iydan tawdiza la'laha
12	metul dfuljana dzeshmeshta hade la wa baljud jasiruza dkadishe mmale ela af myatar bzawdyaza saguiyya'za la'laha
13	metul bukya guer dzeshmeshta hade la'laha mshabjin d'eshtabadton lzawdiza dasvarzeh damshija we'shtawtafton bafshituzjon amhon wam kulnash
14	wasluza mkarvin jlafaykon bjuba saguiyya' metul suga' dtaybuzeh d'alaha dalaykon
15	taybu den la'laha al mawhavzeh dla mezmalla
