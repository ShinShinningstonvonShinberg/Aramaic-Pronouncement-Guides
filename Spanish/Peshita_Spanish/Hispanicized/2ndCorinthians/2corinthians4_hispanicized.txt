1	metul hana la ma'na lan bzeshmeshta hade d'ajidinan ayj rajme dahwaw layn
2	ela aslinan kasyazah dvehtza wla mhaljinan bjaruza wla najlinan melzeh d'alaha ela bgalyuza dashrara mjawenan nafshan ljulhon reyane davnaynasha kdam alaha
3	we'n den mjasay u ewangueliyyawn dilan la'ylen d'avdin u mjasay
4	aylen d'alaheh dalma hana awar madayhon al dla mhaymnin dla nednaj lhon nuhreh d'ewangueliyyawn dshuvjeh damshija dhuyyu dmuzeh d'alaha
5	la wa guer nafshan majrsinan ela lamshija yeshu maran lnafshan den davdaykon jnan metul yeshu
6	metul d'alaha hu d'emar dmen jeshuja nuhra nednaj hu dnaj blebawazan dneznahar bidaza dshuvjeh d'alaha bparsupeh dyeshu mshija
7	iz lan den simza hade bma'na djespa drabuza djayla men alaha tehwe wla menan
8	bjulmedem guer meza'lsinan ela la mezjankinan mettarfinan ela la jayyavin nan
9	mezradpinan ela la meshtavkinan mestajpinan ela la avdinan
10	bjulsvan mizuzeh dyeshu bfagrayn shkilinan d'af jayyawy dyeshu bfagrayn nezglon
11	en jnan guer jayye lmawta meshtalminan metul yeshu hajana af jayyawy dyeshu nezglon bfagran hana dma'ez
12	hasha mawta ban mezjapat wjayye bjon
13	af jnan hajil dajda y ruja dhaymanuza iz lan ayj dajziv dhaymnez metul hana af mallez mhaymninan metul hana af mmallinan
14	wyadinan dhaw man d'akim lmaran yeshu af lan byad yeshu nkim wankarvan amjon lwazeh
15	kulmedem guer metulazjon u djad mezyatra taybuza byad saguiyye' tesgue tawdiza lshuvjeh d'alaha
16	metul hana la ma'na lan af en barnashan guer barayya mezjabal ela dmen lgaw mezjadaz yum men yum
17	ulsaneh guer dsavna hana kad tav sur wkalil shuvja raba dla saja lalam almin mtayyev lan
18	dla jadenan bhalen dmezjasyan ela bhalen dla mezjasyan dmezjasyan guer dsavna enen dla mezjasyan den dalalam enen
