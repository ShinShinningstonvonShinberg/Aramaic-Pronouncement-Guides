1	mekil ajay baenan menjon wmezkashfinan ljon bmaran yeshu d'ayj dkabelton menan aykan wale ljon dazhaljon wzeshpron la'laha dyatira'iz tawsfon
2	yadin ton guer aylen pukdane yavn ljon bmaran yeshu
3	hanaw guer sevyaneh d'alaha kadishuzjon wadzehon parikin men kul sanyuza
4	wnehwe yada nash nash menjon lmekna ma'neh bkadishuza wvi'ykara
5	wla bjashe dregza ayj sharka damme hanon dla yadin la'laha
6	wla tehon mshijin lmebar walmelav nash la'ju bhade svuza metul dmaran u tavua al halen kulhen aykana d'af men kdam savna emarn ljon wsahedn
7	la guer krajon alaha ltanpuza ela lkadishuza
8	mekil man dtalem la wa lvarnasha talem ela la'laha haw dyav bjon rujeh kadishta
9	al juba den d'aje la snikiton lmejtav ljon aton guer knumjon malfe ton d'alaha dzajvon jad ljad
10	af avdin ton hajana ljulhon aje davjulah makedawniyya bae na den menjon ajay dzezyatron
11	wzezjapton dzehon shlen wanen bsuranaykon wahwayton paljin b'iydaykon aykana dfakednajon
12	dzehon mhaljin b'eskima lwaz barayye wal nash la testankon
13	save na den dzedon ajay dal aylen ddamkin la tehwe karya ljon ayj sharka dnasha dsavra layt lhon
14	en guer mhaymninan dyeshu miz wkam hajana af alaha la'ylen dadmejw byeshu mayte ameh
15	hade den amrinan ljon bmelzeh dmaran dajnan aylen dmeshtajrinan bme'zizeh dmaran djainan la nadrej la'ylen dadmejw
16	metul dhu maran bfukdana wavkala drish mala'je wavkarna d'alaha najez men shmayya wmize dvamshija nkumon lukdam
17	whaden jnan aylen dmeshtajrinan djainan nezjtef amhon ajjda banane lu'wreh dmaran b'a'ar whajana bjulsvan am maran nehwe
18	hwaw hajil mvay'in jad ljad bmele halen
