1	wa'ton yadin ton ajay dmalanan dalwazjon la wa srika'iz hwa
2	ela lukdam jashnan we'staarnan ayj dyaditon bfilipaws whaden b'aguna raba maleln amjon bparesiyya d'alahan svarzeh damshija
3	buyya'an guer la wa men tayuza afla men tanfuza afla bnejla
4	ela ayj ma davkenan la'laha dzezhayman svarzeh hajana mmallinan la ayj dlavnay nasha neshpar ela la'laha haw dvake lebawazan
5	afla guer memzum ezjashajn bmamlla shadala ayj dyadin ton wla belza dyanuza alaha sahed
6	wla bayn shuvja men bnaynasha la menjon wla men jrane kad meshkjin wayn yakire lmehwa ayj shlije damshija
7	ela hwayn baynazjon makije wa'yj mrabyaniza damjabva bneh
8	hajana af jnan mjabvinan wamsawjinan lmetal ljon la baljud svarzeh d'alaha ela af nafshan metul djabivayn ton
9	ahdin ton guer ajayn dla'en wayn wamlin bavad idayn blilya wvi'ymama dal jad menjon la ni'kar
10	aton sahdin wa'laha aykana ajresn ljon svarzeh d'alaha dajya'iz wji'na'iz wadla reshyan hwayn lwaz kulhon mhaymne
11	aykana dyadin ton dmen jad jad menjon ayj ava men bnawy baen wayn wmalen wayn blebjon wamsahdinan ljon
12	dazhaljon ayj dya'e la'laha haw dakrajon lmalkuzeh walshuvjeh
13	metul hana af jnan mawdenan amina'iz la'laha dmelza d'alaha dkabelton menan la wa ayj melza davnaynasha kabeltunah ela aykana d'iyzeh sharira'iz melza d'alaha whi bavada mestara bjon b'aylen damhaymnin
14	aton den ajay etdamiton lidaza d'alaha dvihud halen davyeshu mshija dhajan saybarton af aton men bnay sharbazjon aykana d'af henon men ihudayye
15	hanon dalmaran yeshu mshija ktalw wlanviyye dmenhon wlan rdafw wla'laha la shafrin wavidin sakuvla ljulhon bnay nasha
16	djalen lan danmalel am amme dnijon lamshalamu jtahayhon bjulsvan mati den layhon rugsa dama ljarza
17	jnan den ajayn hwayn yazme menjon savna dshaza b'apayn wla bleban wyatira'iz ezjapatn lmejsa apaykon bjuba saguiyya'
18	wasvayn dni'ze lwazjon ena pawlaws jda svan wzarten wawjany satana
19	aynaw guer savran wjaduzan wajlila dshuvharan ela en aton kdam maran yeshu bme'zizeh
20	aton enon guer teshbujtan wjaduzan
