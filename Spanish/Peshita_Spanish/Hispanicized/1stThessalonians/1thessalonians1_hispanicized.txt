1	pawlaws wsilwanaws wtimaze'as lita dzesalawnikayye dva'laha ava wavmaran yeshu mshija taybuza amjon washlama
2	mawdenan la'laha bjulsvan al kuljon wmetdajrinan ljon baslawazan amina'iz
3	wahdinan kdam alaha ava vade dhaymanuzjon wamla djubjon wamsaybranuza dsavrjon davmaran yeshu mshija
4	yadinan guer gavyuzjon ajay jabivawy d'alaha
5	metul damsabranuzan la wa bmele baljud hwaz lwazjon ela af bjayla wavruja dkudsha wvapyasa sharira af aton yadin ton aykana hwayn baynazjon metulazjon
6	wa'ton ban etdamiton wavmaran dkabelton melza b'uwlsana raba wavjaduza druja dkudsha
7	wahwayton dmuza ljulhon mhaymne d'iyz bmakedwaniyya wva'ja'iyya
8	menjon guer eshtamaz melzeh dmaran la baljud bmakedwaniyya wva'ja'iyya ela bjul azar haymanuzjon dva'laha eshtamaz aykana dla nestnek dni'mar laykon medem
9	henon guer meshtaen ayna malana hwa lan lwazjon wa'ykana ezpniton lwaz alaha men dejlaz pzajre dzefljon la'laha jayya wsharira
10	kad msaketon lavreh men shmayya lyeshu haw d'akim men bez mize dhu mfase lan men rugsa d'aze
