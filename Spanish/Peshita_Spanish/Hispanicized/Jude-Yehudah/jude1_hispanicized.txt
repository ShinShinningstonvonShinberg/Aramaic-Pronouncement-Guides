1	ihuda avda dyeshu mshija aja den dyakuv lamme krayya dva'laha ava rjimin wavyeshu mshija ntirin
2	rajme washlama wjuba nesgue ljon
3	jabivay kad kulah yasifuza aved na lmejtav ljon al jayye dilan dgawa ananki iz li lmejtav ljon kad mpis na d'aguna tebdon jlaf haymanuza ayda dajda svan eshtalmaz lkadishe
4	knaw guer nashin malanuza aylen dmen shurayya kademw ezkzevw bjuyyava hana nasha rashie daltaybuzeh d'alahan mahpjin ltanpuza wavhaw d'iyzawy baljudawy mara alaha wmaran yeshu mshija kafrin
5	lmahaduzjon den save na kad yadin ton kulhen d'alaha kad jda svan lama men mesren prak ha dzarten la'ylen dla haymenw awbed
6	walmala'je aylen dla ntarw rishanuzhon ela shvakw umra dilhon ldina dyawma raba b'asure la idie tjez amtana natar
7	aykana dasdum wamura wamdinaza dajdarayhen dvah badmuza dhalen sani we'sal bazar besra jrina siman tjez tajwiza dnura dalalam kad mjayvan ldina
8	bah badmuza af halen davjelme meshtragrguin lvesra man mtanfin lmaruza den talmin walzeshbujta mgadfin
9	mija'yel den rish mala'je haw dam ajelkarsa kad d'aen mmalel wa metul pagreh dmushe la amraj dnayte lawy dina dgudafa ela emar dnegur baj marya
10	halen den b'aylen dla yadin mgadfin b'aylen den dajyana'iz ayj jaywaza jarshaza mpasin bhen mezjablin
11	wa lhon dvu'wrjeh dka'yen esalw wvazar tayuzeh dvelam b'agra eshtarajw wavesyayyuzeh dkuraj evadw
12	halen izayhon aylen dvanyajazhon kad mjatmin mezparpin kad dla dejlza nafshhon raen nane dla metra dmen ruje pahyan ilane d'awpi ebhon d'iyzayhon dla pi're dmizw tenyanuz waslekw men ekarayhon
13	galle asise dyama davyad ruthon mjawen behtazhon kawkve matyane lhalen damtana djeshuja lalam lhon ntir
14	eznabi den af lhalen haw d'iyzawy dshava men adam jnuj kad amar dha marya aze brebwaza dkadishe
15	dnebed dina al kul walmakasu ljul nafshaza metul kulhon vade aylen d'arshw wmetul kulhen mele kashyaza aylen dmalelw jatayye rashie
16	halen enon dmartnin wadlin bjul sevwan kad ayj rguigaza dilhon mhaljin wfumhon mmalel gnijaza wamshabjin lfarsupe metul yuzrane
17	aton den jabivay etdarw lmele aylen dkadem eze'mar men shlijawy dmaran yeshu mshija
18	d'amrin waw ljon davjarza dsavne nehon aylen damvasjin d'ayj rguigaza dilhon asin bazar rusha
19	halen izayhon aylen damfarshin nafshanayye druja layt lhon
20	aton den jabivay bhaymanuza diljon kadishta ezbanaw men drish bruja kadisha kad msalen
21	nafshan den bjuba d'alaha netar kad msakenan lajnaneh dmaran yeshu mshija ljayye dilan dalalam
22	walmenhon man men nura jtufw
23	kad den mettwen ezrajamw layhon bdejlza kad sanen ton af ljutina dmen besra damjatma
24	lhaw den dmeshkaj dannatar lan dla shuraza wadla kuzma wankim dla muma
25	baljudawy alaha parukan byad yeshu mshija maran kdam teshbujteh bjaduza leh shuvja u'wjdana wi'ykara wrabuza af hasha wavjulhon alme amin
