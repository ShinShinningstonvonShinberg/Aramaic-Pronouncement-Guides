1	pawlaws shlija la men bnaynasha wla byad barnasha ela byad yeshu mshija wa'laha avu haw d'akimeh men bez mize
2	wjulhon aje damy lidaza d'iyz bgalatiyya
3	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha ava wmen maran yeshu mshija
4	haw dyav nafsheh jlaf jtahayn danfasen men alma hana bisha ayj sevyaneh d'alaha avon
5	dleh shuvja lalam almin amin
6	metdamar na aykana bagal mezhafkin ton men mshija haw dakrajon btaybuzeh lajriza svarza
7	ayda dla izeh ela nasha hu iz ddaljin ljon wsaven danshajlfon svarzeh damshija
8	af en jnan den aw mala'ja men shmayya nsabarjon lvar men ma dsabarnajon nehwe jrem
9	aykana dmen lukdam emrez whasha tuv amar na d'en nash msabar ljon lvar men ma dkabelton nehwe jrem
10	hasha guer lavnaynasha hu mpis na aw la'laha aw lavnaynasha bae na d'eshpar elu guer dama lhasha lavnaynasha shafar wiz avda damshija la hawe wiz
11	mawda na ljon den ajay dasvarza d'estabraz meny la hwaz men barnasha
12	afla guer ena men barnasha kabeltah wileftah ela bguelyana dyeshu mshija
13	shmaton guer hupajay dmen kdim dvihudayyuza dyatira'iz radef wiz iteh d'alaha wjarev wiz lah
14	wmezyatar wiz bihudayyuza tav men saguiyye' bnay shnay d'iyz wa btuhmy wyatira'iz ta'en wiz byulpana d'avahay
15	kad sva den haw man dfarshany men kres emy wakrany btaybuzeh
16	dnegle breh bi d'esabriwy bamme bar shazeh la gliz lvesra wladma
17	wla esez lu'wrishlem lwaz shlije dmen kdamay ela esez la'raviyya wzuv hefkez ldarmsuk
18	wmen bazar tlaz shnin esez lu'wrishlem d'ejse lji'fa wkawiz lwazeh yawmaza jameshtasar
19	lajrin den men shlije la jsiz ela en lyakuv aju dmaran
20	halen den djazev na ljon ha kdam alaha dla mjadev na
21	men bazar halen eziz la'zrawaza dsuriyya wadkilikiyya
22	wla yadan way li b'apin idaza dvihud halen dvamshija
23	ela hade baljud shamin waw dhaw dmen kdim radef wa lan hasha ha msabar haymanuza ha dmen kdam savna msajef wa
24	wamshabjin waw bi la'laha
