1	tuv den men bazar arbaesre shnin selkez lu'wrishlem am barnava wdevrez amy ltitaws
2	selkez den bguelyana wagliz lhon svarza dmajres na bamme wjawizah la'ylen dmestavrin waw dmedem izayhon baynay walhon dam srika'iz rehtez aw rahet na
3	af titaws damy d'armayya wa la eze'nes dnegsur
4	metul den aje dagale dalw layn dnegshon ji'ruza d'iyz lan byeshu mshija aykana danshabdunany
5	afla mle shaa ezrmin lshubadhon dashrarah dasvarza nkawe lwazjon
6	hanon den dmestavrin d'iyzayhon medem man den izayhon la btil li alaha guer b'apay bnaynasha la nasev henon den halen medem la awsefw li
7	ela jranya'iz jsaw guer d'ezhaymnez svarza durluza ayj d'ezhayman ki'fa bagsurta
8	haw guer djapet lji'fa bashlijuza dagsurta japet af li bashlijuza damme
9	wjad idaw taybuza d'ezyahbaz li yakuv wji'fa wyujanan hanon dmestavrin waw d'iyzayhon amude yamina dshawtafuza yavw li walvarnava dajnan bamme whenon bagsurta
10	baljud dalmeskine hwayn ahdinan we'zbtel li dhi hade ebdih
11	kad eza den ki'fa la'ntiyyawji b'apawy akesteh metul dmettaklin waw beh
12	dadla ni'zon nasha men lwaz yakuv am amme ajel wa kad ezaw den ngad wa nafsheh wafrash metul ddajel wa men hanon dmen gsurta
13	we'zrmiw waw ameh lhade af sharka dihudayye hajana d'af barnava ezdvar wa lmesav b'apayhon
14	wjad jsiz dla asin trisa'iz bashrareh d'ewangueliyyawn emrez lji'fa lin kulhon en at dihudayya at arma'iz jayye at wla ihuda'iz aykana ales at lamme dihuda'iz nijon
15	jnan guer dmen kyanan ihudayye nan wla hwayn men amme jatayye
16	metul dyadinan dla mesdadak barnasha men vade dnamusa ela bhaymanuza dyeshu mshija af jnan beh byeshu mshija haymenn dmen haymanuzeh damshija nesdadak wla men vade dnamusa metul dmen vade dnamusa la mesdadak kul bsar
17	en den kad baenan dnesdadak bamshija eshtjajn lan af jnan jatayye maden yeshu mshija mshamshana w dajtiza jas
18	en guer la'ylen dsezrez tuv lhen bane na jawiz al nafshy davar al pukdana na
19	ena guer bnamusa lnamusa mizez dla'laha ije
20	wam mshija skif na wmekil la wa ena jay na ela jay bi mshija whana dhasha jay na bavsar bhaymanuza w davreh d'alaha jay na haw d'ajvan wyav nafsheh jlafayn
21	la talem na taybuzeh d'alaha en guer byad namusa y sadikuza mshija magan miz
