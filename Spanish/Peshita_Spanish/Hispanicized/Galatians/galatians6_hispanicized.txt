1	ajay en nash menjon nezkadam bsajluza aton aylen davruj aton azknu bruja makijta wahwayton shirin ddalma af aton teznason
2	watanw yukra dajdade dhajana mmaleton namuseh damshija
3	en guer nash savar d'iyzawy medem kad la izawy nafsheh mate
4	ela nash vadeh nehwe bake whaden baynawy lnafsheh nehwe shuvhareh wla bajrane
5	kulnash guer mawbla dnafsheh neshkul
6	neshtawtaf den haw dshama melza lhaw man dmashma leh bjulhen tavaza
7	la teton alaha la mezbasaj medem dsara guer bar nasha haw u jased
8	man dvavsar sara men besra jvala w jased wman davruj sara men ruja jayye dalalam nejsud
9	wjad avdinan dtav la waz ma'na lan nehwe guer savna dnejsud wla tema'n lan
10	hasha hajil ad savna iz lan nefluj tavaza lwaz kulnash yatira'iz lwaz bnay bayta dhaymanuza
11	jsaw aylen kzivan kezbez ljon b'iyday
12	aylen dsaven dneshtavhron bvesra henon alsin ljon dzezgasron dvaljud baskifeh damshija la nezradpon
13	afla guer henon halen dgasrin natrin namusa ela saven dzezgasron davvesrjon diljon neshtavhron
14	li den la nehwe li d'eshtavhar ela baskifeh dmaran yeshu mshija dveh alma skif li we'na skif na lalma
15	la guer gsurta izeh medem wla urluza ela briza jdaza
16	wa'ylen dalhana shvila shalmin shlama nehwe layhon wrajme wal israyyel d'alaha
17	mekil li nash amla la narme ena guer kuzmazeh dmaran yeshu bfagry shkil na
18	taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija am rujjon ajay amin
