1	kumw hajil bji'ruza ha damshija jarran wla tezkadnon tuv bnira davduza
2	ha ena pawlaws amar na ljon d'en tezgasron mshija medem la mahne ljon
3	msahed na den tuv ljul barnash dmezgsar djayyav u djuleh namusa nebed
4	ezbatalton ljon men mshija aylen davnamusa mesdadkiton wmen taybuza nfalton
5	jnan guer bruja dmen haymanuza lsavra dsadikuza mkawenan
6	bamshija guer yeshu la gsurta medem izeh wla urluza ela haymanuza dmezgamra bjuba
7	shapir rahtin hwayton manu dawedjon dlashrara la tettpison
8	pyasjon la wa men haw dakrajon
9	jmira kalil kulah gvilta majma
10	ena tjil na laykon bmaran dmedem jrin la mezraeton wa'yna ddalaj ljon hu nsaybriwy ldina man d'iyzawy
11	ena den ajay elu dakil gsurta majres wiz lmana mezrdef wiz dalma ezbatal leh keshleh daskifa
12	eshtuf den af mefsak nefskon hanon ddaljin ljon
13	aton den lji'ruza w ezkriton ajay baljud la tehwe ji'ruzjon lelaz besra ela bjuba hwayton meshtabdin jad ljad
14	kuleh guer namusa bajda melza mezmale bha dzajev lkarivaj ayj nafshaj
15	en den jad ljad mnakziton wa'jliton jsaw dalma jad men jad tsufon
16	amar na den davruja hwayton mhaljin wregza dvesra la men mzum tebdon
17	besra guer ra'eg medem dnaje lruja wruja raga medem dnaje lvesra wazrayhon sakuvle enon jad djad dla medem dsaveton tehon avdin
18	en den bruja metdabrin ton la hwayton tjez namusa
19	idiin enon guer vadawy dvesra d'iyzayhon sanyuza tanfuza sajnuza
20	puljana dafzajre jarashuza beldvavuza jeryana tnana jemta esyana palagwaza sedke
21	jsama ketla rawayyuza smara wjul dalhalen damen wa'ylen dhalen sarin ayj dmen lukdam emrez ljon af hasha amar na dmalkuzeh d'alaha la yartin
22	pi're den druja izayhon juba jaduza shlama magraz ruja basimuza tavuza haymanuza
23	makijuza msaybranuza al halen namusa la sim
24	aylen den ddamshija enon besrhon skafw am kulhon ki'vawy warguigazeh
25	nije hajil bruja walruja neshlam
26	wla nehwe srikay shuvja dmaklin jad al jad wjasmin jad bjad
