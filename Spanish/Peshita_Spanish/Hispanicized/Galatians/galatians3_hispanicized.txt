1	aw jasiray reyana galatayye manu jsam bjon dha ayj haw damsar sir wa kdam aynaykon yeshu mshija kad sliv
2	hade baljud save na d'eda menjon men vade dnamusa nsavton ruja aw men shema dhaymanuza
3	hajana sajlin ton dshariton bruja whasha bavsar mshalmin ton
4	halen kulhen iki saybarton we'shtuf den iki
5	haw hajil dyav bjon ruja wsaar bjon jayle men vade dnamusa aw men shema dhaymanuza
6	aykana dhaymen avraham la'laha we'zjashbaz leh lsadiku
7	daw hajil d'aylen dmen haymanuza enon henon enon bnayya d'avraham
8	metul guer dkadem ida alaha davhaymanuza w mesdadkin amme kadem sabar la'vraham ayj d'emar kzava kadisha dvaj nezbarjon kulhon amme
9	maden mhaymne w mezbarjin b'avraham mhaymna
10	aylen guer dmen vade enon dnamusa tjez lawtza enon kziv guer dlit u kulman dla nebed kul dajziv bnamusa hana
11	dla den mesdadak nash bnamusa lwaz alaha hade galya y metul dajziv dsadika bhaymanuza nije
12	namusa den la wa men haymanuza ela man dnebed aylen dajzivan beh nije bhen
13	lan den mshija savnan men lawtza dnamusa whu hwa jlafayn lawtza kziv guer dlit u kulman dmettle bkaysa
14	davamme tehwe burkzeh d'avraham byeshu mshija wajnan nesav shudayya druja bhaymanuza
15	ajay ayj dvez bnaynasha amar na ddiyyaziki dvarnasha d'eshtarraz nash la masle aw mshajlef bah medem
16	la'vraham den ezmlej mulkana walsareh wla emar leh dalsarayk ayj dalsaguiyye' ela lsaraj ayj daljad haw d'iyzawy mshija
17	hade den amar na ddiyyaziki dmen kdim eshtarraz men alaha bamshija namusa hu dahwa men bazar arba ma' wazlazin shnin la meshkaj dnasleh wanvatel mulkana
18	en den men namusa y yartuza mekil la waz men mulkana la'vraham den bmulkana w yav leh alaha
19	mana hajil namusa metul mastyanuza ettawsaf dama dni'ze sara haw dleh hwa shudayya we'zihev hu namusa byad mala'je b'iyda dmesayya
20	mesayya den la wa djad alaha den jad u
21	namusa hajil sakuvla w dmulkana d'alaha jas elu guer ezihev wa namusa ayna dmeshkaj wa lmajayyu sharira'iz men namusa hawya waz sadikuza
22	ela jvash kzava kulmedem tjez jtiza dmulkana bhaymanuza dyeshu mshija nezihev la'ylen damhaymnin
23	adla den ti'ze haymanuza namusa natar wa lan kad jvishinan lhaymanuza dazida hwaz lmezglayyu
24	namusa hajil tar'a hwa lan lwaz mshija dmen haymanuza nesdadak
25	kad ezaz den haymanuza la hwayn tjez tar'e
26	kuljon guer bnayya aton d'alaha bhaymanuza dyeshu mshija
27	aylen guer dvamshija madton lamshija lveshton
28	layt ihudayya wla armayya layt avda wla bar ji're layt dejra wla nekbza kuljon guer jad aton byeshu mshija
29	we'n damshija ton mekil sareh aton d'avraham wyarte bmulkana
