1	amar na den dajma savna dyarta tle la prish men avde kad mare u djulhon
2	ela tjez epitrawpe izawy wrabay bate dama lsavna dsam avu
3	hajana af jnan kad yalude wayn tjez estuksawy dalma mshabdin hwayn
4	kad mta den shulameh dsavna shadar alaha lavreh wahwa men atza wahwa tjez namusa
5	dla'ylen dazjez namusa enon nesben wankabel simaz bnayya
6	wdi'yzaykon den bnayya shadar alaha ruja davreh llebawazjon ha dkarya ava avon
7	mekil la hwayton avde ela bnayya we'n bnayya af yarte d'alaha byad yeshu mshija
8	haden guer kad la yadin wayton la'laha plajton lhanon dmen kyanhon la waw alahe
9	hasha den didaton la'laha wyatira'iz d'ezidaton men alaha tuv hfajton ljon al hanon estukse mare wmeskine wmen drish saveton lmeshtabadu lhon
10	yawme wyarje wsavne washnayya natriton
11	dajel na dalma srika'iz li'yz bjon
12	hwaw ajwazy metul d'af ena ajwazjon hwiz ajay bae na menjon la medem askelton bi
13	yadin ton guer dvajrihuz besry sabartjon wiz men kdim
14	wnesyuna dvesry la shatton wla nadton ela ayj dalmala'ja d'alaha kabeltunany wa'yj dalyeshu mshija
15	aykaw hajil tuvjon sahed na guer laykon d'elu meshkja waz aynaykon jasen hwayton wyahbin li
16	dalma beldvava wiz ljon d'ajrsez ljon shrara
17	jasmin bjon la wa lshapirta ela lmejbashjon u saven d'aton tehon jasmin bhon
18	shapir u den dzezjasmon bshapiraza bjulsvan wla emazy dalwazjon na baljud
19	bnay aylen dmen drish mjabel na dama dnettsir bjon mshija
20	save wiz den d'ehwe lwazjon hasha we'shajlef baz kaly metul dzamih na bjon
21	emarw li aton aylen dsaven dnehon tjez namusa leh lnamusa la shamin ton
22	kziv guer dla'vraham tren bnin hwaw leh jad men amza wjad men ji'rta
23	ela haw dmen amza bavsar eziled haw den dmen ji'rta bmulkana hwa
24	halen den izayhen pela'za dzarten diyyazikas jda dmen tur sinay yalda lavduza d'iyzeh hagar
25	hagar guer tura hu dsinay dva'raviyya wshalma lhade urishlem wfalja avduza hi wavneh
26	ha den urishlem elata ji'rta y d'iyzeh eman
27	kziv guer d'ezbasamy karza ha dla yalda we'zpasjy waga ha dla mjabla metul dasguiw bneh dsadiza yatir men bneh davilta
28	jnan den ajay ayj isjak bnay mulkana jnan
29	wa'yj dhaden haw dilid wa bvesra radef wa lhaw davruja hajana af hasha
30	ela mana emar kzava apekeh la'mza wlavrah metul dla ni'raz brah d'amza am brah dji'rta
31	jnan hajil ajay la hwayn bnay amza ela bnay ji'rta
