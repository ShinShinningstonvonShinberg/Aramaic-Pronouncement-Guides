1	mana hajil amrinan al avraham risha d'avahaza d'eshkaj bavsar
2	elu guer avraham men vade esdadak wa iz wa leh shuvhara ela la lwaz alaha
3	mana guer emar kzava dhaymen avraham la'laha we'zjashbaz leh lsadiku
4	lman dfalaj den la mezjshev leh agreh ayj davtaybu ela ayj man dmettjiv leh
5	lhaw den dla plaj ela haymen baljud bman damsadek ljatayye mezjashba leh haymanuzeh lji'nu
6	aykana d'af dawid emar al tuveh dgavra ayna d'alaha jashev leh sadikuza dla vade kad amar
7	dtuvayhon la'ylen d'eshtvek lhon awlhon we'zkasiw jtahayhon
8	wtuvawy lgavra dla nejshuv leh alaha jtizeh
9	hana hajil tuva al gsurta hu aw al urluza amrinan guer d'ezjashbaz la'vraham haymanuzeh lji'nu
10	aykana hajil ezjashbaz leh bagsurta aw burluza la wa bagsurta ela burluza
11	aza hu guer shaklah lagsurta wjazma dji'nuza dhaymanuzeh davurluza dnehwe ava ljulhon aylen damhaymnin men urluza dzezjshev af lhon lji'nu
12	wa'va lagsurta la wa la'ylen dmen gsurta enon baljud ela af la'ylen dshalmin lekvaza dhaymanuza durluza d'avon avraham
13	la wa guer bnamusa hwa mulkana la'vraham walsareh dnehwe yarta lalma ela bji'nuza dhaymanuzeh
14	elu guer halen dmen namusa hwaw yarte srika waz haymanuza wamvatal wa mulkana
15	namusa guer mabdana hu drugsa kar dlayt guer namusa afla var namusa
16	metul hana bhaymanuza davtaybuza nesdadak wnehwe sharir mulkana ljuleh sareh la la'yna dmen namusa hu baljud ela af la'yna dmen haymanuza hu d'avraham d'iyzawy ava djulan
17	aykana dajziv dsamtaj ava lsuga' damme kdam alaha haw dhayment beh dmaje mize wkare la'ylen dla izayhon ayj izayhon
18	wadla savra lsavra haymen dnehwe ava lsuga' damme ayj dajziv dhajana nehwe saraj
19	wla ezkrah bhaymanuzeh kad mezbake bfagreh miza dahwa bar ma' shnin wavmarba miza dsara
20	wavmulkana d'alaha la ezpalag ayj jsir haymanuza ela ezjayyal bhaymanuza wyav teshbujta la'laha
21	wa'shar dmedem damlaj leh alaha meshkaj lmegmar
22	metul hana ezjashbaz leh lji'nu
23	wla wa metulazeh baljud ezkazbaz hade d'ezjashbaz haymanuzeh lji'nu
24	ela af metulazan d'aflan zid u dnejshuv aylen dhaymenn bman d'akim lmaran yeshu mshija men bez mize
25	dhu eshtlem metul jtahayn wkam metul dansadkan
