1	magel na ljon den lfuvi jazan d'iyzeh mshamshaniza dita dkenjre'was
2	dazkablunah bmaran ayj dsadek lkadishe wavjul svu dvaya menjon tkumon lah metul d'af hi kayyumta hwaz lsaguiyye' af li
3	sha'lw bashlama dapriskela wda'kelas palje damy byeshu mshija
4	dhenon halen jlaf nafshy sawrayhon yavw wla wa ena baljuday mawde na lhon ela af kulhen idaza damme
5	whavw shlama lita d'iyz bvaython sha'lw bashlama d'epenetaws jabivy ayna d'iyzawy rishiza d'aja'iyya bamshija
6	sha'lw bashlama dmariyya ayda dsagui le'yaz bjon
7	sha'lw bashlama d'andrawnikaws wadyuniyya jyanay dahwaw shvayya amy widiin enon bashlije wvamshija kdamay hwaw
8	sha'lw bashlama d'ampliyyaws jabivy bmaran
9	sha'lw bashlama d'uwrvanaws palja daman bamshija wde'stajus jabivy
10	sha'lw bashlama d'apeli gavya bmaran sha'lw bashlama davnay bayteh d'aristavulaws
11	sha'lw bashlama dherawdiyyawn jyany sha'lw bashlama davnay bayteh dnarkisaws aylen d'iyzayhon bmaran
12	sha'lw bashlama datrufena wdatrufasa dli'yen bmaran sha'lw bashlama dpersis jabivty ayda dsagui le'yaz bmaran
13	sha'lw bashlama drufas gavya bmaran wde'meh dileh wdily
14	sha'lw bashlama d'asunkritaws wdaflegawn wadherma wadpatrava wadhermi wda'je damhon
15	sha'lw bashlama dfilalagaws wadyuliyya wadni'raws wadjazeh wda'lumpa wadjulhon kadishe damhon
16	sha'lw bashlama jad djad bnushakta kadishta sha'lan bashlamjon kulhen idazeh damshija
17	baena den menjon ajay dzesdahron men aylen dfalagwaza wmajshule avdin lvar men yulpana d'aton ilefton dzezrajkon ljon menhon
18	aylen guer dhajana enon la mshamshin lmaran yeshu mshija ela ljarshon wavmele basimaza wavvurkaza maten lebawaza dafshite
19	meshtamanuzjon den diljon ljul barnash ezyadaz jade na hajil bjon wsave na dzehon jakimin ltavaza wzamimin lvishaza
20	alaha den dashlama neshjkiwy bagal lsatana tjez reglaykon taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija tehwe amjon
21	sha'lin bashlamjon timaze'aws palja damy wlukiyyaws wi'yasawn wsawsipatraws jyanay
22	sha'el na bashlamjon ena tertiyyaws djezbez egarta bmaran
23	sha'el bashlamjon ga'iyyaws mkablany wadjulah ita sha'el bashlamjon erastaws rabayta damdita wkuwa'rtaws aja
24	taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija am kuljon amin
25	la'laha den haw dmeshkaj danshararjon b'ewangueliyyawn dily haw d'ezkres al yeshu mshija bguelyana drasa dmen savnay alme mjasay wa
26	ezgli den bsavna hana byad kzave danviyye wavfukdaneh d'alaha dalalam ezida ljulhon amme lmashma dhaymanuza
27	dhuyyu jakima baljudawy shuvja byad yeshu mshija lalamalmin amin
