1	metul hana layt laj mapak bruja aw barnasha d'aen javreh bhaw guer dda'en at javraj nafshaj u mjayyev at af at guer dda'en at bhen u mezhapaj at
2	wyadinan d'iyzawy dineh d'alaha bkushta al aylen davhalen mezhapjin
3	mana den mezjashav at aw barnasha dda'en at la'ylen davhalen mezhapjin kad af at bhen mezhapaj at d'at teruk men dineh d'alaha
4	aw al uzra dvasimuzeh wal magraz rujeh wal azra dyav laj mamraj at wla yada at dvasimuzeh d'alaha lazyavuza hu maytya laj
5	ela metul kashyuz lebaj dla ta'ev sa'em at laj simza drugsa lyawma drugsa walguelyana ddina ki'na d'alaha
6	haw dfara ljulnash ayj vadawy
7	la'ylen dvamsaybranuza davade tave teshbujta wi'ykara wla mezjablanuza baen yahev lhon jayye dalalam
8	aylen den dasen wla mettpisin lashrara ela lawla mettpisin nefru enon rugsa wjemza
9	u'wlsana wturafa ljul barnash dfalaj bishaza lihudayye lukdam wla'rmayye
10	teshbujta den wi'ykara washlama ljul dfalaj tavaza lihudayye lukdam wla'rmayye
11	la guer iz masav b'ape lwaz alaha
12	aylen guer dadla namusa jtaw af dla namusa ni'vdon wa'ylen davnamusa jtaw men namusa netdinon
13	la wa guer shamuawy dnamusa ki'nin kdam alaha ela avudawy dnamusa mesdadkin
14	en guer amme dnamusa layt lhon men kyanhon nebdon dnamusa hanon djad namusa layt wa lhon lnafshhon hwaw namusa
15	whenon mjawen vadeh dnamusa kad kziv al lebhon wmashda layhon ti'rthon kad majshvazhon mjawnan aw nafkan ruja lajdade
16	byawma dda'en alaha kasyaza davnaynasha ayj ewangueliyyawn dily byad yeshu mshija
17	en at den dihudayya mezkre at wmettnij at al namusa wmeshtavhar at b'alaha
18	dyada at sevyaneh wfaresh at walyaza dyalif at men namusa
19	we'ttjelt al nafshaj damdabrana at dawire wnuhra d'aylen d'iyzayhon bjeshuja
20	wraduyya djasiray reyana wmalfana datlayye wi'yz laj dumya didaza wdashrara bnamusa
21	at hajil dmalef at lajrane lnafshaj la malef at wadmajres at dla negnvon at ganev at
22	wda'mar at dla nguron at ga'ar at wa't dsha'et at pzajre mjales at bez makdsha
23	wa't dmeshtavhar at bnamusa bhaw davar at al namusa la'laha w msaar at
24	shmeh guer d'alaha metulazjon u mezgadaf bez amme ayj dajziv
25	gsurta guer mahnya en namusa tegmur en tebar laj den men namusa gsurtaj hwaz lah urluza
26	en u den durluza tetar pukdaneh dnamusa la ha urluza mezjashba lah gsurta
27	watdon urluza dmen kyanah gamra namusa laj dvajzava wvagsurta avar at al namusa
28	la wa guer man davguelya haw u ihudayya afla ayda dmezjasya bvesra gsurta
29	ela haw u ihudayya ayna davjesya hu wagsurta ayda dadleba y bruj wla bajzava ayda dzeshbujtah la waz men bnay nasha ela men alaha
