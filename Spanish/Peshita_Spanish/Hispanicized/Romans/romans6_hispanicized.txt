1	mana hajil ni'mar nkawe bah bajtiza dtaybuza tezyatar
2	jas aylen guer dmizn lajtiza aykana nije bah tuv
3	aw la yadin ton d'aylen damadn byeshu mshija bmawteh u madn
4	ezkvarn ameh bmamudiza lmawta d'aykana dkam yeshu mshija men bez mize bzeshbujta d'avu hajana af jnan bjayye jaze nhalej
5	en guer ajjad eznsevn ameh badmuza dmawteh hajana af bakyamteh nehwe
6	yadinan guer dvarnashan atika esdkef ameh dnezbatal pagra dajtiza dzuv la nshamesh lajtiza
7	ayna dmiz guer ezjarar leh men jtiza
8	en hajil mizn am mshija nhaymen dameh am mshija nije
9	yadinan guer damshija kam men bez mize wzuv la ma'ez wmawta la meshtalat beh
10	dmiz guer lajtiza hu miz jda svan wadjay jay u la'laha
11	hajana af aton jshuvw nafshjon dmize ton lajtiza wjayye ton la'laha bmaran yeshu mshija
12	la hajil tamlej jtiza bfagrjon miza ayj dzeshtamon larguigazeh
13	wa'f la ttayvon hadamaykon sayna dawla lajtiza ela tayyevw nafshjon la'laha ayj nasha dmen mize jyayton whadamaykon sayna nehon lji'nuzeh d'alaha
14	wajtiza la meshtalta laykon la guer izaykon tjez namusa ela tjez taybuza
15	mana hajil nejte dla hwayn tjez namusa ela tjez taybuza jas
16	la yadin ton dalman damtayvin ton nafshjon dzeshtamon leh lavduza dileh aton avde dhaw dmeshtamin ton leh en lajtiza we'n lmashma edna dji'nuza
17	taybu den la'laha davde wayton dajtiza we'shtmaton men leba ladmuza dyulpana d'eshtlemton leh
18	wjad ezjararton men jtiza eshtabadton lji'nuza
19	ayj dvez bnaynasha amar na metul krihuza dvesrjon d'aykana dtayyevton hadamaykon lavduza dtanpuza wadawla hajana af hasha tayyevw hadamaykon lavduza dji'nuza wadkadishuza
20	kad avde wayton guer dajtiza mjarre wayton lji'nuza
21	wmana adsha iz wa ljon haden ayna dyawmana bahtin ton beh jarzeh guer izeh mawta
22	whasha d'ezjararton men jtiza wahwayton avde la'laha iz ljon pi're kadishe djarzhon jayye dalalam
23	te'gurta den dajtiza mawta w wmawhavzeh d'alaha jayye dalalam bmaran yeshu mshija
