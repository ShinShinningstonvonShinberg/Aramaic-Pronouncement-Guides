1	pawlaws avda dyeshu mshija karya washlija d'ezpresh le'wangueliyyawn d'alaha
2	dmen kdim mlaj byad nviyyawy bajzave kadishe
3	al breh haw d'eziled bavsar men sara dvez dawid
4	we'zida bra d'alaha bjil wavruj kdush dkam men bez mize yeshu mshija maran
5	dveh nsavn taybuza washlijuza bjulhon amme ayj dneshtamon lhaymanuza dashmeh
6	d'af aton menhon aton krayya byeshu mshija
7	ljulhon davrumi jabivawy d'alaha krayya wkadishe shlama wtaybuza amjon men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
8	lukdam mawde na la'lahy byeshu mshija al kuljon dhaymanuzjon eshtamaz bjuleh alma
9	sahed u li guer alaha dleh mshamesh na bruj b'ewangueliyyawn davreh dadla shalwa bjulsvan metdjar na ljon baslawazy
10	wmezjanan na d'en men kadu tezpzaj li urja bsevyaneh d'alaha d'ize lwazjon
11	metul dtav swe na d'ejsejon we'tel ljon mawhavta druj dvah teshtarron
12	wa'jjda nezbayya bhaymanuza diljon wdily
13	save na den ajay dzedon dsavnin saguiyya'n sviz d'ize lwazjon we'zkliz dama lhasha d'af bjon nehwe li adsha ayj davsharka damme
14	yawnayye wvarbrayye jakime wsajle daljulnash jayyav na d'ajres
15	whajana mezjapat na d'af ljon davrumi esabar
16	la guer bahez na beh b'ewangueliyyawn metul djayla w d'alaha ljayye djul damhaymnin beh en men ihudayye lukdam we'n men armayye
17	ki'nuzeh guer d'alaha beh mezgalya men haymanuza lhaymanuza ayj dajziv dji'na men haymanuza nije
18	mezgle w guer rugseh d'alaha men shmayya al kuleh awlhon wrushhon davnaynasha hanon dkushta bawla ajidin
19	metul didiuzeh d'alaha galya y bhon alaha guer glah bhon
20	kasyazeh guer d'alaha men tarmyazeh dalma lveryazeh bsukala mezjasyan wjayleh wa'lahuzeh dalalam dnehon dla mapak bruj
21	metul didaw la'laha wla ayj dla'laha shabju wa'wdiw leh ela estarakw bmajshvazhon we'zjashaj lebhon dla mestakal
22	wjad savrin bnafshhon djakimin enon shtaw lhon
23	wjalefw teshbujteh d'alaha dla mezjabal badmuza dsalma dvarnasha dmezjabal wvadmuza dfarajza wda'rbaz regleh wadrajsha d'ara
24	metul hana ashlem enon alaha larguigaza tama'za dlebhon dansaron pagrayhon bhon
25	wjalefw shrareh d'alaha bjadavuza wadjelw wshameshw lveryaza tav men dalvaruhen dleh teshbjan wvurkan lalam almin amin
26	metul hana ashlem enon alaha lji've dsara nekvazhon guer jalef jshajza dajyanhen wavmedem dla mjan ezjashaj
27	wzuv af dejrayhon hajana shvakw jshajza dajyana dnekvaza we'shtarajw bregza jad al jad wdejra al dejra behtza vadw wfurana dsadek wa ltayuzhon baknumhon kablu
28	wa'yj dla danw bnafshhon dnedon la'laha ashlem enon alaha lmada dasrikuza dnehon avdin medem dla wale
29	kad mlen kul awaluza wsanyuza wmariruza wvishuza waluvuza wajsama wketla wjeryana wnejla wmajshvaza bishaza
30	wretna wme'jalkarsa wasni'in la'laha msarane jzire shavhrane meshkjay bishaza jasiray reyana dla'vahayhon la mettpisin
31	wdakyama layt lhon wla juba wla shayna wla rajme iz bhon
32	aylen djad yadin dineh d'alaha dla'ylen d'ayj halen sarin lmawta mjayyev la wa baljud avdin lhen ela af meshtawtfin la'ylen dhalen avdin
