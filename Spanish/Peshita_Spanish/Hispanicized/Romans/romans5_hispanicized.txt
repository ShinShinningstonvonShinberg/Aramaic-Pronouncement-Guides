1	metul d'esdadakn hajil bhaymanuza nehwe lan shlama lwaz alaha bmaran yeshu mshija
2	dveh ezkaravn bhaymanuza ltaybuza hade dvah kaminan wmeshtavhrinan bsavra dzeshbujteh d'alaha
3	wla hajana baljud ela af b'uwlsanin meshtavhrinan dyadinan d'uwlsana msaybranuza gamar ban
4	wamsaybranuza bukya wvukya savra
5	savra den la mavhez metul djubeh d'alaha mshapa al lebawazan bruja dkudsha d'ezyahbaz lan
6	en den mshija metul krihuzan bsavna hana jlaf rashie miz
7	lmajsen guer nash jlaf rashie ma'ez jlaf tave guer taj mamraj nash lammaz
8	harka mjawe alaha jubeh dalwazan d'en kad jatayye izayn wayn mshija jlafayn miz
9	kma hajil yatira'iz nesdadak hasha badmeh wveh nezpase men rugsa
10	en guer kad izayn beldvave ezrai aman alaha bmawta davreh kma hajil yatira'iz bzaruzeh nije bjayyawy
11	wla hajana baljud ela af neshtavhar b'alaha byad maran yeshu mshija dveh u hasha kabeln taruza
12	aykana guer davyad jad barnasha elaz jtiza lalma wavyad jtiza mawta whajana bjulhon bnay nasha var mawta bha djulhon jtaw
13	dama guer lnamusa jtiza kad izeh waz balma la jshiva waz jtiza metul dlayt wa namusa
14	ela amlej mawta men adam wadama lmushe af al aylen dla jtaw badmuza davar namuseh d'adam haw d'iyzawy dmuza dhaw dazid
15	ela la wa ayj shuraza hajana mawhavta en guer metul shurazeh djad saguiyye' mizw kma hajil yatira'iz taybuzeh d'alaha wmawhavzeh metul jad barnasha yeshu mshija bsaguiyye' tezyatar
16	wla ayj sajluza djad hajana mawhavta dina guer dahwa men jad ljuyyava hwa mawhavta den men jtahe saguiyye' hwaz lji'nu
17	en guer metul sajluza djad amlej mawta yatira'iz aylen dansavw suga' dtaybuza wadmawhavza wadji'nuza bjayye namljon byad jad yeshu mshija
18	ajsna hajil dmetul sajluza djad hwa juyyava ljulhon bnaynasha hajana metul ki'nuza djad tehwe sajuza ljayye ljulhon bnaynasha
19	aykana guer dmetul la meshtamanuzeh djad barnasha jatayye saguiyye' hwaw hajana af metul meshtamanuzeh djad saguiyye' ki'ne hawen
20	malana den dahwa lnamusa dzesgue jtiza wjar dsegyaz jtiza taman ezyatraz taybuza
21	d'ayj d'amljaz jtiza bmawta hajana tamlej taybuza bji'nuza ljayye dalalam byad maran yeshu mshija
