1	jayyavinan hajil jnan jaylzane djurhana damjile neshkul wla lnafshan neshpar
2	ela nash menan lkariveh neshpar btavaza ayj dalvenyana
3	metul d'af mshija la wa lnafsheh shfar ela ayj dajziv djesda damjasdanayk nfal lay
4	kul medem guer dmen kdim ezkzev lyulpana hu dilan ezkzev dvamsaybranuza wavvuyya'a dajzave savra nehwe lan
5	alaha den damsaybranuza wadvuyya'a netel ljon dshawyuza tezjashvon jad al jad byeshu mshija
6	davjad reyan wavjad pum tshabjon la'laha avu dmaran yeshu mshija
7	metul hade hwaw mkarvin wtanin lajdade aykana d'af mshija karevjon lzeshbujteh d'alaha
8	amar na den dyeshu mshija shamesh gsurta jlaf shrareh d'alaha aykana dnashar mulkana d'avahaza
9	wamme nshabjon la'laha jlaf rajme dahwaw layhon ayj dajziv d'awde laj bamme wlashmaj esamar
10	wzuv emar ezbamw amme am ameh
11	wzuv emar shabajw lmarya kuljon amme shabja kulhen emwaza
12	wzuv eshaya emar dnehwe ekara li'yshay wman dankum nehwe risha lamme walawy nsabron amme
13	alaha den dsavra nemlejon kulah jaduza washlama bhaymanuza dzezyatron bsavreh bjayla druja dkudsha
14	mpas na den af ena laykon ajay d'af aton mlen ton u tavza wamshamleton bjulah idaza wmeshkjin ton af lajrane lmartayyu
15	kalil den maraja'iz kezbez ljon ajay aykana d'ahedjon btaybuza d'ezyahbaz li men alaha
16	d'ehwe mshamshana lyeshu mshija bamme we'fluj le'wangueliyyawn d'alaha dnehwe kurbana damme mkabal wamkadash bruja dkudsha
17	iz li hajil shuvhara byeshu mshija lwaz alaha
18	la guer mamraj na d'imar medem dla sar b'iyday mshija lmashma damme bmelza wvavade
19	bjayla d'azwaza wadzedmraza wavjayla drujeh d'alaha aykana dmen urishlem ezkrej dama li'lurikawn we'male svarzeh damshija
20	kad mezjapat na esabar la kar d'ezkri shmeh damshija dla evne al sheze'sta nujrata
21	ela aykana dajziv d'aylen dla eze'mar lhon lawy nejsuneh wa'ylen dla shmaw nettpison
22	metul hana ettajsez savnin saguiyya'n d'ize lwazjon
23	hasha den metul dduka layt li bhalen azrawaza waswe wiz men kdam shnayya saguiyya'za d'ize lwazjon
24	ma d'asel na la'spaniyya msabar na d'ize we'jsejon wa'ton tlaunany lzaman ma dkalil men sagui ezbasmez bajsazjon
25	hasha den asel na lu'wrishlem d'eshamesh lkadishe
26	svaw guer halen davmakedawniyya wva'ja'iyya dshawtafuza tehwe lhon am meskine kadishe d'iyz b'uwrishlem
27	svaw metul d'af jayyavin lhon en guer badruj eshtawtafw amhon amme jayyavin enon d'af bdavsar nshamshon enon
28	hade hajil ma dgamrez wjatmez lhon adsha hana avar na laykon la'spaniyya
29	yada na den d'emazy d'aze na lwazjon bmulayya hu dvurkza d'ewangueliyyawn damshija aze na
30	bae na den menjon ajay bmaran yeshu mshija wavjuba druja dzemlon amy basluza dajlafay lwaz alaha
31	d'ezpase men aylen dla mettpisin dvihud wzeshmeshta dmawbel na lkadishe dvu'wrishlem tezkabal shapir
32	wi'ze lwazjon bjaduza bsevyaneh d'alaha we'ttnij amjon
33	alaha den dashlama nehwe am kuljon amin
