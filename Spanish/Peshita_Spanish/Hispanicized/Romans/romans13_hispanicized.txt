1	kul nfesh lshultane drabuza teshtabad layt guer shultana dla wa men alaha hu wa'ylen shultane d'iyzayhon men alaha hu pkidin
2	man dka'em hajil lukval shultana lukval pukdana d'alaha ka'em whalen dkamin lukvalhon dina nesvon
3	dayyane guer la waw dejlza lavade tave ela lvishe save at hajil dla tedjal men shultana ved tavza wzeshbujta tehwe laj meneh
4	mshamshana hu guer d'alaha ela laj ltavaza we'n bishta aved at djal la wa guer srika'iz asir lsafsira mshamshana hu guer d'alaha wzavua drugsa la'ylen davdin bishaza
5	wmetul hana alsa lan dneshtabad la metul rugsa baljud ela af metul ti'rtan
6	metul hana af ksef risha yahbin ton mshamshane enon guer d'alaha dalayhen dhalen kamin
7	pruw hajil ljulnash ayj dmetjiv leh lman dajsef risha ksef risha walman dmajsa majsa walman ddejlza dejlza walman d'iykara ikara
8	walnash medem la tjuvon ela jad ljad lmajavu man dmajev guer javreh namusa mali
9	wa'f ha guer d'emar dla tgur wla tektul wla tegnuv wla terag we'n iz pukdana jrina bhade melza meshtalam dzerjam lkarivaj ayj nafshaj
10	juba lkariveh bishta la saar metul djuba mulayyeh u dnamusa
11	wa'f hade daw dsavna hu wshaza y mekil dnetir men shenzan hasha hu guer ezkaravw lan jayyayn yatir men djad haymenn
12	lilya mekil var wi'ymama krev nnij menan hajil vade djeshuja wnelbash sayneh dnuhra
13	wa'yj dvi'ymama b'eskima nhalej la basmara wla brawayyuza wla bmadmja tanfa wla bajsama wavjeryana
14	ela luvshu lmaran yeshu mshija wla ti'spon dvesrjon larguigaza
