1	mekil layt jayyavuza la'ylen dla mhaljin bavsar byeshu mshija
2	namusa guer druja djayye davyeshu mshija jarraj men namusa dajtiza wadmawta
3	metul damjil wa guer namusa byad krihuzeh dvesra shadar alaha lavreh badmuza dvesra dajtiza metulazah dajtiza danjayvih lajtiza bvesreh
4	dji'nuza dnamusa ban tezmale dla wa bavsar mhaljinan ela bruj
5	aylen guer dvavsar enon davsar u mezraen wa'ylen dadruj enon druj u mezraen
6	tariza guer dvesra mawta hu wzariza druja jayye washlama
7	metul dzariza dvesra beldvavuza y lwaz alaha lnamusa guer d'alaha la meshtabda metul dla meshkja
8	wa'ylen dvavsar enon lmeshpar la'laha la meshkjin
9	aton den la wayton bavsar ela bruj en sharira'iz rujeh d'alaha amra bjon en den nash layt beh rujeh damshija hana la wa dileh
10	we'n den mshija bjon pagra miz u metul jtiza ruja den jayya y metul ki'nuza
11	we'n rujeh dhaw man d'akim lmaran yeshu mshija men bez mize amra bjon haw man d'akimeh lyeshu mshija men bez mize af lfagraykon mize naje metul rujeh damra bjon
12	hasha ajay jayyavinan law lvesra dvavsar nhalej
13	en guer bavsar ja'en ton zidin ton u lammaz we'n bruj hupajay pagra mmizin ton ja'en ton
14	aylen guer davruja d'alaha metdabrin halen bnayya enon d'alaha
15	la guer nsavton ruja davduza tuv ldejlza ela nsavton ruja dsimaz bnayya dvah karenan ava avon
16	whi ruja mashda lrujan d'iyzayn bnayya d'alaha
17	we'n bnayya af yarte yarte d'alaha wavnay yartuzeh dyeshu mshija d'en nejash ameh af ameh neshtabaj
18	mezrae na guer dla shawen jashawy dsavna hana lzeshbujta ayda dazida dzezgle ban
19	kulah guer briza msabra wamsakya lguelyana davnawy d'alaha
20	briza guer eshtabdaz lasrikuza la bsevyanah ela metul man dshabdah al savra
21	d'af hi briza tezjarar men avduza dajvala bajrure dzeshbujta davnayya d'alaha
22	yadinan guer djulhen beryaza mettanjan wamjablan dama lyawmana
23	wla baljud henen ela af jnan d'iyz ban rishiza druja mettanjinan bnafshan wamsakenan lsimaz bnayya walfurkana dfagrayn
24	metul davsavra w jyayn savra den dmezjse la wa savra en guer jasenan leh mana msakenan leh
25	en den lmedem dla mezjse msabrinan bamsaybranuza mkawenan
26	hajana af hi ruja madra lajrihuzan mana guer nsale ayj ma dwale la yadinan ela hi ruja msalya jlafayn bzenjaza dla mezmallan
27	dma'esh den lebawaza hu yada mana i tariza druja d'ayj sevyaneh u d'alaha msalya jlaf kadishe
28	yadinan den d'aylen dmajvin la'laha bjul medem madar lhon ltavza la'ylen dkadem sam dnehon krayya
29	wmen lukdam ida enon warsham enon badmuza dsurta davreh dhu nehwe bujra d'aje saguiyye'
30	la'ylen den dkadem rsham lhon kra wla'ylen dakra lhon sadek wla'ylen dsadek lhon shabaj
31	mana hajil ni'mar al halen en alaha jlafayn manu dalkuvlan
32	we'n al breh la jas ela jlaf kulan ashlmeh aykana la kul medem ameh netel lan
33	manu nekbul al gvayya d'alaha alaha msadek
34	manu mjayyev mshija miz wkam wi'yzawy al yamina d'alaha wvae jlafayn
35	manu nefrshany men jubeh damshija ulsana aw jvushya aw rdufya aw kafna aw artelayyuza aw kindinaws aw sayfa
36	ayj dajziv dmetulazaj kulyum matinan we'zjshevn ayj emre lnejsza
37	ela bhalen kulhen sakainan byad man d'ajvan
38	mpas na guer dla mawta wla jayye wla mala'je wla shultane wla jayle wla dkaman wla dazidan
39	wla rawma wla umka afla briza jriza teshkaj tefrshany men jubeh d'alaha davmaran yeshu mshija
