1	aw la yadin ton ajay lyaday namusa guer mmalel na dnamusa shalit u al gavra kma djay
2	ayj atza d'asira y bvalah kma djay bnamusa en den miz balah ezjarraz men namusa dvalah
3	en den kad jay balah tekaf lagvar jrin hwaz lah gayyarza en den nmuz balah ezjarraz men namusa wla izeh gayyarza en tehwe lgavra jrina
4	whasha ajay af aton mitton lnamusa bfagreh damshija dzehon lajrin ayna dkam men bez mize dzetlon pi're la'laha
5	kad bvesra wayn guer ki've dajtahe davnamusa mezjaptin waw bhadamayn dfi're netel lmawta
6	hasha den ezbataln men namusa wmizn lhaw d'ajid wa lan danshamesh mekil bjazuza druja wla batikuz kzava
7	mana hajil amrinan namusa jtiza hu jas ela lajtiza la yelpez ela byad namusa la guer regza yada wiz elu la namusa d'emar dla terag
8	wavhana pukdana eshkjaz lah jtiza elza wguemraz bi kul rega belad namusa guer jtiza miza waz
9	ena den jay wiz dla namusa men kdim kad eza den pukdana jtiza jyaz we'na mizez
10	we'shtjaj li pukdana haw djayye lmawta
11	jtiza guer belza d'eshkjaz lah byad pukdana atyazany wveh ktalzany
12	namusa maden kadish u wfukdana kadish u wji'yn wtav
13	tavza hajil li lmawta hu hwaz jas ela jtiza dzezjse dajtiza hi davtavza guemraz bi mawta dyatira'iz tezjayyav jtiza bfukdana
14	yadinan guer dnamusa druj u ena den davsar na wamsaban na lajtiza
15	medem dsaar na guer la yada na wla wa medem dsave na aved na ela medem dsane na haw u aved na
16	we'n medem dla save na aved na sahed na al namusa dshapir u
17	hasha den la wa ena saar na hade ela jtiza damra bi
18	yada na hu guer dla amra bi hanaw den bvesry tavza d'esbe guer btavza pshik li d'esrih den la meshkaj na
19	la wa guer ltavza dsave na d'ebed aved na ela bishta dla save na d'ebed lah aved na
20	we'n medem dla save na aved na la wa ena aved na ela jtiza damra bi
21	meshkaj na hajil lnamusa dshalem lreyany haw dsave dnebed tavza metul dvishta kariva y li
22	jade na guer bnamuseh d'alaha bvarnasha dalgaw
23	jase na den namusa jrina bhadamay dmakrev lukval namusa dreyany wshave li lnamusa dajtiza d'iyz bhadamay
24	dawya na barnasha manu nfaseny men pagra hana dmawta
25	mawde na la'laha byad maran yeshu mshija hasha hajil ena breyany avda na dnamusa d'alaha bvesry den izay avda dnamusa dajtiza
