1	mana y hajil yatiruzeh dihudayya aw mana yuzranah dagsurta
2	sagui bjul medem lukdam d'ezhayman melawy d'alaha
3	en menhon guer la haymenw dalma badla haymenw haymanuzeh d'alaha batelw
4	jas izawy guer alaha sharira wjul barnash dagal aykana dajziv dzehwe ki'yn bmelayk wzeske kad danin laj
5	en den awlan ki'nuzeh d'alaha mkim mana ni'mar lma awal u alaha dmayte rugseh ayj barnasha hu mmalel na
6	jas we'n la aykana ndon alaha lalma
7	en guer shrareh d'alaha ezyatar bdagaluzy lzeshbujteh dileh lmana hajil ena ayj jatayya mettdin na
8	aw dalma ayj damgadfin layn wa'mrin d'amrinan dnebed bishaza dni'zyan tavaza hanon ddinhon ntir u lji'nuza
9	mana hajil ajidinan yatira dkademn psakn al ihudayye wal armayye dazjez jtiza enon kulhon
10	ayj dajziv dlayt ki'na afla jad
11	wla dmestakal wla dvae la'laha
12	kulhon staw ajjda we'stliw wlayt daved tavza afla jad
13	kavre pzije gagrazhon wleshanayhon nakulzanin wjemza d'espes tjez sefwazhon
14	pumhon mle lawtza wmerza
15	wreglayhon kalilan lme'shad dma
16	shjaka wduwana b'uwrjazhon
17	u'wrja dashlama la idaw
18	wdejlzeh d'alaha layt kdam aynayhon
19	yadinan den dmedem d'emar namusa la'ylen davnamusa enon emar djul pum nestjar walma kuleh nezjayyav la'laha
20	metul dmen vadawy dnamusa la mesdadak kul bsar kdamawy men namusa guer ezyadaz jtiza
21	hasha den dla namusa ki'nuzeh d'alaha ezgalyaz wmashed leh hu namusa wanviyye
22	ki'nuzeh den d'alaha byad haymanuza y dyeshu mshija ljulnash af al kulnash damhaymen beh layt guer purshana
23	metul djulhon jtaw wjasirin men teshbujteh d'alaha
24	wmesdadkin btaybuza magan wavfurkana d'iyzawy byeshu mshija
25	hana dkadem sameh alaha jusayya bhaymanuza dadmeh metul jtahayn dmen kdim jtayn
26	b'azra dyav lan alaha bmagraz rujeh lzajwiza dji'nuzeh davsavna hana dhu nehwe ki'na wansadek bji'nuza lman davhaymanuza hu dmaran yeshu mshija
27	aykaw hajil shuvhara ezbatal leh b'ayna namusa davade la ela bnamusa dhaymanuza
28	mezraenan hajil davhaymanuza hu mesdadak barnasha wla bavade dnamusa
29	lma guer alaha dihudayye w baljud wadamme la in af damme
30	metul djad u alaha damsadek gsurta bhaymanuza af urluza bah bhaymanuza
31	lma hajil namusa hu mvatlinan bhaymanuza jas ela namusa hu mkiminan
