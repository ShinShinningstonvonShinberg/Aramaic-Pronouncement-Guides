1	ajay sevyana dleby wvauzy dalwaz alaha jlafayhon dnijon
2	mashed na guer layhon datnana d'alaha iz bhon ela law bidaza
3	ki'nuzeh guer d'alaha la idaw ela baw dji'nuza dnafshhon nkimon wmetul hana lji'nuzeh d'alaha la eshtabadw
4	sajeh guer dnamusa mshija hu lji'nuza ljul damhaymen
5	mushe guer hajana kzav ki'nuza davnamusa dman dnebed halen nije bhen
6	ki'nuza den davhaymanuza hajana amra dla ti'mar blebaj dmanu slek lashmayya wa'jez lamshija
7	wmanu njez lazhuma dashyul wa'sek lamshija men bez mize
8	ela mana emar kariv u laj pezgama lfumaj wallebaj hade y melza dhaymanuza dmajrsinan
9	we'n tawde bfumaj bmaran yeshu wazhaymen blebaj d'alaha akimeh men bez mize tije
10	leba guer damhaymen beh mesdadak wfuma dmawde beh jayye
11	emar guer kzava djul damhaymen beh la nevhaz
12	wavhade la prash la lihudayye wla la'rmayye jad u guer marya djulhon datir bjul dkare leh
13	kul guer dnekre shmeh dmarya nije
14	aykana hajil nekron la'yna dla haymenw beh aw aykana nhaymnon lhaw dla shamu aw aykana neshmon dla majrsana
15	aw aykana najrson en la neshtaljon ayj dajziv dma ya'yan reglayhon damsabray shlama wdamsabray tavaza
16	ela la wa kulhon eshtmaw lasvarza d'ewangueliyyawn eshaya guer emar mary manu haymen lvaz kalan
17	mekil haymanuza men mashma edna y wmashma edna men melza d'alaha
18	ela amar na dalma la shmaw wha bjulah ara nefkaz baz kalhon wavsawpeh dzivel melayhon
19	ela amar na dalma la ida israyyel kadmayya mushe hajana emar atenjon bam dla am wavama dla mettpis arguesjon
20	eshaya den amraj we'mar d'ezjsiz la'ylen dla ba'uny we'shtajjez la'ylen dalay la sha'elw
21	li'ysrayyel den emar dfeshtez iday yawma kuleh lwaz ama dmezjre wla mettpis
