1	ela amar na dalma dajkeh alaha lameh jas af ena guer men israyyel na men sareh d'avraham men sharbza dvenyamin
2	la djak alaha lameh ayna dmen kdim idi wa leh aw la yadin ton bajzava d'eliyya mana emar kad kavel wa la'laha al israyyel wa'mar
3	mary lanviyyayk katelw walmadbjayk sajefw we'na hu baljuday eshtajrez wvaen lnafshy
4	we'ze'mar leh bguelyana dha shevkez lnafshy shava alfin gavrin aylen dal burkayhon la brejw wla sguedw lvala
5	hajana af bhana savna sharkana hu eshtjar bagviza dtaybuza
6	en den btaybuza la wa men vade we'n la taybuza la izeh taybuza en den bavade la wa men taybuza we'n la vada la izawy vada
7	mana hajil ha dvae wa israyyel la eshkaj gviza den eshkjaz sharkhon den ezawarw blebhon
8	aykana dajziv dyav lhon alaha ruja mdaraniza wayne dla nevjron bhen we'dne dla neshmon dama lyawma dyawmana
9	wdawid tuv emar nehwe pazurhon kdamayhon lfaja wfuranhon lzukalza
10	nejshjan aynayhon dla nejson wjashon bjulsvan nehwe kfif
11	amar na den dalma ettkelw ayj dneplon jas ela bzuklazhon dilhon hwaw jayye lamme latnanhon
12	we'n tuklazhon hwaz uzra lalma wjayyavuzhon uzra lamme kma hajil shumlahon
13	ljon den amar na lamme ena d'iyzay shlija damme lzeshmeshty mshabaj na
14	dalma aten lavnay besry wa'je nashin menhon
15	en guer mestalyanuzhon taruza lalma hwaz kma hajil punahon ela jayye dmen bez mize
16	we'n den rishiza kadisha af gvilta we'n ekara kadish u af sawke
17	we'n men sawke ezpshaj wa't dsayta at dvara ettemt bdukyazhen wahwayt shawtafa lekareh walshumneh dsayta
18	la teshtavhar al sawke en den meshtavhar at la wa at shkil leh lekara ela hu ekara shkil laj
19	wajvar ti'mar dsawke d'ezpshaj d'ena bdukyazhen ettem
20	shapir halen metul dla haymen ezpshaj at den bhaymanuza kamt la tetrim breyanaj ela djal
21	en alaha guer al sawke dmen kyanhen la jas dalma afla layk nejus
22	jsi hajil basimuzeh wkashyuzeh d'alaha al aylen danfalw kashyuza layk den basimuza en tkawe bah bvasimuza we'n la af at tezpshaj
23	whanon en la nkaon bjasiruz haymanuzhon af henon nettamon meshkaj guer alaha dzuv natem enon
24	en guer at dmen sayta at dvara haw dvajyanaj ezpshajt wadla bajyanaj ettemt bsayta tava kma hajil hanon en nettamon bsayta dajyanhon
25	save na guer dzedon ajay rasa hana dla tehon jakimin breyan nafshjon dawiruz leba men azar kalil hwaz li'ysrayyel dama dneul mulayya damme
26	whaden kuleh israyyel nije aykana dajziv dni'ze men sehyon paruka wnahpej awla men yakuv
27	whaden tehwe lhon diyyaziki ha dmen lwazy ma dshevkez lhon jtahayhon
28	b'ewangueliyyawn den beldvave enon metulazjon wavgavyuza jabivayn enon metul avahaza
29	la guer hafka alaha bmawhavzeh wavkeryaneh
30	aykana guer d'af aton la mettpisin wayton la'laha men kdim whasha ezjnenton metul la mettpisanuzhon dhanon
31	hajana af halen la ettpisw hasha lrajme dalaykon d'af layhon nehon rajme
32	jvash guer alaha ljulnash bla mettpisanuza dal kul nash nrajem
33	aw umka duzra wjejmza wmada d'alaha dnash la mash dinawy u'wrjazeh la mezakvan
34	manu guer ida reyaneh dmarya aw manu hwa leh bel melka
35	wmanu kadem yav leh wjen nsav meneh
36	metul djul meneh wjul beh wjul b'iydeh dleh teshbjan wvurkan lalam almin amin
