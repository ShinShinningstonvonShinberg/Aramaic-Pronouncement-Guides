1	ajay la bmasav b'ape tehon ajidin lhaymanuza dzeshbujteh dmaran yeshu mshija
2	en guer neul lajnushtjon nash deskazeh ddahva aw dma'nawy shapire wneul meskina bma'ne sa'e
3	wazjuron bhaw dalvish ma'ne shapire wzi'mron leh at harka tev shapir walmeskina ti'mron leh at kum lhal aw tev laj harka kdam kuvsha dreglayn
4	la ha ezpalagton ljon bnafshjon wahwayton mfarshane dmajshvaza bishaza
5	shmaw ajay jabive la wa lmeskine dalma atire den bhaymanuza gva alaha dnehon yarte bmalkuza ha damlaj alaha la'ylen drajmin leh
6	aton den shattuna lmeskina la ha atire meshtalen laykon whenon nagdin ljon lvez dina
7	la ha henon mgadfin al shma tava d'ezkri laykon
8	we'n namusa d'alaha bhade mshalmiton ayj dajziv dzerjam lkarivaj ayj nafshaj shapir avdin ton
9	en den b'ape nasbin ton jtiza avdin ton wmezkawnin ton men namusa ayj avray al namusa
10	ayna guer djuleh namusa natar wvajda shara ljuleh namusa ezjayyav
11	haw guer d'emar dla tgur hu emar dla tektul en den la ga'ar at ela katel at hwayt laj avar al namusa
12	hajana hwayton mmallin whajana hwayton sarin ayj nasha davnamusa dji'ruza zidin ton lmetdanu
13	dina guer hawe dla rajme al haw dla aved rajme meshtalen ton brajme al dina
14	mana henyana ajay en nash amar d'iyz li haymanuza wavade layt leh dalma meshkja haymanuzeh dzajewy
15	we'n aja aw jaza nehon artelain wjasirin saybarta dyawma
16	wni'mar lhon nash menjon selw bashlama shjanw wasvaw wla tetlon lhon snikuzeh dfagra mana henyana
17	hajana af haymanuza dla vade miza y baljudeh
18	amar guer nash laj iz laj haymanuza wli iz li vade jawany haymanuzaj dla vade we'na mjawe na laj haymanuzy men vaday
19	mhaymen at djad u alaha shapir aved at af shi'de mhaymnin wralin
20	save at den dzeda aw barnasha jalasha dhaymanuza dla vade miza y
21	avon avraham la wa men vade esdadak d'asek li'ysjak breh al madbja
22	jase at dhaymanuzeh sayaz lavadawy wmen vade haymanuzeh ezgamraz
23	washlem kzava d'emar dhaymen avraham b'alaha we'zjashbaz leh lsadiku wrajma d'alaha ezkri
24	jase at dmen vade mesdadak barnasha wla wa men haymanuza baljud
25	hajana af rajav saniza la wa men vade esdadkaz dkablaz lgashushe wvu'wrja jriza apkaz enon
26	aykana dfagra dla ruja miz u hajana af haymanuza dla vade miza y
