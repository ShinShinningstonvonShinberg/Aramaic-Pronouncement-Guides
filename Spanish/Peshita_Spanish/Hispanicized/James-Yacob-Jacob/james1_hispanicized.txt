1	yakuv avdeh d'alaha wadmaran yeshu mshija lzartasre sharvan dasrian bamme shlam
2	kul jadwa tehwe ljon ajay kad telon lnesyune mshajlfe wsaguiyye'
3	yadin ton guer dvukya dhaymanuza makne ljon msaybranuza
4	lah den lamsaybranuza nehwe lah vada mshamlya dzehon gmirin wamshalmanin wavmedem la hwayton jasirin
5	en nash den menjon jsir men jejmza nesha'l men alaha dyahev ljul pshita'iz wla mjased wmezyahba leh
6	nesha'l den bhaymanuza kad la mezpalag haw guer dmezpalag dame lgalle dyama dshagsha lhon ruja
7	wla nasbar haw barnasha dnasev medem men marya
8	ayna daflig breyaneh washguish bjulhen urjazeh
9	neshtavhar den aja makija brumrameh
10	watira bmukajeh metul d'ayj habava desba hajana avar
11	danaj guer shemsha bjumeh wmawbesh leh lesba whabaveh nafel wshufra djesweh aved hajana af atira jame bhupajawy
12	tuvawy lgavra damsaybar nesyune dma d'ezbjar nasev klila djayye haw damlaj alaha la'ylen drajmin leh
13	la ni'mar nash kad meznase dmen alaha meznase na alaha guer la mnasay bvishaza whu lnash la mnase
14	ela nash nash men regzeh u meznase wmezragrag wmezngued
15	whade regza batna wyalda jtiza jtiza den ma d'ezgamraz yalda mawta
16	la teton ajay jabive
17	kul mawhavta tavza wamshamlayta men lel najta men ava dnahire haw dlayt lwazeh shujlafa medem afla telaniza dshugnayya
18	huyyu sva wyaldan bmelza dkushta dnehwe rishiza dveryazeh
19	wa'ton ajay jabive kulnash menjon nehwe msarhav lmeshma wmawjar lammalalu wmawjar lmergas
20	rugseh guer dgavra sadikuza d'alaha la aved
21	metul hana arjekw menjon kulah tanfuza wsuga' dvishuza wavmakijuza kabelw melza dansiva bajyanan dhi meshkja dzaje enen nafshazjon
22	hwaw den avude dmelza wla shamue baljud wla taton nafshazjon
23	en nash guer nehwe shamuah dmelza wla avudah hana dame lhaw dajsa apawy bmajsiza
24	jsa guer nafsheh wavar wata d'aykana hwa
25	kul den djar bnamusa mshalmana dji'ruza wkawi beh la hwa shamua dshema dmette ela avuda davade whana tuvana nehwe bavadeh
26	we'n nash savar damshamesh la'laha wla ajed leshaneh ela mate leh lebeh dhana srika y teshmeshteh
27	teshmeshta guer djiza wkadishta kdam alaha ava hade y lmesar yazme wa'rmlaza b'uwlsanayhon walmetar nash nafsheh men alma dla tulsha
