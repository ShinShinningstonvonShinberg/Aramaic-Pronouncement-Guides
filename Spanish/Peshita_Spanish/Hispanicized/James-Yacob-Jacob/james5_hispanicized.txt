1	aw atire aylelw wavjaw al duwane d'azen laykon
2	uzrjon guer ezjabal wasri wma'naykon eze'jelw men sasa
3	wdahvjon wsi'mjon ashjez leh wshujthon hawya lsahduza laykon whi zida dze'jul besrjon nura kaneshton ljon lyawmaza jrayye
4	ha agra dfale dajsadw arazjon haw datlamton kae wagaza djasude le'dnawy dmarya sva'uz elaz
5	bsemton guer al ara we'zlaavton wzarsiton pagraykon ayj dalyawma dnejsza
6	jayyevton waktalton lsadika wla kam lukvaljon
7	aton den ajay agarw rujjon dama lme'zizeh dmarya ayj akara damsake lfi're yakire d'areh wmagar rujeh layhon dama dnasev metra bjirayya walkishayya
8	hajana af aton agarw rujjon wlebawazjon shararw kerbaz lah guer me'zizeh dmaran
9	la tetanjon jad al jad ajay dla tetdinon ha guer dina kdam tara ka'em
10	dmuza lanviyye savw ljon ajay lmagraz ruja d'uwlsanaykon hanon dmalelw bashmeh dmarya
11	ha guer yahbinan tuva la'ylen dsaybarw shmaton msaybranuzeh d'iyawv wjarza davad leh marya jsayton metul damrajman u marya wamrajfan
12	kdam kul medem den ajay la hwayton yamen la bashmayya wla b'ara afla bmawmaza jriza ela tehwe melazjon in in wla la dla tezjayvon tjez dina
13	we'n nash menjon nehwe b'uwlsana nehwe msale we'n jade nehwe msamar
14	we'n krih nekre lkashishe dita wansalon lawy wnemshjuneh meshja bashmeh dmaran
15	wasluza dhaymanuza majlma leh lhaw dajrih wamkim leh maran we'n jtahe vidin leh meshtavkin leh
16	hwayton den mawden sajlwazjon jad ljad wahwayton msalen jad al jad dzeza'son rav u guer jaylah dasluza ayda dsadika msale lah
17	af iliyya barnasha hwa jashusha ajwazan wsali dla nejuz metra al ara wla njez tlaz shnin weshta yarjin
18	wzuv sali washmayya yavw metra wa'ra yehbaz pi'reh
19	ajay en nash menjon nete men urja dkushta wnafnewy nash men tayuzeh
20	neda dhaw dmahpej ljatayya men tayuza d'uwrjeh maje nafsheh men mawta wate suga' dajtahawy
