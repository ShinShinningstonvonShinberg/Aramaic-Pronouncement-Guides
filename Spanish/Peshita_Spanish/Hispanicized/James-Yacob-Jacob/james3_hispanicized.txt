1	la saguiyye' malfane nehon bjon ajay ela hwayton yadin ddina yatira jayyavinan
2	saguiyya'za guer meshtarinan kulan kul davmelza la shara hana izawy gavra gmira dmeshkaj nshabed af kuleh pagreh
3	ha guer pgude bfuma drajsha ramenan ayj dneshtabdon lan wjuleh gushmhon mahpjinan
4	af elfe ashinaza kad dviran lhen ruje kashyaza men kaysa sura meznatfan la'zar dja'ar sevyaneh dhaw damdabar
5	hajana af leshana hadama w sura wmeshtale af nura surta ave saguiyye' mawkda
6	wleshana nura w walma dajtiza ayj ava w whu leshana kad izawy bhadamayn mjatem leh ljuleh pagran wmawked yubale dsharvazan drahtin ayj guigle wyaked af hu bnura
7	kulhon guer kyane djaywaza wadfarajza wrajsha dyama wadyavsha meshtabdin lajyana dnashuza
8	leshana den nash la eshkaj dnejbshiwy bishta hade dla mettajsa mle u sama dmawta
9	beh mvarjinan lmarya wa'va wveh latinan lavnaynasha dvadmuza d'alaha vidin
10	wmeneh men puma nafkan burkaza wlawtaza la wale ajay dhalen hajana nestaran
11	dalma meshkja dmen jad mabua nepkon mayya jlayya wmarire
12	aw dalma meshkja tita ajay dsayte tebed aw gfeta ti'ne hajana af la mayya malije meshkjin dnezavdon jlayya
13	manu menjon djakim warde njawe vadawy bhupaje shapire bjejmza makijta
14	en den jsama marira iz bjon aw jeryana blebaykon la tezjatron al kushta watdaglon
15	metul dhade jejmza men lel la nejtaz ela izeh aranata men jushave dnafsha wmen shi'de
16	ayka guer d'iyz jsama wjeryana taman af dlujya wjulmedem dvish
17	jejmza den dmen lel dajya y wmalya shlama wmakija wmeshtamanya wmalya rajme wfi're tave wadla palguza y wva'pe la nasba
18	pi're den dsadikuza bshayna mesdarin la'ylen davdin shlama
