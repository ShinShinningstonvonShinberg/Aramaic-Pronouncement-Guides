1	bjad bshaba den ezaz maryam magdlata bsafra ad jeshuj lvez kvura wajsaz lji'fa dashkila men kavra
2	wrehtaz ezaz lwaz shemon ki'fa walwaz haw talmida jrina drajem wa yeshu wa'mra lhon dshaklu lmaran men haw bez kvura wla yada na ayka samu
3	wanfak shemon whaw talmida jrina wa'zen waw lvez kvura
4	wrahtin waw trayhon ajjda haw den talmida rhet kadmeh lshemon we'za kadmayya lvez kvura
5	wa'dik jsa ketane kad simin meal den la al
6	eza den shemon bazreh wal lvez kvura wajsa ketane kad simin
7	wsudara haw dajsik wa brisheh la am ketane ela kad krij wsim lastar bajda duka
8	haden al af haw talmida d'eza kadmayya lvez kvura wajsa whaymen
9	la guer dakil yadin waw men kzave dazid wa lamkam men mize
10	we'salw henon talmide tuv ldukazhon
11	maryam den kama waz lwaz kavra wvajya wjad bajya adikaz bkavra
12	wajsaz tren mala'je bjeware dyazbin jad men esadawy wjad men reglawy ayka dsim wa pagreh dyeshu
13	wa'mrin lah atza mana bajya aty amra lhon dshaklu lmary wla yada na ayka samu
14	hade emraz we'zpanyaz lvestrah wajsaz lyeshu dka'em wla yada waz dyeshu u
15	amar lah yeshu atza mana bajya aty walman baya aty hi den sevraz dganana hu wa'mra leh mary en at shkalta emar li ayka samta isal eshkliwy
16	amar lah yeshu maryam we'zpanyaz wa'mra leh evra'iz rabuli dmeze'mar malfana
17	amar lah yeshu la tezkarvin li la guer dakil selkez lwaz avy sely den lwaz ajay we'mary lhon salek na lwaz avy wa'vujon wa'lahy wa'lahjon
18	haden ezaz maryam magdlata wsabraz lzalmide dajsaz lmaran wadhalen emar lah
19	kad hwa den ramsha dyawma haw djad bshaba wzare ajidin waw d'ayka d'iyzayhon waw talmide metul dejlza dihudayye eza yeshu kam baynazhon we'mar lhon shlama amjon
20	hade emar wjawi enon idawy wsetreh wajdiw talmide dajsaw lmaran
21	emar lhon den tuv yeshu shlama amjon aykana dshadrany avy af ena mshadar na ljon
22	wjad emar halen nfaj bhon we'mar lhon kabelw ruja dkudsha
23	en teshbkon jtahe lnash neshtavkon leh we'n te'jdon dnash ajidin
24	ta'wma den jad men tresarta haw dmeze'mar ta'ma la wa taman wa amhon kad eza yeshu
25	wa'mrin leh talmide jsayn lmaran hu den emar lhon ela jase na b'iydawy dukyaza dsese wrame na bhen sevazy wmawshet na idy bdafneh la mhaymen na
26	wvazar tmanya yawmin tuv lgaw waw talmide wza'wma amhon we'za yeshu kad ajidin tare kam bamsaza we'mar lhon shlama amjon
27	we'mar lza'wma ayta sevaj lharka wajsi iday wa'yta idaj wa'wshet bgaby wla tehwe la mhaymna ela mhaymna
28	wana ta'wma we'mar leh mary wa'lahy
29	amar leh yeshu hasha dajsaytany hayment tuvayhon la'ylen dla jsa'uny whaymenw
30	saguiyya'za den azwaza jranyaza vad yeshu kdam talmidawy aylen dla kzivan bajzava hana
31	af halen den dajzivan dazhaymnon dyeshu hu mshija breh d'alaha wma dhaymenton nehon ljon bashmeh jayye dalalam
