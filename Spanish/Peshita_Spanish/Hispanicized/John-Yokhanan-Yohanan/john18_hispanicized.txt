1	halen emar yeshu wanfak am talmidawy levra darguelta dkedron azar d'iyz waz ganza ayka dal hu wzalmidawy
2	yada wa den af ihuda mashlmana ldukza ha metul dsagui savna kanesh wa taman yeshu am talmidawy
3	hu hajil ihuda dvar espir wmen lwaz rabay kahne wafrishe dvar dajshe we'za lzaman am naftire wlampide wsayna
4	yeshu den dyada wa kul medem d'aze lawy nfak we'mar lhon lman baen ton
5	amrin leh lyeshu nasrayya amar lhon yeshu ena na ka'em wa den af ihuda mashlmana amhon
6	wjad emar lhon yeshu d'ena na esalw lvestarhon wanfalw al ara
7	tuv sha'el enon yeshu lman baen ton henon den emarw lyeshu nasrayya
8	amar lhon yeshu emrez ljon d'ena na we'n li baen ton shvukw lhalen asin
9	dzeshlam melza d'emar d'aylen dyavt li la awbdez menhon afla jad
10	shemon den ki'fa iz wa lawy safsira wshamtah wamja lavdeh drab kahne wshaklah edneh dyamina shmeh den davda malej
11	we'mar yeshu lji'fa sim safsira bjelzah kasa dyav li avy la eshtewy
12	haden espir wkiliyyarje wdajshe dihudayye ajdu lyeshu wa'sru
13	wa'ytyu lwaz janan lukdam metul dajmu wa dkayyafa haw d'iyzawy wa rab kahne dshata ha
14	izawy wa den kayyafa haw damlaj lihudayye dfakaj djad gavra nmuz jlaf ama
15	shemon den ki'fa wjad men talmide jrane azen waw bazreh dyeshu lhaw den talmida yada wa leh rab kahne wal am yeshu ldarta
16	shemon den ka'em wa lvar lwaz tara wanfak haw talmida jrina dyada wa leh rab kahne we'mar lnatraz tara wa'leh lshemon
17	emraz den laymza natraz tara lshemon lma af at men talmidawy at dhana gavra amar lah la
18	wkamin waw avde wdajshe wsamin waw nura dneshjnon metul dkarish wa ka'em wa den af shemon amhon wshajen
19	rab kahne den sha'leh lyeshu al talmidawy wal yulpaneh
20	we'mar leh yeshu ena in bagle mallez am ama wavjulsvan alfez bajnushta wavhaykla ayka djulhon ihudayye mezkanshin wmedem btushya la mallez
21	mana msha'el at li sha'el lhanon dashmaw mana mallez amhon ha henon yadin kul medem d'emrez
22	wjad halen emar jad men dajshe dka'em wa mja al pakeh lyeshu we'mar leh hajana yahev at pezgama lrab kahne
23	na yeshu we'mar leh en bisha'iz mallez ashed al bishta we'n den shapir lmana mjaytany
24	janan den shadar lyeshu kad asir lwaz kayyafa rab kahne
25	wshemon ki'fa ka'em wa wshajen wa'mrin leh lma af at jad men talmidawy at whu kfar we'mar la wiz
26	amar leh jad men avde drab kahne jyaneh dhaw dafsak wa shemon edneh la ena jsizaj ameh bganza
27	wzuv kfar shemon wvah bshaza kra tarnagla
28	aytyu den lyeshu men lwaz kayyafa lapretawrin wi'yzawy wa safra whenon la alw lapretawrin dla neztawshon ad ajlin pesja
29	nfak den pilataws lvar lwazhon we'mar lhon mana me'jal-karsa iz ljon al gavra hana
30	naw wa'mrin leh elu la aved bishaza wa afla laj mashlmin wayn leh
31	amar lhon pilataws duvru aton wdunu ayj namusjon amrin leh ihudayye la shalit lan lmektal lnash
32	dzeshlam melza d'emar yeshu kad mawda b'ayna mawta zid danmuz
33	al den pilataws lapretawrin wakra lyeshu we'mar leh at u malkhon dihudayye
34	amar leh yeshu men nafshaj emart hade aw jrane emarw laj lay
35	amar leh pilataws lma ena ihudayya na bnay amaj u wrabay kahne ashlmuj li mana vadt
36	amar leh yeshu malkuzy dily la hwaz men hana alma elu men alma waz hana malkuzy mezkatshin waw mshamshanay dla eshtlem lihudayye hasha den malkuzy dily la waz meka
37	amar leh pilataws maden malka at amar leh yeshu at emart dmalka na ena lhade ilid na walhade eziz lalma d'ashed al shrara kul man d'iyzawy men shrara shama kaly
38	amar leh pilataws manaw shrara wjad emar hade nfak leh tuv lwaz ihudayye we'mar lhon ena afla jda elza meshkaj na beh
39	yada den iz ljon djad eshre ljon bfesja saven ton hajil eshre ljon lhana malka dihudayye
40	wakaw kulhon wa'mrin la lhana ela lvar-aba izawy wa den hana bar-aba gayyasa
