1	iz wa den jad dajrih laasar men bez-anya kriza ajuh dmaryam wadmarta
2	maryam den hade izeh ha dmeshjaz bvesma reglawy dyeshu wshawyaz bsarah ajuh wa dhade laasar dajrih wa
3	wshadaren tartayhen ajwazeh lwaz yeshu wa'mran maran ha haw drajem at krih
4	yeshu den emar hana kurhana la hwa dmawta ela jlaf teshbujta d'alaha dneshtabaj breh d'alaha metulazeh
5	majev wa den hu yeshu lmarta walmaryam wallaasar
6	wjad shma dajrih katar bdukza d'iyzawy wa tren yawmin
7	wvazarken emar lzalmidawy taw ni'sal tuv lihud
8	amrin leh talmidawy raban hasha ihudayye baen waw lmergmaj wzuv asel at lzaman
9	amar lhon yeshu la tartaesre shain iz byawma we'n nash mhalej b'iymama la mettkel metul djase nuhreh dalma hana
10	en nash den blilya nhalej mettkel metul dnahira layt beh
11	halen emar yeshu wvazarken emar lhon laasar rajman shjev ela asel na d'airiwy
12	amrin leh talmidawy maran en dmej mezjlem
13	hu den yeshu emar al mawteh whenon svarw dal madmja hu dshenza emar
14	haden emar lhon yeshu pshika'iz laasar miz leh
15	wjade na dla hwiz taman metulazjon dazhaymnon ela halejw lzaman
16	amar ta'wma dmeze'mar ta'ma lzalmide javrawy ni'sal af jnan nmuz ameh
17	we'za yeshu lvez-anya we'shkaj d'arba leh yawmin d'iyzawy bvez kvura
18	izeh waz den bez-anya al guev urishlem kad parika menah ayj estdawaza jameshtasar
19	wsaguiyye' men ihudayye ati'in waw lwaz marta wmaryam dnemlon blebhen metul ajuhen
20	marta den kad shemaz dyeshu eza nefkaz lu'wreh maryam den bvayta yazba waz
21	we'mraz marta lyeshu mary elu tnan wayt la ma'ez wa ajy
22	ela af hasha yada na dajma dzesha'l la'laha yahev laj
23	amar lah yeshu ka'em ajujy
24	amra leh marta yada'na dka'em bnujama byawma jrayya
25	amar lah yeshu ena na nujama wjayye man damhaymen bi afen nmuz nije
26	wjul djay wamhaymen bi lalam la nmuz mhaymnaty hade
27	amra leh in mary ena mhaymna na d'at u mshija breh d'alaha d'aze lalma
28	wjad emraz halen esaz kraz lmaryam jazah kasya'iz wa'mra lah raban eza wkare lejy
29	wmaryam kad shemaz kamaz gal wa'zya waz lwazeh
30	hu den yeshu la dakil eza wa lakriza ela bah wa bha dukza d'erazeh marta
31	af hanon den ihudayye d'iyz waw amah bvayta damvay'in waw lah dajsaw lmaryam dagal kamaz nefkaz esalw lhon bazrah svarw guer dalkavra asa lmevka
32	hi den maryam kad ezaz ayka d'iyzawy wa yeshu wajsazeh neflaz al reglawy wa'mra leh elu tnan wayt mary la ma'ez wa ajy
33	yeshu den kad jsah dvajya wlihudayye hanon d'ezaw amah dvajen ezasas brujeh wa'si nafsheh
34	we'mar ayka samtuna wa'mrin leh maran ta jsi
35	wa'zyan way demawy dyeshu
36	wa'mrin waw ihudayye jsaw kma rajem wa leh
37	nashin den menhon emarw la meshkaj wa hana dfataj aynawy dhaw samya nebed d'af hana la nmuz
38	yeshu den kad mezasas baynawy wleh eza lvez kvura whu bez kvura izawy wa marza wji'fa sima waz al tareh
39	we'mar yeshu shkulw ki'fa hade amra leh marta jazeh dhaw miza mary men kadu sri leh arba leh guer yawmin
40	amar lah yeshu la emrez lejy d'en thaymnin tejsen shuvjeh d'alaha
41	washkalw ki'fa ha whu yeshu arim aynawy lel we'mar ava mawde na laj dashmatany
42	we'na yada na davjulsvan shama at li ela metul kensha hana dka'em amar na halen danhaymnon d'at shadartany
43	wjad emar halen ka bkala rama laasar ta lvar
44	wanfak haw miza kad asiran idawy wreglawy bfeskyaza wa'pawy asiran bsudara amar lhon yeshu shra'u washvukw asel
45	wsaguiyye' men ihudayye d'ezaw lwaz maryam kad jsaw medem davad yeshu haymenw beh
46	wnashin menhon esalw lwaz prishe we'marw lhon medem davad yeshu
47	we'zkanashw rabay kahne wafrishe wa'mrin waw mana nebed dhana gavra azwaza saguiyya'za aved
48	we'n shavkin nan leh hajana kulhon nasha mhaymnin beh wa'zen rumayye shaklin azran waman
49	jad den menhon dashmeh kayyafa rab kahne wa dha shata we'mar lhon aton la yadin ton medem
50	wla mezjashvin ton dfakaj lan djad gavra nmuz jlaf ama wla kuleh ama ni'vad
51	hade den men svuz nafsheh la emar ela metul drab kahne wa dha shata eznabi dazid wa yeshu danmuz jlaf ama
52	wla baljud jlaf ama ela d'af bnayya d'alaha damvadrin njanesh lajda
53	wmen haw yawma ezjashavw waw dnektluna
54	hu den yeshu la mhalej wa galya'iz bez ihudayye ela esal leh men taman la'zra dkariv ljurba ljarja dmezkre afrem wzaman mezhapaj wa am talmidawy
55	kariv wa den pesja dihudayye waslekw saguiyye' men kurya lu'wrishlem kdam adida dandakon nafshhon
56	wvaen waw leh lyeshu wa'mrin waw jad ljad bhaykla mana savrin ton dla aze ladida
57	rabay kahne den wafrishe pkadw waw d'en nash neda aykaw nvadek lhon ayj dne'jduna
