1	iz wa den taman jad gavra men prishe nikadimaws shmeh wa arjuna dihudayye
2	hana eza lwaz yeshu blilya we'mar leh rabi yadinan dmen alaha eshtadart malfana la guer nash meshkaj halen azwaza lmebad d'at aved at ela man d'alaha ameh
3	na yeshu we'mar leh amin amin amar na laj d'en nash la meziled men drish la meshkaj dnejse malkuzeh d'alaha
4	amar leh nikadimaws aykana meshkaj dneziled gavra sava dalma meshkaj tuv ljarsa d'emeh dzarten savnin lmeal wneziled
5	na yeshu we'mar leh amin amin amar na laj d'en nash la meziled men mayya wruja la meshkaj dneul lmalkuza d'alaha
6	medem dilid men besra besra hu wmedem dilid men ruja ruja hu
7	la tetdamar d'emrez laj dwale ljon lmeziladu men drish
8	ruja azar dsavya nashba wkalah shama at ela la yada at aymeka azya wla'yka asa hajana izawy kulnash dilid men ruja
9	na nikadimaws we'mar leh aykana meshkjan halen lmehwa
10	na yeshu we'mar leh at u malfaneh d'iysrayyel whalen la yada at
11	amin amin amar na laj dmedem dyadin nan mmallin nan wmedem dajsayn mashdin enajnan wsahduzan la mkablin ton
12	en dva'ra emrez ljon wla mhaymnin ton aykana en imar ljon dvashmayya thaymnunany
13	wla nash slek lashmayya ela haw danjez men shmayya breh dnasha haw d'iyzawy bashmayya
14	wa'ykana d'arim mushe jewya bmadbra hajana zid lmettramu breh dnasha
15	djul nash damhaymen beh la ni'vad ela nehon leh jayye dalalam
16	hajana guer ajev alaha lalma aykana dlavreh ijidayya netel djul man damhaymen beh la ni'vad ela nehon leh jayye dalalam
17	la guer shadar alaha lavreh lalma danduniwy lalma ela dnije lalma b'iydeh
18	man damhaymen beh la mettdin wman dla mhaymen men kadu din u dla haymen bashmeh dijidayya breh d'alaha
19	hanaw den dina dnuhra eza lalma wa'jevw bnaynasha ljeshuja yatir men dalnuhra izayhon waw guer vadayhon bishe
20	kul guer dsanyaza aved sane lnuhra wla aze lnuhra dla nezkasson vadawy
21	haw den daved shrara aze lwaz nuhra dnezidon vadawy dva'laha vidin
22	bazar halen eza yeshu wzalmidawy la'ra dihud wzaman mezhapaj wa amhon wmamed
23	af yujanan den mamed wa bin-yawn dal guev shalim metul dmayya iz waw taman saguiyye' wa'zen waw wamdin
24	la guer dakil nfal wa yujanan bez asire
25	hwaz waz den baza ljad men talmidawy dyujanan am ihudayya jad al tadkiza
26	we'zaw lwaz yujanan we'marw leh raban haw damaj wa bevra dyurdnan d'at shedt lawy ha af hu mamed wsaguiyye' azen lwazeh
27	na yujanan we'mar lhon la meshkaj barnasha lmesav men svuz nafsheh medem ela en ezihev leh men shmayya
28	aton sahdin ton li d'emrez d'ena la hwiz mshija ela shlija na dakdamawy
29	man d'iyz leh kalza jazna hu rajmeh den djazna haw dka'em wsa'ez leh jaduza rabza jade metul kaleh djazna hade hajil jaduza dily ha mmalya
30	lhaw u wale lmerba wli lmevsar
31	haw guer dmen lel eza lel men kul u whaw dmen ara men ara hu wmen ara mmalel haw dmen shmayya eza lel men kul u
32	wmedem dajsa washma mashed wsahduzeh la nash mkabel
33	haw den dkabel sahduzeh jzam d'alaha sharira hu
34	ayna guer d'alaha shadreh mele w d'alaha mmalel la wa guer bjayla yav alaha ruja
35	ava majev lavra wjul medem yav b'iydawy
36	man damhaymen bavra iz leh jayye dalalam wman dla mettpis lavra la nejse jayye ela rugseh d'alaha nkawe lawy
