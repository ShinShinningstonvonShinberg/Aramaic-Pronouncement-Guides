1	haden pilataws nagdeh lyeshu
2	we'stratiyyute gdalw klila men kube wsamw leh brisheh wjasyu najte d'argwana
3	wa'mrin waw shlam laj malka dihudayye wmajen waw leh al pakawy
4	wanfak pilataws tuv lvar we'mar lhon ha mapek na leh ljon lvar dzedon dla meshkaj na bazreh afla jda elza
5	wanfak yeshu lvar kad iz lawy klila djube wnajte d'argwana we'mar lhon pilataws ha gavra
6	kad den jsa'u rabay kahne wdajshe kaw wa'mrin sluva sluva amar lhon pilataws dvarw aton wsukfu ena guer la meshkaj na beh elza
7	amrin leh ihudayye lan namusa iz lan wa'yj davnamusan jayyav u mawta davad nafsheh breh d'alaha
8	kad shma den pilataws hade melza yatira'iz djel
9	wal tuv lapretawrin we'mar lyeshu aymeka at yeshu den pezgama la yav leh
10	amar leh pilataws amy la mmalel at la yada at dshalit na d'eshrej wshalit na d'eskfaj
11	amar leh yeshu layt wa laj lay shultana af la jad elu la ihiv wa laj men lel metul hana haw man d'ashlmany laj raba y jtizeh men dilaj
12	wmetul hade save wa pilataws dneshrewy ihudayye den kaen waw d'en lhana share at la hwayt rajmeh dkesar kul man guer dnafsheh malka aved sakuvla hu dkesar
13	kad shma den pilataws hade melza apkeh lyeshu lvar wizev al bim bdukza dmezkarya rsifta dji'fe evra'iz den meza'mra gfifta
14	waruvta waz dfesja wi'yz way ayj shae shez we'mar lihudayye ha malkjon
15	henon den kaen waw shkula shkula sluva sluva amar lhon pilataws lmalkjon eskuf amrin rabay kahne layt lan malka ela en kesar
16	haden ashlmeh lhon dneskfuna wdavru lyeshu wa'pku
17	kad shkil skifeh ldukza dmezkarya karkafza evra'iz den meza'mra gagulta
18	azar dsakpu wameh tren jranin jad meka wjad meka walyeshu bamsaza
19	wajzav af luja pilataws wsam al skifeh kziv wa den hajana hana yeshu nasrayya malka dihudayye
20	walhana dapa saguiyye' men ihudayye kra'u metul dkariva waz lamdita dukza d'esdkef bah yeshu wajziva wa evra'iz wyawna'iz waruma'iz
21	we'marw rabay kahne lpilataws la tejtuv dmalka hu dihudayye ela dhu emar dmalka ena dihudayye
22	amar pilataws medem djezbez kezbez
23	estratiyyute den kad sakpu lyeshu shkalw najtawy wavadw la'rba mnawan mnaza ljad men estratiyyute kutineh den izeh waz dla jita men lel skirta kulah
24	we'marw jad ljad la nesdkih ela nepes leh mepas dmanu tehwe washlem kzava d'emar dfalegw najtay baynazhon wal lvushy armiw pesa halen vadw estratiyyute
25	kaman way den lwaz skifeh dyeshu emeh wjazah d'emeh wmaryam ha dakleyyawpa wmaryam magdlata
26	yeshu den jsa le'meh walzalmida haw drajem wa dka'em we'mar le'meh atza ha brejy
27	we'mar lzalmida haw ha emaj wmen ha shaza davrah talmida haw lwazeh
28	bazar halen ida yeshu djulmedem eshtalam wadnezmale kzava emar she na
29	wma'na sim wa damle jala henon den mlaw esfuga men jala wsamw al supa wkarevw lwaz pumeh
30	kad den shkal haw jala yeshu emar ha mshalam wa'rken risheh wa'shlem rujeh
31	ihudayye den metul daruvta waz amrin la nvuzon pagre halen al skifyahon metul dshabza nagha yawma wa guer raba yawma dshabza ha wavaw men pilataws danzabron shakayhon dhanon skife wnajzon enon
32	we'zaw estratiyyute wzabarw shakawy dkadmayya wadhaw jrina d'esdkef ameh
33	wjad ezaw lwaz yeshu jsaw dmiz leh men kadu wla tabarw shakawy
34	ela jad men estratiyyute mja bdafneh blukata wmejda nfak dma wmayya
35	wman dajsa ashed wsharira y sahduzeh whu yada dashrara emar d'af aton thaymnon
36	halen guer hway dnezmale kzava d'emar dgarma la nettvar beh
37	wzuv kzava jrina d'emar danjuron bman dadkarw
38	bazar halen yawsef haw dmen ramza ba men pilataws metul dzalmida wa dyeshu wamtashe wa men dejlza dihudayye dneshkul pagreh dyeshu wa'pes pilataws we'za washkal pagreh dyeshu
39	we'za af nikadimaws haw d'eza wa men kdim lwaz yeshu blilya wa'yti ameh juntza dmura wadalway ayj ma' litrin
40	wshaklu lfagreh dyeshu wjarju bjetane wavvesme aykana d'iyz yada lihudayye dnekbron
41	iz waz den bha dukza d'esdkef bah yeshu ganza wvah bganza bez kvura jaza dnash dakil la ettsim wa beh
42	wsamu taman lyeshu metul dshabza a'la waz wmetul dkariv wa kavra
