1	breshiz izawy wa melza whu melza izawy wa lwaz alaha wa'laha izawy wa hu melza
2	hana izawy wa breshiz lwaz alaha
3	kul b'iydeh hwa wveladawy afla jda hwaz medem dahwa
4	beh jayye hwa wjayye izayhon nuhra davnaynasha
5	whu nuhra bjeshuja manhar wjeshuja la adrjeh
6	hwa barnasha d'eshtadar men alaha shmeh yujanan
7	hana eza lsahduza dneshad al nuhra djulnash nhaymen b'iydeh
8	la hu hwa nuhra ela dneshad al nuhra
9	izawy wa guer nuhra dashrara dmanhar ljulnash d'aze lalma
10	balma wa walma b'iydeh hwa walma la yadeh
11	ldileh eza wdileh la kablu
12	aylen den dkablu yav lhon shultana davnayya d'alaha nehon la'ylen damhaymnin bashmeh
13	aylen dlaw men dma wla men sevyana dvesra wla men sevyana dgavra ela men alaha eziledw
14	wmelza besra hwa wa'guen ban wajsayn shuvjeh shuvja ayj dijidayya dmen ava damle taybuza wkushta
15	yujanan shed lawy waka we'mar hanaw haw d'emrez dvazary aze wahwa leh kdamay metul dkadma u meny
16	wmen malyuzeh jnan kulan nsavn wtaybuza jlaf taybuza
17	metul dnamusa byad mushe ezihev shrara den wtaybuza byad yeshu mshija hwa
18	alaha la jsa nash memzum ijidayya alaha haw d'iyzawy buba d'avu hu eshtai
19	whade hi sahduzeh dyujanan kad shadarw lwazeh ihudayye men urishlem kahne wlewayye dansha'luna at man at
20	wa'wdi wla kfar wa'wdi dlaw ena na mshija
21	wsha'lu tuv mana hajil iliyya at we'mar la izay nviyya at we'mar la
22	we'marw leh wmanu at dnetel pezgama la'ylen dshadron mana amar at al nafshaj
23	emar ena kala dkare bmadbra d'ashwaw urjeh dmarya aykana d'emar eshaya nviyya
24	hanon den d'eshtadarw men prishe waw
25	wsha'lu we'marw leh mana hajil mamed at en at la izayk mshija wla iliyya wla nviyya
26	na yujanan we'mar lhon ena mamed na bmayya baynazjon den ka'em haw d'aton la yadin ton leh
27	hanaw haw dvazary aze wahwa leh kdamay haw d'ena la shawe na d'eshre arke damsanawy
28	halen bvez-anya hway bevra dyurdnan ayka dmamed wa yujanan
29	walyawma dvazreh jsa yujanan lyeshu d'aze lwazeh we'mar ha emreh d'alaha haw dshakel jtizeh dalma
30	hanaw d'ena emrez lawy dvazary aze gavra wahwa leh kdamay metul dkadma u meny
31	we'na la yada wiz leh ela dnezida li'ysrayyel metul hana eziz ena davmayya amed
32	wa'shed yujanan we'mar dajsiz lruja dnajta men shmayya ayj yawna wkawyaz lawy
33	we'na la yada wiz leh ela man dshadrany d'amed bmayya hu emar li d'ayna djase at dnajta ruja wamkawya lawy hanaw mamed bruja dkudsha
34	we'na jsiz wa'shdez dhanaw breh d'alaha
35	walyawma jrina ka'em wa yujanan wazren men talmidawy
36	wjar byeshu kad mhalej we'mar ha emreh d'alaha
37	washmaw trayhon talmidawy kad emar we'salw lhon bazreh dyeshu
38	we'zpni yeshu wajsa enon d'azen bazreh we'mar lhon mana baen ton amrin leh raban ayka hawe at
39	amar lhon taw wzejson henon den ezaw wajsaw ayka dhawe walwazeh hwaw yawma haw wi'yz way ayj shae sar
40	jad den men hanon dashmaw men yujanan we'salw bazreh dyeshu izawy wa andre'was aju dshemon
41	hana jsa lukdam lshemon aju we'mar leh eshkajna lamshija
42	wa'ytyeh lwaz yeshu wjar beh yeshu we'mar at u shemon breh-dyawna at tezkre ki'fa
43	walyawma jrina sva yeshu lmepak laglila we'shkaj lfilipaws we'mar leh ta bazary
44	hu den filipaws izawy wa men bez-sayyada men mditeh d'andre'was wadshemon
45	wfilipaws eshkaj lnazanai'yl we'mar leh haw dajzav lawy mushe bnamusa wvanviyye eshkajna dyeshu u bar yawsef men nasraz
46	amar leh nazanai'yl men nasraz meshkaj medem dtav nehwe amar leh filipaws ta wzejse
47	wajsa yeshu lnazanai'yl kad aze lwazeh we'mar lawy ha sharira'iz bar israyyel dnejla layt beh
48	amar leh nazanai'yl men aymeka yada at li amar leh yeshu adla nekrej filipaws kad tjez tita at jsizaj
49	na nazanai'yl we'mar leh rabi at u breh d'alaha at u malkeh d'iysrayyel
50	amar leh yeshu al d'emrez laj dajsizaj tjez tita mhaymen at drawrvan men halen tejse
51	amar leh amin amin amar na ljon dmen hasha tejson shmayya dafzijin wmala'jawy d'alaha kad salkin wnajtin lwaz breh dnasha
