1	ena na gfeta dashrara wa'vy u palaja
2	kul shvishta dvi pi're la yahba shakel lah wa'yda dyahba pi're mdake lah dfi're saguiyye' tayte
3	aton men kadu djen ton metul melza dmallez amjon
4	kawaw bi we'na bjon aykana dashvishta la meshkja dzetel pi're men nafshah ela mkawya bagfeta hajana af la aton ela tkaon bi
5	ena na gfeta wa'ton shvishte man damkawe bi we'na beh hana mayte pi're saguiyye' metul dadla ena la meshkjin ton lmebad medem
6	ela den nash mkawe bi meshtde lvar ayj shvishta dyavsha wlaktin ramen lah bnura dzi'kad
7	en den tkaon bi wmelay nkawyan bjon kul ma dzesbon lmesha'l nehwe ljon
8	bhade meshtabaj ava dfi're saguiyye' tayton wzehon talmiday
9	aykana d'ajvany avy af ena ajevtjon kawaw brejmazy dily
10	en pukdanay tetron tkaon bjuba dily aykana d'ena netrez pukdanawy d'avy wamkawe na bjubeh
11	halen mallez amjon djadwazy tehwe bjon wzeshtamle jadwazjon
12	hanaw pukdany dzajvon jad ljad aykana d'ena ajevtjon
13	juba drab men hana layt dnash nafsheh nsim jlaf rajmawy
14	aton rajmay aton en tebdon kul damfaked na ljon
15	la mekil kare na ljon avde metul davda la yada mana aved mareh rajmay den krizjon metul djul dshemez men avy awdatjon
16	la wa aton gvaytunany ela ena hu gvizjon wsamtjon d'af aton ti'sun tayton pi're wfi'raykon nkaon djul dzeshe'lon la'vy bshemy netel ljon
17	halen mfaked na ljon dzajvon jad ljad
18	we'n alma sane ljon daw dakdamaykon li sna
19	we'lu men alma wayton alma ldileh rajem wa ela la hwayton men alma ena guer gvizjon men alma metul hana sane ljon alma
20	ehadw melza d'ena emrez ljon dlayt avda drab men mareh en li rdafw af ljon nerdfon we'n melazy ntarw af diljon netron
21	ela halen kulhen nebdon bjon metul shemy dily dla yadin lman dshadrany
22	elu ena la eziz mallez amhon layt waz lhon jtiza hasha den layt lhon elza al apay jtizhon
23	man dli sane wa'f la'vy sane
24	we'lu vade la evdez linayhon aylen dnash jrin la vad layt wa lhon jtiza hasha den wajsaw wasnaw af li wa'f la'vy
25	dzezmale melza dajziva bnamushon dasna'uny magan
26	ma den d'eza paraklita haw d'ena mshadar na ljon men lwaz avy ruja dashrara haw dmen lwaz avy nafek hu nashed lay
27	af aton sahdin ton dmen shurayya amy aton
