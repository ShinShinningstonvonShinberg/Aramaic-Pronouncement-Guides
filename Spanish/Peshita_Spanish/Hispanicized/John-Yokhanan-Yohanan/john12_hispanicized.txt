1	yeshu den kdam shta yawmin dfesja eza lvez-anya ayka d'iyzawy wa laasar haw d'akim men bez mize hu yeshu
2	wavadw leh taman jshamiza wmarta mshamsha waz wlaasar jad men smije wa dameh
3	maryam den sheklaz shatifta dvesma dnardin rishayya sagui dmayya wmeshjaz reglawy dyeshu wshawyaz bsarah reglawy we'zmli bayta men rijeh dvesma
4	we'mar ihuda sjaryuta jad men talmidawy haw dazid wa dnashlmiwy
5	lmana la esdaban meshja hana bazlaz ma' dinarin we'zihev lmeskine
6	hade den emar la wa metul dal meskine btil wa leh ela metul dganava wa wagluskma lwazeh wa wmedem dnafel wa beh hu tin wa
7	emar den yeshu shvukeh lyawma dakvury ntarzeh
8	bjulsvan guer meskine iz ljon amjon li den la bjulsvan iz ljon
9	washmaw kenshe saguiyye' men ihudayye dzaman u yeshu we'zaw la metul yeshu baljud ela af dnejson llaasar haw d'akim men bez mize
10	we'zraiw waw rabay kahne d'af llaasar nektluna
11	metul dsaguiyye' men ihudayye metulazeh asin waw wamhaymnin byeshu
12	walyawma jrina kensha saguiyya' ayna d'aze wa ladida kad shmaw dyeshu aze lu'wrishlem
13	shkalw sawke ddekle wanfakw lu'wreh wkaen waw wa'mrin ushana brij u d'aze bashmeh dmarya malka d'iysrayyel
14	eshkaj den yeshu jmara wizev lawy aykana dajziv
15	la tedjlin baz sehyon ha malkejy aze lejy warjiv al ila bar azana
16	halen den la idaw talmidawy bhaw savna ela kad eshtabaj yeshu etdjarw talmidawy dhalen kzivan way lawy whalen vadw leh
17	wsahed wa kensha haw dameh wa dakra llaasar men kavra wa'kimeh men bez mize
18	wmetul hade nfakw lkuvleh kenshe saguiyye' dashmaw d'aza hade vad
19	prishe den amrin waw jad ljad jasen ton dla mawtrin ton medem dha alma kuleh esal leh bazreh
20	iz waw den af men amme nasha bhon daslekw lmesgad badida
21	halen ezaw krevw lwaz filipaws haw dmen bez-sayyada daglila wsha'lu wa'mrin leh mary saven nan nejse lyeshu
22	we'za hu filipaws we'mar la'ndre'was wa'ndre'was wfilipaws emarw lyeshu
23	yeshu den na we'mar lhon ezaz shaza dneshtabaj breh dnasha
24	amin amin amar na ljon dafredta djetza ela nafla wmata b'ara baljudeh pasha en den mata pi're saguiyye' maytya
25	man drajem nafsheh nawbdih wman dsane nafsheh balma hana netrih ljayye dalalam
26	en li nash mshamesh ni'ze bazary wa'yka d'ena izay taman nehwe af mshamshany man dli mshamesh nyakriwy ava
27	hasha nafshy ha shguisha wmana imar avy pasany men hade shaza ela metul hana eziz lhade shaza
28	ava shabaj shmaj wkala eshtma men shmayya shabjez wzuv mshabaj na
29	wjensha dka'em wa shmaw wa'mrin rama hwa jrane den amrin mala'ja malel ameh
30	na yeshu we'mar lhon la wa metulazy hwa kala hana ela metulazjon
31	hasha dineh u dalma hana hasha arjuna dalma hana meshtde lvar
32	we'na ma d'ettrimez men ara egued kulnash lwazy
33	hade den emar danjawe b'ayna mawta ma'ez
34	amrin leh kenshe jnan shman men namusa damshija lalam mkawe aykana amar at dazid u dnettrim breh dnasha manu hana breh dnasha
35	amar lhon yeshu kalil jrin savna nuhra amjon u halejw ad iz ljon nuhra dla jeshuja nadrejjon wman damhalej bjeshuja la yada la'yka asel
36	ad iz ljon nuhra haymenw bnuhra davnawy dnuhra tehon halen malel yeshu we'sal ettashi menhon
37	wjad halen kulhen azwaza vad kdamayhon la haymenw beh
38	dzezmale melza d'eshaya nviyya d'emar mary manu haymen lsheman wadraeh dmarya lman ezgli
39	metul hana la meshkjin waw danhaymnon metul dzuv emar eshaya
40	dawarw aynayhon wa'jshejw lebhon dla nejson baynayhon wnestaklon blebhon wnezpnon wa'se enon
41	halen emar eshaya kad jsa shuvjeh wmalel lawy
42	af men rishe den saguiyye' haymenw beh ela metul prishe la mawden waw dla nehon lvar men knushta
43	rjemw guer shuvja davnaynasha yatir men shuvjeh d'alaha
44	yeshu den ka we'mar man damhaymen bi la wa bi mhaymen ela bman dshadrany
45	wman dli jase jsa lman dshadrany
46	ena nuhra eziz lalma djul man damhaymen bi la nkawe bjeshuja
47	wman dshama melay wla natar lhen ena la d'aen na leh la guer eziz d'edon lalma ela d'aje lalma
48	man dtalem li wla mkabel melay iz man dda'en leh melza dmallez hi dana leh byawma jrayya
49	d'ena men nafshy la mallez ela ava dshadrany hu yav li pukdana mana imar wmana emalel
50	wyada na dfukdaneh jayye enon dalalam aylen hajil dammalel na aykana d'emar li avy hajana mmalel na
