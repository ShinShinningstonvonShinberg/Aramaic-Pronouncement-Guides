1	bazar halen jawi tuv nafsheh yeshu lzalmidawy al yama dtiveriyyaws jawi den hajana
2	izayhon waw ajjda shemon ki'fa wza'wma dmeze'mar ta'ma wnazanay'il haw dmen katne daglila wavnay savday wazren jranin men talmide
3	amar lhon shemon ki'fa asel na esud nune amrin leh af jnan azenan amaj wanfakw waslekw lasfita wavhaw lilya medem la sadw
4	kad den hwa safra kam yeshu al yad yama wla ydaw talmide dyeshu u
5	we'mar lhon yeshu tlayye lma iz ljon medem lmelas amrin leh la
6	amar lhon armaw msidtjon men gaba dyamina dasfita wmeshkjin ton wa'rmiw wla eshkajw lmegdah lamsidta men suga' dnune d'ejdaz
7	we'mar talmida haw drajem wa leh yeshu lji'fa hana maran u shemon den kad shma dmaran u nsav kutineh mja bjasawy metul dartelayya wa washda nafsheh byama dni'ze lwaz yeshu
8	jrane den talmide basfita ezaw la guer rajikin waw sagui men ara ela ayj ma'zen amin wnagdin waw lah lamsidta ha dnune
9	kad den slekw la'ra jsaw gumre kad siman wnuna kad sim layhen wlajma
10	we'mar lhon yeshu aytaw men hanon nune dsadton hasha
11	waslek shemon ki'fa wnagdah lamsidta la'ra kad malya nune rawrve ma' wjamshin wazlaza wavhana kuleh yukra la estaryaz msidta ha
12	we'mar lhon yeshu taw eshtaraw nash den men talmide la mamraj wa dansha'liwy dmanu dyadin waw dmaran u
13	krev den yeshu washkal lajma wnune wyav lhon
14	hade dazlaz savnin ezjsi yeshu lzalmidawy kad kam men bez mize
15	kad den eshtariw emar yeshu lshemon ki'fa shemon bar-yawna rajem at li yatir men halen amar leh in mary at yada at drajem na laj amar leh ri li emray
16	amar leh tuv dzarten savnin shemon bar-yawna rajem at li amar leh in mary at yada at drajem na laj amar leh yeshu ri li erbay
17	amar leh dazlaz savnin shemon bar-yawna rajem at li wjeryaz leh lji'fa d'emar leh dazlaz savnin drajem at li we'mar leh mary kul medem at jajem at at yada at drajem na laj amar leh yeshu ri li nkawazy
18	amin amin amar na laj djad tle wayt at lnafshaj asar wayt jasayk wamhalej wayt la'yka dsave at ma den dase'vt tefshut idayk wajrin ne'sur laj jasayk wnawblaj la'yka dla save at
19	hade den emar danjawe b'ayna mawta zid danshabaj la'laha wjad emar halen emar leh ta bazary
20	we'zpni shemon ki'fa wajsa lzalmida haw drajem wa yeshu d'aze bazreh haw danfal wa bajshamiza al jadyeh dyeshu we'mar mary manu mashlem laj
21	lhana kad jsa ki'fa emar lyeshu mary whana mana
22	amar leh yeshu en save na dankawe hana dama d'aze na laj ma laj at ta bazary
23	wnefkaz hade melza bez aje dhaw talmida la ma'ez yeshu den la wa dla ma'ez emar ela d'en save na dankawe hana dama d'aze na laj ma laj
24	hanaw talmida d'ashed al halen kulhen wa'f kzav enen wyadin nan dsharira y sahduzeh
25	iz den af jranyaza saguiyya'za davad yeshu aylen d'elu jda jda mezkazban way af la hu alma ayj dsavar na safek wa lajzave dmezkazbin waw
