1	ida den yeshu dashmaw prishe dzalmide saguiyye' vad wmamed yatir men yujanan
2	kad la wa hu yeshu mamed wa ela talmidawy
3	wshavkah lihud we'sal leh tuv laglila
4	mabda waz leh den dni'ze nebar al bez shamrayye
5	we'za lamdita dshamrayye dmezkarya shajar al guev kriza dyav wa yakuv lyawsef breh
6	wi'yz wa taman mina dmayya dyakuv hu den yeshu l'e wa men amla d'uwrja wizev wa leh al mina wi'yz way shez shain
7	we'zaz atza men shamrin dzemle mayya we'mar lah yeshu hav li mayya eshte
8	talmidawy guer alw waw lamdita dnesbnon lhon saybarta
9	amra leh ha atza shamrata aykana at ihudayya at wmeny sha'el at lmeshta d'iyzay atza shamrata la guer mezjashjin ihudayye am shamrayye
10	na yeshu we'mar lah elu yada wayty mawhavta d'alaha wmanu hana d'emar lejy hav li eshte aty sha'la wayty leh wyahev wa lejy mayya jayye
11	amra leh atza ha mary la dawla iz laj wvira amika aymeka laj mayya jayye
12	lma at rav at men avon yakuv haw dhu yav lan bira hade whu menah eshti wavnawy waneh
13	na yeshu we'mar lah kul dneshte men halen mayya tuv neshe
14	kul den dneshte men mayya d'ena etel leh la neshe lalam ela mayya hanon dyahev na leh nehon beh mina dmayya dnavin ljayye dalalam
15	amra leh atza ha mary hav li men halen mayya dla tuv eshe wla hwiz azya dalya men harka
16	amar lah yeshu sely kra lvalejy wza lharka
17	amra leh layt li bala amar lah yeshu shapir emarty dlayt li bala
18	jamsha guer balin hwaw lejy whana d'iyz lejy hasha la hwa balejy hade sharirta emarty
19	amra leh ha atza mary jasya na danviyya at
20	avahayn bhana tura sguedw wa'ton amrin ton dvu'wrishlem u azar dwale lmesgad
21	amar lah yeshu atza haymeniny d'azya shaza dla bhana tura wa'f la b'uwrishlem tesgdon la'va
22	aton sagdin ton lmedem dla yadin ton jnan den sagdin nan lma dyadin nan djayye men ihudayye enon
23	ela azya shaza whasha izeh emazy dsagude sharire nesgdon la'va bruja wvashrara af ava guer d'ayj halen u sagude bae
24	ruja hu guer alaha wa'ylen dsagdin leh bruja wvashrara wale dnesgdon
25	amra leh atza ha yada na damshija aze wma d'eza hu malef lan kulmedem
26	amar lah yeshu ena na dammalel na amejy
27	wjad mmalel ezaw talmidawy wmetdamrin waw dam atza mmalel la den nash emar mana bae at aw mana mmalel at amah
28	wshevkaz kulzah atza we'saz lamdita wa'mra lnasha
29	taw jsaw gavra d'emar li kul medem devdez lma huyyu mshija
30	wanfakw nasha men mdita wa'zen waw lwazeh
31	wvaynaz halen baen waw meneh talmidawy wa'mrin leh raban las
32	hu den emar lhon iz li me'julta d'ejul ayda d'aton la yadin ton
33	amrin talmide baynayhon lma nash ayti leh medem lme'jal
34	amar lhon yeshu me'julty dily izeh d'ebed sevyaneh dman dshadrany we'shalmiwy lavadeh
35	la aton amrin dvazar arba yarjin aze jsada ha amar na ljon d'arimw aynaykon wajsaw araza dajwar wmati lajsada men kadu
36	wa'yna djased agra nasev wjanesh pi're ljayye dalalam wsarua wjasuda ajjda nejdon
37	bhade guer izeh melza dashrara dajrin u sara wajrin jased
38	ena shadartjon lmejsad medem dla wa aton l'iton beh jrane guer l'iw wa'ton alton al amlhon dhanon
39	men ha mdita den saguiyye' haymenw beh shamrayye metul melzah d'atza ha dmashda waz d'emar li kulmedem devdez
40	wjad ezaw lwazeh hanon shamrayye baw meneh dnehwe lwazhon wahwa lwazhon tren yawmin
41	wsaguiyye' haymenw beh metul melzeh
42	wa'mrin waw la'tza ha dmekil la wa metul melzejy mhaymnin nan beh jnan guer shman widan dhanaw sharira'iz mshija majyaneh dalma
43	wvazar tren yawmin nfak yeshu men taman we'sal laglila
44	hu guer yeshu shed danviyya bamditeh la mezyakar
45	kad den eza laglila kablu glilayye dajsaw azwaza kul davad b'uwrishlem badida ezaw waw guer wa'f henon ladida
46	eza den tuv yeshu lkatne daglila ayka davad mayya jamra wi'yz wa bajfarnajum ved malka jad davreh krih wa
47	hana shma d'eza yeshu men ihud laglila we'sal lwazeh wvae wa meneh dnejuz wna'se lavreh kariv wa guer lammaz
48	amar leh yeshu en azwaza wzedmraza la tejson la thaymnon
49	amar leh haw ved malka mary juz adla ma'ez leh talya
50	amar leh yeshu sel braj jay u whaymen haw gavra bmelza d'emar leh yeshu we'sal
51	kad den njez wa aru avdawy wsabru wa'mrin leh braj jya
52	wsha'el enon b'ayna edana ezjlem amrin leh ezmaly bashva shain shvakzeh eshaza
53	wida avu davha shaza dvah emar leh yeshu davraj jya whaymen hu wvayteh kuleh
54	hade tuv aza dzarten vad yeshu kad eza men ihud laglila
