1	yeshu den esal ltura dsayte
2	bsafra den tuv eza lhaykla wjuleh ama aze wa lwazeh wjad yazev malef wa lhon
3	aytiw den safre wafrishe la'tza d'ettajdaz bgawra wjad akimuh bamsaza
4	amrin leh malfana hade atza ettajdaz galya'iz beh bsurana dgawra
5	wavnamusa den dmushe paked dalda'yj halen nergum at hajil mana amar at
6	hade emarw kad mnasen leh aykana dzehwe lhon dankatrguna yeshu den kad lzajt ezghen majtev wa al ara
7	kad den katarw kad msha'lin leh ezpshet we'mar lhon ayna menjon d'iyzawy dla jtah kadmayya neshde leh ki'fa
8	wzuv kad ezghen kazev wa al ara
9	henon den kad shmaw nafkin waw jad jad kad shariw men kashishe we'shtavkaz atza ljudeh kad izeh bamsaza
10	kad den ezpshet yeshu emar lah la'tza ayka izayhon la nash jayvejy
11	ha den emraz wla nash marya emar den yeshu afla ena mjayyev na lejy sely wmen hasha tuv la tejten
12	tuv den malel amhon yeshu we'mar ena na nuhreh dalma man dvazary aze la nhalej bjeshuja ela neshkaj leh nuhra djayye
13	amrin leh prishe at al nafshaj mashed at sahduzaj la hwaz sharira
14	na yeshu we'mar lhon afen ena mashed na al nafshy sharira y sahduzy metul dyada na men aymeka eziz wla'yka asel na aton den la yadin ton men aymeka eziz wla la'yka asel na
15	aton pagrana'iz danin ton ena lnash la d'aen na
16	we'n d'aen na den diny sharir u metul dla wiz baljuday ela ena wa'vy dshadrany
17	wavnamusjon den kziv dsahduza dazren gavrin sharira y
18	ena na dsahed na al nafshy wa'vy dshadrany sahed lay
19	amrin leh aykaw avuj na yeshu we'mar lhon wla li yadin ton wla la'vy elu li yadin wayton af la'vy yadin wayton
20	halen mele malel bez gasa kad malef bhaykla wla nash ajdeh la guer dakil ezaz waz shazeh
21	amar lhon tuv yeshu ena asel na wzevunany wazmuzon bajtahaykon wa'yka d'ena asel na aton la meshkjin ton lme'za
22	amrin ihudayye lma kay nafsheh katel d'amar d'ayka d'ena asel na aton la meshkjin ton lme'za
23	we'mar lhon aton men dalzajt aton we'na men dalel na aton men hana aton alma ena la wiz men hana alma
24	emrez ljon dazmuzon bajtahaykon ela guer thaymnon d'ena na tmuzon bajtahaykon
25	amrin ihudayye at man at amar lhon yeshu afen dshariz d'emalel amjon
26	sagui iz li laykon lmi'mar wlamdan ela man dshadrany sharir u we'na aylen dshemez meneh halen u mmalel na balma
27	wla idaw dal ava emar lhon
28	amar lhon tuv yeshu emazy dazrimuneh lavreh dnasha haden tedon d'ena na wmedem men svuz nafshy la aved na ela aykana d'alfany avy hajwaz u mmalel na
29	wman dshadrany amy izawy wla shavkany baljuday avy metul d'ena medem dshafar leh aved na bjulsvan
30	kad halen mmalel wa saguiyye' haymenw beh
31	we'mar yeshu lhanon ihudayye dhaymenw beh en aton tjatron bmelazy sharira'iz talmiday aton
32	wzedon shrara whu shrara njararjon
33	amrin leh sareh jnan d'avraham wmen mzum avduza lnash la plija lan aykana amar at dzehon bnay ji're
34	amar lhon yeshu amin amin amarna ljon djulman daved jtiza avdah u dajtiza
35	wavda la mkawe lalam bvayta bra den lalam mkawe
36	en hu hajil davra njararjon sharira'iz tehon bnay ji're
37	yada na dsareh aton d'avraham ela baen ton lmektlany metul dalmelazy la safkin ton
38	ena medem dajsiz lwaz avy mmalel na wa'ton medem dajsayton lwaz avujon avdin ton
39	naw wa'mrin leh avon dilan avraham u amar lhon yeshu elu bnawy wayton d'avraham vadawy d'avraham avdin wayton
40	hasha den ha baen ton lmektlany lgavra dsharirta mallez amjon ayda dshemez men alaha hade avraham la vad
41	aton den avdin ton vade d'avujon amrin leh jnan men sanyuza la hwayn jad ava iz lan alaha
42	amar lhon yeshu elu alaha wa avujon majvin wayton li ena guer men alaha nefkez we'ziz wla wa men svuz nafshy eziz ela hu shadrany
43	metul mana melazy la meshtawdin ton al dla meshkjin ton shamin melazy
44	aton men ava ajelkarsa izaykon wregzeh d'avujon saven ton lmebad haw dmen breshiz katel nasha hu wvashrara la ka'em metul dashrara layt beh emazy dammalel kadavuza men dileh hu mmalel metul ddagala hu af avuh
45	ena den dashrara mmalel na la mhaymnin ton li
46	manu menjon makes li al jtiza we'n shrara mmalel na aton lmana la mhaymnin ton li
47	man dmen alaha izawy mele d'alaha shama metul hana aton la shamin ton metul dla hwayton men alaha
48	naw ihudayye wa'mrin leh la shapir amrin enajnan dshamrayya at wdaywa iz laj
49	amar lhon yeshu li daywa la iz ela la'vy myakar na wa'ton msarin li
50	ena den la bae na shuvjy iz u dvae wda'en
51	amin amin amar na ljon dman dmelazy natar mawta la nejse lalam
52	amrin leh ihudayye hasha idan ddaywa iz laj avraham miz wanviyye wa't amar at dman dmelazy natar mawta la netam lalam
53	lma at rav at men avon avraham dmiz wmen nviyye dmizw manu aved at nafshaj
54	amar lhon yeshu en ena mshabaj na nafshy shuvjy la wa medem izawy avy damshabaj li haw d'amrin ton d'alahan u
55	wla idatuna ena den yada na leh we'n amar na dla yada na leh hawe na li kadava ajwazjon ela yada na leh wmelzeh natar na
56	avraham avujon msawaj wa dnejse yawmy wajsa wajdi
57	amrin leh ihudayye dakil bar jamshin shnin la wayt wla'vraham jsayt
58	amar lhon yeshu amin amin amar na ljon dadla nehwe avraham ena izay
59	washkalw ki'fe dnergmuna wyeshu ettashi wanfak men haykla wavar baynazhon we'sal
