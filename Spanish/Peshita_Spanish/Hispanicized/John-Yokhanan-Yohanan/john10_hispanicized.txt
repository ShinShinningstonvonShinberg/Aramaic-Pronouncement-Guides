1	amin amin amar na ljon dman dla a'el men tara latyara dana ela salek men duka jranya haw ganava hu wgayyasa
2	haw den da'el men tara raya hu dana
3	walhana natar tara pazaj leh tara wana shama kaleh werbawy kare bashmahayhon wmapek lhon
4	wma d'apek aneh kdameh asel werbawy dileh asin bazreh metul dyadin kaleh
5	bazar nujrayya den la asa ana ela arka meneh dla yada kaleh dnujrayya
6	hade pele'za emar lhon yeshu henon den la idaw mana malel amhon
7	amar lhon den tuv yeshu amin amin amar na ljon d'ena na tareh dana
8	wjulhon aylen d'ezaw ganave enon wgayyase ela la shemaz enon ana
9	ena na tara wvi en nash neul nije wneul wnepuk wreya neshkaj
10	ganava la aze ela dnegnuv wadnektul wadnawbed ena eziz djayye nehon lhon wmedem dyatir nehwe lhon
11	ena na raya tava raya tava nafsheh sa'em jlaf aneh
12	aguira den dla hwa raya wlaw dileh enon erbe ma dajsa d'iva d'aze shavek ana warek wa'ze d'iva jatef wamvadar lah lana
13	aguira den arek metul d'aguira hu wla btil leh al ana
14	ena na raya tava wyada na ldily wmezida na men dily
15	aykana dyada li avy we'na yada na la'vy wnafshy sa'em na jlaf ana
16	iz li den af erbe jrane aylen dla hwaw men tyara hana wa'f lhon wale li lmaytayyu enon wneshmon kaly wzehwe ana kulah jda wjad raya
17	metul hana avy rajem li d'ena sa'em na nafshy dzuv esvih
18	la wa nash shakel lah meny ela ena sa'em na lah men sevyany shalit na guer d'asimih wshalit na dzuv esvih dhana pukdana kablez men avy
19	wahwaz tuv palguza baynay ihudayye metul mele halen
20	wa'mrin waw saguiyye' menhon ddaywa iz leh wmeshna shna mana shamin ton leh
21	jrane den amrin waw halen mele la way ddaywana lma daywa meshkaj ayne dsamya lamfataju
22	hwa den i'da djudaza b'uwrishlem wsazwa wa
23	wamhalej wa yeshu bhaykla b'estwa dashlemon
24	wjadru ihudayye wa'mrin leh dama le'mazy nasev at nafshan en at u mshija emar lan galya'iz
25	na yeshu we'mar lhon emrez ljon wla mhaymnin ton wavade d'ena aved na bashmeh d'avy henon sahdin lay
26	ela aton la mhaymnin ton metul dla hwayton men erbay aykana d'emrez ljon
27	erbe dily kaly shamin we'na yada na lhon whenon azen bazary
28	we'na yahev na lhon jayye dalalam wla ni'vdon lalam wla nash nejtuf enon men iday
29	avy guer dyav li men kul rav u wla nash meshkaj dmen ideh d'avy nejtuf
30	ena wa'vy jad jnan
31	washkalw tuv ihudayye ki'fe lmergmeh
32	amar lhon yeshu saguiyye' vade shapire men lwaz avy jawizjon metul ayna vada menhon ragmin ton li
33	amrin leh ihudayye la wa metul vade shapire ragmin nan laj ela metul damgadef at wjad izayk bar nasha aved at nafshaj alaha
34	amar lhon yeshu la wa hajana kziv bnamusjon d'ena emrez d'alahe aton
35	en lhanon emar alahe metul dalwazhon hwaz melza d'alaha wla meshkaj kzava dneshtre
36	la'yna d'ava kadsheh wshadreh lalma aton amrin ton damgadef at al d'emrez ljon davreh na d'alaha
37	ela aved na vade d'avy la thaymnunany
38	en den aved na afen li la mhaymnin ton lhon lavade haymenw dzedon wazhaymnon d'avy bi we'na b'avy
39	wavaw waw tuv dne'jduna wanfak leh men bez idayhon
40	we'sal leh levra dyurdnan ldukza ayka d'iyzawy wa yujanan men kdim kad mamed wa wahwa taman
41	we'zaw nasha saguiyye' lwazeh wa'mrin waw dyujanan af la jda aza vad kulmedem den d'emar yujanan al gavra hana sharir u
42	wsaguiyye' haymenw beh
