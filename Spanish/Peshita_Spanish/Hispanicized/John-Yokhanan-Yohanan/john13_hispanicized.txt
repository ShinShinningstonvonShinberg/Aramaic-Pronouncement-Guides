1	kdam den i'da dfesja yada wa yeshu damtaz shaza danshane men hana alma lwaz avu wa'jev ldileh davhana alma wadama ljarza ajev enon
2	wjad hwaz jshamiza rme wa leh lsatana blebeh dihuda bar shemon sjaryuta dnashlmiwy
3	hu den yeshu metul dyada wa djul medem yav ava b'iydawy wadmen alaha nfak walwaz alaha asel
4	kam men jshamiza wsam najtawy washkal seduna mja bjasawy
5	wa'rmi mayya bamshagta wshari lamshagu regle dzalmidawy wamshawe wa bseduna damje bjasawy
6	kad den eza lwaz shemon ki'fa amar leh shemon at mary reglay mshig at li
7	na yeshu we'mar leh medem daved na at la yada at hasha bazarken den teda
8	amar leh shemon ki'fa lalam la mshig at li reglay amar leh yeshu en la mshig na laj layt laj amy mnaza
9	amar leh shemon ki'fa maden mary la baljud reglay tshig li ela af iday af rishy
10	amar leh yeshu haw dasje la snik ela reglawy baljud nshig kuleh guer dje w af aton kuljon djayya aton ela la kuljon
11	yada wa guer yeshu lhaw dmashlem leh metul hana emar dla wa kuljon djayya aton
12	kad den ashig reglayhon shkal najtawy we'stmej we'mar lhon yadin ton mana evdez ljon
13	aton karen ton li raban wmaran wshapir amrin ton izay guer
14	en ena hajil marjon wrabjon ashiguez ljon reglaykon kma aton jayyavin ton dazshigon regle jad djad
15	hana guer tufsa yehbez ljon d'aykana d'ena evdez ljon af aton tebdon
16	amin amin amar na ljon dlayt avda drab men mareh wla shlija drab men man dshadreh
17	en halen tedon tuvane aton en tebdon enen
18	la wa al kuljon amar na yada na guer la'ylen dagviz ela dajzava neshlam dhaw d'ajel amy lajma arim lay ekveh
19	men hasha amar na ljon men kdam dnehwe dma dahwa thaymnon d'ena na
20	amin amin amar na ljon dman damkabel lman damshadar na li mkabel wman dli mkabel mkabel lman dshadrany
21	halen emar yeshu we'zasas brujeh wa'shed we'mar amin amin amar na ljon djad menjon nashlmany
22	jarw den talmide jad bjad metul dla yadin waw dal manu emar
23	iz wa den men talmidawy jad dasmij wa bubeh haw drajem wa leh yeshu
24	lhana rmas shemon ki'fa dansha'liwy dmanu haw d'emar lawy
25	wanfal haw talmida al jadyeh dyeshu we'mar leh mary manu hana
26	na yeshu we'mar haw u dsava na lajma yahev na leh wasva yeshu lajma wyav lihuda bar shemon sjaryuta
27	wvazar lajma haden ezalal beh satana we'mar leh yeshu medem daved at ved bagal
28	hade den la nash ida men hanon smije dal mana emar leh
29	nashin guer svarw metul dagluskma se'dawy hwa dihuda dmefkad pkad leh dnesben medem dmezbe ladida aw dnetel medem lmeskine
30	hu den ihuda nsav lajma bar shazeh wanfak leh lvar lilya wa den kad nfak
31	we'mar yeshu hasha eshtabaj breh dnasha wa'laha eshtabaj beh
32	we'n alaha eshtabaj beh wa'f alaha mshabaj leh beh wmejda mshabaj leh
33	bnay kalil jrin amjon na wzevunany wa'ykana d'emrez lihudayye dla'yka d'ena asel na aton la meshkjin ton lme'za wa'f ljon amar na hasha
34	pukdana jaza yahev na ljon dahwayton majvin jad ljad aykana d'ena ajevtjon af aton tajvon jad ljad
35	bhade neda kul nash dzalmiday aton en juba nehwe bjon jad lwaz jad
36	amar leh shemon ki'fa maran la'yka asel at na yeshu we'mar leh la'yka d'asel na la meshkaj at hasha dzi'ze bazary ljarza den ti'ze
37	amar leh shemon ki'fa mary lmana la meshkaj na d'ize bazraj hasha nafshy jlafayk sa'em na
38	amar leh yeshu nafshaj jlafay sa'em at amin amin amar na laj dla nekre tarnagla dama dzejpur bi tlaz savnin
