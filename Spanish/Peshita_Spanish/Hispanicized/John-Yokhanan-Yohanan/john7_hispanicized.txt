1	bazar halen mhalej wa yeshu baglila la guer save wa lamhalaju bihud metul dihudayye baen waw lmektleh
2	wkariv wa adida damtale dihudayye
3	we'marw leh ajawy lyeshu shana laj meka wsel lihud dnejson talmidayk vade daved at
4	layt guer nash daved medem btushya wsave hu davguelya nehwe en halen aved at jawa nafshaj lalma
5	af la guer ajawy haymenw waw beh byeshu
6	amar lhon yeshu savny dily dama lhasha la mta savnjon den diljon bjul edan mtayyav
7	la meshkaj alma lmesnajon li den sane metul d'ena mashed na lawy davadawy bishin enon
8	aton sakw ladida hana ena la salek na hasha ladida hana metul dsavna dily la dakil shlem
9	halen emar wfash leh baglila
10	kad den slekw ajawy ladida haden af hu slek la bguelya ela ayj davtushya
11	ihudayye den baen waw leh badida wa'mrin waw aykaw haw
12	wretna saguiyya' metulazeh iz wa bjensha iz waw guer d'amrin dtav u wajrane amrin waw la ela mate lama
13	la den nash galya'iz mmalel wa lawy metul dejlza dihudayye
14	kad den plagw yawmaza dadida slek yeshu lhaykla wmalef wa
15	wmetdamrin waw ihudayye wa'mrin aykana yada hana sefra kad la ilef
16	na yeshu we'mar yulpany la hwa dily ela dhaw dshadrany
17	man dsave dnebed sevyaneh mestakal yulpany en men alaha hu aw ena men svuz nafshy mmalel na
18	man dmen svuz reyaneh mmalel shuvja lnafsheh bae haw den dshuvja dman dshadreh bae sharir u wawla blebeh la iz
19	la wa mushe yav ljon namusa wla nash menjon natar namusa
20	mana baen ton lmektlany na kensha wa'mrin daywa iz laj manu bae lmektlaj
21	na yeshu we'mar lhon jad vada evdez wjuljon metdamrin ton
22	metul hana mushe yav ljon gsurta la wa metul dmeneh y men mushe ela dmen avahaza y wavshabza gasrin ton barnasha
23	en barnasha mezgsar byawma dshabza metul dla neshtre namusa dmushe lay ratnin ton djuleh barnasha ajlmez byawma dshabza
24	la tehon danin bmasav b'ape ela dina ki'na dunw
25	wa'mrin waw nasha men urishlem law hanaw haw dvaen lmektal
26	wha galya'iz mmalel wmedem la amrin leh dalma idaw kashishayn dhanaw sharira'iz mshija
27	ela lhana yadin nan men aymeka hu mshija den emazy d'aze la nash yada men aymeka hu
28	wa'rim yeshu kaleh kad malef bhaykla we'mar wli yadin ton wmen aymeka na yadin ton wmen svuz nafshy la eziz ela sharir u man dshadrany haw d'aton la yadin ton leh
29	ena den yada na leh dmen lwazeh ena whu shadrany
30	wavaw lme'jdeh wla nash armi lawy idayya metul dla dakil ezaz waz shazeh
31	saguiyye' den men kensha haymenw beh wa'mrin mshija ma d'aze lma dyatiran men halen azwaza daved hana aved
32	washmaw prishe ljenshe dammallin lawy halen wshadarw henon wrabay kahne dajshe dne'jduna
33	we'mar yeshu kalil tuv savna amjon ena wa'sel na lwaz man dshadrany
34	wzevunany wla teshkjunany wa'yka d'ena izay la meshkjin ton lme'za
35	amrin ihudayye bnafshhon la'yka zid hana lmi'sal dajnan la meshkjin nan leh lma kay la'zrawaza damme zid dni'sal wnalef ljanfe
36	mana hi hade melza d'emar dzevunany wla teshkjunany wa'yka d'ena izay aton la meshkjin ton lme'za
37	byawma den raba d'iyzawy jrayya dadida ka'em wa yeshu wkae wa'mar en nash she ni'ze lwazy wneshte
38	kul man damhaymen bi aykana d'emarw kzave nahrawaza dmayya jayye nerdon men karseh
39	hade den emar al ruja dazidin waw lamkabalu aylen damhaymnin beh la guer dakil ezyahbaz waz ruja metul dla dakil eshtabaj wa yeshu
40	saguiyye' den men kenshe dashmaw melawy amrin waw hanaw sharira'iz nviyya
41	jrane amrin waw hanaw mshija jrane amrin dalma men glila aze mshija
42	la wa kzava emar dmen sareh ddawid wmen bez-ljem kriza dileh ddawid aze mshija
43	wahwaz waz palguza bjenshe metulazeh
44	wi'yz waw nashin menhon dsaven waw dne'jduna ela la nash armi lawy idayya
45	we'zaw dajshe hanon lwaz rabay kahne wafrishe we'marw lhon kahne lmana la aytituna
46	amrin lhon dajshe la memzum hajana malel barnasha ayj dammalel gavra hana
47	amrin lhon prishe lma af aton tayton
48	lma nash men rishe aw men prishe haymenw beh
49	ela en ama hana dla yada namusa litin enon
50	amar lhon nikadimaws jad menhon haw d'eza wa lwaz yeshu blilya
51	dalma namusa dilan mjayyev lvarnasha ela en neshma meneh lukdam wneda mana vad
52	naw wa'mrin leh lma af at men glila at bsi wajsi danviyya men glila la ka'em
53	esal hajil kuljad lvayteh
