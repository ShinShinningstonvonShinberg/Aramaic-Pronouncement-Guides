1	walyawma dazlaza hwaz meshtuza bkatne mdita daglila we'meh dyeshu taman waz
2	wa'f hu yeshu wzalmidawy ezkriw lah lmeshtuza
3	wajsar wa jamra wa'mra leh emeh lyeshu jamra layt lhon
4	amar lah yeshu ma li wlejy atza la dakil ezaz shaazy
5	amra emeh lamshamshane medem d'amar ljon vedw
6	iz way den taman agane dji'fa shez dsiman lzadkiza dihudayye d'ajdan tren tren revin aw tlaza
7	amar lhon yeshu mlaw enen mayya la'gane wamlaw enen dama lel
8	amar lhon sluw mekil wa'ytaw lrish smaja wa'ytiw
9	wjad tem haw rish smaja mayya hanon dahwaw jamra wla yada wa men aymeka wa mshamshane den yadin waw dhenon mlaw enon lmayya kra rish smaja ljazna
10	we'mar leh kul nash lukdam jamra tava mayte wma darwiw haden ayna davsir at den ntarta ljamra tava dama lhasha
11	hade y aza kadmata davad yeshu bkatne daglila wa'wda shuvjeh whaymenw beh talmidawy
12	bazar hade njez lajfarnajum hu we'meh wa'jawy wzalmidawy wzaman waw kalil yawmaza
13	wkariv wa pesja dihudayye waslek lu'wrishlem yeshu
14	we'shkaj bhaykla lhalen damsabnin tawre werbe wyawne wlamarfane dyazbin
15	wavad leh praguela men javla waljulhon apek men haykla walerbe walzawre wlamarfane we'shad urfanhon wfazurayhon hfaj
16	walhanon damsabnin yawne emar shkulw halen meka wla tebduneh lvayteh d'avy bez te'gurta
17	we'tdjarw talmidawy dajziv datnaneh dvaytaj ajlany
18	naw den ihudayye we'marw leh mana aza mjawe at lan dhalen aved at
19	na yeshu we'mar lhon szurw haykla hana wlazlaza yawmin ena mkim na leh
20	amrin leh ihudayye la'rbin wshez shnin ezbni haykla hana wa't lazlaza yawmin mkim at leh
21	hu den emar wa al haykla dfagreh
22	kad kam den men bez mize etdjarw talmidawy dhade emar wa whaymenw lajzave walmelza d'emar yeshu
23	kad izawy wa den yeshu b'uwrishlem bfesja badida saguiyye' haymenw beh dajsaw azwaza daved
24	hu den yeshu la mhaymen wa lhon nafsheh metul dhu yada wa ljulnash
25	wla snik wa dnash neshad leh al kul barnash hu guer yada wa mana iz bvarnasha
