1	halen malel yeshu wa'rim aynawy lashmayya we'mar avy ezaz shaza shabaj braj davraj nshabjaj
2	aykana dyavt leh shultana al kul bsar djul ma dyavt leh netel leh jayye dalalam
3	halen enon den jayye dalalam dnedunaj d'at at alaha dashrara baljudayk wman dshadart yeshu mshija
4	ena shabajtaj b'ara vada haw dyavt li d'ebed shalemteh
5	whasha shabajayny at avy lwazaj bhaw shuvja d'iyz wa li lwazaj men kdam dnehwe alma
6	awdez shmaj lavnay nasha hanon dyavt li men alma dilaj waw wli yavt enon wantarw melzaj
7	hasha yedez djul ma dyavt li men lwazaj u
8	dmele dyavt li yehbez lhon whenon kabelw widaw sharira'iz dmen lwazaj nefkez whaymenw d'at shadartany
9	we'na layhon bae na la wa al alma bae na ela al hanon dyavt li ddilaj enon
10	wjul medem ddily u dilaj u wdilaj dily u wamshabaj na bhon
11	mekil la hwiz balma whalen balma enon we'na lwazaj aze na ava kadisha tar enon bashmaj haw dyavt li dnehon jad aykana dajnan
12	kad amhon hwiz balma ena natar wiz lhon bashmaj la'ylen dyavt li netrez wnash menhon la evad ela breh d'avdana dnezmale kzava
13	hasha den lwazaj aze na whalen mmalel na balma dzehwe jadwazy mshamlya bhon
14	ena yehbez lhon melzaj walma sna enon dla waw men alma aykana d'ena la wiz men alma
15	la wa dzeshkul enon men alma bae na ela dzetar enon men bisha
16	la waw guer men alma aykana d'ena la wiz men alma
17	ava kadesh enon bashraraj dmelzaj dilaj shrara y
18	aykana dli shadart lalma af ena shadrez enon lalma
19	wal apayhon ena mkadesh na nafshy dnehon af henon mkadshin bashrara
20	wla wa al apay halen bae na baljud ela af al apay aylen damhaymnin bi bmelazhon
21	djulhon nehon jad aykana d'at avy bi we'na baj d'af henon ban jad nehon danhaymen alma d'at shadartany
22	we'na shuvja dyavt li yehbez lhon dnehon jad aykana dajnan jad jnan
23	ena bhon wa't bi dnehon gmirin ljad wadneda alma d'at shadartany wda'jevz enon aykana d'af li ajevt
24	ava hanon dyavt li save na d'azar d'ena af henon nehon amy dnehon jasen shuvja dily haw dyavt li d'ajevtany men kdam tarmyazeh dalma
25	avy ki'na walma la yadaj ena den idataj whenon idaw d'at shadartany
26	wa'wdez enon shmaj wmawda na djuba haw d'ajevtany nehwe bhon we'na ehwe bhon
