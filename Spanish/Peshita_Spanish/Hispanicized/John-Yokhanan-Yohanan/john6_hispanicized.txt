1	bazar halen esal yeshu levra dyama daglila dtiveriyyaws
2	we'salw bazreh kenshe saguiyye' metul djasen waw azwaza daved bajrihe
3	waslek yeshu ltura wzaman yazev wa am talmidawy
4	kariv wa den adida dfesja dihudayye
5	wa'rim aynawy yeshu wajsa kensha saguiyya' d'aze lwazeh we'mar lfilipaws aymeka nesben lajma dne'jlon halen
6	hade den emar kad mnase leh hu guer yada wa mana zid lmebad
7	amar leh filipaws dma'zen dinarin lajma la safek lhon kad kalil kalil jad jad menhon nesav
8	amar leh jad men talmidawy andre'was aju dshemon ki'fa
9	iz tnan talya jad d'iyz lawy jamesh grisan dasare wazren nunin ela halen mana enon lhalen kulhon
10	amar lhon yeshu vedw nasha kulhon dnestamkon esba den sagui wa bah bdukza ha we'stmejw gavre bmenyana jamsha alfin
11	washkal yeshu lajma wvarej wfaleg lhanon dasmijin whajana af men nune kma dasvaw
12	wjad svaw emar lzalmidawy kaneshw ksayye dizarw dla ni'vad medem
13	wjaneshw wamlaw tresar kufinin ksayye aylen dizarw lhanon d'ejalw men jamsha lajmin dasare
14	hanon den nasha dajsaw aza davad yeshu amrin waw sharira'iz hanaw nviyya d'aze lalma
15	yeshu den ida dazidin dni'zon nejtfuna wnebduna malka wshani leh ltura hu baljudawy
16	wjad hwa ramsha njezw talmidawy lyama
17	wizevw basfita wa'zen waw levra lajfarnajum wjeshkaz waz lah wla ati wa lwazhon yeshu
18	yama den esdkef wa layhon metul druja rabza neshbaz waz
19	wadvarw ayj estdawaza esrin wjamsha aw tlazin wajsaw lyeshu kad mhalej al yamza wjad krev lwaz sfithon djelw
20	hu den yeshu emar lhon ena na la tedjlon
21	wasvaw waw dankabluna basfita wvah bshaza sfita ha hwaz lwaz ara ha d'asin waw lah
22	walyawma dvazreh haw kensha dka'em wa bevra dyama jsaw dasfita jriza layt wa taman ela en ha daslekw lah talmide wadla al wa amhon yeshu am talmidawy lasfita
23	ezay way den elfe jranyaza men tveriyyaws al guev dukza ha d'ejalw bah lajma kad barej yeshu
24	wjad jsa haw kensha dla hwa taman yeshu afla talmidawy slekw lhalen elfe we'zaw lajfarnajum wvaen waw leh lyeshu
25	wjad eshkju bevra dyama amrin leh raban emazy ezayt lharka
26	na yeshu we'mar lhon amin amin amar na ljon dvaen ton li la wa metul dajsayton azwaza ela d'ejalton lajma wasvaton
27	la tefljon me'julta d'avda ela me'julta damkawya ljayye dalalam ayda davreh dnasha netel ljon lhana guer ava jzam alaha
28	amrin leh mana nebed dnefluj vade d'alaha
29	na yeshu we'mar lhon hanaw vada d'alaha dazhaymnon bman dhu shadar
30	amrin leh mana aza aved at dnejse wanhaymen baj mana saar at
31	avahayn manna ejalw bmadbra aykana dajziv dlajma men shmayya yav lhon lme'jal
32	amar lhon yeshu amin amin amar na ljon dla wa mushe yav ljon lajma men shmayya ela avy yahev ljon lajma dkushta men shmayya
33	lajmeh guer d'alaha izawy haw danjez men shmayya wyahev jayye lalma
34	amrin leh maran bjulsvan hav lan lajma hana
35	amar lhon yeshu ena na lajma djayye man d'aze lwazy la nejpan wman damhaymen bi la neshe lalam
36	ela emrez ljon dajsaytunany wla mhaymnin ton
37	kul dyav li avy lwazy ni'ze wman dalwazy ni'ze la apkeh lvar
38	dnejtez men shmayya la wa d'ebed sevyany ela d'ebed sevyaneh dman dshadrany
39	hanaw den sevyaneh dman dshadrany djul dyav li la awbed meneh ela akimiwy byawma jrayya
40	hanaw guer sevyaneh d'avy djul djase lavra wamhaymen beh nehon leh jayye dalalam we'na akimiwy byawma jrayya
41	ihudayye den ratnin waw lawy d'emar d'ena na lajma dnejtez men shmayya
42	wa'mrin waw la wa hana yeshu breh dyawsef haw dajnan yadin la'vu wle'meh wa'ykana amar hana dmen shmayya nejtez
43	na yeshu we'mar lhon la tertnon jad am jad
44	la nash meshkaj dni'ze lwazy ela en nagdeh ava dshadrany we'na akimiwy byawma jrayya
45	kziv guer banviyya dnehon kulhon malfe d'alaha kul man dshama hajil men ava wyalef meneh aze lwazy
46	la wa djase nash la'va ela man dmen alaha izawy haw u jase la'va
47	amin amin amar na ljon dman damhaymen bi iz leh jayye dalalam
48	ena na lajma djayye
49	avahaykon ejalw manna bmadbra wmizw
50	hanaw den lajma danjez men shmayya dne'jul nash meneh wla nmuz
51	ena na lajma jayya dmen shmayya nejtez we'n nash ne'jul men hana lajma nije lalam wlajma ayna d'ena etel pagry u dal apay jayyawy dalma yahev na
52	nasen waw den ihudayye jad am jad wa'mrin aykana meshkaj hana pagreh dnetel lan lme'jal
53	we'mar lhon yeshu amin amin amar na ljon d'ela te'jlon pagreh davreh dnasha wzeshton dmeh layt ljon jayye baknumjon
54	man d'ajel den men pagry wshaze men demy iz leh jayye dalalam we'na akimiwy byawma jrayya
55	pagry guer sharira'iz izawy me'julta wdemy sharira'iz izawy mashtya
56	man d'ajel pagry wshaze demy bi mkawe we'na beh
57	aykana dshadrany ava jayya we'na jay na metul ava wman dne'jlany af hu nije metulazy
58	hanaw lajma danjez men shmayya la wa ayj d'ejalw avahaykon manna wmizw man d'ajel hana lajma nije lalam
59	halen emar bajnushta kad malef bajfarnajum
60	wsaguiyye' dashmaw men talmidawy amrin kashya y melza hade manu meshkaj lmeshmah
61	yeshu den ida bnafsheh dratnin al hade talmidawy we'mar lhon hade majshla ljon
62	en tejson hajil lavreh dnasha dsalek la'zar d'iyzawy wa men kdim
63	ruja y dmajya pagra la mahne medem mele d'ena mallez amjon ruja enen wjayye enen
64	ela iz nasha menjon dla mhaymnin yada wa guer yeshu men kdim man enon aylen dla mhaymnin wmanu haw dmashlem leh
65	we'mar wa lhon metul hana emrez ljon dla nash meshkaj dni'ze lwazy ela ihiv leh men avy
66	metul hade melza saguiyye' men talmidawy esalw lvestarhon wla mhaljin waw ameh
67	we'mar yeshu lazresarteh lma af aton saven ton lmi'sal
68	na shemon ki'fa we'mar mary lwaz man ni'sal mele djayye dalalam iz laj
69	wajnan haymenn widan d'at u mshija breh d'alaha jayya
70	amar lhon yeshu la wa ena gvizjon lazresar wmenjon jad satana hu
71	emar wa den al ihuda bar shemon sjaryuta hu guer zid wa dnashlmiwy jad men tresar
