1	mawda na ljon den ajay ewangueliyyawn dsabartjon wkabeltuna wkamton beh
2	wveh ja'en ton b'ayda melza sabartjon en ahdin ton en la wa srika'iz haymenton
3	ashlmez ljon guer men lukdam ayj medem dkablez damshija miz al apay jtahayn aykana dajziv
4	wde'zkvar wkam lazlaza yawmin ayj dajziv
5	we'zjsi lji'fa wvazreh lazresar
6	wvazarken ezjsi lyatir men jameshma' ajin ajjda dsaguiyye' menhon kayyamin enon dama lhasha wmenhon dmejw
7	wmen bazar halen ezjsi lyakuv wvazreh lashlije kulhon
8	ljarza den djulhon ayj dalyajta ezjsi af li
9	ena na guer surhon dashlije wla shawe na d'ezkre shlija metul dredpez iteh d'alaha
10	btaybuzeh den d'alaha izay medem d'iyzay wtaybuzeh dvi la hwaz srika ela yatir men kulhon li'yz la ena ela taybuzeh damy
11	en ena hajil we'n henon hajana majrsinan whajana haymenton
12	en den mshija mezkres dkam men bez mize aykana iz bjon nasha d'amrin layt jayyaz mize
13	we'n jayyaz mize layt afla mshija kam
14	we'n mshija la kam srika y karusuzan srika af haymanuzjon
15	meshtajjin nan den af sahde dagale d'alaha d'ashedn al alaha d'akim lamshija kad la akim
16	en mize guer la kamin afla mshija kam
17	we'n mshija la kam batala y haymanuzjon wadakil bajtahaykon aton
18	wajvar af aylen dadmejw bamshija evadw lhon
19	we'n bhalen u jayye baljud msabrinan bamshija dawenan u men kulhon bnaynasha
20	hasha den mshija kam men bez mize wahwa rishiza ddamke
21	wa'ykana davyad barnasha hwa mawta hajana af byad barnasha hawya jayyaz mize
22	aykana guer dva'dam kulhon bnaynasha matin hajana af bamshija kulhon ja'en
23	nash nash btekseh rishiza hwa mshija bazarken aylen ddamshija enon bme'zizeh
24	whaden tehwe jarza ma dmashlem malkuza la'laha ava ma dvatel kul rish wjul shultan wjul jaylin
25	zid u guer dnamlej dama dansim beldvavawy kulhon tjez reglawy
26	wajrayya beldvava mezbatal mawta
27	kul guer shabed tjez reglawy ma d'emar den djulmedem meshtabad leh idia dastar men haw dshabed leh kul
28	wma d'eshtabad leh kul haden hu bra neshtabad lhaw dshabed leh kul dnehwe alaha kul bjul
29	we'la mana nebdon hanon damdin jlaf mize en mize la kamin mana amdin jlaf mize
30	walmana af jnan bjul shaa bkundinaws kaminan
31	yame na bshuvharjon ajay d'iyz li bmaran yeshu mshija djulyum ma'ez na
32	en ayj dvez bnaynasha eshtdiz ljaywaza b'efesaws mana ezhniz en mize la kamin ne'jul wneshte mjar guer matinan
33	la teton mjablan reyane basime shuyaza bishaza
34	airw lebjon sadika'iz wla tejton iz guer nasha didaza d'alaha layt bhon lvehtazjon u amar na
35	ni'mar nash menjon d'aykana kamin mize wva'yna pagra azen
36	sajla sara dsara at en la ma'ez la jayye
37	whaw medem dsara at la wa haw pagra dazid lmehwa sara at ela predta artelata djete aw dasare aw dsharka dsarune
38	alaha den yahev leh pagra aykana dsave waljad jad men sarune pagra dajyaneh
39	la wa den kul pgar shwe jrin u guer pagra dvar nasha wajrin davira wajrin dfarajza wajrin dnune
40	wi'yz pagre shmayyane wi'yz pagre aranayye ela jrin u shuvja dashmayyane wajrin d'aranayye
41	wajrin u shuvja dshemsha wajrin shuvja dsahra wajrin shuvja djawkve wjawkva men kawkva myatar u bshuvja
42	hajana af jayyaz mize mesdarin bajvala kamin dla jvala
43	mesdarin bsara kamin bshuvja mesdarin bajrihuza kamin bjayla
44	mesdarin pagra nafshanayya ka'em pagra rujanayya iz guer pagra danfesh wi'yz pagra druj
45	hajana af kziv hwa adam barnasha kadmayya lanfesh jayya wa'dam jrayya lruja majyaniza
46	ela la wa lukdam rujanayya ela nafshanayya whaden rujanayya
47	barnasha kadmayya afrana dmen ara barnasha dazren marya men shmayya
48	aykana d'iyzawy haw afrana hajana af afrane wa'ykana d'iyzawy haw dmen shmayya hajana af shmayyane
49	wa'yj dalveshn dmuza dhaw dmen afra hajana nelbash dmuza dhaw dmen shmayya
50	hade den amar na ajay dvesra wadma malkuza dashmayya lmi'raz la meshkjin wla jvala yarez la mezjablanuza
51	ha amar na ljon rasa law kulan nedmaj kulan den nezjalaf
52	jarifa'iz ayj rfaf ayna bkarna jrata kad tekre wankumon mize dla jvala wajnan nezjalaf
53	zid u guer hana dmezjabal dnelbash la mezjablanuza whana dma'ez dnelbash la mayyuzuza
54	ma dlavesh den hana dmezjabal la mezjablanuza whana dma'ez la mayyuzuza haden tehwe melza y dajziva d'ezbla mawta bsajuza
55	aykaw uksaj mawta aw ayka y sajuzejy shyul
56	ukseh den dmawta jtiza y wjayla dajtiza namusa hu
57	taybu den la'laha dyav lan sajuza byad maran yeshu mshija
58	mekil ajay wjabivay hwaw msharrin wla tehon metsiin ela hwaw mezyatrin bjulsvan bavadeh dmarya kad yadin ton damljon la wa srik bmarya
