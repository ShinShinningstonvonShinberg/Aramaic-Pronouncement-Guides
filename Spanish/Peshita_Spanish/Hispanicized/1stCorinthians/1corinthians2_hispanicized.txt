1	we'na ajay kad eziz lwazjon la bmamlla rawrva afla bjejmza sabartjon raseh d'alaha
2	wla danez nafshy baynazjon ayj dmedem yada na ela en lyeshu mshija af leh kad skif
3	we'na bdejlza sagui'za wvarziza hwiz lwazjon
4	wmelazy wjarusuzy la hwaz bampisanuza dmele djejmza ela bzajwiza druja wadjayla
5	dla tehwe haymanuzjon bjejmza davnaynasha ela bjayla d'alaha
6	jejmza den mmallinan bagmire jejmza la dhana alma afla dshalitanawy dalma hana dmezbatlin
7	ela mmallinan jejmza d'alaha bras ha damjasya waz wkadem wa parshah alaha men kdam alme lshuvja dilan
8	ha djad men shalitane dalma hana la yadah elu guer yaduh law lmarah dzeshbujta sakpin waw
9	ela ayj dajziv dayna la jsaz we'dna la shemaz wal leba dvarnasha la slek medem dtayyev alaha la'ylen drajmin leh
10	lan den gala alaha brujeh ruja guer kulmedem basya af umkawy d'alaha
11	ayna hu guer barnasha dyada davvarnasha ela en ruja dvar nasha dveh hajana af dva'laha nash la yada ela en rujeh d'alaha
12	jnan den la wa ruja dalma nsavn ela ruja dmen alaha dneda mawhvaza dmen alaha ezihev lan
13	aylen d'af mmallinan la wa byulpana dmele djejmza davnaynasha ela byulpana druja walrujane rujanyaza mfajminan
14	barnasha guer dvanfesh u la mkabel rujanyaza shatyuza enen guer leh wla meshkaj dneda davruj metdin
15	rujana den kul medem d'aen whu men nash la metdin
16	manu guer ida reyaneh dmarya dnalfiwy lan den reyana damshija iz lan
