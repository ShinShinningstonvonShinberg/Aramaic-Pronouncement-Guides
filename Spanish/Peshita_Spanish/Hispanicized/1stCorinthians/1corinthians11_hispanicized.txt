1	etdamaw bi aykana d'af ena bamshija
2	mshabaj na ljon den ajay davjul medem mahed ton li wa'ykana d'ashlmez ljon pukdane ajidin ton
3	save na den dzedon djul gvar risheh mshija hu wrishah d'atza gavra hu wrisheh damshija alaha hu
4	kul gavra damsale aw meznabe kad mjasay risheh mavhez risheh
5	wjul atza damsalya aw meznabya kad gle rishah mavhza rishah shawya y guer am ha dagri rishah
6	en guer la mezkasya atza af testapar en den mshakar u la'tza lmestaparu aw lmegra tezkase
7	gavra guer la jayyav danjase risheh metul dadmuza hu wzeshbujta d'alaha atza den teshbujta y dgavra
8	la guer izawy gavra men atza ela atza men gavra
9	wa'fla guer gavra ezbri metul atza ela atza metul gavra
10	metul hana jayyava atza dshultana nehwe al rishah metul mala'je
11	bram den la gavra lvar men atza afla atza lvar men gavra bmaran
12	aykana guer d'atza men gavra hajana af gavra byad atza kulmedem den men alaha hu
13	dunw baynaykon lnafshjon ya'e la'tza djad gle rishah tsale la'laha
14	afla hu kyana malef ljon dgavra ma dka'em sareh sara hu leh
15	wa'tza ma damrabay sarah teshbujta y lah metul dsarah jlaf tajsiza hu ezihev lah
16	en nash den mezjre al halen lan layt yada ayj hana wla liteh d'alaha
17	hade den damfaked na la ayj mshabaj na ljon metul dla wa lakdamaykon ezayton ela lavsiruza hu njetton
18	lukdam guer ma dmezkanshin ton bita palagwaza shama na d'iyz baynazjon wmedem medem mhaymen na
19	zidin enon guer af jeryane dnehon baynazjon d'aylen davken bjon nezyadon
20	ma hajil dmezkanshin ton law ayj dsadek lyawmeh dmaran ajlin ton wshazen
21	ela nash nash jshamizeh kadem ajel lah whawe jad kfen wjad rwe
22	dalma bate layt ljon dze'jlon wzeshton aw biteh d'alaha basen ton wmavhzin ton la'ylen dlayt lhon mana imar ljon eshabajjon bhade la mshabaj na
23	ena guer kablez men maran haw medem d'ashlmez ljon dmaran yeshu bhaw lilya dmeshtlem wa nsav wa lajma
24	wvarej waksa we'mar savw ajulw hanaw pagry dal apaykon mezkse hajana hwayton avdin ldujrany
25	hajana men bazar d'ajshemw af kasa yav we'mar hana kasa izawy diyyaziki jdaza bdemy hajana hwayton avdin kul emazy dshazen ton ldujrany
26	kul emazy guer d'ajlin ton lajma hana wshazen ton kasa hana mawteh u dmaran mahdin ton dama lme'zizeh
27	ayna hajil d'ajel men lajmeh dmarya wshaze men kaseh wla shawe leh mjayyav u ladmeh dmarya walfagreh
28	metul hana nehwe nash bake nafsheh whaden ajel men lajma hana wshaze men kasa hana
29	man d'ajel guer wshaze meneh kad la shawe juyyava w lnafsheh ajel wshaze dla prash pagreh dmarya
30	metul hana sagui'in bjon mare wajrihe wsaguiyye' ddamkin
31	elu guer nafshan danin wayn la metdinin wayn
32	kad metdininan den men maran mezrdayyu mezrdenan dla am alma nezjayyav
33	mekil ajay emazy dmezkanshin ton lme'jal hwayton mkawen jad ljad
34	man den dajfen bvayteh nelas dla ljayyavuza tehon mezkanshin al sharka den ma d'eziz epakedjon
