1	pawlaws karya washlija dyeshu mshija bsevyaneh d'alaha wsawszenis aja
2	lita d'alaha davkawrinzaws krayya wkadishe davyeshu mshija mkadshin waljulhon aylen dkaren shmeh dmaran yeshu mshija bjul azar dilhon wdilan
3	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
4	mawde na la'lahy bjulsvan jlafaykon al taybuza d'alaha d'ezyahbaz ljon byeshu mshija
5	davjul medem zarton beh bjul mela wavjul idaza
6	ayj dsahduzeh damshija eshtarraz bjon
7	dla ezbasarton bajda men mawhvazeh ela msaken ton lguelyaneh dmaran yeshu mshija
8	dhu nshararjon dama lajrata dadla reshyan tehon byawmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
9	mhayman w alaha dvi'ydeh ezkriton lshawtafuza davreh yeshu mshija maran
10	bae na den menjon ajay bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija dzehwe jda melza ljuljon wla nehwyan bjon palagwaza ela tehon gmirin bajda tariza wavjad reyana
11	shlajw li guer laykon ajay men bez kla'e djeryane iz baynazjon
12	hade den amar na d'iyz menjon d'amar ena dpawlaws na wi'yz d'amar ena d'apalaw na wi'yz d'amar ena dji'fa na wi'yz d'amar ena damshija na
13	dalma ezpalag leh mshija aw lma pawlaws esdkef al apaykon aw bashmeh dpawlaws madton
14	mawde na la'lahy dalnash menjon la amdez ela lakrispaws walga'iyyaws
15	dla nash ni'mar davshemy amdez
16	amdez den af lvayteh d'estefana tuv den la yada na en lnash jrin amdez
17	la guer shadrany mshija lmamadu ela lamsabaru la bjejmaz mele dla nestarak skifeh damshija
18	melza guer daskifa la'vide shatyuza y lan den la'ylen djainan jayla y d'alaha
19	kziv guer d'awbed jejmza djakime we'glus tariza dsakulzane
20	ayka hu jakima aw ayka hu safra aw ayka hu darusha dalma hana la ha ashtyah alaha jejmzeh dalma hana
21	metul guer davjejmzeh d'alaha la ida alma bjejmza la'laha sva alaha davshatyuza djarusuza naje la'ylen damhaymnin
22	metul dihudayye azwaza sha'lin wa'rmayye jejmza baen
23	jnan den majrsinan mshija kad skif tukalta lihudayye wla'rmayye shatyuza
24	lhanon den dakren ihudayye wa'rmayye mshija jayla d'alaha wjejmza d'alaha
25	metul dshatyuzeh d'alaha jakima y men bnaynasha wajrihuzeh d'alaha jaylzanya y men bnay nasha
26	jsaw guer af kratjon ajay dla sagui'in bjon jakime bavsar wla sagui'in bjon jaylzane wla sagui'in bjon bnay tuhma raba
27	ela gva alaha lsajlawy dalma dnavhez ljakime wagva krihawy dalma dnavhez ljaylzane
28	wagva la'ylen davsir tuhmhon balma walmaslayya wla'ylen dlayt enon danvatel la'ylen d'iyzayhon
29	dla neshtavhar kul bsar kdamawy
30	af aton den meneh aton byeshu mshija haw dahwa lan jejmza men alaha wsadikuza wkadishuza wfurkana
31	ayj dajziv dman dmeshtavhar bmarya neshtavhar
