1	hatw bazar juba wtanw bmawhvaza druja yatira'iz den dzeznabon
2	man dammalel guer bleshana la wa lavnaynasha mmalel ela la'laha la guer nash shama medem dammalel ela bruj rasa mmalel
3	ayna den dmeznabe lavnaynasha mmalel benyana wlubava wvuyya'a
4	dammalel bleshana nafsheh hu bane wadmeznabe ita bane
5	save na den djuljon tmallon bleshane yatira'iz den dzeznabon rav u guer ayna dmeznabe men haw dammalel bleshana en la mfashek en den mfashek ita bane
6	whasha ajay en ize lwazjon we'malel amjon bleshane mana mawtar na ljon ela en emalel amjon aw bguelyana aw bidaza aw banviyyuza aw byulpana
7	af sevwaza guer aylen dlayt bhen nafsha wyahban kala en abuva we'n kizara en purshana la avdan bez kinta lajvarzah aykana mezida medem dmesdmar aw medem dmeznkesh
8	we'n karna tekre kala dla prish manu nettayyav lakrava
9	hajana af aton en ti'mron melza bleshana wla tehwe mfashka aykana mezida medem d'amrin ton haweton ljon ayj haw dam a'ar mmallin ton
10	ha guer guense dleshane saguiyye' iz balma wlayt jad menhon dla kala
11	we'n la yada na jayleh dkala hawe na li barbrayya lhaw dammalel wa'f haw dammalel hawe leh li barbrayya
12	hajana af aton metul dtanane aton dmawhvaza druja lvenyana dita baw dzezyatron
13	whaw dammalel bleshana nsale danfashek
14	en hu guer dahwiz msale bleshana rujy u msalya maday den dla pi'rin u
15	mana hajil ebed esale brujy we'sale af bmaday we'samar brujy we'samar af bmaday
16	we'n la en mvarej at bruj haw dammale dukzeh dhedyuta aykana ni'mar amin al tawdizaj dilaj metul dmana amar at la yada
17	at guer shapir mvarej at ela javraj la mezbane
18	mawde na la'laha dyatir men kuljon mmalel na bleshane
19	ela bita save na djamesh melin bmaday emalel d'af jrane alef yatir men rebu melin bleshana
20	ajay la hwayton tlen breyanaykon ela lvishaza hwaw yalude wavreyanaykon hwaw gmirin
21	bnamusa kziv davmamlla nujrayya wavleshana jrina emalel ameh am ama hana afla hajana neshmunany amar marya
22	maden leshane la'za hu simin la lamhaymne ela la'ylen dla mhaymnin nviyyawaza den la wa la'ylen dla mhaymnin ela la'ylen damhaymnin
23	en hu hajil dzezkanash kulah ita wjulhon bleshane nmallon wnelon hedyute aw aylen dla mhaymnin la amrin dhalen shnaw lhon
24	we'n kuljon tehon meznaben wneul lwazjon hedyuta aw man dla mhaymen mezbse men kuljon wmezkawan men kuljon
25	wjasyaza dlebeh mezgalyan whaden nepel al apawy wnesgud la'laha wni'mar sharira'iz alaha iz bjon
26	amar na hajil ajay d'emazy dmezkanshin ton la'yna menjon d'iyz leh masmura ni'mar wa'yna d'iyz leh yulpana wa'yna d'iyz leh guelyana wa'yna d'iyz leh leshana wa'yna d'iyz leh pushaka kulhen lvenyana nehwyan
27	we'n bleshana nash nmalel tren nmallon wjad sagui tlaza wjad jad nmallon wjad nfashek
28	we'n layt damfashek neshtuk leh bita haw dammalel bleshana wvaynawy lnafsheh wla'laha nmalel
29	nviyye den tren aw tlaza nmallon wsharka nfarshon
30	we'n lajrina nezgle kad yazev kadmayya neshtuk leh
31	meshkjin ton guer kuljon djad jad teznabon djulnash ni'laf wjulnash nezbayya
32	ruja guer danviyye lanviyye meshtabda
33	metul d'alaha la wa dashgushya ela dashlama ayj davjulhen idaza dkadishe
34	neshaykon bita nehwyan shatikan la guer mapas lhen danmallan ela dneshtabdan aykana d'af namusa emar
35	we'n medem savyan dni'lpan bvatayhen nsha'lan lvalayhen behtza y guer dneshe bita nmallan
36	aw dalma menjon w nefkaz melzeh d'alaha aw lwazjon w baljud mtaz
37	en nash den menjon savar danviyya hu aw druja hu neda halen djazev na ljon dfukdane enon dmaran
38	en nash den la yada la neda
39	tanw hajil ajay lmeznabayyu wlammalalu bleshane la tejlon
40	kulmedem den b'eskima wavteksa nehwe
