1	save na den dzedon ajay d'avahayn kulhon tjez nana hwaw wjulhon byama varw
2	wjulhon byad mushe madw banana wavyama
3	wjulhon jda me'julta druja ejalw
4	wjulhon jad mashtya druja eshtiw shazen waw guer men ki'fa druja d'azya waz amhon ki'fa den ha hu hwa mshija
5	ela la wa bsug'hon estvi alaha nfalw guer bmadbra
6	halen den tufsa lan u hwaw dla hwayn raguin bishaza aykana dhenon ragw
7	wa'fla nehwe paljay pzajre aykana d'af menhon plajw ayj dajziv dizev ama lme'jal walmeshta wkamw lmeshtayyu
8	wa'fla nsane aykana dmenhon saniw wanfalw bjad yawma esrin wazlaza alfin
9	wla nnase lamshija aykana dmenhon nasiw wa'wbedw enon jwawaza
10	wa'fla tertnon aykana dmenhon rtenw we'vadw b'iyday mjablana
11	halen den kulhen dagdash lhon ltufsan hway we'zkzev metul marduza dilan djarzhon dalme layn matyaz
12	mekil man dsavar dkam nesdhar dla nepel
13	nesyuna la matijon ela davnaynasha mhayman u den alaha dla narpejon dzeznason yatir man medem damsen ton ela nebed lnesyunjon mapkana aykana dzeshkjon lamsaybaru
14	metul hana jabivay rukw men puljana dafzajre
15	ayj daljakime amar na dunw aton medem d'amar na
16	kasa haw dzawdiza damvarjinan la wa shawtafuza izawy dadmeh damshija wlajma haw dkasenan la wa shawtafuza izawy dfagreh damshija
17	aykana djad u lajma haw hajana kulan jad jnan pgar kulan guer men haw u jad lajma nasbinan
18	jsaw li'ysrayyel dvavsar la wa aylen d'ajlin devje hawen shawtafe lmadbja
19	mana hajil amar na dafzajra medem izawy aw devja dafzajra medem u la
20	ela haw medem ddavjin janfe lshi'de w davjin wla la'laha la den save na dzehon shawtafe lshi'de
21	la meshkjin ton dzeshton kasa dmaran wjasa dshi'de wla meshkjin ton dzeshtawtfon bfazura dmaran wavfazura dshi'de
22	aw dalma matanu matninan lmaran dalma jasininan meneh
23	kul medem shalit li ela la kul medem pakaj kul medem shalit li ela la kul medem mvane
24	la nash dnafsheh nehwe bae ela kulnash af djavreh
25	kulmedem dmesdaban bmakelawn hwayton ajlin dla ukava metul ti'rta
26	dmarya y guer ara bamla'ah
27	en nash den men janfe kare ljon wsaven ton lmi'sal kulmedem dmetsim kdamaykon ajulw dla ukava metul ti'rta
28	en nash den ni'mar ljon dhana dadvija hu la te'jlon metul haw d'amar ljon wmetul ti'rta
29	ti'rta den amar na la wa diljon ela dhaw d'amar lmana guer ji'ruzy metdina men ti'rta dajrane
30	en ena btaybuza mezjashaj na mana mezgadaf na al medem d'ena mawde'na
31	en ajlin ton hajil we'n shazen ton we'n medem avdin ton kulmedem lzeshbujta d'alaha hwayton avdin
32	dla tukla hwaw lihudayye wla'rmayye waliteh d'alaha
33	ayj d'af ena bjul medem ljulnash shafar na wla bae na medem dli pakaj ela medem dalsaguiyye' pakaj dnijon
