1	we'na ajay la eshkjez d'emalel amjon ayj dam rujane ela ayj dam pagrane wa'yj dalyalude bamshija
2	jalva ashkizjon wla yehbez ljon me'julta la guer dakil meshkjin wayton ela afla hasha meshkjin ton
3	dakil guer bavsar aton ayka guer d'iyz bjon jsama wjeryana wfalagwaza la ha pagrane ton wvavsar mhaljin ton
4	ma d'amar guer nash nash menjon ena dpawlaws na wajrina amar na d'apalaw na la ha pagrane ton
5	manu guer pawlaws aw manu apalaw ela mshamshane dvi'ydayhon haymenton wnash nash ayj dyav leh marya
6	ena nesbez wa'palaw ashki ela alaha rabi
7	la hajil haw dnasev izawy medem wla haw dmashke ela alaha damrabe
8	man dnasev den wman dmashke jad enon wnash ayj amleh agreh mkabel
9	am alaha guer paljinan wfuljana d'alaha wvenyana d'alaha aton enon
10	wa'yj taybuza d'alaha d'ezyahbaz li samez sheze'sta ayj ardijla jakima jrina den leh bane kulnash den nejse aykan bane leh
11	sheze'sta guer jriza star men hade dsima nash la meshkaj lamsam d'iyzeh yeshu mshija
12	we'n nash den bane al hade sheze'sta dahva aw si'ma aw ki'fe yakiraza aw kayse aw mira aw jabza
13	vada djulnash mezgle yawma guer haw gale leh metul davnura mezgle wavadeh djulnash aykana izawy nura tefrshiwy
14	wa'yna dankawe vadeh haw dvane agreh nkabel
15	wa'yna davadeh ni'kad nejsar hu den neshtawsav hajana den ayj dmen nura
16	la yadin ton dhaykleh aton d'alaha wrujeh d'alaha amra bjon
17	wman damjabel haykleh d'alaha mjabel leh alaha haykleh guer d'alaha kadish u d'aton enon
18	nash la nate nafsheh man dsavar bjon djakim u balma hana nehwe leh sajla dnehwe jakima
19	jejmzeh guer dalma hana leluza y lwaz alaha kziv u guer d'ajed jakime bjaruzhon
20	wzuv marya yada majshvazhon djakime dasrikan enen
21	metul hana la nash neshtavhar bavnaynasha kulmedem guer diljon u
22	en pawlaws we'n apalaw we'n ki'fa we'n alma we'n jayye we'n mawta we'n dkaman we'n dazidan kul medem diljon u
23	wa'ton damshija wamshija d'alaha
