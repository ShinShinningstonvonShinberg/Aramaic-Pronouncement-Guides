1	hajana hwayn jshivin ljon ayj mshamshane damshija wrabay bate drasawy d'alaha
2	harka mekil mezbe brabay bate dnash kad mhayman neshtjaj
3	li den hade bsiruza y li dmenjon etdin aw men kul bar nash ela afla ena nafshy d'aen na
4	la guer medem bnafshy jshish na ela law bhade esdadkez dayyany guer marya hu
5	metul hana la men kdam savna tehon danin dama dni'ze marya haw dmanhar kasyazeh djeshuja wgale majshvazhon dlebawaza whaden nehwe shuvja lnash nash men alaha
6	halen den ajay metulazjon u samez al parsupa dily wda'palaw dvan ti'lpon dla tezraon yatir men ma dajziv wnash al javreh la netrim metul nash
7	manu guer bsaj aw mana iz laj dla nsavt we'n nsavt lmana meshtavhar at ayj haw dla nsavt
8	men kadu svaton ljon wazarton wveladayn amlejton eshtuf den amlejton d'af jnan namlej amjon
9	savar na guer dlan lashlije jrayye w saman alaha ayj dalmawta dahwayn te'atrawn lalma walmala'je wlavnaynasha
10	jnan shatayya metul mshija aton den jakime bamshija jnan krihe wa'ton jaylzane aton meshtabjin wajnan mestarin
11	dama lhade shaza kafninan washenan wartelainan wmezkapjinan wvez kyama layt lan
12	wla'enan kad paljinan b'iydayn msarin lan wamvarjinan radpin lan wamsaybrinan
13	msajen lan wvaenan menhon ayj nfaza dalma hwayn wjupara djulnash dama lhasha
14	la wa ayj d'avhezjon kazev na halen ela ayj dlavnayya jabive marte na
15	en guer rebu tar'e nehon ljon bamshija ela la saguiyye' avahe byeshu guer mshija ena hu awledtjon basvarza
16	bae na hajil menjon dvi tetdamon
17	metul hana shadrez lwazjon ltimaze'aws d'iyzawy bery jabiva wamhaymna bmarya dhu nahedjon urjazy dvamshija ayj medem dmalef na bjulhen idaza
18	ayj haw den dla aze na lwazjon ezjzarw nasha menjon
19	ela en marya save bagal aze na lwazjon we'da la melazhon dhalen damrimin nafshhon ela jaylhon
20	malkuzeh guer d'alaha la waz bmelza ela bjayla
21	aykana saven ton bjutra ize lwazjon aw bjuba wavruja makijta
