1	we'mar wa yeshu lzalmidawy la meshkja dla ni'zon majshule wa den lhaw dvi'ydeh ni'zon
2	pakaj wa leh elu rajya dajmara talya bsawreh washde byama aw dnajshel ljad men halen sure
3	esdarw bnafshjon en nejte ajuj k'i beh we'n ta'ev shvuk leh
4	we'n shva savnin byawma naskel baj washva savnin byawma nezpne lwazaj wni'mar dza'ev na shvuk leh
5	we'marw shlije lmaran awsef lan haymanuza
6	amar lhon en iz waz ljon haymanuza ayj predta djardla amrin wayton lzuza hana d'ezar we'znasb byama wmeshtma wa ljon
7	manu den menjon d'iyz leh avda ddavar padana aw drae ana we'n ni'ze men jakla amar leh mejda var estak
8	ela amar leh tayyev li medem d'ajshem wa'sur jasayk shameshayny dama d'elas we'shte wvazarken af at telas wzeshte
9	lma taybuzeh mkabel dhaw avda davad medem d'ezpked leh la savar na
10	hajana af aton ma davadton kulhen aylen dafkidan ljon emarw davde jnan batile dmedem djayyavin wayn lmebad vadn
11	wahwa djad asel yeshu lu'wrishlem avar wa bez shamrayye laglila
12	wjad kariv lmeal lakriza jda aru esra nashin garbe wkamw men rujka
13	wa'rimw kalhon wa'mrin raban yeshu ezrajam layn
14	wjad jsa enon emar lhon selw jawaw nafshjon ljahne wjad asin etdakiw
15	jad den menhon kad jsa d'etdaki hfaj leh wavkala rama mshabaj wa la'laha
16	wanfal al apawy kdam reglawy dyeshu kad mawde leh whu hana shamrayya hwa
17	na den yeshu we'mar la wa esra hawen halen d'etdakiw ayka enon tesha
18	lma prashw dni'zon netlon teshbujta la'laha ela hana dmen ama w nujrayya
19	we'mar leh kum sel haymanuzaj ajyazaj
20	wjad sha'lu lyeshu men prishe emazy azya malkuzeh d'alaha na we'mar lhon la azya malkuzeh d'alaha banturaza
21	wla amrin ha harka y wha har taman i ha guer malkuzeh d'alaha lgaw menjon i
22	we'mar lzalmidawy ni'zon yawmaza dzezragrgon lmejsa jad men yawmaza davreh dnasha wla tejson
23	we'n ni'mron ljon ha harka w wha har taman u la ti'sun
24	aykana guer dvarka barek men shmayya wjuleh tjez shmayya manhar hajana nehwe breh dnasha byawmeh
25	lukdam den zid u dnejash saguiyya'za wnestle men sharbza hade
26	wa'ykana dahwa byawmazeh dnuj hajana nehwe byawmazeh davreh dnasha
27	d'ajlin waw wshazen wnasbin neshe wyahbin lgavre dama lyawma dal nuj ljewela we'za tawfana wa'wbed ljul nash
28	wa'ykana tuv dahwa byawmazeh dlut d'ajlin waw wshazen wsavnin wamsabnin wnasbin waw wvanen
29	byawma den danfak lut men sdum amtar marya nura wjevriza men shmayya wa'wbed ljulhon
30	hajana nehwe byawma dmezgle breh dnasha
31	bhaw yawma man dve'gara w wma'nawy bvayta la nejuz dneshkul enon wman davjakla w la nezhfej lvestreh
32	etdarw la'tzeh dlut
33	man dsave dnaje nafsheh nawbdih wman dnawbed nafsheh najeh
34	amar na ljon davhaw lilya tren nehon bajda arsa jad netdvar wajrina neshtvek
35	tarten nehwyan tajnan ajjda jda tetdvar wajriza teshtvek
36	tren nehon bjakla jad netdvar wajrina neshtvek
37	naw wa'mrin leh la'yka maran amar lhon ayka dfagra taman nezkanshon neshre
