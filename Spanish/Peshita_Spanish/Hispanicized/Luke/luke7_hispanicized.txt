1	wjad shalem mele kulhen lmashmazeh dama al yeshu lajfarnajum
2	avdeh den dkentruna jad vid wa bisha'iz ayna dyakir wa lawy wkariv wa lammaz
3	washma al yeshu wshadar lwazeh kashishe dihudayye wvae wa meneh ayj dni'ze naje lavdeh
4	henon den kad ezaw lwaz yeshu baen waw meneh btila'iz wa'mrin shawe w dzebed leh hade
5	rajem guer laman wa'f bez knushta hu bna lan
6	yeshu den asel wa amhon kad den la sagui rajik men bayta shadar lwazeh kentruna rajmawy we'mar leh mary la temal la guer shawe na dzeul tjez matlaly
7	metul haw ena la shwiz dalwazaj ize ela emar bmelza wneza'se tali
8	af ena guer gavra na damshabad na tjez shultana wi'yz tjez iday estratiyyute wa'mar na lhana dsel wa'sel wlajrina dza wa'ze walavdy ved hade waved
9	kad shma den yeshu halen etdamar beh we'zpni we'mar ljensha d'aze bazreh amar na ljon d'af la bez israyyel eshkjez ayj hade haymanuza
10	wahfajw hanon d'eshtadarw lvayta we'shkajw lavda haw dajrih hwa kad jlim
11	wahwa lyawma dvazreh asel wa lamdita dashmah na'in wzalmidawy ameh wjensha saguiyya'
12	wjad krev lzara damdita jsa kad mlawen miza dijidayya wa le'meh whi emeh armalta hwaz wjensha saguiyya' davnay mdita amah
13	jsah den yeshu we'zrajam leh we'mar lah la tevken
14	we'sal krev larsa whanon dashkilin waw leh kamw wa'mar layma laj amar na kum
15	wizev haw miza wshari lammalalu wyahbeh le'meh
16	we'jdaz dejlza lnasha kulhon wamshabjin waw la'laha wa'mrin danviyya raba kam ban wasar alaha lameh
17	wnefkaz lawy melza hade bjulah ihud wavjuleh azra dajdarayhon
18	we'shtaiw lyujanan talmidawy halen kulhen
19	wakra yujanan lazren men talmidawy wshadar enon lwaz yeshu we'mar at u haw d'aze aw lajrin u msaken nan
20	we'zaw lwaz yeshu wa'mrin leh yujanan mamdana shadran lwazaj wa'mar at u haw d'aze aw lajrin u msaken nan
21	bah den bha shaza saguiyye' asi men kurhane wmen majwaza wmen ruje bishaza walsaguiyye' smayya yav wa lmejsa
22	wana yeshu we'mar lhon selw emarw lyujanan kulmedem dajsayton washmaton dasmayya jasen wajguire mhaljin wgarbe metdaken wjarshe shamin wmize kamin wmeskine mestabrin
23	wtuvawy lman dla nezkshel bi
24	kad den esalw talmidawy dyujanan shari lmi'mar ljenshe al yujanan mana nfakton ljurba lmejsa kanya dmen ruja mettsi
25	we'la mana nfakton lmejsa gavra dnajte rakije lvish ha aylen dvalvushe mshabje wavfunake izayhon bez malke enon
26	we'la mana nfakton lmejsa nviyya in amar na ljon wyatir men nviyye
27	hanaw dalawy kziv dha ena mshadar na mala'jy kdam parsupaj dnazken urja kdamayk
28	amar na ljon dlayt nviyya biliday neshe drab men yujanan mamdana sura den bmalkuza d'alaha rav u meneh
29	wjuleh ama dashmaw af majse sadekw la'laha damadw mamudizeh dyujanan
30	prishe den wsafre tlamw bnafshhon sevyana d'alaha dla ezmedw meneh
31	lman hajil edame lnasha dsharbza hade walman damen
32	damen latlayye dyazbin bshuka wkaen ljavrayhon wa'mrin smarn ljon wla rakedton we'layn ljon wla bjayton
33	eza guer yujanan mamdana la ajel lajma wla shaze jamra wa'mrin ton shi'da iz beh
34	eza breh dnasha ajel wshaze wa'mrin ton ha gavra akula wshaze jamra wrajma dmajse wadjatayye
35	we'sdadkaz jejmza men kulhon bneh
36	eza den ba meneh jad men prishe dnelas ameh wal lvayteh dafrisha haw we'stmej
37	wa'tza jatata iz waz bamdita ha wjad yedaz davvayteh dafrisha haw smij nesbaz shatifta dvesma
38	wkamaz bestreh lwaz reglawy wvajya waz wsharyaz bdemeh msaba reglawy wavsara drishah mshawen lhen wamnashka waz reglawy wmashja besma
39	kad jsa den prisha haw dakra ezjashav bnafsheh we'mar hana elu nviyya hwa yada wa man i wma tebah djatata y atza ha dkerbaz leh
40	na den yeshu we'mar leh shemon medem iz li d'imar laj hu den emar leh emar rabi amar leh yeshu
41	tren jayyave iz waw ljad mare jawba jad jayyav wa dinare jameshma' wajrina dinare jamshin
42	wadlayt wa lhon lmefra lazrayhon shvak ayna hajil menhon yatir najviwy
43	na shemon we'mar savar na dhaw d'eshtvek leh sagui amar leh yeshu trisa'iz dant
44	we'zpni lwaz ha atza we'mar lshemon jase at atza hade lvaytaj elez mayya lreglay la yavt hade den bdemeh reglay savaz wavsarah shawyaz enen
45	at la nshaktany hade den ha men delaz la shelyaz reglay lamnashaku
46	at meshja lrishy la mshajt hade den bmeshja dvesma reglay meshjaz
47	jlaf hade amar na laj dashvikin lah jtaheh saguiyye' metul d'ajvaz sagui haw den dkalil meshtvek leh kalil majev
48	we'mar lha atza shvikin lejy jtahayky
49	shariw den hanon dasmijin amrin bnafshhon manu hana d'af jtahe shavek
50	yeshu den emar lha atza haymanuzejy ajyazejy sely bashlama
