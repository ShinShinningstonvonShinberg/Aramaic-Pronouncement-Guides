1	jar den yeshu batire aylen dramen waw bez gasa kurbanayhon
2	wajsa af armalta jda meskinta d'armyaz shamune tren
3	we'mar shrara amar na ljon dhade armalta meskinta armyaz yatir men kulnash
4	kulhon guer halen men ma dyatir wa lhon armiw bez kurbana d'alaha hade den men jasiruzah kul dkanya waz armyazeh
5	wjad amrin waw nashin al haykla davji'fe shapiraza wavkurbane msabaz emar lhon yeshu
6	halen djasen ton ni'zon yawmaza davhon la teshtvek ki'f al ki'f dla testzar
7	wamsha'lin waw leh wa'mrin malfana emazy halen nehwyan wmana y aza ma dkarivan halen dnehwyan
8	hu den emar lhon jsaw lma teton saguiyye' guer ni'zon bshemy wni'mron d'ena na mshija wsavna krev la den ti'sun bazarhon
9	wma dshamin ton krave washgushye la tedjlon zidan enen guer halen lukdam lmehwa ela la dakil mtaz jarza
10	nkum guer ama al ama wmalku al malku
11	wsawe rawrve nehon bduka duka wjafne wmawtane wnehwyan dejlaza wsurade wa'zwaza rawrvaza men shmayya nezjasyan wsazwe rawrve nehon
12	kdam den halen kulhen narmon laykon idayya wnerdfunajon wnashlmunajon lajnushaza walvez asire wankarvunajon kdam malke whigmune metul shemy
13	hawya ljon den lsahduza
14	simw den blebjon dla tehon mezyalfin lmapak ruja
15	ena guer etel ljon puma wjejmza ayda dla neshkjon lamkam lkuvlah kulhon beldvavaykon
16	nashlmunajon den avahaykon wa'jaykon wajyanaykon wrajmaykon wanmizon menjon
17	wzehon sni'in men kul nash metul shemy
18	wmenza men rishjon la ti'vad
19	bamsaybranuzjon den teknon nafshjon
20	ma den dajsayton lu'wrishlem dajdirin lah jayla haden daw dakrev leh jurbah
21	haden aylen dvihud enon nerkon ltura wa'ylen davgawah enon nerkon wdavkurya la nelon lah
22	dyawmaza enon halen dazvaza dneshlam kul ma dajziv
23	wa den la'ylen dvatnan wla'ylen dmaynkan bhanon yawmaza nehwe guer ulsana raba b'ara wrugsa al ama hana
24	wneflon bfuma djarba wneshtvon ljul azar w'urishlem tehwe metdisha men amme dama dneshlmon savne damme
25	wnehwyan azwaza bshemsha wavsahra wavjawkve wva'ra ulsana damme wfushaj idayya men tawhza dkala dyama
26	wsawa dmapek nafshaza davnaynasha men dejlza dmedem dazid lme'za al ara wnettsion jayle dashmayya
27	whaden nejsuna lavreh dnasha d'aze banane am jayla saguiyya' wshuvja raba
28	ma den dshari halen dnehwyan ezlabvw wa'rimw rishaykon metul dakrev leh purkanjon
29	we'mar wa lhon mazla jsaw lzita waljulhon ilane
30	dma dmafrin mejda menhon mestaklin ton dakrev leh kayta
31	hajana af aton ma dajsayton halen dhawyan daw dkariva y malkuza d'alaha
32	amin amar na ljon dla tebar sharbza hade dama dhalen kulhen nehwyan
33	shmayya wa'ra nebron wmelay la nebran
34	esdarw den bnafshjon dla mzum ni'kron lebawazjon b'asutuza wavrawayyuza wavsefza dalma wmen shelya ni'ze laykon yawma haw
35	ayj safajza guer nespaj al kulhon aylen dyazbin al apeh djulah ara
36	hwaw hajil shahrin bjulsvan wamsalen dzeshon lmerak men halen dazidan lmehwa wazkumon kdam breh dnasha
37	b'iymama den malef wa bhaykla wavlilya nafek wa b'aez btura dmezkre dvez sayte
38	wjuleh ama mkadmin waw lwazeh lhaykla lmeshma melzeh
