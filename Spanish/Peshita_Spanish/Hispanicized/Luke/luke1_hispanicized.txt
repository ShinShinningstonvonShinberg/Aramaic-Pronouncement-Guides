1	metul dsaguiyye' svaw dnajtvon tashyaza dsurane aylen dajnan mpasin nan bhon
2	ayj medem d'ashlemw lan hanon dmen kdim hwaw jasayye wamshamshane dilah dmelza
3	ezjsi af li metul dkariv wiz yasifa'iz ljulhon djul medem btekseh ejtuv laj nasija te'awfile
4	dzeda shrara dmele d'ettalmadt lhen
5	hwa byawmazeh dherawdes malka dihuda kahna jad dashmeh wa sjarya men teshmeshta dvez aviyya wa'tzeh men bnazeh d'ahron shmah wa elishva
6	trayhon den sadikin waw kdam alaha wamhaljin bjulhon pukdanawy wavji'nuzeh dmarya dla edla
7	bra den layt wa lhon metul d'elishva karza waz wazrayhon saguiyya'y byawmazhon waw
8	hwa wa den kad mjahen wa bteksa dzeshmeshteh kdam alaha
9	bayada djahnuza mta dansim besme wal lhaykleh dmarya
10	wjuleh kensha dama msale wa lvar bedana dvesme
11	we'zjsi leh lasjarya mala'ja dmarya dka'em men yamina dmadbja dvesme
12	we'shtguesh sjarya kad jsa wdejlza neflaz lawy
13	we'mar leh mala'ja la tedjal sjarya metul d'eshtamaz sluzaj wa'tzaj elishva ti'lad laj bra wzekre shmeh yujanan
14	wzehwe laj jaduza wa'rwasa wsaguiyye' nejdon bmawladeh
15	nehwe guer rav kdam marya wjamra wshajra la neshte wruja dkudsha nezmle ad hu bjarsa d'emeh
16	wsaguiyye' men bnay israyyel nafne lwaz marya alahhon
17	whu ni'sal kdamawy bruja wavjayla d'iliyya nviyya dnafne leba d'avahe al bnayya wla'ylen dla mettpisin lidaza dji'ne wantayyev lmarya ama gmira
18	we'mar sjarya lmala'ja aykana eda hade ena guer izay sava wa'tazy saguiyya'z byawmazah i
19	wana mala'ja we'mar leh ena na gavri'yel dka'em na kdam alaha we'shtaljez d'emalel amaj we'sabraj halen
20	mekil tehwe shatik wla teshkaj lammalalu dama lyawma dhalen nehwyan al dla hayment lmelay halen dmezmalyan bsavnhen
21	ama den ka'em wa wamsake lasjarya wzamihin hwaw al tawjarteh davhaykla
22	kad nfak den sjarya la meshkaj wa danmalel amhon we'stakalw djeswa jsa bhaykla whu mermas rames wa lhon wkawi kad jresh
23	wjad ezmaliw yawmaza dzeshmeshteh esal lvayteh
24	wahwa men bazar yawmaza hanon betnaz elishva atzeh wamtashya waz nafshah yarje jamsha wa'mra waz
25	dhalen vad li marya byawmaza djar bi lmesav jesdy dvez bnay nasha
26	byarja den deshta eshtlaj gavri'yel mala'ja men lwaz alaha laglila lamdita dashmah nasraz
27	lwaz bzulta damjira lgavra dashmeh yawsef men bayteh ddawid washmah lavzulta maryam
28	wal lwazah mala'ja we'mar lah shlam lejy malyaz taybuza maran amejy brijaz bneshe
29	hi den kad jsaz ezrahbaz bmelzeh wmezjashva waz dmana w shlama hana
30	we'mar lah mala'ja la tedjlin maryam eshkajty guer taybuza lwaz alaha
31	ha guer tkablin batna wzi'ldin bra wzekren shmeh yeshu
32	hana nehwe rav wavreh delayya nezkre wnetel leh marya alaha kursyeh ddawid avu
33	wnamlej al bayteh dyakuv lalam walmalkuzeh suf la nehwe
34	amra maryam lmala'ja aykana tehwe hade dgavra la jjim li
35	na mala'ja we'mar lah ruja dkudsha ti'ze wjayleh delayya naguen layky metul hana haw dmeziled bejy kadisha hu wavreh d'alaha nezkre
36	wha elishva ajyantejy af hi batna bra bsaybuzah whana yarja deshta lah lha dmezkarya karza
37	metul dla tel la'laha medem
38	amra maryam ha ena amzeh dmarya nehwe li ayj melzaj we'sal mala'ja men lwazah
39	kamaz den maryam bhon byawmaza hanon we'saz btila'iz ltura lamdita dihud
40	welaz lvayteh dasjarya wshe'laz shlamah d'elishva
41	wahwa djad shemaz elishva shlamah dmaryam das ula bjarsah we'zmalyaz elishva ruja dkudsha
42	wakaz bkala rama we'mraz lmaryam mvarajta aty bneshe wamvaraj u pi'ra davjarsejy
43	aymeka li hade d'emeh dmary ti'ze lwazy
44	ha guer kad nfal kala dashlamejy b'ednay bjaduza rabza das ula bjarsy
45	wtuveh la'yda dhaymnaz dhawe shulama la'ylen d'ezmalel amah men lwaz marya
46	we'mraz maryam mawrva nafshy lmarya
47	wjedyaz rujy b'alaha majyany
48	djar bmujaja d'amzeh ha guer men hasha tuva netlan li sharbaza kulhen
49	davad lwazy rawrvaza haw djaylzan wkadish shmeh
50	wajnaneh ldare wsharbaza al aylen ddajlin leh
51	vad sajuza badraeh wvadar jziray bzariza dlebhon
52	sajef takife men kursawaza wa'rim makije
53	kafne saba tavaza watire shra sfika'iz
54	adar li'ysrayyel avdeh we'tdjar jnaneh
55	ayj dmalel am avahayn am avraham wam sareh lalam
56	kawyaz den maryam lwaz elishva ayj yarje tlaza whefkaz lvaytah
57	elishva den hwa wa lah savna dzi'lad wyeldaz bra
58	washmaw shvaveh wavnay tuhmah d'asgui alaha jnaneh lwazah wjaden waw amah
59	wahwa lyawma dazmanya we'zaw lmegsreh ltalya wkaren waw leh bashma d'avu sjarya
60	wanaz emeh wa'mra lhon la hajana ela nezkre yujanan
61	we'marw lah dlayt nash bsharbzejy dmezkre bashma hana
62	warmasw la'vu d'aykana save danshamewy
63	wash'el penkiza wajzav we'mar yujanan u shmeh we'tdamarw kulnash
64	wmejda ezpzaj pumeh wleshaneh wmalel wvarej la'laha
65	wahwaz dejlza al kulhon shvavayhon wavjuleh tura dihud halen mezmallan way
66	wjulhon dashmaw mezjashvin waw blebhon wa'mrin mana kay nehwe talya hana wi'ydeh dmarya iz waz ameh
67	we'zmli sjarya avu ruja dkudsha we'znabi we'mar
68	mvaraj u marya alaheh d'iysrayyel dasar ameh wavad leh purkana
69	wa'kim lan karna dfurkana bvayteh ddawid avdeh
70	ayj dmalel bfuma danviyyawy kadishe dmen alam
71	dnefrkan men beldvavayn wmen ida djulhon sana'yn
72	wavad jnaneh am avahayn wehad ldiyyazikawy kadishaza
73	wmawmaza dima la'vraham avon dnetel lan
74	dnezprek men ida daveldvavayn wadla dejla nefluj kdamawy
75	kulhon yawmazan bji'nuza wavsadikuza
76	wa't talya nviyyeh delayya tezkre ti'sal guer kdam parsupeh dmarya daztayyev urjeh
77	dnetel mada djayye lameh bshuvkana dajtahayhon
78	brajme dajnana d'alahan davhon nesran denja men rawma
79	lmanharu la'ylen davjeshuja wavtelale dmawta yazbin dnezrus reglayn b'uwrja dashlama
80	talya den rave wa wmezjayyal bruja wavjurba izawy wa dama lyawma dzajwizeh dalwaz israyyel
