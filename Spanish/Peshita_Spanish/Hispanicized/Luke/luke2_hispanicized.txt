1	hwa den byawmaza hanon wanfak pukdana men agustaws kesar dnezkzev kuleh ama d'uwjdaneh
2	hade majtvanuza kadmata hwaz bhigmanuza dkurinaws bsuriyya
3	wa'sel wa kulnash dnezkzev bamditeh
4	slek wa den af yawsef men nasraz mdita daglila lihud lamditeh ddawid dmezkarya bez-ljem metul d'iyzawy wa men bayteh wmen sharbzeh ddawid
5	am maryam mjirteh kad batna dzaman nezkzev
6	wahwa djad taman enon ezmaliw yawmazah dzi'lad
7	wyeldaz brah bujra wajrajteh basrure wa'rmyazeh b'uwrya metul dlayt wa lhon dukza ayka dashren waw
8	raawaza den iz waw beh b'azra dashren waw taman wnatrin matarza dlilya al maryazhon
9	wha mala'ja d'alaha eza lwazhon wzeshbujteh dmarya anhraz layhon wadjelw dejlza rabza
10	we'mar lhon mala'ja la tedjlon ha guer msabar na ljon jaduza rabza dzehwe ljuleh alma
11	eziled ljon guer yawmana paruka d'iyzawy marya mshija bamditeh ddawid
12	whade ljon aza meshkjin ton ula dajrij basrure wsim b'uwrya
13	wmen shely ezjsiw am mala'ja jaylawaza saguiyye' dashmayya kad mshabjin la'laha wa'mrin
14	teshbujta la'laha bamrawme wal ara shlama wsavra tava lavnay nasha
15	wahwa djad esalw men lwazhon mala'je lashmayya malelw raawaza jad am jad wa'mrin nerde dama lvez-ljem wnejse lmelza hade dahwaz ayj dmarya awda lan
16	we'zaw msarhva'iz we'shkajw lmaryam walyawsef walula dsim b'uwrya
17	wjad jsaw awdaw lmelza d'ezmallaz amhon lawy al talya
18	wjulhon dashmaw etdamarw al aylen d'ezmalel lhon men raawaza
19	maryam den natra waz kulhen mele halen wamfajma blebah
20	wahfajw raawaza hanon kad mshabjin wamhallin la'laha al kul dajsaw washmaw aykana d'ezmalal amhon
21	wjad mlaw tmanya yawmin dnezgsar talya ezkri shmeh yeshu d'ezkri men mala'ja kdam dnezbten bjarsa
22	wjad ezmaliw yawmaza dzadkizhon ayj namusa dmushe asku lu'wrishlem dankimuna kdam marya
23	ayj dajziv bnamusa dmarya djul dejra pazaj marba kadisha dmarya nezkre
24	wadnetlon devjza aykana d'amir bnamusa dmarya sawga dshufnine aw tren parugue dyawna
25	gavra den jad iz wa b'uwrishlem shmeh hwa shemon wgavra hana ki'yn wa wsadik wamsake wa lvuyya'eh d'iysrayyel wruja dkudsha iz waz lawy
26	wa'mir wa leh men ruja dkudsha dla nejse mawta dama dnejse lamshijeh dmarya
27	hu hana eza wa bruja lhaykla wjad malin leh avahawy lyeshu talya dnebdon jlafawy aykana dafkid bnamusa
28	kableh al draawy wvarej la'laha we'mar
29	mekil shara at leh lavdaj mary ayj melzaj bashlama
30	dha jsay aynay jnanaj
31	haw dtayyevt bparsupa djulhen emwaza
32	nuhra lguelyana damme wshuvja lamaj israyyel
33	yawsef den we'meh tamihin waw al aylen dmezmallan way lawy
34	wvarej enon shemon we'mar lmaryam emeh ha hana sim lmapulta wlakyama dsaguiyye' b'iysrayyel wla'za djeryana
35	wavnafshejy den dilejy tebar rumja ayj dnezgalyan majshvaza dlebawaza dsaguiyye'
36	wjana den nviza barzeh dafnu'il men shavta d'ashir af hi kashishaz byawmazah waz washva shnin am balah jyaz men bzuluzah
37	wahwaz armalta ayj shnin tmani'yn wa'rba wla parka waz men haykla wavsawma wvasluza palja waz b'iymama wavlilya
38	wa'f hi kamaz bah bshaza wa'wdyaz lmarya wammalla waz lawy am kulnash damsake wa lfurkanah d'uwrishlem
39	wjad shalemw kul medem ayj davnamusa dmarya hfajw laglila lnasraz mdithon
40	talya den rave wa wmezjayyal bruja wmezmale jejmza wtaybuza d'alaha iz waz lawy
41	wnashawy bjul shna asin waw lu'wrishlem badida dfesja
42	wjad hwa bar shnin tartaesre slekw aykana damadin waw ladida
43	wjad shlemw yawmaza hfajw lhon yeshu den talya pash leh b'uwrishlem wyawsef we'meh la idaw
44	savrin waw guer dam bnay lwizhon u wjad ezaw marde yawma jad ba'u lwaz nashuzhon walwaz man dyada lhon
45	wla eshkju wahfajw lhon tuv lu'wrishlem wvaen waw leh
46	wmen bazar tlaza yawmin eshkju bhaykla kad yazev mesaz malfane wshama menhon wamsha'el lhon
47	wzamihin hwaw kulhon aylen dshamin waw leh bjejmzeh wavfezgamawy
48	wjad jsa'u tmahw we'mraz leh emeh bery lmana vadt lan hajana dha avuj we'na bturafa saguiyya' baen hwayn laj
49	amar lhon mana baen hwayton li la yadin ton dvez avy wale li d'ehwe
50	henon den la eshtawdaw lmelza d'emar lhon
51	wanjez amhon we'za lnasraz wmeshtabad wa lhon emeh den natra waz kulhen mele blebah
52	yeshu den rave wa bkawmzeh wavjejmzeh wavtaybuza lwaz alaha wavnaynasha
