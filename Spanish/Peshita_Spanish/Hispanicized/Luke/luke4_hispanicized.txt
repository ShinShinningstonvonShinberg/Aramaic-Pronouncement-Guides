1	yeshu den kad mle ruja dkudsha hfaj men yurdnan wadvarzeh ruja ljurba
2	yawmaza arbin dneznase men ajelkarsa wla les medem bhanon yawmaza wjad shalem enon ljarza kfen
3	we'mar leh ajelkarsa en breh at d'alaha emar lji'fa hade dzehwe lajma
4	na yeshu we'mar leh kziv w dla wa blajma baljud jayye barnasha ela bjul pezgam d'alaha
5	wa'skeh satana ltura rama wjawyeh kulhen malkwaza d'ara bedana sura
6	we'mar leh ajelkarsa laj etel shultana hana kuleh wshuvjeh dli mashlam walman d'esbe yahev na leh
7	en hajil tesgud kdamay dilaj nehwe kuleh
8	na yeshu we'mar leh kziv w dalmarya alahaj tesgud wleh baljudawy tefluj
9	wa'ytyeh lu'wrishlem wa'kimeh al kenfa dhaykla we'mar leh en breh at d'alaha arma nafshaj meka lzajt
10	kziv guer dalmala'jawy nfaked layk dannatrunaj
11	wal draayhon neshklunaj dla tetkel reglaj bji'fa
12	na den yeshu we'mar leh amir w dla tnase lmarya alahaj
13	wjad shalem ajelkarsa kulhon nesyunawy prak men lwazeh ad savna
14	wahfaj yeshu bjayla druja laglila wanfak lawy teba bjuleh azra dajdarayhon
15	whu malef wa bajnushazhon wmeshtabaj wa men kul nash
16	we'za lnasraz ayka d'ezrabi wal aykana damad wa lajnushta byawma dshabza wkam lmekra
17	we'zihev leh sefra d'eshaya nviyya wafzaj yeshu sefra we'shkaj dukza ayka dajziv
18	rujeh dmarya lay wmetul hade mashjany lamsabaru lmeskine wshaljany lma'sayyu lazviray leba walmajrasu lashvayya shuvkana wlawire jsayya wlamshararu lazvire bshuvkana
19	walmajrasu shata mkabalta lmarya
20	wajraj sefra wyahbeh lamshamshana we'sal izev kulhon den dvajnushta aynayhon jaran way beh
21	wshari lmi'mar lwazhon dyawmana eshtalam kzava hana b'ednaykon
22	wsahdin hwaw leh kulhon wmetdamrin waw bmele dtaybuza dnafkan way men pumeh wa'mrin waw la wa hana bar yawsef
23	amar lhon yeshu kvar ti'mron li mazla hana asya asa nafshaj wjul dashman davadt bajfarnajum ved af harka bamditaj
24	w den emar amin amar na ljon dlayt nviyya dmezkabal bamditeh
25	shrara guer amar na ljon dsagui armlaza iz way b'iysrayyel byawmay iliyya nviyya kad ettjedw shmayya shnin tlaz wyarje shta wahwa kafna raba bjulah ara
26	walwaz jda menhen la eshtadar iliyya ela lsarpaz dsaydan lwaz atza armalta
27	wsaguiyye' garbe iz waw bez israyyel byawmay elisha nviyya wjad menhon la etdaki ela en naman armayya
28	wjad shmaw halen hanon dvajnushta ezmliw jemta kulhon
29	wkamw apku lvar men mdita wa'ytyu dama lagvina dtura haw damdithon banya waz lawy dneshduna men shkifa
30	hu den var baynazhon we'sal
31	wanjez lajfarnajum mdita daglila wmalef wa lhon bshabe
32	wzamihin hwaw byulpaneh damshalta waz melzeh
33	wi'yz wa bajnushta gavra d'iyz wa beh ruja dshi'da tanfa wasak bkala rama
34	we'mar shvukayny ma lan wlaj yeshu nasrayya ezayt lmawbaduzan yada na laj man at kadisheh d'alaha
35	waj'a beh yeshu we'mar sjur pumaj wfuk meneh washda shi'da bamsaza wanfak meneh kad la sraj beh medem
36	wzemha raba ejad ljulnash wammallin waw am jdade wa'mrin mana y kay melza hade davshultana wavjayla pakda lruje tanfaza wnafkan
37	wanfak lawy teba bjuleh azra dajdarayhon
38	wjad nfak yeshu men knushta al lvayteh dshemon wajmazeh dshemon alisa waz b'eshaza rabza wavaw meneh metulazah
39	wkam lel menah waj'a b'eshazah washvakzah wmejda kamaz wamshamsha waz lhon
40	maravay shemsha den kulhon aylen d'iyz wa lhon krihe dajrihin bjurhane mshajlfe aytiw enon lwazeh hu den al jad jad menhon ideh sa'em wa wma'se wa lhon
41	wnafkin waw af shi'de men saguiyye' kad maskin wa'mrin d'at u mshija breh d'alaha wja'e wa bhon wla shavek wa lhon dni'mron dyadin dhuyyu mshija
42	walsafreh dyawma nfak esal leh la'zra jurba wjenshe baen waw leh we'zaw dama lwazeh wa'jdu dla ni'sal leh men lwazhon
43	hu den yeshu emar lhon d'af lamdinaza jranyaza wale li lamsabaru malkuzeh d'alaha dal hade w eshtadrez
44	whu majres wa bajnushaza daglila
