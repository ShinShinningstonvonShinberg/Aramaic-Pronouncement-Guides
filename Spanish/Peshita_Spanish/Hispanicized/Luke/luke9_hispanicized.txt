1	wakra yeshu lazresarteh wyav lhon jayla wshultana al kulhon shi'de wjurhane lma'sayyu
2	wshadar enon lmajrasu malkuzeh d'alaha walma'sayyu krihe
3	we'mar lhon medem la teshklon lu'wrja la shavta wla tarmala wla lajma wla kespa wla tarten kutinyan nehwyan ljon
4	wla'yna bayta da'lin ton leh taman hwaw wmen taman pukw
5	walman dla mkablin ljon ma dnafkin ton men mdita ha af jela men reglaykon pesw layhon lsahduza
6	wanfakw shlije wmezkarkin waw bkurya wvamdinaza wamsabrin waw wma'sen bjul duk
7	shma den herawdes tetrarja kulhen dhawyan way b'iydeh wmetdamar wa metul d'amrin waw nashin dyujanan kam men bez mize
8	jrane den amrin waw d'iliyya ezjsi wajrane danviyya men nviyye kadmayye kam
9	we'mar herawdes risheh dyujanan ena peskez manu den hana dhalen shama na lawy wsave wa dnejsewy
10	wjad hfajw shlije eshtaiw lyeshu kulmedem davadw wadvar enon baljudayhon la'zra jurba dvez-sayyada
11	kenshe den kad idaw esalw bazreh wkabel enon wammalel wa amhon al malkuza d'alaha wla'ylen dasnikin waw al asyuza ma'se wa
12	kad den shri yawma lmesla krevw talmidawy wa'mrin leh shri ljenshe dni'sun lkurya dajdarayn waljafrune dneshron bhon wneshkjon lhon saybarta metul dva'zra jurba izayn
13	amar lhon yeshu havw lhon aton lme'jal henon den amrin layt lan yatir men jamsha lajmin wazren nunin ela en esalnan wasvanan saybarta lhana kuleh ama
14	hawen waw guer ayj jamsha alfin gavrin amar lhon yeshu asmejw enon smaje jamshin nashin basmaja
15	wavadw hajwaz talmide wa'smejw ljulhon
16	wansav yeshu hanon jamsha lajmin wazren nunin wjar bashmayya wvarej waksa wyav lzalmidawy dansimon ljenshe
17	we'jalw kulhon wasvaw washkalw ksayye medem d'awtarw tresar kufinin
18	wjad msale baljudawy wzalmidawy ameh sha'el enon we'mar manu amrin lay kenshe d'iyzay
19	naw wa'mrin leh dyujanan mamdana wajrane d'iliyya jrane den danviyya jad men nviyye kadmayye kam
20	amar lhon aton den manu amrin ton d'iyzay na shemon we'mar mshijeh d'alaha
21	hu den k'a bhon wsahar enon dhade lnash la ni'mron
22	we'mar lhon dazid u breh dnasha dsaguiyya'za nejash wadnestle men kashishe wrabay kahne wsafre wnektluna walyawma dazlaza nkum
23	wa'mar wa kdam kulnash man dsave dni'ze bazary nejpur bnafsheh wneshkul skifeh kulyum wni'ze bazary
24	man guer dsave dnafsheh naje mawbed lah man den dnawbed nafsheh metulazy hana maje lah
25	mana guer nezadar bar nasha dni'zar alma kuleh nafsheh den nawbed aw nejsar
26	man dnevhaz bi den wavmelay nevhaz beh breh dnasha ma d'aze bshuvja d'avu am mala'jawy kadishe
27	shrara amar na ljon d'iyz nasha dkamin harka dla netmon mawta dama dnejson malkuzeh d'alaha
28	hwa den bazar mele halen ayj tmanya yawmin dvar yeshu lshemon walyakuv walyujanan waslek ltura lamsalayyu
29	wjad hu msale ezjalaf jeswa d'apawy wnajtawy jwarw wmavrkin waw
30	wha tren gavrin mmallin waw ameh d'iyzayhon mushe wi'liyya
31	d'ezjsiw bzeshbujta amrin waw den al mapkaneh dazid wa dneshtalam b'uwrishlem
32	wikarw waw lhon bshenza shemon whanon dameh walmajsen ettirw wajsaw shuvjeh walhanon tren nashin dkamin waw lwazeh
33	wjad shariw lmefrash meneh emar shemon lyeshu rabi shapir u lan dharka nehwe wnebed tlaz matlin laj jda walmushe jda wli'liyya jda wla yada wa mana amar
34	wjad amar halen hwaz nana wa'tlaz layhon wadjelw kad jsaw lmushe wli'liyya dalw banana
35	wkala hwa men nana d'amar hanaw bery jabiva leh shmaw
36	wjad hwa kala eshtjaj yeshu baljudawy whenon shzekw walnash la emarw bhanon yawmaza medem dajsaw
37	wahwa lyawma dvazreh kad najtin men tura pga bhon kensha saguiyya'
38	wgavra jad men kensha haw ka we'mar malfana bae na menaj ezpany lay bery dijidayya w li
39	wruja adya lawy wmen shelya kae wamjarek shenawy wmarez walmajsen parka meneh ma dashjakzeh
40	waviz men talmidayk dnapkuna wla eshkajw
41	na den yeshu we'mar on sharbza dla mhaymna wamakalta dama le'mazy ehwe lwazjon we'saybarjon kareva lja lavraj
42	wjad mkarev leh armyeh daywa haw wmaseh waj'a yeshu bruja ha tanfza wa'syeh ltalya wyahbeh la'vu
43	we'tdamarw kulhon brabuzeh d'alaha wjad kulnash metdamar wa al kul daved yeshu emar lzalmidawy
44	simw aton mele halen b'ednaykon breh guer dnasha zid dneshtlem b'iyday bnay nasha
45	henon den la eshtawduh lmelza hade metul damjasya waz menhon dla nedunah wdajlin waw dansha'luna leh al melza hade
46	welaz bhon majshavta dmanu kay rav bhon
47	yeshu den ida majshavta dlebhon wansav talya wa'kimeh lwazeh
48	we'mar lhon man damkabel talya ayj hana bshemy li u mkabel wman dli mkabel mkabel lman dshadrany ayna guer dasur bjuljon hana nehwe rav
49	wana yujanan we'mar raban jsayn nash dmapek daywe bashmaj wajlayna al dla eza aman bazraj
50	amar lhon yeshu la tejlon man guer dla wa lukvaljon jlafaykon u
51	wahwa djad mezmalen yawmaza dsulakeh azken parsupeh dni'sal lu'wrishlem
52	wshadar mala'je kdam parsupeh we'salw alw lakriza dshamrayye ayj dnazknon leh
53	wla kablu metul dparsupeh lu'wrishlem sim wa lmi'sal
54	wjad jsaw yakuv wyujanan talmidawy amrin leh maran save at dni'mar wzejuz nura men shmayya watsif enon ayj d'af iliyya vad
55	we'zpni waj'a bhon we'mar la yadin ton d'ayda aton ruja
56	breh guer dnasha la eza lmawbadu nafshaza ela lmajayyu we'salw lhon lakriza jriza
57	wjad asin b'uwrja emar leh nash ize bazraj la'zar d'asel at mary
58	amar leh yeshu lzale neke iz lhon walfarajza dashmayya matlla lavreh den dnasha layt leh ayka dnesmuj risheh
59	we'mar lajrina ta bazary hu den emar leh mary apes li lukdam isal ekbur avy
60	amar leh yeshu shvuk mize kavrin mizayhon wa't sel sabar malkuzeh d'alaha
61	amar leh jrina ize bazraj mary lukdam den apes li isal eshalem lavnay bayty wi'ze
62	amar leh yeshu la nash rame ideh al jarba dfadana wja'ar lvestreh wjashaj lmalkuzeh d'alaha
