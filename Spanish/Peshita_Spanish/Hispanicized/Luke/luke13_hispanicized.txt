1	bhaw den savna ezaw nashin emarw leh al glilayye hanon dfilataws jlat demhon am devjayhon
2	wana yeshu we'mar lhon savrin ton dhalen glilayye jatain waw yatir men kulhon glilayye dhajana hwa enon
3	la amar na ljon den d'af kuljon ela tzuvon hajana ti'vdon
4	aw hanon tmantasar danfal layhon magdla bshiluja wkatel enon savrin ton djatain waw yatir men kulhon bnaynasha damrin b'uwrishlem
5	la amar na ljon den d'ela tzuvon kuljon ajwazhon ti'vdon
6	we'mar mazla hana tita iz waz lnash dansiva bjarmeh we'za ba bah pi're wla eshkaj
7	we'mar lfalaja ha tlaz shnin aze na bae na pi're bzita hade wla meshkaj na psukeh lmana mvatla ara
8	amar leh palaja mary shvukeh af hade shata ad efljih we'sablih
9	we'n evdaz pi're we'la lmanjay tefskih
10	kad den malef yeshu bshabza bajda men knushaza
11	iz waz taman atza d'iyz waz lah ruja djurhana shnin tmanaesre wajfifa hwaz wla meshkja waz dzezpshet lagmar
12	jsah den yeshu wakrah we'mar lah atza sharyaty men kurhanejy
13	wsam ideh leh wmejda ezpashtaz wshabjaz la'laha
14	na den rab knushta kad mezjamaz al d'asi bshabza yeshu we'mar ljenshe shta enon yawmin davhon wale lmeflaj bhon hwayton azen meza'sen wla byawma dshabza
15	yeshu den na we'mar leh nasev b'ape jad jad menjon bshabza la share tawreh aw jmareh men urya wa'sel mashke
16	hade den dvarzeh i d'avraham wa'srah ajelkarsa ha tmanaesre shnin la wale wa dzeshtre men hana asurya byawma dshabza
17	wjad halen amar wa bahtin waw kulhon aylen dkamin waw lkuvleh wjuleh ama jade wa bjulhen tmihaza dhawyan way b'iydeh
18	emar wa den yeshu lmana damya malkuza d'alaha walmana edamih
19	damya lafredta djardla ha dansav gavra armyah bganzeh warvaz wahwaz ilana raba wfarajza dashmayya aknaz bsawkeh
20	tuv emar yeshu lmana edamih lmalkuza d'alaha
21	damya lajmira dnesbaz atza temraz bkamja dazlaz sa'in dama djuleh jma
22	wrade wa bkurya wvamdinaza kad malef wa'sel lu'wrishlem
23	sha'leh den nash d'en surin enon aylen dja'en
24	yeshu den emar lhon ezkashw lmeal bzara alisa amar na ljon guer dsaguiyye' nevon lmeal wla neshkjon
25	men shaza dankum mare bayta wne'jud tara wzehon kamin lvar wnakshin bzara wazsharon lmi'mar maran maran pzaj lan wnene hu wni'mar amar na ljon dla yada na ljon aymeka aton
26	wazsharon lmi'mar kdamayk ejaln we'shtin wavshukayn aleft
27	wni'mar ljon dla yada na ljon aymeka aton prukw ljon meny paljay shukra
28	taman nehwe bejya wjurak shene kad tejson la'vraham wli'ysjak walyakuv waljulhon nviyye bmalkuza d'alaha aton den tehon mapkin lvar
29	wni'zon men madnja wmen marva wmen taymna wmen garvya wnestamkon bmalkuza d'alaha
30	wha iz jrayye dnehon kadmayye wi'yz kadmayye dnehon jrayye
31	beh bhaw yawma krevw nasha men prishe wa'mrin leh puk sel laj meka metul dherawdes save lmektlaj
32	amar lhon yeshu selw emarw lzala hana dha mapek na shi'de wa'swaza aved na yawmana wamjar walyawma dazlaza meshtamle na
33	bram wale li dyawmana wamjar esur walyawma jrina isal metul dla meshkja danviyya ni'vad lvar men urishlem
34	urishlem urishlem katlaz nviyye wragmaz la'ylen dashlijin lwazah kma savnin sviz lamjanashu bnayky ayj tarnagulta djansha parugueh tjez guefeh wla svayton
35	ha meshtvek ljon baytjon jarba amar na ljon guer dla tejsunany dama dzi'mron brij u d'aze bashmeh dmarya
