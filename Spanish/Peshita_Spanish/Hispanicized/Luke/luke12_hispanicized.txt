1	wjad ezkanashw rebwaza djenshe saguiyye' aykana dandayshon jad ljad shari yeshu lmi'mar lzalmidawy lukdam esdarw bnafshjon men jmira dafrishe d'iyzawy masav b'ape
2	layt den medem dajse dla nezgle wla damtashay dla nezida
3	kul guer davjeshuja emarton bnahira neshtma wmedem davzawane b'edne lajeshton al egare nezkres
4	amar na ljon den lrajmay la tedjlon men aylen dkatlin pagra wmen bazarken layt lhon medem yatir lmebad
5	ejawejon den men man tedjlon men haw dmen bazar dkatel shalit lmarmayyu bguihana in amar na ljon dmen hana djalw
6	la jamesh seprin mesdabnan bazren asarin wajda menhen la taya kdam alaha
7	diljon den af mene dsara drishjon kulhen manyan enen la hajil tedjlon dmen suga' dsepre myatrin ton
8	amar na ljon den djul dnawde bi kdam bnaynasha af breh dnasha nawde beh kdam mala'jawy d'alaha
9	ayna den djafar bi kdam bnaynasha nezkfar beh kdam mala'jawy d'alaha
10	wjul dni'mar melza al breh dnasha neshtvek leh man dal ruja den dkudsha ngadef la neshtvek leh
11	ma damkarvin ljon den lajnushaza kdam rishe wshalitane la ti'spon aykana tepkon ruja aw mana ti'mron
12	ruja guer dkudsha nalefjon bha shaza medem dwale dzi'mron
13	emar leh den nash men haw kensha malfana emar la'jy paleg amy yartuza
14	yeshu den emar leh gavra manu akimany laykon dayyana wamfalgana
15	we'mar lzalmidawy esdarw men kulah yanuza metul dla wa byatiruza dnejse iz jayye
16	we'mar mazla lwazhon gavra jad atira alaz leh areh allaza saguiyya'za
17	wmezjashav wa bnafsheh we'mar mana ebed dlayt li ayka d'ejmul allazy
18	we'mar hade ebed estur bez kfasay we'vne wa'wrev enon we'jmul taman kuleh vury wtavazy
19	wi'mar lnafshy nafshy iz lejy tavaza saguiyya'za dsiman lashnayya saguiyya'za ettnijy ajuly eshta ezbamy
20	amar leh den alaha jsir reyana bhana lilya nafshaj tavin lah menaj whalen dtayyevt lman nehwyan
21	hajana w man dsa'em leh simaza wva'laha la atir
22	we'mar lzalmidawy metul hana ljon amar na la ti'spon lnafshjon mana te'jlon wla lfagrjon mana telbshon
23	nafsha guer yatira men saybarta wfagra men lvusha
24	ezbakaw bnave dla sarin wla jasdin wlayt lhon tawane wvez kfase wa'laha mzarse lhon kma hajil aton yatirin ton men parjaza
25	ayna den menjon kad yasef meshkaj lmawsafu al kawmzeh amza jda
26	en den af la surta meshkjin ton mana al sharka yaspin ton
27	ezbakaw bshushane aykana ravyan dla la'yan wla aslan amar na ljon den d'af la shlemawn bjuleh shuvjeh ezkasi ayj jda men halen
28	en den lamira dyawmana izawy bjakla wamjar nafel bzanura alaha hajana malbesh kma yatir ljon suray haymanuza
29	wa'ton la tevon mana te'jlon wmana teshton wla nefhe reyanjon bhalen
30	halen guer kulhen amme w dalma baen af ljon den avujon yada dmezbayan ljon halen
31	bram baw malkuzeh d'alaha whalen kulhen mettawsfan ljon
32	la tedjal gsara sura dasva avujon dnetel ljon malkuza
33	sabenw kenyanjon whavw sedkza vedw ljon kise dla balen wsimza dla gasa bashmayya ayka dganava la karev wsasa la mjabel
34	ayka guer d'iyzeh simazjon taman nehwe af lebjon
35	nehon asirin jasaykon wmanhrin shragaykon
36	wahwaw damen lnasha damsaken lmarhon d'emazy nefne men bez meshtuza dma d'eza wankash mejda neftjon leh
37	tuvayhon lavde hanon aylen dni'ze marhon wneshkaj enon kad irin amin amar na ljon dne'sur jasawy wnasmej enon wnebar nshamesh enon
38	we'n bmatarza dzarten aw dazlaz ni'ze wneshkaj hajana tuvayhon lavde hanon
39	hade den daw d'elu yada wa mare bayta b'ayda matarza aze ganava mettir wa wla shavek wa dnezplesh bayteh
40	af aton hajil hwaw mtayve davha shaza dla savrin ton aze breh dnasha
41	amar leh shemon ki'fa maran lwazan amar at leh lmazla hana aw af lwaz kulnash
42	amar leh yeshu manu kay iz rabayta mhaymna wjakima dankimiwy mareh al teshmeshteh dnetel parsa bsavneh
43	tuvawy lhaw avda dni'ze mareh neshkjiwy daved hajana
44	sharira'iz amar na ljon dankimiwy al kuleh kenyaneh
45	en den ni'mar avda haw blebeh dmary mawjar lme'za wanshare lmemja lavde wla'mhaza dmareh wanshare lmelas walmeshta walmerwa
46	ni'ze mareh davda haw byawma dla savar wavshaza dla yada wneflguiwy wansim mnazeh am hanon dla mhaymnin
47	avda den ayna dyada sevyana dmareh wla tayyev leh ayj sevyaneh nevla saguiyya'za
48	haw den dla yada waved medem dshawe lmajwaza nevla majwaza suryaza kul guer d'ezihev leh sagui sagui nettva meneh walhaw d'agelw leh sagui yatira'iz nezbon b'iydeh
49	nura eziz d'arme b'ara wsave na elu men kadu jebaz
50	wmamudiza iz li d'emad wsagui alis na dama dzeshtamle
51	savrin ton dshayna eziz d'arme b'ara amar na ljon dla ela palguza
52	men hasha guer nehon jamsha bvayta jad dafliguin tlaza al tren wazren al tlaza
53	nezpleg guer ava al breh wavra al avu ema al barzah wvarza al emah jmaza al kalzah wjalza al jmazah
54	we'mar ljenshe ma dajsayton nana ddanja men marva mejda amrin ton metra aze whawe hajana
55	wma dnashba taymna amrin ton juma hawe whawe
56	nasbay b'ape parsupa d'ara wdashmayya yadin ton dzefrshon savna den hana aykana la parshin ton
57	lmana den men nafshjon la danin ton kushta
58	ma guer d'asel at am beldinaj lwaz arjuna ad b'uwrja at hav te'gurta wzezparak meneh dalma nawblaj lwaz dayyana wdayyana nashlmaj lgabayya wgabayya narmej bez asire
59	wa'mar na laj dla tepuk men taman dama dzetel shamuna jrayya
