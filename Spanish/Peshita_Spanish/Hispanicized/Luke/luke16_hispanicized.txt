1	we'mar mazla lwaz talmidawy gavra jad iz wa atira wi'yz wa leh rabayta we'ze'jelw leh karsawy dkenyaneh mfaraj
2	wakra mareh we'mar leh manaw hana dshama na layk hav li jushbana drabaz baytuzaj la guer meshkaj at mekil rabayta dzehwe li
3	amar haw rabayta bnafsheh mana ebed dmary shakel lah meny rabaz baytuza d'ejpur la meshkaj na walmejdar bahez na
4	yedez mana ebed dma dnefkez men rabaz baytuza nkablunany bvatayhon
5	wakra jad jad men jayyave dmareh we'mar lkadmayya kma jayyav at lmary
6	amar leh ma' mazryan meshja amar leh sav kzavaj wzev bagal kzuv jamshin mazryan
7	we'mar lajrina wa't mana jayyav at lmary amar leh ma' kurin jete amar leh kabel kzavaj wzev kzuv tmani'yn kurin
8	wshabaj maran lrabayta dawla djakima'iz vad bnawy guer dalma hana jakimin enon men bnawy dnuhra bsharbazhon hade
9	wa'f ena amar na ljon davedw ljon rajme men mamuna hana dawla dma dagmar nkablunajon bamtalayhon dalalam
10	man davkalil mhayman af bsagui mhayman u wman davkalil awal af bsagui awal u
11	en hajil bmamuna dawla mhaymne la hwayton shrara ljon manu mhaymen
12	we'n badla diljon la eshtjajton mhaymne diljon manu netel ljon
13	layt avda dmeshkaj lazren marawan lmeflaj aw guer ljad nesne wlajrina nerjam aw ljad nyakar wlajrina nshut la meshkjin ton lmeflaj la'laha walmamuna
14	prishe den kad shmaw halen kulhen metul drajmin waw kespa mmaykin waw beh
15	yeshu den emar lhon aton enon damsadkin nafshhon kdam bnay nasha alaha den yada lebawazjon dmedem dram bez bnaynasha kdam alaha ndid u
16	namusa wanviyye dama lyujanan men haden malkuza d'alaha mestabra wjul lah javes dneul
17	pshik u den dashmayya wa'ra nebron aw azuza jda men namusa tebar
18	kul man dshare atzeh wnasev jriza ga'ar wjul man dnasev shvikta ga'ar
19	gavra den jad atira iz wa wlavesh wa busa wa'rgwana wjulyum mezbasmin wa ga'ya'iz
20	wmeskina jad iz wa dashmeh laasar warme wa lwaz tareh dhaw atira kad mmajay bshujne
21	wmezya'av wa dnemle karseh men partuze dnaflin men pazureh dhaw atira ela af kalbe azen waw mlajjin shujnawy
22	hwa den wmiz haw meskina wa'wblu mala'je lubeh d'avraham af haw den atira miz we'zkvar
23	wjad meshtanak bashyul arim aynawy men rujka wajsa la'vraham wallaasar bubeh
24	wakra bkala rama we'mar avy avraham ezrajam lay wshadar llaasar dnesbu rish seveh bmayya wanratev li leshany dha meshtanak na bshalheviza hade
25	amar leh avraham bery etdar dkabelt tavazaj bjayyayk wlaasar bishazeh whasha ha mettnij harka wa't meshtanak
26	wam halen kulhen hawza rabza sima baynayn waljon d'aylen dsaven meka dnebron lwazjon la neshkjon wa'fla dmen taman nebron lwazan
27	amar leh maden bae na menaj avy dazshadriwy lvez avy
28	jamsha guer ajin iz li ni'sal nsahed enon dla af henon ni'zon ldukza hade dzashnika
29	amar leh avraham iz lhon mushe wanviyye neshmon enon
30	hu den emar leh la avy avraham ela en nash men mize ni'sal lwazhon tabin
31	amar leh avraham en lmushe wlanviyye la shamin af la en nash men mize nkum mhaymnin leh
