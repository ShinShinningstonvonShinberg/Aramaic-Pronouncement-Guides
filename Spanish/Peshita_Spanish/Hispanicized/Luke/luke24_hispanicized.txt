1	bjad bshaba den bshafra ad jeshuj ezay lvez kvura wa'yti herume halen dtayyev way wi'yz way amhen neshe jranyaza
2	we'shkaj ki'fa damagla men bez kvura
3	walen wla eshkja lfagra dyeshu
4	wahwa djad henen tamihan al hade ha tren gavrin kamw lel menhen wmavrek wa lvushhon
5	wahway bdejlza wajfay apayhen b'ara wa'mrin lhen mana bayan ten jayya am mize
6	laytawy tnan kam leh ehaden dmalel amjen kad hu baglila
7	we'mar wa dazid u breh dnasha dneshtlem b'iyday nasha jatayye wnestlev wlazlaza yawmin nkum
8	whenen etdjaren lmelawy
9	wahfaj men kavra we'maren halen kulhen lajdasar walsharka
10	izayhen way den maryam magdlata wyujan wmaryam emeh dyakuv wsharka damhen halen d'emar way lashlije
11	we'zjsi baynayhon mele halen ayj shanyaza wla haymenw enen
12	shemon den kam warhet lkavra wa'dik jsa ketane dsimin baljud we'sal kad metdamar bnafsheh al medem dahwa
13	wha tren menhon beh byawma asin waw lakriza dashmah ema'us wfarika men urishlem estdawaza shtin
14	whenon mmallin waw jad am jad al halen kulhen dagdash
15	wjad henon mmallin wvaen jad am jad eza hu yeshu wmati enon wamhalej wa amhon
16	waynayhon ajidan hway dla nestakluna
17	we'mar lhon mana enen mele halen dammallin ton jad am jad kad mhaljin ton wajmirin ton
18	na jad menhon dashmeh kleyyawpa we'mar leh at u kay baljudayk nujrayya men urishlem dla yada at medem dahwa bah bhanon yawmaza
19	amar lhon mana amrin leh al yeshu haw dmen nasraz gavra dahwa nviyya wjaylzan wa bmelza wvavade kdam alaha wakdam kuleh ama
20	wa'shlmu rabay kahne wkashishe ldina dmawta wsakpu
21	jnan den savrin wayn dhuyyu zid wa dnefrkiwy li'ysrayyel wha tlaza yawmin ha men dhalen kulhen hway
22	ela af neshe menan azmhan kadem way guer lvez kvura
23	wjad la eshkaj pagreh ezay amran lan dmala'je jsayn taman wa'mrin lawy djay u
24	wa'f nasha menan esalw lvez kvura we'shkajw hajana ayj ma d'emar neshe leh den la jsaw
25	haden emar lhon yeshu aw jasiray reyana wyakiray leba lamhaymanu bjulhen aylen dmalelw nviyye
26	la wa halen zid hwa dansaybar mshija wadneul lzeshbujteh
27	wshari wa men mushe wmen kulhon nviyye wamfashek wa lhon al nafsheh men kulhon kzave
28	wakrevw waw lakriza ha d'asin waw lah whu masbar wa lhon d'ayj dalduka rajika asel wa
29	wa'lsu wa'mrin leh push lwazan metul dyawma hasha rjen leh lmejshaj wal dankawe lwazhon
30	wahwa djad estmej amhon nsav lajma wvarej waksa wyav lhon
31	wmejda ezpataj aynayhon we'shtawdu whu eshtkel leh menhon
32	wa'mrin waw jad ljad la wa leban yakir wa bgawan kad mmalel aman b'uwrja wamfashek lan kzave
33	wkamw bah bshaza wahfajw lu'wrishlem we'shkajw lajdasar dajnishin wla'ylen damhon
34	kad amrin dsharira'iz kam maran we'zjsi lshemon
35	wa'f henon eshtaiw aylen dahway b'uwrja wa'ykana ezida lhon kad ksa lajma
36	wjad halen mmallin waw yeshu kam baynazhon we'mar lhon shlama amjon ena na la tedjlon
37	whenon ezrhevw wahwaw bdejlza savrin waw guer druja jasen
38	wa'mar lhon yeshu mana metsiin ton wmana salkan majshvaza al lebawazjon
39	jsaw iday wreglay d'ena na gushunany wdaw dalruja besra wgarme layt lah ayj djasen ton d'iyz li
40	wjad halen emar jawi enon idawy wreglawy
41	wjad dama lhasha la mhaymnin waw men jadwazhon wmettamhin waw emar lhon iz ljon tnan medem lme'jal
42	henon den yavw leh mnaza men nuna dtawya wmen kakariza ddevsha
43	wansav ejal linayhon
44	we'mar lhon halen enen mele dmallez amjon kad lwazjon wiz dwale w dneshtalam kul medem dajziv bnamusa dmushe wvanviyye wavmasmure lay
45	haden pzaj reyanhon lmestakalu kzave
46	we'mar lhon dhajana kziv whajana sadek wa dnejash mshija wdankum men bez mize lazlaza yawmin
47	wadnezkres bashmeh tyabuza lshuvkana dajtahe bjulhon amme wshurayya nehwe men urishlem
48	wa'ton enon sahde dhalen
49	we'na eshadar laykon mulkana d'avy aton den kawaw b'uwrishlem mdita dama dzelbshon jayla men rawma
50	wa'pek enon dama lvez-anya wa'rim idawy wvarej enon
51	wahwa djad mvarej lhon ezpresh menhon waslek lashmayya
52	henon den sguedw leh wahfajw lu'wrishlem bjaduza rabza
53	wavjulsvan izayhon waw bhaykla kad mshabjin wamvarjin la'laha amin
