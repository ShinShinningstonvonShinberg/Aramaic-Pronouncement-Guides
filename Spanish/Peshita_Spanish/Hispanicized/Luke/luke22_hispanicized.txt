1	kariv wa den adida dfatire dmezkre pesja
2	wvaen waw rabay kahne wsafre d'aykana nektluna dajlin waw guer men ama
3	al wa den satana bihuda dmezkre sjaryuta d'iyzawy wa men menyana dazresar
4	we'sal malel am rabay kahne wsafre wrabay jayle dhaykla ayj dnashlmiwy lhon
5	wajdiw wa'kimw dnetlon leh kespa
6	we'shtawdi lhon wvae wa pela dnashlmiwy lhon belad men kensha

45	wkam men sluzeh we'za lwaz talmidawy we'shkaj enon kad damkin men akza
