1	wjad al yeshu wavar b'iyriju
2	gavra jad dashmeh sakay atira wa wrav majse
3	wsave wa dnejse lyeshu dmanu wla meshkaj wa men kensha metul davkawmzeh sur wa sakay
4	warhet kadmeh lyeshu waslek leh lzita pakihta dnejsewy metul dhajwaz zid hwa dnebar
5	wjad eza lha dukza yeshu jsa we'mar leh estarv juz sakay yawmana guer wale davvaytaj ehwe
6	we'starhav njez wkableh kad jade
7	kad jsaw den kulhon ratnin waw wa'mrin dalwaz gavra jatayya al shra
8	kam den sakay we'mar lyeshu ha mary pelguz nejsay yahev na lmeskine waljulnash medem dguelsez jad b'arba para na
9	amar leh yeshu yawmana hwaw jayye lvayta hana metul d'af hana breh u d'avraham
10	eza guer breh dnasha dneve wnaje haw medem d'abid wa
11	wjad shamin waw halen awsef lmi'mar mazla metul dkariv wa lu'wrishlem wsavrin waw davha shaza zida dzezgle malkuzeh d'alaha
12	we'mar gavra jad bar tuhma raba esal la'zra rajika dnesav leh malkuza wnehpuj
13	wakra esra avdawy wyav lhon esra mnin we'mar lhon ettarw ad aze na
14	bnay mditeh den sanen waw leh wshadarw isgade bazreh wa'mrin la saven nan dnamlej layn hana
15	wjad nsav malkuza wahfaj emar dnekron leh hanon avdawy dyav lhon kespa dneda mana kul jad jad menhon ettagar
16	we'za kadmayya we'mar mary manyaj esra mnin awtar
17	amar leh iw avda tava davkalil eshtjajt mhayman tehwe shalit al esra karjin
18	we'za dazren we'mar mary manyaj jamsha mnin vad
19	emar af lhana af at tehwe shalit al jamsha karjin
20	we'za jrina we'mar mary ha manyaj haw d'iyz wa lwazy kad sim bseduna
21	dejlez guer menaj dgavra at kashya wshakel at medem dla samt wjased at medem dla srat
22	amar leh men pumaj edunaj avda bisha yada wayt li dgavra na kashya wshakel na medem dla samez wjased na medem dla serez
23	lmana la yavt kespy al pazura we'na aze wiz tava leh am rebyazeh
24	walhanon dkamin kdamawy emar savw meneh manya whavw lhaw d'iyz lwazeh esra mnin
25	amrin leh maran iz lwazeh esra mnin
26	amar lhon amar na ljon daljul man d'iyz leh nezihev leh wmen haw dlayt leh af haw d'iyz leh neznsev meneh
27	bram lhanon beldvavay aylen dla svaw d'amlej layhon aytaw enon wkatelw enon kdamay
28	wjad emar halen yeshu nfak lakdamawy dni'sal lu'wrishlem
29	wjad mati lvez-pague wvez-anya al guev tura dmezkre dvez sayte shadar tren men talmidawy
30	we'mar lhon selw lakriza ha dalkuvlan wjad a'lin ton lah ha meshkjin ton ila d'asir dnash memzum la rjev lawy shraw ayta'u
31	we'n nash msha'el ljon lmana sharen ton leh hajana emarw leh lmaran mezbe
32	we'salw hanon d'eshtadarw we'shkajw aykana d'emar lhon
33	wjad sharen leh lila amrin lhon marawy mana sharen ton ila haw
34	we'marw lhon dalmaran mezbe
35	wa'ytyu lwaz yeshu wa'rmiw al ila ma'nayhon wa'rkvu lyeshu lawy
36	kad den asel parsin waw ma'nayhon b'uwrja
37	wjad krev lmajatza dtura dvez sayte shariw kuleh kensha dzalmide jaden wamshabjin la'laha bkala rama al kul jayle dajsaw
38	wa'mrin waw brij u malka d'aze bashmeh dmarya shlama bashmayya wshuvja bamrawme
39	nasha den men prishe men baynay kenshe amrin leh rabi k'i bzalmidayk
40	amar lhon amar na ljon d'en halen neshtkon ki'fe nekyan
41	wjad krev wajsah lamdita bja leh
42	we'mar elu kay yadaty aylen d'iyzayhen dashlamejy afen bhana yawmejy hasha den ezkasi lhen men aynayky
43	ni'zon lejy den yawmaza dnejdrunejy beldvavayky wne'lsunejy men kul duka
44	wnesjfunejy wlavnayky bgawejy wla neshbkon bejy ki'f al ki'f jlaf dla idaty savna dsuranejy
45	wjad al lhaykla shari lmapaku la'ylen dsavnin beh wamsabnin
46	we'mar lhon kziv dvayty bez sluza izawy aton den vadtuna marza dlestayye
47	whu malef wa kul yum bhaykla rabay kahne den wsafre wkashishe dama baen waw lmawbaduzeh
48	wla meshkjin waw mana nebdon leh kuleh guer ama tle wa beh lmeshmeh
