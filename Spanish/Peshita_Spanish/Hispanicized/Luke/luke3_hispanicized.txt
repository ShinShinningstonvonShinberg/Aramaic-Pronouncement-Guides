1	bashnaz jamshaesre den dmalkuzeh dtiveriyyaws kesar bhigmanuza dpantiyyaws-pilataws bihud kad risha rviayya herawdes baglila wfilipaws aju risha rviayya b'iyturiyya wva'zra datrajawna wlusaniyya risha rviayya d'aviline
2	brabuz kahnuza djanan wadkayyafa hwaz melza d'alaha al yujanan bar sjarya bjurba
3	we'za bjuleh azra dajdaray yurdnan kad majres mamudiza dazyavuza lshuvkana dajtahe
4	aykana dajziv bajzava dmele d'eshaya nviyya d'emar kala dkare bjurba tayyevw urjeh dmarya wazrusw bafkaza shvile la'lahan
5	kulhon najle nezmlon wjulhon ture wramaza nezmakjon wnehwe arma lshafya wa'zra aska lafkaza
6	wnejse kul bsar jayye d'alaha
7	we'mar wa ljenshe aylen d'azen waw lwazeh lmemad yalda d'ajedne manu jawijon lmerak men rugsa dazid
8	vedw hajil pi're dshawen lazyavuza wla tsharon lmi'mar bnafshjon d'ava iz lan avraham amar na ljon guer dmen halen ki'fe meshkaj alaha lamkamu bnayya la'vraham
9	ha den narga sim al ekara d'iylane kul ilana hajil dfi're tave la aved mezpsek wavnura nafel
10	wamsha'lin waw leh kenshe wa'mrin mana hajil nebed
11	na we'mar lhon man d'iyz leh tarten kutinyan netel lman dlayt leh wman d'iyz leh saybarta hajana nebed
12	we'zaw af majse lmemad wa'mrin leh malfana mana nebed
13	hu den emar lhon la tezbon medem yatir al ma dafkid ljon lmezba
14	wamsha'lin waw leh paljay estratiyya wa'mrin mana nebed af jnan amar lhon la bnash tettagron walnash la teshkon wnespkan ljon afsawnyazjon
15	kad den masbar wa ama al yujanan wjulhon mezjashvin waw blebhon ddalma huyyu mshija
16	na yujanan we'mar lhon ena ha mamed na ljon bmayya aze den haw djaylzan meny haw dla shawe na d'eshre arke damsanawy hu namedjon bruja dkudsha wavnura
17	haw d'ajid rafsha b'iydeh wamdake edrawy wjete kanesh la'wsrawy wzevna nawked bnura dla daka
18	af jranyaza den saguiyya'za malef wa wamsabar lama
19	herawdes den tetrarja metul dmezkses wa men yujanan al herawdiyya ataz filipaws aju wal kulhen bishaza daved wa
20	awsef af hade al kulhen wjavsheh lyujanan bez asire
21	hwa den kad mad kuleh ama wa'f yeshu mad wjad msale ezpzajw shmayya
22	wnejtaz ruja dkudsha lawy badmuz gushma dyawna wkala hwa men shmayya d'amar at u bery jabiva dvaj estviz
