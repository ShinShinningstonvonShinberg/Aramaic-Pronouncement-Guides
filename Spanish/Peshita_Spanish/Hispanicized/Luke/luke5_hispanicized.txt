1	hwa den kad knash lawy kensha lmeshma melza d'alaha whu ka'em wa al yad yamza dguenesar
2	jsa sfine tarten dkaman al guev yamza wsayyade daslekw menhen wamshiguin msidazhon
3	wajda menhen dshemon hwaz ki'fa waslek yeshu izev bah we'mar dnedbrunah kalil men yavsha lmayya wyazev wa wmalef men sfita ljensha
4	wjad shzek men mamlleh emar lshemon dvarw lumka wa'rmaw msidtjon lsayda
5	na shemon we'mar leh rabi lilya kuleh li'yn wmedem la ejadn al melzaj den rame na msidta
6	wjad hade vadw jvashw nune saguiyye' dtav wmestarya waz msidthon
7	warmasw ljavrayhon dvasfita jriza dni'zon nadron enon wjad ezaw mlaw enen sfine tartayhen ayj dkarivan way lmetba
8	kad jsa den shemon ki'fa nfal kdam reglawy dyeshu we'mar leh bae na menaj mary pruk laj meny dgavra na jatayya
9	temha guer ajdeh wa waljulhon dameh al sayda haw dnune dsadw
10	hajwaz den af lyakuv walyujanan bnay savday d'iyzayhon waw shawtafe dshemon emar den yeshu lshemon la tedjal men hasha bnay nasha tehwe sa'ed ljayye
11	wkarevw enen sfine la'ra washvakw kul medem we'zaw bazreh
12	wjad hwa yeshu bajda men mdinaza eza gavra damle kuleh garva jsa lyeshu wanfal al apawy wvae wa meneh we'mar leh mary en save at meshkaj at lamdakayyuzy
13	wafshat ideh yeshu krev leh we'mar leh save na etdaka wvar shazeh esal meneh garveh we'tdaki
14	wfakdeh dalnash la ti'mar ela sel jawa nafshaj ljahne wkarev jlaf tadkizaj aykana dfaked mushe lsahduzhon
15	wanfak lawy teba yatira'iz wmezkanash wa ama saguiyya' lmeshma meneh walmeza'sayyu men kurhanayhon
16	hu den mshane wa ldavra wamsale
17	wahwa bjad men yawmaza kad malef wa yeshu yazbin waw prishe wmalfay namusa d'ezaw waw men kul kurya daglila wdihud wdu'wrishlem wjayla dmarya izawy wa lma'sayyuzhon
18	wnasha aytiw barsa gavra jad msharya wvaen waw dnalon nsimuna kdamawy
19	wjad la eshkajw d'aykana naluna metul suga' dama slekw lhon le'gara wshabu am arseh men tatlila lamsaza kdamawy dyeshu
20	kad jsa den yeshu haymanuzhon emar lhaw msharya gavra shvikin laj jtahayk
21	wshariw safre wafrishe mezjashvin wa'mrin manu hana dammalel gudafa manu meshkaj lmeshbak jtahe ela en alaha baljud
22	yeshu den ida majshvazhon wana we'mar lhon mana mezjashvin ton blebjon
23	ayda pshika lmi'mar dashvikin laj jtahayk aw lmi'mar kum halej
24	dzedon den dshalit u breh dnasha b'ara dneshbuk jtahe amar lamsharya laj amar na kum shkul arsaj wsel lvaytaj
25	wmejda kam linayhon washkal arseh we'sal lvayteh kad mshabaj la'laha
26	wzemha ejad ljulnash wamshabjin waw la'laha we'zmliw dejlza wa'mrin dajsayn yawmana tedmraza
27	bazar halen nfak yeshu wajsa majsa dashmeh lewi dyazev bez majse we'mar leh ta bazary
28	washvak kul medem wkam esal bazreh
29	wavad leh lewi bvayteh kubala raba wi'yz wa kensha saguiyya' dmajse wdajrane dasmijin hwaw amhon
30	wratnin waw safre wafrishe wa'mrin lzalmidawy lmana am majse wjatayye ajlin ton wshazen
31	wana yeshu we'mar lhon la mezbe asya lajlime ela la'ylen dvish bish vidin
32	la eziz d'ekre lsadike ela ljatayye lazyavuza
33	henon den amrin leh lmana talmidawy dyujanan samin amina'iz wamsalen af dafrishe dilaj den ajlin wshazen
34	hu den emar lhon la meshkjin ton lavnawy dagnuna kma djazna amhon dzebdon dansumon
35	ni'zon den yawmaza kad nettrim jazna menhon haden nsumon bhanon yawmaza
36	we'mar lhon mazla dla nash ka'ed urkaza men ma'na jaza wrame al ma'na blayya dla ljaza ka'ed wlavlayya la shalma urkaza dmen jaza
37	wla nash rame jamra jaza bseke blayyaza we'n den la mvasa jamra jaza lseke whu jamra meze'shed wseke avdan
38	ela jamra jaza bseke jataza ramen wazrayhon meznatrin
39	wla nash shaze jamra atika wmejda bae jaza amar guer atika basim
