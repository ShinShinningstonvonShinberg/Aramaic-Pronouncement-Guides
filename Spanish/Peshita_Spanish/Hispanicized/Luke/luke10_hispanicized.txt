1	bazar halen prash yeshu men talmidawy jrane shavin wshadar enon tren tren kdam parsupeh ljul azar wamdina dazid hwa lmi'sal
2	we'mar lhon jsada sagui wfale surin baw hajil men mare jsada dnapek pale lajsadeh
3	selw ha ena mshadar na ljon ayj emre baynay d'ive
4	la teshklon ljon kise wla tarmale wla msane wvashlama dnash b'uwrja la teshe'lon
5	wla'yna bayta da'lin ton lukdam emarw shlama lvayta hana
6	we'n iz taman bar shlama nettnij lawy shlamjon en den la laykon nehpuj
7	beh den bvayta hwaw kad lasin ton wshazen men dilhon shawe w guer pala agreh wla tshanon men bayta lvayta
8	wla'yda mdita da'lin ton wamkablin ljon lasw medem dmettsim ljon
9	wa'saw la'ylen dajrihin bah we'marw lhon kerbaz laykon malkuzeh d'alaha
10	la'yda mdita den da'lin ton wla nkablunajon pukw ljon lshuka we'marw
11	wa'f jela dadvek lan breglayn men mditjon nafsin nan ljon bram hade daw dkerbaz lah laykon malkuzeh d'alaha
12	amar na ljon dlasdum nehwe nij byawma haw aw lamdita ha
13	wa lejy kawrasin wa lejy bez-sayyada d'elu bsur wavsaydan hwaw jayle dahwaw bjen kvar den bsake wavketma tavw
14	bram lsur walsaydan nehwe nij bdina aw ljen
15	wa'ty kfarnajum ha dadama lashmayya ettrimty dama lashyul tetajten
16	man daljon shama li shama wman daljon talem li u talem wman dli talem talem lman dshaljany
17	wahfajw hanon shavin dshadar bjaduza rabza wa'mrin leh maran af shi'de meshtabdin lan bashmaj
18	hu den emar lhon jase wiz leh lsatana danfal ayj barka men shmayya
19	ha yahev na ljon shultana dahwayton dashin jwawaza wekarve wjuleh jayleh daveldvava wmedem la naharjon
20	bram bhade la tejdon dshi'de meshtabdin ljon ela jdaw dashmahaykon ezkzevw bashmayya
21	bah bshaza rwas yeshu bruja dkudsha we'mar mawde na laj avy mara dashmayya wda'ra djasit halen men jakime wsakulzane waglayt enen lyalude in avy dhajana hwa sevyana kdamayk
22	we'zpni lwaz talmidawy we'mar lhon kul medem eshtlem li men avy wla nash yada manu bra ela en ava wmanu ava ela en bra walman d'en nesbe bra dnegle
23	we'zpni lwaz talmidawy baljudayhon we'mar tuvayhen layne djasyan medem d'aton jasen
24	amar na ljon guer danviyye saguiyye' wmalke svaw dnejson medem djasen ton wla jsaw walmeshma medem d'aton shamin wla shmaw
25	wha safra jad kam dannasewy we'mar malfana mana ebed d'iraz jayye dalalam
26	hu den yeshu emar leh bnamusa aykana kziv aykana kare at
27	na we'mar leh dzerjam lmarya alahaj men kuleh lebaj wmen kulah nafshaj wmen kuleh jaylaj wmen kuleh reyanaj walkarivaj ayj nafshaj
28	amar leh yeshu trisa'iz emart hade ved wzije
29	hu den kad sva lamsadaku nafsheh emar leh wmanu karivy
30	amar leh yeshu gavra jad najez wa men urishlem li'yriju wanfalw lawy lestayye wshalju wamja'u wshavku kad kalil kayyama beh nafsha we'salw
31	wagdash kahna jad najez wa b'uwrja ha wajsa wavar
32	whajana af lewayya eza mta lha dukza wajsa wavar
33	nash den shamrayya kad rade wa eza ayka d'iyzawy wa wajsa we'zrajam lawy
34	we'zkarav wasav majwazeh wansal layhen jamra wmeshja wsameh al jmareh wa'ytyeh lfutka we'zbtel leh lawy
35	walsafreh dyawma apek tren dinarin yav lfutkayya we'mar leh isaf dileh we'n medem yatir tapek ma dhafej na yahev na laj
36	manu hajil men halen tlaza mezjse laj dahwa kariva lhaw danfal b'iyday gayyase
37	hu den emar haw d'ezrajam lawy amar leh yeshu sel af at hajana hwayt aved
38	wahwa djad henon raden b'uwrja al lakriza jda wa'tza dashmah marta kabelzeh bvaytah
39	wi'yz waz lah jaza dashmah maryam we'zaz yezbaz lah lwaz reglawy dmaran wshama waz melawy
40	marta den anya waz bzeshmeshta sagui'za we'zaz amra leh mary la btil laj djazy shvakzany baljuday lamshamashu emar lah madra li
41	na den yeshu we'mar lah marta marta yaspaty warhivaty al saguiyya'za
42	jda y den dmezbaya maryam den mnaza tavza gvaz lah ha dla teznsev menah
