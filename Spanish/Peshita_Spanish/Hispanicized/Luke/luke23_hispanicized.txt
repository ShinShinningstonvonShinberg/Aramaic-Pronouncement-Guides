1	wkamw kuleh kenshhon wa'ytyu lwaz pilataws
2	wshariw ajlin karsawy wa'mrin lhana eshkajn dmate aman wjale dajsef risha lkesar la netel wa'mar al nafsheh dmalka w mshija
3	pilataws den sha'leh we'mar leh at u malka dihudayye amar leh at emart
4	we'mar pilataws lrabay kahne waljensha ena medem elza la meshkaj na al gavra hana
5	henon den maskin waw wa'mrin dshagsheh laman kad malef bjulah ihud wshari men glila wadama lharka
6	pilataws den kad shma shma daglila sha'el d'en gavra hu glilayya
7	wjad ida dmen tjez shultana hu dherawdes shadreh lwazeh dherawdes metul dvu'wrishlem wa bhanon yawmaza
8	herawdes den kad jsa lyeshu jdi tav save wa guer lmejsyeh men savna saguiyya' metul dshama wa lawy saguiyya'za wmasbar wa dmedem aza nejse meneh
9	wmele saguiyya'za msha'el wa leh yeshu den medem pezgama la aziveh
10	kamin waw den rabay kahne wsafre wasisa'iz ajlin waw karsawy
11	herawdes den shateh hu wfaljawy wjad mvasaj albsheh najte dasjuriza wshadreh lwaz pilataws
12	wavhaw yawma hwaw rajme pilataws wherawdes am jdade beldvavuza hwaz guer men kdim baynazhon
13	kra den pilataws lrabay kahne wla'rjune walama
14	we'mar lhon karevton li gavra hana ayj mahpej amjon wha ena akevteh linaykon welza medem la eshkjez bgavra hana men kul dmarshen ton beh
15	ela afla herawdes shadarteh guer lwazeh wha la medem dshawe lmawta sir leh
16	erdewy hajil we'shbkiwy
17	yada guer iz wa dneshre lhon jad badida
18	kaw den kuleh kensha wa'mrin shkula lhana washri lan lvar-aba
19	haw ayna dmetul estasis dahwaz bamdita wketla rme wa bez asire
20	tuv den malel amhon pilataws kad save dneshre lyeshu
21	henon den kaen waw wa'mrin skufa skufa
22	hu den dazlaz savnin emar lhon mana guer dvish vad hana medem elza dshawya lmawta la eshkjez beh erdewy hajil we'shbkiwy
23	henon den tajbin waw bkala rama wsha'lin waw leh dneskfuna washen wa kalhon dilhon wadrabay kahne
24	pilataws den pkad dzehwe she'lazhon
25	washra lhon lhaw dmetul estasis wketla rme wa bez asire haw dashe'lw lyeshu den ashlem lsevyanhon
26	wjad mawblin leh ejadw lshemon kurinayya d'aze men kriza wsamw lawy skifa dnetan bazreh dyeshu
27	wa'ze wa bazreh suga' dama wneshe aylen dmarkdan way wa'lyan lawy
28	we'zpni yeshu lwazhen we'mar bnaz urishlem la tevkyan lay bram al nafshjen bjayyen wal bnayken
29	dha azen yawmaza davhon ni'mron tuvayhen lakraza waljarsaza dla iled wlatdayya dla aynekw
30	haden tsharon lmi'mar lture dfelw layn walramaza djasayyenan
31	d'en bkaysa rativa halen avdin byabisha mana nehwe
32	wa'zen waw ameh tren jranin avday bishaza dnezkatlon
33	wjad ezaw ldukza jda dmezkarya karkafza sakpu taman walhanon avday bishaza jad men yamineh wjad men semaleh
34	hu den yeshu emar wa ava shvuk lhon la guer yadin mana avdin wfalegw najtawy wa'rmiw layhon pesa
35	ka'em wa den ama wjase wammaykin waw beh af arjune wa'mrin lajrane aji naje nafsheh en huyyu mshija gavyeh d'alaha
36	wamvasjin waw beh af estratiyyute kad karbin lwazeh wamkarvin leh jala
37	wa'mrin leh en at u malka dihudayye aja nafshaj
38	iz wa den af kzava dajziv lel meneh yawna'iz waruma'iz wevra'iz hanaw malka dihudayye
39	jad den men hanon avday bishaza daslivin waw ameh mgadef wa lawy wa'mar en at u mshija pasa nafshaj wfasa af lan
40	waj'a beh javreh we'mar leh af la men alaha dajel at d'af at beh at bdina
41	wajnan ki'na'iz ayj dshawen wayn guer wa'yj davadn ezpran hana den medem dasne la vid leh
42	we'mar lyeshu etdajrayny mary ma d'aze at bmalkuzaj
43	amar leh yeshu amin amar na laj dyawmana amy tehwe bfardaysa
44	iz way den ayj shae shez wahwa jeshuja al kulah ara dama lazsha shain
45	wshemsha jshej we'stri apay tara dhaykla men msazeh
46	waka yeshu bkala rama we'mar avy b'iydayk sa'em na rujy hade emar washlem
47	kad jsa den kentruna medem dahwa shabaj la'laha we'mar sharira'iz hana gavra sadika wa
48	wjulhon kenshe aylen dajnishin waw lajsaza hade kad jsaw medem dahwa hfajw kad tarpin al jadihon
49	wkamin waw men rujka kulhon yaduawy dyeshu wneshe aylen d'ezay way ameh men glila wjasen waw halen
50	gavra den jad dashmeh yawsef bulewti men ramza mdita dihud gavra hwa tava wsadika
51	hana la shalem wa lsevyanhon walsuranhon wamsake wa lmalkuza d'alaha
52	hana krev lwaz pilataws wash'el pagreh dyeshu
