1	emar lhon den af mazla davjul edan nsalon wla tema'n lhon
2	dayyana jad iz wa bamdita jda dmen alaha la dajel wa wmen bnaynasha la mezkajad wa
3	armalta den jda iz waz bamdita ha wa'zya waz lwazeh wa'mra tvaayny men bel diny
4	wla save wa savna saguiyya' bazarken den emar bnafsheh en men alaha la dajel na wmen bnaynasha la mezkajad na
5	afen metul dmale'ya li hade armalta ezbih dla bjul edan tehwe azya mahra li
6	we'mar maran shmaw mana emar dayyana dawla
7	alaha den la yatira'iz nebed tvaza lagvawy dkaren leh b'iymama wavlilya wmagar rujeh layhon
8	amar na ljon dnebed tvazhon bagal bram ni'ze breh dnasha wneshkaj kay haymanuza al ara
9	we'mar wa mazla hana lukval nasha aylen dazjilin al nafshhon d'iyzayhon sadike wvasen al kulnash
10	tren gavrin slekw lhaykla lamsalayyu jad prisha wajrina majsa
11	whaw prisha ka'em wa baynawy lnafsheh whalen msale wa alaha mawde na laj dla wiz ayj sharka dnasha jatufe waluve wgayyare wla ayj hana majsa
12	ela sa'em na tren bshabza wamasar na kul medem dkane na
13	haw den majsa ka'em wa men rujka wla save wa af la aynawy nrim lashmayya ela taref wa al jadyeh wa'mar alaha junayny ljatayya
14	amar na ljon danjez hana msadak lvayteh yatir men haw prisha kul nash guer danrim nafsheh nezmakaj wjul dnamej nafsheh nettrim
15	karevw waw leh den af yalude dnekruv lhon wajsaw enon talmidawy waja'w bhon
16	hu den yeshu kra enon we'mar lhon shvukw tlayye azen lwazy wla tejlon enon dda'ylen guer d'ayj halen enon dilhon hi malkuza dashmayya
17	amin amar na ljon dman dla nkabel malkuza d'alaha ayj talya la neul lah
18	wsha'leh jad men rishane we'mar leh malfana tava mana ebed d'iraz jayye dalalam
19	amar leh yeshu mana kare at li tava layt tava ela en jad alaha
20	pukdane yada at la tektul wla tgur wla tegnuv wla teshad sahduza dshukra yakar la'vuj wle'maj
21	amar leh halen kulhen netrez enen men talyuzy
22	kad shma den halen yeshu emar leh jda jasira laj sel saben kul medem d'iyz laj whav lmeskine wzehwe laj simza bashmayya wza bazary
23	hu den kad shma halen keryaz leh atir wa guer tav
24	wjad jsa yeshu djeryaz leh emar aykana atla la'ylen d'iyz lhon nejse dnelon lmalkuzeh d'alaha
25	dlila y lgamla dvajrura damjata neul aw atira lmalkuzeh d'alaha
26	amrin leh aylen dashmaw wmanu meshkaj lmeja
27	yeshu den emar aylen dalwaz bnay nasha la meshkjan lwaz alaha meshkjan lmehwa
28	amar leh shemon ki'fa ha jnan shvakn kul medem we'zayn bazraj
29	amar leh yeshu amin amar na ljon dlayt nash dshavek bate aw avahe aw aje aw atza aw bnayya metul malkuza d'alaha
30	wla nkabel b'afe saguiyye' bhana savna wavalma d'aze jayye dalalam
31	wadvar yeshu lazresarteh we'mar lhon ha salkinan lu'wrishlem wmeshtalman kulhen dajzivan banviyye al breh dnasha
32	neshtlem guer lamme wanvasjon beh wnerkon b'apawy
33	wannagduna wansaruna wnektluna walyawma dazlaza nkum
34	henon den jda men halen la estakalw ela mjasya waz menhon melza hade wla yadin waw halen dmezmallan amhon
35	wjad kariv li'yriju samya jad yazev wa al yad urja wjadar
36	washma kal kensha davar wamsha'el wa dmanu hana
37	amrin leh yeshu nasrayya avar
38	waka we'mar yeshu breh ddawid ezrajam lay
39	wa'ylen d'asin waw kdamawy dyeshu ka'en waw beh dneshtuk hu den yatira'iz kae wa breh ddawid ezrajam lay
40	wkam yeshu wafkad dnaytuna lwazeh wjad krev lwazeh sha'leh
41	we'mar leh mana save at ebed laj hu den emar mary d'ejse
42	wyeshu emar leh jsi haymanuzaj ajyazaj
43	wvar shazeh jsa wa'ze wa bazreh wamshabaj la'laha wjuleh ama dajsa yav wa shuvja la'laha
