1	wahwa men bazar halen mezkrej wa yeshu bamdinaza wavkurya wmajres wa wamsabar malkuza d'alaha wazresarteh ameh
2	wneshe halen d'eza'si men kurhane wmen ruje bishaza maryam dmezkarya magdlata ha dshava shi'din nfakw menah
3	wyujan ataz kusa rabayteh dherawdes wshushan wajranyaza saguiyya'za aylen damshamshan way lhon men kenyanayhen
4	wjad kensha saguiyya' knash wa wmen kul mdinan azen waw lwazeh emar bmazle
5	nfak sarua lmesra sareh wjad sra iz danfal al yad urja we'tdish we'jalzeh parajza
6	wajrina nfal al shua wvar shazeh ia wadlayt wa leh taliluza ivesh
7	wajrina nfal bez kube wiaw ameh kube wjanku
8	wajrina nfal b'ara tavza wshapirta wia wavad pi're jad bama' halen kad amar kae wa dman d'iyz leh edne dneshma neshma
9	wsha'lu talmidawy dmanaw mazla hana
10	hu den emar lhon ljon u ihiv lmeda rasa dmalkuzeh d'alaha lhanon den sharka bfela'za meze'mar djad jasen la nejson wjad shamin la nestaklon
11	hanaw den mazla sara izawy melza d'alaha
12	hanon den dal yad urja izayhon hanon dshamin melza wa'ze beldvava shakel melza men lebhon dla nhaymnon wnijon
13	halen den dal shua halen enon dma dashmaw bjaduza mkablin lah lmelza wekara layt lhon ela dsavna y haymanuzhon wvasvan nesyuna mezkashlin
14	haw den danfal bez kube halen enon aylen dshamin melza wavsefza wavuzra wvarguigazeh dalma mezjankin wfi're la yahbin
15	haw den dva'ra tavza halen enon aylen davleba shafya wtava shamin melza wa'jdin wyahbin pi're bamsaybranuza
16	la nash manhar shraga wamjape leh bma'na aw sa'em leh tjez arsa ela sa'em leh lel men mnarta djul da'el nejse nuhreh
17	layt guer medem dajse dla nezgle wla damtashay dla nezida wni'ze lguelya
18	jsaw aykana shmaton man d'iyz leh guer nezihev leh wman dlayt leh af haw dsavar d'iyz leh neshtkel meneh
19	ezaw den lwazeh emeh wa'jawy wla meshkjin waw danmallon ameh metul kensha
20	we'marw leh emaj wa'jayk kamin lvar wsaven lmejsyaj
21	hu den na we'mar lhon halen enon emy wa'jay aylen dshamin melza d'alaha wavdin lah
22	hwa den bjad men yawmaza slek yeshu izev basfita hu wzalmidawy we'mar lhon nebar lhaw evra dyamza
23	wjad raden dmej leh hu yeshu wahwaz alala druja byamza wkariva waz sfita lmetba
24	wakrevw airu wa'mrin leh raban raban avdinan hu den kam waj'a bruje wavmajshule dyama wnajw wahwa shelya
25	we'mar lhon ayka y haymanuzjon henon den kad dajilin metdamrin waw wa'mrin jad ljad manu kay hana d'af lruje paked walmajshule walyama wmeshtamin leh
26	wardaw we'zaw la'zra dgadrayye d'iyzawy bevra lukval glila
27	wjad nfak la'ra pga beh gavra jad men mdita d'iyz beh daywa men savna saguiyya' wma'ne la lavesh wa wavvayta la amar wa ela bvez kvure
28	kad jsa den lyeshu ka wanfal kdamawy wavkala rama emar ma lan wlaj yeshu breh d'alaha mrayma bae na menaj la tshankany
29	paked wa leh guer yeshu lruja tanfa lmepak men barnasha sagui wa guer savna men dashve wa leh wmeze'sar wa bshishlaza wavjevle mezntar wa wamfasek wa asurawy wmetdvar wa men shi'da ljurba
30	sha'leh den yeshu man shmaj amar leh legyon metul ddaywe saguiyye' alilin waw beh
31	wvaen waw meneh dla nefkud lhon lmi'sal lazhuma
32	iz wa den taman bakra dajsire saguiyye' draya btura wvaen waw meneh dnapes lhon dvajsire nelon wa'pes lhon
33	wanfakw shi'de men gavra walw bajsire wzersaz bakra ha kulah lashkifa wanfalw byamza we'zjanakw
34	kad jsaw den raawaza medem dahwa rakw we'shtaiw bamdinaza wavkurya
35	wanfakw nasha dnejson medem dahwa we'zaw lwaz yeshu we'shkju lgavra haw danfakw shi'dawy kad lvish wamnakaf wyazev lwaz reglawy dyeshu wadjelw
36	we'shtaiw lhon aylen dajsaw aykana eza'si gavra haw daywana
37	wvaen waw meneh kuleh kensha dgadrayye dni'sal leh men lwazhon metul ddejlza rabza ejdaz enon hu den yeshu slek lasfita wahfaj men lwazhon
38	haw den gavra danfakw meneh shi'de bae wa meneh dalwazeh nehwe washra yeshu we'mar leh
39	hfuj lvaytaj we'shtaa medem davad laj alaha we'sal wmajres wa bjulah mdita medem davad leh yeshu
40	kad hfaj den yeshu kableh kensha saguiyya' kulhon guer leh jarin waw
41	wgavra jad dashmeh yu'arash rish knushta nfal kdam reglawy dyeshu wvae wa meneh dneul lvayteh
42	barza guer ijidata iz waz leh ayj baz shnin tartaesre wkariva waz lammaz wjad asel ameh hu yeshu kensha saguiyya' javes wa leh
43	atza den jda dazri wa dmah shnin tartaesre ha dvez asawaza kuleh kenyanah apkaz wla eshkjaz dmen nash teza'se
44	ezkarvaz men bestreh wkerbaz ljenfa dma'neh wmejda kamaz mardiza dadmah
45	we'mar yeshu manu krev li wjad kulhon kafrin amar leh shemon ki'fa wadameh raban kenshe alsin laj wjavsin wa'mar at manu krev li
46	hu den emar nash krev li ena guer yedez djayla nfak meny
47	ha den atza kad jsaz dla tazeh ezaz kad ratiza wneflaz segdaz leh we'mraz lin ama kuleh metul ayda elza kerbaz wa'ykana mejda eza'syaz
48	hu den yeshu emar lah ezlabvy brazy haymanuzejy ajyazejy sely bashlama
49	wad hu mmalel eza nash men dvez rab knushta we'mar leh mizaz lah barzaj la tamel lmalfana

53	wgajkin waw lawy dyadin dmizaz lah
