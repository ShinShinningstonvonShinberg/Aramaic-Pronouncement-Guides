1	wahwa djad al lvayta djad men rishe dafrishe dne'jul lajma byawma dshabza whenon natrin waw leh
2	wha gavra jad dajnish wa mayya iz wa kdamawy
3	wana yeshu we'mar lsafre wlafrishe d'en shalit bshabza lma'sayyu
4	henon den shzekw wa'jdeh hu wa'syeh washra
5	we'mar lhon manu menjon dnepel breh aw tawreh bvira byawma dshabza wla mejda dale masek leh
6	wla eshkajw lmetal leh pezgama al hade
7	we'mar wa mazla lwaz hanon damsamnin taman al djase wa lhon damgaben dukyaza drish smaje
8	emazy dmesdaman at men nash lvez meshtuza la ti'sal testmej laj brish smaja dalma nehwe msaman taman nash damyakar menaj
9	wni'ze haw man dlaj wleh kra wni'mar laj dhav dukza lhana wzevhaz kad ka'em at wa'jed at dukza jrata
10	ela ma d'esdamant sel estak laj bjarza dma d'aze haw dakraj ni'mar laj rajemy ezala lel we'stak wzehwe laj teshbujta kdam kulhon dasmijin amaj
11	metul djul danrim nafsheh nezmakaj wjul dnamej nafsheh nettrim
12	emar den af lhaw dakra ma daved at sharuza aw ajshamiza la tehwe kare rajmayk afla ajayk aw jyanayk wla shvavayk atire dalma wa'f henon nekrunaj wnehwe laj purana hana
13	ela ma daved at kubala kri lmeskine sguife jguise smayya
14	wtuvayk dlayt lhon dnefrunaj nehwe guer puranaj bakyama dsadike
15	kad shma den jad men hanon dasmijin halen emar leh tuvawy lman dne'jul lajma bmalkuzeh d'alaha
16	amar leh yeshu gavra jad vad ajshamiza rabza wakra lsaguiyye'
17	wshadar avdeh bedana d'ajshamiza dni'mar la'ylen dakren ha kulmedem mtayyav ljon taw
18	wshariw men jad kulhon lmeshta'lu amar leh kadmayya kriza sevnez wa'lis na d'epuk ejseh bae na menaj shvukayny dmeshte'l na
19	jrina amar jamsha sawguin tawre sevnez wa'sel na d'evke enon bae na menaj shvukayny dmeshte'l na
20	wajrina amar atza nesbez wmetul hade la meshkaj na d'ize
21	we'za haw avda we'mar lmareh halen haden rgues mare bayta we'mar lavdeh puk bagal lshuke walviryaza damdita wa'el lja lmeskine walmaje've wlamjagre wlawire
22	we'mar avda mary hwa ayj dafkadt wzuv iz azra
23	we'mar mara lavdeh puk lu'wrjaza walvez syague wa'lus dnelon dnezmle bayty
24	amar na ljon guer djad men hanon nasha dakren waw la netmon men ajshamizy
25	wjad asin waw ameh kenshe saguiyye' ezpni we'mar lhon
26	man d'aze lwazy wla sane la'vu wle'meh wla'jawy wla'jwazeh wla'tzeh wlavnawy wa'f lnafsheh talmida la meshkaj dnehwe li
27	wman dla shakel sliveh wa'ze bazary talmida la meshkaj dnehwe li
28	manu guer menjon dsave dnevne magdla wla lukdam yazev jashev nefkazeh en iz leh lamshalamuzeh
29	dla kad nsim sheze'sta wla neshkaj lamshalamu kul djasen nehon mvasjin beh
30	wa'mrin dhana gavra shari lmevna wla eshkaj lamshalamu
31	aw manu malka d'asel lakrava lmezkatashu am malka javreh wla lukdam mezrae d'en meshkaj besra alfin lme'ra lhaw d'aze lawy besrin alfin
32	we'n den la ad hu rajik meneh mshadar isgade wvae al shlama
33	hajana kulnash menjon dla shavek kuleh kenyaneh la meshkaj dnehwe li talmida
34	shapira y melja en den af melja tefkah bmana tezmlaj
35	la la'ra wla lsevla asa lvar shaden lah man d'iyz leh edne dneshma neshma
