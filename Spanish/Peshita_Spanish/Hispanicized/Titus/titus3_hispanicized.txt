1	wahwayt mahed lhon dalrishe walshalitane neshtamon wneshtabdon wadnehon zidin ljul vad tav
2	wal nash la ngadfon wla nehon mezkatshin ela nehon makijin wavjulmedem njaon basimuzhon lwaz kulhon bnaynasha
3	af jnan guer men kdim dla reyana wayn wadla pyasa wtaen wayn wamshabdin wayn la'rguigaza mshajlfaza wavvishuza wvajsama mezhapjin wayn wasni'in wayn af jad ljad sanen wayn
4	kad den ezgalyaz basimuzeh wamrajmanuzeh d'alaha majyanan
5	la bavade dsadikuza davadn ela brajmawy dileh ajyan basjaza dmawlada dmen drish wavjudaza druja dkudsha
6	ha d'eshad layn atira'iz byad yeshu mshija majyanan
7	davtaybuzeh nesdadak wnehwe yarte bsavra bjayye dalalam
8	mhaymna y melza wavhalen save na d'af at hwayt msharar lhon dnezbtel lhon lmeflaj vade tave aylen dhaymenw b'alaha halen enen tavan wmawtran lavnaynasha
9	men baza den sajlza wmen tashyaza dsharvaza wmen jeryane wmen tajtushe dsafre eshtal yuzran guer layt bhen wasrikan enen
10	men gavra heresiyyawta men bazar jda svan wzarten dmarte at leh eshtal
11	wahwayt yada dman dhajana w makam u wjate whu jayyev nafsheh
12	ma dshadrez lwazaj la'rtema aw ltujikaws nezbtel laj dzi'ze lwazy lnikapawlis taman guer samez breyany d'aste
13	al sina den safra wal apalaw nezbtel laj dazlawe enon shapir dmedem la nejsar lhon
14	wni'lpon af aylen ddilan enon dnehon paljin vade tave bsevwaza d'alsan dla nehon dla pi're
15	sha'lin bashlamaj kulhon aylen damy enon sha'l bashlama djulhon aylen drajmin lan bhaymanuza taybuza am kuljon amin
