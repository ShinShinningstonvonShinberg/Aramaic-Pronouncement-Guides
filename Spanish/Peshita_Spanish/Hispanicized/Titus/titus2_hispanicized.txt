1	at den malel medem dya'e lyulpana jlima
2	wa'lef dnehon kashishe irin breyanayhon wnehon najpin wnehon djen wnehon jlimin bhaymanuza wavjuba wvamsaybranuza
3	wa'f kashishaza hajana dnehwyan b'eskima dya'e ldejlaz alaha wla nehwyan ajlan karse wla nehwyan mshabdan ljamra saguiyya' wnehwyan malfan shapiraza
4	wamnakfan la'ylen dtalyan dnerjman balayhen wavnayhen
5	wnehwyan najpan wkadishan wnehwyan yaspan shapir dvatayhen wmeshtabdan lvalayhen dla nash ngadef al melzeh d'alaha
6	wmen aylen datlen hajana bi dnehon najpin
7	bjulmedem den dmuza bnafshaj jawa bjul vade tave wavmalfanuzaj tehwe laj melza jlimta
8	dnajpa wla mjabla wla nash basar lah d'ayna dka'em lkuvlan nevhaz kad la neshkaj dni'mar layn medem dasne
9	avde lmarayhon neshtabdon bjulmedem wneshtafron wla hwaw masen
10	wla hwaw ganbin ela njaon shrarhon tava bjulmedem dansabzon bjulmedem yulpaneh d'alaha majyanan
11	ezgalyaz guer taybuzeh d'alaha majyaz kul lavnaynasha kulhon
12	wradya lan dnejpur brusha wvarguigaza dalma wnije balma hana bnajpuza wavji'nuza wavdejlaz alaha
13	kad msakenan lsavra brija walguelyana dzeshbujteh d'alaha raba wmajyanan yeshu mshija
14	dhu yav nafsheh jlafayn dnefrkan men kul awla wandaken lnafsheh ama jaza djasem bavade tave
15	halen malel wavi wjawen bjul pukdan wla nash navse baj
