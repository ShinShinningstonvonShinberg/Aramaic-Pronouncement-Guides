1	hwaw den af nviyye dagale bama ayj d'af bjon nehon malfane dagale aylen dmalin heresis d'avdana wavmara dasvan enon kafrin kad mayten al nafshhon avdana msarhva
2	wsaguiyye' ni'sun bazar tanfuza dilhon halen dmetulazhon urja dashrara tezgadaf
3	wavaluvuza wavmele dvedya nettagron bjon halen ddinhon men kdim la batel wa'vdanhon la na'em
4	en guer alaha al mala'je dajtaw la jas ela bshishlaza damtana aguen enon bzajtayyaza wa'shlem enon dneznatron ldina dshunaka
5	wal alma kadmayya la jas ela lnuj dazmanya karusa dji'nuza ntar kad tawfana al alma drashie ayti
6	wjad lamdinaza dasdum wadamura awked wvahfujya jayyev enen kad tajwiza lrashie dazidin dnehon sam
7	af llut sadika dmezkapaj wa men hupaja davtanpuza dhalen dadla namusa izayhon waw pasi
8	bajsaza guer wavshema kad amar wa ki'na haw baynazhon yum men yum lnafsheh sadikta bavade dla namus mshanek wa
9	yada u marya dnefruk men ulsana la'ylen ddajlin leh lawale den lyawma ddina kad meshtankin netar
10	yatira'iz den la'ylen dvazar besra bregza dtam'uza asin wal maruza mvasrin maraje wamshakle dmen teshbujta la siin kad mgadfin
11	ayka dmala'je davjayla wavushna rawrvin menhon la mayten layhon men marya dina dgudafa
12	halen den d'ayj jaywaza jarshaza waw bajyana ljarba wlajvala kad b'aylen dla yadin mgadfin bajvala dilhon nezjablon
13	kad aylen dhawe bhon awla agra dawla haniyyuza jshiv lhon busama dahwa b'iymama mjatme wmalyay mume djad mezbasmin banyajazhon mezpankin
14	kad ayne iz lhon dmalyan gawra wajtahe dla mawpen kad mshadlin lnafshaza dla smijan wleba damdarash baluvuza iz lhon bnayya dlawtza
15	djad shvakw urja trista shgaw we'salw b'uwrja dvelam bar bur haw d'agra dawla ajev
16	maksanuza den dahwaz leh lmezavranuzeh azana dla kala davkala davnaynasha mallaz klaz lshatyuzeh danviyya
17	halen enon mine dla mayya nane dmen alala mezradpan halen damtana djeshuja ntir lhon
18	kad guer gunje dasrikuza mmallin mshadlin barguigaza tanfaza dvesra la'ylen dvamle kalil arkin men aylen davtayuza mezhapjin
19	wji'ruza lhon meshtawden kad henon avde izayhon dajvala lhaw guer medem dnash sje leh lhana af mshabad
20	en guer kad rakw men tanpwazeh dalma bshudaeh dmaran yeshu mshija wfarukan bhen bhalen kad mezarslin tuv mesdjen hwaz lah jarzhon dvisha men kadmata
21	pakaj wa lhon guer dla neshtawdon lu'wrja dsadikuza aw djad eshtawdaw lvazra nehpjon men pukdana kadisha d'eshtlem lhon
22	gdash lhon den halen dmazla sharira djalba dahfaj al tyuveh wajsirta dasjaz burgala dasyana
