1	shemon petraws avda washlija dyeshu mshija la'ylen dalhaymanuza shawyaz b'iykara aman eshtwiw bsadikuza dmaran wfarukan yeshu mshija
2	taybuza washlama nesgue ljon bshudaa dmaran yeshu mshija
3	ayj man djulhen aylen d'iyzayhen djayla alahayya lwaz jayye wdejlaz alaha yav byad shudaa dhaw dakra lan bzeshbujta dileh wamyatruza
4	dvi'ydayhen shudayye rawrve wyakire ljon yav davyad halen tehon shawtafe dajyana alahayya kad arkin ton men jvala darguigaza davalma
5	whi den hade kad shkaltana kuleh malin ton awsefw al haymanuzjon myatruza al den myatruza idaza
6	al den idaza mjamsnanuza al den mjamsnanuza msaybranuza al den msaybranuza dejlaz alaha
7	al den dejlaz alaha rejmaz ajuza al den rejmaz ajuza juba
8	halen guer kad shjijan ljon wyatiran la wa batile afla dla pi're mkiman ljon bshudaeh dmaran yeshu mshija
9	haw guer dla shjijan leh halen samya izawy dla jase data dukayya dajtahawy kadmayye
10	wal ha yatira'iz ajay isafw davyad vadaykon tave kratjon wagvizjon mshararta tebdon kad guer halen avdin ton la mzum meshtarin ton
11	hajana guer atira'iz mezyava ljon maalta dmalkuza dalalam dmaran wfarukan yeshu mshija
12	wal hade la mame'n na men dmahed na ljon amina'iz al halen kad tav af yadin ton wasmijin ton al shrara hana
13	dji'na den estavraz li dajma d'iyzay bfagra hana airjon buhdana
14	kad yada na dundana dfagry bagal hawe aykana d'af maran yeshu mshija awdany
15	yasef na den d'af amina'iz wa iz ljon d'af men bazar mapkana dily uhdana dhalen tehon avdin
16	la wa guer kad bazar mazle davidin b'uwmanuza asinan awdanajon jayleh wme'zizeh dmaran yeshu mshija ela kad jasayye wayn drabuza dileh
17	kad guer nsav men alaha ava ikara wzeshbujta kad kala eza leh d'ayj hana men teshbujta pa'yaz brabuzah dhanaw bery haw jabiva haw dveh estviz
18	af jnan leh lhana kala shmanan men shmayya d'eza leh kad izayn wayn ameh btura kadisha
19	wi'yz lan dsharira af melza danviyyuza ha dshapir avdin ton kad bah jarin ton ayj dvashraga dmanhar b'azra amuta dama d'iymama nanhar wshemsha nednaj blebawazjon
20	kad hade lukdam yadin ton djul nviyyuza shrayya dajzava dilah la hawya
21	la wa guer bsevyana dvarnasha ezaz men mzum nviyyuza ela kad men ruja kadisha meznagdin malelw kadishe bnaynasha d'alaha
