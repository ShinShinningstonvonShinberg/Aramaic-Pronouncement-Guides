1	hade men kadu jabivay egarta dzarten kazev na ljon halen davhen mir na buhdana lreyanjon shafya
2	dzezahdon lmele dkadem eze'mar men nviyye kadishe walfukdaneh dmaran wfarukan davyad shlije
3	kad hade lukdam yadin ton dni'zon bjarza dyawmaza mvasjane damvasjin kad ayj rguigaza dilhon mhaljin
4	wa'mrin d'ayka izawy mulkana dme'zizeh men kad guer avahazan shjevw kulmedem hajana mjatar men shurayyah davriza
5	taya lhon guer hade kad saven dashmayya izayhon waw men kdim wa'ra men mayya wavyad mayya kamaz bmelza d'alaha
6	halen dvi'ydayhon alma dhaden taf bmayya we'vad
7	shmayya den dhasha wa'ra bmelza dileh asinin kad lnura meznatrin lyawma ddina wda'vdana davnaynasha rashie
8	hade den jda la tetejon jabivay djad yawma lmarya ayj alef shnin izawy wa'lef shnin ayj yawma jad
9	la meshtawjar marya bmulkanawy ayj dnashin shujara masbrin ela magar rujeh metulazjon badla save dnash ni'vad ela kulnash lazyavuza ni'ze
10	aze den yawmeh dmarya ayj ganava haw dveh shmayya men shely avrin estukse den kad yakdin neshtron wa'ra wavade dvah teshtjaj
11	kad hajil halen kulhon meshtren d'ayj aylen sadek ljon dzehon bhupajaykon kadishe wavdejlaz alaha
12	kad msaken ton wsawjin ton lme'ziza dyawmeh d'alaha haw dveh shmayya kad mezbajrin bnura neshtron we'stukse kad yakdin nshujon
13	lashmayya den jaze wa'ra jdaza ayj mulkana dileh msakenan halen davhon sadikuza amra
14	metul hana jabivay kad lhalen msaken ton isafw dadla kuzma wadla muma leh teshtajjon bashlama
15	walmagraz rujeh dmarya purkana tejshvon aykana d'af ajon jabiva pawlaws ayj jejmza d'ezyahbaz leh kzav ljon
16	aykana davjulhen egrazeh malel bhen al halen aylen d'iyz bhen medem dasik lsukala halen d'aylen dadla yulpana enon wla smijin makmin lhen ayj d'af lhalen kzave dsharka lwaz avdana dilhon
17	aton hajil jabivay kad kadmin ton yadin tarw nafshjon ddalma kad asin ton bazar tayuza d'aylen dadla namus enon teplon men samka diljon
18	hwayton den mezraben btaybuza wvidaza dmaran wfarukan yeshu mshija wda'laha ava dleh teshbujta af hasha wavjulsvan walyawmay alma amin
