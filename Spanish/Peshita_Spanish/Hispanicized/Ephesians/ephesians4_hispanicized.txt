1	bae na hajil menjon ena asira bmaran dazhaljon aykana dya'e lkeryana d'ezkriton
2	bjulah makijuz reyana wnijuza wnaguiruz ruja wahwayton msaybrin jad ljad bjuba
3	wahwayton jfitin lmetar awyuza druja bajsaka dashlama
4	dzehon bjad pagra wvajda ruja ayj ma d'ezkriton bjad savra dkeryanjon
5	jad u guer marya wajda haymanuza wajda mamudiza
6	wjad alaha ava djul wal kul wavyad kul wavjulan
7	ljad jad menan den ezyahbaz taybuza ayj mshujza dmawhavzeh damshija
8	metul hana amir daslek lamrawma washva shviza wyav mawhvaza lavnaynasha
9	daslek den mana y ela en d'af njez lukdam lzajtayyazah d'ara
10	haw danjez huyyu haw d'af slek lel men kulhon shmayya danshalem kul
11	whu yav iz dashlije wi'yz danviyye wi'yz damsabrane wi'yz draawaza wi'yz dmalfane
12	lagmara dkadishe lavada dzeshmeshta lvenyana dfagra damshija
13	dama djulan nehwe jad medem bhaymanuza wvidaza davreh d'alaha wjad gavra gmira bamshujza dkawmza dshumlayyeh damshija
14	wla nehwe yalude dmesdasin wmeshtanen ljul ruj dyulpane njile davnaynasha hanon davjaruzhon mestanin dnaton
15	ela hwayn sharirin bjuban djul medem dilan nerbe bamshija dhuyyu risha
16	wmeneh kuleh pagra mezrakav wmezkatar bjul sharyan ayj mawhavta dmezyahba bamshujza ljul hadam lzarbiza dileh dfagra davjuba neshtalam benyaneh
17	hade den amar na wamsahed na bmarya dmen hasha la tehon mhaljin ayj sharka damme damhaljin basrikuz reyanhon
18	wjeshujin bmadayhon wnujrain enon men jayyawy d'alaha metul dlayt bhon idaza wmetul wiruz lebhon
19	hanon dafsakw savrhon wa'shlemw nafshhon lfajsuza walfuljana djulah tanfuza byanuzhon
20	aton den la wa hajana ileftuna lamshija
21	en sharira'iz shmatuna wveh ilefton ayj ma d'iyzawy kushta byeshu
22	ela daznijon menjon hupajaykon kadmayye lvarnasha haw atika dmezjabal barguigaza dtuyay
23	wzezjadzon bruja dmadaykon
24	wzelbshon lvarnasha jaza haw dva'laha ezbri bsadikuza wavjasyuza dkushta
25	metul hana anijw menjon kadavuza wmalelw kushta nash am kariveh hadame jnan guer jad djad
26	rgasw wla tejton wshemsha al rugsjon la nerav
27	wla tetlon azra la'jel-karsa
28	wa'yna dganev wa mekil la negnuv ela nel'e b'iydawy wnefluj tavaza dnehwe leh lmetal lman dasnik
29	kul mela sanya men pumjon la tepuk ela ayda dshapira wjashja lvenyana dzetel taybuza la'ylen dshamin
30	wla tehon mikin lruja kadishta d'alaha d'ezjzemton beh lyawma dfurkana
31	kulah mariruza wjemza wrugsa wrawba wgudafa neshtaklan menjon am kulah bishuza
32	wahwayton basimin jad lwaz jad wamrajmanin wahwayton shavkin jad ljad aykana d'alaha bamshija shvak lan
