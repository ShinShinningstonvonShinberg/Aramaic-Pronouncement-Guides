1	metul hana ena pawlaws asira ena dyeshu mshija al apaykon amme
2	en shmaton mdabranuza dtaybuzeh d'alaha d'ezyahbaz li bjon
3	davguelyana ezida li rasa aykana djezbez ljon basuryaza
4	ayj ma dmeshkjiton kad karen ton lmestakalu idazy davraseh damshija
5	haw davdare jrane la ezida lavnaynasha ayj dhasha ezgli lashlijawy kadishe wlanviyyawy bruj
6	dnehon amme bnay yartuzeh wshawtafe dfagreh wadmulkana d'ezihev beh byad ewangueliyyawn
7	haw d'ena hwiz mshamshaneh ayj mawhavta dtaybuzeh d'alaha d'ezyahbaz li men mabdanuza djayleh
8	li dasura na djulhon kadishe ezyahbaz taybuza hade d'esabar bamme uzreh damshija haw dla mezakav
9	we'nahar ljulnash ayda ha mdabranuza drasa haw dajse wa men alme b'alaha djul bra
10	davyad ita tezida jejmzeh d'alaha malyaz purshane la'rjaws walshultane dvashmayya
11	ha dated wa men alme wavdah byeshu mshija maran
12	haw dveh iz lan paresiyya wkarivuza bzujlana dhaymanuzeh
13	metul hana sha'elna dla tema'n li b'uwlsanay dal apaykon dhade hi teshbujtjon
14	wja'efna burkay lwaz avu dmaran yeshu mshija
15	haw dmeneh meshtamha kul avahuza dvashmayya wva'ra
16	dnetel ljon ayj uzra dzeshbujteh davjayla teshtarron brujeh davvarnashjon dalgaw
17	nemar mshija bhaymanuza wavlebawazjon bjuba kad nehwe sharir ekarjon wsheze'szjon
18	dzeshkjon lmadraju am kulhon kadishe manaw rawma wumka u'wrka wafzayya
19	wzedon rabuza didaza djubeh damshija wzezmalon bjuleh mulayya d'alaha
20	lhaw den damse bjayla yatir men kul lmebad lan wyatir men ma dsha'linan wranenan ayj jayleh dmestar ban
21	leh teshbujta biteh byeshu mshija bjulhon dare dalmay almin amin
