1	pawlaws shlija dyeshu mshija bsevyaneh d'alaha la'ylen d'iyzayhon b'efesaws kadishe wamhaymne byeshu mshija
2	shlama amjon wtaybuza men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
3	mvaraj u alaha avu dmaran yeshu mshija haw dvarjan bjul burkan druj bashmayya bamshija
4	aykana dkadem gvan beh men kdam tarmyazeh dalma dnehwe kadishe wadla mum kdamawy wavjuba kadem rashman leh
5	wsaman lavnayya byeshu mshija ayj dashfar lsevyaneh
6	dneshtabaj shuvja dtaybuzeh haw d'ashpa layn byad jabiveh
7	dveh iz lan purkana wvadmeh shuvkana dajtahe ayj uzra dtaybuzeh
8	ha d'ezyatraz ban bjul jejma wavjul sukal
9	wa'wdan rasa dsevyaneh haw dkadem wa sam dnesur beh
10	lamdabranuza dmulahon dsavne djulmedem men drish nezjadaz bamshija dvashmayya wva'ra
11	wveh jnan ezgvin ayj dkadem rashman wasva haw djul saar ayj tariza dsevyaneh
12	dnehwe jnan aylen dkademn sabarn bamshija lhedra dzeshbujteh
13	d'af aton beh shmaton melza dkushta d'iyzeh svarza djayyaykon wveh haymenton we'zjzemton bruja dkudsha damlija waz
14	ha d'iyzeh rahvuna dyartuzan lfurkana d'aylen dja'en walshuvja d'iykareh
15	metul hana af ena ha men dshemez haymanuzjon davmaran yeshu mshija wjubjon dalwaz kadishe
16	la meshtale na lmawdayyu al apaykon walmehadjon baslawazy
17	d'alaheh dmaran yeshu mshija ava dzeshbujta netel ljon ruja djejmza wadguelyana bidazeh
18	wnenhran ayne dlebawazjon dzedon manaw savra dkeryaneh wmanu uzra dshuvja dyartuzeh bkadishe
19	wmana hi yatiruza drabuza djayleh ban b'aylen damhaymninan ayj mabdanuza dzukfa djayleh
20	davad bamshija wa'kimeh men bez mize wa'wtveh men yamineh bashmayya
21	lel men kulhen arjaws wshultane wjayle wmarawaza walel men kul shem dmeshtamah la baljud balma hana ela af bdazid
22	wshabed kulmedem tjez reglawy wleh d'iyzawy lel men kul yahbeh risha lita
23	d'iyzeh gushmeh wshumlayya dhaw djul bjul mshamle
