1	bnayya eshtaw la'vahaykon bmaran hade guer ki'na
2	whanaw pukdana kadmayya damlij yakar la'vuj wle'maj
3	dnehwe laj dshapir wni'rkon jayyayk al ara
4	avahe la targson bnaykon ela rabaw enon bmarduza wavyulpana dmaran
5	avde eshtaw lmaraykon dvavsar bdejlza wvarziza wvafshituz leba ayj dlamshija
6	la bmajse ayna ayj haw dlavnaynasha shafrin ton ela ayj avde damshija davdin sevyana d'alaha
7	wshameshw enon men kulah nafshjon bjuba ayj dalmaran wla ayj dlavnaynasha
8	kad yaditon dhaw medem daved nash dshapir hu mezpra men maran en avda u we'n bar ji're
9	af aton marayya hajana hwayton avdin lavdaykon hwayton shavkin lhon sajluza metul dyadin ton d'af marjon diljon izawy bashmayya wmasav b'ape layt lwazeh
10	mekil ajay ezjayyalw bmaran wavzukfa djayleh
11	walvashw kuleh sayna d'alaha aykana dzeshkjon lamkam lukval senazeh d'ajel karsa
12	metul dzajtushjon la wa am besra wadma ela am arjaws wam shalitane wam ajiday alma dhana jeshuja wam ruje bishaza dazjez shmayya
13	metul hana lvashw kuleh sayna d'alaha dzeshkjon te'ron bisha wjad matdin ton bjulmedem tkumon
14	kumw hajil wajsukw jasaykon bkushta walvashw sheryana dsadikuza
15	wsa'nw breglaykon tuyyaveh d'ewangueliyyawn dashlama
16	wam halen savw ljon sakra dhaymanuza dvah tezmson jayla lmadaju kulhon gue'rawy yakde dvisha
17	wsimw sanwarta dfurkana wa'judw sayfa druja d'iyzawy melza d'alaha
18	wavjul slawan wavjul bawan salaw bjulsvan bruj wvah basluza hwayton shahrin bjuledan kad msaleton amina'iz wmezkashfin al apay kulhon kadishe
19	af lay dzezihev li melza bmaftaj pumy din bagle ajres rasa dasvarza
20	haw d'ena isgadeh bshishlaza davparesiyya emalliwy ayj dwale li lammalaluzeh
21	dzedon den af aton medem dalwazy wmedem dsaar na ha mawda ljon tujikaws aja jabiva wamshamshana mhaymna bmaran
22	dleh shadrez lwazjon leh al hade dzedon ma dalwazy wanvayya lebawazjon
23	shlama am ajayn wjuba am haymanuza men alaha ava wmen maran yeshu mshija
24	taybuza am kulhon aylen dmajvin lmaran yeshu mshija dla jval amin
