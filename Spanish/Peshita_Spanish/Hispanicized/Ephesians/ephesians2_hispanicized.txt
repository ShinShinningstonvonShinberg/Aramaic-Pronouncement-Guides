1	wa'f ljon dmizin wayton bajtahaykon wavsajlwazjon
2	bhalen dmen kdim halejton hwayton bhen ayj almayyuzeh dalma hana wa'yj sevyaneh drish shultana d'a'ar wadruja hade dmezjapta bavnayya dla mettpisanuza
3	bhanon vade d'af jnan ezhapajn bhon men kdim barguigaza dvesran wavdin wayn sevyana dvesran wadzarizan wavnayya wayn drugsa malya'iz ayj sharka
4	alaha den datir brajmawy metul jubeh saguiyya' d'ajvan
5	kad mizin wayn bajtahayn ajyan am mshija wavtaybuzeh parkan
6	wa'kiman ameh wa'wtvan ameh bashmayya byeshu mshija
7	danjawe lalme d'azen rabuza duzra dtaybuzeh wvasimuzeh dahwaz layn byeshu mshija
8	btaybuzeh u guer ezprekn bhaymanuza whade la waz menjon ela mawhavta y d'alaha
9	la men vade dla nash neshtavhar
10	briza jnan guer dileh d'ezbrin byeshu mshija lavade tave hanon dmen kdim tayyev alaha davhon nhalej
11	metul hade hwayton ahdin d'aton amme men kdim davsar hwayton wmezkren wayton urluza men ha dmezkarya gsurta wi'yzeh vad idayya bvesra
12	wi'yzaykon wayton bhaw savna dla mshija wnujrain wayton men dubare d'iysrayyel wa'jsnayye hu ldiyyaziki dmulkana wadla svar wayton wadla alah balma
13	hasha den byeshu mshija aton dmen kdim rajikin wayton karive hwayton badmeh damshija
14	huyyu guer shaynan haw davad tartayhen jda washra syaga dka'em wa bamsaza
15	waveldvavuza bvesreh wnamusa dfukade bfukdanawy batel dlazrayhon nevre baknumeh ljad barnasha jaza wavad shayna
16	wrai lazrayhon bjad pgar am alaha wvaskifeh ktal beldvavuza
17	we'za sabar shlama ljon lrajike walkarive
18	metul dveh u hwa lan kurava lazren bajda ruj lwaz ava
19	mekil la hwayton ajsnayye wla tawtave ela bnay mdita dkadishe wavnay bayteh d'alaha
20	we'zbniton al sheze'sta dashlije wdanviyye whu hwa rish karna dvenyana yeshu mshija
21	wveh mezrakav kuleh benyana wrave lhaykla kadisha bmarya
22	kad af aton beh mezbnen ton lmamra d'alaha bruj
