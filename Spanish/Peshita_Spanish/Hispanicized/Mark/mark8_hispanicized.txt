1	bhanon den yawmaza kad kensha saguiyya' iz wa wla iz wa medem dne'jlon kra lzalmidawy we'mar lhon
2	mezrajam na al kensha hana dha tlaza yawmin kawiw lwazy wlayt lhon mana ne'jlon
3	we'n hu dshare na lhon kad samin lvatayhon apin b'uwrja nasha guer menhon men rujka ati'in
4	amrin leh talmidawy aymeka meshkaj nash harka bjurba dansaba lajma lhalen kulhon
5	wsha'el enon hu kma lajmin iz ljon amrin leh shava
6	wafkad ljenshe dnestamkon al ara wansav hanon shava lajmin wvarej waksa wyav lzalmidawy dansimon wsamw ljenshe
7	wi'yz waw nune kalil wa'f layhon barej we'mar dansimon enon
8	we'jalw wasvaw washkalw tawtare daksayye shava espridin
9	izayhon waw den nasha d'ejalw ayj arba alfin
10	washra enon waslek mejda lasfita am talmidawy we'za la'zra ddalmanuza
11	wanfakw prishe wshariw lmeva ameh wsha'lin waw leh aza men shmayya kad mnasen leh
12	we'ttanaj brujeh we'mar mana baya aza sharbza hade amin amar na ljon dla tezihev lah aza lsharbza hade
13	washvak enon waslek lasfita we'salw lhaw evra
14	wataw dnesvon lajma we'la jda grista layt wa amhon basfita
15	wfaked enon we'mar lhon jsaw esdarw men jmira dafrishe wmen jmireh dherawdes
16	wmezjashvin waw jad am jad wa'mrin dlajma layt lan
17	yeshu den ida we'mar lhon mana ranen ton dlajma layt ljon la dama lhasha yadin ton wla mestaklin ton dakil leba kashya iz ljon
18	wayne iz ljon wla jasen ton we'dne iz ljon wla shamin ton wla ahdin ton
19	kad halen jamsha lajmin ksiz ljamsha alfin kma kufinin daksayye kad mlen shkalton amrin leh tresar
20	amar lhon wjad shava la'rba alfin kma espridin daksayye kad mlen shkalton amrin shava
21	amar lhon aykaw la dama lhasha mestaklin ton
22	we'za lvez-sayyada wa'ytiw leh samya wvaen waw meneh dnekruv leh
23	we'jad b'iydeh dsamya wa'pkeh lvar men kriza wrak baynawy wsam ideh wsha'leh dmana jase
24	jar we'mar jase na bnay nasha ayj ilane damhaljin
25	tuv sam ideh al aynawy wazken wjase wa kul medem nahira'iz
26	wshadreh lvayteh we'mar af la lakriza teul wla ti'mar lnash bakriza
27	wanfak yeshu wzalmidawy lkurya dkesariyya'-dfilipaws wamsha'el wa lzalmidawy b'uwrja wa'mar lhon manu amrin lay nasha d'iyzay
28	henon den emarw dyujanan mamdana wajrane d'iliyya wajrane jad men nviyye
29	amar lhon yeshu aton den manu amrin ton lay d'iyzay na shemon we'mar leh at u mshija breh d'alaha jayya
30	waj'a bhon dalnash la ni'mron lawy
31	wshari wa lmalafu enon dazid u breh dnasha dnejash sagui wadnestle men kashishe wmen rabay kahne wmen safre wnezktel wlazlaza yawmin nkum
32	win bagle melza mmalel wa wdavreh ki'fa wshari lmeja' beh
33	hu den ezpni wjar bzalmidawy waj'a bshemon we'mar sel laj lvestary satana dla rane at d'alaha ela davnay nasha
34	wakra yeshu ljenshe am talmidawy we'mar lhon man dsave dni'ze bazary nejpur bnafsheh wneshkul skifeh wni'ze bazary
35	kul man dsave guer dnaje nafsheh nawbdih wjul dnawbed nafsheh metulazy wmetul svarzy najeh
36	mana guer nezadar barnasha en alma kuleh ni'zar wnafsheh nejsar
37	aw mana netel barnasha tajlufa dnafsheh
38	kul guer dnevhaz bi wavmelay bsharbza hade jatata wgayyarza wa'f breh dnasha nevhaz beh ma d'aze bshuvja d'avu am mala'jawy kadishe
