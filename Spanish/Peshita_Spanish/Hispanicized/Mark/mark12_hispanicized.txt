1	wshari danmalel amhon bmazle gavra jad nsav karma wa'jdreh syaga wajfar beh masarta wavna beh magdla wa'wjdeh lfalaje wajsak
2	wshadar lwaz palaje avdeh bsavna dmen pi're djarma nesav
3	henon den mja'u wshadru kad sfik
4	wshadar tuv lwazhon avda jrina wa'f lhaw ragmu wsalfu wshadru bsara
5	wshadar tuv jrina af lhaw katlu walsaguiyye' avde jrane shadar wmenhon mjaw menhon den katelw
6	jarza den jad bra jabiva iz wa leh wshadreh lwazhon jrayyaz emar guer kvar nevhzon men bery
7	hanon den palaje emarw bnafshhon hanaw yarta taw nektliwy wzehwe dilan yartuza
8	wansavw katlu wa'pku lvar men karma
9	mana hajil nebed mare karma ni'ze nawbed lhanon palaje wnetliwy karma lajrane
10	wa'fla kzava hana krayton dji'fa d'asliw banayye hi hwaz lrisha dsawiza
11	men lwaz marya hwaz hade wi'yzeh tedmurta baynayn
12	wvaen waw lme'jdeh wadjelw men ama idaw guer dalayhon emar mazla hana wshavku we'salw
13	wshadarw lwazeh nasha men safre wmen dvez herawdes dansuduna bmelza
14	henon den ezaw wsha'lu malfana yadin nan dsharir at wla shkil at sefza lnash la guer ja'ar at bparsupe davnay nasha ela bashrara urja d'alaha malef at shalit lmetal ksef risha lkesar aw la netel aw la netel
15	hu den ida nejlhon we'mar lhon mana mnasen ton li aytaw li dinara ejse
16	wa'ytiw leh amar lhon dmanu salma hana wajzava henon den emarw dkesar
17	amar lhon yeshu dkesar havw lkesar wda'laha la'laha wazmahw waw beh
18	we'zaw sadukayye lwazeh hanon d'amrin dakyamta layt wamsha'lin waw leh wa'mrin
19	malfana mushe kzav lan d'en ma'ez aju dnash wshavek atza wavnayya la shavek nesav aju atzeh wankim sara la'ju
20	shava ajin iz waw kadmayya nsav atza wmiz wla shvak sara
21	wdazren nasbah wmiz kad af la hu shvak sara wdazlaza hajwaz
22	wshavazayhon nasbuh wla shvakw sara jrayyaz kulhon mizaz af hi atza
23	bakyamta hajil d'ayna menhon tehwe atza shavazayhon guer nasbuh
24	amar lhon yeshu la wa metul hade ten ton dla yadin ton kzave wla jayleh d'alaha
25	ma guer dkamw men mize la nasbin neshe af la neshe hawyan lgavre ela izayhon ayj mala'je dvashmayya
26	al mize den dkamin la krayton bajzava dmushe aykana men sanya emar leh alaha ena na alaheh d'avraham wa'laheh d'iysjak wa'laheh dyakuv
27	wla hwa alaha dmize ela djayye aton hajil sagui taen ton
28	wakrev jad men safre washma enon ddarshin wajsa dshapir aziv lhon pezgama wsha'leh aynaw pukdana kadmayya djulhon
29	amar leh yeshu kadma men kulhon pukdane shma israyyel marya alahan marya jad u
30	wadzerjam lmarya alahaj men kuleh lebaj wmen kulah nafshaj wmen kuleh reyanaj wmen kuleh jaylaj hanaw pukdana kadmayya
31	wdazren ddame leh dzajev lkarivaj ayj nafshaj pukdana jrina drab men halen layt
32	amar leh haw safra shapir rabi bashrara emart djad u wlayt jrin lvar meneh
33	wadnerjmiwy nash men kuleh leba wmen kuleh reyana wmen kulah nafsha wmen kuleh jayla wadnerjam kariveh ayj nafsheh yatira y men kulhon yakde wdevje
34	yeshu den jsa djakima'iz mfane pezgama na we'mar leh la wayt rajik men malkuza d'alaha wla nash tuv amraj dansha'liwy
35	wana yeshu we'mar kad malef bhaykla aykana amrin safre damshija breh u ddawid
36	hu guer dawid emar bruja dkudsha d'emar marya lmary tev laj men yaminy dama d'esim beldvavayk kuvsha tjez reglayk
37	hu hajil dawid kare leh mary wa'ykana breh izawy wjuleh kensha shama wa leh basima'iz
38	wavyulpaneh amar wa lhon esdarw men safre dsaven dve'stle nhaljon wrajmin shlama bshuke
39	wrish mawtve bajnushaza wrish smaje bajshamyaza
40	hanon d'ajlin bate d'armlaza belza dmawrjin slawazhon henon nkablon dina yatira
41	wjad izev yeshu lukval bez gasa ja'ar wa aykana kenshe ramen urfana bez gasa wsaguiyye' atire ramen waw sagui
42	we'zaz armalta jda meskinta armyaz tren mnin d'iyzayhon shamune
43	wakra yeshu lzalmidawy we'mar lhon amin amar na ljon dhade armalta meskinta yatir men kulhon nasha dramen armyaz bez gasa
44	kulhon guer men medem dyatir lhon armiw hade den men jasiruzah kul medem d'iyz wa lah armyazeh kuleh kenyanah
