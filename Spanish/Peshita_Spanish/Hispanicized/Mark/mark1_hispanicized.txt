1	risha d'ewangueliyyawn dyeshu mshija breh d'alaha
2	ayj dajziv b'eshaya nviyya ha mshadar na mala'jy kdam parsupaj dnazken urjaj
3	kala dkare bmadbra tayyevw urjeh dmarya wa'shwaw shvilawy
4	hwa yujanan bmadbra mamed wmajres mamudiza dazyavuza lshuvkana dajtahe
5	wnafka waz lwazeh kulah kawr dihud wjulhon bnay urishlem wmamed wa lhon byurdnan kad mawden bajtahayhon
6	hu den yujanan lvish wa lvusha dsara dgamle wa'sir wa erakza dmeshka bjasawy wme'julteh izeh waz kamse wdevsha dvara
7	wmajres wa wa'mar ha aze bazary djaylzan meny haw dla shawe na d'eghan eshre arke damsanawy
8	ena amedtjon bmayya hu den namedjon bruja dkudsha
9	wahwa byawmaza hanon eza yeshu men nasraz daglila we'zmed byurdnan men yujanan
10	wmejda daslek men mayya jsa d'estdekw shmayya wruja ayj yawna dnejtaz lawy
11	wkala hwa men shmayya at u bery jabiva baj estviz
12	wmejda apekteh ruja lmadbra
13	wahwa taman bmadbra yawmaza arbin kad meznase men satana wi'yzawy wa am jaywaza wamshamshin waw leh mala'je
14	bazar d'eshtlem den yujanan eza leh yeshu laglila wmajres wa svarza dmalkuzeh d'alaha
15	we'mar shlem leh savna wamtaz malkuza d'alaha tuvw whaymenw basvarza
16	wjad mhalej jdaray yama daglila jsa lshemon wla'ndre'was aju dramen msidaza byama izayhon waw guer sayyade
17	we'mar lhon yeshu taw bazary we'bedjon sayyade davnay nasha
18	wmejda shvakw msidazhon we'salw bazreh
19	wjad var kalil jsa lyakuv bar savday walyujanan aju wa'f lhon basfita damzaknin msidazhon
20	wakra enon wmejda shvakw lsavday avuhon basfita am aguire we'salw bazreh
21	wjad alw lajfarnajum mejda malef wa bshabe bajnushazhon
22	wzamihin hwaw byulpaneh malef wa lhon guer ayj mshalta wla ayj safrayhon
23	wi'yz wa bajnushthon gavra d'iyz beh ruja tama'za waka
24	we'mar ma lan wlaj yeshu nasrayya ezayt lmawbaduzan yada na laj man at kadisheh d'alaha
25	waj'a beh yeshu we'mar sjur pumaj wfuk meneh
26	washdazeh ruja tanfza wakaz bkala rama wnefkaz meneh
27	we'tdamarw kulhon wvaen waw jad am jad wa'mrin dmana y hade wmanu yulpana hana jaza davshultana wa'f lruje tanfaza paked wmeshtaman leh
28	wmejda nfak tebeh bjuleh azra daglila
29	wanfakw men knushta we'zaw lvayteh dshemon wda'ndre'was am yakuv wyujanan
30	wajmazeh dshemon ramya waz b'eshaza we'marw leh leh
31	wakrev ajdah b'iydah wa'kimah wmejda shvakzah eshazah wamshamsha waz lhon
32	bramsha den bmaravay shemsha aytiw lwazeh kulhon aylen dvisha'iz vidin wdaywane
33	wamdita kulah knisha waz al tara
34	wa'si lsaguiyye' dvisha'iz vidin waw bjurhane mshajlfe wdaywe saguiyye' apek wla shavek wa lhon ldaywe danmallon metul dyadin waw leh
35	wavsafra kadem kam tav we'sal la'zra jurba wzaman msale wa
36	wvaen waw leh shemon wadameh
37	wjad eshkju amrin leh kulhon nasha baen laj
38	amar lhon halejw lkurya wlamdinaza dkarivan d'af taman ajres lhade guer eziz
39	wmajres wa bjulhen knushazhon bjulah glila wmapek shi'de
40	we'za lwazeh garba wanfal al reglawy wvae wa meneh we'mar leh en save at meshkaj at lamdakayyuzy
41	hu den yeshu ezrajam lawy wafshat ideh krev leh we'mar save na etdaka
42	wvah bshaza esal garveh meneh we'tdaki
43	waj'a beh wa'pkeh
44	we'mar leh jsi lma lnash amar at ela sel jawa nafshaj ljahne wkarev kurbana jlaf tadkizaj aykana dfaked mushe lsahduzhon
45	hu den kad nfak shari wa majres sagui wa'tbah lmelza aykana dla neshkaj wa yeshu galya'iz dneul lamdita ela lvar hwa b'azra jurba wa'zen waw lwazeh men kul duka
