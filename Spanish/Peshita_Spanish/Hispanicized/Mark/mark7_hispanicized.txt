1	wajnashw lwazeh prishe wsafre d'ezaw men urishlem
2	wajsaw nashin men talmidawy d'ajlin lajma kad la mshagan idayhon we'zadalw
3	kulhon guer ihudayye wafrishe en hu davtila'iz la mshiguin idayhon la lasin metul d'ajidin mashlmanuza dkashishe
4	wmen shuka ela amdin la lasin wsaguiyya'za jranyaza iz aylen dkabelw dnetron mamudyaza djase wadkeste wadma'nay njasha wadarsaza
5	wsha'lu safre wafrishe lmana talmidayk la mhaljin ayj mashlmanuza dkashishe ela kad la mshagan idayhon ajlin lajma
6	hu den emar lhon shapir eznabi laykon eshaya nviyya nasbay b'ape ayj dajziv dama hana bsefwazeh u myakar li lebhon den sagui rajik meny
7	wasrika'iz dajlin li kad malfin yulpane dfukdane davnay nasha
8	shvakton guer pukdana d'alaha wa'jidin ton mashlmanuza davnay nasha mamudyaza djase wadkeste wsaguiyya'za aylen dalhalen damyan
9	amar lhon shapir talmin ton pukdana d'alaha dazkimon mashlmanuzjon
10	mushe guer emar dyakar la'vuj wle'maj wman damsaje la'va wle'ma mawta nmuz
11	aton den amrin ton en ni'mar gavra la'vu aw le'meh kurbany medem dmeny ti'zar
12	wla shavkin ton leh dnebed medem la'vu aw le'meh
13	wmaslen ton melza d'alaha metul mashlmanuza d'ashlemton waddamyan lhalen saguiyya'za avdin ton
14	wakra yeshu ljensha kuleh we'mar lhon shumuny kuljon we'stalw
15	layt medem dalvar men barnasha hu wa'el leh dmeshkaj msayyev leh ela medem dnafek meneh haw u msayyev lvar nasha
16	man d'iyz leh edne dneshma neshma
17	kad den al yeshu lvayta men kensha sha'lu talmidawy al mazla haw
18	amar lhon hajana af aton atlin ton la yadin ton djul medem dmen lvar a'el lvar nasha la meshkaj msayyev leh
19	dla wa llebeh a'el ela ljarseh wmeshtde bzadkiza damdakya kulah me'julta
20	medem den dnafek men barnasha haw u msayyev lvar nasha
21	men lgaw guer men leba davnay nasha nafkan majshvaza bishaza gawra sanyuza ganavuza ketla
22	aluvuza bishuza nejla sajnuza ayna bishta gudafa shavhranuza shatyuza
23	halen kulhen bishaza men lgaw u nafkan wamsayvan leh lvarnasha
24	men taman kam yeshu we'za lazjuma dsur wadsaydan wal lvayta jad wla save wa dnash neda beh wla eshkaj dnetshe
25	mejda guer shemaz atza jda metulazeh d'iyz waz lvarzah ruja tanfza we'zaz neflaz kdam reglawy
26	hi den atza izeh waz janfza men puniki dsuriyya wvaya waz meneh dnapek shi'da men barzah
27	we'mar lah yeshu shvuky lukdam dnesbon bnayya la waz guer shapira dnesav lajma davnayya wnarme ljalbe
28	hi den naz wa'mra leh in mary af kalbe men tjez pazure ajlin partuze davnayya
29	amar lah yeshu sely metul hade melza nfak leh shi'da men barzejy
30	we'saz lvaytah we'shkjaz barzah kad ramya barsa wnapik menah shi'dah
31	tuv nfak yeshu men tjuma dsur wadsaydan we'za lyama daglila bazjuma desraz-mdinaza
32	wa'ytiw leh jarsha jad pi'ka wvae waw meneh dansim lawy ida
33	wnagdeh men kensha baljudawy wa'rmi sevazeh b'ednawy wrak wakrev lleshaneh
34	wjar bashmayya we'ttanaj we'mar leh ezpaj
35	wvah bshaza ezpataj ednawy we'shtari esara dleshaneh wmalel pshika'iz
36	wsahar enon dalnash la ni'mron wajma dhu msahar wa lhon henon yatir majrsin waw
37	wyatira'iz metdamrin waw wa'mrin djul medem shapir aved ljarshe aved dneshmon wadla mmallin danmallon
