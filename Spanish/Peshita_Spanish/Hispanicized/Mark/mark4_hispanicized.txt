1	tuv den shari wa malef al yad yama we'zkanashw lwazeh kenshe saguiyye' ayj dnesak netev leh basfita byama wjuleh kensha ka'em wa al ara al yad yama
2	wmalef wa lhon bmazle sagui wa'mar wa byulpaneh
3	shmaw ha nfak sarua lmesra
4	wjad sra iz danfal al yad urja we'zaz parajza we'jalzeh
5	jrina den nfal al shua ayka dlayt ara sagui wvar shazeh blas metul dlayt wa umka d'ara
6	kad dnaj den shemsha jma wmetul dlayt wa leh ekara ivesh
7	wajrina nfal bez kube waslekw kube wjanku wfi're la yav
8	jrina den nfal al ara tavza waslek warva wyav pi're iz dazlazin wi'yz deshtin wi'yz dama'
9	we'mar wa man d'iyz leh edne dneshma neshma
10	kad hwaw den baljudayhon sha'lu henon dameh am tresarteh mazla haw
11	we'mar lhon yeshu ljon ihiv lmeda rasa dmalkuzeh d'alaha lvarayye den kul medem bmazle hawe
12	djad jasen nejson wla nejson wjad shamin neshmon wla nestaklon dalma nezpnon wneshtavkon lhon jtahayhon
13	we'mar lhon la yadin ton leh lmazla hana wa'ykana kulhon mazle tedon
14	sarua dasra melza sra
15	halen den dal yad urja halen enon dmesdara bhon melza wma dashmaw mejda aze satana wshakel lah lmelza dasria blebhon
16	whanon dal shua esdraw halen enon dma dashmaw melza mejda bjaduza mkablin lah
17	wlayt lhon ekara bnafshhon ela dsavna enon wma dahwa ulsana aw rdufya metul melza gal mezkashlin
18	whanon dvez kube mesdarin halen enon hanon dashmaw melza
19	wrenya dalma hana wtuyay duzra wsharka darguigaza jranyaza a'lan jankan lah lmelza wadla pi're hawya
20	whanon dva'ra tavza esdraw halen enon dshamin melza wamkablin wyahbin pi're bazlazin wvashtin wvama'
21	we'mar lhon dalma aze shraga dazjez sa'za nettsim aw tjez arsa la wa dal mnarta nettsim
22	layt guer medem datshe dla nezgle wla wa btushya wla mezgle
23	en nash iz leh edne dneshma neshma
24	we'mar lhon jsaw mana shamin ton bha kyalta damjilin ton mettjil ljon wmettawsaf ljon la'ylen dshamin
25	man d'iyz leh guer nezihev leh wman dlayt leh af haw d'iyz leh neshtkel meneh
26	we'mar wa hajana y malkuza d'alaha ayj nash dnarme sara b'ara
27	wnedmaj wankum blilya wvi'ymama wsara nerbe wni'raj kad hu la yada
28	ara guer maytya leh lfi'ra wlukdam hawe esba wvazreh shebla jrayyaz den jetza mshamlayta bshebla
29	ma dashmen den pi'ra mejda azya magla dmati jsada
30	we'mar lmana ndameh lmalkuza d'alaha wva'yna mazla namtlih
31	ayj predta y djardla ha dma d'esdaraz b'ara surya y men kulhon sarune dal ara
32	wma d'esdaraz salka whawya raba men kulhon yarkune wavda sawke rawrvaza ayj dzeshkaj davtelalah parajza teshkan
33	bmazle d'ayj halen mmalel wa yeshu amhon mazle ayj dmeshkjin waw lmeshma
34	wadla mazle la mmalel wa amhon lzalmidawy den baynawy walhon mfashek wa kulmedem
35	we'mar lhon bhaw yawma bramsha nebar lan levra
36	washvakw ljenshe wdavru kad basfita hu wasfine jranyaza iz way amhon
37	wahwaz alala rabza wruja wgalle naflin waw basfita wkariva waz dzezmle
38	hu den yeshu al besadya dmej wa bjarzah dasfita we'zaw akimu wa'mrin leh raban la btil laj d'avdin nan
39	wkam waj'a bruja we'mar lyama shli sguir at wshelyaz ruja wahwa nawja raba
40	we'mar lhon lmana dajulzanin ton hajan walmana layt bjon haymanuza
41	wadjelw dejlza rabza wa'mrin waw jad ljad manu kay hana druje wyama meshtamin leh
