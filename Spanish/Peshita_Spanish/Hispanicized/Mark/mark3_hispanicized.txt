1	wal tuv yeshu lajnushta wi'yz wa taman gavra jad dyabisha ideh
2	wnatrin waw leh d'en ma'se leh bshabza nkatrguna
3	we'mar lhaw gavra dyabisha ideh kum bamsaza
4	emar den af lhon shalit bshabza lmebad dtav aw dvish nafsha lmajayyu aw lmawbadu henon den shatikin waw
5	wjar bhon bjemta kad karya leh al kashyuz lebhon we'mar lhaw gavra pshut idaj wafshat wzeknaz ideh
6	wanfakw prishe bar shazeh am dvez herawdes wmelka nsavw lawy aykana dnawbduna
7	wyeshu am talmidawy esal leh lwaz yama wama saguiyya' men glila nakpeh wa wmen ihud
8	wmen urishlem wmen adum wmen evra dyurdnan wmen sur wmen saydan kenshe saguiyye' dashmaw waw kul daved ezaw lwazeh
9	we'mar lzalmidawy dankarvon leh sfita metul kenshe dla nejbsuna
10	saguiyye' guer ma'se wa dama dnehon naflin lawy metul dnezkarvon leh
11	wa'ylen d'iyz way lhon majwaza druje tanfaza ma dajsa'u naflin waw wkaen wa'mrin at u breh d'alaha
12	wsagui ka'e wa bhon dla negluna
13	waslek ltura wakra la'ylen dasva we'zaw lwazeh
14	wagva tresar dnehon ameh wdanshadar enon dnajrson
15	wadnehon shalitin dna'son krihe wnapkon daywe
16	wshami lshemon shma ki'fa
17	walyakuv bar savday walyujanan aju dyakuv sam lhon shma bnay-rgueshy d'iyzawy bnay rama
18	wa'ndre'was wfilipaws wvar-tulmay wmatay wza'wma wyakuv bar jalfay wzaday wshemon knanayya
19	wihuda sjaryuta haw d'ashlmeh we'zaw lvayta
20	we'zkanashw kenshe tuv aykana dla neshkjon waw lajma lme'jal
21	washmaw jyanawy wanfakw lme'jdeh amrin waw guer dmen hawneh nfak
22	wsafre aylen dmen urishlem njezw amrin waw belsvuv iz beh wavrisha ddaywe mapek daywe
23	wakra enon yeshu wavmazle emar lhon aykana meshkaj satana lsatana lmapaku
24	en malkuza guer al nafshah tezpalag la meshkja lamkam malkuza ha
25	we'n bayta al nafsheh nezpalag la meshkaj bayta haw lamkam
26	we'n hu dsatana kam al nafsheh we'zpalag la meshkaj lamkam ela jarzeh i
27	la nash meshkaj dneul lvez jasina wnejtuf ma'nawy ela en lukdam ljasina ne'sur whaden bayteh nebus
28	amin amar na ljon djulhon jtahe wgudafe dangadfon bnay nasha neshtavkon lhon
29	man den dangadef al ruja dkudsha layt leh shuvkana lalam ela mjayyav u ldina dalalam
30	metul d'amrin waw druja tanfza iz beh
31	we'zaw emeh wa'jawy kamin lvar wshadarw dnekruna lhon
32	yazev wa den jdarawy kensha we'marw leh ha emaj wa'jayk lvar baen laj
33	wana we'mar lhon man i emy wman enon ajay
