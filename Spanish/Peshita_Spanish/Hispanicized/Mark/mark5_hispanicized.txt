1	we'za levra dyama la'zra dgadrayye
2	wjad nfak men sfita pga beh men bez kvure gavra d'iyz beh ruja tanfza
3	wamar wa bez kvure wavshishlaza nash la meshkaj wa lme'sreh
4	metul djul emazy davsutme wavshishlaza meze'sar wa shishlaza mzabar wa wsutme mfasek wa wla nash meshkaj wa lmejbsheh
5	wavjul svan blilya wvi'ymama bvez kvure wavture izawy wa wkae wa wamsalef nafsheh bji'fe
6	kad jsa den lyeshu men rujka rhet sgued leh
7	waka bkala rama we'mar ma li wlaj yeshu breh d'alaha mrayma mawme na laj b'alaha dla tshankany
8	amar wa leh guer puk men barnasha ruja tanfa
9	wsha'leh aykana shmaj amar leh legyon shman metul dsaguiyye' nan
10	wvae wa meneh sagui dla nshadriwy lvar men azra
11	iz wa den taman lwaz tura bakra rabza dajsire draya
12	wvaen waw meneh hanon shi'de wa'mrin shadaryan al hanon jsire davhon neul
13	wa'pes lhon wanfak ruje halen tanfaza wal bajsire wrehtaz ha bakra lashkifa wneflaz byama ayj tren alfin we'zjanakw bmayya
14	whanon draen waw lhon rakw we'marw bamdita wa'f bkurya wanfakw lmejsa medem dahwa
15	we'zaw lwaz yeshu wajsa'u lhaw dshi'dawy kad lvish wamnakaf wyazev haw d'iyz wa beh legyon wadjelw
16	we'shtaiw lhon hanon dajsaw d'aykana hwa lhaw dshi'dawy wa'f al hanon jsire
17	wshariw baen meneh dni'sal leh men tjumhon
18	wjad slek lasfita bae wa meneh haw dshi'dawy dameh nehwe
19	wla shavkeh ela emar leh sel lvaytaj lwaz nashayk we'shtaa lhon medem davad laj marya wde'zrajam layk
20	we'sal wshari majres besraz-mdinaza medem davad leh yeshu wjulhon tamihin hwaw
21	wjad var yeshu basfita lhaw evra tuv ezkanashw lawy kenshe saguiyye' kad izawy al yad yama
22	we'za jad dashmeh yu'arash men rabay knushta wjad jsa nfal lwaz reglawy
23	wvae wa meneh sagui wa'mar leh brazy bisha'iz vida ta sim idaj leh wzezjlem wzije
24	we'sal ameh yeshu wdabik wa leh kensha saguiyya' wjavsin waw leh
25	atza den jda d'iyzeh waz bmardiza dadma shnin tartaesre
26	ayda dsagui sevlaz men asawaza saguiyye' wa'pkaz kul medem d'iyz lah wmedem la ezadraz ela af yatira'iz eza'lsaz
27	kad shemaz al yeshu ezaz bjevsa djensha men bestreh kerbaz lalvusheh
28	amra waz guer d'afen lalvusheh karba na jayya na
29	wmejda yevshaz mina dadmah wa'rgshaz bfagrah d'eza'syaz men mjuzah
30	yeshu den mejda ida bnafsheh djayla nfak meneh we'zpni lwaz kensha we'mar manu krev lma'nay
31	wa'mrin leh talmidawy jase at ljenshe djavsin laj wa'mar at manu krev li
32	wja'ar wa dnejse manu hade vad
33	ha den atza kad dajila wratiza dyedaz ma dahwa lah ezaz neflaz kdamawy we'mraz leh kuleh shrara
34	hu den emar lah brazy haymanuzejy ajyazejy sely bashlama wahwayty jlima men mjuzejy
35	wad hu mmalel ezaw men dvez rab knushta wa'mrin dvarzaj mizaz lmana mekil mamel at lmalfana
36	yeshu den shma lmelza d'emarw we'mar lhaw rab knushta la tedjal baljud haymen
37	wla shvak lnash dni'sal ameh ela lshemon ki'fa walyakuv walyujanan aju dyakuv
38	we'zaw lvayta dhaw rab knushta wajsa darhivin wvajen wmayllin
39	wal we'mar lhon mana rhivin ton wvajen tliza la mizaz ela damka y
40	wgajkin waw lawy hu den apek ljulhon wadvar la'vuh datliza wle'mah walhanon dameh wal la'yka dramya waz tliza
41	we'jad b'iydah datliza we'mar lah tliza kumy
42	wvar shazeh kamaz tliza wamhalja waz izeh waz guer baz shnin tartaesre wmetdamrin waw dumara raba
