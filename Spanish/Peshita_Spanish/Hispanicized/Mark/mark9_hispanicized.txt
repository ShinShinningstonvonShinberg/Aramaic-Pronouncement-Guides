1	we'mar wa lhon amin amar na ljon d'iyz nasha dkamin tnan dla netmon mawta dama dnejson malkuzeh d'alaha d'ezaz bjayla
2	wvazar shta yawmin dvar yeshu lji'fa walyakuv walyujanan wa'sek enon ltura rama baljudayhon we'zjalaf linayhon
3	wmashar wa lvusheh wmajwar tav ayj talga aykana davnay nasha lmajwaru b'ara la meshkjin
4	we'zjsiw lhon iliyya wmushe kad mmallin am yeshu
5	we'mar leh ki'fa rabi shapir u lan dharka nehwe wnebed tlaz matlin laj jda walmushe jda wli'liyya jda
6	la den yada wa mana amar izayhon waw guer bdejlza
7	wahwaz nana wmatla waz layhon wkala men nana d'amar hanaw bery jabiva leh shmaw
8	wmen shelya kad jarw talmide lnash la jsaw ela lyeshu baljudawy amhon
9	wjad najtin men tura mfaked wa lhon dalnash la ni'mron medem dajsaw ela en ma dkam breh dnasha men mize
10	wa'jduh lmelza bnafshhon wvaen waw dmana y hade melza dma dkam men bez mize
11	wamsha'lin waw leh wa'mrin mana hajil amrin safre d'iliyya wale dni'ze lukdam
12	amar lhon iliyya aze lukdam djulmedem nazken wa'ykana kziv al breh dnasha dsagui nejash wnestle
13	ela amar na ljon d'af iliyya eza wavadw beh kul ma dasvaw aykana dajziv lawy
14	wjad eza lwaz talmide jsa lwazhon kensha saguiyya' wsafre kad darshin amhon
15	wvar shazeh kuleh kensha jsa'u wazwahw warhetw she'lw bashlameh
16	wamsha'el wa lsafre mana darshin ton amhon
17	wana jad men kensha we'mar malfana aytiz bery lwazaj d'iyz leh ruja dla mmalla
18	wa'yka dmadrja leh javta leh wmarez wamjarek shenawy wyavesh we'mrez lzalmidayk dnapkuna wla eshkajw
19	na yeshu we'mar leh on sharbza dla mhaymna dama le'mazy ehwe lwazjon wadama le'mazy esaybarjon ayta'u lwazy
20	wa'ytyu lwazeh wjad jsazeh ruja bar shazeh jvatzeh wanfal al ara wmezbaak wa wmarez
21	wsha'el yeshu la'vu dajma leh savna ha men dhajana hu amar leh ha men talyuzeh
22	wsavnin saguiyya'n armyazeh bnura wavmayya dzawbdiwy ela medem dmeshkaj at adarayny we'zrajam lay
23	amar leh yeshu en meshkaj at dazhaymen kul medem meshkaj dnehwe lman damhaymen
24	wvar shazeh ka avu dtalya kad baje wa'mar mhaymen na mary adar ljasiruz haymanuzy
25	kad jsa den yeshu drahet ama wmezkanash lwazeh k'a bha ruja tanfza we'mar lah ruja jareshza dla mmalla ena paked na lejy puky meneh wzuv la telin leh
26	waka shi'da haw sagui wshajkeh wanfak wahwa ayj miza ayj dsaguiyye' ni'mron dmiz leh
27	hu den yeshu ajdeh b'iydeh wa'kimeh
28	kad al den lvayta yeshu sha'lu talmidawy baljudayhon lmana jnan la eshkajn lmapakuzeh
29	amar lhon hana guensa bmedem la meshkaj lmepak ela bsawma wvasluza
30	wjad nfak men taman avrin waw baglila wla save wa dnash neda beh
31	malef wa guer lzalmidawy wa'mar lhon davreh dnasha meshtlem b'iyday nasha wnektluna wma d'ezktel byawma dazlaza nkum
32	henon den la yadin waw lah lmelza wdajlin waw dansha'luna
33	we'zaw lajfarnajum wjad alw lvayta msha'el wa lhon dmana mezjashvin hwayton b'uwrja baynazjon
34	henon den shatikin waw ezjriw waw guer b'uwrja jad am jad dmanu rav bhon
35	wizev yeshu wakra lazresar we'mar lhon man dsave dnehwe kadmayya nehwe jrayya djul nash wamshamshana djul nash
36	wansav talya jad wa'kimeh bamsaza wshakleh al draawy we'mar lhon
37	kul man dankabel ayj hana talya bshemy li hu mkabel wman dli mkabel la wa li mkabel ela lman dshadrany
38	amar leh yujanan rabi jsayn nash dmapek shi'de bashmaj wajlayna al dla nkef lan
39	amar lhon yeshu la tejluna layt guer nash daved jayle bshemy wmeshkaj gal amar lay dvish
40	man dla wa hajil lukvaljon jlafaykon u
41	kul den dnashkejon kasa dmayya baljud bashma ddamshija aton amin amar na ljon dla nawbed agreh
42	wjul man dnajshel ljad men halen sure damhaymnin bi pakaj wa leh elu ramya waz rajya dajmara bsawreh washde byama
43	en den majshla laj idaj psukeh pakaj u laj pshiga dzeul ljayye aw kad iz laj tarten idin ti'sal lguihana
44	ayka dzawlahon la mata wnurhon la daka
45	we'n reglaj majshla laj psukeh pakaj u laj dzeul ljayye jguisa aw kad iz laj tarten reglin tepel bguihana
46	ayka dzawlahon la mata wnurhon la daka
47	we'n aynaj majshla laj jsih pakaj u laj dvajda aynaj teul lmalkuzeh d'alaha aw kad iz laj tarten aynin tepel bguihana dnura
48	ayka dzawlahon la mata wnurhon la daka
49	kul guer bnura nezmlaj wjul devjza bmelja tezmlaj
50	shapira y melja en den melja tefkah bmana tezmlaj tehwe bjon melja wavshayna hwaw jad am jad
