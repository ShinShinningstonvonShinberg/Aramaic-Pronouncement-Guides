1	wmejda bsafra vadw melka rabay kahne am kashishe wam safre wam kulah knushta we'sarw lyeshu wa'wblu wa'shlmu lpilataws
2	wsha'leh pilataws at u malka dihudayye hu den na we'mar leh at emart
3	wa'jlin waw karsawy rabay kahne bsaguiyya'za
4	hu den pilataws tuv sha'leh we'mar leh la mfane at pezgama jsi kma mashdin layk
5	hu den yeshu medem pezgama la yav aykana dnetdamar pilataws
6	mad wa den bjul i'da lmeshra lhon asira jad ayna dsha'lin
7	wi'yz wa jad dmezkre bar-aba d'asir wa am avday estasin hanon dketla b'estasin vadw
8	wakaw ama wshariw lmesha'l ayj damad wa aved lhon
9	hu den pilataws na we'mar saven ton eshre ljon malka dihudayye
10	yada wa guer pilataws dmen jsama ashlmu rabay kahne
11	rabay kahne den yatira'iz japetw ljenshe dalvar-ava neshre lhon
12	hu den pilataws emar lhon mana hajil saven ton ebed lhana dkaren ton malka dihudayye
13	henon den tuv kaw skufa
14	hu den pilataws emar lhon mana guer dvish vad whenon yatira'iz kaen waw skufa
15	pilataws den sva dnebed sevyana ljenshe washra lhon lvar-aba wa'shlem lhon lyeshu kad mnagad dnesdkef
16	estratiyyute den awblu lgaw darta d'iyzeh pretawriyyan wakraw ljulah espir
17	wa'lbshu argawne wagdalw samw leh klila djube
18	wshariw lmesha'l bashlameh shlam malka dihudayye
19	wmajen waw leh al risheh bkanya wrakin waw b'apawy wvarkin waw al burkayhon wsagdin leh
20	wjad basajw beh ashlju argawne wa'lbshu ma'nawy wa'pku dneskfuna
21	wshajarw jad davar wa shemon kurinayya d'aze wa men kriza avu d'aleksandraws wadrufaws dneshkul skifeh
22	wa'ytyu lgagulta dukza dmezpashka karkafza
23	wyavw leh lmeshta jamra dajlit beh mura hu den la nsav
24	wjad sakpu palegw ma'nawy wa'rmiw layhon pesa manu mana nesav
25	iz wa den shae tlaz kad sakpu
26	wajziva waz elza dmawteh bajzava hana w malka dihudayye
27	waskafw ameh tren lestayye jad men yamineh wjad men semaleh
28	washlem kzava d'emar dam awale ezjshev
29	wa'f aylen den davrin waw mgadfin waw lawy wamnidin rishayhon wa'mrin on share haykla wvane leh lazlaza yawmin
30	pasa nafshaj wjuz men skifa
31	whajana af rabay kahne gajkin waw jad am jad wsafre wa'mrin jrane aji nafsheh la meshkaj lmajayyu
32	mshija malkeh d'iysrayyel nejuz hasha men skifa dnejse wanhaymen beh wa'f hanon den daskifin waw ameh mjasdin waw leh
33	wjad hway shez shain hwa jeshuja al kulah ara dama lshae tsha
34	wvazsha shain ka yeshu bkala rama we'mar il il lmana shvaktany d'iyzeh alahy alahy lmana shvaktany
35	wnashin dashmaw men hanon dkamin amrin waw li'liyya kra
36	rhet den jad wamla esfuga jala we'sar bkanya dnashkewy we'marw shvukw nejse en aze iliyya majez leh
37	hu den yeshu ka bkala rama washlem
38	wa'pay tara dhaykla estri lazren men lel dama lzajt
39	kad jsa den kentruna haw dka'em wa lwazeh dhajana ka washlem emar sharira'iz hana gavra breh hwa d'alaha
40	iz way den af neshe men rujka djasyan way maryam magdlata wmaryam emeh dyakuv sura wadyawsi wshalum
41	hanen djad u baglila nakifan way leh wamshamshan leh wajranyaza saguiyya'za daslek way ameh lu'wrishlem
42	wjad hwa ramsha daruvta d'iyzeh kdam shabza
43	eza yawsef haw dmen ramza myakra bulewti ayna d'af hu msake wa lmalkuza d'alaha wa'mraj wal lwaz pilataws wash'el pagreh dyeshu
44	pilataws den tmah d'en men kadu miz wakra lkentruna wsha'leh d'en men kdam edana miz
45	wjad ilef yav pagreh lyawsef
46	wasvan yawsef ketana wa'jzeh wjarjeh beh wsameh bkavra dankir wa bshua waguel ki'fa al tareh dkavra
47	maryam den magdlata wmaryam ha dyusi jsay ayka d'ettsim
