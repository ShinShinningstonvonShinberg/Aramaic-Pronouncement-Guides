1	wal tuv yeshu lajfarnajum lyawmaza wjad shmaw davvayta hu
2	ezkanashw saguiyye' aykana dla eshkaj ajed enon afla kdam tara wammalel wa amhon melza
3	we'zaw lwazeh wa'ytiw leh msharya kad shkilin leh bez arba
4	wadla eshkajw lmezkaravu lwazeh metul kensha slekw lhon le'gara wa'rimw tatlila d'azar d'iyzawy wa yeshu wshabuh arsa darme wa bah msharya
5	kad jsa den yeshu haymanuzhon emar lhaw msharya bery shvikin laj jtahayk
6	iz waw den taman men safre wafrishe dyazbin wmezraen waw blebhon
7	dmana hana mmalel gudafa manu meshkaj lmeshbak jtahe ela en jad alaha
8	yeshu den ida brujeh dhalen mezraen bnafshhon we'mar lhon mana mezraen ton halen blebjon
9	ayda pshika lmi'mar lamsharya dashvikin laj jtahayk aw lmi'mar dkum shkul arsaj whalej
10	dzedon den dshalit u breh dnasha b'ara lmeshbak jtahe emar lamsharya
11	laj amar na kum shkul arsaj wsel lvaytaj
12	wkam bar shazeh washkal arseh wanfak lin kulhon aykana dnetdamron kulhon wanshabjon la'laha kad amrin dla memzum jsayn hajana
13	wanfak tuv lwaz yama wjuleh kensha azen waw lwazeh wmalef wa lhon
14	wjad avar jsa llewi bar jalfay dyazev bez majse we'mar leh ta bazary wkam esal bazreh
15	wahwa djad smij bvayteh saguiyye' majse wjatayye smijin hwaw am yeshu wam talmidawy izayhon waw guer saguiyye' we'zaw bazreh
16	wsafre wafrishe kad jsa'u dlaes am majse wam jatayye emarw lzalmidawy manaw am majse wjatayye ajel wshaze
17	kad shma den yeshu emar lhon la snikin jlime al asya ela aylen dvish bish vidin la eziz d'ekre lsadike ela ljatayye
18	talmidawy den dyujanan wafrishe samin waw we'zaw wa'mrin leh lmana talmidawy dyujanan wdafrishe samin wzalmidayk dilaj la samin
19	amar lhon yeshu lma meshkjin bnawy dagnuna kma djazna amhon u dansumon la
20	ni'zon den yawmaza dma d'eshtkel menhon jazna haden nsumon bhaw yawma
21	la nash rame urkaza jdaza wja'et al ma'na blayya dla nasba malyuzah ha jdaza men blayya whawe sedka yatira
22	wla nash rame jamra jaza bseke blayyaza dla jamra msare lseke wseke avdan wjamra meze'shed ela ramen jamra jaza bseke jataza
23	wahwa djad asel yeshu bshabza bez sare talmidawy mhaljin waw wmalguin sheble
24	wa'mrin leh prishe jsi mana avdin bshabza medem dla shalit
25	amar lhon yeshu la memzum krayton mana vad dawid kad estnek wajfen hu wadameh
26	aykana al lvayteh d'alaha kad avizar rab kahne wlajma dfazureh dmarya ejal haw dla shalit lme'jal ela en ljahne wyav af la'ylen dameh waw
27	we'mar lhon dshabza metul barnasha ezbaryaz wla wa barnasha metul shabza
28	mareh u hajil wa'f dshabza breh dnasha
