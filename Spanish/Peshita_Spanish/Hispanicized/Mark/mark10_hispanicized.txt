1	wkam men taman we'za lazjuma dihud levra dyurdnan we'salw lzaman lwazeh kenshe saguiyye' wmalef wa lhon tuv ayj damad wa
2	wakrevw prishe mnasen leh wamsha'lin d'en shalit lgavra dneshbuk atzeh
3	amar lhon mana pakedjon mushe
4	henon den amrin mushe apes lan dnejtuv kzava dshuvkana wneshre
5	na yeshu we'mar lhon lukval kashyuz lebjon kzav ljon pukdana hana
6	men breshiz den dejra wnekbza vad enon alaha
7	metul hana neshbuk gavra la'vu wle'meh wnekaf la'tzeh
8	wnehon trayhon jad bsar mekil la waw tren ela jad bsar
9	medem d'alaha hajil saweg barnasha la nfaresh
10	wsha'lu tuv talmidawy bvayta al hade
11	we'mar lhon kul man dneshre atzeh wnesav jriza ga'ar
12	we'n atza teshre balah wzehwe lajrina gara
13	wamkarvin waw leh tlayye dnekruv lhon talmidawy den ka'en waw bhalen damkarvin lhon
14	yeshu den jsa we'zbe'sh leh we'mar lhon shvukw tlayye azen lwazy wla tejlon enon dda'ylen guer d'ayj halen enon izeh malkuza d'alaha
15	amin amar na ljon djul dla nkabel malkuza d'alaha ayj talya la neul lah
16	washkal enon al draawy wsam ideh layhon wvarej enon
17	wjad rade b'uwrja rhet jad nfal al burkawy wamsha'el wa leh wa'mar malfana tava mana ebed d'izar jayye dalalam
18	amar leh yeshu mana kare at li tava layt tava ela en jad alaha
19	pukdane yada at la tgur la tegnuv la tektul wla teshad sahduza dagalza la tetlum yakar la'vuj wle'maj
20	hu den na we'mar leh malfana halen kulhen netrez enen men talyuzy
21	yeshu den jar beh wa'jveh we'mar leh jda jasira laj sel saben kul medem d'iyz laj whav lmeskine wzehwe laj simza bashmayya wsav sliva wza bazary
22	hu den ezkmar bmelza hade we'sal kad aika leh iz wa leh guer nejse saguiyye'
23	jar den yeshu bzalmidawy we'mar lhon kma atla la'ylen d'iyz lhon nejse dnelon lmalkuza d'alaha
24	talmide den metdamrin waw al melawy wana tuv yeshu we'mar lhon bnay kma atla la'ylen dazjilin al nejsayhon dnelon lmalkuzeh d'alaha
25	pshik u lgamla dneul bajrura damjata aw atira lmalkuzeh d'alaha lmeal
26	henon den yatira'iz metdamrin waw wa'mrin baynayhon manu meshkaj lmeja
27	jar den bhon yeshu we'mar lhon lwaz bnay nasha hade la meshkja ela lwaz alaha kul medem guer meshkja lwaz alaha
28	wshari ki'fa lmi'mar ha jnan shvakn kul medem wankefnaj
29	na yeshu we'mar amin amar na ljon dlayt nash dshavek bate aw aje aw ajwaza aw ava aw ema aw atza aw bnayya aw kurya metulazy wmetul svarzy
30	wla nkabel jad bama' hasha bsavna hana bate wa'je wa'jwaza we'mhaza wavnayya wkurya am rdufya wavalma d'aze jayye dalalam
31	saguiyye' den kadmayye dnehon jrayye wajrayye kadmayye
32	kad salkin waw den b'uwrja lu'wrishlem hu yeshu kdim hwa lhon wmettamhin waw wa'sin waw bazreh kad dajilin wadvar lazresarteh wshari dni'mar lhon medem dazid dnehwe leh
33	dha salkin nan lu'wrishlem wavreh dnasha meshtlem lrabay kahne walsafre wanjayvuna lmawta wnashlmuna lamme
34	wanvasjon beh wannagduna wnerkon b'apawy wnektluna walyawma dazlaza nkum
35	wakrevw lwazeh yakuv wyujanan bnay savday wa'mrin leh malfana saven nan djul dnesha'l tebed lan
36	amar lhon mana saven ton ebed ljon
37	amrin leh hav lan djad netev men yaminaj wjad men semalaj bshuvjaj
38	hu den emar lhon la yadin ton mana sha'lin ton meshkjin ton dzeshton kasa d'ena shaze na wmamudiza d'ena amed na temdon
39	amrin leh meshkjinan amar lhon yeshu kasa dshaze na teshton wmamudiza damed na temdon
40	dzetvon den men yaminy wmen semaly la hwaz dily lmetal ela la'ylen damtayva
41	wjad shmaw esra shariw ratnin al yakuv wyujanan
42	wakra enon yeshu we'mar lhon yadin ton d'aylen dmestavrin rishe damme marayhon enon wrawrvanayhon shalitin layhon
43	la den hajana nehwe baynazjon ela man dsave bjon dnehwe raba nehwe ljon mshamshana
44	wa'yna menjon dsave dnehwe kadmayya nehwe avda djulnash
45	af breh guer dnasha la eza dneshtamash ela danshamesh wadnetel nafsheh purkana jlaf saguiyye'
46	we'zaw li'yriju wjad nfak yeshu men iriju hu wzalmidawy wjensha saguiyya' timay bar-timay samya yazev wa al yad urja wjadar
47	washma dyeshu u nasrayya wshari lmeka walmi'mar breh ddawid ezrajam lay
48	wja'en waw beh saguiyye' dneshtuk hu den yatira'iz kae wa wa'mar breh ddawid ezrajam lay
49	wkam yeshu wafkad dnekruna wakra'u lsamya wa'mrin leh ezlabv kum kare laj
50	hu den samya shda lvasheh wkam eza lwaz yeshu
51	amar leh yeshu mana save at ebed laj hu den samya emar leh rabi d'ejse
52	wyeshu emar leh jsi haymanuzaj ajyazaj wmejda ezjsi leh wa'sel wa b'uwrja
