1	wjad nfak yeshu men haykla emar leh jad men talmidawy malfana ha jsi aylen ki'fe wa'ylen benyane
2	yeshu den emar leh jase at halen benyane rawrve la meshtavka harka ki'f al ki'f dla mestazra
3	wjad izev yeshu btura dsayte lukval haykla sha'lu ki'fa wyakuv wyujanan wa'ndre'was baljudayhon
4	emar lan emazy halen nehwyan wmana aza ma dkarivan halen kulhen lmeshtalamu
5	hu den yeshu shari lmi'mar lhon jsaw dalma nash natejon
6	saguiyye' guer ni'zon bshemy wni'mron d'ena na walsaguiyye' naton
7	ma den dashmaton krave wteba dki'rse la tedjlon zid u dnehwe ela la dakil jarza
8	nkum guer ama al ama wmalku al malku wnehon sawe bduka duka wnehon kafne washgushye halen risha enen djevle
9	jsaw den aton nafshjon nashlmunajon guer ldayyane wvajnushazhon teznagdon wakdam malke whigmune tkumon metulazy lsahduzhon
10	lukdam den zida dzezkres svarzy bjulhon amme
11	ma damkarvin ljon den dnashlmunajon la tkadmon ti'spon mana tmallon wla ternon ela medem dmezihev ljon bha shaza haw malelw la wa guer aton mmallin ela ruja dkudsha
12	nashlem den aja la'ju lmawta wa'va lavreh wankumon bnayya al avahayhon wanmizon enon
13	wzehon sni'in men kulnash metul shemy man den dansaybar dama ljarza hu nije
14	ma den dajsayton aza tanfza djurba ha d'amira bdani'yel nviyya dkama ayka dla wale haw dkare nestakal haden aylen dvihud enon nerkon ltura
15	wman dve'gara w la nejuz wla neul lmeshkal medem men bayteh
16	wman davjakla w la nezhfej lvestreh dneshkul lvasheh
17	wa den lvatnaza wla'ylen dmaynkan bhanon yawmaza
18	salaw den dla nehwe rukijon bsazwa
19	nehwe guer byawmaza hanon ulsana dla hwa ajwazeh men rish briza davra alaha dama lhasha wla nehwe
20	we'lu la marya djari yawmaza hanon la jayye wa kul bsar ela metul gvayya dagva kari yawmaza hanon
21	haden en nash ni'mar ljon dha harka w mshija wha hartaman la thaymnon
22	nkumon guer mshije ddagaluza wanviyye djadavuza wnetlon azwaza wzedmraza wnaton en meshkja af lagvayya
23	aton den esdarw ha kadmez emrez ljon kul medem
24	bhanon den yawmaza bazar ulsana haw shemsha nejshaj wsahra la netel nuhreh
25	wjawkve neplon men shmayya wjaylawaza dashmayya nettsion
26	whaden nejsuna lavreh dnasha kad aze banane am jayla raba wam shuvja
27	haden nshadar mala'jawy wanjanesh lagvawy men arbazayhen ruje men rishah d'ara wadama lrishah dashmayya
28	men tita den ilafw pele'za dma draj sawkeh wafraw tarfeh yadin ton damta kayta
29	hajana af aton ma dajsayton halen dhawyan daw dkariva y al tara
30	amin amar na ljon dla tebar sharbza hade dama dhalen kulhen nehwyan
31	shmayya wa'ra nebron wmelay la nebran
32	al den yawma haw wal shaza ha nash la yada afla mala'je dashmayya wla bra ela en ava
33	jsaw ettirw wsalaw la guer yadin ton emazy u savna
34	ayj gavra w guer dajsak washvak bayteh wyav shultana lavdawy walnash nash vadeh walzara paked dnehwe ir
35	ettirw hajil dla yadin ton emazy aze mareh dvayta bramsha aw bfelgueh dlilya aw bmakra tarnagla aw bsafra
36	dalma ni'ze men shelya wneshkajjon kad damkin ton
37	medem daljon den amar na ljuljon u amar na hwayton irin
