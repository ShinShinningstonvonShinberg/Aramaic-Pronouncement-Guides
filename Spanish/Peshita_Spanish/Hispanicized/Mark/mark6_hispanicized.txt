1	wanfak men taman we'za lamditeh wdabikin waw leh talmidawy
2	wjad hwaz shabza shari lmalafu bajnushta wsaguiyye' dashmaw etdamarw wa'mrin waw aymeka leh halen lhana wa'yda y jejmza d'ezyahbaz leh djayle d'ayj halen b'iydawy nehon
3	la wa hana nagara brah dmaryam wa'ju dyakuv wadyawsi wdihuda wadshemon wla ha ajwazeh tnan lwazan wmezkashlin waw beh
4	we'mar lhon yeshu layt nviyya dasir ela en bamditeh wvez jyanawy wavvayteh
5	wla meshkaj wa dnebed taman af la jad jayla ela en dal krihe kalil sam ideh wa'si
6	wmetdamar wa bjasiruz haymanuzhon wmezkrej wa bkurya kad malef
7	wakra lazresarteh wshari danshadar enon tren tren wyav lhon shultana al ruje tanfaza dnapkon
8	wfaked enon dla neshklon medem lu'wrja ela en shavta baljud la tarmala wla lajma wla njasha bjisayhon
9	ela nese'non telare wla nelbshon tarten kutinyan
10	we'mar lhon dla'yna bayta da'lin ton taman hwaw dama dnafkin ton men taman
11	wjul man dla nkablunajon wla neshmunajon ma dnafkin ton men taman pesw jela davzajtayya dreglaykon lsahduzhon wa'min amar na ljon dnehwe nij lasdum walamura byawma ddina aw lamdita ha
12	wanfakw waw wa'jresw danzuvon
13	wshi'de saguiyye' mapkin waw wmashjin waw bmeshja krihe saguiyye' wma'sen waw
14	washma herawdes malka al yeshu ezida wa leh guer shmeh we'mar wa yujanan mamdana kam men bez mize metul hana jayle mestarin beh
15	jrane amrin waw d'iliyya hu wajrane danviyya hu ayj jad men nviyye
16	kad shma den herawdes emar yujanan haw d'ena peskez risheh hu kam men bez mize
17	hu guer herawdes shadar wa ajdeh lyujanan wa'sreh bez asire metul herawdiyya ataz filipaws aju ha dansav
18	emar wa guer yujanan lherawdes dla shalit laj dzesav ataz ajuj
19	hi den herawdiyya ljima waz leh wsaven waz lmektleh wla meshkja waz
20	herawdes guer dajel wa men yujanan al dyada wa dgavra w sadika wkadisha wamnatar wa leh wsaguiyya'za shama wa leh waved wvasima'iz shama wa leh
21	wahwa yawma idia kad herawdes bvez yaldeh jshamiza vad wa lrawrvanawy walkiliyyarje walrishe daglila
22	welaz barzah dherawdiyya rakdaz wshefraz leh lherawdes wla'ylen dasmijin ameh we'mar malka latliza sha'ly meny medem dsavya aty we'tel lejy
23	wima lah dmedem dzeshe'lin etel lejy dama lfelgah dmalkuzy
24	hi den nefkaz wa'mra le'mah mana eshe'liwy amra lah risheh dyujanan mamdana
25	wmejda elaz bavtiluza lwaz malka wa'mra leh savya na bhade shaza dzetel li al pinka risheh dyujanan mamdana
26	wjeryaz leh sagui lmalka metul den mawmaza wmetul smije la sva dneglsih
27	ela mejda shadar malka espuklatra wafkad dnayte risheh dyujanan we'sal paskeh risheh dyujanan bez asire
28	wa'yti bfinka wyav latliza whi tliza yehbaz le'mah
29	washmaw talmidawy we'zaw shkalw shladeh wsamw bvez kvura
30	we'zkanashw shlije lwaz yeshu we'marw leh kul ma davadw wjul ma d'alefw
31	we'mar lhon taw ljon ni'sal ldavra baljudayn we'ttnijw kalil iz waw guer saguiyye' d'asin wa'zen wlayt wa lhon azra af la lme'jal
32	we'salw la'zra jurba basfita baljudayhon
33	wajsaw enon saguiyye' kad asin we'shtawdaw enon wavyavsha rhetw men kulhen mdinaza kdamawy lzaman
34	wanfak yeshu jsa kenshe saguiyye' we'zrajam layhon ddamen hwaw lerbe dlayt lhon raya wshari wa lmalafu enon saguiyya'za
35	wjad hwa edana saguiyya' krevw lwazeh talmidawy wa'mrin leh dhana azra jurba w wedana sagui
36	shri enon dni'sun la'gurse dajdarayn walkurya wnesbnon lhon lajma layt lhon guer medem lme'jal
37	hu den emar lhon havw lhon aton lmelas amrin leh ni'sal nesben dma'zen dinarin lajma wnetel lhon lasin
38	hu den emar lhon selw jsaw kma lajmin iz ljon harka wjad jsaw amrin leh jamsha lajmin wazren nunin
39	wafkad lhon dnasmjon ljulnash smajin smajin al esba
40	we'stmejw smajin smajin dama' ma' wadjamshin jamshin
41	wansav hanon jamsha lajmin wazren nunin wjar bashmayya wvarej waksa lajma wyav lzalmidawy dansimon lhon whanon tren nunin palegw ljulhon
42	we'jalw kulhon wasvaw
43	washkalw ksayye tresar kufinin kad mlen wmen nune
44	izayhon waw den d'ejalw lajma jamsha alfin gavrin
45	wmejda elas lzalmidawy dneskon lasfita wni'sun kdamawy levra lvez-sayyada ad share hu ljenshe
46	wjad shra enon esal ltura lamsalayyu
47	kad hwa den ramsha sfita izeh waz mesaz yama whu baljudawy al ara
48	wajsa enon dmeshtankin kad raden ruja guer lukvalhon waz wavmatarza rviata dlilya eza lwazhon yeshu kad mhalej al mayya wsave wa dnebar enon
49	hanon den jsa'u damhalej al mayya wasvarw lhon djeswa w dagala wakaw
50	kulhon guer jsa'u wadjelw wvar shazeh malel amhon we'mar lhon ezlabvw ena na la tedjlon
51	waslek lwazhon lasfita wshelyaz ruja wtav metdamrin waw wzahirin bnafshhon
52	la guer estakalw waw men lajma haw metul dlebhon mabay wa
53	wjad varw evra ezaw la'ra dguenesar
54	wjad nfakw men sfita bar shazeh estaklu nashay azra
55	warhetw bjulah ara ha wshariw lmaytayyu la'ylen dvisha'iz vidin kad shkilin lhon barsaza la'yka dshamin waw d'iyzawy
56	wa'yka da'el wa lkurya wlamdinaza bshuke samin waw krihe wvaen waw meneh d'afen ljenfa dalvusheh nekrvon wjulhon aylen dkarbin waw leh meza'sen waw
