1	wjad evraz shabza maryam magdlata wmaryam dyakuv wshalum svan herume dni'zyan nemshjana
2	bshafra den bjad bshaba ezay lvez kvura kad dnaj shemsha
3	wa'mran way bnafshhen man den aguel lan ki'fa men tara dvez kvura
4	wjar jsay damagla ha ki'fa raba waz guer tav
5	walen lvez kvura wajsay layma dyazev men yamina watif estla jewarta wazmah
6	hu den emar lhen la tedjlan lyeshu nasrayya bayan ten haw d'esdkef kam leh la wa tnan ha dukza ayka dsim wa
7	ela selen emaren lzalmidawy walji'fa dha kadem ljon laglila taman tejsuna aykana d'emar ljon
8	wjad shma rak wanfak men kavra ajid wa lhen guer tehra warziza walnash medem la emaren dajilan way guer
9	bshafra den bjad bshaba kam we'zjsi lukdam lmaryam magdlata ha dshava shi'din apek wa menah
10	whi esaz sabraz lhanon dameh waw d'avilin hwaw wvajen
11	whenon kad shmaw d'amran djay we'zjsi lhen la haymenw enen
12	bazar halen ezjsi lazren menhon badmuza jriza kad mhaljin wa'sin lakriza
13	whenon esalw emarw lsharka af la lhanon haymenw
14	jrayyaz den ezjsi lajdasar kad smijin wjased ljasiruz haymanuzhon walkashyuz lebhon dalhanon dajsa'u dkam la haymenw
15	we'mar lhon selw lalma kuleh wa'jresw svarzy bjulah briza
16	ayna damhaymen wamed jayye wa'yna dla mhaymen mezjayyav
17	azwaza den la'ylen damhaymnin halen nekfan bshemy shi'de napkon wavleshane jaze nmallon
18	wajwawaza neshklon we'n sama dmawta neshton la nahar enon wi'ydayhon nsimon al krihe wnezjalmon
19	yeshu den maran men bazar dmalel amhon lashmayya slek wizev men yamina d'alaha
20	henon den nfakw wa'jresw bjul duka wmaran madar wa lhon wmashar melayhon b'azwaza davdin waw
