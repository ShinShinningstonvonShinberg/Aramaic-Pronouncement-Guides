1	pawlaws asireh dyeshu mshija wtimaze'aws aja lfilimawn jabiva wfalja daman
2	wla'pya jabivtan wla'rjipaws palja daman walita davvaytaj
3	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
4	mawde na la'lahy bjulsvan wmetdjar na laj baslawazy
5	ha men dshemez haymanuzaj wjuba d'iyz laj lwaz maran yeshu walwaz kulhon kadishe
6	dzehwe shawtafuza dhaymanuzaj yahba pi're bavade wvidaza djul tavan d'iyz ljon byeshu mshija
7	jaduza guer sagui'za iz lan wvuyya'a davyad juvaj ettnijw rajme dkadishe
8	metul hade paresiyya sagui'za iz li bamshija d'efkud laj aylen dsadkan
9	metul juba den meva u bae na menaj ena pawlaws d'iyzay sava ayj dyada at hasha den af asira dyeshu mshija
10	wvae na menaj al bery ayna dyeldez b'asuray anisimaws
11	haw dvasvan layt wa laj beh jashju hasha den af laj af li tav jashaj
12	wshadarteh laj at den ayj dalyalda dily hajana kabela
13	save wiz guer dalwazy ejdiwy dnehwe mshamesh li jlafayk b'asure d'ewangueliyyawn
14	belad melkaj den la sviz medem lmebad dla ayj dvaktira tehwe tavzaj ela bsevyanaj
15	kvar den af metul hade shani dshaza dalalam te'jdiwy
16	la mekil ayj avda ela yatir men avda aja jabiva dily jad kma dilaj wvavsar wavmaran
17	en hajil izayk li shawtafa kabela ayj dli
18	we'n medem jasraj aw jayyav hade lay jshuv
19	ena pawlaws kezbez b'iyday ena para na dla imar laj d'af nafshaj jayyav at li
20	in ajy ena ettnij baj bmaran anij rajmay bamshija
21	metul dazjil na dshama at li kezbez laj wyada na dyatir men ma d'amar na aved at
22	bajda den af tayyev li bez mashrya msabar na guer dvaslawazjon mezihev na ljon
23	sha'el bashlamaj epafra shavya damy byeshu mshija
24	wmarkaws wa'ristarjaws wdima wluka madranay
25	taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija am rujjon amin
