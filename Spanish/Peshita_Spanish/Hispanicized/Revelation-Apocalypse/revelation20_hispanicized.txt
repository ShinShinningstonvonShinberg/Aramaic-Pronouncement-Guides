1	wajsiz jrina mala'ja danjez men shmayya d'iyz lawy klida dazhuma wshishalta rabza b'iydeh
2	wlavkeh lzanina jewya kadmayya haw d'iyzawy ajelkarsa wsatana wa'sreh alef shnin
3	wa'rmyeh bazhuma we'jad watva lel meneh dla tuv nate ljulhon amme bazar halen ihiv lmeshryeh kalil savna
4	wajsiz mawtve wizevw layhon wdina ezihev lhon wnafshaza halen d'ezpsek metul sahduza dyeshu wmetul melza d'alaha wda'ylen dla sguedw ljayyuza wla lsalmah wla nsavw rushma al bez aynayhon aw al idayhon dajyaw wa'mlejw am mshija alef shnin
5	whade y kyamta kadmata
6	tuvana w wkadisha man d'iyz leh mnaza bakyamta kadmata wal halen layt shultana lmawta tenyana ela nehon kahne d'alaha wdamshija wnamljon ameh alef shnin
7	wma d'eshtalam alef shnin neshtre satana men jvushyeh
8	wnepuk lmatayyu ljulhon amme b'arba sawyazah d'ara lgug walmagug wlamjanashu enon lakrava aylen dmenyanhon ayj jala dyama
9	waslekw al pzayyah d'ara wjadruh lamdita dmashriza dkadishe wlamdita jabivta wnejtaz nura men shmayya men alaha we'jlaz enon
10	wa'jelkarsa matyanhon ezrmi byamza dnura wjevriza ayka djayyuza wanviyya dagala wneshtankon imama wlilya lalam almin
11	wajsiz kursya raba jewara wladyazev lel meneh haw dmen kdam apawy erkaz ara washmayya wa'zar la eshtjaj lhon
12	wajsiz lmize rawrve wasure dkamw kdam kursya wsefre ezpzajw wajrina sefra ezpzaj d'iyzawy ddina we'tdinw mize men aylen dajzivan bsefra ayj vadayhon
13	wyav yama mize dveh wmawta washyul yavw mize dse'ydayhon we'tdin jad jad menhon ayj vadayhon
14	wmawta washyul ezrmiw byamza dnura hana d'iyzawy mawta tenyana
15	wa'yna dla eshtjaj darshim bajzava djayye ezrmi byamza dnura
