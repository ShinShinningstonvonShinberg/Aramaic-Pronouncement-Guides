1	we'za jad men shava mala'je d'iyz layhon shva savurin wmalel amy lmi'mar ta bazary ejawej dina dsaniza dyazba al mayya saguiyye'
2	damah saniw malkeh d'ara warwiw kulhon amureh d'ara men jamra dsanyuzah
3	wa'pkany ljurba bruj wajsiz atza dyazba al jayyuza sumakta dmalya shmahe dgudafa d'iyz lah rishe shava karnaza den sar
4	wa'tza iz wa damatfa argawne wasjuriza dmadhvan bdahva wji'fe tavaza wmarganyaza wi'yz lah kasa ddahva al idah wamle tam'uza wsuyyava dsanyuzah
5	wal bez ayneh kziv rasa bavel rabza ema dsanyaza wadsuyyaveh d'ara
6	wajsiz atza drawya men dma dkadishe wmen dma dsahdawy dyeshu we'tdamrez dumara raba kad jsizah
7	we'mar li mala'ja lmana etdamart ena amar na laj ra'sa d'atza wadjayyuza datina lah d'iyz lah shava rishin wasar karnan
8	jayyuza dajsayt izeh waz wlayteh zida dzesak men yama wla'vdana asa wnetdamron amray al ara hanon dla kzivin shmahayhon bsefra djayye men tarmyazeh dalma djasen jayyuza d'iyzeh waz wlayteh wkerbaz
9	harka hawna ldi'yz leh jejmza shava rishin shava enon turin ayka dyazba atza layhon
10	wmalke shava enon jamsha nfalw wjad izawy haw jrina la dakil eza wma d'eza kalil ihiv leh lamjataru
11	wzanina wjayyuza ha d'iyzeh wlayteh wha dazmanya wmen shava y wla'vdana asa
12	wasar karnan dajsayt esra malkin enon aylen dmalkuza la dakil nsavw ela shultana ayj malke jda shaza shaklin am jayyuza
13	halen jad sevyana iz lhon wjayla wshultana dilhon ljayyuza yahbin
14	halen am emra nakrvon we'mra neske enon metul dmare hu dmarawaza wamlej malke wadameh krayya wagvayya wamhaymne
15	we'mar li mayya dajsayt dalayhon yazba saniza amme wjenshe we'mwaza wleshane izayhon
16	wasar karnaza dajsayt ljayyuza halen nesnyan lsaniza wjaravta wartelata nebdunah wvesrah ne'jlon wnawkdunah bnura
17	alaha guer yahev blebawazhon dnebdon sevyaneh wnebdon sevyanhon jad wnetlon malkuzhon ljayyuza ha dama dneshtamlyan melawy d'alaha
18	wa'tza ayda dajsayt mdita rabza ayda d'iyz lah malkuza al malkeh d'ara
