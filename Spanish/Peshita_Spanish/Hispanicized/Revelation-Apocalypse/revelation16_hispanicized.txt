1	wshemez kala raba men haykla d'amar lshava mala'jin selw wa'shudw shva savurin djemzeh d'alaha al ara
2	we'sal kadmayya we'shad savureh al ara wahwa shujna bisha wji'vana al nasha d'iyz lhon rushma djayyuza wa'ylen dsagdin lsalmah
3	wmala'ja dazren eshad savureh byama wahwa yama ayj miza wjul nafsha jayza mizaz byama
4	wmala'ja dazlaza eshad savureh bnahrawaza wavaynaza dmayya wahwaw dma
5	wshemez lmala'ja dmayya d'amar sadik at haw d'iyzawy wi'yzawy wa wjasya dhalen dant
6	metul dadma danviyye wadkadishe eshadw wadma yavt lhon lmeshta shawen enon
7	wshemez lmadbja d'amar in marya alaha ajid kul sharirin wsadikin dinayk
8	wmala'ja d'arba eshad savureh al shemsha we'zihev leh dnajem lavnaynasha bnura
9	we'zjmemw bnaynasha bjuma raba wgadefw lashma d'alaha d'iyz leh shultana al majwaza halen wla tavw lmetal leh teshbujta
10	wmala'ja djamsha eshad savureh al kursyah djayyuza wahwaz malkuzah jeshujta wamlasin waw leshanayhon men ki'va
11	wgadefw lashma d'alaha dashmayya men ki'vayhon wmen shujnayhon wla tavw men vadayhon
12	wmala'ja deshta eshad savureh al nahra raba praz wiveshw mawy dzettayyav urja dmalke men madnjay shemsha
13	wajsiz men pumeh dzanina wmen pumah djayyuza wmen pumeh danviyya dagala ruje tlaz la dajyaza ayj urde
14	izayhen guer ruje dshi'de aylen davdan azwaza d'asan al malke dzi'vel lamjanashu enon lakrava dyawma haw raba d'alaha ajid kul
15	ha aze ayj ganava tuvawy lhaw dir wnatar ma'nawy dla artel nhalej wnejson behtzeh
16	wanjanesh enon la'zra dmezkre evra'iz magdu
17	wmala'ja dshava eshad savureh b'a'ar wanfak kala raba men haykla men kdam kursya d'amar hwa
18	wahwaw barke wrame wnawda hwa raba d'ajwazeh la hwa men dahwaw bnaynasha al ara d'ayj hana sawa hajana rav wa
19	wahwaz mdita rabza lazlaz mnawan wamdinaza damme nfaly wvavel rabza etdajraz kdam alaha lmetal lah kasa djamra djemzeh wadrugseh
20	wjul gasarta erkaz wture la eshtjajw
21	wvarda raba ayj kakra njez men shmayya al bnaynasha wgadefw bnaynasha la'laha al mjuza dvarda metul draba y mjuzah tav
