1	wajsiz jriza aza bashmayya rabza wazmihta mala'je d'iyz layhon majwaza shva jrayyaza davhen eshtamlyaz jemzeh d'alaha
2	wajsiz ayj yama dasguguiza dafzija bnura waldasjaw men jayyuza wmen salmah wmen menyana dashmah dkamin lel men yama dasguguiza wi'yz layhon kizarawy d'alaha
3	wamshabjin teshbujta dmushe avdeh d'alaha wzeshbujta d'emra wa'mrin rawrvin wzamihin vadayk marya alaha ajid kul ki'nin wsharirin vadayk malka dalme
4	man la nedjal laj marya wanshabaj lashmaj metul d'at u baljud jasya metul djulhon amme ni'zon wnesgdon kdamayk metul dazris at
5	wmen bazar halen jsiz we'zpzaj haykla dmashkna dsahduza bashmayya
6	wanfakw shava mala'jin men haykla hanon d'iyz layhon shva majwan kad lvishin ketana dajya wanhira wa'sirin al jadyayhon esara ddahva
7	wajda men arba jaywaza yehbaz lshava mala'jin shva savurin dmalyan jemzeh d'alaha d'iyzawy jayya lalam almin amin
8	we'zmli haykla men tnana dzeshbujteh d'alaha wmen jayleh wlayt damse wa lmeal lhaykla dama dneshtamlyan shva majwan dshava mala'jin
