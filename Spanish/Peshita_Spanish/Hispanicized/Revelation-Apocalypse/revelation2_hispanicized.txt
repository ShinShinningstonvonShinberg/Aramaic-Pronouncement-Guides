1	walmala'ja davita d'efesaws kzuv hajana amar haw d'ajid shava kawkvin b'iydeh haw damhalej baynaz mnaraza ddahva
2	yada na vadayk wamlaj wamsaybranuzaj wadla mset lmetan lvishe wnasiz la'ylen d'amrin nafshhon dashlije izayhon wlaytayhon we'shkajt enon dagale
3	wamsaybranuza iz laj watent metul shemy wla li'yt
4	ela iz li layk djubaj kadmayya shvakt
5	etdar men ayka nfakt waved vade kadmayye we'nden la aze na layk wamsi na mnartaj ela tzuv
6	ela hade iz laj dsanet vade dni'kulite aylen d'ena sane na
7	haw d'iyz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza wladsaje etel men kaysa djayye lme'jal haw d'iyzawy bfardaysa d'alaha
8	walmala'ja dita dasmurna kzuv hajana amar kadmayya wajrayya haw dahwa miza wajya
9	yada na ulsanaj wmeskinuzaj ela atira at walgudafa dmen aylen d'amrin nafshhon ihudayye ihudayye kad la izayhon ela knushta dsatana
10	bmedem la tedjal men aylen dazid at lmejash ha zid ajelkarsa dnarme menjon bvez jvushya dzeznason wnehwe ljon ulsana yawmin esra hwaw mhaymne dama lmawta we'tel ljon klila djayye
11	d'iyz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza d'ayna dsaje la nehar men mawta tenyana
12	walmala'ja davita dpergama kzuv hajana amar haw d'iyz leh jarba jarifta dazren pumeh
13	yada na ayka amart azar djursyeh dsatana wa'jid at bshemy wavhaymanuzy la kfart wavyawmaza ezjrit wsahda dily mhaymna metul djul sahda dily mhaymna ayna dmenjon ezktel
14	ela iz li layk suryaza d'iyz laj taman d'ajidin malpanuza dvelam haw d'alef lvalak dnarme keshla kdam bnay israyyel lme'jal devjay pzajre wlamsanayyu
15	hajana iz af laj d'ajidin yulpana dni'kulite hajwaz
16	tuv hajil we'nden la aze na layk mejda wa'krev amhon bjarba dfumy
17	wdi'yz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza dladsaje etel men manna haw dmatshay we'tel leh jushbana jewara wal jushbana shma jaza dajzava dla nash yada ela haw dansav
18	walmala'ja davita davze'watira kzuv hajana amar breh d'alaha haw d'iyz leh ayna ayj shalheviza dnura wreglawy ayj njasha levnayya
19	yada na vadayk wjubaj whaymanuzaj wzeshmeshtaj wamsaybranuzaj wavadayk jrayye saguiyye' enon men kadmayye
20	ela iz li layk sagui dashvakt la'tzaj isvel ha d'amra al nafshah danviza y wmalfa wmatya lavday lamsanayyu wme'jal devjay pzajre
21	wyehbez lah savna lazyavuza wla savya lamzav men sanyuzah
22	ha rame na lah barsa wla'ylen dgarin amah b'uwlsana raba ela netton men vadayhon
23	wlavneh ektul bmawta wyadan kulhen idaza d'ena na base kulyaza wleba we'tel ljon ljulnash ayj vadaykon
24	ljon amar na lsharka davze'watira kulhon aylen dlayt lhon yulpana hana aylen dla idaw amikazeh dsatana ayj d'amrin la arme laykon yukra jrina
25	haw hajil d'iyz ljon ajudw dama d'aze na
26	wdasja wantar vaday etel leh shultana al amme
27	lmera enon bshavta dfarsla wa'yj ma'nay pajara teshjkon hajana guer we'na nesbez men avy
28	we'tel leh ljawkav safra
29	d'iyz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza
