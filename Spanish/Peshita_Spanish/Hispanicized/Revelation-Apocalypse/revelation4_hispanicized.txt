1	men bazar halen jsiz wha tara pzija bashmayya wkala haw dshemez ayj shifura malel amy lmi'mar sak lharka we'jawej ma dihiv lmehwa bazar halen
2	wmejda hwiz bruj wha kursya sim bashmayya wal kursya yazev
3	wadyazev ayj dmuza djeswa dji'fa dyashpeh wadsardawn wkeshta danana dajdarawy djursya dmuz jeswa dasmaragde
4	wajdar kursya kursawaza esrin wa'rba walayhon den djursawaza esrin wa'rba kashishin dyazbin datifin ma'ne jeware wal karkfazhon klile ddahva
5	wmen kursawaza nafkin rame wvarke wkale wshava nahire dyakdin kdamawy djursya aylen d'iyzayhon shva rujin d'alaha
6	wakdam kursya yama dasguguiza ayj dumya daglida wavmesaz kursya wajdarawy djursya arba jaywan dmalyan ayne men kdamayhen wmen bestarhen
7	jayyuza kadmata damya la'rya wjayyuza dzarten dmuza degla wjayyuza dazlaz iz lah ape ayj dvarnasha wjayyuza d'arba dmuza dneshra dfaraj
8	arbatayhen jaywaza kuljda menhen kama wi'yz lah men tefreh walel shta guepin judrana'iz wmen lgaw malyan ayne wshelya layt lhen imama wlilya lmi'mar kadish kadish kadish marya alaha ajid kul haw d'iyzawy wa wi'yzawy wa'ze
9	wma dyav arbatayhen jaywaza teshbujta wi'ykara wkubal taybuza ladyazev al kursya wladjay lalam almin amin
10	neplon esrin wa'rba kashishin kdam man dyazev al kursya wnesgdon lalam almin amin lman djay wnarmon klilayhon kdam kursya kad amrin
11	dshawet u maran wa'lahan lmesav teshbujta wi'ykara wjayla metul d'at brayt kul wavyad sevyanaj hway we'zbri
