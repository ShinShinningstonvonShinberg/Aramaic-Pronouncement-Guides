1	wkamez al jala dyama wajsiz dsalka jayyuza men yama d'iyz lah sar karnan washva karkfan wal karnazah esra ta'guin wal karkfazah shma dgudafa
2	wjayyuza ha dajsiz dmuza waz dnemra wregleh ayj ddeba wfumah ayj d'aryawaza wyav lah tanina jayleh wjursyeh wshultana raba
3	wajda men karkfazah ayj pita lmawta wamjuza dmawtah eza'syaz we'tdamraz kulah ara bazar jayyuza
4	wasguedw lzanina dyav shultana ljayyuza wasguedw ljayyuza lmi'mar manu ddame ljayyuza hade wmanu meshkaj lmakravu amah
5	we'zihev lah puma dammalel rawrvaza wgudafa we'zihev lah shultana lmebad yarje arbin wazren
6	wfezjaz pumah lamgadafu kdam alaha dazgadefy bashma wavmashrya d'aylen dashren bashmayya
7	we'zihev lah lmebad krava am kadishe walmeska enon we'zihev lah shultana al kulhen sharbaza we'mwaza wleshane wamme
8	wnesgdon lah kulhon amureh d'ara hanon aylen dla kzivin bajzava djayye haw d'emra ktila kdam tarmyazeh dalma
9	man d'iyz leh edne neshma
10	man davshevya mawbel bshevya asel wa'yna davjarba katel bjarba nezktel harka y haymanuza wamsaybranuza dkadishe
11	wajsiz jayyuza jriza dsalka men ara wi'yz wa lah tarten karnan wdamya le'mra wammalla waz ayj tanina
12	wshultana djayyuza kadmata kuleh tebed kdamawy wzebed la'ra wladamrin bah wnesgdon ljayyuza kadmata ha d'ezjalmaz mjuza dmawtah
13	wzebed azwaza rawrvaza aykana dnura tebed lmejaz men shmayya al ara kdam bnaynasha
14	wzate ladamrin al ara byad azwaza d'ezihev lah lmebad kdam jayyuza lmi'mar ladamrin al ara lmebad salma ljayyuza ayda d'iyz lah mjuza djarba wajyaz
15	we'zihev lah dzetel ruja lsalma djayyuza wzebed djul dla nesgdon leh lsalma djayyuza nezkatlon
16	wzebed ljulhon sure wrawrve atire wmeskine marayya wavde dnezihev lhon rushma al idayhon dyamina aw al bez aynayhon
17	dla nash nesben aw nsaben tuv ela ayna d'iyz lawy rushma dashma djayyuza aw menyana dashmah
18	harka izeh jejmza wdi'yz beh hawna nejshviwy lmenyana djayyuza menyana w guer dvarnasha shezma' weshtin wshez
