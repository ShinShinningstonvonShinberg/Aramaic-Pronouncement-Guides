1	wjad pzaj tava dshava hwa shezka bashmayya ayj pelguz shaa
2	wajsiz lshava mala'jin aylen dakdam alaha kamin waw d'ezihevw lhon shava shifurin
3	wajrina mala'ja eza wkam al madbja wi'yz leh pirma ddahva we'zihev leh besme saguiyye' dnetel baslawaza djulhon kadishe al madbja dakdam kursya
4	waslek etra dvesme baslawaza dkadishe men yad mala'ja kdam alaha
5	wansav mala'ja lpirma wamla men nura dal madbja wa'rmi al ara wahwaw rame wkale wvarke wnawda
6	wshava mala'jin dalayhon shava shifurin tayyevw nafshhon lmasaku
7	whaw kadmayya asek wahwa barda wnura dafzijin bmayya we'zrmiw al ara wzulzah d'ara iked wzulza d'iylane iked wjul esba d'ara iked
8	wdazren sak wahwa ayj tura raba dyaked nfal byama wahwa tulzeh dyama dma
9	wmiz tulza djul beryaza davyama d'iyz beh nafsha wzulza d'elfa ezjabal
10	wdazlaza sak wanfal men shmayya kawkva raba dyaked ayj shalheviza wanfal al tulza dnahrawaza wal aynaza dmayya
11	washmeh djawkva meze'mar apsizna wahwa tulzhon dmayya ayj apsinzin wsuga' davnaynasha mizw metul d'ezmarmarw mayya
12	wda'rba sak wavla tulzeh dshemsha wzulzeh dsahra wzulza djawkve wajshejw tulzhon wyawma la jawi tulzeh wlilya hajwaz
13	wshemez lneshra jad dfaraj bashmayya d'amar wa wa wa lamureh d'ara men kala dshifure dazlaza mala'jin dazidin lmasaku
