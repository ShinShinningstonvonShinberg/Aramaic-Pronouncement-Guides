1	wadjamsha sak wajsiz kawkva danfal men shmayya al ara we'zihev leh klida dvi'rawy dazhuma
2	waslek tnana men b'ire ayj tnana d'atuna raba dmeshtgar wajshej shemsha wa''ar men tnana dvi're
3	wmen tnana nfakw kamse al ara we'zihev lhon shultana d'iyz lekarve d'ara
4	we'ze'mar lhon dla nahron lesbah d'ara waljul yurak afla li'ylane ela en lavnaynasha aylen dlayt lhon jazma d'alaha bez aynayhon
5	we'zihev lhon dla nektlon enon ela neshtankon yarje jamsha wzashnikhon ayj tashnika dekarva ma dnafla al nash
6	wavyawmaza hanon nevon bnaynasha lmawta wla neshkjuna wnezragrgon lammaz wneruk mawta menhon
7	wadmuza dkamse ayj dmuza drajsha damtayvin lakrava wal rishayhon ayj klila dadmuza ddahva wa'payhon ayj ape dnasha
8	wsara iz lhon ayj sara dneshe wshenayhon ayj d'aryawaza
9	wi'yz wa lhon sheryana ayj sheryana dfarsla wkala dguepayhon ayj kala dmarkvaza drajsha saguiyye' drahtin lakrava
10	wi'yz lhon dunbyaza ayj dmuza dekarva wukse den bdunbyazhon wshultanhon lmaharu lavnaynasha yarje jamsha
11	wi'yz layhon malka mala'jeh dazhuma dashmeh evra'iz avadu wa'rma'iz shma leh iz share
12	wa jad esal ha tuv azen tren wa
13	bazar halen mala'ja deshta sak wshemez kala jad men arba karnazeh dmadbja ddahva dakdam alaha
14	d'amar lmala'ja shzizayya d'iyz leh shifura shri la'rba mala'je d'asirin al nahra raba praz
15	we'shtriw arba mala'jin aylen damtayvin lshaza walyawma walyarja walshata dnektlon tulza davnaynasha
16	wmenyana djaylawaza dfarashe tarten rebu rebwan shemez menyanhon
17	whajana jsiz rajsha bjeswa wladyazbin layhon iz sheryana dnura wkarjedna djevriza wkarkfaza drajshhon ayj karkfaza d'aryawaza wmen pumhon nafka nura wjevriza waznana
18	wmen halen tlaz majwan ezkatalw tulza davnaynasha wmen nura wmen kevriza wmen tnana dnafek men pumhon
19	metul dshultana drajsha bfumhon wa'f bdunbyazhon
20	wsharka davnaynasha dla ezktalw bmajwaza halen wla tavw men vad idayhon dla nesgdon ldaywe wlafzajre ddahva wadsi'ma wdanjasha wadkaysa wadji'fa aylen dla lmejsa wla lmeshma msen aw lamhalaju
21	wla tavw men ketlayhon wmen jarshayhon wmen sanyuzhon
