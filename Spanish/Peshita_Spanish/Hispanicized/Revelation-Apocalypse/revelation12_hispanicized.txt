1	wa'za rabza ezjasyaz bashmayya atza datifa shemsha wsahra tjez regleh wajlila djawkve tresar al rishah
2	wvatna wkaya wamjablan af meshtanka dzi'lad
3	we'zjasyaz aza jriza bashmayya wha tanina raba dnura d'iyz leh shava rishin wasar karnaza wal rishawy shava ta'guin
4	wdunbeh garsha lzulza djawkve dvashmayya wa'rmi enon al ara wzanina ka'em wa kdam atza dazida dzi'lad dma dyeldaz ne'jliwy lavrah
5	wyeldaz bra dejra haw dazid lmera ljulhon amme bshavta dfarsla we'zjtef brah lwaz alaha walwaz kursyeh
6	wa'tza erkaz ljurba azar d'iyz wa lah taman dukza damtayva men alaha danzarsunah yawmin alef wma'zen weshtin
7	wahwa krava bashmayya mija'yel wmala'jawy makrvin am tanina wzanina wmala'jawy akrevw
8	wla ezmsiw wla azra eshtjaj lhon bashmayya
9	we'zrmi tanina raba haw jewya risha haw dmezkre ajelkarsa wsatana haw d'ati ljulah ara we'zrmi al ara wmala'jawy ameh ezrmiw
10	wshemez kala raba men shmayya d'emar ha hwa shusava wjayla wmalkuza d'alahan d'ezrmi masura d'ajayn haw dmasar wa lhon lilya wi'ymama kdam alahan
11	whanon sjaw badma d'emra wavyad melza dsahduzeh wla ajevw nafshhon dama lmawta
12	metulhana shmayya ezpasjw wa'ylen davhon shren wa la'ra walyama al dnajez ajelkarsa lwazhon d'iyz leh jemta rabza kad yada dkalil savna iz leh
13	wjad jsa tanina d'ezrmi al ara rdaf la'tza ayda dyeldaz dejra
14	we'zihev la'tza tren guepin dneshra raba dzefrajy ljurba ldukzah lmettarsayyu taman edan edanin wfelguz edan men kdam apawy djewya
15	wa'rmi jewya men pumeh bazar atza mayya ayj nahra dashkilaz mayya nebdih
16	wadraz ara la'tza wfezjaz ara pumah wavlazeh lnahra haw d'armi tanina men pumeh
17	wargues tanina al atza we'sal lmebad krava am sharka dsarah halen dnatrin pukdanawy d'alaha wi'yz lhon sahduzeh dyeshu
