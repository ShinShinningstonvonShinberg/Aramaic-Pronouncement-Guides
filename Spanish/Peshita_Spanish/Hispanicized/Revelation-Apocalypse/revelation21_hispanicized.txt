1	wajsiz shmayya jataza wa'ra jdaza shmayya guer kadmata wa'ra kadmata esalw wyama laytawy tuv
2	wlamdita kadishta urishlem jdaza jsizah dnajta men shmayya men sed alaha damtayva ayj kalza msabatta lvalah
3	wshemez kala raba men shmayya d'amar ha mashrya d'alaha am bnaynasha wshare amhon whenon ama dileh nehon whu alaha amhon wnehwe lhon alaha
4	whu nelje kul deme men aynayhon wmawta la nehwe mekil wla evla wla rawba wla ki'va tuv nehwe al apeh
5	we'sez we'mar li dyazev al kursya ha jaza aved na kul we'mar li kzuv halen mele mhaymnaza wshariraza izayhen
6	we'mar li hway ena alaf we'na taw rishiza wshulama ldashe na etel men ayna dmayya jayye magan
7	wdasja hu ni'raz halen we'hwe leh alaha wnehwe li bra
8	lkanutzane den wla mhaymne wawale wamsayve wkatule wjarashe wsanayye wfaljay pzajre wjulhon dagale mnazhon byamza yakedta dnura wjevriza ayda d'iyzeh mawta tenyana
9	we'za jad men shava mala'jin aylen d'iyz layhon shva savurin dmalyan shva majwaza jrayyaza wmalel amy lmi'mar ta ejawej ljalza atzeh d'emra
10	wa'wblany bruj ltura raba wrama wjawyany lamdita kadishta urishlem dnajta men shmayya men sed alaha
11	wi'yz lah teshbujta d'alaha wnuhrah ayj dmuza dji'fa yakirta ayj yashpeh ayj dumya dakrustalaws
12	wi'yz lah shura raba wrama wi'yz lah tare tresar wal tare mala'je tresar washmahayhon kzive aylen d'iyzayhon shmahe dazresar shavte d'iysrayyel
13	men madnja tare tlaza wmen garvya tare tlaza wmen taymna tare tlaza wmen marva tare tlaza
14	wshura damdita iz leh sheze'se tartaesre walayhen tresar shmahe dashlijawy davra
15	whaw dammalel wa amy iz wa lawy kanya damshujza ddahva lmemshjah lamdita walshurah
16	wamdita mraba'iz sima u'wrkah ayj pzayyah wmashjah lamdita bkanya al tresar alfin estdawaza urkah wafzayyah wrumah shwen enon
17	wmashjeh lshurah ma' wa'rbin wa'rba amin bamshujza dnasha ayda d'iyzeh dmala'ja
18	wdumsa dshurah yashpeh wamdita ddahva dajya badmuza dasguguiza djiza
19	wsheze'se dshura damdita bji'fe yakiraza msabzan wsheze'sza kadmata yashpeh wadzarten sapila wdazlaz karjedna wda'rba smaragda
20	wadjamesh sardawn wtefra wadshez sardawn wdashva ki'f dahva wdazmane berula wdazsha tupandiyyawn wdasar krusprasa dajdaesre yukinzaws dzartasre amuzisas
21	wazresar tare wzartasre marganyaza jda lajda wjuljad men tare iz wa men jda marganiza wshuka den damdita ddahva dajya ayj sguguiza iz bah
22	whaykla la jsiz bah marya guer alaha ajid kul hu izawy hayklah
23	wle'mra wlamdita la mezbe shemsha wla sahra dnanhron lah teshbujteh guer d'alaha anharzah washragah izawy emra
24	wamhaljin amme bnuhrah wmalke d'ara mayten lah teshbujta
25	wzareh la nettajdon b'iymama lilya guer la nehwe taman
26	wnayton lah teshbujta wi'ykara damme
27	wla nehwe taman kul tma wadaved msayvuza wdagaluza ela en aylen dajzivan bajzaveh d'emra
