1	wajsiz jrina mala'ja danjez men shmayya wamataf nana wkeshta dashmayya al risheh wjesweh ayj shemsha wreglawy ayj amude dnura
2	wi'yz leh b'iydeh kzavuna pzija wsam regleh dyamina al yama dsemala den al ara
3	waka bkala rama ayj arya dgasar wjad ka malelw shava ramin bkalayhon
4	wjad malelw shava ramin mtayyev wiz lmejtav wshemez kala men shmayya dshava d'amar jzum haw ma dmalelw shava ramin wla tejtviwy
5	wmala'ja haw dajsiz dka'em al yama wal yavsha d'arim ideh lashmayya
6	wima bhaw djay lalam almin haw davrah lashmayya wadvah wla'ra wadvah dzuv savna la nehwe
7	ela byawmaza dmala'ja dshava ma dazid lmesak we'shtalam raseh d'alaha haw dsabar lavdawy nviyye
8	wkala shemez men shmayya tuv dammalel amy wa'mar sel sav lajzavuna dvi'ydeh dmala'ja dka'em al ara wal yama
9	we'sez lwaz mala'ja kad amar na leh lmetal li lajzavuna we'mar li sav wa'jula wnamar laj karsaj ela bfumaj nehwe ayj devsha
10	wnesbez lajzavuna men ideh dmala'ja we'jalteh wi'yz wa bfumy ayj devsha jalya wjad ejalteh meraz karsy
11	we'mar li ihiv laj tuv savna lmeznabayyu al amme we'mwaza wleshane wmalke saguiyye'
