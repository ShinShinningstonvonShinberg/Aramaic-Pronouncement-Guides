1	wmen bazar halen shemez kala raba djenshe saguiyye' bashmayya d'amrin haleluyya purkana wzeshbujta wjayla la'lahan
2	metul dsharirin wji'nin dinawy metul ddan lsaniza rabza ayda djablaz la'ra bsanyuzah wazva dma davdawy men ideh
3	dzarten emarw haleluyya waznanah slek lalam almin
4	wanfalw esrin wa'rba kashishin wa'rba jaywan wasguedw la'lahan dyazev al kursya wa'mrin amin haleluyya
5	wkala men kursya d'amar shabajw la'lahan kulhon avdawy wdajlay shmeh kulhon sure am rawrve
6	wshemez kala ayj djenshe saguiyye' wa'yj kala dmayya saguiyye' wa'yj kala drame jaylzane d'amrin haleluyya metul d'amlej marya alaha ajid kul
7	jadenan wmezpasjinan netel leh teshbujta metul d'ezaz meshtuzeh d'emra wa'tzeh tayvaz nafshah
8	we'zihev lah dzezataf busa dajya wanhira busa guer trisaza enen dkadishe
9	we'marw li kzuv tuvayhon la'ylen dlajshamiza dmeshtuzeh d'emra izayhon krayya we'mar li halen mele dshariran d'alaha izayhen
10	wneflez kdam reglawy wsegdez leh we'mar li la knazaj izay wda'jayk halen d'iyz lhon sahduza dyeshu la'laha sgud yatira'iz sahduza guer dyeshu izeh ruja danviyyuza
11	wajsiz shmayya dafzij wha susya jewara wadyazev lawy mezkre mhaymna wsharira wavji'nuza d'aen wmakrev
12	aynawy den ayj shalheviza dnura wal risheh ta'gue saguiyye' wi'yz leh shma kziva ayna dla yada ela en hu
13	wamataf ma'na dasli badma wmezkre shmeh melza d'alaha
14	wjaylawaza dashmayya nakifin waw leh al rajsha jeware walvishin busa jewara wdajya
15	wmen pumhon nafka jarba jarifta dvah nektlon lamme whu nere enon bshavta dfarsla whu d'aesh masarta drugseh d'alaha ajid kul
16	wi'yz leh al ma'nawy al atmazeh shma kziva malka dmalke wmara dmarawaza
17	wajsiz jrina mala'ja dka'em bshemsha waka bkala rama we'mar lfarajza dfarja mesaz shmayya taw ezkashw lajshamiza rabza d'alaha
18	dze'jlon besra dmalke wvesra drishay alfe wvesra dashine wvesra drajsha wda'ylen dyazbin layhon wvesra dji're wadavde wdasure wadrawrve
19	wajsiz ljayyuza waljaylawazah wmalke d'ara walfaljayhon damjanshin lmebad krava am haw dyazev al susya wam paljawy
20	we'ttsidaz jayyuza wanviyya dagala ameh haw davad azwaza kdameh davhen ati la'ylen dansavw rushma djayyuza wla'ylen dasguedw lsalmah wanjezw trayhon we'zrmiw byamza dnura dyakda wadjevriza
21	wadsharka den ezkatalw bjarbeh dhaw dyazev al susya b'ayda dnafka men pumeh wjulah tayra sevaz men besrhon
