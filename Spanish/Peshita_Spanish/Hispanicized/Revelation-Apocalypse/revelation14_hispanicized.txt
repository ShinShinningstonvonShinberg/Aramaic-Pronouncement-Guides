1	wajsiz wha emra ka'em al tura dsehyon wameh ma' wa'rbin wa'rba alfin d'iyz layhon shmeh washma d'avu kziv al bez aynayhon
2	wshemez kala men shmayya ayj kala dmayya saguiyye' wa'yj kala drama raba kala ayna dshemez ayj kizaruda dnakesh bkizarawy
3	wamshabjin ayj teshbujta jdaza kdam kursya wakdam arba jaywan wakdam kashishe wla nash ezmsi lmi'lfah lzeshbujta ela en ma' wa'rbin wa'rba alfin svinay men ara
4	halen enon aylen dam neshe la ettawashw bzule guer izayhon halen dnakpu le'mra kul kar dni'sal halen esdvenw men nasha rishiza la'laha wle'mra
5	davfumhon la eshtajjaz dagaluza dla mum guer enon
6	wajsiz jrina mala'ja dfaraj mesaz shmayya wi'yz leh lawy svarza dalalam lamsabaru al yazbay ara wal kul am we'mwan wsharvan wleshan
7	lmi'mar bkala raba djalw men alaha whavw leh teshbujta metul d'ezaz shaza ddineh wasgudw ldavad shmayya wa'ra wyama waynaza dmayya
8	wajrina dazren nkif wa leh wa'mar neflaz neflaz bavel rabza ayda dmen jemta dsanyuzah ashkyaz ljulhon amme
9	wajrina mala'ja dazlaza nkef lhon lmi'mar bkala raba ayna dasgued ljayyuza walsalmah washkal rushmah bez aynawy
10	af hu neshte men jamra djemzeh dmarya damsig dla jelta bjasa drugseh wneshtanak bnura wjevriza kdam mala'je kadishe wakdam emra
11	waznana dzashnikhon lalam almin nesak wlayt lhon nfi'sha imama wlilya aylen dsagdin ljayyuza walsalmah walman dashkal rushma dashmah
12	harka izeh msaybranuza dkadishe aylen dantarw pukdanawy d'alaha whaymanuzeh dyeshu
13	wshemez kala men shmayya d'amar kzuv tuvayhon lmize aylen danadw bmaran men hasha in amar ruja metul dnettnijon men amlayhon
14	wha nana jewarta wal nana yazev dmuza dvarnasha wi'yz leh al risheh klila ddahva wal ideh magalza jarifta
15	wajrina mala'ja nfak men haykla waka bkala raba ladyazev al nana shadar magalzaj wajsud metul d'ezaz shaza lmejsad
16	wa'rmi haw dyazev al nana magalzeh al ara we'zjasdaz ara
17	wajrina mala'ja nfak men haykla dvashmayya walawy iz magalza jarifta
18	wajrina mala'ja nfak men madbja d'iyz leh shultana al nura waka bkala raba ldi'yz leh magalza jarifta shadar at magalzaj jarifta waktuf lasgule djarmah d'ara metul darvay envawy
19	wa'rmi mala'ja magalzeh al ara waktaf ljarmah d'ara wa'rmi bmasarta rabza djemzeh d'alaha
20	we'ttdishaz masarta lvar men mdita wanfak dma men masarta dama lafgude drajsha al alef wma'zen estdawan
