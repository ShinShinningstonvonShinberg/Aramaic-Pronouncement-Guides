1	wmen bazar hade jsiz arba mala'jin kamin al arba sawyazah d'ara wa'jidin la'rbaz ruje dla neshav ruja al ara wla al yama wla al kul ilan
2	wajsiz jrina mala'ja daslek men madnjay shemsha wi'yz leh jazma d'alaha jayya waka bkala rama la'rba mala'je hanon d'ezihev lhon dnahron la'ra walyama
3	we'mar la tahron la'ra wla lyama wa'fla li'ylane dama dnejtum lavdawy d'alaha bez aynayhon
4	wshemez menyana dajzime ma' wa'rbin wa'rba alfin men kul sharvan d'iysra'yel
5	men sharbzeh dihuda tresar alfin men sharbzeh druvel tresar alfin men sharbzeh dgad tresar alfin
6	men sharbzeh d'ashir tresar alfin men sharbzeh dnaftali tresar alfin men sharbzeh damnashe tresar alfin
7	men sharbzeh dshemon tresar alfin men sharbzeh d'iysajar tresar alfin men sharbzeh dlewi tresar alfin
8	men sharbzeh dasvawlon tresar alfin men sharbzeh dyawsef tresar alfin men sharbzeh dvenyamin tresar alfin jzime
9	bazarken jsiz kensha saguiyya' ayna dalmenyaneh layt damse wa men kul am wsharba we'mwan wleshanin dkamin kdam kursya wakdamawy d'emra wamatfin estle jewaraza wvi'ydayhon dekle
10	wkaen bkala raba wa'mrin purkana la'lahan wladyazev al kursya wle'mra
11	wjulhon mala'je kamin waw jdarawy djursya wadkashishe wda'rba jaywan wanfalw kdam kursya al apayhon
12	kad amrin amin teshbujta wvurkza wjejmza wkubal taybuza wi'ykara wjayla wushna la'lahan lalam almin amin
13	wana jad men kashishe we'mar li halen datifin estle jewaraza man enon wmen ayka ezaw
14	we'mrez leh mary at yada at we'mar li halen enon aylen d'ezaw men ulsana raba wjalelw estlayhon wjawarw enen badma d'emra
15	metul hana izayhon kdam kursya d'alaha wamshamshin leh imama wlilya bhaykleh wadyazev al kursya naguen layhon
16	la nejpnon wla neshon wshemsha layhon la nepel wla kul shawba
17	metul d'emra davmesaz kursya nere enon wanshabel enon sed jayye wsed aynaza dmayya wnelje kul deme men aynayhon
