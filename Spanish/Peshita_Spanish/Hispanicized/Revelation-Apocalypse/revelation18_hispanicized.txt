1	wmen bazar halen jsiz jrina mala'ja danjez men shmayya d'iyz leh shultana raba wa'ra nehraz men teshbujteh
2	waka bkala raba neflaz neflaz bavel rabza wahwaz mamra lshi'de wanturza ljul ruje la dajyaza wsanyaza
3	metul dmen jamra dsanyuzah mesgaz ljulhon amme wmalkeh d'ara amah saniw wzagare d'ara men jayla dashnayyah zarw
4	wshemez jrina kala men shmayya d'amar pukw men gawah amy dla teshtawtfon bajtaheh dalma tesvon men majwazah
5	metul dadvekw bah jtahe dama lashmayya we'tdjar alaha awleh
6	puruh aykana d'af hi peraz wufw lah afa al vadeh bjasa haw dmesgaz msugw lah afa
7	al medem dshabjaz nafshah we'shtalyaz d'ayj hajan shunaka we'vla metul davlebah amra dyazba na malkza wa'rmalta laytay we'vla la ejse
8	metulhana bjad yawma ni'zyan leh majwaza mawta we'vla wjafna wavnura ti'kad metul djaylzan marya ddanah
9	wnevkunah wnarkdon leh malkeh d'ara hanon dsaniw amah we'shtaliw ma djasen tnana dyakdanah
10	kad kamin men kvul men dejlza dzashnikah wni'mron wa wa wa mdita rabza bavel mdita ashinta metul dvajda shaa eza dinejy
11	wzagare d'ara nevkon wneza'vlon leh wmawbalhon layt dsaven tuv
12	mawbla ddahva wadsi'ma wadji'fe yakiraza wadmarganyaza wadvusa wda'rgwana wshi'rayya dasjuriza wjul kis dvesma wjul ma'n dshena wjul ma'n dkaysa yakira wanjasha wfarsla wshisha
13	wkunimawn wvesme wmurawn walvunta wjamra wmeshja wasmida werbe wrajsha wmarkvaza wfagre wnafshaza davnaynasha
14	we'bejy regza dnafshejy esal menejy wjul dshamin washvij esal menejy wla tuv tejsen enon
15	wla neshkjon enon tagare dhalen dazarw menah men kvul nkumon men dejlza dshunakah kad bajen wa'vilin
16	wa'mrin wa wa mdita rabza damatfa busa wa'rgwana wasjuryaza dmadhvan bdahva wji'fe yakiraza wmarganyaza
17	metul dvajda shaa estarak uzra d'ayj hana wjul mdabray elfe wjul asay b'elfe ldukyaza we'lfare wjul davyama paljin men rujka kamw
18	wavja'uh kad jasen tnana dyakdanah wa'mrin man i ddamya lamdita rabza
19	wa'rmiw afra al rishayhon wakaw kad bajen wa'vilin wa'mrin wa wa mdita rabza ayda dvah zarw aylen d'iyz lhon elfa byama men ikarah dvajda shaa jerbaz
20	ezpasjw leh shmayya wkadishe washlije wanviyye metul ddan alaha dinjon menah
21	washkal jad men mala'je jaylzane ki'fa rabza ayj rajya wa'rmi byama we'mar hajana bji'fa teshtde bavel mdita rabza wla teshkaj tuv
22	wkala dkizara wadshifura wdasnay smara wadmasuke la neshtma bejy tuv
23	wnuhra dashraga la nezjse lejy tuv wkala djazna wkala djalza la neshtma bejy tuv metul dzagarayky iz waw rawrvaneh d'ara metul davjarshayky atity ljulhon amme
24	wvah eshtjaj dma danviyye wkadishe daktilin al ara
