1	wjawyany nahra dmayya jayye dajya af nahira ayj glida wnafek men kursyeh d'alaha wde'mra
2	wmesaz shukeh meka wmeka al nahra kaysa djayye daved pi're tresar wavjul iraj yahev pi'rawy wtarfawy la'syuza damme
3	wjul jerma la nehwe taman wjursyeh d'alaha wde'mra bah nehwe wavdawy nshamshuna
4	wnejson apawy washmeh al bez aynayhon
5	wlilya la nehwe taman wla nezbe lhon nuhra washraga wnuhreh dshemsha metul dmarya alaha manhar lhon wmalkhon lalam almin
6	we'mar li halen mele mhaymnan wshariran wmarya alaha drujaza danviyye kadishe shadar lmala'jeh lamjawayyu lavdawy ma dihiv lmehwa bagal
7	wha aze na bagal tuvawy lman dnatar mele danviyyuza dajzava hana
8	ena yujanan dajsa washma halen wjad jsiz wshemez neflez lmesgad kdam reglawy dmala'ja damjawe li halen
9	we'mar li jsi la knazaj izay wda'jayk nviyye wda'ylen dnatrin halen mele dajzava hana la'laha sgud
10	we'mar li la tejtum mele danviyyuza dajzava hana savna guer krev
11	wadmawel tuv nawel wadsa tuv nestasa wsadika tuv nebed sadikuza wkadisha tuv nezkadash
12	ha aze na mejda wa'gry amy we'tel ljulnash ayj vadeh
13	ena alaf we'na taw kadmayya wajrayya wshurayya wshulama
14	tuvayhon ladavdin pukdanawy nehwe shultanhon al kaysa djayye wavzara nelon lamdita
15	wsanayye wkatule wfaljay pzajre lvar wtame' wjarashe wjul jasayyay wavday dagaluza
16	ena yeshu shadrez lmala'jy dansahed bjon halen kdam idaza ena na ekara wsharbzeh ddawid wameh wjawkav safra nahira
17	wruja wjalza amrin ta wadshama ni'mar ta wdashe ni'ze wnesav mayya jayye magan
18	msahed na ljul dshama melza danviyyuza dajzava hana d'ayna dansim layhen nsim lawy alaha majwaza dajzivan bajzava hana
19	wa'yna damvasar men mele dajzava danviyyuza hade nvasar alaha mnazeh men kaysa djayye wmen mdita kadishta aylen dajzivan bajzava hana
20	amar kad msahed halen in aze na bagal ta marya yeshu
21	taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija am kulhon kadishawy amin
