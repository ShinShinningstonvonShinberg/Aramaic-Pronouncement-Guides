1	wajsiz kad pzaj emra jad men shava tavin wshemez lajda men arba jaywan d'amra ayj kala drame ta wajsi
2	wshemez wajsiz wha susya jewara wadyazev lawy iz leh keshta we'zihev leh klila wanfak saka wsaje wadneske
3	wjad pzaj tava dazren shemez ljayyuza dzarten d'amra ta
4	wanfak susya sumaka wladyazev lawy ezihev leh lmesav shlama men ara dlajdade nnakson we'zyahbaz leh jarba rabza
5	wjad ezpzaj tava dazlaza shemez ljayyuza dazlaz d'amra ta wha susya ukama wadyazev lawy iz ma'saza b'iydeh
6	wshemez kala men bez jaywaza d'amar kaba djete bdinara wazlaza kabin dasara bdinara waljamra walmeshja la tahar
7	wjad pzaj tava d'arba shemez kala djayyuza d'amra ta
8	wajsiz susya yuraka washmeh dhaw dyazev lawy mawta washyul nkifa leh we'zihev leh shultana al ruvah d'ara dankatel bjarba wavjafna wavmawta wavjayyuza d'ara
9	wjad pzaj ltava djamsha jsiz lzajt men madbja lnafshaza d'ezkatal metul melza d'alaha wmetul sahduza dyeshu ha d'iyz wa lhon
10	wakaw bkala raba wa'mrin dama le'mazy marya kadisha wsharira la danaz wzavat dman men amureh d'ara
11	we'zyahbaz ljuljad jad menhon estla jewarta we'ze'mar dnettnijon ad edan svan sur dama dmeshtamlen af knawazhon wa'jayhon aylen dazidin lmezkatalu ayj d'af hanon
12	wajsiz kad pzaj tava deshta wnawda raba wa wshemsha ayj saka dsara ukam hwa wsahra kuleh hwa leh ayj dma
13	wjawkve dashmayya nfalw al ara ayj tita dshadya pakueh men ruja ashinta ma dmettsia
14	washmayya ezpresh wa'yj kzave ezkrejw wjul tur wjul gasarta men dukazhon ettsiw
15	wmalke d'ara wrawrvane wrishay alfe watire wjaylawaza wjul avde wavnay ji're tashiw nafshhon bamare wavshue dture
16	wa'mrin lture wshue dfelw layn wtashaw lan men kdam apawy d'emra
17	metul d'eza yawma raba drugshon wmanu meshkaj lamkam
