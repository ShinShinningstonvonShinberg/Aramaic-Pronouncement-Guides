1	wajsiz al yamineh dhaw dyazev al kursya kzava darshim men lgaw wmen lvar watvi tave shava
2	wajsiz jrina mala'ja jaylzana dmajres bkala rama man shawe lmeftaj kzava walmeshra tavawy
3	wlayt d'ezmsi bashmayya wla b'ara wla dalzajt men ara lmeftaj lajzava walmeshra tavawy walmejsyeh
4	wvaje wiz sagui metul dlayt d'eshtjaj dshawe lmeftaj lajzava walmeshra tavawy
5	wjad men kashishe emar li la tevke ha sja arya men shavta dihuda ekara ddawid neftaj kzava wtavawy
6	wajsiz bmesaz kursya wda'rba jaywan wadkashishe emra dka'em ayj njisa wi'yz leh karnaza shva wayne shva aylen d'iyzayhen shva ruje d'alaha dmeshtadran ljulah ara
7	we'za wansav kzava men ideh dhaw dyazev al kursya
8	wjad shakleh lajzava arba jaywan wesrin wa'rba kashishin nfalw kdamawy d'emra kad iz ljuljad jad menhon kizara wsavura ddahva dmalya besme aylen d'iyzayhen slawaza dkadishe
9	damshabjin teshbujta jdaza wa'mrin shawet u lmesviwy lajzava walmeshra tavawy al d'eznjest wasvantan badmaj la'laha men kul sharbaza wamme we'mwaza
10	wavadt enon la'lahan malkuza wjahne wmalke wnamljon al ara
11	wajsiz wshemez ayj kala dmala'je saguiyye' jdaray kursya wadjaywaza wadkashishe wi'yzawy wa menyanhon rebu rebwan wa'lef alfin
12	wa'mrin bkala rama shawet u emra njisa lmesav jayla wuzra wjejmza wushna wi'ykara wzeshbujta wvurkza
13	wjul briza dvashmayya wva'ra wdalzajt men ara wdavyama izeh wjul davhon wshemez d'amrin ladyazev al kursya wle'mra dvurkza wi'ykara wzeshbujta u'wjdana lalam almin
14	wa'rba jaywan d'amran amin wkashishe nfalw wasguedw
