1	we'zihev li kanya dmuza dshavta wka'em wa mala'ja wa'mar kum wamshuj lhaykla d'alaha walmadbja wla'ylen dsagdin beh
2	waldarta dalgaw men haykla apek men lvar wla temshjih metul d'ezyahbaz lamme wlamdita kadishta ndushon yarje arbin wazren
3	we'tel lazren sahday lmeznabayyu yawmin alef wma'zen weshtin kad tifin sake
4	halen enon tren saytin wzarten mnaran dakdam mara djulah ara kamin
5	wman dvae dnahar enon nafka nura men pumhon wa'jla laveldvavayhon wla'yna dsave dnahar enon hajan ihiv lhon lmezktalu
6	whalen iz lhon shultana dne'jdon lashmayya dla nejuz metra byawmaza danviyyuzhon wi'yz lhon shultana dnahpjon mayya ladma wadnemjon la'ra bjul majwan kma dnesbon
7	wma dshamliw sahduzhon jayyuza dsalka men yama tebed amhon krava wzeske enon wzektul enon
8	washladayhon al shuke damdita rabza ayda dmezkarya rujana'iz sdum wmesren ayka dmarhon estlev
9	wjasen men emwaza wsharbaza wleshane wamme lashladayhon tlaza yawmin wfelgueh wlashladayhon la neshbkon lmettsamu bkavre
10	wamureh d'ara nejdon layhon wnezpasjon wmawhvaza nshadron lajdade metul tren nviin dshanekw lamureh d'ara
11	wmen bazar tlaza yawmin wfelgueh ruja jayza men alaha elaz bhon wkamw al reglayhon wruja djayye neflaz layhon wdejlza rabza hwaz al aylen djasen lhon
12	washmaw kala raba men shmayya d'amar lhon sakw lja waslekw lashmayya banana wmasdin bhon beldvavayhon
13	wavshaza ha hwa sawa raba wjad men esra damdita nfalw we'zkatalw bsawa shmahe dgavre alfe shava wadsharka hwaw bdejlza wyavon teshbujta la'laha dvashmayya
14	ha tren wa esalw wha wa dazlaza aze mejda
15	wmala'ja dshava sak wahwaw kale rawrve bashmayya d'amrin hwaz malkuzeh dalma d'alahan wdamshijeh wa'mlej lalam almin
16	wesrin wa'rba kashishe aylen dakdam alaha yazbin al kursawazhon nfalw al apayhon wasguedw la'laha
17	lmi'mar mawdenan laj marya alaha ajid kul d'iyzawy wi'yzawy wa dansavt bjaylaj raba wa'mlejt
18	wamme rguesw we'za rugsaj wsavna dmize dnetdinon wzetel agra lavdayk nviyye walkadishe waldajlay shmaj lasure am rawrve wazjabel la'ylen djabelw la'ra
19	we'zpzaj haykla bashmayya we'zjasyaz kivuza ddiyyaziki dileh bhaykla wahwaw barke wrame wkale wnawda wvarda raba
