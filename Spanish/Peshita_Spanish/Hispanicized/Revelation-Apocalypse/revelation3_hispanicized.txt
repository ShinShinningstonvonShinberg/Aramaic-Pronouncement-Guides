1	walmala'ja davita dsardis kzuv hajana amar haw d'iyz leh shva rujin d'alaha wshava kawkve yada na vadayk washma d'iyz laj wadjayya at wadmiza at
2	wahwi ira wkayyem dsharka d'aylen dazid wayt lammaz la guer eshkajtaj damshamlen vadayk kdam alaha
3	etdar aykan shmat wansavt esdar wzuv we'nden la tetir aze na layk ayj ganava wla teda ayda shaza aze layk
4	ela iz li kalil shmahe bsardis aylen dla taweshw ma'nayhon wamhaljin kdamay bjeware wshawen enon
5	dsaje hajana mezataf ma'ne jeware wla elje shmeh men sefra djayye wa'wde bashmeh kdam avy wakdam mala'jawy
6	ayna d'iyz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza
7	walmala'ja dita dfiladelfiyya kzuv hajana amar kadisha sharira haw d'iyz leh klide ddawid ayna dfazaj wlayt d'ajed wa'jed wlayt dfazaj
8	yada na vadayk wha yehbez kdamayk tara pzija ayna dla nash mse lme'jdeh metul dkalil jayla iz laj wmelazy ntart wavshemy la kfart
9	wha yahev na men knushta dsatana men aylen d'amrin al nafshhon dihudayye enon wla izayhon ela mdaglin ha ebed lhon dni'zon wnesgdon kdam reglayk wnedon d'ena ajevtaj
10	al dantart melza damsaybranuzy we'na etraj men nesyuna dazid dni'ze al kulah ti'vel dannase lamureh d'ara
11	aze na mejda ajud haw ma d'iyz laj dla nash nesav klilaj
12	wadsaje ebdeh amuda bhaykla d'alaha walvar la nepuk tuv we'jtuv lawy shma d'alahy washma damdita jaza urishlem ayda dnajta men alahy washma dily jaza
13	wdi'yz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza
14	walmala'ja dita dla'idikiyya kzuv hajana amar amin sahda mhaymna wsharira wrishiza davrizeh d'alaha
15	yada na vadayk la karira at wla jamima dwale wa d'aw karira tehwe aw jamima
16	wi'yzayk pashura wla karira wla jamima zid na lamzavuzaj men pumy
17	metul d'emart datira at wezrez wal medem la snik na wla yada at d'at w mjila wdawya wmeskina wartelayya
18	malej na laj dzesben meny dahva davjir men nura dzetar wma'ne jeware lmezatafu wla tezgle behtza dartelayyuzaj washyafa kjul dzejse
19	ena la'ylen drajem na makes na wrade na tan hajil wzuv
20	ha kamez al tara we'kush en nash shama bkaly wneftaj tara we'ul wa'jshem ameh whu amy
21	wadsaje etel leh lmetav amy al kursya dily aykana d'ena sjiz wyezbez am avy al kursya dileh
22	man d'iyz leh edne neshma mana ruja mmalel lidaza
