1	kul damhaymen dyeshu izawy mshija men alaha ilid wjul dmajev lyaluda majev af lhaw d'eziled meneh
2	wavhade yadinan dmajvinan lavnawy d'alaha ma dla'laha majvinan wavdinan pukdanawy
3	hana hu guer jubeh d'alaha dnetar pukdanawy wfukdanawy la yakirin
4	metul djul dilid men alaha saje leh lalma whade y sajuza dasjazeh lalma haymanuzan
5	manu guer dsaje leh lalma ela haw damhaymen dyeshu breh u d'alaha
6	hanaw d'eza byad mayya wadma yeshu mshija la wa bmayya baljud ela bmayya wadma
7	wruja mashda dhi ruja izeh shrara
8	wi'yzayhon tlaza sahdin ruja wmayya wadma wazlatayhon bjad enon
9	en sahduza davnaynasha mkablinan jad kma sahduzeh d'alaha draba y whade i sahduzeh d'alaha d'ashed al breh
10	kulman damhaymen bavreh d'alaha iz leh hade sahduza bnafsheh kul dla mhaymen la'laha dagala avdeh badla haymen lsahduza d'ashed alaha al breh
11	whade y sahduza djayye dalalam yav lan alaha whenon jayye bavreh izayhon
12	kul d'ajid lavra ajid af ljayye wjul dla ajid lavreh d'alaha layt leh jayye
13	halen kezbez ljon dzedon djayye dalalam iz ljon la'ylen dhaymenton bashmeh davreh d'alaha
14	whade paresiyya iz lan lwazeh djul dsha'linan leh ayj sevyaneh shama lan
15	we'n mpasinan dshama lan al medem dsha'linan meneh tjilinan dkabeln men kadu she'lazan dshe'ln meneh
16	en nash nejse la'ju djate jtaha dla mjayyav lmawta nesha'l wmezyahbin leh jayye la'ylen dla wa ayj dalmawta jaten iz guer jtaha dmawta la wa al hana amar na dneve nash
17	kul awla guer jtiza hu wi'yz jtaha dla wa dmawta hu
18	wyadinan djul dilid men alaha la jate haw guer dilid men alaha natar nafsheh wvisha la mezkarav leh
19	yadinan dmen alaha jnan walma kuleh bvisha hu sim
20	wyadinan davreh d'alaha eza wyav lan mada dneda lsharira wnehwe beh bsharira bavreh yeshu mshija hana hu alaha sharira wjayye dalalam
21	bnay tarw nafshjon men dejlaz pzajre
