1	jabivay la ljul rujin thaymnon ela hwayton parshin ruje en men alaha izayhen metul dsaguiyye' nviyye dagale nfakw beh balma
2	bhade mezyada rujeh d'alaha kul ruja dmawdyan dyeshu mshija eza bavsar men alaha y
3	wjul ruja dla mawdya dyeshu eza bavsar layteh men alaha ela hade men mshija y dagala haw dashmaton d'aze whasha balma izawy men kadu
4	aton den men alaha izaykon bnayya wasjayton enon metul drab haw davjon men haw davalma
5	whalen men alma enon metul hana men alma mmallin walma lhon shama
6	jnan den men alaha jnan whaw dyada la'laha shama lan whaw dla izawy men alaha la shama lan bah bhade mestaklinan lruja dashrara walruja dmatyanuza
7	jabivay najev jad ljad metul djuba men alaha hu wjul man dmajev men alaha ilid wyada la'laha
8	metul d'alaha juba hu wjul dla majev la yada la'laha
9	bhade ezida jubeh d'alaha dalwazan dlavreh ijidayya shadar alaha lalma dnije b'iydeh
10	bhana izawy juba la wa dajnan ajevn la'laha ela hu ajvan wshadar lavreh jusayya al apay jtahayn
11	jabivay en hajana ajvan alaha af jnan jayyavinan jad ljad lmajavu
12	la'laha memzum nash la jsa en den najev jad ljad alaha ban mkawe wjubeh meshtamle ban
13	wavhade yadinan dveh mkawenan whu mkawe ban dmen rujeh yav lan
14	wajnan jsayn wmashdinan d'ava shadar lavreh paruka lalma
15	kul dmawde byeshu dhuyyu breh d'alaha alaha beh mkawe whu mkawe b'alaha
16	wajnan haymenn widan juba d'iyz leh la'laha se'dayn alaha guer juba hu wjul damkawe bjuba b'alaha mkawe
17	wavhade meshtalam jubeh aman dgalyuz ape tehwe lan byawma ddina metul d'ayj dahwa hu hajana af jnan izayn bhana alma
18	dejlza bjuba layt ela juba mshamlya lvar shade lah ldejlza metul ddejlza bkenta izeh haw den ddajel la mshamlay bjuba
19	jnan hajil najev la'laha metul dhu kadmayyaz ajvan
20	en den nash ni'mar dmajev na la'laha wla'ju sane dagala hu haw guer dla'ju dmezjse la majev la'laha haw dla mezjse aykana meshkaj dnajev
21	whana pukdana kabeln meneh djul dmajev la'laha najev af la'ju
