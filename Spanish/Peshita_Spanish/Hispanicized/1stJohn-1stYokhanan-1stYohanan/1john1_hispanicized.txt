1	msabrinan ljon lhaw d'iyzawy wa men breshiz haw dashman wajsayna baynayn jsayn wgashn b'iydayn haw d'iyzawy melza djayye
2	wjayye ezgliw wajsayn wsahdinan wmajrsinan ljon jayye dalalam hanon d'iyzayhon waw lwaz ava we'zgliw lan
3	wmedem dajsayn washman mawdinan af ljon dzehwe ljon shawtafuza aman wshawtafuzan den dilan izeh am ava wam breh yeshu mshija
4	whalen kazbinan ljon djaduzan davjon tehwe mshamlya
5	whade y svarza dashman meneh wamsabrinan ljon d'alaha nuhra w wjeshuja kul kuleh layt beh
6	we'n amrinan dshawtafuza iz lan ameh wamhaljinan bjeshuja dagale nan wlaw bashrara radenan
7	en den bnuhra mhaljinan ayj dhu izawy bnuhra iz lan shawtafuza am jdade wadma dyeshu breh mdake lan men kulhon jtahayn
8	we'n ni'mar dlayt lan jtiza nafshan matenan washrara layt ban
9	en den mawdenan bajtahayn mhayman u wsadik dneshbuk lan jtahayn wandaken men kuleh awlan
10	we'n amrinan dla jtayn avdinan leh dagala wmelzeh layt lwazan
