1	wajsaw dajma sagui jubeh d'ava lwazan davnayya kran af avdan metul hana alma la yada lan metul d'afla leh ida
2	jabivay hasha bnawy jnan d'alaha wla ezgli dama lhasha mana zidinan dnehwe yadinan den dma d'ezgli badmuzeh hawenan wjasenan leh ayj medem d'iyzawy
3	wjul ayna d'iyz leh lawy hana savra mdake nafsheh ayj dhu dje u
4	man den daved jtiza awla saar jtiza guer kulah awla y
5	wyadin ton dhaw d'ezgli dnesav jtahayn wajtiza beh layt
6	wjul dveh mkawe la jate wjul djate la jsa wla yadeh
7	bnay la nash natejon haw daved sadikuza sadika hu ayj d'af hu mshija izawy sadika
8	haw dsaar jtiza men satana hu metul dmen rishiza hu satana jatayya hu wmetul hana ezjsi breh d'alaha dneshre vadawy dsatana
9	kul d'eziled men alaha jtiza la aved metul dsareh beh izawy wla meshkaj lmejta dmen alaha eziled
10	bhade mezparshin bnawy d'alaha men bnawy dsatana kul dla aved sadikuza wla majev la'ju laytawy men alaha
11	dhanaw pukdana dashmaton men kdim dzajvon jad ljad
12	law ayj ka'yen haw d'iyzawy wa men bisha waktal la'ju wmetul mana katleh ela metul dvishin waw vadawy wda'ju sadikin
13	wla tetdamron ajay en sane ljon alma
14	jnan yadinan dshaninan men mawta ljayye bhade dmajvinan la'jayn haw dla majev la'ju mkawe bmawta
15	kul guer dsane la'ju katel nasha hu wyadin ton djul dkatel nasha la meshkjin mkawen beh jayye dalalam
16	bhade yadinan jubeh dalwazan dhu yav nafsheh jlafayn wa'f jnan sadek lan dal apay ajayn netel nafshazan
17	wa'yna d'iyz leh kenyana dalma wnejse la'ju dasnik wne'jud rajmawy meneh aykana iz beh jubeh d'alaha
18	bnay la najev jad ljad bmele wavleshana ela bavade wvashrara
19	wavhade meshtawdinan dmen shrara izayn wakdam dni'ze hu mpisinan leban
20	d'en hu leban basar lan kma alaha drab men leban wyada kulmedem
21	jabivay en leban la basar lan galyan enen apayn kdam alaha
22	wjulmedem dsha'linan nasbinan meneh metul dnatrinan pukdanawy wshapiraza sarinan kdamawy
23	whana u pukdaneh danhaymen bashma davreh yeshu mshija wnajev jad ljad ayj dfakdan
24	wa'yna dnatar pukdanawy beh mezntar whu share beh wavhade mestaklinan dshare ban men rujeh ha dyav lan
