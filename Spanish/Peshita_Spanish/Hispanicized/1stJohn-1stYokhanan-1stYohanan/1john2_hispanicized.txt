1	bnay halen kazev na ljon dla tejton we'n nash nejte iz lan paraklita lwaz ava yeshu mshija sadika
2	huyyu guer jusayya dal apay jtahayn wlaw jlafayn dilan baljud ela af jlaf kuleh alma
3	wavhade margshinan didana en natrinan pukdanawy
4	haw guer d'amar didazeh wfukdanawy la natar dagala hu washrara layt beh
5	haw den dnatar melzeh bhana mshalam sharira'iz jubeh d'alaha bhade guer yadinan dveh jnan
6	haw d'amar dveh izay wale leh d'ayj haljazeh dileh nhalej
7	jabivay law pukdana jaza kazev na ljon ela pukdana atika haw d'iyz wa ljon men breshiz pukdana den atika izawy melza ha dashmaton
8	tuv pukdana jaza kazev na ljon haw d'iyzawy sharira beh wavjon djeshuja var leh wnuhra sharira shari mezjse
9	man d'amar hajil d'iyzawy bnuhra wsane la'ju bjeshuja izawy dama lhasha
10	haw den dmajev la'ju bnuhra mjatar wjeshla layt beh
11	haw den dsane la'ju bjeshuja izawy wavjeshuja mhalej wla yada la'yka asel metul djeshuja sami enen aynawy
12	kazev na ljon bnayya d'eshtvekw ljon jtahaykon metul shmeh
13	kazev na ljon avahe didatuna lhaw d'iyzawy wa men breshiz kazev na ljon layme dasjaytuna lvisha kezbez ljon tlayye didaton la'va
14	kezbez ljon avahe didaton lhaw dmen breshiz kezbez ljon layme djaylzane ton wmelzeh d'alaha sharya bjon wasjaytuna lvisha
15	la terjmon lalma wla lmedem d'iyz beh man guer drajem lalma jubeh d'ava layt beh
16	kul medem guer d'iyz beh balma regza hu dfagra wregza dayne wshuvhara dalma halen dla way men ava ela meneh enen men alma
17	wavar alma hu wregzeh haw den daved sevyaneh d'alaha mkawe lalam
18	bnay savna hu jrayya wa'yj medem dashmaton d'aze mshija dagala whasha hwaw lhon saguiyye' mshije dagale wmen hade yadinan dsavna hu jrayya
19	menan nfakw ela law menan waw elu guer menan waw lwazan mjatrin waw ela nfakw menan dzezida dlaw menan waw
20	wa'ton mshijuza iz ljon men kadisha wfarshin ton u ljul nash
21	la kezbez ljon dla yadin ton leh lashrara ela dyadin ton leh wadjulah dagaluza la waz meneh men shrara
22	manu dagala ela en ayna djafar dyeshu la wa mshija hana hu mshija dagala haw djafar b'ava kafar af bavra
23	whaw djafar bavra afla b'ava mhaymen man dmawde bavra af b'ava mawde
24	wa'ton medem dashmaton men kdim nkawe lwazjon en guer mkawe lwazjon medem dashmaton men kdim af aton mkawen ton b'ava wvavra
25	whanaw shudayya d'eshtawdi lan jayye dalalam
26	halen den kezbez ljon metul aylen dmaten ljon
27	wa'f aton en tkawe lwazjon mshijuza dkabelton meneh la snikin ton dnash nalefjon ela ayj mshijuza d'iyzeh men alaha hi malfa ljon al kulmedem wsharira y wlayt bah dagaluza wa'yj d'alefjon kawaw beh
28	whasha bnay kawaw beh dma dmezgle la nevhaz meneh ela tehwe lan galyuz ape bme'zizeh
29	en idaton dsadik u daw d'af kul daved sadikuza meneh izawy
