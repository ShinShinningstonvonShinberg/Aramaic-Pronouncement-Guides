1	men hasha ajayn salaw layn dmelzeh dmaran tehwe rahta wmeshtabja bjul duka ayj dalwazjon
2	wadnezpase men bnaynasha bishe wanaze law guer djulnash i haymanuza
3	mhayman u den marya dhu nnatarjon wanshawsevjon men bisha
4	tjilinan den laykon bmaran dmedem damfakdinan ljon vadton af avditon
5	wmaran nezrus lebawazjon ljubeh d'alaha wlamsabranuzeh damshija
6	mfakdinan ljon den ajay bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija dahwayton parikin men kul aja ayna dvish bish mhalej wla ayj pukdane dkabelw menan
7	aton guer yadin ton aykana wale lmetdamayyu ban dla halejn bish bish baynazjon
8	afla lajma dmagan ejaln men nash menjon ela bamla wavle'uza blilya wvi'ymama paljin wayn dal nash menjon la ni'kar
9	la wa metul dla shalit lan ela davnafshan netel ljon tufsa dvan tetdamon
10	af kad lwazjon wayn guer hade mfakdin wayn ljon djul dla save dnefluj afla nelas
11	shaminan guer d'iyz bjon nasha dvish bish mhaljin wmedem la paljin ela en srikaza
12	lhon den lhalen mfakdinan wvaenan menhon bmaran yeshu mshija davshelya nehon paljin wa'jlin lajmhon
13	ton den ajay la tema'n ljon lmebad dshapir
14	we'n nash la meshtma lmelayn halen dve'garta nezpresh ljon hana wla hwayton mezjaltin ameh dnevhaz
15	wla ayj beldvava te'jduneh ela hwayton marten leh ayj dla'ja
16	hu den mareh dashlama netel ljon shlama bjulsvan bjulmedem maran am kuljon
17	shlama bajzavaz iday ena pawlaws kezbez d'iyzawy aza davjulhen egrazy hajana kazev na
18	taybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija am kuljon amin
