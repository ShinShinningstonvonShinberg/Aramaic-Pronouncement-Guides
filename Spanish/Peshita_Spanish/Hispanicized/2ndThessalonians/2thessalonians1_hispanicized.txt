1	pawlaws wsilwanaws wtimaze'aws lita dzesalawnikayye dva'laha avon wmaran yeshu mshija
2	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
3	lmawdayyu la'laha bjulsvan jayyavinan laykon ajay ayj ma dwale dyatira'iz ravya haymanuzjon wsague juba djuljon djulnash lwaz javreh
4	ayj d'af jnan bjon nehwe meshtavhrin nan bidaza d'alaha al haymanuzjon wal msaybranuzjon davjulah rdifuzjon u'wlsanaykon damsaybrin ton
5	lzajwiza ddina ki'na d'alaha dzeshon lmalkuzeh ha dal apeh jashiton
6	we'n ki'na kdam alaha dnefru ulsana la'lusaykon
7	waljon dmeza'lsiton naje aman bguelyaneh dmaran yeshu mshija dmen shmayya am jayla dmala'jawy
8	ma daved tvaza bgusala dnura men hanon dla idaw la'laha wmen hanon dla eshtawdaw lasvarzeh dmaran yeshu mshija
9	dhenon bdina nezparon avdana dalalam men parsupeh dmaran wmen shuvja djayleh
10	ma d'aze dneshtabaj bkadishawy wanjawe tedmrazeh bamhaymnawy dzezhayman sahduzan dalaykon bhaw yawma
11	metulhana bjulsvan msalenan laykon dnashwejon alaha lkeryanjon wnemlejon kuleh sevyana dtavaza wavade dhaymanuza bjayla
12	dneshtabaj bjon shmeh dmaran yeshu mshija af aton beh ayj taybuzeh d'alahan wmaran yeshu mshija
