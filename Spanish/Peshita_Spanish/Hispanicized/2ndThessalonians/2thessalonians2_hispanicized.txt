1	baenan den menjon ajay al me'zizeh dmaran yeshu mshija wal knushya dilan dalwazeh
2	dla gal tetsion breyanaykon wla tezwhon la men melza wla men ruja wla men egarta d'ayj dmen lwazan i dha lam mati yawmeh dmaran
3	lma nash natejon bjad men eskimin metul d'en la ti'ze lukdam maruduza wnezgle barnasha dajtiza breh d'avdana
4	haw dalkuvla w wmeshtale al kul man dmezkre alah wdejla aykana d'af bhaykla d'alaha ayj alaha netev wanjawe al nafsheh ayj d'alaha hu
5	la ahditon djad lwazjon wiz halen emrez wiz ljon
6	whasha yadin ton ma d'ajid dnezgle hu bsavneh
7	rasa guer dawla men kadu shari lmezjapatu baljud en haw ma dhasha ajid neshtkel men msaza
8	whaden nezgle awala haw dmaran yeshu nsifiwy bruj pumeh wanvatliwy bguelyana dme'zizeh
9	me'zizeh guer dhaw bmabdanuza y dsatana bjul jil wa'zwaza wzedmraza dagalaza
10	wavjulah tuyay dawla dhawya b'avide al dla kabelw juba dkushta dveh nijon
11	metulhana nshadar lhon alaha mabdanuza dtuyay danhaymnon lshukra
12	wnetdinon kulhon aylen dla haymenw bkushta ela estviw bawla
13	jnan den jayyavinan lmawdayyu la'laha bjulsvan al apaykon ajayn jabivawy dmaran dagvajon alaha men rishiza ljayye bkadishuza druja wavhaymanuza dashrara
14	lhalen u guer krajon bamsabranuza dilan dzehon teshbujta lmaran yeshu mshija
15	mekil ajay ezkamw wjamsenw bfukdane dilefton en bmelza we'n b'egarta dilan
16	hu den maran yeshu mshija wa'laha avon haw d'ajvan wyav lan buyya'a dalalam wsavra tava btaybuzeh
17	nvayya lebawazjon wankayyem bjul mela wavjul vad tav
