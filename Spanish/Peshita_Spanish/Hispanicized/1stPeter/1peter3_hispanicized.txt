1	hajana af aten neshe eshtabaden lvalayken d'aylen dla mettpisin lmelza bdubarayken shapire dla amla teknyan enon
2	kad jasen davdejlza wavnajpuza metdabran ten
3	wla testabzan bsevte barayye dagdule dsarayken aw djeshlaza ddahva aw dalvushe myatre
4	ela estabazen bvarnasha kasya dleba bruja makijta dla mezjabla sevta damyatar kdam alaha
5	hajana guer af men kdim neshe kadishaza aylen damsabran way b'alaha msabzan way nafshazhen wmeshtabdan way lvalayhen
6	aykana dsara meshtabda waz la'vraham wkarya waz leh mary ha dhawyan ten lah bnaza bavade tave kad la mettsian ten men kul dejla
7	wa'ton gavre hajana marw am neshaykon bidaza wa'yj dalma'ne mjile b'iykara ajudw enen metul d'af hanen amjon yartan mawhavta djayye dalalam dla tehon mettaklin baslawazjon
8	shulama den dzehon kuljon b'awyuza wahwayton jashin am aylen djashin wrajmin jad ljad wahwayton rajmzanin wmakijin
9	walnash bishta jlaf bishta la tefron wa'fla sujiza jlaf sujiza ela dalkuvla dhalen hwayton mvarjin lhade guer ezkriton dvurkza ti'rton
10	man dsave hajil jayye wrajem yawmaza tave lmejsa netar leshaneh men bishta wsefwazeh la nmallan nejla
11	nebar men bishta wnebed tavza wneve shlama wnerhat bazreh
12	metul daynawy dmarya al sadike we'dnawy lmeshma enon wa'pawy dmarya al bishe
13	wmanu dnebed ljon bishta en tehon tanane dtavaza
14	we'n hu dzejshon al apay ki'nuza tuvaykon wla tedjlon men aylen damdajlin ljon wla teshtagshon
15	ela kadeshw blebawazjon lmarya mshija wahwayton mtayvin lmapak bruja ljul dzava ljon melza al savra dhaymanuzjon bmakijuza wavdejlza
16	kad iz ljon ti'rta tavza aykana dhanon dammallin laykon ayj dal nasha bishe nevhzon ayj nasha dtalmin ldubaraykon shapire dvamshija
17	adra y ljon guer djad avdin ton vade tave tesblon bishaza en hajana w sevyana d'alaha wla kad avdin ton bishaza
18	metul d'af mshija jda svan miz jlaf jtahayn sadika jlaf jatayye dankarevjon la'laha wmiz bafgar wajya bruj
19	wa'jres lnafshaza aylen d'ajidan way bashyul
20	halen dmen kdim la ettpis way byawmazeh dnuj kad magraz rujeh d'alaha pekdaz dzehwe kivuza al savra dazyavuzhon wazmane baljud nafshan alen lah wajyay bmayya
21	d'af aton beh bhaw tufsa jayyen ton bmamudiza law kad pagra mshiguin ton men sa'za ela kad mawden ton b'alaha bzi'rta djiza wvakyamteh dyeshu mshija
22	haw d'ezali lashmayya wi'yzawy al yamina d'alaha we'shtabadw leh mala'je wshalitane wjaylawaza
