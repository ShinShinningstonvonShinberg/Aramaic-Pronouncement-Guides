2	dla mekil larguigaza davnaynasha nije kma savna d'iyzawy bfagra ela lsevyana d'alaha
3	safek guer savna haw davar daflajton beh sevyana djanfe b'asutuza wavrawayyuza wavsajnuza wvasmara wavfuljana dshi'de
4	wha hasha metdamrin wamgadfin laykon badla meshtarjiton amhon bha asutuza kadmata
5	hanon dyahbin pezgama la'laha haw dazid lamdan mize wjayye
6	metul hana guer estabar af lmize dnetdinon ayj bnaynasha bavsar wnijon b'alaha bruj
7	matyaz lah den jarza djul metul hana eznakafw we'ttirw lasluza
8	wakdam kul medem juba jarifa lwaz jdade nehwe ljon juba guer mjape suga' dajtahe
9	wahwayton rajmin ajsnayye dla retna
10	wjulnash menjon mawhavta dkabel men alaha nshamesh bah ljavrawy ayj rabay bate tave dtaybuza mfarashta d'alaha
11	kulman dammalel ayj melza d'alaha nmalel wjulman damshamesh ayj dmen jayla haw d'alaha yahev leh davjul davdin ton neshtabaj alaha byad yeshu mshija haw ddileh i teshbujta wi'ykara lalam almin amin
12	jabivay la tetdamron bnesyune dhawen ljon ayj dmedem nujra gadesh ljon metul dalvujranjon u hawen
13	ela jdaw dmeshtawtfin ton ljashawy damshija dhajana af bguelyana dzeshbujteh tejdon wzerwson
14	we'n mezjasdin ton al apay shmeh damshija tuvaykon druja mshabajta d'alaha mettnija laykon
15	baljud la nash menjon ayj katula aw ayj ganava aw ayj aved bishaza nehwe ja'esh
16	en den ja'esh ayj kristyana la nevhaz ela nshabaj la'laha beh bhana shma
17	metul dsavna w danshare dina men bayteh d'alaha en den menan mshare ayda y jarza d'aylen dla mettpisin lasvarzeh d'alaha

19	metul hana aylen djashin ayj sevyaneh d'alaha naglon leh nafshazhon bavade shapire ayj dalvaruyya mhaymna
