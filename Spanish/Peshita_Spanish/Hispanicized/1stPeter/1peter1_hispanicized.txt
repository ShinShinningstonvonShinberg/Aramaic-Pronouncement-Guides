1	petraws shlija dyeshu mshija lagvayya wzawtave dasriin bpantaws wavgalatiyya wavkapawdkiyya wva'siyya wavvizuniyya
2	aylen d'ezgviw bamkadmuz idazeh d'alaha ava bkadishuza druja dnehon lmashmaza wlarsas dmeh dyeshu mshija taybuza washlama nesgue lwazjon
3	mvaraj u alaha avu dmaran yeshu mshija haw dvajnaneh saguiyya' awldan men drish bakyamteh dyeshu mshija lsavra djayye
4	walyartuza dla mezjabla wla mettanfa wla jamya ha damtayva ljon bashmayya
5	kad ntirin ton bjayla d'alaha wavhaymanuza ljayye damtayvin dnezglon lsavne jrayye
6	davhon tejdon lalam afen bhana savna kalil mettikin ton bnesyune mshajlfe daden laykon
7	aykana dvujrana dhaymanuzjon nezjse damyatar men dahva snina d'ezbki bnura lzeshbujta wli'ykara walkulasa bguelyaneh dyeshu mshija
8	haw dla jsaytuna wmajvin ton leh wavhaymanuzeh rawsin ton bjaduza mshabajta dla mezmalla
9	dazkablon purana dhaymanuzjon jayye dnafshazjon
10	hanon jayye dakevw waw layhon nviyye kad eznabiw al taybuza dazida hwaz dzezihev ljon
11	wavsaw dva'yna savna mjawya wmashda rujeh damshija damra bhon dazidin jashawy damshija wzeshbujteh dmen bazarken
12	we'zgli lhon kul dvasen waw metul dlaw lnafshhon baen waw ela lan dilan meznaben waw aylen dhasha ezgli ljon byad aylen dsabarnajon bruja dkudsha d'eshtadar men shmayya davhen bhalen mezragrguin af mala'je dandaykon
13	metul hana jsukw jase dzaryazjon we'ttirw gmira'iz wsabarw al jaduza d'azya ljon bguelyaneh dmaran yeshu mshija
14	ayj bnayya meshtamane wla teshtawtfon tuv larguigazjon kadmayyaza aylen draguin hwayton dla bidaza
15	ela hwaw kadishin bjulhon hupajaykon ayj dkadish haw man dakrajon
16	metul dajziv dahwayton kadishin aykana d'af ena kadish na
17	we'n hu d'ava karen ton haw dlayt kdamawy masav b'ape wda'en ljulnash ayj vadawy bdejlza etdabarw bsavna hana dzawtavuzjon
18	kad yadin ton dla bjespa dvale wla bdahva ezprekton men vadaykon srike hanon dkabelton men avahaykon
19	ela badma yakira d'emra dmuma wtulsha layt beh d'iyzawy mshija
20	haw damkadam wa prish lhade men kdam tarmyazeh dalma we'zgli bajrathon dsavne metulazjon
21	aylen dvi'ydeh haymenton b'alaha haw d'akimeh men bez mize wyav leh shuvja dhaymanuzjon wsavrjon nehwe al alaha
22	kad nehwyan kadishan nafshazjon bmeshtamanuzeh dashrara wnehwyan malyan juba dla masav b'ape dmen leba dajya wagmira tehon majvin jad ljad
23	ayj nasha dmen drish eziledton la men sara dvale ela men ayna dla bale bmelza jayza d'alaha dkayyama lalam
24	metul djul bsar mira wjulah ya'yuzeh ayj ufya djakla yavesh mira wjame ufya
25	wmelzeh d'alahan kayyama lalmin whade y melza ha d'estabarton
