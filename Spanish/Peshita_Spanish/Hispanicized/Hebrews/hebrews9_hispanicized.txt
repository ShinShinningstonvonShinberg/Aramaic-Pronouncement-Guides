1	bkadmata den iz wa bah pukade dzeshmeshta wvez kudsha almanayya
2	mashkna guer kadmayya d'ezved iz wa beh mnarta wfazura waljem ape wmezkre wa bez kudsha
3	mashkna den gawayya dalgaw men apay tara dazren mezkre wa kdush kudshe
4	wi'yz wa beh bez besme ddahva wkivuza ddiyyaziki dakrima kulah bdahva wi'yz bah kesta ddahva ha d'iyz wa bah manna wshavta d'ahron haw d'afra wluje ddiyyaziki
5	walel menah kruve dshuvja dmatlin al jusayya la wa den savna w dni'mar al jda jda men halen dhajana mazknan way
6	walmashkna barayya bjulsvan a'lin waw kumre wamshalmin waw teshmeshthon
7	lmashkna den dalgaw meneh jda w bshata baljudawy a'el wa rabkumre badma haw damkarev wa jlaf nafsheh wajlaf sajluzeh dama
8	bhade den mawda waz ruja dkudsha dla ezgalyaz dakil urja dkadishe kma savna d'iyz wa kyama lmashkna kadmayya
9	wi'yzawy wa mazla hana lsavna haw dveh kurbane wdevje mezkarvin waw aylen dla meshkjin waw lmegmar ti'rteh dman damkarev lhon
10	ela bme'jla wavmashtya baljud wavmamudyaza dasnin snin d'iyzayhen pukade dvesra dsimin dama lsavna dzurasa
11	mshija den d'eza hwa rabkumre dtavaza dasar wal lmashkna raba wamshalmana dla vid b'iydayya wla wa men halen beryaza
12	wla al badma dsifrayye wadegle ela badma dnafsheh al jda svan lvez makdsha we'shkaj purkana dalalam
13	en guer dma dsifrayye wadegle wketma daguelta mezrses wa al aylen dmettami'yn waw wamkadesh lhon ldukayya dvesrhon
14	kma hajil yatira'iz dmeh damshija davruja dalalam nafsheh karev dla mum la'laha ndake ti'rtan men vade mize danshamesh la'laha jayya
15	metul hana hu hwa mesayya ddiyyaziki jdaza davmawteh hu hwa purkana la'ylen davarw al diyyaziki kadmata dnesvon mulkana aylen d'ezkriw lyartuza dalalam
16	ayka guer d'iyz diyyaziki mawta w mjawya dhaw davdah
17	al miza den baljud meshtarra metul dajma djay haw davdah layt bah jashju
18	metul hana afla kadmata dla dma eshtarraz
19	kad ezpked guer kuleh pukdana men mushe lama kuleh bnamusa nsav wa mushe dma daguelta wmayya bamra dasjuriza wsupa wras al sefre wal ama kuleh
20	we'mar lhon hana w dma ddiyyaziki ha d'ezpakdaz ljon men alaha
21	af al mashkna wal kulhon ma'ne dzeshmeshta meneh men dma ras
22	metul djulmedem badma w metdake bnamusa wadla shupa dma layt shuvkana
23	ananki i guer dhalen dadmuza enen dashmayyanyaza bhalen metdakyan halen den shmayyanyaza bdevje damyatrin men halen
24	la wa guer lvez makdsha david b'iydayya al mshija d'iyzawy dmuza dhaw sharira ela leh lashmayya al dnezjse kdam parsupeh d'alaha jlafayn
25	afla dankarev nafsheh savnaza saguiyya'za ayj daved wa rab kumre wa'el bjul shna lvez makdsha badma dla dileh
26	we'n la jayyav wa dsavnaza saguiyya'za nejash men shurayyeh dalma hasha den bjarzeh dalma jda w svan karev nafsheh badvijuzeh danvatlih lajtiza
27	wa'ykana dsim lavnaynasha dajda svan nmuzon wmen bazar mawthon dina
28	hajana af mshija jda svan ezkarav wvaknumeh dvaj jtahe dsaguiyye' dzarten den savnin dla jtahin mezjse ljayyayhon d'aylen damsaken leh
