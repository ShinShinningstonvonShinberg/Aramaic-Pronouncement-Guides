1	hana den malkisdek izawy mlej shalim kumra d'alaha mrayma whu areh la'vraham kad hfaj men jarba dmalke wvarjeh
2	wleh prash avraham masare men kulmedem d'iyz wa ameh mezpashak den shmeh malka dji'nuza wzuv mlej shalim d'iyzawy malka dashlama
3	dla avu wla emeh ezkzevw bsharvaza wla rishiza dyawmawy wla shulama djayyawy ela badmuza davreh d'alaha mkawya kumruzeh lalam
4	jsaw den kma rav hana d'avraham rish avahaza leh yav masare drishyaza
5	aylen guer men bnay lewi damkablin waw kumruza pukdana iz wa lhon dnamusa dnesvon masare men ama henon men ajayhon kad af henon men jaseh d'avraham nfakw
6	hana den dla kziv bsharvazhon masare shkal men avraham wvarjeh lhaw dkabel mulkana
7	dla jeryana den haw davsir mezbaraj men haw damyatar meneh
8	wharka bnaynasha dmatin nasbin masare lhal den haw d'ashed lawy kzava djay u
9	wa'yj nash ni'mar byad avraham af lewi haw dmasare nasev wa af hu ezasar
10	dakil guer bjaseh hwa d'avu kad areh lmalkisdek
11	elu hajil gmiruza byad kumruza dlewayye izeh waz dvah sim namusa lama lmana mezbe wa kumra jrina dankum badmuzeh dmalkisdek emar den dvadmuzeh d'ahron nehwe
12	ela aykana dahwa shujlafa bjumruza hajana hwa shujlafa af bnamusa
13	haw guer d'eze'mar lawy halen men sharbza w jriza eziled dnash memzum menah la shamesh bmadbja
14	galya y guer dmen ihuda dnaj maran men sharbza dla emar leh mushe medem al kumruza
15	wyatira'iz tuv idia bha d'emar dvadmuzeh dmalkisdek ka'em kumra jrina
16	haw dla wa bnamusa dfukdane pagranayye hawe ela bjayla djayye dla meshtren
17	mashed guer lawy d'at u kumra lalam badmuzeh dmalkisdek
18	shujlafa den dahwa lfukdana kadmayya metul mjiluzeh wadyuzran layt wa beh
19	medem guer la gmar namusa al den jlafawy savra damyatar meneh dveh mezkarvinan la'laha
20	wsharreh lan bmawmaza
21	hanon guer dla mawmaza hwaw kumre hana den bmawmaza ayj d'emar leh byad dawid dima marya wla ndaguel d'at u kumra lalam badmuzeh dmalkisdek
22	hana kuleh myatra diyyaziki hade dahwa bah arava yeshu
23	whanon hwaw kumre saguiyye' metul dmatin waw wla meshtavkin waw dankaon
24	hana den metul dalalam kayyam la avra kumruzeh
25	wmeshkaj lmajayyu lalam la'ylen dmezkarvin b'iydeh la'laha jay u guer bjulsvan wmasek sluza jlafayhon
26	d'ayj hana guer kumra af sadek wa lan dajya dla bishu wadla tulsha dfarik men jtahe wamrayyam lel men shmayya
27	wlayt leh ulsana kulyum ayj rabay kumre dlukdam jlaf jtahawy nkarev devje whaden jlaf ama hade guer avdah jda svan bnafsheh dkarev
28	namusa guer bnaynasha hu krihe mkim kumre melza den dmawmaza dahwaz bazar namusa bra gmira lalam
