1	namusa guer telaniza iz wa beh dtavaza dazidan la wa knuma dilhen dsevwaza metul hana kad bjul shna henon kad henon devje mezkarvin waw la memzum eshkajw dnegmron la'ylen damkarvin lhon
2	elu guer gamrin waw kvar den ettnijw men kurbanayhon metul dla mekil tarya waz lhon ti'rthon bajtahe la'ylen dajda svan etdakiw lhon
3	ela bhon bdevje madkrin jtahayhon bjul shna
4	la guer meshkaj dma dzawre wadsifrayye lamdakayyu jtahe
5	metul hana kad a'el lalma emar bdevje wkurbane la svayt pagra den albeshtany
6	wyakde shalme dajlaf jtahe la she'lt
7	haden emrez dha ena aze na davrish kzave kziv lay d'ebed sevyanaj alaha
8	men lel emar ddevje wkurbane wyakde shalme dajlaf jtahe la svayt hanon dmezkarvin waw bnamusa
9	wvazreh emar dha aze na d'ebed sevyanaj alaha bhade batel lkadmata dankim ladzarten
10	bhana guer sevyaneh ezkadashn bkurbana dfagreh dyeshu mshija dajda svan
11	kul rab kumre guer dka'em wa wamshamesh kulyum henon bhenon devje mkarev wa aylen dmen mzum la meshkjin waw lamdakayyu jtahe
12	hana den jad devja karev jlaf jtahe wizev al yamina d'alaha lalam
13	wamkawe mekil dama dnettsimon beldvavawy kuvsha tjez reglawy
14	bjad guer kurbana gmar la'ylen dmezkadshin beh lalam
15	sahda lan den af ruja dkudsha d'emraz
16	hade y diyyaziki d'etel lhon men bazar yawmaza hanon amar marya etliwy lnamusy bmadayhon wal lebawazhon ejtviwy
17	wawlhon wajtahayhon la etdjar lhon
18	ayka den d'iyz shuvkana dajtahe la mezbe kurbana dajlaf jtahe
19	iz lan hajil ajay galyuz ape bmalana dvez kudsha badmeh dyeshu
20	u'wrja djayye djadez lan hasha b'apay tara d'iyzawy besreh
21	wi'yz lan kumra raba al bayteh d'alaha
22	nekruv hajil bleba sharira wavzujlana dhaymanuza kad rsisin lebawazan wadjen men ti'rta bishta wmasjay pagran bmayya djayya
23	wanjamsen bzawdiza dsavran wla nestle mhayman u guer man damlaj lan
24	wanjur jad bjad bguraga djuba wdavade tave
25	wla nehwe shavkin knushyan ayj d'iyz yada lnash nash ela baw jad men jad yatira'iz kma djaseton dakrev yawma haw
26	en guer bsevyaneh nejte nash men bazar dkabel idaza dashrara layt mekil devjza dzezkarav jlaf jtahe
27	ela zid u dina djila watnana dnura d'ajel laveldvave
28	en guer ayna davar al namusa dmushe al pum tren wazlaza sahdin dla rajmin ma'ez
29	kma savrin ton yatira'iz msam brisha nkabel ayna ddasheh lavreh d'alaha wajshav dma ddiyyaziki dileh ayj djulnash dvah ezkadash wsaar lruja dtaybuza
30	yadinan lhaw d'emar ddily i tvaza we'na efru wzuv dandon marya lameh
31	dejlza y rabza lmepal b'iydawy d'alaha jayya
32	etdarw hajil lyawmaza kadmayye hanon davhon kabelton mamudiza wa'guna raba saybarton djashe bjesda wvu'wlsane
33	wdahwayton jeswane wa'f eshtawtafton lnasha dhalen saybarw
34	wje'v ljon al aylen d'asirin wajtufya dnejsaykon bjaduza saybarton metul dyadin ton d'iyz ljon kenyana bashmayya damyatar u wla avar
35	la hajil tawbdon galyuz ape d'iyz ljon dazid lah agra saguiyya'
36	msaybranuza w guer mezbaya ljon dzebdon sevyana d'alaha wzesvon mulkana
37	metul dkalil hu savna wtav sur dni'ze haw d'aze wla nawjar
38	ki'na den men haymanuza dily nije we'n mezkata leh la savya beh nafshy
39	jnan den la hwayn dkutaa dmawbla la'vdana ela dhaymanuza dmaknya lan nafshan
