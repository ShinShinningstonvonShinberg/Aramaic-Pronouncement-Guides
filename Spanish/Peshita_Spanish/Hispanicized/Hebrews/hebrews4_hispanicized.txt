1	nedjal hajil dalma kad kayyam mulkana dmaalta dlanyajteh neshtjaj nash menjon dfa'esh men dalmeal
2	af jnan guer estabarn ayj d'af henon ela la awtraz enon lhanon melza dashmaw metul dla mmasga waz bhaymanuza lhanon dshamuh
3	a'linan den lanyajta jnan aylen dhaymenn aykana den emar ayj dimiz brugsy dla nelon lanyajty dha vadawy d'alaha men shurayyeh dalma hwaw
4	ayj d'emar al shabza d'etnij alaha byawma shviayya men vadawy kulhon
5	wharka tuv emar dla nelon lanyajty
6	metul hajil d'iyz wa azra dnash nash neul leh whanon d'estabarw kadmayye la alw badla ettpisw
7	tuv yawma jrina sa'em men bazar savna saguiyya' aykana dmen lel kziv d'emar dawid dyawmana en bkaleh teshmon la tkashon lebawazjon
8	elu guer yeshu bar non anij wa enon la amar wa men bazarken al yawma jrina
9	maden kayyam u lmashbazu lameh d'alaha
10	ayna guer dal lanyajteh ettnij af hu men vadawy ayj d'alaha men dileh
11	nezjapat hajil dneul lha nyajta dla nepel badmuza dhanon dla ettpisw
12	jayya y guer melzeh d'alaha wjul sara wjarifa tav men safsira dazren pumeh wa'la dama lfurshana dnafsha wadruja wadsharyaza wadmuja wadgarme wdana majshvaza wzaryaza dleba
13	wlayt briza dtashya men kdameh ela kul medem artel wagle kdam aynawy dleh yahbinan pezgama
14	metul d'iyz lan hajil rab kumre raba yeshu mshija breh d'alaha daslek lashmayya njamsen bzawdizeh
15	la guer iz lan rabkumre dla mse dnejash am krihuzan ela damnasay bjulmedem ajwazan star men jtiza
16	nezkarav hajil bagle in ljursya dtaybuzeh dnesav rajme wneshkaj taybuza ludrana bsavna d'uwlsana
