1	risha den djulhen iz lan rab kumre ayna dizev men yamina djursya drabuza bashmayya
2	wahwa mshamshana dvez kudsha wadmashkna dashrara haw dakva alaha wla barnasha
3	kul rab kumre guer dka'em dankarev kurbane wdevje metul hana sadka waz af lhana dnehwe iz leh medem dankarev
4	we'lu b'ara wa afla kumra hawe wa metul d'iyzayhon waw kumre damkarvin waw kurbane ayj davnamusa
5	hanon damshamshin ladmuza waltelanyaza dhalen dvashmayya ayj d'eze'mar lmushe kad aved wa mashkna dajsi waved kulmedem badmuza ha d'ezjasyaz laj btura
6	hasha den teshmeshta damyatra men ha kabel yeshu mshija ajma damyatra af diyyaziki ha david bah mesayya wavshudayye damyatrin men dha ezyahbaz
7	elu guer izeh waz kadmata dla reshyan layt wa azra lhade dzarten
8	rashe lhon guer we'mar dha yawmaza azen amar marya we'gmur al bayta dvez israyyel wal bayta dvez ihuda diyyaziki jdaza
9	la ayj ha diyyaziki dyehbez la'vahayhon byawma d'ejdez b'iydhon wa'pkez enon men ara dmesren metul dhenon la kawiw bdiyyaziki dily af ena bsiz bhon emar marya
10	hade den diyyaziki d'etel lvayta dvez israyyel bazar yawmaza hanon amar marya etliwy lnamusy bmadayhon wal lebawazhon ejtviwy we'hwe lhon ena alaha whenon nehon li ama
11	wla nalef nash lvar mditeh afla la'ju wni'mar dda lmarya metul djulhon nedunany men surhon wadama lkashishhon
12	we'jase enon men awlhon wajtahayhon tuv la etdjar lhon
13	bha d'emar jdaza lkadmata atkah wa'yna dazek wase'v kariv u lajvala
