1	kul rab kumre guer dmen bnaynasha hawe jlaf bnaynasha ka'em al aylen dda'laha enen dankarev kurbane wdevje jlaf jtahe
2	ayna dmeshkaj dnamej nafsheh wnejash am aylen dla yadin wtaen metul d'af hu krihuza lvish
3	wmetulazah jayyav u d'aykana dajlaf ama hajana af jlaf nafsheh nkarev al jtahawy
4	wla wa lnafsheh nash nasev ikara ela ayna dmezkre men alaha aykana d'ahron
5	hajana af mshija la wa nafsheh shabaj dnehwe rab kumre ela haw d'emar leh dvery at ena yawmana iledtaj
6	ayj d'af bdukza jriza emar d'at u kumra lalam badmuzeh dmalkisdek
7	af kad besra lvish wa bauza wzajshefta bagaza jaylzaniza wavdeme karev wa lman dmeshkaj wa men mawta dnajewy we'shtma
8	wjad tav bra izawy men dejlza wjashe dasval yalpah lmeshtamanuza
9	whajana ezgmar wahwa ljulhon aylen dmeshtamin leh elza djayye dalalam
10	we'shtamah men alaha rab kumre badmuzeh dmalkisdek
11	lawy den al hana malkisdek saguiyya' y lan melza lmi'mrah waska lamfashakuzah metul dahwayton ljon krihe bmashmazjon
12	jayyavin hwayton guer malfane lmehwa metul dsavna ljon byulpana hasha den tuv snikin ton dzi'lpon aylen enen kzivaza kadmayyaza drish melawy d'alaha wahwayton ljon snike al jalva wla al me'julta sharirta
13	kulnash den dme'julteh jalva w la mapas bmelza dji'nuza metul dshavra w
14	dagmire y den me'julta sharirta aylen dmetul damdarshin eznapakw regshayhon lmefrash tavza wvishta
