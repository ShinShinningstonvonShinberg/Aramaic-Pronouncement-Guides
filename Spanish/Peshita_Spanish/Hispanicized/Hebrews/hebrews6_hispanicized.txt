1	metul hana neshbuk shurayya dmelzeh damshija wni'ze lagmiruza aw lma tuv sheze'sta jriza marmeton lazyavuza men vade mize walhaymanuza dva'laha
2	walyulpana dmamudiza wdasyam ida wlakyamta dmen bez mize waldina dalalam
3	en marya mapes nebed hade
4	ela la meshkjin hanon dajda svan lmamudiza njezw watemw mawhavta dmen shmayya wansavw ruja dkudsha
5	watemw melza tavza d'alaha wjayla dalma dazid
6	dzuv nejton dmen drish nezjadzon lazyavuza wmen drish neskfon lavreh d'alaha wansaron
7	ara guer d'eshtyaz metra d'eza lah savnin saguiyya'n wa'wyaz esba djashaj lhanon dmetulazhon mezpalja mkabla burkza men alaha
8	en hu den dzapek kurtbe wdardre hawya lah maslayta wla rajika men lawtza ela jarzah yakdana hu
9	mpisinan den laykon ajay aylen dshapiran wkarivan ljayye afen hajana mmallinan
10	la wa guer awal alaha dnete vadaykon wjubjon haw djawiton bashmeh dshameshton lkadishe wamshamshiton
11	savenan den dnash nash menjon hi hade jfituza njawe lshumlayya dsavrjon dama ljarza
12	wadla tezkata ljon ela dzehon mmaryane lhanon davhaymanuza wavnaguiruz ruja hwaw yarte dmulkana
13	la'vraham guer kad mlaj leh alaha metul dlayt wa leh drab meneh dni'me beh ima bnafsheh
14	we'mar damvaraju ebarjaj wmasgayyu asguej
15	whajana egar rujeh wkabel mulkana
16	bnaynasha guer badrab menhon yamen wal kul jeryan dhawe baynazhon shulama sharira bmawmaza hawe leh
17	metul hana yatira'iz sva alaha danjawe lyarte dmulkana dshudayyeh la meshtajlaf wjavsheh bmawmaza
18	davzarten sevwan dla meshtajlfan dla meshkaj alaha dandaguel bhen buyya'a raba nehwe lan d'ezgawasn beh wne'jud savra damlij lan
19	haw d'iyzawy lan ayj ewkina dalvij bnafshan dla tetsi wa'el lgaw men apay tara
20	kar dkadem al jlafayn yeshu wahwa kumra lalam badmuzeh dmalkisdek
