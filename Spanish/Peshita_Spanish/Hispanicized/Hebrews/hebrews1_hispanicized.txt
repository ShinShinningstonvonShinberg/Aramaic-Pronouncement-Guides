1	bjul mnawan wavjul demwan malel alaha am avahayn banviyye men kdim
2	wavhalen yawmaza jrayye malel aman bavreh dleh sam yarta djulmedem wveh vad lalme
3	dhuyyu semja dshuvjeh wsalma d'iyzuzeh wa'jid kul bjayla dmelzeh whu baknumeh vad dukayya dajtahayn wizev al yamina drabuza bamrawme
4	whana kuleh irev men mala'je ayj ma damyatar shma direz men dilhon
5	la'yna guer men mala'je memzum emar alaha dvery at ena yawmana iledtaj wzuv d'ena ehwe leh la'va whu nehwe li lavra
6	tuv den emazy dmael bujra lalma emar dleh nesgdon kulhon mala'je d'alaha
7	al mala'je den hajana emar davad mala'jawy ruj wamshamshanawy nur yakda
8	al bra den emar djursyaj dilaj alaha lalam almin shavta pshita shavta dmalkuzaj
9	rjemt ki'nuza wasnayt awla metul hana mashjaj alaha alahaj meshja djaduza yatir men javrayk
10	wzuv at men brishiz samt sheze'seh d'ara washmayya vad idayk enon
11	henon nebron wa't kayyam at wjulhon ayj najta nevlon
12	wa'yj tajsiza teuf enon henon nezjalfon wa't ayj d'iyzayk at washnayk la negmran
13	lman den men mala'je memzum emar dzev men yaminy dama d'esim beldvavayk kuvsha tjez reglayk
14	la ha kulhon izayhon ruje dzeshmeshta dmeshtadrin bzeshmeshta metul aylen dazidin lmi'raz jayye
