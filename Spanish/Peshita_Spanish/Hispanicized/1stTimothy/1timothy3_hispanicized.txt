1	mhaymna y melza d'en nash ra'eg kashishuza vade tave ra'eg
2	wale den dnehwe kashisha ayna dmuma la meshtjaj beh wahwa bala dajda atza ayna dir reyaneh wanjef wamtakas wrajem ajsnayye wmalfan
3	wla avar al jamra wla rahta ideh lmemja ela nehwe makij wla nasa wla rajem kespa
4	wamdabar bayteh shapir wa'jid bnawy bshubada bjulah dajyuza
5	en guer bayta dnafsheh la yada dandabar shapir aykana meshkaj iteh d'alaha dandabar
6	wla nehwe tle tulmadeh dla netrim wnepel bdineh dsatana
7	wale den d'af sahduza shapirta iz leh men barayye dla nepel bjesda wavfaje dsatana
8	wa'f mshamshane hajana nehon djen wla nmallon tarten wla nehon slen ljamra saguiyya' wla nerjmon yuzrane tanpe
9	ela ne'jdon rasa dhaymanuza bzi'rta djiza
10	whenon halen nezbkon lukdam whaden nshamshon kad izayhon dla reshyan
11	hajana af neshe nehwyan najpan wnehwe ir reyanhen wnehwyan mhaymnan bjul medem wla nehwyan ajlan karse
12	mshamshane nehon ayna dajda atza hwaz leh wdabar bnawy wvayteh shapir
13	aylen guer dshameshw shapir darga tava kanen lnafshhon wmagle ape saguiyya' bhaymanuza dyeshu mshija
14	halen kazev na laj kad msabar na dvagal aze na lwazaj
15	we'n hu den dmawjar na dzeda aykana wale lmezhapaju bvayteh d'alaha d'iyzeh ita d'alaha jayya amuda wsheze'sza dashrara
16	wsharira'iz rav hu rasa hana dji'nuza d'ezgli bavsar we'sdadak bruj we'zjsi lmala'je we'zkres bez amme we'zhayman balma we'stalak bshuvja
