1	aylen d'iyzayhon tjez nira davduza lmarayhon bjul ikar ne'jdon dla nehwe shmeh d'alaha wyulpaneh mezgadaf
2	aylen den d'iyz lhon marayya mhaymne la nevson bhon al d'ajayhon enon ela yatira'iz nshamshon enon al damhaymne enon wjabive halen dmettnijin bzeshmeshthon halen alef wavi menhon
3	en den iz nash dmalef yulpana jrina wla mezkarav lmele jlimaza dmaran yeshu mshija walyulpana ddejlaz alaha
4	hana metrim kad medem la yada ela krih badrasha wvavaza dmele dmenhen hawe jsama wjeryana wgudafa wamsam breyana bisha
5	washjaka davnaynasha aylen damjabal reyanhon waglisin men kushta wsavrin dzegurta y dejlaz alaha at den ezrak men halen
6	te'gurtan guer dilan raba y d'iyzeh dejlaz alaha bajshajza dmeszan
7	medem guer la aeln lalma widia d'afla dnapek meneh meshkjinan
8	metul hana safka lan me'julta wzajsiza
9	aylen den dsaven lmetar naflin bnesyune wavfaje wvarguigaza saguiyya'za dsajlan wamsagfan wamtaban lavnaynasha bajvala wva'vdana
10	ekara den djulhen bishaza izeh rejmaz kespa wi'yz nasha d'ezragragw leh wmen haymanuza taw wnafshhon aelw lda'wane saguiyye'
11	at den aw barnasha d'alaha men halen ruk what bazar sadikuza wvazar ki'nuza wvazar haymanuza wvazar juba wvazar msaybranuza wvazar makijuza
12	we'zkatash b'aguna tava dhaymanuza wa'drej jayye dalalam dalhon ezkrit wa'wdiz tawdiza tavza kdam sahde saguiyye'
13	msahed na laj kdam alaha haw dmaje kul wyeshu mshija haw d'ashed kdam pantiyyaws-pilataws sahduza shapirta
14	dzetriwy pukdana dla tulsha wadla muma dama lguelyaneh dmaran yeshu mshija
15	haw dazid bsavneh njawewy alaha mvarja wjaylzana baljudawy malka dmalke wmara dmarawaza
16	haw dhu baljudawy la mezjabal wamar bnuhra dnash la meshkaj dnezkarav leh wnash men bnaynasha la jsa wa'fla mse lmejsyeh haw dleh ikara wshultana lalam almin amin
17	latire dalma hana paked dla netrimon breyanayhon wla nettajlon al uzra dlayt lawy tujlana ela al alaha jayya haw dyav lan kul atira'iz lanyajan
18	wnebdon vade tave wnetron bsurane shapire wnehon dlilin lmetal walmeshtawtafu
19	wansimon lnafshhon sheze'sta tavza lmedem dazid dnadrjon jayye sharira
20	aw timaze' esdar bmedem d'ezgel laj waruk men bnaz kale srikaza wmen hfajaza didaza dagalza
21	aylen guer dmeshtawden lah taw lhon men haymanuza taybuza amaj amin
