1	pawlaws shlija dyeshu mshija bfukdana d'alaha majyanan wdamshija yeshu savran
2	ltimaze'aws bra sharira bhaymanuza taybuza wrajme washlama men alaha avon wamshija yeshu maran
3	biz wiz menaj kad asel na lmakedawniyya dazkawe b'efesaws wazfaked lnash nash dla nalfon yulpane mshajlfe
4	wla nezrmon lshuyaza walzashyaza dsharvaza dsaja layt lhon halen djeryane w avdan yatira'iz wla benyana bhaymanuza d'alaha
5	sajeh den dfukdana izawy juba dmen leba dajya wmen ti'rta tavza wmen haymanuza sharirta
6	wmenhen men halen taw nasha wastaw lmele srikaza
7	bdavaw lmehwa malfane dnamusa kad la mestaklin medem dammallin wla medem dalawy mezjren
8	yadinan den dnamusa shapir u en nash ajwazeh dnamusa netdabar beh
9	kad yada dalji'ne namusa la sim ela lawale walmarude walrashie waljatayye walanaze wla'ylen dla djen wla'ylen dmajen la'vahayhon wla'ylen dmajen le'mhazhon walkatule
10	walsanayye walshajbay am dejre walganbay bnay ji're waldagale walavray al mawmaza waljulmedem d'iyzawy sakuvla lyulpana jlima
11	d'ewangueliyyawn dzeshbujteh d'alaha mvarja haw d'ena ezhaymnez
12	wmawde na leh djaylany lmaran yeshu mshija djashbany mhaymna wsamany lzeshmeshteh
13	li dmen kdim mgadfan wiz wraduf wamsaran ela ezjannez metul djad la yada wiz evdez dla haymanuza
14	segyaz bi den taybuzeh dmaran whaymanuza wjuba davyeshu mshija
15	mhaymna y melza wshawya y lamkabaluzah dyeshu mshija eza lalma lmajayyu ljatayye dkadmahon ena na
16	ela metul hana rajem lay dvi kadma njawe yeshu mshija kulah magraz rujeh lzajwiza d'aylen dazidin danhaymnon beh ljayye dalalam
17	lmalka den dalme lhaw dla mezjabal wla mezjse dhuyyu jad alaha ikara wzeshbujta lalam almin amin
18	hana pukdana magel na laj bery timaze'aws ayj nviyyawaza kadmayyaza dahway layk dzefluj bhen paljuza hade shapirta
19	bhaymanuza wavzi'rta tavza aylen guer dalhade djakw menhon men haymanuza estarakw
20	ayj humene'aws wa'leksandraws halen d'ashlmez lsatana dnezrdon dla nehon mgadfin
