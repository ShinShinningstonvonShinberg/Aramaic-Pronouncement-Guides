2	walkashishaza ayj emhaza wla'ylen dtalyan ayj dla'jwazaj bjulah dajyuza
3	la'rmlaza yakar la'ylen d'armlaza enen bashrara
4	we'n iz armalta d'iyz lah bnayya aw bnay bnayya ni'lpon lukdam dvavnay baython nesdadkon wnefron juvle la'vahayhon hade guer mkabla kdam alaha
5	ayda den dsharira'iz armalta y wamshawjadta hade savrah al alaha w wa'mina baslawaza wavvawaza blilya wvi'ymama
6	ayda den dfalja estraniyya miza y kad jayya
7	halen hwayt mfaked lhen dadla reshyan nehwyan
8	en nash guer d'aylen ddileh enon wyatira'iz d'aylen d'iyzayhon bnay bayta dhaymanuza la yasef hana kfar leh bhaymanuza wvish u men aylen dla mhaymnin
9	hwayt hajil gave armalta ayda dla bsira men shtin shnin ayda daljad u gavra hwaz
10	wi'yz lah sahduza davade shapire en rabyaz bnayya en kablaz ajsnayye en ashigaz reglayhon dkadishe en arwjaz la'lise en haljaz bjul vad tav
11	men armlaza den aylen dtalyan eshtal mestaryan lhen guer al mshija wvayan lmehwa lgavre
12	wdinhen kayyam u datlam haymanuzhen kadmata
13	yalpan den af jvananuza kad mezkarkan bez bate wla baljud jvananuza ela af dnasgyan mamlla wanfarkan srikaza wanmallan medem dla wale
14	save na hajil d'aylen dtalyan nesdawgan wni'ldan bnayya wandabran batayhen wla netlan laveldvava afla jda elza dsujiza
15	al dhasha guer shariw nash nash lmesta bazar satana
16	en nash mhaymna aw mhaymanta iz lhon armlaza nzarson enen wla ni'kran al ita dla'ylen d'armlaza enen dashrara tespak
17	kashishe aylen dshapir metdabrin li'ykara fifa neshon yatira'iz aylen dla'en bmelza wavyulpana
18	emar guer kzava dla tevlum tawra badrajta wshawe u pala agreh
19	al kashisha ktigranuza la tkabel ela al pum tren wazlaza sahdin
20	la'ylen djaten kdam kulnash kawen d'af sharka dnasha nedjlon
21	msahed na laj kdam alaha wmaran yeshu mshija wmala'jawy gvayya dhalen tetar wla nezkadam reyanaj lmedem wla tesur medem bmasav b'ape
22	ida bagal al nash la tsim wla teshtawtaf bajtahe nujrayye nafshaj tar bdajyuza
23	wmekil mayya la teshte ela jamra kalil wayt shaze metul estumjaj wmetul kurhanayk amine
