1	marayya vedw shawyuza wji'nuza lwaz avdaykon wahwayton yadin d'af ljon iz u mara bashmayya
2	basluza eza'nw wahwayton irin ton bah wmawden
3	wamsalen af layn d'alaha neftaj lan tara dmelza lammalalu rasa damshija dmetulazeh asir na
4	d'aglyewy we'malliwy aykana dwale li
5	bjejmza halejw lwaz barayye wasvenw ki'rsjon
6	wmelazjon bjulsvan btaybuza ayj davmelja tehwe mmadja wahwayton yadin lnash nash aykana wale ljon lamzavu pezgama
7	medem dalwazy den nawdajon tujikaws aja jabiva wamshamshana mhaymna wajnazan bmarya
8	hana dshadrez lwazjon leh al hade dneda ma dalwazjon wanvayya lebawazjon
9	am anisimaws aja mhaymna wjabiva haw d'iyzawy menjon henon nawdunajon medem dalwazan
10	sha'el bashlamjon aristarjaws shavya damy wmarkaws bar dadeh dvarnava haw d'ezpakadton lawy d'en aze lwazjon tkabluna
11	wyeshu haw dmezkre yustaws halen d'iyzayhon men gsurta whenon baljud adruny bmalkuzeh d'alaha whenon hwaw li buyya'a
12	sha'el bashlamjon epafra haw dmenjon avda damshija kad bjulsvan amel jlafaykon basluza dazkumon gmire wamshamlayya bjuleh sevyaneh d'alaha
13	sahed na guer lawy datnana saguiyya' iz leh jlafaykon wajlaf hanon davladikiyya wadvi'yrapawlis
14	sha'el bashlamjon luka asya jabivan wdima
15	sha'lw bashlama d'aje davladikiyya wadnumfi wadita davvayteh
16	wma d'ezkaryaz egarta hade lwazjon vedw d'af bita dladikayye tezkre wha d'ezkazbaz men ladikiyya kra'uh aton
17	we'marw la'rjipaws d'esdar bzeshmeshta ha dkabelt bmaran dzehwe mshamle lah
18	shlama hana b'iyda dily dpawlaws hwayton ahdin la'suray taybuza amjon amin
