1	pawlaws shlija dyeshu mshija bsevyaneh d'alaha wtimaze'aws aja
2	la'ylen d'iyz bkulasaws aje kadishe wamhaymne byeshu mshija shlama amjon wtaybuza men alaha avon
3	mawdenan la'laha avu dmaran yeshu mshija bjulsvan wamsalenan laykon
4	ha men dashman haymanuzjon davyeshu mshija wjubjon dalwaz kulhon kadishe
5	metul savra haw dantir ljon bashmayya haw dmen kdim shmaton bmelza dkushta dasvarza
6	ha d'ezkarsaz ljon ayj d'af ljuleh alma wravya wyahba pi're aykana d'af bjon men yawma dashmaton we'shtawdaton taybuzeh d'alaha bkushta
7	ayj ma dilefton men epafra knazan jabiva dhu izawy jlafaykon mshamshana mhaymna damshija
8	whu awdan jubjon davruja
9	metulhana af jnan men yawma dashman la shalenan lamsalayyu laykon walmesha'l dzezmlon idaza dsevyaneh d'alaha bjul jejma wavjul sukal druj
10	dazhaljon ayj dsadek wzeshpron la'laha bjul vadin tavin wzetlon pi're wzerbon bidaza d'alaha
11	wavjul jil tezjaylon ayj rabuza dshuvjeh bjul msaybranu wavmagraz ruj wavjaduza
12	tawdon la'laha ava d'ashwayn lamnaza dyartuza dkadishe bnuhra
13	wfarkan men shultaneh djeshuja wa'ytyan lmalkuza davreh jabiva
14	haw dveh iz lan purkana wshuvkana dajtahe
15	haw dhuyyu dmuza d'alaha dla mezjse wvujra djulhen beryaza
16	wveh ezbri kulmedem dvashmayya wva'ra kul dmezjse wjul dla mezjse en mawtve we'n marawaza we'n arjaws we'n shultane kulmedem b'iydeh wveh ezbri
17	whuyyu dmen kdam kul wjulmedem beh ka'em
18	whuyyu risha dfagra dita d'iyzawy risha wvujra dmen bez mize dnehwe kadma bjul
19	dveh hu sva kuleh mulayya lmemar
20	wvi'ydeh lamraayyu leh kulmedem wshayyen badma daskifeh b'iydawy en dva'ra we'n dvashmayya
21	af ljon dmen kdim nujrayye wayton waveldvave breyanaykon metul vadaykon bishe shayyenjon hasha
22	bfagra dvesreh wavmawteh dankimjon kdamawy kadishin dla mum wadla reshyan
23	en tkaon bhaymanuzjon kad sharira sheze'szjon wla mettsiiton men savra d'ewangueliyyawn haw dashmaton d'ezkres bjulah briza dazjez shmayya haw d'ena pawlaws hwiz mshamshaneh
24	wjade na bjashe dal apaykon wammale na jasiruza d'uwlsanawy damshija bvesry jlaf pagreh d'iyzawy ita
25	ha d'ena hwiz mshamshanah ayj mdabranuza d'alaha d'ezyahbaz li bjon d'eshamle melzeh d'alaha
26	rasa haw damjasay wa men alme wmen dare hasha den ezgli lkadishawy
27	la'ylen dasva alaha dnawda manaw uzra dshuvjeh drasa hana bamme dhuyyu mshija davjon savra dshuvjan
28	haw dajnan majrsinan wmalfinan wamsaklinan ljul barnash bjul jejma dankim ljul barnash kad gmir byeshu mshija
29	bhade w guer af amel na wmezkatash na bamadranuza djayla dmezihev li
