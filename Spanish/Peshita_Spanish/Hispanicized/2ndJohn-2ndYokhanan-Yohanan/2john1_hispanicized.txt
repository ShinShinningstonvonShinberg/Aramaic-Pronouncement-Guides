1	kashisha lagviza kuriyya wlavneh aylen d'ena majev na bashrara la wa den ena baljuday ela kulhon aylen dyadu lashrara
2	metul shrara ayna damkawe ban waman izawy lalam
3	tehwe aman taybuza wrajme washlama men alaha ava wmen maran yeshu mshija breh d'ava bashrara wavjuba
4	jdiz sagui d'eshkjez men bnayky damhaljin bashrara aykana dfukdana nsavnan men ava
5	whasha mpis na lejy kuriyya la wa ayj pukdana jaza kazev na lejy ela haw d'iyz wa lan men shurayya dnajev jad ljad
6	whana izawy juba danhalej ayj pukdanawy hana pukdana izawy ayj ma dashmaton men shurayya dveh hwayton mhaljin
7	metul dsaguiyye' matyane nfakw beh balma aylen dla mawden dyeshu mshija eza bavsar hana izawy matyana wa'ntijristaws
8	esdarw bnafshjon dla tawbdon medem daflajton ela agra mshalmana tezparon
9	kul ayna davar wla mkawe byulpaneh damshija alaha layt beh haw damkawe byulpaneh hana wla'va wlavra iz leh
10	en nash aze lwazjon whana yulpana la mayte la tkabluna bvayta wajdi laj la ti'mron leh
11	haw guer d'amar leh jdi laj mshawtaf u lavadawy bishe
12	kad saguiyya'za iz wa li lmejtav ljon la biz davyad kartisa wadyuza ela msabar na d'ize lwazjon wfuma lwaz puma nmalel djaduza dilan tehwe mshamlya
13	sha'lin shlamejy bnayya djazejy gviza amin
