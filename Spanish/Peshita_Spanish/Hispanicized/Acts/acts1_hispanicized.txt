1	kzava kadmayya kezbez aw te'awfile al kulhen aylen dshari maran yeshu mshija lmebad walmalafu
2	dama lyawma haw dveh estalak men bazar dfaked wa enon lashlije aylen dagva bruja dkudsha
3	hanon d'af jawi lhon nafsheh kad jay men bazar djash b'azwaza saguiyya'za byawmin arbin kad mezjse wa lhon wa'mar al malkuza d'alaha
4	wjad ejal amhon lajma paked enon dmen urishlem la nefrkon ela dankaon lshudayyeh d'ava haw dashmaton meny
5	dyujanan amed bmayya wa'ton temdon bruja dkudsha la bazar yawmaza saguiyye'
6	henon den kad knishin sha'lu wa'mrin leh maran en bhana savna mafne at malkuza li'ysrayyel
7	amar lhon hu la waz diljon hade lmeda savna aw savne aylen d'ava sam enon bshultana dnafsheh
8	ela kad ti'ze ruja dkudsha laykon tkablon jayla wzehon li sahde b'uwrishlem wavjulah ihud wa'f bez shamrayye wadama lsawpeh d'ara
9	wjad halen emar kad jasen leh estalak wanana kabelzeh we'zkasi men aynayhon
10	wjad jarin waw bashmayya kad hu asel wa eshtjajw tren gavrin kamin lwazhon balvushe jewara
11	wa'mrin lhon gavre glilayye mana kamin ton wjarin bashmayya hana yeshu d'estalak menjon lashmayya hajana ni'ze ayj ma dajsaytuna daslek lashmayya
12	wmen bazarken hfajw lhon lu'wrishlem men tura dmezkre dvez sayte d'iyzawy al guev urishlem wfarik menah ayj shava estdawan
13	wmen bazar dalw slekw lhon leliza ha dhawen waw bah petraws wyujanan wyakuv wa'ndre'was wfilipaws wza'wma wmatay wvar-tulmay wyakuv bar jalfay wshemon tanana wihuda bar yakuv
14	halen kulhon ajjda aminin waw basluza bajda nfesh am neshe wam maryam emeh dyeshu wam ajawy
15	wavhon byawmaza hanon kam shemon ki'fa mesaz talmide iz wa den taman kensha dnasha ayj ma' wesrin we'mar
16	gavre ajayn sadek wa dnezmale kzava ayna dkadem emar ruja dkudsha bfumeh ddawid al ihuda haw dahwa mdabrana lhanon d'ejadw lyeshu
17	metul damne wa aman wi'yz wa leh pesa bzeshmeshta hade
18	hanaw dakna leh kriza men agra dajtiza wanfal al apawy al ara we'zprez men msazeh we'ze'shed kuleh gwayyeh
19	whi hade ezyadaz ljulhon damrin b'uwrishlem whajana ezkaryaz kriza ha bleshaneh d'azra jkal dma d'iyzawy turgamah kuryaz dem
20	kziv guer bsefra dmasmure ddayreh tehwe jarba wamur la nehwe bah wzeshmeshteh nesav jrin
21	walya y hajil ljad men halen gavre dahwaw aman bhana savna kuleh dveh al wanfak layn maran yeshu
22	d'akef men mamudizeh dyujanan dama lyawma d'estalak men lwazan dhu nehwe aman sahda dakyamteh
23	wa'kimw tren lyawsef dmezkre barshaba d'eshtami yustaws walmatiyya
24	wjad saliw emarw at marya yada davlebawaza djul jawa jad ayna dgave at men halen trayhon
25	dhu nkabel pesa dzeshmeshta washlijuza dmenah prak ihuda dni'sal leh la'zreh
26	wa'rmiw pese wselkaz lmatiyya we'zmni am jdasar shlije
