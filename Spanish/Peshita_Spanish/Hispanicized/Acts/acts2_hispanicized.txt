1	wjad ezmaliw yawmaza dpentikawsti kad knishin waw kulhon ajjda
2	hwa men shelya men shmayya kala ayj ruja asista we'zmli wa meneh kuleh bayta haw dveh yazbin waw
3	we'zjsiw lhon leshane dmezpalguin waw ayj nura wizevw al jad jad menhon
4	we'zmliw kulhon bruja dkudsha wa'kefw waw lammalalu bleshan leshan ayj ma druja yahev wa lhon lammalalu
5	iz waw den gavre damrin b'uwrishlem ddajlin men alaha ihudayye men kul amme dazjez shmayya
6	wjad hwa kala haw knash kuleh ama we'shtguesh metul dshama wa nash nash menhon dammallin waw bleshanayhon
7	tahirin hwaw den kulhon wmetdamrin kad amrin waw jad ljad halen kulhon dammallin la ha glilayye enon
8	aykana jnan shamin nan nash nash leshaneh dveh ilidin nan
9	parzwayye wmadayye wa'lanayye wa'ylen damrin bez-nahrin ihudayye wkapudkayye wadmen azra dpantaws wda'siyya
10	wadmen azra dafruguiyya wadpamfuliyya wadmesren wda'zrawaza dluvi dkarivin lkurini wa'ylen d'ezaw men rumi ihudayye wguiyyure
11	wadmen kriti warvayye ha shamin nan menhon dammallin bleshanayn dilan tedmrazeh d'alaha
12	metdamrin waw den kulhon wzawirin kad amrin jad ljad dmana y hade svuza
13	jrane den mmaykin waw bhon kad amrin halen me'riza eshtiw warwiw
14	bazarken kam shemon ki'fa am jdasar shlijin wa'rim kaleh we'mar lhon gavre ihudayye wjulhon damrin b'uwrishlem hade tezida ljon wsuzw melay
15	la guer aykana d'aton savrin halen rwen dha dama lhasha tlaz enen shain
16	ela hade i d'amira byu'yel nviyya
17	nehwe byawmaza jrayye amar alaha eshud rujy al kul bsar wneznabon bnaykon wavnazjon wgadudaykon jeswane nejson wkashishaykon jelme nejlmon
18	wal avday wal amhazy eshud rujy byawmaza hanon wneznabon
19	we'tel azwaza bashmayya wagvarwaza al ara dma wnura wetra daznana
20	shemsha nezjalaf bamtana wsahra badma adla ni'ze yawmeh dmarya raba wadjila
21	wnehwe kul dnekre shmeh dmarya nije
22	gavre bnay israyyel shmaw mele halen yeshu nasrayya gavra dmen alaha ezjsi lwazjon bjayle wva'zwaza wvagvarwaza aylen d'alaha vad baynazjon b'iydeh ayj d'aton yadin ton
23	lhana dafrish wa lah lhade bamkadmuz idazeh wavsevyaneh d'alaha ashlemtuna b'iyday rashie waskafton waktalton
24	alaha den akimeh washra jevleh dashyul metul dla meshkja waz dnettjed bah bashyul
25	dawid guer emar lawy mkadem wiz jase lmary bjulsvan dal yaminy u dla esu
26	metul hana ezbasam leby wrewsaz teshbujty wa'f pagry naguen al savra
27	metul dla shavek at lnafshy bashyul wla yahev at ljasyaj dnejse jvala
28	glayt li urja djayye temleny basimuza am parsupaj
29	gavre ajayn mapas lmi'mar in bagle lwazjon al rish avahaza dawid dmiz wa'f ezkvar wvez kvureh izawy lwazan dama lyawmana
30	nviyya hwa guer wyada wa dmawmaza ima leh alaha dmen pi're djarsaj awtev al kursyaj
31	wkadem jsa wmalel al kyamteh damshija dla eshtvek bashyul afla pagreh jsa jvala
32	lhana yeshu akim alaha wajnan kulan sahdawy
33	whuyyu davyamineh d'alaha ettrim wansav men ava shudayya dal ruja dkudsha we'shad mawhavta hade dha jasen ton wshamin ton
34	la wa guer dawid slek lashmayya metul dhu emar d'emar marya lmary tev laj men yaminy
35	dama d'esim beldvavayk kuvsha lreglayk
36	sharira'iz hajil neda kuleh bez israyyel dmarya wamshija avdeh alaha lhana yeshu d'aton skafton
37	wjad shmaw halen ezgnajw blebhon we'marw lshemon walsharka dashlije mana nebed ajayn
38	amar lhon shemon tuvw wamadw nash nash menjon bashmeh dmarya yeshu lshuvkan jtahe dazkablon mawhavta druja dkudsha
39	ljon guer hwa shudayya wlavnaykon waljulhon aylen drajikin aylen dhu alaha nekre enon
40	wavmele jranyaza saguiyya'za msahed wa lhon wvae wa menhon kad amar jyaw men sharbza hade makamta
41	wnashin menhon zida'iz kabelw melzeh whaymenw wamadw we'ttawsafw bhaw yawma ayj tlaza alfin nafshan
42	wa'minin waw byulpana dashlije wmeshtawtfin waw basluza wvaksayya d'ewjaristiyya
43	whawya waz dejlza ljul nfesh wa'zwaza saguiyya'za wagvarwaza hawyan way byad shlije b'uwrishlem
44	wjulhon aylen dhaymenw waw ajjda hwaw wjulmedem d'iyz wa lhon dgawa hwa
45	wa'ylen d'iyz wa lhon kenyana msabnin waw leh wamfalguin waw lnash nash ayj medem dasnik wa
46	wjulyum aminin waw bhaykla bajda nfesh wavvayta kasen waw prista wamkablin waw saybarta kad rawsin wvavriruza dlebhon
47	mshabjin waw la'laha kad ihivin brajme kdam kuleh ama wmaran mawsef wa kulyum la'ylen dja'en bita
