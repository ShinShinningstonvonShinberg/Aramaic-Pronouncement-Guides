1	iz wa den bita d'antiyyajiyya nviyye wmalfane barnava wshemon dmezkre niguer wlukiyyas dmen kurini mdita wmana'yel bar mrabyanawy dherawdes tetrarja wsha'wal
2	wjad henon samin waw wmezkashfin la'laha emraz lhon ruja dkudsha prushw li lsha'wal walvarnava lavada ayna d'ena kriz enon
3	wvazar dsamw wsaliw samw layhon ida wshadarw enon
4	whenon kad eshtlajw men ruja dkudsha njezw lhon lselawkiyya wmen taman rdaw byama dama lkupraws
5	wjad alw lsalamini mdita msabrin waw melzeh dmaran bajnushaza dihudayye wyujanan mshamesh wa lhon
6	wjad ezkarkuh ljulah gasarta dama lpafaws mdita eshkajw gavra jad jarasha ihudayya d'iyzawy wa nviyya dagala dashmeh hwa barshuma
7	hana dabik wa lgavra jakima d'iyzawy wa anzupataws wmezkre wa serguiyyaws-pawlaws wakra wa anzupataws lsha'wal walvarnava wvae wa dneshma menhon melza d'alaha
8	ka'em wa den lukvalhon hu hana jarasha barshuma dmettargam shmeh elumas metul dsave wa danakmiwy la'nzupataws men haymanuza
9	sha'wal den haw d'ezkri pawlaws ezmli wa bruja dkudsha wjar beh
10	we'mar aw damle kul nejlin wjulhen bishaza breh d'ajel karsa waveldvava djulah ki'nuza la shale at lamakamu urjazeh trisaza dmarya
11	whasha ideh dmarya layk wzehwe sme wla tejse shemsha dama lsavna wvah bshaza nfal lawy amtana wjeshuja wmezkrej wa wvae dmanu ne'jud b'iydeh
12	wjad jsa anzupataws medem dahwa etdamar whaymen byulpaneh dmarya
13	pawlaws den wvarnava rdaw byama men pafaws mdita we'zaw lhon lpergui mdita dpamfuliyya wafrash menhon yujanan we'sal leh lu'wrishlem
14	henon den nfakw men pergui we'zaw la'ntiyyajiyya mdita dpisidiyya walw lajnushta wizevw byawma wshabza
15	wvazar d'ezkri namusa wanviyye shlajw lhon kashishe dajnushta we'marw gavre ajayn en hu d'iyz ljon melza dvuyya'a malelw am ama
16	wkam pawlaws wa'nif ideh we'mar gavre bnay israyyel wa'ylen ddajlin men alaha shmaw
17	alaheh dama hana gva la'vahazan wa'rim wa'wrev enon kad hwaw tawtave b'ara dmesren wvadraa rama apek enon menah
18	wzarsi enon bmadbra arbin shnin
19	wahgam shava ammin b'ara dajnan wyav lhon arhon lyurtana
20	wa'rba ma' wjamshin shnin yav lhon dayyane dama lashmu'yel nviyya
21	whaden she'lw lhon malka wyav lhon alaha lsha'wal bar kish gavra men sharbza dvenyamin shnin arbin
22	wnasbeh wa'kim lhon ldawid malka wa'shed lawy we'mar d'eshkjez ldawid breh d'iyshay gavra ayj leby hu nebed kulhon sevyanay
23	men sareh dhana akim alaha li'ysrayyel ayj ma d'eshtawdi lyeshu paruka
24	wshadar lyujanan dnajres kdam me'zizeh mamudiza dazyavuza ljuleh ama d'iysrayyel
25	wjad mshalem wa yujanan teshmeshteh amar wa manu savrin ton d'iyzay la wiz ena ela ha aze bazary haw dla shawe na d'eshre arke damsanawy
26	gavre ajayn bnay sharbzeh d'avraham wa'ylen ddajlin amjon la'laha ljon u eshtadraz melza djayye
27	halen guer amure d'uwrishlem wrishanayhon la argueshw bah afla bajzave danviyye dmezkren bjul shaba ela danu wshalemw halen dajzivan
28	wjad la eshkajw elza dmawta medem she'lw men pilataws dnektluna
29	wjad shalemw kulmedem dajziv wa lawy ajzu men sliva wsamu bez kvura
30	alaha den akimeh men bez mize
31	we'zjsi yawmaza saguiyye' la'ylen daslekw ameh men glila lu'wrishlem whenon enon hasha sahdawy lwaz ama
32	wa'f jnan ha msabrinan ljon dshudayya haw dahwa lwaz avahazan
33	ha shamlyeh alaha lan lavnayhon d'akim lyeshu ayj dajziv bmasmura dazren dvery at ena yawmana iledtaj
34	whajana akimeh alaha men bez mize dzuv la nehpuj nejse jvala ayj d'emar d'etel ljon taybuzeh ddawid mhaymanta
35	wzuv emar bdukza jriza dla yavt ljasyaj dnejse jvala
36	dawid guer bsharbzeh shamesh sevyaneh d'alaha washjev we'ttawsaf al avahawy wajsa jvala
37	hana den d'akim alaha la jsa jvala
38	daw hajil ajay dveh bhana mezkres ljon shuvkan jtahe
39	wmen kul dla eshkajton bnamusa dmushe dzesdadkon bhana kul damhaymen mesdadak
40	esdarw hajil dalma ni'ze laykon medem dajziv banviyye
41	dajsaw mvasrane wzezmhon wzezjablon davada aved na byawmaykon ayna dla thaymnon en nash meshtae ljon
42	wjad nafkin waw men se'dayhon baw menhon dalshabza jriza nmallon amhon mele halen
43	wmen d'eshtaryaz knushta saguiyye' ihudayye esalw bazarhon wa'f guiyyure ddajlin waw men alaha whenon mmallin waw wampisin lhon dnehon nakifin ltaybuzeh d'alaha
44	walshabza jriza kenshaz kulah mdita lmeshma melzeh d'alaha
45	wjad jsaw ihudayye kensha saguiyya' ezmliw jsama wkamin waw lukval mele d'amar wa pawlaws wamgadfin waw
46	emar den pawlaws wvarnava in bagle ljon u wale wa kadmayyaz dzeze'mar melzeh d'alaha ela metul ddajen ton lah menjon wafsakton al nafshjon dla shawen ton ljayye dalalam ha mezpnenan lan lwaz amme
47	hajana guer pakdan maran ayj dajziv dsamtaj nuhra lamme dzehwe ljayye dama lsawpeh d'ara
48	wjad shamin waw amme jaden waw wamshabjin la'laha whaymenw aylen dsimin waw ljayye dalalam
49	wmelzeh dmarya mezmalla waz bjuleh haw azra
50	ihudayye den garegw lrishe damdita walneshe atiraza aylen ddajlan way amhon la'laha wa'kimw rdufya al pawlaws wal barnava wa'pekw enon men tjumayhon
51	wjad nfakw nfasw layhon jela dreglayhon we'zaw lhon li'ykaniwan mdita
52	wzalmide mezmlen waw jaduza wruja dkudsha
