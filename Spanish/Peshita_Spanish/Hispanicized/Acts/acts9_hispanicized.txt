1	sha'wal den dakil mle wa lujama wjemza dketla al talmidawy dmaran
2	wash'el leh egraza men rab kahne dnetel leh ldarmsuk lajnushaza d'en hu dneshkaj draden bhade urja gavre aw neshe ne'sur nayte enon lu'wrishlem
3	wjad asel wa wshari mmate ldarmsuk men tjez shelya asleg wa lawy nuhra men shmayya
4	wanfal al ara washma kala d'amar leh sha'wal sha'wal mana radef at li kshe w laj lamvaatu lukse
5	na hu we'mar man at mary wmaran emar ena na yeshu nasrayya haw d'at radef at
6	ela kum ul lamdita wzaman nezmalal amaj al ma dwale laj lmebad
7	wgavre d'asin waw ameh b'uwrja kamin waw kad tamihin metul dkala baljud shamin waw nash den la mezjse wa lhon
8	wkam sha'wal men ara wla mezjse wa leh medem kad aynawy pzijan way wjad ajidin b'iydawy alu ldarmsuk
9	wla mezjse wa leh tlaza yawmin wla ejal wla eshti
10	iz wa den bah bdarmsuk talmida jad dashmeh hwa jananya wmarya emar leh bjeswa jananya we'mar ha ena mary
11	wmaran emar leh kum sel lshuka dmezkre trisa wavi bvayta dihuda lsha'wal d'iyzawy men tarsaws mdita ha guer kad hu msale
12	jsa bjeswa lgavra dashmeh jananya dal wsam lawy ida ayj dnezpatjan aynawy
13	we'mar jananya mary shemez men saguiyye' al gavra hana dajma bishaza asbel lkadishayk b'uwrishlem
14	wha af harka iz leh shultana men rabay kahne dne'sur ljulhon aylen dkaren shmaj
15	we'mar leh marya kum sel metul dma'na hu li gavya dneshkul shemy bamme wavmalke wvez bnay israyyel
16	ena guer ejawewy kma zid lmejash metul shemy
17	haden jananya esal lvayta lwazeh wsam lawy ida we'mar leh sha'wal ajy maran yeshu shadrany haw d'ezjsi laj b'uwrja kad aze at ayj dnezpatjan aynayk wzezmle ruja dkudsha
18	wvar shazeh nfal men aynawy medem ddame laklafe we'zpataj aynawy wkam mad
19	wkabel saybarta we'zjayyal wahwa yawmaza lwaz talmide hanon d'iyz waw bdarmsuk
20	wvar shazeh majres wa bajnushaza dihudayye al yeshu dhuyyu breh d'alaha
21	wzamihin waw kulhon aylen dshamin waw leh wa'mrin waw la wa hana haw dradef wa ljulhon aylen dkaren waw lashma hana b'uwrishlem af lharka leh al hade mshadar wa ayj dne'sur nawbel enon lrabay kahne
22	sha'wal den yatir mezjayyal wa wamsi wa lhon lihudayye hanon damrin waw bdarmsuk kad mjawe wa dhanaw mshija
23	wjad sguiw leh taman yawmaza vadw lawy nejla ihudayye dnektluna
24	ezbadak leh den lsha'wal aparsna dvaen waw lmebad leh wnatrin waw tare damdita imama wlilya dnektluna
25	haden samu talmide b'esprida wshabu men shura blilya
26	we'sal leh lu'wrishlem wsave wa lmeznakafu lzalmide wjulhon dajlin waw meneh wla mhaymnin waw dzalmida hu
27	barnava den ajdeh wa'ytyeh lwaz shlije we'shtai lhon aykana b'uwrja jsa lmarya wda'ykana malel ameh wa'ykana bdarmsuk in bagle malel bashmeh dyeshu
28	wa'el wa amhon wnafek b'uwrishlem
29	wammalel wa bashmeh dyeshu in bagle wdaresh wa am ihudayye aylen dyadin waw yawna'iz henon den saven waw lmektleh
30	wjad idaw aje aytyu blilya lkesariyya wmen taman shadru ltarsaws
31	bram den ita davjulah ihud wvaglila wavshamrin iz wa bah shlama kad mezbanya waz wradya bdejlaz alaha wavvuyya'a druja dkudsha sagya waz
32	wahwa djad mezkrej wa shemon bamdinaza njez af lwaz kadishe damrin blud mdita
33	we'shkaj gavra jad dashmeh aniyyas darme wa barsa wamsharay shnin tmane
34	we'mar leh shemon aniyya ma'se laj yeshu mshija kum wshawa arsaj wvar shazeh kam
35	wajsa'u kulhon damrin blud wavsarawna we'zpniw lwaz alaha
36	iz wa den talmidta jda byawpi mdita dashmah hwa tviza hade atira waz bavade tave wavsedkaza davda waz
37	ezkarhaz den bhon bhanon yawmaza wmizaz wa'sjyuh wsamuh beliza
38	washmaw waw talmide dshemon blud u mdita ha d'iyzeh al guev yawpi wshadarw waw lwazeh gavre tren dnevon meneh dla tema'n leh dni'ze se'dayhon
39	wkam shemon esal amhon wjad eza asku leliza wajnash kam lhen jdarawy kulhen armlaza kad bajyan wamjawyan leh kutinyaza wmartute halen dyahba waz lhen tviza kad jayya
40	shemon den apek ljulhon nasha lvar waked al burkawy wsali we'zpni lwaz shlada we'mar tviza kumy hi den pezjaz ayneh wjad jsazeh lshemon yezbaz
41	wa'wshet lah ideh wa'kimah wakra lkadishe wla'rmlaza wyahbah lhon kad jayya
42	we'zyadaz hade bjulah mdita wsaguiyye' haymenw bmaran
43	hwa den bah byawpi yawmaza la surin kad shre wa bez shemon bursayya
