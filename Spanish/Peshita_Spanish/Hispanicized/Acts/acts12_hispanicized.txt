1	bhaw den savna armi wa idayya al nashin davita ayj dnave'sh lhon herawdes malka haw dmezkane wa agripaws
2	waktal bsaypa lyakuv aju dyujanan
3	wjad jsa dshefraz hade lihudayye awsef wa lme'jad af lshemon ki'fa wi'yzayhon waw yawmaza dfatire
4	wa'jdeh wa'rmyeh bez asire wa'shlem leh shzzasar stratawtin dnetruneh dmen bazar pesja nashlmiwy lama dihudayye
5	wjad hu shemon mezntar wa bez asire sluza aminta mezkarva waz men ita jlafawy la'laha
6	wveh bhaw lilya dalsafra zid wa dnashlmiwy kad dmej wa shemon bez tren estratiyyawtin wa'sir wa bzarten shishlan wajrane natrin waw tare dvez asire
7	mala'ja dmarya kam lel meneh wnuhra asleg bjuleh bayta wdakreh bgabeh wa'kimeh we'mar leh kum gal wanfal shishlaza men idawy
8	we'mar leh mala'ja asur jasayk wsa'n telarayk wavad hajana wzuv emar leh ezaf tajsizaj wza bazary
9	wanfak wa'sel wa bazreh kad la yada wa dsharira waz ha dhawya waz byad mala'ja savar wa guer djeswa jase wa
10	wjad varw matarza kadmata wadzarten ezaw dama lzara dfarsla we'zpzaj lhon men svuz nafsheh wjad nfakw wavarw shuka jad prak men lwazeh mala'ja
11	haden eshtawda shemon we'mar hasha yedez bkushta dmarya shadar mala'jeh wfaltany men ideh dherawdes malka wmen medem dmezjashvin waw lay ihudayye
12	wjad estakal eza leh lvayta dmaryam emeh dyujanan haw d'ezkani markaws metul d'aje saguiyye' taman knishin waw wamsalen
13	wankash bzara ddarta wnefkaz dzenewy tliza dashmah rawdi
14	we'shtawdaz kaleh dshemon wavjaduzah la pezjaz leh tara ela hefkaz brehta wa'mra lhon shemon ha ka'em al tara ddarta
15	amrin lah msa saty lejy whi mezjarya waz dhade hajana y amrin lah kvar mala'jeh u
16	wshemon nakesh wa bzara wanfakw jsa'u wazmahw lhon
17	wamnif wa lhon ideh ayj dneshtkon lhon wal we'shtai lhon aykana marya apkeh men bez asire we'mar lhon eshtaaw halen lyakuv wla'jayn wanfak esal leh la'zar jrin
18	wjad hwa safra hwa wa rawba saguiyya' bez estratiyyawte al shemon dmana hwa leh
19	herawdes den kad ba wla eshkjeh dan enon lnature wafkad danmuzon wanfak leh men ihud wi'yzawy wa bkesariyya
20	wmetul darguis wa al surayye wal saydanayye ezkanashw we'zaw lwazeh ajjda wa'pisw lavlastaws kaytunkaneh dmalka wshe'lw meneh dnehwe lhon shayna metul dfurnasa d'azrhon men malkuzeh wa dherawdes
21	byawma den idia lvesh wa herawdes lvusha dmalkuza wizev al bim wammalel wa am kensha
22	ama den kuleh kae wa wa'mar halen bnaz kale d'alaha enen wla way davnaynasha
23	wajlaf dla yav teshbujta la'laha bah bshaza mja mala'jeh dmarya warfaz bzawle wmiz
24	wasvarza d'alaha mezkarsa waz wravya
25	barnava den wsha'wal pnaw men urishlem la'ntiyyawji men bazar dshalemw teshmeshthon wadvarw amhon lyujanan haw d'ezkani markaws
