1	wahwa djad salkin shemon ki'fa wyujanan ajjda lhaykla bedana dasluza dazsha shain
2	wha gavra jad jguira dmen kres emeh shkilin waw nasha aylen damadin waw mayten wsamin leh bzara dhaykla dmezkre shapira dnehwe sha'el sedkza men hanon da'lin lhaykla
3	hana kad jsa lshemon walyujanan da'lin lhaykla bae wa menhon dnetlon leh sedkza
4	wjarw beh shemon wyujanan we'marw leh jur ban
5	hu den jar bhon kad savar wa lmesav menhon medem
6	amar leh shemon dahva wsi'ma layt li ela medem d'iyz li yahev na laj bashmeh dyeshu mshija nasrayya kum halej
7	wa'jdeh b'iydeh dyamina wa'kimeh wvah bshaza shar reglawy wekvawy
8	washwar kam whalej wal amhon lhaykla kad mhalej wamshawar wamshabaj la'laha
9	wajsa'u kuleh ama kad mhalej wamshabaj la'laha
10	we'shtawdaw dhuyyu haw jadura dyazev wa kulyum wsha'el sedkza al tara dmezkre shapira we'zmliw temha wdumara al medem dahwa
11	wjad ajid hwa lshemon walyujanan rhet kuleh ama kad tahir lwazhon le'stwa dmezkre dashlemon
12	wjad jsa shemon na we'mar lhon gavre bnay israyyel mana metdamrin ton bhana aw ban mana jarin ton ayj haw davjayla dilan aw bshultanan vadn hade danhalej hana
13	alaheh u d'avraham wdi'ysjak wadyakuv alaha d'avahazan shabaj lavreh yeshu haw d'aton ashlemton wajfarton beh kdam apawy dfilataws kad hu sadek wa dneshrewy
14	aton den bkadisha wsadika kfarton wshe'lton ljon lgavra katula dnezihev ljon
15	walhaw risha djayye ktalton dleh akim alaha men bez mize wajnan kulan sahdawy
16	wavhaymanuza dashmeh lhana djasen ton wyadin ton hu ashar wa'si whaymanuza dveh yehbaz leh hade jlimuza kdam kuljon
17	bram hasha ajay yada na davtuyay vadton hade ayj davadw rishaykon
18	wa'laha ayj medem dkadem ajres bfum kulhon nviyye dnejash mshijeh mali hajana
19	tuvw hajil we'zpnaw aykana dnezton jtahaykon wni'zon ljon savne danyajta men kdam parsupeh dmarya
20	wanshadar ljon la'yna damtayyav wa ljon lyeshu mshija
21	dleh wale lashmayya dankablon dama lmulayya dsavne djulhen aylen dmalel alaha bfuma danviyyawy kadishe dmen alam
22	mushe guer emar danviyya nkim ljon marya men ajaykon ajwazy leh shmaw bjul ma danmalel amjon
23	wzehwe kul nafsha ayda dla teshma lanviyya haw ti'vad nafsha ha men amah
24	wanviyye kulhon men shmu'yel wa'ylen dmen bazreh hwaw malelw wa'jresw al yawmaza hanon
25	aton enon bnayhon danviyye waddiyyaziki ayda dsam alaha la'vahazan kad emar la'vraham davsaraj nezbarjan kulhen sharbaza d'ara
26	ljon men kdim akim wshadar alaha lavreh kad mvarej ljon en tezpnon watzuvon men bishazjon
