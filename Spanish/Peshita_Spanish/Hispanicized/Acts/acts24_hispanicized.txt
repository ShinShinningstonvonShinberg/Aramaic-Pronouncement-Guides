1	wmen bazar yawmaza jamsha njez jananya rab kahne am kashishe wam tertulaws rhitra wa'wdaw lhigmawna al pawlaws
2	wjad ezkri akef wa tertulaws mkatreg leh wa'mar bsuga' dshayna amrinan metulazaj wzaknaza saguiyya'za way lama hana bashkal tanaj
3	wjulan bjul duk mkablinan taybuzaj nasija piliks
4	dla den nale'yj bsaguiyya'za bae na menaj dzeshma lmakijuzan bfasikaza
5	eshkajn guer lgavra hana d'iyzawy mshajtana wamir shgushya ljulhon ihudayye davjulah ara risha w guer dyulpana dnasrayye
6	walhayklan sva lamsayyavu wjad ejadna bayn danduniwy ayj davnamusan
7	eza den lusiyyaws kiliyyarja wvaktira saguiyya' men idayn adyeh wlaj shadreh
8	wafkad laktirganawy dni'zon lwazaj wmeshkaj at kad msha'el at leh dzi'laf meneh al kulhen halen damkatrguinan leh
9	arivw den lawy af henon ihudayye kad amrin dhalen hajana enen
10	warmas higmuna lpawlaws danmalel wana pawlaws we'mar men shnayya saguiyya'za yada na d'iyzayk dayyana dama hana wmetul hana jadya'iz nafek na ruja al apay nafshy
11	kad meshkaj at lmeda dlayt li yatir men tresar yawmin dselkez lu'wrishlem lmesgad
12	wla eshkjuny dammalel na am nash bhaykla wa'fla kensha djanesh na la bajnushthon wla bamdita
13	wla danjaon matya b'iydayhon kdamayk al medem dhasha mkatrguin li
14	bram den hade mawde na dveh bhana yulpana d'amrin beh palaj na la'laha d'avahay kad mhaymen na ljulhen dajzivan bnamusa wvanviyye
15	wjad iz li savra al alaha ayna d'af henon halen msabrin dazida dzehwe kyamta dmen bez mize dji'ne wadawale
16	metul hana af amel na dzi'rta djiza tehwe li kdam alaha wakdam bnay nasha amina'iz
17	lashnin den saguiyya'n eziz lwaz bnay ama dily d'etel sedkza we'karev kurbana
18	we'shkjuny halen bhaykla kad mdakay na la am kensha afla bashgushya
19	ela en dashgashw nashin ihudayye d'ezaw men asiyya aylen dwale wa dankumon amy kdamayk wankatrgon medem d'iyz lhon
20	aw henon halen ni'mron mana eshkajw bi sajluza kad kamez kdam kenshhon
21	ela en hade jda melza dakiz kad ka'em na baynazhon dal kyamta dmize metdin na yawmana kdamaykon
22	piliks den metul dyada wa lah lu'wrja hade malya'iz tahi enon kad amar dma d'eza kiliyyarja shama na baynazjon
23	wafkad lkentruna dnetriwy lpawlaws banyaja wadla nash men yaduawy nezkle dnehwe mshamesh leh
24	wmen bazar kalil yawmaza shadar piliks wdursila atzeh d'iyzeh waz ihudata wakraw leh lpawlaws washmaw meneh al haymanuza damshija
25	wjad mmalel amhon al sadikuza wal kadishuza wal dina dazid ezmli dejlza piliks we'mar dhasha sel we'mazy dnehwe li azra eshadar bazraj
26	savar wa guer dshujda mezihev wa leh men pawlaws metul hade af amina'iz mshadar wa mayte leh wammalel ameh
27	wjad mali leh tarten shnin jrina higmuna eza wa ldukzeh dmezkre wa parkiyyaws-fistaws piliks den ayj dnebed taybuza bihudayye shavkeh lpawlaws kad asir
