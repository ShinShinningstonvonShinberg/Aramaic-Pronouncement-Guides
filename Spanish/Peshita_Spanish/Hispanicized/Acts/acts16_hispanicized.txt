1	wmati wa lderbi mdita wallustra iz wa den taman talmida jad dashmeh timaze'aws brah dihudata jda mhaymanta wa'vu armayya wa
2	wjulhon talmide dmen lustra wmen ikaniwan mashdin waw lawy
3	lhana sva wa pawlaws dnedbriwy ameh wansav gasreh metul ihudayye d'iyz waw b'azra yadin waw guer kulhon la'vu d'armayya wa
4	wjad asin waw bamdinaza majrsin waw wmalfin lhon dnehon natrin pukdane aylen dajzavw shlije wkashishe dvu'wrishlem
5	bram idaza mezkayman way bhaymanuza wsagyan way bmenyana kulyum
6	halejw den bafruguiyya wavgalatiyya azrawaza wajlaz enon ruja dkudsha dla nmallon melza d'alaha b'asiyya
7	wjad ezaw lmusiyya azra saven waw dni'sun men taman lvizuniyya wla apsaz lhon rujeh dyeshu
8	wjad nfakw men musiyya njezw lhon latrawa's azra
9	wavjeswa dlilya ezjsi lpawlaws ayj gavra jad makedawniyya dka'em wvae meneh kad amar dza lmakedawniyya wadarayny
10	kad den jsa pawlaws hana jeswa mejda svayn lmepak lmakedawniyya metul d'estakaln dmaran kran dansabar enon
11	wardayn men trawa's wazrasn lsamazraki wmen taman lyawma jrina ezayn lne'apawlis mdita
12	wmen taman lfilipaws dhi i risha dmakedawniyya wi'yzeh kawlawniyya hwayn den bah bhade mdita yawmaza idie
13	wanfakn byawma dshabza lvar men tara damdita al yad nahra metul dzaman mezjse wa bez sluza wjad izevn mmallin wayn am neshe dajnishan way taman
14	wa'tza jda msabnaz argwana ddajla waz men alaha shmah hwa ludiyya men te'watira mdita dhade pzaj lebah maran wshama waz medem d'amar pawlaws
15	wemdaz waz hi wavnay baytah wvaya waz menan wa'mra d'en hu dsharira'iz tjilin ton dhaymnez bmaran taw shraw ljon bvayty wsagui elaszan
16	wahwa djad asinan lvez sluza pegaz ban laymza jda d'iyz wa lah ruja dkesme wavda waz lmareh te'gurta sagui'za bkesma dkasma waz
17	wa'zya waz bazar pawlaws wvazrn wkaya waz wa'mra halen nasha avdawy enon d'alaha mrayma wamsabrin ljon urja djayye
18	whajana avda waz yawmaza saguiyye' we'ttfir pawlaws we'mar lah lruja ha paked na lejy bashmeh dyeshu mshija dzepkin menah wvah bshaza nefkaz
19	wjad jsaw mareh danfak leh menah savra dze'gurthon ajdu lpawlaws walshila wangadw aytiw enon lshuka
20	wkarevw enon le'stratigue walrishe damdita wa'mrin waw dhalen nasha mdaljin lah lamditan metul dihudayye enon
21	wmajrsin lan yade aylen dla mapas lan lamkabalu walmebad metul drumayye nan
22	wjensha saguiyya' ezkanashw layhon haden estratigue sadekw najtayhon wafkadw dannagdon enon
23	wjad naguedw enon sagui armiw enon bez asire wfakedw lnatar bez asire dnetar enon shira'iz
24	hu den kad kabel hana pukdana ael jvash enon bvayta gawayya dvez asire we'sar reglayhon bsada
25	wavfelgueh dlilya pawlaws wshila msalen waw wamshabjin waw la'laha wshamin waw lhon asire
26	wmen shelya sawa raba hwa we'ttsi sheze'se dvez asire we'zpzajw mejda tare kulhon wa'surayhon djulhon eshtriw
27	wjad ettir natar bez asire wajsa dafzijin tare dvez asire nsav safsira wava dnektul nafsheh metul dsavar wa darakw lhon asire
28	wakra pawlaws bkala rama we'mar leh la tebed lnafshaj medem dvish metul djulan harka jnan
29	wa'nhar leh shraga washwar wal kad ra'ez wanfal al reglayhon dpawlaws wadshila
30	wa'pek enon lvar we'mar wa lhon maray mana wale li lmebad ayj d'ije
31	whenon amrin waw leh haymen bmaran yeshu mshija wzije at wvaytaj
32	wmalelw ameh melza dmarya wam kulhon bnay bayteh
33	wvah bshaza beh blilya dvar asji enon men negdhon wvar shazeh mad hu wavnay bayteh kulhon
34	wadvar asek enon lvayteh wsam lhon pazura wrawes wa hu wavnay bayteh bhaymanuza d'alaha
35	wjad hwa safra shadarw estratigue lshaklay shavte dni'mron lrab asire shri lhalen nasha
36	wjad shma rab bez asire al emar leh hi hade melza lpawlaws dshadarw estratigue ayj dzeshtron whasha pukw selw bashlama
37	amar leh pawlaws dla sajlu nagdon lin alma kuleh lnasha rumayye wa'rmyon bez asire whasha matshya'iz mapkin lan la guer ela henon ni'zon napkunan
38	we'salw shaklay shavte we'marw le'stratigue mele halen d'eze'mar lhon wjad shmaw drumayye enon djelw
39	we'zaw lwazhon wavaw menhon dnepkon wanshanon lhon men mdita
40	wjad nfakw men bez asire alw lwaz ludiyya wajsaw taman la'je wvayya'w enon wanfakw
