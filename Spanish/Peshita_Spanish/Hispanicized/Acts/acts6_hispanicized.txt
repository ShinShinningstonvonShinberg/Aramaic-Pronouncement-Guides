1	wavhon byawmaza hanon kad sguiw talmide ratenw waw yawnayye talmide al evrayye dmezbasyan way armlazhon bzeshmeshta djulyum
2	wakraw tresar shlije ljuleh kensha dzalmide we'marw lhon la shapir dneshbuk melza d'alaha wanshamesh pazure
3	bsaw hajil ajay wagvaw shava gavrin menjon d'iyz layhon sahduza wamlen rujeh dmarya wjejmza wankim enon al hade svuza
4	wajnan nehwe aminin basluza wavzeshmeshta dmelza
5	wshefraz hade melza kdam kuleh ama wagvaw le'stefanaws gavra damle wa haymanuza wruja dkudsha walfilipaws wlaprajaraws walnikanawr waltimawn walparmena walnikala'was guiyyura antiyyawjayya
6	halen kamw kdamayhon dashlije wjad saliw samw layhon ida
7	wmelzeh d'alaha ravya waz wsague wa menyana dzalmide b'uwrishlem tav wama saguiyya' men ihudayye meshtma wa lhaymanuza
8	estefanaws den mle wa taybuza wjayla waved wa azwaza wzedmraza bama
9	wkamw waw nasha men knushta dmezkarya dlivertinu wkurinayye wa'leksandrayye wadmen kilikiyya wmen asiyya wdarshin waw am estefanaws
10	wla meshkjin waw lamkam lukval jejmza wruja dammalla waz beh
11	haden shadarw lgavre wa'lefw enon dni'mron dajnan shmana d'emar mele dgudafa al mushe wal alaha
12	washgashw lama walkashishe walsafre we'zaw wkamw lawy wajtafw aytyu lmesaz kensha
13	wa'kimw sahde dagale d'amrin hana gavra la shale lammalalu mele lukval namusa wal azra hana kadisha
14	jnan guer shmana d'emar dyeshu hana nasrayya hu neshrewy la'zra hana wanjalef yade d'ashlem ljon mushe
15	wjarw beh kulhon hanon dyazbin waw bajnushta wajsaw parsupeh ayj parsupa dmala'ja
