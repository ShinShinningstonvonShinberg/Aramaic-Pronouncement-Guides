1	we'shtamaz waz lashlije wla'je dvihud d'af amme kabelw melza d'alaha
2	wjad slek shemon lu'wrishlem danin waw ameh hanon dmen gsurta
3	kad amrin dalwaz nasha urle al wales amhon
4	wa'kef wa shemon bazar bazar lmi'mar lhon
5	djad msale wiz byawpi jsiz bjeswa danjez wa ma'na jad ayna ddame wa ljetana wa'sir wa b'arba karnazeh wsha'ev wa men shmayya we'za dama lwazy
6	wjarez beh wjase wiz d'iyz beh jaywaza d'arbaz reglayhen wrajsha d'ara wa'f parajza dashmayya
7	wshemez wiz kala d'amar wa li shemon kum kus wa'jul
8	we'mrez jas mary dmemzum la al lfumy datma wdamsayyav
9	wzuv kala emar li men shmayya dmedem d'alaha daki at la tsayyev
10	hade hwaz tlaz savnin we'stalak leh kulmedem lashmayya
11	wvah bshaza tlaza gavrin d'eshtadarw lwazy men kawrniliyyaws men kesariyya ezaw wkamw al tara ddarta dashre wiz bah
12	we'mar li ruja dsel amhon dla pulaga we'zaw amy af halen shta ajin waln lvayteh dgavra
13	we'shtai lan aykana jsa bvayteh mala'ja dkam we'mar leh dshadar lyawpi mdita wa'yta lshemon dmezkre ki'fa
14	whu nmalel amaj mele davhen tije at wjuleh baytaj
15	wjad akfez wiz taman lammalalu agnaz ruja dkudsha layhon ayj ma dalayn men kdim
16	we'tdajrez melzeh dmaran d'emar wa dyujanan amed bmayya aton den temdon bruja dkudsha
17	en hajil alaha shawya'iz yahbah mawhavta lamme aylen dhaymenw bmaran yeshu mshija ayj d'af lan ena man wiz d'espak wiz d'ejle la'laha
18	wjad halen mele shmaw shliw lhon wshabajw la'laha wa'mrin waw dajvar af lamme alaha yav tyabuza ljayye
19	hanon den d'ezbadarw waw men ulsana dahwa wa al estefanaws matiw waw dama lfuniki wa'f la'zra dkupraws wla'ntiyyajiyya kad am nash la mmallin waw melza ela baljud am ihudayye
20	iz waw den nasha menhon men kupraws wmen kurini halen alw waw la'ntiyyawji wammallin waw am yawnayye wamsabrin waw al maran yeshu
21	wi'yz waz amhon ideh dmarya wsaguiyye' haymenw we'zpniw lwaz marya
22	we'shtamaz waz hi hade le'dnayhon davnay ita dvu'wrishlem wshadarw lvarnava la'ntiyyawji
23	wjad eza lzaman wajsa taybuzeh d'alaha jdi wvae wa menhon davjuleh lebhon nehon nakifin lmaran
24	metul dgavra wa tava wamshamlay wa bruja dkudsha wavhaymanuza we'ttawsaf wa ama saguiyya' lmaran
25	whu nfak wa ltarsaws lmeva lsha'wal
26	wjad eshkjeh aytyeh ameh la'ntiyyajiyya wshata kulah ajjda knishin waw bita wa'lefw ama saguiyya' men haden kadmayyaz ezkriw b'antiyyawji talmide kristyane
27	wavyawmaza hanon ezaw men urishlem lzaman nviyye
28	wkam jad menhon dashmeh hwa agavaws wa'wda enon bruj djafna raba hawe bjulah ara wahwa kafna hana byawmay klawdiyyaws kesar
29	bram den talmide ayj ma d'iyz wa lnash nash menhon prashw danshadron lzeshmeshta d'aje aylen damrin bihud
30	wshadarw byad barnava wsha'wal lkashishe dzaman
