1	wafkad lawy pistas dneshtadar lwaz kesar li'ytaliyya wa'shlmeh lpawlaws wla'sire jrane ameh lgavra jad kentruna men espir sevasti dashmeh hwa yuliyyaws
2	wjad hwaz dnerde njezn le'lfa d'iyzeh waz men adrmnutaws mdita wa'sa waz la'zra d'asiyya wal wa aman le'lfa aristarjaws makedawniyya dmen zesalawniki mdita
3	walyawma jrina matin lsaydan we'zjashaj kentruna bamrajmanuza lwaz pawlaws wa'pes leh dni'sal lwaz rajmawy wnettnij
4	wmen taman rdayn wmetul druje sakuvlan way ezkrejn al kupraws
5	wavarn yama dkilikiyya wadpamfuliyya wmatin lmura mdita dlukiyya
6	we'shkaj taman kentruna elfa men aleksandriyya d'asa waz li'ytaliyya wa'wtvan bah
7	wmetul dyakira'iz radya waz lyawmaza saguiyye' lmajsen matin lukval knidaws gasarta wmetul dla shavka waz ruja dni'sal trisa'iz ezkrejn al kriti lukval salmawni mdita
8	walmajsen kad radenan jdareh matin ldukza dmezkarya lmi'ne shapire wkariva waz lah mdita dashmah la'se'a
9	wahwayn taman savna saguiyya' dama davar af yawma dsawma dihudayye wahwa leh kenta dnerde nash byama wmalej wa lhon pawlaws
10	wa'mar gavre jase na dvu'wlsana wavjusrana saguiyya' hawya mardizan law baljud dmawblah d'elfan ela af dnafshazan dilan
11	kentruna den lkuverniti walmarah d'elfa shama wa yatir men melawy dpawlaws
12	wmetul dla ahen wa haw lmi'na lmastayyu beh sazwa saguiyye' menan saven waw dnerde men taman we'nhu dmeshkjin danmanon wnaston balmi'na jad d'iyzawy wa bakriti wmezkre wa puniks wja'ar wa lzaymna
13	wjad neshbaz ruja dzaymna wasvarw dammaten ayj sevyanhon raden waw jdaray kriti
14	wmen bazar kalil nfak layn mashva dalala dmezkre tufanikaws ewraklidawn
15	we'zjatpaz elfa wla eshkjaz dazkum lukval ruja wyavn la'yda dhi
16	wjad varn gasarta jda dmezkarya kuda lmajsen eshkajn ejadn lkarkura
17	wjad shkalnah mjaysin wayn wmazknin lah le'lfa wmetul ddajlin wayn ddalma nepel bmajatteh dyama ajezna la'rmenawn whajwaz raden wayn
18	wjad kam leh layn kimuna kashya lyawma jrina shdayn ma'nin byama
19	walyawma dazlaza ma'ne dilah d'elfa b'iydayn shdayn
20	wjad ejad leh sazwa yawmaza yatire wla shemsha mezjse wa wla sahra wla kawkve savra djayyayn kul kuleh ezpsek wa leh
21	wjad nash medem la mestaybar wa haden kam pawlaws baynazhon we'mar elu ettpiston li gavre la raden wayn men kriti wmezjaskin wayn men jusrana wmen ulsana hana
22	whasha malej na dzehon dla aka nafsha guer menjon jda la avda ela en elfa
23	ezjsi li guer blilya hana mala'jeh d'alaha haw ddileh na wleh palaj na
24	we'mar li la tedjal pawle zid u laj lamkam kdam kesar wha yav laj alaha mawhavta ljul draden amaj
25	metul hana ezlabavw gavre mhaymen na guer b'alaha dhajana hawe ayj ma d'ezmalal amy
26	bram lgasarta jda iz lan dneshtde
27	wmen bazar arbzasar yawmin datayn we'ttarafn bhadriyyaws yama bfelgueh dlilya svarw malaje dla'ra mezkarvin waw
28	wa'rmiw ewkinas we'shkajw kawmin esrin wzuv kalil rdaw we'shkajw kawmin jamshaesre
29	wjad dajilin wayn dalma neshtjaj lan bdukyaza d'iyz bhen shue armiw men jarzah d'elfa ewkinas arba wamsalen waw dnehwe yawma
30	malaje den baw lmerak menah men elfa wa'jezw menah lkarkura lyama belza dni'sun bah wne'srunah le'lfa b'ara
31	wjad jsa pawlaws emar lkentruna wle'stratiyyawte d'en halen b'elfa la mjatrin aton la meshkjin ton dzijon
32	haden psakw estratiyyawte ljavleh dkarkura men elfa wshavkuh taya
33	hu den pawlaws dama dahwa safra mpis wa ljulhon dankablon saybarta kad amar lhon yawmana ha arbzasar yawmin men kenta medem la tim ljon
34	metul hade bae na menjon dazkablon me'julta lkuyyama djayyaykon menza guer men risha djad menjon la avda
35	wjad halen emar nsav lajma wshabaj la'laha kdam kulhon waksa wa'kef lme'jal
36	we'zbayya'w kulhon wkabelw tursayya
37	izayn wayn den b'elfa ma'zen wshavin wshez nafshan
38	wjad svaw me'julta akelw men elfa washkalw jete washdaw byama
39	wjad hwa yawma sapane ayda y ara la eshtawdaw ela jarw al guev yavsha kenfa jda dyama ayka dranen waw d'en meshkja nedjunah le'lfa
40	wafsakw ewkinas men elfa wa'rpiw enen byama washraw rajbe dsawkane wazlaw armenawn sura lruja dnashba wraden waw la'pay yavsha
41	wgueshaz elfa bdukza drama baynaz tren umkin dyama we'zjaryaz bah wkam leh gabah kadmayya wla mettsi wa gabah den jrayya eshtri men ktira dgalle
42	wasvaw waw estratiyyawte dankatlon enon la'sire dla narmon sajwa wnerkon lhon menhon
43	wkentruna kla enon men hade metul dsave wa dnaje lpawlaws wla'ylen dmeshkjin waw lmarmayyu sajwa pkad lhon davkadmayye nesjon wnebron la'ra
44	walsharka al dape wal kayse jrane d'elfa abarw enon whajana kulhon eshtawsavw la'ra
