1	we'mar agripaws lpawlaws mapas laj lammalalu al apay nafshaj haden pawlaws pshat ideh wnafek wa ruja wa'mar
2	al kul ma dmezrshe na men ihudayye malka agripe savar na al nafshy dtuvana na dakdamayk yawmana nafek na ruja
3	yatira'iz dyada na dampas at bjulhon sitime wnamuse dihudayye metul hana bae na menaj davmagraz ruja teshmany
4	yadin enon guer af henon ihudayye en saven dneshdon dubaray dmen talyuzy dahwaw li men shurayya bamy wvu'wrishlem
5	metul dmen nugra mpasin bi wyadin davyulpana rishayya dafrishe jiz
6	whasha al savra dshudayya dahwa wa la'vahazan men alaha ka'em na wmetdin na
7	dal hana savra tartaesre sharvan msabran danmanan baslawaza jfitaza d'iymama wadlilya walawy al hana savra mezrshe na men iday ihudayye malka agripe
8	mana danin ton la wale danhaymen damkim alaha mize
9	ena guer men kdim samez breyany dsakuvle saguiyye' esur lukval shmeh dyeshu nasrayya
10	hade devdez af b'uwrishlem wkadishe saguiyye' armiz bez asire bshultana dkablez men rabay kahne wjad mezkatlin waw menhon eshtawtfez la'ylen djayyevw enon
11	wavjul knusha meshtanad wiz bhon kad ales wiz dnehon mgadfin bashmeh dyeshu wavrugsa saguiyya' damle wiz layhon af lamdinaza jranyaza nafek wiz lmerdaf enon
12	wjad asel wiz metul hade ldarmsuk bshultana wavmapsanuza drabay kahne
13	bfelgueh dyawma b'uwrja jsiz men shmayya aw malka d'asleg lay wal kul damy waw nuhra damyatar men dshemsha
14	wanfaln kulan al ara wshemez kala kad amar li evra'iz sha'wal sha'wal mana radef at li kshe u laj lamvaatu lukse
15	we'na emrez man at mary wmaran emar li ena hu yeshu nasrayya d'at radef at
16	we'mar li kum al reglayk metul hade guer ezjsiz laj d'akimaj mshamshana wsahda dmedem dajsaytany wdazid at dzejseny
17	we'pasej men ama dihudayye wmen amme jrane dalwazhon mshadar na laj
18	dazfataj aynayhon ayj dnehpjon men jeshuja lnahira wmen shultaneh dsatana lwaz alaha wankablon shuvkan jtahe wfesa am kadishe bhaymanuza dvi
19	metul hade malka agripe la kamez bjeryana lukval jeswa shmayyana
20	ela ajrsez men lkudmin lhanon davdarmsuk walhanon dvu'wrishlem wdavjulhen kurya dihud wa'f lamme ajrsez danzuvon wnezpnon lwaz alaha wnebdon vade dshawen lazyavuza
21	wal apay halen ajduny ihudayye bhaykla wsaven waw lmektlany
22	adrany den alaha dama lyawma hana wha ka'em na wamsahed na lasura walraba kad medem lvar men mushe wanviyye la amar na ela aylen dhenon emarw dazidan dnehwyan
23	dnejash mshija wnehwe rishiza dakyamta dmen bez mize wdazid dnajres nuhra lama walamme
24	wjad hajana nafek wa pawlaws ruja ka pistaws bkala rama shnayt laj pawle sefra saguiyya' avdaj dzeshne
25	amar pawlaws la shniz nasija pistaws ela melay shrara wzaknuza mmalel na
26	wa'f malka agripaws yatira'iz yada layhen dhalen wmetul hana in bagle mmalel na kdamawy metul dajda men halen mele la savar na dtayan leh dla wa btushya siran
27	mhaymen at malka agripe lanviyye yada na damhaymen at
28	amar leh agripaws bkalil medem mpis at li d'ehwe kristyana
29	wpawlaws emar bae wiz men alaha bkalil wavsagui la wa laj baljud ela af ljulhon aylen dshamin li yawmana dnehon ajwazy lvar men asure halen
30	wkam leh malka whigmawna wverniki wa'ylen dyazbin waw amhon
31	wjad prakw men taman mmallin waw jad am jad wa'mrin dmedem dshawe lmawta aw la'sure la vad gavra hana
32	we'mar agripaws lfistaws meshkaj wa gavra hana dneshtre elu bguen kesar la kra
