1	wafrashn menhon wardayn trisa'iz lkaw gasarta walyawma jrina ezayn lrawdaws wmen taman lpa'tara
2	we'shkajn taman elfa d'asa lfuniki waslekn lah wardayn
3	wmatin dama lwaz kupraws gasarta washvaknah lsemala we'zayn lsuriyya wmen taman matin lsur taman guer iz wa lah le'lfa lamnaju tanah
4	wjad eshkajn taman talmide kawin lwazhon yawmaza shava whalen amrin waw kulyum lpawlaws bruj dla ni'sal lu'wrishlem
5	wmen bazar halen yawmaza nfakn dni'sal b'uwrja wamlawen waw lan kulhon henon wneshayhon wavnayhon dama lvar men mdita wakedw al burkayhon al yad yama wsaliw
6	wnashekn lajdade waslekn le'lfa wahfajw henon lvatayhon
7	jnan den rdayn men sur we'zayn laku mdita wyavn shlama la'je dzaman washrayn se'dayhon yawma jad
8	walyawma jrina nfakn we'zayn lkesariyya waln shrayn bvayteh dfilipaws msabrana ayna d'iyzawy wa men shava
9	wi'yz way leh bnaza bzulaza arba dmeznabyan way
10	wjad izayn taman yawmaza saguiyye' njez wa men ihud nviyya jad dashmeh hwa agavaws
11	wal lwazan washkal erakza djasawy dpawlaws we'sar regle dnafsheh wi'ydawy we'mar hajana amar ruja dkudsha dgavra marah derakza hade hajana ne'sruneh ihudayye b'uwrishlem wnashlmuneh b'iyday amme
12	wjad halen mele shman bayn meneh jnan wavnay azra dla ni'sal lu'wrishlem
13	haden na we'mar pawlaws mana avdin ton dvajen ton wshajkin ton leh lleby ena guer la wa d'eze'sar baljud mtayyav na ela af d'emuz b'uwrishlem jlaf shmeh dmaran yeshu
14	wjad la ettpis lan bheln lan we'marn dsevyaneh dmaran nehwe
15	wvazar yawmaza halen ettayyavn waslekn lan lu'wrishlem
16	we'zaw waw aman nasha talmide men kesariyya kad dvirin amhon aja jad men talmide kadmayye dashmeh hwa mnasawn wi'yzawy wa men kupraws dankablan bvayteh
17	wjad ezayn lu'wrishlem kablon aje jadya'iz
18	walyawma jrina aln am pawlaws lwaz yakuv kad iz wa lwazeh kulhon kashishe
19	wyavn lhon shlama wmeshtae wa lhon pawlaws bazar bazar kul ma davad alaha bamme bzeshmeshteh
20	wjad shmaw shabajw la'laha we'marw leh jase at ajon kma rebwan iz bihud aylen dhaymenw wjulhon halen tanane enon dnamusa
21	eze'mar lhon den layk dmalef at dnefrkon men mushe kulhon ihudayye davamme kad amar at dla nehon gasrin bnayhon wla bayade dnamusa nehon mhaljin
22	metul hajil dmeshtama y lhon d'ezayt lja
23	ved medem d'amrinan laj iz lan gavre arba dandir lhon dnetdakon
24	dvar enon wsel etdaka amhon wa'pek layhon nafkaza ayj dnegron rishayhon wmezyada ljulnash dmedem d'eze'mar layk dagal u wa't lnamusa shalem at wnatar
25	al aylen den dhaymenw men amme jnan kzavn dnehon natrin nafshhon men dadvija wmen sanyuza wmen jnika wmen dma
26	haden pawlaws dvar enon lgavre halen lyawma jrina we'tdaki amhon wal esal lhaykla kad mawda lhon mulayya dyawmaza dzadkiza dama d'ezkarav kurbana dnash nash menhon
27	wjad mati yawma dshava ihudayye dmen asiyya jsa'u bhaykla wgariw lawy ama kuleh wa'rmiw lawy idayya
28	kad mvagnin wa'mrin gavre bnay israyyel adarw hana w gavra dlukval ama dilan malef bjul duka wlukval namusa wlukval azra hana wa'f la'rmayye ael lhaykla wsayveh la'zra hana kadisha
29	kademw waw guer jsaw ameh latrawfimas efesayya bamdita wsavrin waw dam pawlaws al lhaykla
30	we'shtagshaz kulah mdita we'zkanashw kuleh ama wa'jdu lpawlaws wgaru lvar men haykla wvar shazeh ettjedw tare
31	wjad bae wa kensha lmektleh eshtamaz lkiliyyarja d'espir djulah mdita ettsiaz lah
32	wvar shazeh dvar lkentruna wle'stratiyyawte saguiyye' warhetw layhon wjad jsaw lkiliyyarja wle'stratiyyawte bhelw men dmajen waw leh lpawlaws
33	wakrev lwazeh kiliyyarja wa'jdeh wafkad dne'sruneh bzarten shishlan wamsha'el wa lawy dmanu wmana vad
34	wkaen waw lawy nasha men ajlaws medem medem wmetul kazhon la meshkaj wa lmeda ayda y sharirta wafkad wa dnawbluneh lmashriza
35	wjad mati pawlaws ldargue tanu estratiyyawte metul ktira dama
36	aze wa guer bazreh ama saguiyya' wkaen waw wa'mrin shkula
37	wjad mati lmeal lmashriza hu pawlaws emar lkiliyyarja en mapes at li emalel amaj hu den emar leh yawna'iz yada at
38	la wayt at haw mesrayya dakdam yawmaza halen asit wa'pekt lmadbra arba alfin gavre avday bishaza
39	amar leh pawlaws ena gavra na ihudayya men tarsaws dkilikiyya mdita idita dvah ilid na bae na menaj apes li lammalalu lama
40	wjad apes leh kam pawlaws al dargue wa'si wa lhon ideh wjad bhelw malel amhon evra'iz we'mar lhon
