1	wavarw al amfipawlis wa'palawniyya mdinaza we'zaw lzesalawniki ayka d'iyz wa knushta dihudayye
2	wal pawlaws aykana damad wa lwazhon wshabe tlaz malel amhon men kzave
3	kad mfashek wa wamjawe dazid wa mshija dnejash wdankum men bez mize whuyyu yeshu mshija hana damsabar na ljon
4	wnashin menhon haymenw wankefw lpawlaws walshila wsaguiyye' men yawnayye aylen ddajlin waw men alaha wa'f neshe idiaza la suryan
5	wajsamw waw ihudayye wa'kefw lhon nasha bishe men shuke damdita wavadw ajlaws saguiyya' wdalajw waw lamdita we'zaw wkamw lhon al bayteh d'iyasawn wvaen waw dnapkon enon men taman wnashlmon enon la'jlaws
6	wjad la eshkajw enon taman garu waw li'yasawn wla'je d'iyz waw taman wa'ytiw enon lwaz rishe damdita kad kaen waw dhalen enon daljulah ara dalajw wha tuv lharka ezaw
7	wamkablanhon hanaw iyyasawn wjulhon halen lukval pukdanawy dkesar kamin kad amrin d'iyz malka jrina yeshu
8	etdalajw den rishe damdita wjuleh ama kad shmaw halen
9	wansavw arave men iyyasawn wa'f men aje whaden shraw enon
10	aje den bar shazeh beh blilya shraw lpawlaws walshila lverawa' mdita wjad ezaw lzaman a'lin waw lajnushta dihudayye
11	ji'rain waw guer hanon ihudayye dzaman men ihudayye hanon d'iyz waw bzesalawniki wshamin waw menhon melza kulyum jadya'iz kad mfarshin waw men kzave d'en halen hajana izayhen
12	wsaguiyye' menhon haymenw whajana af men yawnayye gavre saguiyye' wneshe idiaza
13	wjad ydaw hanon ihudayye dmen zesalawniki dmelzeh d'alaha ezkarsaz men pawlaws bverawa' mdita ezaw af lzaman wla shliw lamsau wlamdalaju lnashuza
14	walpawlaws shra'u aje dnejuz leh lyama wkawi wa bah bamdita ha shila wtimaze'aws
15	whanon d'ezlawiw leh lpawlaws ezaw ameh dama la'zinaws mdita wjad nafkin men se'dawy kabelw meneh egarta lwaz shila wtimaze'aws dvagal ni'sun lwazeh
16	hu den pawlaws kad mkawe wa b'azinews mezmarmar wa brujeh kad jase wa damdita kulah malya pzajre
17	wammalel wa bajnushta am ihudayye wam aylen ddajlin men alaha wavshuka am aylen dmestakblin waw kulyum
18	wa'f pilasawfe dmen yulpaneh d'epikawras wajrane dmezkren staw'ikaw darshin waw ameh wnash nash menhon amrin waw mana save hana mlaket mele wajrane amrin waw d'alahe nujrayye majres metul dalyeshu wlakyamteh majres wa lhon
19	wa'jdu wa'ytyu lvez dina dmezkre ariyyaws-pagaws kad amrin leh meshkjinan lmeda manaw hana yulpana jaza dmajres at
20	mele guer nujrayyaza sara at bmashmazan wsavenan lmeda mana enen halen
21	azinayye den kulhon wa'ylen d'azen lzaman nujrayye al medem jrin la btil lhon ela lmi'mar walmeshma medem jdez
22	wjad kam pawlaws b'ariyyaws-pagaws emar gavre azinayye jase na ljon davjulhen yatirin ton bdejlaz shi'de
23	kad guer mezkrej wiz wjase wiz bez dejlazjon eshkjez laza jda dajziv wa leh d'alaha gnisa haw hajil djad la yadin ton dajlin ton leh leh lhana ena msabar na ljon
24	alaha guer davad alma wjul ma d'iyz beh whuyyu mara dashmayya wda'ra bhaykle davad idayya la share
25	wla meshtamash men iday bnaynasha wal medem la snik metul dhu yav ljulnash jayye wnafsha
26	wmen jad dem vad alma kuleh davnaynasha dnehon amrin al apay ara kulah wfaresh savne bfukdaneh wsam tjume dumra davnaynasha
27	dnehon baen la'laha wamakvin wmen beryazeh meshkjin leh metul d'af la wa rajik men kul menan
28	beh u guer jainan wmettsiinan wi'yzayn ayj d'af nasha men jakime dalwazjon emarw dmeneh u tuhman
29	nasha hajil dtuhman men alaha w la jayyavinan lmesbar daldahva aw lsi'ma aw lji'fa daglifa b'uwmanuza wvidaza dvarnasha damya alahuza
30	savne guer dtayuza abar alaha wavsavna hana mfaked ljulhon bnaynasha djul nash bjul duka nzuv
31	metul d'akim yawma dveh zid dandon ara kulah bji'nuza byad gavra ayna dafrash wa'fni ljul nash lhaymanuzeh bda'kimeh men bez mize
32	wjad shmaw kyamta dmen bez mize menhon mmaykin waw wmenhon amrin waw basvan jrin shaminan laj al hade
33	whajana nfak pawlaws men baynazhon
34	wnashin menhon nakpu whaymenw jad den menhon izawy wa diyyanusiyyaws men dayyane d'ariyyaws-pagaws wa'tza jda dashmah hwa damaris wajrane amhon
