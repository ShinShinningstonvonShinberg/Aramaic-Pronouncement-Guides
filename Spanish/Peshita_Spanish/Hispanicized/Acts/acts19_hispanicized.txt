1	wjad izawy wa apalaw bkawrinzaws ezkrej pawlaws b'azrawaza elayye we'za le'fesaws wamsha'el wa lzalmide aylen d'eshkaj taman
2	d'en kabelton ruja dkudsha men dhaymenton naw wa'mrin leh wa'fla en iz ruja dkudsha shmi lan
3	amar lhon wavmana madton amrin bmamudizeh dyujanan
4	amar lhon pawlaws yujanan amed mamudiza dazyavuza lama kad amar wa danhaymnon b'ayna d'aze bazreh d'iyzawy yeshu mshija
5	wjad halen shmaw madw bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
6	wsam layhon ida pawlaws we'za ruja dkudsha layhon wammallin waw bleshan leshan wmeznaben waw
7	hawen waw den kulhon nasha tresar
8	wal wa pawlaws lajnushta wammalel wa in bagle yarje tlaza wampis wa al malkuzeh d'alaha
9	wnashin menhon mezkashen waw wmezjren wamsajen lu'wrja d'alaha kdam kensha damme haden arjek pawlaws wafrash menhon lzalmide wjulyum mmalel wa amhon b'esjawli dgavra dashmeh turanaws
10	whade hwaz shnin tarten dama dashmaw melza dmarya kulhon damrin b'asiyya ihudayye wa'rmayye
11	wjayle rawrve aved wa alaha b'iydeh dpawlaws
12	hajana d'af men najte dal gushmeh sudare aw ruke mayten waw wsamin al krihe wfarkin waw menhon kurhane wa'f shi'de nafkin waw
13	svaw waw den af nasha ihudayye aylen dmezkarkin waw wmawmen al shi'de dnawmon bashmeh dmaran yeshu al aylen d'iyz way lhon ruje tanfaza kad amrin waw mawmenan ljon bashmeh dyeshu ayna dmajres pawlaws
14	iz wa den shava bnawy dgavra jad ihudayya rab kahne dashmeh hwa skewa davdin waw hade
15	wana shi'da haw bisha we'mar lhon lyeshu meshtawda na walpawlaws yada na aton den man aton
16	washwar layhon gavra haw d'iyz wa beh ruja bishta we'zjayyal layhon wshapel enon wjad shlijin wafiin rakw men bayta haw
17	whade ezyadaz waz ljulhon ihudayye wa'rmayye damrin b'efesaws wneflaz dejlza al kulhon wmezramram wa shmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
18	wsaguiyye' men aylen dhaymenw azen waw wmeshtaen sajlwazhon wmawden bmedem davdin waw
19	saguiyye' den af jarashe kaneshw kzavayhon wa'ytiw awkedw enon kdam kulnash wajshavw dmayhon waslek kespa rebwaza jamesh
20	whajana bjayla raba takpa waz wsagya haymanuzeh d'alaha
21	kad den eshtalam halen sam pawlaws breyaneh dnezkrej bjulah makedawniyya wva'ja'iyya wni'sal lu'wrishlem we'mar dma d'esez lzaman wale li d'af rumi ejse
22	wshadar wa tren nashin men hanon damshamshin waw leh lmakedawniyya ltimaze'aws wle'rastaws hu den kawi savna b'asiyya
23	hwa wa den bhaw savna shgushya saguiyya' al urjeh d'alaha
24	iz wa den taman aved si'ma jad dashmeh wa dimitriyyaws daved wa nawse dsi'ma la'rtemis wmawtar wa lavnay umanuzeh yuzrana raba
25	hana kanesh enon lavnay umanuzeh kulhon wla'ylen dfaljin amhon we'mar lhon gavre yadin ton dze'gurtan kulah men hana y puljana
26	wa'f aton shamin ton wjasen ton dla wa baljud lavnay efesaws ela af lsuga' djulah asiyya apis hana pawlaws wa'hpej kad amar dlaw alahe enon hanon dvi'yday bnaynasha mezavdin
27	wlaw hade svuza baljud mezparsya wvatla ela af hu haykla d'artemis alahta rabza mezjshev leh ayj la medem wa'f hi alahta djulah asiyya wjulhon amme sagdin lah metshita
28	wjad shmaw halen ezmliw jemta wkaen waw wa'mrin raba y artemis d'efesayye
29	we'shtagshaz kulah mdita warhetw ajjda we'salw lze'atrawn wajtafw awbelw amhon lga'iyyaws wla'ristarjaws gavre makedawnayye bnay lwizeh dpawlaws
30	wpawlaws save wa dneul lze'atrawn wajla'u talmide
31	wa'f rishe d'asiyya metul drajmawy waw shadarw baw meneh dla netel nafsheh dneul lze'atrawn
32	kenshe den d'iyz waw bze'atrawn tav shguishin waw wajrane jranyaza kaen waw saguiyye' guer menhon la yadin waw metul mana ezkanashw
33	ama den dihudayye d'iyz waw taman akimw menhon lgavra ihudayya dashmeh aleksandraws wjad kam anif ideh wsave wa dnepuk ruja lama
34	wjad idaw dihudayya w kaw kulhon bjad kala ayj shain tarten draba y artemis d'efesayye
35	wshali enon risha damdita kad amar gavre efesayye manu guer men bnaynasha dla yada lamdita d'efesayye djumrza y d'artemis rabza walsalmah dmen shmayya njez
36	metul hajil dlukval hade nash la meshkaj lmi'mar wale ljon dzehon shlen wla tebdon medem bsurhava
37	aytiton guer lgavre halen kad la haykle jalesw wla sajiw la'lahtan
38	en den hana dimitriyyaws wavnay umanuzeh iz lhon dina am nash ha anzupataws bamdita umane enon nekrvon wandunon jad am jad
39	we'n hu dmedem jrin baen ton bdukza dihiva men namusa lajnushya meshtre
40	metul d'af hasha bkundinaws kaminan dnezrshe ayj shagushe dla meshkjinan dnepuk ruja al kensha dyawma hana d'ezkanashn batala'iz we'shtgueshn dla elza
41	wjad halen emar shra ljensha
