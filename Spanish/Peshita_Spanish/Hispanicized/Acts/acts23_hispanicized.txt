1	wjad jar pawlaws bjenshhon emar gavre ajay ena bjul ti'rta tavza etdabrez kdam alaha dama lyawmana
2	wjananya kahna pkad lhanon dkamin al guebeh dnemjuneh lpawlaws al pumeh
3	wpawlaws emar leh zid u alaha dnemjej esza mjawarta wa't yazev at d'aen at li ayj davnamusa kad avar at al namusa wfaked at dnemjunany
4	wa'ylen dkamin waw taman amrin leh ljahna d'alaha msaje at
5	amar lhon pawlaws la yada wiz ajay djahna w kziv u guer dalrisha damaj la tlut
6	wjad ida pawlaws dmeneh dama izawy dsadukayye wmeneh dafrishe ka wa bjensha gavre ajay ena prisha na bar prishe wal savra dakyamta dmize metdin na
7	wjad hade emar nfalw jad bjad prishe wsadukayye we'zpleg ama
8	sadukayye guer amrin dlayt kyamta wla mala'je wla ruja prishe den mawden bjulhen
9	wahwa kala raba wkamw nasha safre men gaba dafrishe wnasen waw amhon wa'mrin la meshkjinan medem dvish bhana gavra en den ruja aw mala'ja malel ameh mana iz bah bhade
10	wjad hwa shgushya raba baynazhon djel wa kiliyyarja dalma nfashjuneh lpawlaws washlaj lrumayye dni'zon nejtfuneh men msazhon wnaluneh lmashriza
11	wjad hwa lilya ezjsi leh maran lpawlaws we'mar leh ezjayyal metul d'ayj d'ashedt lay b'uwrishlem hajana zid at d'af brumi tashed
12	wjad hwa safra ezkanashw waw nashin men ihudayye wa'jremw layhon dla ne'jlon wla neshton dama dnektluneh lpawlaws
13	hawen waw den hanon d'akimw bmawmaza hana kyama yatir men arbin gavrin
14	we'zkaravw lwaz kahne walwaz kashishe wa'mrin waw djerma ajremn layn dmedem la netam dama dnektul lpawlaws
15	whasha baw aton wrishe dajnushta men kiliyyarja dnaytewy lwazjon ayj baen ton dzevson sharira'iz suraneh wajnan mtayvinan dnektliwy adla nemte lwazjon
16	washma wa bar jazeh dpawlaws aparsna hana wal lmashriza wvadek lpawlaws
17	wshadar pawlaws kra ljad men kentrune we'mar leh awbel lalayma hana lwaz kiliyyarja iz leh guer medem dni'mar leh
18	wdavreh kentruna lalayma wa'leh lwaz kiliyyarja we'mar pawlaws asira krany wvae meny d'ayte hana layma lwazaj d'iyz leh medem dni'mar laj
19	wa'jdeh b'iydeh kiliyyarja lalayma wnagdeh ljad gaba wamsha'el wa leh dmana iz laj dzi'mar li
20	we'mar leh layma ihudayye ezjashavw dnevon menaj dzajez lpawlaws mjar ljenshhon ayj saven medem yatir dni'lpon meneh
21	at hajil la tettpis lhon ha guer yatir men arbin gavrin menhon natrin leh bajmi'na wa'jremw al nafshhon dla ne'jlon wla neshton dama dnektluneh wha mtayvin wamkawen lshudayyaj
22	washra kiliyyarja lalayma kad pakdeh dnash la neda dhalen badekt li
23	wakra lazren kentrunin we'mar lhon selw atedw rumayye ma'zen dni'sun lkesariyya wfarashe shavin wshadyay byamina ma'zen dnepkon men tlaz shain blilya
24	tayyevw den af bira ayj dnarkvon lpawlaws wanfaltuneh lwaz piliks higmawna
25	wajzav egarta yav lhon d'iyz bah hajana
26	klawdiyyaws lusiyyaws lfiliks higmuna nasija shlam
27	lgavra hana ejadw ihudayye ayj dnektluneh wkamez ena am rumayye wfarekteh kad yelpez drumayya w
28	wjad bae wiz lmeda elza dmetulazah rashen waw leh ajetteh ljenshhon
29	we'shkjez dal sitime dnamushon rashen waw leh welza dshawya la'sure aw lmawta layt wa lwazeh
30	wjad ezbadak li nejla bajmi'na davadw lawy ihudayye mejda shadarteh lwazaj wfakdez laktigranawy dni'zon wni'mron ameh kdamayk hwi jlim
31	haden rumayye ayj d'ezpakadw davru lpawlaws blilya wa'ytyu la'ntipatras mdita
32	walyawma jrina shraw parashe lragale javrayhon dnehpjon lmashriza
33	wa'ytyu lkesariyya wyavw egarta lhigmawna wa'kimu kdamawy lpawlaws
34	wjad kra egarta msha'el wa leh dmen ayda huparjiyya izawy wjad ilef dmen kilikiyya
35	emar leh shama na laj ma d'ezaw ktigranayk wafkad dnetruneh bapretawriyyan dherawdes
