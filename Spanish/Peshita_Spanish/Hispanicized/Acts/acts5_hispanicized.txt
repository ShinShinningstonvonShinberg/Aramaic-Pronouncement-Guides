1	wgavra jad dashmeh hwa jananya am atzeh dashmah hwa shapira saben wa krizeh
2	washkal men timeh wtashi kad rguisha waz beh atzeh wa'yti meneh men kespa wsam kdam reglayhon dashlije
3	we'mar leh shemon jananya manaw dhajana mla satana lebaj datdaguel bruja dkudsha wattashe men kespa dadmeh dakriza
4	la wa dilaj hwaz adla tesdaban wmen d'esdabnaz tuv at shalit wayt al dmeh lmana samt blebaj dzebed svuza hade la daguelt bavnay nasha ela b'alaha
5	wjad shma jananya halen mele nfal wmiz wahwaz dejlza rabza bjulhon halen dashmaw
6	wkamw aylen dalaymin bhon wjanshu wa'pekw kavru
7	wmen bazar dahway tlaz shain af atzeh elaz kad la yada waz mana hwa
8	amar lah shemon emar li en bhalen dmayya sabenton kriza hi den emraz in bhalen dmayya
9	amar lah shemon metul d'eshtwiton lamnasayyu rujeh dmarya ha reglayhon dkavurawy dvalejy bzara whenon napkunejy
10	wvah bshaza neflaz kdam reglayhon wmizaz walw layme hanon we'shkjuh kad miza wakfasw awbelw kavruh al guev balah
11	wahwaz dejlza rabza bjulah ita wavjulhon hanon dashmaw
12	whawyan way byad shlije azwaza wagvarwaza saguiyya'za bama wjulhon knishin waw ajjda b'estwa dashlemon
13	wmen nasha jrane nash la mamraj wa dnezkarav lwazhon ela mawrev wa lhon ama
14	wyatir mettawsfan waw aylen damhaymnin waw bmarya kensha dgavre wadneshe
15	aykana davshuke mapkin waw lajrihe kad rmen waw barsaza d'emazy dnehwe aze shemon afen telanizeh taguen layhon
16	azen waw den saguiyye' lwazhon men mdinaza jranyaza dajdaray urishlem kad mayten waw krihe wa'ylen dhawyan way lhon ruje tanfaza wmezjalmin waw kulhon
17	we'zmli wa jsama rab kahne wjulhon dameh d'iyzayhon waw men yulpana dsadukayye
18	wa'rmiw idayya al shlije we'jadw esarw enon bez asire
19	haden blilya mala'ja dmarya pzaj tara dvez asire wa'pek enon we'mar lhon
20	selw kumw bhaykla wmalelw lama kulhen mele halen djayye
21	wanfakw edan shafra walw lhaykla wmalfin waw rab kahne den wa'ylen dameh kraw ljavrayhon walkashishe d'iysrayyel wshadarw lvez asire dnayton enon lashlije
22	wjad esalw aylen d'eshtadarw menhon la eshkajw enon bez asire wahfajw ezaw
23	amrin eshkajn bez asire d'ajid shira'iz wa'f lnature dkamin al tare wafzajn wnash la eshkajn taman
24	wjad shmaw halen mele rabay kahne wa'rjune dhaykla tawirin waw layhon wmezjashvin waw dmana y hade
25	we'za nash awda enon dhanon gavre dajvashton bez asire ha kamin bhaykla wmalfin lama
26	haden esalw arjune am dajshe dnayton enon la baktira dajlin waw guer dalma nergum enon ama
27	wjad aytiw enon akimw enon kdam kuleh kensha wa'kef wa rab kahne lmi'mar lhon
28	la wa mefkad pkadn wayn ljon dalnash la talfon bashma hana aton den ha mlaytunah lu'wrishlem men yulpanjon wsaven ton dzayton layn dmeh dgavra hana
29	na shemon am shlije we'mar lhon la'laha wale lmettpasu yatir men dlavnaynasha
30	alaha d'avahazan akim lyeshu ayna d'aton ktalton kad tlaytuna al kaysa
31	leh lhana akim alaha risha wmajyana wa'rimeh byamineh ayj dnetel tyabuza wshuvkan jtahe li'ysrayyel
32	wajnan sahde nan dmele halen wruja dkudsha haw dyav alaha la'ylen damhaymnin beh
33	wjad shmaw waw halen mele mezgawslin waw brugsa wmezjashvin waw lmektal enon
34	wkam wa jad men prishe dashmeh hwa gamali'yel malef namusa wamyakar men kuleh ama wafkad dnapkon enon lashlije lvar edana sura
35	we'mar lhon gavre bnay israyyel esdarw bnafshjon wajsaw mana wale ljon lmebad al halen nasha
36	men kdam guer hana savna kam wa tawda we'mar al nafsheh dmedem u rav we'salw bazreh ayj arbama' gavrin whu ezktel wa'ylen d'asin waw bazreh ezbadarw wahwaw ayj la medem
37	wkam men bazreh ihuda glilayya byawmaza dmezkazbin waw nasha bajsef risha wa'sti ama saguiyya' bazreh whu miz wjulhon aylen d'asin waw bazreh ezbadarw
38	whasha amar na ljon prukw ljon men halen nasha washvukw lhon d'en u dmen bnaynasha izeh hade majshavta whana vada meshtren wavrin
39	en den men alaha w la matya b'iydaykon dazvatluna dalma teshtajjon ljon dlukval alaha kamin ton
40	we'ttpisw leh wakraw enon lashlije wnaguedw enon wfakedw enon dla nehon mmallin bashma dyeshu washraw enon
41	wanfakw men kdamayhon kad jaden dashwaw waw metul shma dnestaron
42	wla shalen waw kulyum lmalafu bhaykla wavvayta wlamsabaru al maran yeshu mshija
