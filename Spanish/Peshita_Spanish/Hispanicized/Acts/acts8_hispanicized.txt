1	sha'wal den save wa wamshawtaf wa bketleh wahwa wa bhaw yawma rdufya raba lita dvu'wrishlem we'zbadarw kulhon bkurya dihud wa'f bez shamrayye lvar men shlije baljud
2	wakfasw kavru le'stefanaws gavre mhaymne we'ze'velw lawy rawrva'iz
3	sha'wal den radef wa liteh d'alaha kad a'el wa lvate wamgargar lgavre walneshe wamshalmin wa lvez asire
4	whanon d'ezbadarw mezkarkin waw wmajrsin melza d'alaha
5	filipaws den njez leh lamdita dshamrayye wmajres wa lhon al mshija
6	wjad shamin waw melzeh bnaynasha dzaman satin waw leh wmettpisin waw ljul d'amar wa djasen waw azwaza daved wa
7	saguiyye' guer d'ajidan way lhon ruje tanfaza kaen waw bkala rama wnafkan way menhon wajrane msharayya wamjagre eza'siw
8	wjaduza rabza hwaz bha mdita
9	iz wa den taman gavra jad dashmeh simawn damir wa leh bah bamdita savna saguiyya' wavjarshawy mate wa lama dshamrayye kad mawrev wa nafsheh wa'mar d'ena na raba
10	waslen waw lwazeh kulhon rawrve wdakdke wa'mrin waw hanaw jayleh raba d'alaha
11	wmettpisin waw leh kulhon metul dsavna saguiyya' bjarshawy azmah wa enon
12	kad den haymenw lfilipaws damsabar wa malkuza d'alaha bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija amdin waw gavre wneshe
13	wa'f hu simawn haymen wa wamad wnakif wa leh lfilipaws wjad jsa wa azwaza wjayle rawrve dhawen waw b'iydeh tamah wa wmetdamar
14	wjad shmaw shlije dvu'wrishlem dkabelw ama dshamrayye melza d'alaha shadarw lwazhon lshemon ki'fa walyujanan
15	wanjezw wsaliw layhon ayj dankablon ruja dkudsha
16	layt wa guer al jad menhon dakil baljud den amdin waw bashmeh dmaran yeshu
17	haden samin waw layhon ida wamkablin waw ruja dkudsha
18	wjad jsa simawn dvasyam ida dashlije mezyava ruja dkudsha karev lhon kespa
19	kad amar havw af li shultana hana d'ayna d'esim lawy ida nehwe mkabel ruja dkudsha
20	amar leh shemon ki'fa kespaj amaj ni'sal la'vdana metul dasvart dmawhavzeh d'alaha bkenyan alma mezkanya
21	layt laj mnaza afla pesa bhaymanuza hade metul dlebaj la wa tris kdam alaha
22	bram tuv men bishuzaj hade wavi men alaha dalma neshtvek laj nejla dlebaj
23	bjavda guer marirta wavketre dawla jase na d'iyzayk
24	na simawn we'mar baw aton jlafay men alaha dla ni'ze lay medem men halen d'emarton
25	shemon den wyujanan kad sahedw enon wa'lefw melza d'alaha hfajw lhon lu'wrishlem wavkurya saguiyya'za dshamrayye sabarw
26	wmalel mala'ja dmarya am filipaws we'mar leh kum sel lzaymna b'uwrja madbrata dnajta men urishlem lgasa
27	wkam esal wa'reh mhaymna jad d'aze wa men kush shalita dkandak malkza djushayye whu shalit wa al kuleh gasah we'za wa dnesgud b'uwrishlem
28	wjad hfaj dni'sal yazev wa al markavza wkare wa b'eshaya nviyya
29	we'mraz ruja lfilipaws ezkarav wkaf lmarkavza
30	wjad ezkarav shma dkare b'eshaya nviyya we'mar leh d'en mestakal at mana kare at
31	whu emar aykana meshkaj na d'estakal ela en nash narteny wava meneh men filipaws dnesak wnetev ameh
32	pasuka den dajzava dkare wa beh izawy wa hana ayj emra lnejsza ezdvar wa'yj nekya kdam gasusa shatik wa whajana la pzaj pumeh
33	bmukajeh men jvushya wmen dina ezdvar wdareh manu neshtae dmeshtaklin jayyawy men ara
34	we'mar haw mhaymna lfilipaws bae na menaj al manu amrah hade nviyya al nafsheh aw al nash jrin
35	haden filipaws pzaj pumeh wshari meneh men hana kzava msabar leh al maran yeshu
36	wjad henon asin b'uwrja matiw waw ldukza jda d'iyz bah mayya we'mar haw mhaymna ha mayya mana i kaliza d'emad
37	we'mar filipaws en mhaymen at men kuleh leba shalit wana we'mar ena mhaymen na dyeshu mshija breh d'alaha w
38	wafkad wa dazkum markavza wanjezw trayhon lmayya wa'mdeh filipaws lamhaymna haw
39	wjad slekw men mayya rujeh dmarya jetpaz lfilipaws wzuv la jsa mhaymna haw ela asel wa b'uwrjeh kad jade
40	filipaws den eshtjaj b'asawtaws wmen taman mezkrej wa wamsabar bamdinaza kulhen dama d'eza lkesariyya
