1	njezw waw den nasha men ihud wmalfin waw lhon la'je d'en hu dla gasrin ton bayada dnamusa la meshkjin ton lmeja
2	wahwa shgushya saguiyya' wavaza lpawlaws walvarnava amhon wahwaz dneskon pawlaws wvarnava wajrane amhon lwaz shlije wkashishe dvu'wrishlem metul hade baza
3	wlawyaz shadraz enon ita wraden waw bjulah puniki wa'f bez shamrayye kad meshtaen waw al punayya damme wavdin waw jaduza rabza ljulhon aje
4	wjad ezaw lu'wrishlem ezkabalw men ita wmen shlije wmen kashishe we'shtaiw lhon kul ma davad amhon alaha
5	kamw waw den nasha aylen dhaymenw waw men yulpana dafrishe wa'mrin dwale u ljon lmegsar enon wazfakdon enon dnetron namusa dmushe
6	ezkanashw den shlije wkashishe dnejson al melza hade
7	wjad hwaz baza sagui'za kam shemon we'mar lhon gavre ajin aton yadin ton dmen yawmaza kadmayye men pumy dily gva alaha dneshmon amme melza dasvarza wanhaymnon
8	wa'laha dyada davlebawaza ashed layhon wyav lhon ruja dkudsha ayj dlan
9	wmedem la prash baynayn walhon metul ddaki bhaymanuza lebawazhon
10	whasha aton mana mnasen ton la'laha ayj dazsimon nira al sawrayhon dzalmide ayna d'afla avahazan afla jnan eshkajn lmetan
11	ela btaybuzeh dmaran yeshu mshija mhaymninan dnije ajwazhon
12	washzekw kuleh kensha wshamin waw lpawlaws walvarnava dmeshtaen waw kul ma davad alaha b'iydayhon azwaza wagvarwaza bamme
13	wvazar dashzekw kam yakuv we'mar gavre ajayn shumuny
14	shemon eshtai ljon aykana shari alaha lmegba men amme ama lashmeh
15	walhade shalman melayhon danviyye ayj ma dajziv
16	dmen bazar halen ehpuj wa'kim mashkneh ddawid ayna danfal we'vne medem danfal meneh wa'kimiwy
17	ayj dnevon sharkhon davnaynasha lmarya wjulhon amme aylen d'ezkri shemy layhon amar marya davad halen kulhen
18	idiin men alam vadawy d'alaha
19	metul hade ena amar na dla nehon shajkin la'ylen dmen amme mezpnen lwaz alaha
20	ela neshtlaj lhon dnehon parikin men tam'uza dadvija wmen sanyuza wmen dajnika wmen dma
21	mushe guer men dare kadmayye bjul mdina iz wa leh karuse bajnushaza davjul shabin karen leh
22	haden shlije wkashishe am kulah ita gvaw gavre menhon wshadarw la'ntiyyawji am pawlaws wvarnava lihuda dmezkre bar-shaba walshila gavre drishe waw bhon b'aje
23	wajzavw egarta b'iydayhon hajana shlije wkashishe wa'je la'ylen d'iyz b'antiyyawji wavsuriyya wavkilikiyya aje dmen amme shlam
24	shmi lan dnashin menan nfakw wdalajjon bmele wa'hpejw nafshazjon kad amrin dzehon gasrin wnatrin namusa aylen dajnan la pakedn enon
25	metul hana ezjashavn kulan kad knishinan wagvayn gavre wshadarn lwazjon am pawlaws wvarnava jabivayn
26	nasha d'ashlemw nafshazhon jlaf shmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
27	wshadarn amhon lihuda walshila dhenon bmelza ni'mron ljon henen halen
28	hwa guer sevyana lruja dkudsha wa'f lan dla nettsim laykon yukra yatira lvar men halen d'alsan
29	dzezrajkon men dadvija wmen dma wmen jnika wmen sanyuza djad tetron nafshjon men halen shapir tehon hwaw sharirin bmaran
30	hanon den d'eshtlajw ezaw la'ntiyyajiyya wajnashw kuleh ama wyavw egarta
31	wjad kraw jdiw we'zbayya'w
32	wavmelza atirta jayyelw la'je wkayyemw enon dvez ihuda wshila metul d'af henon nviyye waw
33	wjad hwaw taman savna shraw enon aje bashlama lwaz shlije
34	bram hwa sevyaneh dshila dankawe taman
35	pawlaws den wvarnava kawiw waw b'antiyyawji wmalfin waw wamsabrin am jrane saguiyye' melzeh d'alaha
36	wvazar yawmaza emar pawlaws lvarnava nezpne wnesur la'je davjul mdina d'ajresn bah melza d'alaha wnejse mana vidin
37	barnava den save wa dnedbar lyujanan haw d'ezkani markaws
38	pawlaws den la save wa dnedbreh amhon metul dashvak wa enon kad henon bpamfuliyya wla esal amhon
39	metul hana jeryana prashw jad men jad wvarnava dvar lmarkaws wardaw byama we'salw lhon lkupraws
40	pawlaws den gva leh lshila wanfak kad magel men aje ltaybuza d'alaha
41	wrade wa bsuriyya wavkilikiyya wamkayyem idaza
