1	wjad halen mele mmallin waw lama kamw layhon kahne wsadukayye wa'rjune dhaykla
2	kad mezjamzin waw layhon dmalfin lama wmajrsin bamshija al kyamta dmen bez mize
3	wa'rmiw layhon idayya wantarw enon lyawma jrina metul dakrev wa leh ramsha
4	wsaguiyye' dashmaw waw melza haymenw waw wi'yzayhon waw bmenyana ayj jamsha alfin gavrin
5	walyawma jrina ezkanashw arjune wkashishe wsafre
6	wa'f janan rab kahne wkayyafa wyujanan wa'leksandraws wa'ylen d'iyzayhon waw men sharbza drabay kahne
7	wjad akimw enon bamsaza msha'lin waw lhon dva'yna jil aw b'ayna shem vadton hade
8	haden shemon ki'fa ezmli ruja dkudsha we'mar lhon arjunawy dama wkashishe dvez israyyel shmaw
9	en jnan yawmana metdinin nan menjon al shapirta dahwaz lvarnasha kriha davmana hana eza'si
10	hade tezida ljon waljuleh ama d'iysrayyel dvashmeh dyeshu mshija nasrayya haw d'aton skaftuna haw d'akim alaha men bez mize beh bhaw ha ka'em hana kdamaykon kad jlim
11	hanaw ki'fa d'asliton aton banayye whu hwa lrish karna
12	wlayt bnash jrin purkana la guer iz shma jrina tjez shmayya d'ezihev lavnaynasha dveh wale lmeja
13	wjad shmaw melzeh dshemon wadyujanan din bagle amruh estakalw dla yadin sefra whedyute enon wazharw bhon we'shtawdaw enon dam yeshu mezhapjin waw
14	wjasen waw dka'em wa amhon jguira haw d'eza'si wla meshkjin waw medem lmi'mar lukvalhon
15	haden pkadw dnapkon enon men kenshhon wa'mrin waw jad ljad
16	mana nebed lhon lgavre halen ha guer aza gliza dahwaz b'iydayhon ljulhon amureh d'uwrishlem ezyadaz wla meshkjinan dnejpur
17	ela dla yatira'iz nepuk bama teba hana nezlajam lhon dzuv la nmallon bashma hana lnash men bnaynasha
18	wakraw enon wfakedw enon dlagmar la nmallon wla nalfon bshem yeshu
19	naw shemon ki'fa wyujanan we'marw lhon en ki'na kdam alaha daljon neshma yatir men alaha dunw
20	la guer meshkjin nan dma dajsayn washman dla nmalliwy
21	we'zlajamw lhon washraw enon la guer eshkajw lhon elza dansimon brishhon metul ama kulnash guer mshabaj wa la'laha al medem dahwa
22	yatir guer men bar arbin shnin izawy wa gavra haw dahwaz beh hade aza d'asyuza
23	wjad eshtriw ezaw lwaz ajayhon we'shtaiw lhon kul ma d'emarw kahne wkashishe
24	whenon kad shmaw ajjad arimw kalhon lwaz alaha we'marw marya at u alaha davadt shmayya wa'ra wyamme wjul d'iyz bhon
25	wa't u dmalelt byad ruja dkudsha bfum dawid avdaj lmana rgashw amme we'mwaza rnay srikuza
26	kamw malke d'ara wshalitane we'zmalajw ajjda al marya wal mshijeh
27	ezkanashw guer sharira'iz bamdita hade al kadisha braj yeshu ayna d'at mshajt herawdes wpilataws am amme wjensha d'iysrayyel
28	lmebad kul ma d'iydaj wsevyanaj kadem rsham dnehwe
29	wa'f hasha marya jur wajsi llujamayhon whav lavdayk din bagle nehon majrsin melzaj
30	kad idaj mawshet at la'swaza wlagvarwaza wla'zwaza dnehwyan bashmeh davraj kadisha yeshu
31	wjad baw we'zkashafw ettsi azra dveh knishin waw we'zmliw kulhon bruja dkudsha wammallin waw in bagle melza d'alaha
32	iz wa den ljensha dnasha aylen dhaymenw waw jda nfesh wjad reyan wla nash menhon amar wa al nejse dakne wa ddileh enon ela kul medem d'iyz wa lhon dgawa hwa
33	wavjayla raba mashdin waw henon shlije al kyamteh dyeshu mshija wtaybuza rabza iz waz am kulhon
34	wnash layt wa bhon dasrij aylen guer daknen waw kurya wvate msabnin waw wmayten dmayya dmedem dmesdaban
35	wsamin lwaz reglayhon dashlije wmezihev wa lnash nash ayj medem dasnik wa
36	yawsef den haw d'ezkani barnava men shlije dmettargam bra dvuyya'a lewayya men azra dkupras
37	iz wa leh kriza wsabnah wa'yti dmeh wsam kdam reglayhon dashlije
