1	bkesariyya den iz wa gavra jad kentruna dashmeh wa kawrniliyyaws men spira ha dmezkarya italiki
2	wsadik wa wdajel wa men alaha hu wvayteh kuleh waved wa sedkaza saguiyya'za bama wavjulsvan bae wa men alaha
3	hana jsa mala'ja d'alaha bjeswa galya'iz la'pay tsha shain b'iymama dal lwazeh we'mar leh kawrniliyye
4	whu jar beh wadjel we'mar mana mary we'mar leh mala'ja slawazaj wsedkazaj slek ldujrana kdam alaha
5	whasha shadar gavre lyawpi mdita wa'yta lshemon dmezkre ki'fa
6	ha shre bvayteh dshemon bursayya haw d'iyzawy al yad yama
7	wjad esal leh mala'ja dmalel ameh kra tren men bnay bayteh wfalja jad ddajel la'laha ayna dmetdne wa leh
8	we'shtai lhon kulmedem dajsa wshadar enon lyawpi
9	walyawma jrina kad henon raden b'uwrja wkarivin lamdita slek shemon le'gara dansale bshez shain
10	wajfen wsave wa dnelas wjad henon mazknin leh nfal lawy temha
11	wajsa shmayya kad pzijin wma'na jad kad asir b'arba karnan wdame wa ljetana raba wsha'ev wa men shmayya al ara
12	wi'yz wa beh kulhen jaywaza d'arbaz regle wrajsha d'ara wfarajza dashmayya
13	wkala eza leh d'amar shemon kum kus wa'jul
14	wshemon emar jas mary dmemzum la ejlez kul damsayyav watma
15	wzuv dzarten savnin kala hwa lwazeh aylen d'alaha daki at la tsayyev
16	hade den hwaz tlaz savnin we'zali leh ma'na lashmayya
17	wjad metdamar shemon bnafsheh dmanaw jeswa dajsa matiw gavre hanon d'eshtadarw waw men kawrniliyyaws wsha'elw al bayta dashre beh shemon we'zaw wkamw al tara ddarta
18	wkaren waw taman wamsha'lin d'en shemon dmezkre ki'fa harka shre
19	wjad shemon rane wa bjeswa emar leh ruja ha gavre tlaza baen laj
20	kum juz wsel amhon kad la mezpalag reyanaj metul d'ena hu shadrez enon
21	haden njez shemon lwaz gavre hanon we'mar lhon ena hu dvaen ton ayda y elza dmetulazah ezayton
22	amrin leh gavra jad dashmeh kawrniliyyaws kentruna ki'na ddajel men alaha wmashed lawy kuleh ama dihudayye eze'mar leh bjeswa men mala'ja kadisha danshadar nalaj lvayteh wneshma melza menaj
23	wa'el enon shemon wkabel enon kar dashre wa wkam lvazreh dyawma wanfak esal amhon we'salw ameh nash nash men aje dyawpi
24	walyawma jrina al lkesariyya kawrniliyyaws den mkawe wa lhon kad mjanshin leh kulhon bnay tuhmeh wa'f rajme jabive d'iyz waw leh
25	wjad a'el shemon areh kawrniliyyaws wanfal sgued lreglawy
26	wshemon akimeh we'mar leh kum laj wa'f ena barnasha na
27	wjad mmalel ameh al we'shkaj saguiyye' d'ezaw waw lzaman
28	we'mar lhon aton yadin ton dla mapas lgavra ihudayya dnekaf lnasha nujrayya dla wa bar sharbzeh wli alaha jawyany dla imar al nash datma aw msayyav
29	metul hana zida'iz eziz kad shadarton bazary bram msha'el na ljon metul mana shadarton bazary
30	amar leh kawrniliyyaws arba yawmin iz dama lhasha ha men dsa'em na wvazsha shain kad msale na bvayty kam gavra jad kdamay kad lvish jeware
31	we'mar li kawrniliyye eshtamaz sluzaj walsedkazaj dujrana hwa kdam alaha
32	bram shadar lyawpi mdita wa'yta lshemon dmezkre ki'fa ha shre bvayteh dshemon bursayya dal yad yama whu ni'ze nmalel amaj
33	wvar shazeh shadrez lwazaj wa't shapir vadt d'ezayt wha jnan kulan kdamayk wsavenan dneshma kul ma d'ezpked laj men lwaz alaha
34	pzaj den shemon pumeh we'mar bashrara adrjez d'alaha la wa nasev b'ape
35	ela bjulhon amme ayna ddajel meneh wfalaj ki'nuza mkabal u lwazeh
36	melza guer dshadar lavnay israyyel wsabar enon shlama wshayna byad yeshu mshija hanaw marya djul
37	wa'f aton yadin ton bmelza dahwaz bjulah ihud d'akfaz men glila bazar mamudiza d'ajres yujanan
38	al yeshu dmen nasraz d'alaha mashjeh bruja dkudsha wavjayla whuyyu dmezkrej wa wma'se lhanon d'eznjiw men bisha metul d'alaha wa ameh
39	wajnan sahdawy al kul ma davad bjulah ara dihud wdu'wrishlem leh lhana tla'u ihudayye al kaysa wkatlu
40	wleh akim alaha lazlaza yawmin wyahbeh dnezjse in bagle
41	la den ljuleh ama ela lan aylen dmen alaha ezgvin dnehwe leh sahde d'ejaln ameh we'shtin men bazar kyamteh dmen bez mize
42	wfakdan dnajres wansahed lama dhanaw d'ezpresh men alaha dayyana djayye wadmize
43	walawy ashedw kulhon nviyye djulman damhaymen bashmeh nkabel shuvkan jtahe
44	wjad hu shemon mmalel wa halen mele agnaz ruja dkudsha al kulhon dshamin waw melza
45	wazmahw wazharw aje gsire hanon d'ezaw ameh d'af al amme mawhavta druja dkudsha eshtapaz
46	shamin waw lhon guer kad mmallin bleshan leshan wmawrvin waw la'laha we'mar wa shemon
47	dalma mayya meshkaj nash kale dla nemdon hanon dha kabelw ruja dkudsha ayj ma dajnan
48	haden pkad lhon dnemdon bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija wavaw meneh ayj dankawe lwazhon yawmaza
