1	wjad eza pistaws lkesariyya bazar tlaza yawmin slek lu'wrishlem
2	wa'wdu rabay kahne wrishe dihudayye al pawlaws wvaen waw meneh
3	kad sha'lin leh hade taybuza danshadar naytewy lu'wrishlem kad avdin waw kmi'ne b'uwrja dnektluna
4	wfistaws pani pezgama dpawlaws mezntar bkesariyya we'na msarhav na d'ejsuk
5	aylen hajil d'iyz bjon dmatya b'iydayhon nejzon aman wal kul sajlu d'iyz beh bgavra nkatrgon
6	wjad hwa taman yawmaza tmanya aw esra njez leh lkesariyya walyawma jrina izev al bim wafkad dnayton lpawlaws
7	wjad eza jadru ihudayye danjezw men urishlem wrishe saguiyye' wakshayya mayten waw bazreh aylen dla meshkjin waw danjaon
8	wjad pawlaws nafek wa ruja dla askel medem la bnamusa dihudayye wla bhaykla wla bkesar
9	pistaws den metul dsave wa dnarshe taybuza bihudayye emar leh lpawlaws save at dzesak lu'wrishlem wzaman al halen tetdin kdamay
10	na pawlaws we'mar al bim dkesar ka'em na harka hu sadek li lmetdanu la medem jtiz lihudayye ayj d'af at yada at
11	we'n sajluza vida li aw medem dshawe lmawta la meshte'l na men mawta en den layt medem se'day men halen damkatrguin li la nash yahev li lhon mawhvaz bguenawy dkesar kare na
12	haden pistaws malel am bnay melkeh we'mar bguen kesar krayt lwaz kesar asel at
13	wjad hwaw yawmaza njez agripaws malka wverniki lkesariyya dneshe'lon shlameh dfistaws
14	wjad hwaw lwazeh yawmaza eshtai pistaws lmalka dineh dpawlaws kad amar gavra jad asira eshtvek men iday piliks
15	wjad hwiz b'uwrishlem awdaw li lawy rabay kahne wkashishe dihudayye wavaw d'ebed lhon dina meneh
16	we'mrez lhon dlayt yada lrumayye dnetlon barnasha mawhvaz lketla dama dni'ze bel dineh wnaksiwy b'apawy wnezihev leh azra lmapak bruja al ma dmezrshe
17	wjad eziz lja dla tuhayya lyawma jrina yezbez al bim wfekdez dnaytuna li lgavra
18	wkamw ameh ktirganawy wla eshkajw medem reshyana bisha danjaon lawy ayj ma dsavar wiz
19	sitime den medem medem al dejlazhon iz wa lhon lwazeh wal yeshu nash dmiz haw d'amar wa pawlaws djay u
20	wmetul dla ka'em wiz ena al baza dhalen emrez wiz lpawlaws d'en bae at dzi'sal lu'wrishlem wzaman tetdin al halen
21	hu den ba dnezntar ldineh dkesar wfekdez dnezntar dama d'eshadriwy lwaz kesar
22	we'mar agripaws save wiz d'eshmiwy lgavra hana wfistaws emar dlamjar shama at leh
23	walyawma jrina eza agripaws wverniki bsawja saguiyya' wal lvez dina am kiliyyarje wrishe damdita wafkad pistaws we'za pawlaws
24	we'mar pistaws agripaws malka wjuljon gavre d'iyzaykon aman al hana gavra djasen ton kuleh ama dihudayye kablany b'uwrishlem wharka kad kaen dla tuv wale lhana dnije
25	ena den adrjez dmedem dshawe lmawta la sir leh wmetul dhu ba dnezntar ldineh dkesar pekdez dneshtadar
26	wla yada na mana ejtuv lawy lkesar metul hana sviz lmaytayyuzeh kdamaykon wyatira'iz kdamayk malka agripe dma d'eshta'al dineh eshkaj mana ejtuv
27	la guer wale djad mshadrinan gavra asira dla nejtuv sajluzeh
