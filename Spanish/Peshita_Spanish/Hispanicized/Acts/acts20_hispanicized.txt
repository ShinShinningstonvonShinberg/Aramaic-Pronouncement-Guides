1	wvazar dashli shgushya kra pawlaws lzalmide wvayya enon wnashek enon wanfak esal leh lmakedawniyya
2	wjad ezkrej enon la'zrawaza halen wvayya enon bmele saguiyya'za eza leh lhelas azra
3	wahwa taman tlaza yarjin vadw den lawy nejla ihudayye kad zid wa lmi'sal lsuriyya we'zjashav dnehpuj leh lmakedawniyya
4	wanfakw ameh dama la'siyya sawpatraws dmen beraw'a mdita wa'ristarjaws wsekundaws dmen tesalawniki wga'iyyaws dmen derbi mdita wtimaze'aws dmen lustra wmen asiyya tujikaws watrawfimaws
5	halen esalw kdamayn wkawiw lan batrawa's
6	jnan den nfakn men filipaws mdita dmakedawnayye bazar yawmaza dfatire wardayn byama we'zayn latrawa's lyawmaza jamsha wahwayn taman yawmaza shava
7	wavyawma djad bshaba kad knishinan dnekse ewjaristiyya mmalel wa amhon pawlaws metul dalyawma jrina zid wa dnepuk leh wa'gar wa lammalalu dama lfelgueh dlilya
8	wi'yz wa taman lampi'de dnura saguiyye' beliza ha dajnishin hwayn bah
9	wyazev wa layma jad dashmeh hwa ewtujaws bjawza wshama watva bshenza yakirta kad agar wa bmelza pawlaws wavshenzeh nfal wa men tlaza medyarin we'shtkel kad miz
10	wanjez pawlaws nfal lel meneh wapkeh we'mar la tetsion metul dnafsheh beh i
11	kad slek den ksa lajma watem wahwa mmalel dama daslek safra whaden nfak dni'sal byavsha
12	wdavru lalayma kad jay wajdiw beh rawrva'iz
13	jnan den njezn le'lfa wardayn lwada dzasaws metul dmen taman zidin wayn dankabliwy lpawlaws hajana guer pkad wa lan kad esal wa hu byavsha
14	kad den kabelna men tasaws shkalna b'elfa we'zayn lmitulini
15	wmen taman lyawma jrina rdayn lukval kiyyaws gasarta wzuv lyawma jrina ezayn lsamaws wkawin batrawguiliyyawn walyawma jrina ezayn lmilitaws
16	psik wa leh guer lpawlaws dnebrih le'fesaws dalma neshtawjar leh taman metul damsarhav wa d'en meshkja yawma dpentikawsti b'uwrishlem nebdiwy
17	wmenah men militaws shadar ayti lkashishe dita d'efesaws
18	wjad ezaw lwazeh emar lhon aton yadin ton dmen yawma kadmayya delez la'siyya aykana hwiz amjon kuleh savna
19	kad palaj na la'laha bmakijuza sagui'za wavdeme wavnesyune aylen daden waw lay bnejlayhon dihudayye
20	wla bsiz bmedem dfakaj wa lnafshazjon d'ajres ljon wa'lef bshuke wavvate
21	kad msahed wiz lihudayye wla'rmayye al tyabuza dalwaz alaha whaymanuza davmaran yeshu mshija
22	whasha ena asir na bruja wa'sel na lu'wrishlem wla yada na mana ara li bah
23	bram ruja dkudsha bjul mdina msahed li wa'mar d'asure u'wlsane zidin laj
24	ela li la jshiva nafshy medem ayj d'eshalem rehty wzeshmeshta dkablez men maran yeshu d'ashed al svarza dtaybuzeh d'alaha
25	whasha ena yada na dzuv parsupy la jasen ton kuljon aylen d'ezkarkez ajrsez ljon malkuza
26	wmetul hana msahed na ljon yawma dyawmana dadje na men dma djuljon
27	la guer eshte'lez d'awdajon kuleh sevyaneh d'alaha
28	esdarw hajil bnafshjon wavjulah mariza ha d'akimjon bah ruja dkudsha episkawpe dzeron liteh d'alaha ha daknah badmeh
29	ena yada na dmen bazar d'asel na nelon amjon d'ive takife dla jasin al mariza
30	wa'f menjon diljon nkumon gavre mmallay makmaza ayj dnahpjon lzalmide dni'sun bazarhon
31	metul hana hwayton irin wahidin dashnayya tlaz la shliz blilya wvi'ymama kad bdeme marte ena lnash nash menjon
32	whasha magel na ljon la'laha walmelza dtaybuzeh dhi meshkja banya ljon wyahba ljon yurtana am kulhon kadishe
33	kespa aw dahva aw najte la reguez
34	wa'ton yadin ton dlasnikuzy wla'ylen d'iyz amy shamesh halen iday
35	wjulmedem jawizjon dhajana wale lmela' walmi'saf d'aylen dajrihin wlamahadu melawy dmaran yeshu metul dhu emar dtuvawy la'yna dyahev yatir men ayna dnasev
36	wjad halen emar ked al burkawy wsali wjulhon nasha ameh
37	wahwaz bjaza rabza bjulhon wapku wamnashkin waw leh
38	yatira'iz den meshtankin waw al ha melza d'emar dla tuv zidin dnejson parsupeh wlawyu dama le'lfa
