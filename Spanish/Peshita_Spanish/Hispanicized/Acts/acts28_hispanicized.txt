18	whenon kad sha'luny svaw dneshrunany metul dla eshkajw bazary reshyana medem dshawe lmawta

23	wa'kimw leh yawma wajnashw we'zaw lwazeh saguiyye' kar dashre wa wagla lhon al malkuza d'alaha kad msahed wampis lhon al yeshu men namusa dmushe wmen nviyye men safra wadama lramsha
