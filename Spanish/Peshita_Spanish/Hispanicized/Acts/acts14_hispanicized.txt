1	we'zaw walw lhon lajnushta dihudayye whajana malelw amhon ayj danhaymnon saguiyye' men ihudayye wmen yawnayye
2	ihudayye den aylen dla mettpisin waw garegw lamme dnave'shon lhon la'je
3	whenon savna saguiyya' waw taman win bagle mmallin waw al marya whu mashed wa al melza dtaybuzeh b'azwaza wavzedmraza daved wa b'iydayhon
4	wjuleh kensha damdita plig wa menhon iz waw am ihudayye wmenhon nakifin waw lashlije
5	hwa den layhon gsama men amme wmen ihudayye wrishanayhon dansaron enon wnergmon enon bji'fe
6	wjad idaw shaniw we'zgawasw bamdinaza dlukaniyya lustra wderbi wkurya dajdarayhen
7	wzaman msabrin waw
8	wgavra jad yazev wa blustra mdita dasguif wa breglawy jguira dmen kres emeh dmemzum la halej wa
9	hana shma lpawlaws dammalel wjad jsa pawlaws we'shtawda d'iyz beh haymanuza dnije
10	emar leh bkala rama laj amar na bashmeh dmaran yeshu mshija kum al reglayk washwar kam whalej
11	wjensha dama kad jsaw medem davad pawlaws arimw kalhon bleshaneh d'azra wa'mrin alahe etdamiw bavnay nasha wanjezw lwazan
12	wshamiw waw lvarnava mare alahe walpawlaws hermis metul dhu mshare wa bmelza
13	wjumra dmare alahe haw d'iyz wa lvar men mdita ayti tawre wajlile lzara ddarta azar dashren waw wsave wa dandabaj lhon
14	barnava den wpawlaws kad shmaw sadekw najtayhon washwarw wanfakw lhon lwaz ajlaws wkaen waw
15	wa'mrin gavre mana avdin ton af jnan bnaynasha nan jashushe ajwazjon damsabrinan ljon dmen halen batalaza tezpnon lwaz alaha jayya haw davad shmayya wa'ra wyamme wjul d'iyz bhon
16	haw davdare kadmayye shvak wa enon ljulhon amme dni'sun b'uwrjaza dnafshhon
17	kad la shvak nafsheh dla sahdu badaved wa lhon tavaza men shmayya wmajez wa lhon metra wamrabe wa pi're bsavnayhon wmale wa tursayya wvasimuza lebawazhon
18	wjad halen amrin waw men majsen klaw lama dnash la ndabaj lhon
19	ezaw den lzaman ihudayye men ikaniwan wmen antiyyajiyya washgashw layhon lama wragmu lpawlaws wgaru lvar men mdita metul dsavrin waw dmiz leh
20	wajnashw lawy talmide wkam al lamdita walyawma jrina nfak leh men taman am barnava we'zaw lderbi mdita
21	wjad msabrin waw lavnay mdita ha talmedw waw lsaguiyye' wahfajw ezaw lhon llustra mdita wli'ykaniwan wla'ntiyyajiyya
22	kad msharrin waw nafshazhon dzalmide wvaen waw menhon dankaon bhaymanuza wa'mrin waw lhon dvu'wlsana saguiyya' wale lmeal lmalkuzeh d'alaha
23	wa'kimw lhon bjul ita kashishe kad samin waw amhon wamsalen wmaglin waw lhon lmaran haw dhaymenw beh
24	wjad ezkrejw b'azra dpisidiyya ezaw lhon lpamfuliyya
25	wjad malelw bpergui mdita melza dmarya njezw lhon li'ytaliyya
26	wmen taman rdaw byama we'zaw lhon la'ntiyyajiyya metul dmen taman maglin waw ltaybuzeh dmarya lavada haw dshalemw
27	wjad knashw kulah ita meshtaen waw kulmedem davad amhon alaha wdafzaj tara dhaymanuza lamme
28	wsavna saguiyya' waw taman lwaz talmide
