1	pawlaws wtimaze'aws avde dyeshu mshija ljulhon kadishe davyeshu mshija d'iyz bfilipaws am kashishe wamshamshane
2	taybuza amjon washlama men alaha avon wmen maran yeshu mshija
3	mawde na la'lahy al uhdanjon amina
4	davjul bawazy dal apaykon wjad jade na mezkashaf na
5	al shawtafuzjon dve'wangueliyyawn men yawma kadmayya wadama lhasha
6	metul dazjil na al hade dhaw man dshari bjon vade tave hu nshalem dama lyawmeh dmaran yeshu mshija
7	hajana guer sadek li lmezraayyu al kuljon metul davleby simin ton wva'suray wavmapak brujy dal shrareh d'ewangueliyyawn dshawtafay aton btaybuza
8	sahed u li guer alaha d'aykana majev na ljon brajmawy dyeshu mshija
9	whade msale na dzuv nesgue wnezyatar jubjon bidaza wavjul sukal druj
10	dzehon parshin aylen dahnan wzehon djen dla tukla byawmeh damshija
11	wamlen pi're dsadikuza davyeshu mshija lshuvja wli'ykara d'alaha
12	save na den dzedon ajay dsurany dily yatira'iz aze lakdamawy b'ewangueliyyawn
13	hajana d'af asuray ezgliw bamshija bapretawriyyan kulah walsharka djulnash
14	wsuga' d'aje davmaran ettjelw al asuray wa'shijw yatira'iz dla dejla lammalalu melza d'alaha
15	wnash nash men jsama wjeryana nash nash den bsevyana tava wavjuba lamshija majrsin
16	metul dyadin dalmapak bruja d'ewangueliyyawn sim na
17	hanon den davjeryana lamshija majrsin la wa dajya'iz ela dsavrin dmawsfin ulsana la'suray
18	wavhade jdiz wjade na davjul prus en belza we'n bkushta mshija nezkres
19	yada na guer dhalen ljayye meshtajjan li bvauzjon wavmawhavza druja dyeshu mshija
20	aykana damsabar na wamsake na davmedem la evhaz ela bgalyuz ape ayj davjulsvan af hasha nezrawrav mshija bfagry en bjayye we'n bmawta
21	jayyay guer dily mshija w we'n emuz yuzrana w li
22	en den af bhalen jayye dvesra pi're iz li bavaday la yada na mana egbe li
23	alsan li guer tartayhen lmeftar rguig na dam mshija ehwe whade tav pakja waz li
24	ela af d'ekawe bfagry alsa li svuza metulazjon
25	whade tjila'iz yada na dfa'esh na wamkawe na ljaduza diljon walzarbiza dhaymanuzjon
26	djad ize tuv lwazjon nezyatar bi shuvharjon davyeshu mshija baljud
27	aykana dya'e lasvarzeh damshija hwayton metdabrin d'en ize ejsejon we'n parik na eshma laykon dkamin ton bajda ruj wvajda nfesh wmeznasjiton ajjad bhaymanuza d'ewangueliyyawn
28	wavmedem la mettsiin ton men hanon dkamin lkuvlan lzajwiza d'avdanhon waljayye diljon
29	whade men alaha w ezyahbaz ljon dla baljud mhaymanu thaymnon beh bamshija ela af dal apawy tejshon
30	wazsaybron aguna ayj haw dajsayton bi whasha shamiton lay
