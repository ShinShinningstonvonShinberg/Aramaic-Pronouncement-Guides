3	af menaj bae na bar sawgy sharira dzehwe madar lhen dhenen l'i amy b'ewangueliyyawn am klimis wam sharka damadranay hanon dashmahayhon kzivin bajzava djayye

6	dmedem la ti'spon ela bjulsvan basluza wavvauza wavzawdiza she'lazjon nezyadan kdam alaha
7	washlameh d'alaha haw drab men kul mada nnatar lebawazjon wmadaykon byeshu mshija
8	mekil ajay aylen dshariran wa'ylen dnajpan wa'ylen dji'nan wa'ylen ddajyan wa'ylen darjiman wa'ylen dashvijan wa'ylen vade dshuvja wadkulasa halen ezraaw

12	yada na d'ezmakaj yada na u af d'ezyatar bjul wavjul medem mdarash na af bsava af bjafna bamshartjuza wavjasiruza
13	djul medem mse na jayla bamshija damjayyel li
14	bram shapir vadton d'eshtawtafton lu'wlsanay
15	yadin ton den af aton filipisayye davshurayya dasvarza kad nefkez men makedawniyya afla jda men idaza eshtawtaf li bjushban masva wmatla ela aton baljud
16	d'af lzesalawniki jda svan wzarten jshajzy shadarton li
