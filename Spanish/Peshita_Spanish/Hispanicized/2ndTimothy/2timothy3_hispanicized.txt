1	hade den hwayt yada davyawmaza jrayye ni'zon savne kshayya
2	wnehon bnaynasha rajmay nafshhon wrajmay kespa shavhrane rame mgadfane dalnashayhon la mettpisin kafray btaybuza rashie
3	ajlay karse mshabday lregza barirayye sanyay tavaza
4	mashlmane msarhve jzire rajmay rguigaza tav men jubeh d'alaha
5	d'iyz lhon eskima ddejlaz alaha wmen jayleh rajikin aylen dhajana enon djuk enon menaj
6	menhon enon guer halen damjaldin bez bate wshaven neshe datmiran bajtahe wmetdavran larguigaza mshajlfaza
7	davjulsvan yalpan wmemzum lidaza dashrara lme'za la meshkjin
8	aykana den dyanis wyambris kamw lukval mushe hajana af henon kamin lukval shrara nasha damjabal reyanhon waslen men haymanuza
9	ela la ni'zon lakdamayhon shatyuzhon guer mezyada y ljulnash aykana d'af dhanon ezyadaz
10	at den ezayt bazar yulpany wvazar dubaray wvazar sevyany wvazar haymanuzy wvazar naguiruz rujy wvazar juby wvazar msaybranuzy
11	wvazar rdifuzy wvazar jashay wyada at aylen saybrez b'antiyyajiyya wvi'ykanawn wavlustra ayda rdifuza saybrez wmen kulhen pasyany mary
12	wjulhon den aylen dsaven bdejlaz alaha dnijon byeshu mshija mezradpin
13	bnaynasha den bishe wmatyane nawsfon al bishuzhon kad taen wmaten
14	at den kawa b'aylen dileft we'shtarart yada at guer men manu ileft
15	wadmen talyuzaj sefre kadishe yalif at dmeshkjin danjakmunaj ljayye bhaymanuza dyeshu mshija

17	dnehwe gmir barnasha d'alaha waljul vad tav mshalman
