1	pawlaws shlija dyeshu mshija bsevyaneh d'alaha wavmulkana djayye davyeshu mshija
2	ltimaze'aws bra jabiva taybuza wrajme washlama men alaha ava wmen maran yeshu mshija
3	mawde na la'laha haw dleh mshamesh na men avahazy bzi'rta djiza d'amina'iz metdjar na laj baslawazy dlilya wdi'ymama
4	waswe na lmejsyaj wmetdjar na demayk d'ezmle jaduza
5	buhdana dhawe li bhaymanuzaj sharirta ha dashraz lukdam b'ema d'emaj lwa'is wve'maj ewniki mpas na den d'af baj
6	metul hana mahed na laj dazir mawhavta d'alaha ha d'iyz baj basyam idy
7	la guer yav lan alaha ruja ddejlza ela djayla wadjuba wadmartyanuza
8	la hajil tevhaz bsahduzeh dmaran afla bi b'asireh ela shkul bishaza am ewangueliyyawn bjayla d'alaha
9	haw d'ajyan wakran bkeryana kadisha law ayj vadayn ela ayj sevyaneh wtaybuzeh ha d'ezyahbaz lan byeshu mshija men kdam savna dalme
10	we'zgalyaz hasha bguelyaneh dmajyanan yeshu mshija dvatel lmawta wjawi jayye wla mezjablanuza b'ewangueliyyawn
11	haw d'ettsimez beh ena karusa washlija wmalfana damme
12	metul hade msaybar na halen wla bahez na yada na guer bman haymnez wampas na dmatya b'iydawy gulany lmetar li lyawma haw
13	nehwyan laj jawra mele jlimaza dashmat meny bhaymanuza wavjuba davyeshu mshija
14	gulana tava tar bruja dkudsha ha demraz ban
15	yada at hade dahfajw meny kulhon halen dva'siyya d'iyzayhon menhon puguelaws whermaguenws
16	netel maran rajme lvayteh d'anisifawraws dsavnin saguiyya'n anijany wavshishlaza d'asuray la bhez
17	ela af kad eza lrumi bajfituza bany we'shkjany
18	netel leh maran dneshkaj rajme lwaz maran byawma haw wajma dshamshany b'efesaws yatira'iz yada at
