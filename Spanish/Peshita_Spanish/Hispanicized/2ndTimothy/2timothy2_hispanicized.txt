1	at hajil bery ezjayyal btaybuza davyeshu mshija
2	wa'ylen dashmat meny byad sahde saguiyye' hanen agel lnasha mhaymne aylen dmatya b'iydayhon af lajrane lmalafu
3	wsaybar bishaza ayj palja tava dyeshu mshija
4	la nash palaj wmezpakar bsevwaza dalma dneshpar lhaw man dagva
5	we'n mezkatash nash la mezkalal en bnamuseh la mezkatash
6	la'kara dla'e wale leh dkadma men pi'rawy nestaybar
7	estakal medem d'amar na netel laj maran jejmza bjul medem
8	etdar lyeshu mshija dkam men bez mize haw d'iyzawy men sara ddawid ayj ewangueliyyawn dily
9	dveh savel na bishaza dama la'sure ayj aved bishaza ela melzeh d'alaha la asira
10	metul hana kulmedem msaybar na metul gvayya d'af henon neshkjon jayye davyeshu mshija am shuvja dalalam
11	mhaymna y melza en guer mizn ameh af ameh nije
12	we'n nsaybar af namlej ameh en den nejpur beh af hu nejpur ban
13	we'n la nhaymen beh hu bhaymanuzeh mkawe dnejpur guer bnafsheh la meshkaj
14	halen hwayt mahed lhon wamsahed kdam maran dla nehon mezjren bmele dla yuzran lsujafa d'aylen dshamin lhen
15	wnezbtel laj dazkim nafshaj gmira'iz kdam alaha palja dla behtza dmajres trisa'iz melza dashrara
16	wmen mele srikaza dlayt bhen jashju eshtal yatira'iz guer nawsfon al rushhon hanon danen bhen
17	wmelazhon ayj jaladiza nawmi te'jud bsaguiyye' izawy den jad menhon humene'aws wajrina pilitaws
18	halen dataw men shrara kad amrin dakyamta dmize hwaz lah whaymanuza dnash nash mahpjin
19	sheze'sta den sharirta d'alaha kayyama y wi'yz lah jazma hana wyada marya la'ylen ddileh enon wnefruk men awla kul dkare lashmeh dmarya
20	bvayta den raba la wa ma'ne ddahva baljud aw dsi'ma iz beh ela af dkaysa af dfajara menhon li'ykara wmenhon lsara
21	en nash hajil ndake nafsheh men halen hawe ma'na dajya li'ykara dahen ljushaja dmareh wamtayyav ljul vad tav
22	men kulhen rguigaza dtalyuza ruk what bazar ki'nuza whaymanuza wjuba washlama am aylen dkaren lmaran bleba dajya
23	jeryane sajle aylen dadla mardu enon eshtal menhon yada at guer dzajtushe mawldin
24	avdeh den dmaran la jayyav lmezkatashu ela dnehwe makij lwaz kulnash wmalfan wnaguira rujeh
25	dnehwe rade la'ylen dmezjren lkuvleh bmakijuza dalma netel lhon alaha tyabuza wnedon shrara
26	wnehdon nafshhon wnefrkon men pajeh dsatana dveh ettsidw lsevyaneh
