1	wahwa djad shalem yeshu kulhen mele halen emar lzalmidawy
2	yadin ton dvazar tren yawmin hawe pesja wavreh dnasha meshtlem dnesdkef
3	haden ezkanashw rabay kahne wsafre wkashishe dama ldarteh drab kahne dmezkre kayyafa
4	we'zmalajw al yeshu davnejla ne'jduna wnektluna
5	wa'mrin waw la badida dla nehwe shgushya bama
6	wjad hwa yeshu bvez-anya bvayteh dshemon garba
7	kerbaz leh atza d'iyz leh shatifta dmeshja dvesma sagui dmayya wa'shpazah al risheh dyeshu kad smij
8	jsaw den talmidawy we'zbe'sh lhon we'marw lmana avdana hana
9	meshkaj wa guer dnesdaban hana bsagui wnezihev lmeskine
10	yeshu den ida we'mar lhon mana male'yn ton lah la'tza vada shapira evdaz lwazy
11	bjulsvan guer meskine iz ljon amjon li den la bjulsvan iz ljon
12	hade den d'armyaz besma hana al gushmy ayj dalmekbrany evdaz
13	wa'min amar na ljon d'ayka dzezkres svarzy hade bjuleh alma nezmalal af medem devdaz hade ldujranah
14	haden esal jad men tresar dmezkre ihuda sjaryuta lwaz rabay kahne
15	we'mar lhon mana saven ton lmetal li we'na mashlem na leh ljon henon den akimw leh tlazin djespa
16	wmen haden bae wa leh pela dnashlmiwy
17	byawma den kadmayya dfatire krevw talmide lwaz yeshu we'marw leh ayka save at dantayyev laj dzelas pesja
18	hu den emar lhon selw lamdita lwaz plan we'marw leh raban amar savny mta leh lwazaj aved na pesja am talmiday
19	wzalmidawy vadw aykana dafkad lhon yeshu wtayyevw pesja
20	wjad hwa ramsha smij hwa am tresar talmidawy
21	wjad lasin emar amin amar na ljon djad menjon mashlem li
22	wjeryaz lhon tav wshariw lmi'mar leh jad jad menhon lma ena mary
23	hu den na we'mar man dsava ideh amy blagza hu nashlmany
24	wavreh dnasha asel aykana dajziv lawy wa leh den lgavra haw dvi'ydeh breh dnasha meshtlem pakaj wa leh lgavra haw elu la eziled
25	na ihuda mashlmana we'mar dalma ena hu rabi amar leh yeshu at emart
26	kad den lasin shkal yeshu lajma wvarej waksa wyav lzalmidawy we'mar savw ajulw hanaw pagry
27	washkal kasa wa'wdi wyav lhon we'mar savw eshtaw meneh kuljon
28	hanaw demy ddiyyaziki jaza dajlaf saguiyye' meze'shed lshuvkana dajtahe
29	amar na ljon den dla eshte men hasha men hana yalda dagfeta dama lyawma dveh eshtewy amjon jaza bmalkuzeh d'avy
30	wshabajw wanfakw ltur sayte
31	haden emar lhon yeshu aton kuljon tezkashlon bi bhana lilya kziv guer d'emje lraya wnezbadron erbe daneh
32	men bazar dka'em na den kadem na ljon laglila
33	na ki'fa we'mar leh afen kulnash nezkshel baj ena mzum la ezkshel baj
34	amar leh yeshu amin amar na laj davhana lilya kdam dnekre tarnagla tlaz savnin tejpur bi
35	amar leh ki'fa en nehwe li lammaz amaj la ejpur baj whajwaz af kulhon talmide emarw
36	haden eza amhon yeshu ldukza dmezkarya guedsiman we'mar lzalmidawy tevw harka ad isal esale
37	wadvar lji'fa wlazrayhon bnay savday wshari lmezkmaru walmezaku
38	we'mar lhon karya y lah lnafshy dama lmawta kawaw li harka washharw amy
39	wafrak kalil wanfal al apawy wamsale wa wa'mar avy en meshkja nebrany kasa hana bram la ayj d'ena save na ela ayj d'at
40	we'za lwaz talmidawy we'shkaj enon kad damkin we'mar lji'fa hajana la eshkajton jda shaa dzeshhron amy
41	ettirw wsalaw dla telon lnesyuna ruja mtayva pagra den krih
42	tuv esal dzarten savnin sali we'mar avy en la meshkaj hana kasa dnebar ela en eshtizeh nehwe sevyanaj
43	we'za tuv eshkaj enon kad damkin aynayhon guer yakiran way
44	washvak enon we'sal tuv sali dazlaz savnin wlah lmelza emar
45	haden eza lwaz talmidawy we'mar lhon dmajw mekil we'ttnijw ha mtaz shaza wavreh dnasha meshtlem b'iydayhon djatayye
46	kumw ni'sal ha mta haw dmashlem li
47	wad hu mmalel ha ihuda mashlmana jad men tresarta eza wjensha ameh saguiyya' am safsire wjutre men lwaz rabay kahne wkashishe dama
48	wyav wa lhon aza ihuda mashlmana we'mar lhaw dnashek na huyyu leh ajudw
49	wmejda krev lwaz yeshu we'mar shlam rabi wnashkeh
50	hu den yeshu emar leh al ha d'ezayt javry haden ezkaravw wa'rmiw idayhon al yeshu wa'jdu
51	wha jad men hanon dam yeshu awshet ideh washmat safsira wamja lavdeh drab kahne wshaklah edneh
52	haden emar leh yeshu ahpej safsira ldukzah kulhon guer hanon dansavw sayfe bsayfe nmuzon
53	aw savar at dla meshkaj na d'eve men avy wankim li hasha yatir men tartaesre legyunin dmala'je
54	aykana hajil nezmalon kzave dhajana wale dnehwe
55	bha shaza emar yeshu ljenshe ayj dal gayyasa nfakton bsafsire wavjutre dze'jdunany kulyum lwazjon bhaykla yazev wiz wmalef wla ejadtunany
56	hade den dahwaz dnezmalon kzave danviyye haden talmide kulhon shavku warakw
57	whanon d'ajdu lyeshu awblu lwaz kayyafa rab kahne ayka dsafre wkashishe knishin waw
58	shemon den ki'fa asel wa bazreh men rujka dama ldarteh drab kahne wal izev lgaw am dajshe dnejse jarza
59	rabay kahne den wkashishe wajnushta kulah baen waw al yeshu sahde ayj danmizuna
60	wla eshkajw we'zaw saguiyye' sahde dshukra jrayyaz den krevw tren
61	wa'mrin hana emar dmeshkaj na d'eshre haykla d'alaha wlazlaza yawmin evnewy
62	wkam rab kahne we'mar leh la medem mfane at pezgama mana mashdin layk halen
63	yeshu den shatik wa wana rab kahne we'mar leh mawme na laj b'alaha jayya dzi'mar lan en at u mshija breh d'alaha
64	amar leh yeshu at emart amarna ljon den dmen hasha tejsuna lavreh dnasha dyazev men yamina djayla wa'ze al nanay shmayya
65	haden rab kahne sari ma'nawy we'mar ha gadef mana mekil mezben lan sahde ha hasha shmaton gudafeh
66	mana saven ton naw wa'mrin jayyav u mawta
67	haden rakw b'apawy wamkapjin waw leh jrane den majen waw leh
68	wa'mrin eznaba lan mshija manu haw damjaj
69	ki'fa den yazev wa lvar bdarta wkerbaz lwazeh amza jda wa'mra leh af at am yeshu wayt nasrayya
70	hu den kfar kdam kulhon we'mar la yada na mana amra aty
71	wjad nfak lsepa jsazeh jriza wa'mra lhon dzaman wa af hana am yeshu nasrayya
72	wzuv kfar bmawmaza dla yada na leh lgavra
73	men bazar kalil den krevw hanon dkamin we'marw lji'fa sharira'iz af at menhon at af mamllaj guer mawda laj
74	haden shari lmajramu walmi'ma dla yadana leh lgavra wvah bshaza kra tarnagla
75	we'tdjar ki'fa melza dyeshu d'emar leh dakdam dnekre tarnagla tlaz savnin tejpur bi wanfak lvar bja marira'iz
