1	kad njez den men tura nakpu kenshe saguiyye'
2	wha garba jad eza sgued leh we'mar mary en save at meshkaj at lamdakayyuzy
3	wafshat ideh yeshu krev leh we'mar save na etdaka wvah bshaza etdaki garveh
4	we'mar leh yeshu jsi lma lnash amar at ela sel jawa nafshaj ljahne wkarev kurbana ayj dfaked mushe lsahduzhon
5	kad al den yeshu lajfarnajum krev leh kentruna jad wvae wa meneh
6	wa'mar mary tali rme bvayta wamsharay wvisha'iz meshtanak
7	amar leh yeshu ena ize wa'sewy
8	na kentruna haw we'mar mary la shawe na dzeul tjez matlly ela baljud emar bmelza wneza'se tali
9	af ena guer gavra na dazjez shultana wi'yz tjez idy estratiyyute wa'mar na lhana dsel wa'sel wlajrina dza wa'ze walavdy daved hade waved
10	kad shma den yeshu etdamar we'mar lda'zen ameh amin amar na ljon d'af la b'isra'yel eshkjez ayj hade haymanuza
11	amar na ljon den dsaguiyye' ni'zon men madnja wmen marva wnestamkon am avraham wi'ysjak wyakuv bmalkuza dashmayya
12	bneh den dmalkuza nepkon ljeshuja barayya taman nehwe bejya wjurak shene
13	we'mar yeshu lkentruna haw sel aykana dhayment nehwe laj we'za'si talyeh bah bshaza
14	we'za yeshu lvayteh dshemon wajsa lajmazeh dramya wa'jida lah eshaza
15	wakrev li'ydah washvakzah eshaza wkamaz wamshamsha waz leh
16	kad hwa den ramsha karevw kdamawy daywane saguiyye' wa'pek daywayhon bmelza waljulhon aylen dvisha'iz vidin waw asi enon
17	ayj dnezmale medem d'eze'mar byad eshaya nviyya d'emar dhu nesav ki'vayn wjurhanayn netan
18	kad jsa den yeshu kenshe saguiyye' dajdirin leh pkad dni'sun levra
19	wakrev safra jad we'mar leh rabi ize bazraj la'zar d'asel at
20	amar leh yeshu lzale neke iz lhon walfarajza dashmayya matlla breh den dnasha layt leh ayka dnesmuj risheh
21	jrina den men talmidawy emar leh mary apes li lukdam isal ekbur avy
22	yeshu den emar leh ta bazary washvuk lmize kavrin mizayhon
23	wjad slek yeshu lasfita slekw ameh talmidawy
24	wha sawa raba wa byama aykana d'elfa tezkase men galle hu den yeshu damij wa
25	wakrevw talmidawy airu wa'mrin leh maran pasan avdin nan
26	amar lhon yeshu lmana dajulzanin ton suray haymanuza haden kam waj'a bruja wavyama wahwa shelya raba
27	nasha den etdamarw wa'mrin manu hana druje wyama meshtamin leh
28	wjad eza yeshu levra la'zra dgadrayye aru tren daywane dnafkin men bez kvure bishe dtav ayj dla nash neshkaj nebar bha urja
29	wakaw wa'mrin ma lan wlaj yeshu breh d'alaha ezayt lja kdam savna dazshankan
30	iz wa den lhal menhon bakra dajsire saguiyye' draya
31	hanon den shi'de baen waw meneh wa'mrin en mapek at lan apes lan dni'sal lvakra dajsire
32	amar lhon yeshu selw wmejda nfakw walw bajsire wjulah bakra ha tersaz lel lashkifa wanfalw byama wmizw bmayya
33	hanon den draen waw rakw we'salw lamdita wjawiw kulmedem dahwa wadhanon daywane
34	wnefkaz kulah mdita lu'wreh dyeshu wjad jsa'u baw meneh danshane men tjumayhon
