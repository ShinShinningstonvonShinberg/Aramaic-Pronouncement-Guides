1	haden yeshu ezdvar men ruja dkudsha lmadbra dneznase men ajelkarsa
2	wsam arbin yawmin wa'rbin laylawan jrayyaz den kfen
3	wakrev haw damnase we'mar leh en breh at d'alaha emar dhalen ki'fe nehwyan lajma
4	hu den na we'mar kziv dla wa blajma baljud jayye barnasha ela bjul mela dnafka men pumeh d'alaha
5	haden davreh ajelkarsa lamdinaz kudsha wa'kimeh al kenfa dhaykla
6	we'mar leh en breh at d'alaha shdi nafshaj lzajt kziv guer dalmala'jawy nfaked layk wal idayhon neshklunaj dla tetkel bji'fa reglaj
7	amar leh yeshu tuv kziv dla tnase lmarya alahaj
8	tuv davreh ajelkarsa ltura dtav ram wjawyeh kulhen malkwaza dalma wshuvjhen
9	we'mar leh halen kulhen laj etel en tepel tesgud li
10	haden emar leh yeshu sel laj satana kziv guer dalmarya alahaj tesgud wleh baljudawy tefluj
11	haden shavkeh ajelkarsa wha mala'je krevw wamshamshin waw leh
12	kad shma den yeshu dyujanan eshtlem shani leh laglila
13	wshavkah lnasraz we'za mar bajfarnajum al yad yama bazjuma dasvawlon wadnaftali
14	dnezmale medem d'eze'mar byad eshaya nviyya d'emar
15	ara dasvawlon ara dnaftali urja dyama evrawy dyurdnan glila damme
16	ama dyazev bjeshuja nuhra raba jsa wa'ylen dyazbin b'azra wavtelale dmawta nuhra dnaj lhon
17	men haden shari yeshu lmajrasu walmi'mar tuvw kerbaz lah guer malkuza dashmayya
18	wjad mhalej al yad yama daglila jsa tren ajin shemon d'ezkri ki'fa wa'ndre'was aju dramen msidaza byama izayhon waw guer sayyade
19	we'mar lhon yeshu taw bazary we'bedjon dzehon sayyade davnay nasha
20	henon den mejda shvakw msidazhon we'salw bazreh
21	wjad var men taman jsa jrane aje tren yakuv bar savday wyujanan aju b'elfa am savday avuhon damzaknin msidazhon wakra enon
22	henon den mejda shvakw le'lfa wla'vuhon we'salw bazreh
23	wmezkrej wa yeshu bjulah glila wmalef wa bajnushazhon wmajres svarza dmalkuza wma'se kul ki'v wjurhan bama
24	we'shtma tebeh bjulah suriyya wkarevw leh kulhon aylen dvish bish vidin bjurhane mshajlfe wa'ylen d'alisin bzashnike wdaywane wadvar egare wamsharayya wa'si enon
25	we'salw bazreh kenshe saguiyye' men glila wmen esraz-mdinaza wmen urishlem wmen ihud wmen evra dyurdnan
