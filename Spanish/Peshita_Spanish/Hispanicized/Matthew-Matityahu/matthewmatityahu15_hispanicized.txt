1	haden krevw lwaz yeshu prishe wsafre dmen urishlem wa'mrin
2	lmana talmidayk avrin al mashlmanuza dkashishe wla mshiguin idayhon ma d'ajlin lajma
3	na yeshu we'mar lhon lmana af aton avrin ton al pukdana d'alaha metul mashlmanuzjon
4	alaha guer emar yakar la'vuj wle'maj wman damsaje la'vu wle'meh mmaz nmuz
5	aton den amrin ton kul man dni'mar la'va aw le'ma kurbany medem dzezhne meny wla nyakar la'vu aw le'meh
6	wvatelton melza d'alaha metul mashlmanuza diljon
7	nasbay b'ape shapir eznabi laykon eshaya we'mar
8	ama hana bsefwazeh w myakar li lebhon den sagui rajik meny
9	wasrika'iz dajlin li kad malfin yulpane dfukdane davnaynasha
10	wakra ljenshe we'mar lhon shmaw we'stalw
11	la wa medem da'el lfuma msayyev lvarnasha ela medem dnafek men puma haw u msayyev lvarnasha
12	haden krevw talmidawy wa'mrin leh yada at dafrishe dashmaw melza hade ezkshelw
13	hu den na we'mar lhon kul nesbza ayda dla nasbah avy dvashmayya tezkar
14	shvukw lhon smayya enon nagude dasmayya samya den lsamya en nedbar trayhon bgumasa naflin
15	wana shemon ki'fa we'mar leh mary pashek lan mazla hana
16	hu den emar lhon dama lhasha af aton la mestaklin ton
17	la yadin ton dmedem da'el lfuma ljarsa hu asel wmen taman bzadkiza meshtde lvar
18	medem den dmen puma nafek men leba nafek whuyyu msayyev leh lvarnasha
19	men leba hu guer nafkan majshvaza bishaza gawra ketla sanyuza ganavuza sahduz shukra gudafa
20	halen enen damsayvan lvarnasha en nash den nelas kad la mshagan idawy la mestayyav
21	wanfak men taman yeshu we'za lazjuma dsur wadsaydan
22	wha atza knanata men tjume hanon nefkaz kad kaya wa'mra ezrajam lay mary breh ddawid brazy bisha'iz metdavra men shi'da
23	hu den la panyah pezgama wakrevw talmidawy baw meneh wa'mrin shrih dkaya bazran
24	hu den na we'mar lhon la eshtadrez ela lwaz erbe dataw men bez israyyel
25	hi den ezaz segdaz leh we'mraz mary adarayny
26	emar lah la shapir lmesav lajma davnayya walmarmayyu ljalbe
27	hi den emraz in mary af kalbe ajlin men partuze dnaflin men pazure dmarayhon wja'en
28	haden emar lah yeshu aw atza raba y haymanuzejy nehwe lejy ayj dsavya aty we'za'syaz barzah men ha shaza
29	wshani men taman yeshu we'za al guev yama daglila waslek ltura wizev taman
30	wakrevw lwazeh kenshe saguiyye' d'iyz waw amhon jguire wasmayya wjarshe wafshigue wajrane saguiyye' wa'rmiw enon lwaz reglawy dyeshu wa'si enon
31	ayj dnetdamron kenshe hanon djasen jarshe dammallin wafshigue dmezjalmin wajguire damhaljin wasmayya djasen wshabajw la'laha disrayyel
32	hu den yeshu kra lzalmidawy we'mar lhon mezrajam na al kensha hana dha tlaza yawmin kawiw lwazy wlayt lhon ma dne'jlon wde'shre enon kad samin la save na dalma nufon b'uwrja
33	amrin leh talmidawy aymeka lan bjurba lajma dansaba kensha hana kuleh
34	amar lhon yeshu kma lajmin iz ljon amrin leh shava wkalil nune dakdke
35	wafkad ljenshe dnestamkon al ara
36	washkal lhalen shava lajmin walnune wshabaj waksa wyav lzalmidawy wzalmide yavw ljenshe
37	we'jalw kulhon wasvaw washkalw tawtare daksayye mle shava espridin
38	hanon den d'ejalw hawen waw arba alfin gavre star men neshe watlayye
39	wjad shra ljenshe slek le'lfa we'za lazjuma dmagdu
