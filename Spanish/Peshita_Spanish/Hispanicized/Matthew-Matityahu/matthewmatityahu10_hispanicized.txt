1	wakra lazresar talmidawy wyav lhon shultana al ruje tanfaza dnapkon walma'sayyu kul ki'v wjurhan
2	dilhon den dazresar shlije shmahe izayhon halen kadmahon shemon dmezkre ki'fa wa'ndre'was aju wyakuv bar savday wyujanan aju
3	wfilipaws wvar-tulmay wza'wma wmatay majsa wyakuv bar jalfay wlabi d'ezkani taday
4	wshemon knanayya wihuda sjaryuta haw d'ashlmeh
5	lhalen tresar shadar yeshu wfaked enon we'mar b'uwrja djanfe la ti'sun wlamdita dshamrayye la telon
6	selw ljon den yatira'iz lwaz erbe d'evadw men bez israyyel
7	wjad asin ton ajresw we'marw dkerbaz malkuza dashmayya
8	krihe asaw wgarbe dakaw wdaywe apekw magan nsavton magan havw
9	la teknon dahva wla si'ma wla njasha bjisaykon
10	wla tarmala lu'wrja wla tarten kutinyan wla msane wla shavta shawe w guer pala saybarteh
11	la'yda den mdita aw kriza da'lin ton lah sha'lw manu shawe bah wzaman hwaw dama dnafkin ton
12	wma da'lin ton lvayta sha'lw shlameh dvayta
13	we'n hu dshawe bayta shlamjon ni'ze lawy en den la shawe shlamjon laykon nefne
14	man dla den mkabel ljon wla shama melaykon kad nafkin ton men bayta aw men kriza ha pesw jela men reglaykon
15	wa'min amar na ljon dla'ra dasdum wadamura nehwe nij byawma ddina aw lamdita ha
16	ha ena mshadar na ljon ayj emre baynay d'ive hwaw hajil jakime ayj jwawaza wzamime ayj yawne
17	esdarw den men bnaynasha mashlmin ljon guer lvez dayyane wvajnushazhon nnagdunajon
18	wakdam higmune wmalke mkarvin ljon metulazy lsahduza dilhon wadamme
19	emazy den dnashlmunajon la ti'spon aykana aw mana tmallon mezihev ljon guer bha shaza ma dazmallon
20	la wa guer aton mmallin ela ruja d'avujon mmalla bjon
21	nashlem den aja la'ju lmawta wa'va lavreh wankumon bnayya al avahayhon wanmizon enon
22	wzehon sni'in men kulnash metul shemy ayna den dansaybar dama ljarza hu nije
23	ma dradpin ljon den bamdita hade rukw ljon lajriza amin guer amar na ljon dla tshalmon enen kulhen mdinaza dvez israyyel dama dni'ze breh dnasha
24	layt talmida dyatir men rabeh wla avda men mareh
25	safek leh lzalmida dnehwe ayj rabeh walavda ayj mareh en lmareh dvayta kraw belsvuv jad kma lavnay bayteh
26	la hajil tedjlon menhon layt guer medem dajse dla nezgle wdamtashay dla nezida
27	medem d'amar na ljon bjeshuja umru aton bnahira wmedem dve'dnaykon shamin ton ajresw al egare
28	wla tedjlon men aylen dkatlin pagra nafsha den la meshkjin lmektal djalw den yatira'iz men man dmeshkaj dalnafsha walfagra nawbed bguihana
29	la tarten seprin mesdabnan b'asar wajda menhen belad men avujon la nafla al ara
30	diljon den af mene drishjon kulhen manyan enen
31	la hajil tedjlon men sefre saguiyya'za myatrin ton
32	kulnash hajil dnawde bi kdam bnaynasha awde beh af ena kdam avy dvashmayya
33	man den dnejpur bi kdam bnaynasha ejpur beh af ena kdam avy dvashmayya
34	la tesbron d'eziz d'arme shayna b'ara la eziz d'arme shayna ela jarba
35	eziz guer d'eflug gavra al avu wvarza al emah wjalza al jmazah
36	waveldvavawy dgavra bnay bayteh
37	man drajem ava aw ema yatir men dli la shawe li wman drajem bra aw barza yatir men dli la shawe li
38	wjul dla shakel skifeh wa'ze bazary la shawe li
39	man d'eshkaj nafsheh nawbdih wman dnawbed nafsheh metulazy neshkjih
40	man damkabel ljon li mkabel wman dli mkabel lman dshaljany mkabel
41	man damkabel nviyya bshem nviyya agra danviyya nasev wman damkabel sadika bshem sadika agra dsadika nasev
42	wjul dmashke ljad men halen sure kasa dkarire baljud bashma dzalmida amin amar na ljon dla nawbed agreh
