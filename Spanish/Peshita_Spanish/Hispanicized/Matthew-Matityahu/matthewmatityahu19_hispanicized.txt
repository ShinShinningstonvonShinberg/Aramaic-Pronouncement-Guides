1	wahwa djad shalem yeshu mele halen shkal men glila we'za lazjuma dihud levra dyurdnan
2	we'zaw bazreh kenshe saguiyye' wa'si enon taman
3	wakrevw lwazeh prishe wamnasen waw leh wa'mrin en shalit lnash dneshre atzeh bjul ela
4	hu den na we'mar lhon la krayton dhaw davad men breshiz dejra wnekbza vad enon
5	we'mar metul hana neshbuk gavra la'vu wle'meh wnekaf la'tzeh wnehon trayhon jad bsar
6	maden la hwaw tren ela jad pgar medem hajil d'alaha saweg barnasha la nfaresh
7	amrin leh lmana hajil mushe paked dnetel kzava dshuvkana wneshrih
8	amar lhon mushe lukval kashyuz lebjon apes ljon dzeshron neshaykon men breshiz den la hwa hajana
9	amar na ljon den dman dshavek atzeh dla gawra wnasev jriza ga'ar wman dnasev shvikta ga'ar
10	amrin leh talmidawy en hajana iz edlayya baynay gavra la'tza la pakaj lmesav atza
11	hu den emar lhon la kulnash safek lah lmelza hade ela man dihiv leh
12	iz guer mhaymne dmen karsa d'emhon eziledw hajana wi'yz mhaymne dmen bnaynasha hwaw mhaymne wi'yz mhaymne dhenon vadw nafshhon mhaymne metul malkuza dashmayya man dmeshkaj dnespak nespak
13	haden karevw leh tlayye dansim ideh layhon wansale waja'w bhon talmidawy
14	hu den yeshu emar lhon shvukw tlayye azen lwazy wla tejlon enon dda'ylen guer d'ayj halen enon izeh malkuza dashmayya
15	wsam ideh layhon we'sal men taman
16	we'za jad krev we'mar leh malfana tava mana dtav ebed dnehon li jayye dalalam
17	hu den emar leh mana kare at li tava layt tava ela en jad alaha en den save at dzeul ljayye tar pukdane
18	amar leh aylen hu den yeshu emar leh dla tektul wla tgur wla tegnuv wla teshad sahduz shukra
19	wyakar la'vuj wle'maj wzajev lkarivaj ayj nafshaj
20	amar leh haw layma halen kulhen netrez enen men talyuzy mana jsir na
21	amar leh yeshu en save at gmira lmehwa sel saben kenyanaj whav lmeskine wzehwe laj simza bashmayya wza bazary
22	shma den haw layma melza hade we'sal kad karya leh iz wa leh guer kenyana saguiyya'
23	yeshu den emar lzalmidawy amin amar na ljon datla y latira dneul lmalkuz shmayya
24	tuv den amarna ljon dadlil u lgamla lmeal bajrura damjata aw atira dneul lmalkuza d'alaha
25	talmide den kad shmaw tahirin waw tav wa'mrin manu kay meshkaj dnije
26	jar bhon yeshu we'mar lhon lwaz bnaynasha hade la meshkja lwaz alaha den kulmedem meshkja
27	haden na ki'fa we'mar leh ha enajnan shvakn kulmedem we'zayn bazraj mana kay nehwe lan
28	amar lhon yeshu amin amar na ljon d'aton d'ezayton bazary balma jaza ma dyazev breh dnasha al tranaws dshuvjeh tetvon af aton al tresar kursawan watdunon tresar shavte d'iysra'yel
29	wjulnash dshavek bate aw aje aw ajwaza aw ava aw ema aw atza aw bnayya aw kurya metul shemy jad bama' nkabel wjayye dalalam ni'raz
30	saguiyye' den kadmayye dnehon jrayye wajrayye kadmayye
