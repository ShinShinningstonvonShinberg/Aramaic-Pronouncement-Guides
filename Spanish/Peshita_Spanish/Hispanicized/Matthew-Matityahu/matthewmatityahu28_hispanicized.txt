1	bramsha den bshabza dnagah jad bshaba ezaz maryam magdlata wmaryam jriza dnejsyan kavra
2	wha sawa raba hwa mala'ja guer dmarya njez men shmayya wakrev aguel ki'fa men tara wyazev wa leh
3	izawy wa den jesweh ayj barka walvusheh jewar wa ayj talga
4	wmen dejlzeh ettsiw aylen dnatrin waw wahwaw ayj mize
5	na den mala'ja we'mar lneshe aten la tedjlan yada na guer dalyeshu d'esdkef bayan ten
6	la hwa tnan kam leh guer aykana d'emar ta'yen jsayyen dukza dsim wa bah maran
7	wselen bagal emaren lzalmidawy dkam men bez mize wha kadem ljon laglila taman tejsuna ha emrez ljen
8	we'sal gal men kavra bdejlza wavjaduza rabza wrahtan dni'mran lzalmidawy
9	wha yeshu pga bhen we'mar lhen shlam ljen henen den krev ejad reglawy wasgueden leh
10	haden emar lhen yeshu la tedjlan ela selen emaren la'jay dni'sun laglila wzaman nejsunany
11	kad esalen den ezaw nasha men kestunare hanon lamdita we'marw lrabay kahne kul medem dahwa
12	we'zkanashw am kashishe wansavw melka wyavw kespa la sur lkestunare
13	wa'mrin lhon emarw dzalmidawy ezaw ganbu blilya kad damkin nan
14	we'n eshtamaz hade kdam higmuna jnan mpisin nan leh waljon dla sefza avdin nan
15	henon den kad nsavw kespa vadw ayj d'alefw enon wnefkaz melza hade bez ihudayye dama lyawmana
16	talmide den jdasar esalw laglila ltura ayka dwaed enon yeshu
17	wjad jsa'u sguedw leh menhon den ezpalagw waw
18	wakrev yeshu malel amhon we'mar lhon ezihev li kul shultan bashmayya wva'ra wa'ykana dshadrany avy mshadar na ljon
19	selw hajil talmedw kulhon amme wa'medw enon bshem ava wavra wruja dkudsha
20	wa'lefw enon dnetron kul ma dfakedtjon wha ena amjon na kulhon yawmaza dama lshulameh dalma amin
