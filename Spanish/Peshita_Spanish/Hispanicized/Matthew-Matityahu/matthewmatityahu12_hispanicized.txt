1	bhaw savna mhalej wa yeshu bshabza bez sare wzalmidawy kfenw wshariw malguin sheble wa'jlin
2	prishe den kad jsaw enon amrin leh ha talmidayk avdin medem dla shalit lmebad bshabza
3	hu den emar lhon la krayton mana vad dawid kad kfen wa'ylen dameh
4	aykana al lvayta d'alaha wlajma dfazureh dmarya ejal haw dla shalit wa leh lme'jal wla la'ylen dameh ela en ljahne baljud
5	aw la krayton b'uwrata djahne bhaykla majlin lah lshabza wadla edla enon
6	amar na ljon den drab men haykla iz harka
7	elu den yadin hwayton manaw jnana save na wla devjza la mjayvin wayton la'ylen dadla edla enon
8	marah guer dshabza izawy breh dnasha
9	wshani men taman yeshu we'za lajnushthon
10	wgavra jad iz wa taman dyabisha ideh wamsha'lin waw leh wa'mrin d'en shalit bshabza lma'sayyu ayj dne'jlon karsawy
11	hu den emar lhon manu menjon gavra d'iyz leh erba jad we'n nafel bjabara byawma dshabza la ajed wamkim leh
12	kma den yatir barnasha men erba maden shalit u bshabza lmebad dshapir
13	haden emar lhaw gavra pshut idaj wafshat ideh wzeknaz ayj jvartah
14	wanfakw prishe wmelka nsavw lawy ayj dnawbduna
15	yeshu den ida wshani leh men taman we'salw bazreh kenshe saguiyye' wa'si ljulhon
16	waj'a bhon dla negluna
17	dnezmale medem d'eze'mar byad eshaya nviyya d'emar
18	ha avdy d'estviz beh jabivy dsewjaz beh nafshy rujy esim lawy wdina lamme najres
19	la nezjre wla neke wla nash neshma kaleh bshuka
20	kanya ria la nezbar washraga damtaftef la nadej dama dnapek dina lsajuza
21	wvashmeh amme nsabron
22	haden karevw leh daywana jad dajresh wawir wa'syeh aykana djarsha wsamya nmalel wnejse
23	wmetdamrin waw kulhon kenshe wa'mrin dalma hanaw breh ddawid
24	prishe den kad shmaw amrin hana la mapek shi'de ela bavelsvuv risha ddaywe
25	yeshu den ida majshvazhon we'mar lhon kul malku dzezpalag al nafshah tejrav wjul bay wamdina dnezpalag al nafshah la nkum
26	we'n satana lsatana mapek al nafsheh ezpalag aykana hajil kama malkuzeh
27	we'n ena bavelsvuv mapek na daywe bnaykon bmana mapkin lhon metul hana henon nehon ljon dayyane
28	we'n bruja d'alaha ena mapek na daywe kerbaz lah laykon malkuza d'alaha
29	aw aykana nash meshkaj dneul lvez jasina wma'nawy nebus ela en lukdam ne'sriwy ljasina whaden bayteh nebus
30	man dla hwa amy lukvaly u wman dla kanesh amy mvadaru mvadar
31	metul hana amar na ljon djul jtahin wgudafin neshtavkon lavnaynasha gudafa den dal ruja la neshtvek lavnaynasha
32	wjul man dni'mar melza al breh dnasha neshtvek leh kul den dal ruja dkudsha ni'mar la neshtvek leh la balma hana wla balma dazid
33	aw vedw ilana shapira wfi'rawy shapire aw vedw ilana bisha wfi'rawy bishe men pi'rawy hu guer mezida ilana
34	yalda d'ajedne aykana meshkjin ton tavaza lammalalu dvishe ton men tawtaray leba guer mmalel puma
35	gavra tava men simaza tavaza mapek tavaza wgavra bisha men simaza bishaza mapek bishaza
36	amar na ljon guer djul mela batala dni'mron bnay nasha netlon pezgamah byawma ddina
37	men melayk guer tesdadak wmen melayk tezjayyav
38	haden naw nasha men safre wmen prishe wa'mrin leh malfana saven nan dnejse menaj aza
39	hu den na we'mar lhon sharbza bishta wgayyarza aza baya wa'za la tezihev lah ela azeh dyawnan nviyya
40	aykana guer dahwa yawnan bjarseh dnuna tlaza yawmin wazlaza laylawan hajana nehwe breh dnasha blebah d'ara tlaza imamin wazlaza laylawan
41	gavre ninwayye nkumon bdina am sharbza hade wanjayvunah dhenon tavw bjarusuzeh dyawnan wha drab men yawnan tnan
42	malkza dzaymna tkum bdina am sharbza hade wazjayvih d'ezaz men evreh d'ara dzeshma jejmzeh dashlemon wha dyatir men shlemawn harka
43	emazy den druja tanfza tepuk men barnasha mezkarka b'azrawaza dmayya layt bhon wvaya nyaja wla meshkja
44	haden amra ehpuj lvayty men ayka dnefkez wa'zya meshkja dasrik wajmim wamsabaz
45	haden asa davra amah shva ruje jranyan dmenah bishan wa'lan wamran beh whawya jarzeh dgavra haw bisha men kadmateh hajana nehwe lah lsharbza hade bishta
46	kad hu den mmalel ljenshe ezaw emeh wa'jawy kamin lvar wvaen danmallon ameh
47	emar leh den nash ha emaj wa'jayk kamin lvar wvaen danmallon amaj
48	hu den na we'mar lman d'emar leh man y emy wman enon ajay
49	wafshat ideh lwaz talmidawy we'mar ha emy wha ajay
50	kulnash guer daved sevyaneh d'avy dvashmayya huyyu ajy wjazy we'my
