1	kad den eziled yeshu bvez-ljem dihuda byawmay herawdes malka ezaw mgushe men madnja lu'wrishlem
2	wa'mrin aykaw malka dihudayye d'eziled jsayn guer kawkveh bmadnja we'zayn lmesgad leh
3	shma den herawdes malka we'ttsi wjulah urishlem ameh
4	wjanesh kulhon rabay kahne wsafre dama wamsha'el wa lhon d'ayka meziled mshija
5	henon den emarw bvez-ljem dihuda hajana guer kziv banviyya
6	af aty bez-ljem dihuda la hwayty bsira bmalke dihuda menejy guer nepuk malka dhu nerewy lamy isra'yel
7	haden herawdes matshya'iz kra lamgushe wilef menhon b'ayna savna ezjsi lhon kawkva
8	wshadar enon lvez-ljem we'mar lhon selw akevw al talya jfita'iz wma d'eshkajtuna taw jawa'uny d'af ena isal esgud leh
9	henon den kad shmaw men malka esalw wha kawkva haw dajsaw bmadnja asel wa kdamayhon dama d'eza kam lel men ayka d'iyzawy talya
10	kad den jsa'u ljawkva jdiw jaduza rabza dtav
11	walw lvayta wajsa'u ltalya am maryam emeh wanfalw sguedw leh wafzajw simazhon wkarevw leh kurbane dahva wmura walvunta
12	we'zjsi lhon bjelma dla nehpjon lwaz herawdes wvu'wrja jriza esalw la'zrhon
13	kad den esalw ezjsi mala'ja dmarya bjelma lyawsef we'mar leh kum dvar ltalya wle'meh waruk lmesren wzaman hwi dama d'amar na laj zid u guer herawdes lmevyeh ltalya ayj dnawbdiwy
14	yawsef den kam shakleh ltalya wle'meh blelya warak lmesren
15	wahwa taman dama lmawteh dherawdes dnezmale medem d'eze'mar men marya banviyya d'amar dmen mesren kriz lvery
16	haden herawdes kad jsa d'ezbasaj men mgushe ezjamaz tav wshadar katel tlayye kulhon dvez-ljem wadjulhon tjumeh men bar tarten shnin walzajt ayj savna dakev men mgushe
17	haden ezmali medem d'eze'mar byad eramya nviyya d'emar
18	kala eshtma bramza bejya we'lya saguiyya' rajel bajya al bneh wla savya lmezbayya'u metul dla izayhon
19	kad miz den herawdes malka ezjsi mala'ja dmarya bjelma lyawsef bmesren
20	we'mar leh kum dvar ltalya wle'meh wsel la'ra d'iysra'yel mizw lhon guer hanon dvaen waw nafsheh dtalya
21	wyawsef kam dvar ltalya wle'meh we'za la'ra d'iysra'yel
22	kad den shma d'arjela'aws hwa malka bihud jlaf herawdes avu djel dni'sal lzaman we'zjsi leh bjelma dni'sal la'zra daglila
23	we'za mar bamdita dmezkarya nasraz ayj dnezmale medem d'eze'mar banviyya dnasrayya nezkre
