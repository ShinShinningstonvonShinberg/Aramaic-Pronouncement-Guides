1	bhaw den savna shma herawdes tetrarja shemeh dyeshu
2	we'mar lavdawy hanaw yujanan mamdana hu kam men bez mize metul hana jayle mestarin beh
3	haw guer herawdes ejad wa lyujanan wa'sreh wa'rmyeh bez asire metul herawdiyya ataz filipaws aju
4	amar wa leh guer yujanan dla shalit dzehwe laj atza
5	wsave wa lmektleh wdajel wa men ama d'ayj dlanviyya ajidin waw leh
6	kad hwa den bez yaldeh dherawdes rakdaz barzah dherawdiyya kdam smije wshefraz leh lherawdes
7	metul hana bmawmaza ima lah dnetel lah kulmedem dzesha'l
8	hi den metul dmalfa waz le'mah emraz hav li harka bfinka risheh dyujanan mamdana
9	wjeryaz leh lmalka metul den mawmaza wasmije pkad dnezihev lah
10	wshadar paskeh risheh dyujanan bez asire
11	wa'yti risheh bfinka we'zihev latliza wa'ytyazeh le'mah
12	wakrevw talmidawy shkalw shladeh kvarw we'zaw jawiw lyeshu
13	yeshu den kad shma shani men taman b'elfa la'zra jurba baljudawy wjad shmaw kenshe esalw bazreh byavsha men mdinaza
14	wanfak yeshu jsa kenshe saguiyye' we'zrajam layhon wa'si krihayhon
15	kad hwa den ramsha krevw lwazeh talmidawy we'marw leh azra jurba hu wedana var leh shri kenshe dnasha dni'sun lkurya wnesbnon lhon saybarta
16	hu den emar lhon la mezbe lhon lmi'sal havw lhon aton lme'jal
17	henon den emarw leh layt lan tnan ela jamesh grisan wazren nunin
18	amar lhon yeshu aytaw enon li lharka
19	wafkad ljenshe lmestmaju al ara washkal hanon jamsha lajmin wazren nunin wjar bashmayya wvarej waksa wyav lzalmidawy whenon talmide samw ljenshe
20	we'jalw kulhon wasvaw washkalw tawtare daksayye tresar kufinin kad mlen
21	hanon den nasha d'ejalw hawen waw alfe jamsha star men neshe watlayye
22	wmejda elas lzalmidawy dneskon lasfita wni'sun kdamawy levra ad share hu ljenshe
23	wjad shra ljenshe slek ltura baljudawy lamsalayyu wjad jeshkaz baljudawy wa taman
24	we'lfa rajika waz men ara estdawaza saguiyye' kad meshtagsha sagui men galle ruja guer lkuvlah waz
25	bmatarza den rviata dlilya eza lwazhon yeshu kad mhalej al mayya
26	wajsa'u talmidawy damhalej al mayya we'ttsiw wa'mrin waw djeswa hu dagala wmen dejlazhon kaw
27	hu den yeshu barshazeh malel amhon we'mar ezlabvw ena na la tedjlon
28	wana ki'fa we'mar leh mary en at u pkud li ize lwazaj al mayya
29	yeshu den emar leh ta wanjez ki'fa men elfa whalej al mayya dni'ze lwaz yeshu
30	wjad jsa ruja dkashya djel wshari lmetba wa'rim kaleh we'mar mary prukayny
31	wvar shazeh pshat ideh maran wa'jdeh we'mar leh sur haymanuza lmana ezpalagt
32	wjad slekw le'lfa shelyaz ruja
33	we'zaw hanon dve'lfa sguedw leh we'marw sharira'iz breh at d'alaha
34	wardaw we'zaw la'ra dguenesar
35	we'shtawdu nasha d'azra haw wshadarw ljulhen kurya dajdarayhon wkarevw leh kulhon aylen dvish bish vidin
36	wvaen waw meneh dnekrvon afen ljenfa baljud dalvusheh wa'ylen dakrevw eza'siw
