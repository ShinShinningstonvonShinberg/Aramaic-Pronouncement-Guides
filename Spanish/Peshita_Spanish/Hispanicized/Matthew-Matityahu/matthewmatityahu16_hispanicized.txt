1	wakrevw prishe wsadukayye mnasen leh wsha'lin leh aza men shmayya danjawe enon
2	hu den na we'mar lhon ma dahwa ramsha amrin ton sajwa hu semkaz guer shmayya
3	wavsafra amrin ton yawmana sazwa hu semkaz guer shmayya kmira'iz nasbay b'ape parsupa dashmayya yadin ton dzevkon azwaza dsavna hana la yadin ton dzefrshon
4	sharbza bishta wgayyarza aza baya wa'za la mezyava lah ela azeh dyawnan nviyya washvak enon we'sal
5	wjad ezaw talmidawy levra taw dnesvon amhon lajma
6	hu den emar lhon jsaw esdarw men jmira dafrishe wadsadukayye
7	henon den mezraen waw bnafshhon wa'mrin dlajma la nsavw
8	yeshu den ida we'mar lhon mana mezjashvin ton bnafshjon suray haymanuza dlajma la shkalton
9	la dama lhasha estakalton la ahdin ton lhanon jamsha lajmin djamsha alfin wajma kufinin shkalton
10	wla lhanon shava lajmin d'arba alfin wajma espridin shkalton
11	aykan la estakalton dla wa al lajma emrez ljon ela dzesdahron men jmira dafrishe wadsadukayye
12	haden estakalw dla emar dnesdahron men jmira dlajma ela men yulpana dafrishe wadsadukayye
13	kad den eza yeshu la'zra dkesariyya'-dfilipaws msha'el wa lzalmidawy wa'mar manu amrin lay nasha d'iyzay breh dnasha
14	henon den emarw iz d'amrin yujanan mamdana jrane den eliyya wajrane eramya aw jad men nviyye
15	amar lhon aton den manu amrin ton d'iyzay
16	na shemon ki'fa we'mar at u mshija breh d'alaha jayya
17	na yeshu we'mar leh tuvayk shemon breh-dyawna dvesra wadma la gla laj ela avy dvashmayya
18	af ena amar na laj d'at u ki'fa wal hade ki'fa evneh lity wzare dashyul la nejsnunah
19	laj etel klide dmalkuza dashmayya wjul medem dze'sur b'ara nehwe asir bashmayya wmedem dzeshre b'ara nehwe shre bashmayya
20	haden paked lzalmidawy dalnash la ni'mron dhuyyu mshija
21	wmen haden shari yeshu lamjawayyu lzalmidawy dazid u dni'sal lu'wrishlem wsagui nejash men kashishe wmen rabay kahne wsafre wnezktel walyawma dazlaza nkum
22	wdavreh ki'fa wshari lmeja' beh we'mar jas laj mary dzehwe laj hade
23	hu den ezpni we'mar lji'fa sel laj lvestary satana tukalta at li dla mezrae at d'alaha ela davnay nasha
24	haden emar yeshu lzalmidawy man dsave dni'ze bazary nejpur bnafsheh wneshkul skifeh wni'ze bazary
25	man dsave guer dnaje nafsheh nawbdih wman dnawbed nafsheh metulazy neshkjih
26	mana guer mezhane barnasha en kuleh alma nekne wnafsheh nejsar aw mana netel barnasha tajlufa dnafsheh
27	zid u guer breh dnasha dni'ze bzeshbujta d'avu am mala'jawy kadishe whaden nefru lnash nash ayj vadawy
28	amin amar na ljon d'iyz nasha dkamin tnan dla netmon mawta dama dnejson lavreh dnasha d'aze bmalkuzeh
