1	waslek le'lfa wavar eza lamditeh
2	wkarevw leh msharya kad rme barsa wajsa yeshu haymanuzhon we'mar lhaw msharya ezlabv bery shvikin laj jtahayk
3	nasha den men safre emarw bnafshhon hana mgadef
4	yeshu den ida majshvazhon we'mar lhon mana mezjashvin ton bishta blebjon
5	mana guer pshik lmi'mar dashvikin laj jtahayk aw lmi'mar kum halej
6	dzedon den dshultana iz lavreh dnasha b'ara lmeshbak jtahe emar lhaw msharya kum shkul arsaj wsel lvaytaj
7	wkam esal lvayteh
8	kad jsaw den kenshe hanon djelw wshabajw la'laha dyav shultana d'ayj hana lavnaynasha
9	wjad var yeshu men taman jsa gavra dyazev bez majse dashmeh matay we'mar leh ta bazary wkam esal bazreh
10	wjad smijin bvayta ezaw majse wjatayye saguiyye' estmejw am yeshu wam talmidawy
11	wjad jsaw prishe amrin lzalmidawy lmana am majse wjatayye laes rabjon
12	yeshu den kad shma emar lhon la snikin jlime al asya ela aylen dvisha'iz vidin
13	selw ilafw manaw jnana bae na wla devjza la guer eziz d'ekre lsadike ela ljatayye
14	haden krevw leh talmidawy dyujanan wa'mrin lmana jnan wafrishe samin nan sagui wzalmidayk la samin
15	amar lhon yeshu dalma meshkjin bnawy dagnuna lamsam kma djazna amhon azen den yawmaza kad neshtkel menhon jazna whaden nsumon
16	la nash rame urkaza jdaza al najta blayya dla tetuf malyuzah men haw najta wnehwe besa yatira
17	wla ramen jamra jaza bseke blayyaza dla mestaryan seke wjamra meze'shed wseke avdan ela ramen jamra jaza bseke jataza wazrayhon meznatrin
18	kad den halen mmalel wa amhon eza arjuna jad krev sgued leh we'mar bazy hasha mizaz ela ta sim idaj leh wzije
19	wkam yeshu wzalmidawy we'salw bazreh
20	wha atza drade wa dmah shnin tartaesre ezaz men bestreh wkerbaz lkarna dalvusheh
21	amra waz guer bnafshah afen baljud lma'neh karba na meza'sya na
22	yeshu den ezpni jsah we'mar lah ezlabvy bazy haymanuzejy ajyazejy we'za'syaz atza ha men ha shaza
23	we'za yeshu lvayteh d'arjuna wajsa samare wjenshe dmeshtagshin
24	we'mar lhon prukw ljon tliza guer la mizaz ela damka y wgajkin waw lawy
25	wjad apek ljenshe al ajdah b'iydah wkamaz tliza
26	wanfak teba hana bjulah ara ha
27	wjad var yeshu men taman davku smayya tren dkaen wa'mrin ezrajam layn breh ddawid
28	wjad eza lvayta krevw leh hanon smayya amar lhon yeshu mhaymnin ton dmeshkaj na hade lmebad amrin leh in maran
29	haden krev laynayhon we'mar aykana dhaymenton nehwe ljon
30	wmejda ezpataj aynayhon waj'a bhon yeshu we'mar jsaw la nash neda
31	henon den nfakw atbu bjulah ara ha
32	wjad nfak yeshu karevw leh jarsha d'iyz lawy daywa
33	wmen danfak daywa malel haw jarsha we'tdamarw kenshe wa'mrin la mzum ezjsi hajana b'isra'yel
34	prishe den amrin waw brisha ddaywe mapek daywe
35	wmezkrej wa yeshu bamdinaza kulhen wavkurya wmalef wa bajnushazhon wmajres svarza dmalkuza wma'se kul kurhanin wjul ki'vin
36	kad jsa den yeshu ljenshe ezrajam layhon dal'en waw washren ayj erbe dlayt lhon raya
37	we'mar lzalmidawy jsada sagui wfale surin
38	baw hajil men mare jsada dnapek pale lajsadeh
