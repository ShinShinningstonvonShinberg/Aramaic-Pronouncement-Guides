1	wvazar shta yawmin dvar yeshu lji'fa walyakuv walyujanan aju wa'sek enon ltura rama baljudayhon
2	we'shtajlaf yeshu kdamayhon wanhar parsupeh ayj shemsha najtawy den jwarw ayj nuhra
3	we'zjsiw lhon mushe we'liyya kad mmallin ameh
4	na den ki'fa we'mar lyeshu mary shapir u lan daznan nehwe we'n save at nebed tnan tlaz matlin jda laj wajda lmushe wajda le'liyya
5	wad hu mmalel ha nana nahirta atlaz layhon wkala hwa men nana d'amar hanaw bery jabiva dveh estviz leh shmaw
6	wjad shmaw talmide nfalw al apayhon wadjelw tav
7	we'zkarav lwazhon yeshu wakrev lhon we'mar kumw la tedjlon
8	wa'rimw aynayhon walnash la jsaw ela en lyeshu baljudawy
9	wjad najtin men tura paked enon yeshu we'mar lhon lin nash la ti'mron jeswa hana dama dankum breh dnasha men mize
10	wsha'lu talmidawy wa'mrin leh mana hajil safre amrin d'eliyya wale dni'ze lukdam
11	na yeshu we'mar eliyya aze lukdam djul medem nshalem
12	amar na ljon den dha eliyya eza wla yadu wavadw beh kul ma dasvaw hajana af breh dnasha zid dnejash menhon
13	haden estakalw talmide dal yujanan mamdana emar lhon
14	wjad ezaw lwaz kensha krev leh gavra wavrej al burkawy
15	we'mar leh mary ezrajam lay bery d'iyz leh bar egara wvisha'iz vid kma guer savnin bnura nafel wajma savnin bmayya
16	wkarevzeh lzalmidayk wla eshkajw lma'sayyuzeh
17	na yeshu we'mar on sharbza dla mhaymna wamakalta dama le'mazy ehwe amjon wadama le'mazy esaybarjon ayta li lja
18	waj'a beh yeshu wanfak meneh shi'da we'za'si talya men ha shaza
19	haden krevw talmide lwaz yeshu baljudawy we'marw leh lmana jnan la eshkajn lma'sayyuzeh
20	amar lhon yeshu metul la haymanuzjon amin guer amar na ljon d'en tehwe bjon haymanuza ayj predta djardla ti'mron ltura hana dshana meka wanshane wmedem la nejsanjon
21	hana den guensa la nafek ela bsawma wvasluza
22	kad mezhapjin den baglila emar lhon yeshu zid u breh dnasha dneshtlem b'iyday bnay nasha
23	wnektluna walyawma dazlaza nkum wjeryaz lhon tav
24	wjad ezaw lajfarnajum krevw hanon dnasbin tren tren susin dajsef risha lwaz ki'fa we'marw leh rabjon la yahev tren susawy
25	amar lhon in wjad al ki'fa lvayta kadmeh yeshu we'mar leh mana mezjse laj shemon malkeh d'ara men man nasbin majsa wajsef risha man bnayhon aw men nujrayye
26	amar leh shemon men nujrayye amar leh yeshu maden bnay ji're enon bnayya
27	dla den najshel enon sel lyama wa'rma balua wnuna kadmayya dsalek pzaj pumeh wzeshkaj estira ha sav whav jlafay wajlafayk
