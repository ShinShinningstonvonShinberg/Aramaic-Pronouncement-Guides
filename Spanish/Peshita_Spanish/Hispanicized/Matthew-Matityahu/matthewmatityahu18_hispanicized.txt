1	bha shaza krevw talmide lwaz yeshu wa'mrin manu kay rav bmalkuza dashmayya
2	wakra yeshu talya wa'kimeh baynazhon
3	we'mar amin amarna ljon d'ela tezhafkon wzehon ayj tlayye la telon lmalkuza dashmayya
4	man hajil dammakej nafsheh ayj hana talya hu nehwe rav bmalkuza dashmayya
5	wman dankabel ayj talya hana bshemy li mkabel
6	wjul dnajshel ljad men halen sure damhaymnin bi pakaj wa leh dzehwe talya rajya dajmara bsawreh wamtaba bumkawy dyama
7	wa lalma men majshule ananki guer dni'zon majshule wa den lgavra dvi'ydeh ni'zon majshule
8	en den idaj aw reglaj majshla laj psukeh washdih menaj tav u laj dzeul ljayye kad jguis at aw kad pshig wla kad iz laj tarten idin aw tarten reglin tepel bnura dalalam
9	we'n hu daynaj majshla laj jsih washdih menaj tav u laj dvajda ayna teul ljayye wla kad iz laj tarten aynin tepel bguihana dnura
10	jsaw la tevson al jad men halen sure amar na ljon guer dmala'jayhon bashmayya bjulsvan jasen parsupeh d'avy dvashmayya
11	eza guer breh dnasha dnaje medem d'abid wa
12	mana mezjse ljon en nehon lnash ma' erbin wnete jad menhon la shavek teshin wzesha btura wa'sel bae lhaw data
13	we'n neshkjeh amin amarna ljon djade beh yatir men teshin wzesha dla taw
14	hajana layt sevyana kdam avujon dvashmayya dni'vad jad men sure halen
15	en den askel baj ajuj sel akesa baynayk wleh baljud en shamaj izart ajuj
16	we'la shamaj dvar amaj jad aw tren dal pum tren aw tlaza sahdin tkum kul mela
17	en den af la lhanon neshma emar lita en den af la lita neshma nehwe laj ayj majsa wa'yj janfa
18	wa'min amar na ljon djul ma dze'sron b'ara nehwe asir bashmayya wmedem dzeshron b'ara nehwe shre bashmayya
19	tuv amarna ljon d'en tren menjon neshton b'ara al kul svu dneshe'lon nehwe lhon men lwaz avy dvashmayya
20	ayka guer dazren aw tlaza knishin bshemy taman ena baynazhon
21	haden krev lwazeh ki'fa we'mar mary kma savnin en naskel bi ajy eshbuk leh dama lashva savnin
22	amar leh yeshu la amar na laj dama lashva ela dama lshavin savnin shva shva
23	metul hana etdamyaz malkuza dashmayya lgavra malka dasva dnesav jushbana men avdawy
24	wjad shari lmesav karevw leh jad djayyav rebu kakrin
25	wjad layt leh lmefra pkad mareh dnesdaban hu wa'tzeh wavnawy wjul medem d'iyz leh wnefru
26	wanfal haw avda sgued leh we'mar mary agar lay ruja wjul medem para na laj
27	we'zrajam mareh davda haw washra wjawbzeh shvak leh
28	nfak den avda haw we'shkaj ljad men knawazeh djayyav wa leh dinare ma' wa'jdeh wjanek wa leh we'mar leh hav li medem djayyav at li
29	wanfal haw knazeh al reglawy ba meneh we'mar leh agar lay ruja wfara na laj
30	hu den la sva ela esal armyeh bez asire dama dnetel leh ma djayyav leh
31	kad jsaw den knawazhon medem dahwa keryaz lhon tav we'zaw awdaw lmarhon kul dahwa
32	haden kra mareh we'mar leh avda bisha ha kulah jawbza shevkez laj davayt meny
33	la wale wa laj af at dazjon lajnazaj aykana d'ena jantaj
34	wargues mareh wa'shlmeh lamnagdane dama dnefru kul medem djayyav leh
35	hajana nebed ljon avy dvashmayya ela teshbkon nash la'ju men lebjon sajluzeh
