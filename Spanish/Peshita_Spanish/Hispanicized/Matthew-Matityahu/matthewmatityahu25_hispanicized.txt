1	haden tedame malkuza dashmayya lasar bzulan hanen dansav lampidayhen wanfak lu'wra jazna wjalza
2	jamesh den menhen jakiman way wjamesh sajlan
3	whanen sajlaza nsav lampidayhen wla nsav amhen meshja
4	hanen den jakimaza nsav meshja bma'ne am lampidayhen
5	kad awjar den jazna nam kulhen wadmej
6	wavfelgueh dlilya hwaz kaza ha jazna aze pukw lu'wreh
7	haden kam kulhen bzulaza halen wzaken lampidayhen
8	amran den hanen sajlaza ljakimaza haven lan men meshjjen dha dejw lhon lampidayn
9	nay halen jakimaza wa'mran lma la nespak lan waljen ela selen lwaz aylen damsabnin wasvenen ljen
10	wjad esal lmesban eza jazna wa'ylen damtayvan way al ameh lvez jlula we'ttjed tara
11	bjarza den ezay af hanen bzulaza jranyaza wa'mran maran maran pzaj lan
12	hu den na we'mar lhen amin amar na ljen dla yada na ljen
13	ettirw hajil dla yadin ton lyawma haw wla lshaza
14	ayj gavra guer dajsak kra lavdawy wa'shlem lhon kenyaneh
15	iz dyav leh jamesh kakrin wi'yz dzarten wi'yz dajda nash nash ayj jayleh wajsak mejda
16	esal den haw dansav jamesh kakrin ettagar bhen wizar jamesh jranyan
17	whajwaz af haw dzarten ettagar tarten jranyan
18	haw den dansav jda esal jfar b'ara wtashi kespa dmareh
19	bazar den savna saguiyya' eza marhon davde hanon wansav menhon jushbana
20	wakrev haw dansav wa jamesh kakrin wkarev jamesh jranyan we'mar mary jamesh kakrin yavt li ha jamesh jranyan ettagrez layhen
21	amar leh mareh iw avda tava wamhaymna al kalil mhayman wayt al sagui akimaj ul ljaduzeh dmaraj
22	wakrev haw dzarten kakrawy we'mar mary tarten kakrin yavt li ha tarten jranyan ettagrez layhen
23	amar leh mareh iw avda tava wamhaymna al kalil mhayman wayt al sagui akimaj ul ljaduzeh dmaraj
24	krev den af haw dansav jda kakra we'mar mary yada wiz laj dgavra at kashya wjased at ayka dla srat wamjanesh at men ayka dla badart
25	wdejlez we'sez tashizah kakraj b'ara ha iz laj dilaj
26	na mareh we'mar leh avda bisha wajvanana yada wayt djased na ayka dla serez wamjanesh na men ayka dla badrez
27	wale wa laj dzarme kespy al pazura wa'ze wiz ena wzava wiz dily am rebyazeh
28	savw hajil meneh kakra uvuh lhaw d'iyz leh sar kakrin
29	lman guer d'iyz leh nezihev leh wnettawsaf leh whaw den dlayt leh wa'f haw d'iyz leh neshtkel meneh
30	walavda batila apku ljeshuja barayya taman nehwe bejya wjurak shene
31	ma d'aze den breh dnasha bshuvjeh wjulhon mala'jawy kadishe ameh haden netev al tranaws dshuvjeh
32	wnezkanshon kdamawy kulhon amme wanfaresh enon jad men jad ayj raya damfaresh erbe men gdayya
33	wankim erbe men yamineh wagdayya men semaleh
34	haden ni'mar malka lhanon dmen yamineh taw brijawy d'avy irazw malkuza dazida waz ljon men tarmyazeh dalma
35	kefnez guer wyavton li lme'jal washiz wa'shkitunany ajsnayya wiz wjaneshtunany
36	artelayya wiz wjasitunany krih wiz wasartunany wvez asire wiz we'zayton lwazy
37	haden ni'mron leh hanon sadike maran emazy jsaynaj dajfen at wzarsinaj aw dashe at wa'shkinaj
38	we'mazy jsaynaj d'ajsnayya at wjaneshnaj aw dartela at wjasinaj
39	we'mazy jsaynaj kriha aw bez asire we'zayn lwazaj
40	wana malka we'mar lhon amin amar na ljon dajma davadton ljad men halen ajay sure li u vadton
41	haden ni'mar af lhanon dmen semaleh selw ljon meny lite lnura dalalam ha damtayva la'jelkarsa walmala'jawy
42	kefnez guer wla yavton li lme'jal washiz wla ashkitunany
43	wa'jsnayya wiz wla kaneshtunany wartelayya wiz wla kasitunany wajriha wiz wvez asire wiz wla sartunany
44	haden nenon af henon wni'mron maran emazy jsaynaj kafna aw sahya aw ajsnayya aw artelayya aw kriha aw bez asire wla shameshnaj
45	haden nene wni'mar lhon amin amar na ljon dajma dla vadton ljad men halen sure af la li vadton
46	wni'sun halen lzashnika dalalam wsadike ljayye dalalam
