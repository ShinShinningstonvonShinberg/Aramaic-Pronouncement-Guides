1	wanfak yeshu men haykla lmi'sal wakrevw talmidawy mjawen waw leh benyaneh dhaykla
2	hu den emar lhon la ha jasen ton halen kulhen amin amar na ljon dla teshtvek harka ki'f al ki'f dla testzar
3	wjad izev yeshu al tura dsayte krevw talmidawy wa'mrin baynayhon wleh emar lan emazy halen nehwyan wmana y aza dme'zizaj wadshulameh dalma
4	na yeshu we'mar lhon esdarw la nash natejon
5	saguiyye' guer ni'zon bshemy wni'mron d'ena na mshija wsaguiyye' neton
6	zidin ton den lmeshma ki'rse wshema dakrave jsaw la tetdawdon wale guer djulhen nehwyan ela la dakil shulama
7	nkum guer ama al ama wmalkuza al malkuza wnehon kafne wmawtane wsawe bduka duka
8	halen den kulhen risha enen djevle
9	haden nashlmunajon lu'wlsane wnektlunajon wzehon sni'in men kulhon amme metul shemy
10	haden nezkashlon saguiyye' wnesnon jad ljad wnashlmon jad ljad
11	wsaguiyye' nviyye dagale nkumon wnaton lsaguiyye'
12	wmetul sagui'uz awla nfug juba dsaguiyye'
13	man dansaybar den dama ljarza hu nije
14	wzezkres hade svarza dmalkuza bjuleh alma lsahduza djulhon amme whaden ni'ze shulama
15	ma den dajsayton aza tanfza djurba d'eze'mar bdani'yel nviyya dkama bdukza kadishta haw dkare nestakal
16	haden aylen dvihud enon nerkon ltura
17	whaw dve'gara hu la nejuz lmesav davvayteh
18	wa'yna davjakla hu la nezhfej lvestreh lmesav lvasheh
19	wa den lvatnaza wla'ylen dmaynkan bhanon yawmaza
20	salaw den dla nehwe rukijon bsazwa wla bshabza
21	nehwe guer haden ulsana raba ayna dla hwa men rishizeh dalma wadama lhasha wla nehwe
22	we'lu la ezkariw yawmaza hanon la jayye wa kul bsar metul gvayya den nezkaron yawmaza hanon
23	haden en nash ni'mar ljon ha harka hu mshija aw harka la thaymnon
24	nkumon guer mshije dagale wanviyye djadavuza wnetlon azwaza rawrvaza ayj dnaton en meshkja af lagvayya
25	ha kadmez emrez ljon
26	en hajil ni'mron ljon ha bjurba hu la tepkon aw dha bzawana hu la thaymnon
27	aykana guer dvarka nafek men madnja wmezjse dama lmarva hajana tehwe me'zizeh davreh dnasha
28	ayka d'en nehwe pagra taman nezkanshon neshre
29	mejda den bazar ulsana dyawmaza hanon shemsha nejshaj wsahra la njawe nuhreh wjawkve neplon men shmayya wjayle dashmayya nettsion
30	whaden nezjse nisheh davreh dnasha bashmayya whaden narkdan kulhen sharbaza d'ara wnejson lavreh dnasha d'aze al nanay shmayya am jayla wshuvja saguiyya'
31	wanshadar mala'jawy am shifura raba wanjanshon lagvayya dileh men arbaz ruje men rishhon dashmayya wadama lrishhon
32	men tita den ilafw pele'za dmejda dsawkeh rajan wfarin tarfeh yadin ton damta kayta
33	hajana af aton ma dajsayton halen kulhen daw damtaz lah lzara
34	amin amar na ljon dla tebar sharbza hade dama dhalen kulhen nehwyan
35	shmayya wa'ra nebron wmelay la nebran
36	al yawma den haw wal shaza ha nash la yada afla mala'je dashmayya ela ava baljud
37	aykana den dyawmay nuj hajana tehwe me'zizeh davreh dnasha
38	aykana guer d'iyzayhon waw kdam tawfana ajlin wshazen wnasbin neshe wyahbin lgavre dama lyawma dal nuj ljewela
39	wla idaw dama d'eza tawfana washkal ljulhon hajana tehwe me'zizeh davreh dnasha
40	haden tren nehon bakriza jad netdvar wjad neshtvek
41	wzarten nehwyan tajnan brajya jda metdavra wajda meshtavka
42	ettirw hajil dla yadin ton b'ayda shaza aze marjon
43	hade den daw d'elu yada wa mare bayta b'ayda matarza aze ganava mettir wa wla shavek wa dnezplesh bayteh
44	metul hana af aton hwaw mtayvin davshaza dla savrin ton ni'ze breh dnasha
45	manu kay izawy avda mhaymna wjakima d'akimeh mareh al bnay bayteh dnetel lhon saybarta bsavnah
46	tuvawy lavda haw dni'ze mareh neshkjiwy daved hajana
47	amin amar na ljon dankimiwy al kul d'iyz leh
48	en den ni'mar avda haw bisha blebeh dmary mawjar lme'za
49	wanshare lmemja knawazeh wnehwe ajel wshaze am rawayye
50	ni'ze mareh davda haw byawma dla savar wavshaza dla yada
51	wneflguiwy wansim mnazeh am nasbay b'ape taman nehwe bejya wjurak shene
