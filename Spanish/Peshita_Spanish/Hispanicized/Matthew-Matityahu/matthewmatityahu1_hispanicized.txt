1	kzava diliduzeh dyeshu mshija breh ddawid breh d'avraham
2	avraham awled li'ysjak isjak awled lyakuv yakuv awled lihuda wla'jawy
3	ihuda awled lfars walsarj men tamar pars awled ljesrawn jesron awled la'ram
4	aram awled laminadav aminadav awled lnajshawn najshon awled lsalmawn
5	salmawn awled lvaas men rajav baas awled luvid men rauz uvid awled li'yshay
6	ishay awled ldawid malka dawid awled lashlemawn men atzeh d'uwriyya
7	shlemon awled larjavam rjavam awled la'viyya aviyya awled la'sa
8	asa awled lyahushafat yahushafat awled lyuram yuram awled lusiyya
9	usiyya awled lyuzam yuzam awled la'jas ajas awled ljesakya
10	jesakya awled lamnashe mnashe awled la'mon amon awled lyushiyya
11	yushiyya awled lyujanya wla'jawy bgaluza dvavel
12	men bazar galuza den dvavel yujanya awled lshelazi'yl shelazi'yl awled lsurbavel
13	surbavel awled la'viyyud aviyyud awled le'lyakim elyakim awled lasur
14	asur awled lsaduk saduk awled la'jin ajin awled le'liyyud
15	eliyyud awled le'liasar eliasar awled lmazan mazan awled lyakuv
16	yakuv awled lyawsef gavrah dmaryam dmenah eziled yeshu dmezkre mshija
17	kulhen hajil sharbaza men avraham dama ldawid sharbaza arbaesre wmen dawid dama lgaluza dvavel sharbaza arbaesre wmen galuza dvavel dama lamshija sharbaza arbaesre
18	yaldeh den dyeshu mshija hajana hwa kad mjira waz maryam emeh lyawsef adla neshtawtfon eshtajjaz batna men ruja dkudsha
19	yawsef den balah ki'na wa wla sva danfarseh we'zrai wa dmatshya'iz neshreh
20	kad halen den ezrai ezjsi leh mala'ja dmarya bjelma we'mar leh yawsef breh ddawid la tedjal lmesav lmaryam atzaj haw guer d'eziled bah men ruja hu dkudsha
21	ti'lad den bra wzekre shmeh yeshu hu guer najewy lameh men jtahayhon
22	hade den kulah dahwaz dnezmale medem d'eze'mar men marya byad nviyya
23	dha bzulta tevtan wzi'lad bra wnekron shmeh amanu'il dmettargam aman alahan
24	kad kam den yawsef men shenzeh vad aykana dafkad leh mala'jeh dmarya wdavrah la'tzeh
25	wla jajmah dama diledzeh lavrah bujra wakraz shmeh yeshu
