1	jurw den bsedkazjon dla tebdunah kdam bnay nasha ayj dzezjson lhon we'la agra layt ljon lwaz avujon dvashmayya
2	emazy hajil daved at sedkza la tekre karna kdamayk ayj davdin nasbay b'ape bajnushaza wavshuke ayj dneshtabjon men bnay nasha wa'min amar na ljon dkabelw agrhon
3	at den ma daved at sedkza la teda semalaj mana avda yaminaj
4	ayj dzehwe sedkzaj bjesya wa'vuj djase bjesya hu nefraj bguelya
5	wma damsale at la tehwe ayj nasbay b'ape drajmin lamkam bajnushaza wavsawyaza dshuke lamsalayyu dnezjson lavnay nasha wa'min amar na ljon dkabelw agrhon
6	at den emazy damsale at ul lzawanaj wa'jud taraj wsala la'vuj davjesya wa'vuj djase bjesya nefraj bguelya
7	wma damsalen ton la hwayton mfakkin ayj janfe savrin guer davmamlla saguiyya' meshtamin
8	la hajil tedamon lhon avujon guer yada mana mezbe ljon adla teshe'luna
9	hajana hajil salaw aton avon dvashmayya nezkadash shmaj
10	ti'ze malkuzaj nehwe sevyanaj aykana dvashmayya af b'ara
11	hav lan lajma dsunkanan yawmana
12	washvuk lan jawbayn aykana d'af jnan shvakn ljayyavayn
13	wla talan lnesyuna ela pasan men bisha metul ddilaj y malkuza wjayla wzeshbujta lalam almin
14	en guer teshbkon lavnaynasha sajlwazhon neshbuk af ljon avujon dvashmayya
15	en den la teshbkon lavnaynasha afla avujon shavek ljon sajlwazjon
16	emazy den dsamin ton la tehon kmire ayj nasbay b'ape mjablin guer parsupayhon ayj dnezjson lavnaynasha dsamin wa'min amar na ljon dkabelw agrhon
17	at den ma dsa'em at ashig apayk wamshuj rishaj
18	ayj dla tezjse lavnaynasha dsa'em at ela la'vuj davjesya wa'vuj djase bjesya hu nefraj
19	la tsimon ljon simaza b'ara azar dsasa wa'jla mjablin wa'yka dganave palshin wganbin
20	ela simw ljon simaza bashmayya ayka dla sasa wla ajla mjablin wa'yka dganave la palshin wla ganbin
21	ayka guer d'iyzeh simazjon taman w af lebjon
22	shraga dfagra izeh ayna en aynaj hajil tehwe pshita af kuleh pagraj nahir u
23	en den aynaj tehwe bisha kuleh pagraj jeshuja nehwe en hajil nuhra dvaj jeshuja hu jeshujaj kma nehwe
24	la nash meshkaj lazren marawan lmeflaj aw guer ljad nesne wlajrina nerjam aw ljad nyakar wlajrina nshut la meshkjin ton la'laha lmeflaj walmamuna
25	metul hana amar na ljon la ti'spon lnafshjon mana te'jlon wmana teshton wla lfagrjon mana telbshon la ha nafsha yatira men saybarta wfagra men lvusha
26	jurw bfarajza dashmayya dla sarin wla jasdin wla jamlin b'awsre wa'vujon dvashmayya mzarse lhon la ha aton myatrin ton menhon
27	manu den menjon kad yasef meshkaj lmawsafu al kawmzeh amza jda
28	wal lvusha mana yaspin ton ezbakaw bshushane ddavra aykana ravyan dla la'yan wla aslan
29	amar na ljon den d'afla shlemawn bjuleh shuvjeh ezkasi ayj jda menhen
30	en den lamira djakla dyawmana izawy wamjar nafel bzanura alaha hajana malbesh la sagui yatir ljon suray haymanuza
31	la hajil ti'spon aw ti'mron mana ne'jul aw mana neshte aw mana nezkase
32	kulhen guer halen amme w baen lhen avujon den dvashmayya yada d'af ljon mezbayan halen kulhen
33	baw den lukdam malkuzeh d'alaha wsadikuzeh wjulhen halen mettawsfan ljon
34	la hajil ti'spon damjar hu guer mjar yasef dileh safek leh lyawma bishteh
