1	wjad krev lu'wrishlem we'za lvez-pague al guev tura dsayte shadar yeshu tren men talmidawy
2	we'mar lhon selw lakriza hade dalkuvaljon wmejda meshkjin ton jmara d'asira wila amah shraw aytaw li
3	we'n nash amar ljon medem emarw leh dalmaran mezben wmejda mshadar lhon lja
4	hade den dahwaz dnezmale medem d'eze'mar byad nviyya d'emar
5	emarw lvaz sehyon ha malkejy aze lejy makij warjiv al jmara wal ila bar azana
6	we'salw talmide wavadw aykana dafkad lhon yeshu
7	wa'ytiw lajmara walila wsamw al ila najtayhon warjev lawy yeshu
8	wsuga' djenshe mshawen waw ma'nayhon b'uwrja jrane den paskin waw sawke men ilane wramen b'uwrja
9	kenshe den aylen d'asin waw kdamawy wa'zen bazreh kaen waw wa'mrin ushana lavreh ddawid brij u d'aze bashmeh dmarya ushana bamrawme
10	wjad al lu'wrishlem ettsiaz kulah mdita wa'mrin waw manu hana
11	kenshe den amrin waw hanaw yeshu nviyya dmen nasraz daglila
12	wal yeshu lhaykla d'alaha wa'pek ljulhon dsavnin wamsabnin bhaykla wsajef pazure damarfane wjursawaza dhanon damsabnin yawne
13	we'mar lhon kziv u dvayty bez sluza nezkre aton den vadtuna marza dlestayye
14	wkarevw leh bhaykla smayya wajguise wa'si enon
15	kad jsaw den rabay kahne wafrishe tedmraza daved watlayye dkaen bhaykla wa'mrin ushana lavreh ddawid ezbe'sh lhon
16	wa'mrin leh shama at mana amrin halen amar lhon yeshu in men mzum la krayton dmen puma datlayye wadyalude takent teshbujta
17	washvak enon wanfak lvar men mdita lvez-anya wvaz taman
18	bsafra den kad hfaj lamdita kfen
19	wajsa tita jda b'uwrja we'za lwazah wla eshkaj bah medem ela en tarfe baljud we'mar lah la nehon bejy tuv pi're lalam wmejda yevshaz tita ha
20	wajsaw talmide wazharw wa'mrin aykana bar shazeh yevshaz tita
21	na yeshu we'mar lhon amin amar na ljon d'en tehwe bjon haymanuza wla tezpalgon la baljud hade dzita tebdon ela afen ltura hana ti'mron d'eshtal wfel byama tehwe
22	wjul medem dzeshe'lon basluza wazhaymnon tesvon
23	wjad eza yeshu lhaykla krevw leh rabay kahne wkashishe dama kad malef wa'mrin leh b'ayna shultan halen aved at wmanu yav laj shultana hana
24	na yeshu we'mar lhon esha'eljon af ena melza jda we'n ti'mron li wa'f ena amar na ljon b'ayna shultana halen aved na
25	mamudizeh dyujanan aymeka izeh men shmayya y aw men bnaynasha henon den mezraen waw bnafshhon wa'mrin d'en ni'mar men shmayya amar lan wal mana la haymentuna
26	wadni'mar men bnaynasha dajlin enajnan men kensha kulhon guer ayj nviyya ajidin waw leh lyujanan
27	naw wa'mrin leh la yadinan amar lhon yeshu af la ena amar na ljon b'ayna shultana halen aved na
28	mana den mezjse ljon gavra jad iz waw leh bnayya tren wakrev lwaz kadmayya we'mar leh bery sel yawmana pluj bjarma
29	hu den na we'mar la save na bjarza den ettwi we'sal
30	wakrev lwaz jrina we'mar leh hajwaz hu den na we'mar ena mary wla esal
31	manu men halen trayhon vad sevyana d'avu amrin leh haw kadmayya amar lhon yeshu amin amar na ljon dmajse wsanyaza kadmin ljon lmalkuza d'alaha
32	eza guer lwazjon yujanan b'uwrja dji'nuza wla haymentuna majse den wsanyaza haymnu aton den af la kad jsayton ettwiton bjarza dazhaymnon beh
33	shmaw jrina mazla gavra jad iz wa mare bayta wansav karma wa'jdreh syaga wajfar beh masarta wavna beh magdla wa'wjdeh lfalaje wajsak
34	kad den mta savna dfi're shadar lavdawy lwaz palaje danshadron leh men pi're djarmeh
35	we'jadw palaje lavdawy wi'yz damja'u wi'yz dragmu wi'yz dkatlu
36	wzuv shadar jrane avde dsagui'in men kadmayye whajwaz vadw lhon
37	jrayyaz den shadar lwazhon lavreh kad amar kvar nevhzon men bery
38	palaje den kad jsa'u lavra emarw baynazhon hanaw yarta taw nektliwy wne'jud yartuzeh
39	we'jadw apku lvar men karma wkatlu
40	ma d'eza hajil mareh djarma mana nebed lfalaje hanon
41	amrin leh dvish bish nawbed enon wjarma nawjed lajrane palaje aylen dyahbin leh pi're bsavnhon
42	amar lhon yeshu la memzum krayton bajzava dji'fa d'asliw banayye hi hwaz lrisha dsawiza men lwaz marya hwaz hade wi'yzeh tedmurta baynayn
43	metul hana amar na ljon dzeshtkel menjon malkuza d'alaha wzezihev lama daved pi're
44	wman dnepel al ki'fa hade nezra wjul man dhi tepel lawy tedrewy
45	wjad shmaw rabay kahne wafrishe mazlawy idaw dalayhon emar
46	wavaw lme'jdeh wadjelw men kensha metul d'ayj dlanviyya ajidin waw leh
