1	wana tuv yeshu bmazle we'mar
2	etdamyaz malkuza dashmayya lgavra malka davad meshtuza lavreh
3	wshadar lavdawy dnekron lamsamne lmeshtuza wla svaw lme'za
4	tuv shadar avde jrane we'mar emarw lamsamne dha sharuzy mtayva wzawray wamfatmay ktilin wjul medem mtayyav taw lmeshtuza
5	henon den bsaw we'salw iz dlakrizeh wi'yz dalze'gurteh
6	sharka den ejadw lavdawy wsaarw wkatelw
7	kad shma den malka rgues wshadar jaylawazeh awbed lkatule hanon wlamdithon awked
8	haden emar lavdawy meshtuza mtayva whanon damsamnin waw la shawen waw
9	selw hajil lmapkane d'uwrjaza wjul man dmeshkjin ton kraw lmeshtuza
10	wanfakw avde hanon lu'wrjaza wjaneshw kul d'eshkajw bishe wtave we'zmli bez meshtuza smije
11	wal malka dnejse smije wajsa taman gavra dla lvish lvushe dmeshtuza
12	we'mar leh javry aykana alt lja kad najte dmeshtuza layt laj hu den eshtatak
13	haden emar malka lamshamshane asurw idawy wreglawy wa'pku ljeshuja barayya taman nehwe bejya wjurak shene
14	sagui'in enon guer krayya wasurin gvayya
15	haden esalw prishe nsavw melka d'aykana nsuduna bmelza
16	wshadarw lwazeh talmidayhon am dvez herawdes wa'mrin leh malfana yadinan dsharir at u'wrja d'alaha bkushta malef at wla shkil at sefza dnash la guer nasev at b'ape dnasha
17	emar lan hajil aykana mezjse laj shalit lmetal ksef risha lkesar aw la
18	yeshu den ida bishuzhon we'mar mana mnasen ton li nasbay b'ape
19	jawa'uny dinara dajsef risha henon den karevw leh dinara
20	we'mar lhon yeshu dmanu salma hana wajzava
21	amrin dkesar emar lhon havw hajil dkesar lkesar wda'laha la'laha
22	wjad shmaw etdamarw wshavku we'salw
23	bhaw yawma krevw sadukayye wa'mrin leh layt jayyaz mize wsha'lu
24	wa'mrin leh malfana mushe emar lan d'en nash nmuz kad layt leh bnayya nesav aju atzeh wankim sara la'ju
25	iz waw den lwazan aje shava kadmayya shkal atza wmiz wadlayt wa leh bnayya shavkah atzeh la'ju
26	hajwaz af haw dazren wa'f haw dazlaza wadama lshavazayhon
27	bjarza den djulhon mizaz af atza
28	bakyamta hajil la'yna men halen shava tehwe atza kulhon guer nasbuh
29	na yeshu we'mar lhon taen ton dla yadin ton kzave wla jayleh d'alaha
30	bakyamta guer dmize la nasbin neshe afla neshe hawyan lgavre ela ayj mala'je d'alaha bashmayya izayhon
31	al kyamta den dmize la krayton medem d'eze'mar ljon men alaha d'emar
32	d'ena na alaheh d'avraham alaheh d'iysjak alaheh dyakuv wa'laha la wa dmize ela djayye
33	wjad shmaw kenshe mettamhin waw byulpaneh
34	prishe den kad shmaw dshatek lsadukayye ezkanashw ajjda
35	wsha'leh jad menhon dyada namusa kad mnase leh
36	malfana ayna pukdana rav bnamusa
37	yeshu den emar leh dzerjam lmarya alahaj men kuleh lebaj wmen kulah nafshaj wmen kuleh jaylaj wmen kuleh reyanaj
38	hanaw pukdana raba wkadmayya
39	wdazren ddame leh dzerjam lkarivaj ayj nafshaj
40	bhalen tren pukdanin talya urata wanviyye
41	kad knishin den prishe sha'el enon yeshu
42	we'mar mana amrin ton al mshija bar manu amrin leh bar dawid
43	amar lhon wa'ykana dawid bruj kare leh marya amar guer
44	d'emar marya lmary tev laj men yaminy dama d'esim beldvavayk tjez reglayk
45	en hajil dawid kare leh marya aykana breh u
46	wla nash eshkaj dnetel leh pezgama wla nash amraj tuv men haw yawma lamsha'aluzeh
