2	bdina guer ddanin ton tetdinon wvajyalta damjilin ton mettjil ljon
3	mana den jase at guela davayneh d'ajuj wkariza davaynaj la bajar at
4	aw aykana amar at la'juj shvuk apek guela men aynaj wha karita baynaj
5	nasev b'ape apek lukdam karita men aynaj whaden nezbjar laj lmapaku guela men ayneh d'ajuj
6	la tetlon kudsha ljalbe wla tarmon marganyazjon kdam jsire dalma ndushon enen breglayhon wnehpjon nvasunajon
7	sha'lw wnezihev ljon baw wzeshkjon kushw wnezpzaj ljon
8	kul guer dsha'el nasev wadvae meshkaj wla'yna dnakesh mezpzaj leh
9	aw manu menjon gavra dneshe'liwy breh lajma lma ki'fa mawshet leh
10	we'n nuna neshe'liwy lma jewya mawshet leh
11	we'n hajil aton dvishe ton yadin ton mawhvaza tavaza lmetal lavnaykon kma yatira'iz avujon dvashmayya netel tavaza la'ylen dsha'lin leh
12	kul ma dsaven ton dnebdon ljon bnay nasha hajana af aton vedw lhon hanaw guer namusa wanviyye
13	ulw bzara alisa dafze w tara wa'rwija urja ayda dmawbla la'vdana wsaguiyye' enon aylen d'asin bah
14	ma katin tara wa'lisa urja dmawbla ljayye wasure enon aylen dmeshkjin lah
15	esdarw men nviyye dagale d'azen lwazjon balvushe d'emre men lgaw den izayhon d'ive jatufe
16	men pi'rayhon den tedon enon lma laktin men kube enbe aw men kurtbe ti'ne
17	hajana kul ilana tava pi're shapire aved ilana den bisha pi're bishe aved
18	la meshkaj ilana tava pi're bishe lmebad wla ilana bisha pi're tave lmebad
19	kul ilana dla aved pi're tave mezpsek wavnura nafel
20	maden men pi'rayhon tedon enon
21	la wa kul d'amar li mary mary a'el lmalkuza dashmayya ela man daved sevyaneh d'avy dvashmayya
22	saguiyye' ni'mron li bhaw yawma mary mary la bashmaj eznabin wvashmaj shi'de apekn wvashmaj jayle saguiyye' vadn
23	whaden awde lhon dmen mzum la idatjon arjekw ljon meny paljay awla
24	kul hajil dshama melay halen waved lhen netdame lgavra jakima haw davna bayteh al shua
25	wanjez metra we'zaw nahrawaza wanshav ruje we'ztariw beh bvayta haw wla nfal sheze'sawy guer al shua siman way
26	wjul man dshama melay halen wla aved lhen netdame lgavra sajla davna bayteh al jala
