1	kad den hwa safra melka nsavw al yeshu kulhon rabay kahne wkashishe dama ayj danmizuna
2	wa'sru wa'wblu wa'shlmu lpilataws higmuna
3	haden ihuda mashlmana kad jsa d'ezjayyav yeshu ettwi we'sal ahpej halen tlazin djespa lrabay kahne walkashishe
4	we'mar jtiz d'ashlmez dma sakayya henon den emarw leh lan ma lan at yada at
5	washda kespa bhaykla wshani we'sal jnak nafsheh
6	rabay kahne den shaklu ljespa we'marw la shalit dnarmewy bez kurbana metul dtimay dma hu
7	wansavw melka wasvanw beh agurseh dfajara lvez kvura d'ajsnayye
8	metul hana ezkri agursa haw kriza dadma dama lyawmana
9	haden ezmali medem d'eze'mar byad nviyya d'emar dnesbez tlazin djespa dmawy dyakira dkasw men bnay israyyel
10	wyehbez enon la'gurseh dfajara ayj dafkad li marya
11	hu den yeshu kam kdam higmuna wsha'leh higmuna we'mar leh at u malka dihudayye amar leh yeshu at emart
12	wjad ajlin waw karsawy rabay kahne wkashishe medem pezgama hu la pani
13	haden emar leh pilataws la shama at kma mashdin layk
14	wla yav leh pezgama wla bajda mela wal hade etdamar tav
15	bjul i'da den mad wa higmuna dneshre asira jad lama ayna dhenon saven waw
16	asir wa lhon den asira idia dmezkre bar-aba
17	wjad knishin emar lhon pilataws lman saven ton d'eshre ljon lvar-aba aw lyeshu dmezkre mshija
18	yada wa guer pilataws dmen jsama ashlmu
19	kad izev den higmuna al bim dileh sheljaz leh atzeh wa'mra leh la laj walhaw sadika sagui guer jeshez bjelmy yawmana metulazeh
20	rabay kahne den wkashishe apisw ljenshe dneshe'lon lvar-aba lyeshu den dnawbdon
21	wana higmuna we'mar lhon lman saven ton d'eshre ljon men trayhon henon den emarw lvar-aba
22	amar lhon pilataws walyeshu dmezkre mshija mana ebed leh amrin kulhon nesdkef
23	amar lhon higmuna mana guer dvish vad henon den yatira'iz kaw we'marw nesdkef
24	pilataws den kad jsa dmedem la mawtar ela yatira'iz rawba hawe shkal mayya ashig idawy lin kensha we'mar mjasay na men dmeh dhana sadika aton tedon
25	wanaw kuleh ama we'marw dmeh layn wal bnayn
26	haden shra lhon lvar-aba wnagued bafraguele lyeshu wa'shlmeh dnesdkef
27	haden estratiyyute dhigmuna davru lyeshu lapretawrin wajnashw lawy ljuleh espir
28	wa'shlju wa'lbshu jlamis dasjuriza
29	wagdalw klila dawsnayye wsamw brisheh wkanya byamineh wavrajw al burkayhon kdamawy wamvasjin waw beh wa'mrin shlam malka dihudayye
30	wrakw bparsupeh washkalw kanya wmajen waw leh al risheh
31	wjad basajw beh ashlju jlamis wa'lbshu najtawy wa'wblu dnesdkef
32	wjad nafkin eshkajw gavra kurinayya dashmeh shemon lhana shajarw dneshkul skifeh
33	we'zaw ldukza dmezkarya gagulta ha dmezpashka karkafza
34	wyavw leh dneshte jala dajlit bamrarta watem wla sva lmeshta
35	wjad sakpu palegw najtawy bfesa
36	wyazbin waw wnatrin leh taman
37	wsamw lel men risheh elza dmawteh bajzava hanaw yeshu malka dihudayye
38	we'sdkefw ameh tren lestayye jad men yamineh wjad men semaleh
39	aylen den davrin waw mgadfin waw lawy wamnidin rishayhon
40	wa'mrin sazar haykla wvane leh lazlaza yawmin pasa nafshaj en breh at d'alaha wjuz men skifa
41	hajwaz af rabay kahne mvasjin waw am safre wkashishe wafrishe
42	wa'mrin lajrane aji nafsheh la meshkaj lmajayyu en malkeh u d'iysrayyel nejuz hasha men skifa wanhaymen beh
43	tjil al alaha nefrkiwy hasha en save beh amar guer davreh na d'alaha
44	hajwaz af gayyase hanon d'esdkefw ameh mjasdin waw leh
45	men shez shain den hwa jeshuja al kulah ara dama lshae tsha
46	wla'pay tsha shain ka yeshu bkala rama we'mar il il lmana shvaktany
47	nashin den men hanon dkamin waw taman kad shmaw amrin waw hana li'liyya kra
48	wvah bshaza rhet jad menhon washkal esfuga wamlah jala wsamah bkanya wmashke wa leh
49	sharka den amrin waw shvukw nejse en aze iliyya lmefrkeh
50	hu den yeshu tuv ka bkala rama washvak rujeh
51	wmejda apay tara dhaykla estri lazren men lel dama lzajt wa'ra ettsiaz wji'fe estari
52	wvez kvure ezpatajw wfagre saguiyye' dkadishe dashjivin waw kamw
53	wanfakw wvazar kyamteh alw lamdita kadishta we'zjsiw lsaguiyye'
54	kentruna den wadameh dnatrin waw lyeshu kad jsaw sawa wa'ylen dahway djelw tav we'marw sharira'iz hana breh wa d'alaha
55	iz way den af taman neshe saguiyya'za djasyan way men rujka hanen d'ezay way bazreh dyeshu men glila wamshamshan way leh
56	dajda menhen maryam magdlata wmaryam emeh dyakuv wadyawsi we'mhon davnay savday
57	kad hwa den ramsha eza gavra atira men ramza dashmeh yawsef d'af hu ettalmad wa lyeshu
58	hana krev lwaz pilataws wash'el pagreh dyeshu wafkad pilataws dnezihev leh pagra
59	wshakleh yawsef lfagra wjarjeh bajyasa djetana nakda
60	wsameh bvez kvura jaza dileh dankir bji'fa waguelw ki'fa rabza armiw al tara dvez kvura we'salw
61	iz way den taman maryam magdlata wmaryam jriza dyazban way lkuvleh dkavra
62	lyawma den damjar d'iyzawy bazar ruvta ezkanashw rabay kahne wafrishe lwaz pilataws
63	wa'mrin leh maran etdjarn dhaw matyana emar wa kad jay dmen bazar tlaza yawmin ka'em na
64	pkud hajil mesdahrin bkavra dama lazlaza yawmin dalma ni'zon talmidawy negnvuna blilya wni'mron lama dmen bez mize kam wzehwe tuyay jrata bisha men kadmata
65	amar lhon pilataws iz ljon kestunare selw esdarw aykana dyadin ton
66	henon den esalw esdharw bkavra wajzamw ki'fa ha am kestunare
