1	bhon den byawmaza hanon eza yujanan mamdana wmajres wa bjurba dihud
2	wa'mar tuvw kerbaz lah malkuza dashmayya
3	hanaw guer haw d'amir byad eshaya nviyya kala dkare bjurba tayyevw urjeh dmarya wa'shwaw lashvilawy
4	hu den yujanan izawy wa lvusheh dsara dgamle we'sar jase dmeshka al jasawy wme'julteh kamse wdevsha dvara
5	haden nafka waz lwazeh urishlem wjulah ihud wjuleh azra dajdaray yurdnan
6	wamdin waw meneh byurdnan nahra kad mawden bajtahayhon
7	kad jsa den saguiyye' men prishe wmen sadukayye d'azen lmemad emar lhon yalda d'ajedne manu jawijon lmerak men rugsa d'aze
8	vedw hajil pi're dshawen lazyavuza
9	wla tasbron wzi'mron bnafshjon d'ava iz lan avraham amar na ljon guer dmeshkaj alaha men halen ki'fe lamkamu bnayya la'vraham
10	ha den narga sim al ekara d'iylane kul ilana hajil dfi're tave la aved mezpsek wnafel bnura
11	ena mamed na ljon bmayya lazyavuza haw den dvazary aze jasin u meny haw dla shawe na msanawy lmeshkal hu mamed ljon bruja dkudsha wavnura
12	haw drafsha b'iydeh wamdake edrawy wjete kanesh la'wsrawy wzevna mawked bnura dla daka
13	haden eza yeshu men glila lyurdnan lwaz yujanan dnemad meneh
14	hu den yujanan kale wa leh wa'mar ena snik na dmenaj ezmed wa't lwazy ezayt
15	hu den yeshu na we'mar leh shvuk hasha hajana guer ya'e lan danmale kulah ki'nuza whaden shavkeh
16	kad mad den yeshu mejda slek men mayya we'zpzajw leh shmayya wajsa ruja d'alaha dnajta ayj yawna we'zaz lawy
17	wha kala men shmayya d'amar hanaw bery jabiva dveh estviz
