1	haden yeshu malel am kenshe wam talmidawy
2	we'mar lhon al kursya dmushe izevw safre wafrishe
3	kul medem hajil dni'mron ljon dzetron tarw wavedw ayj vadayhon den la tebdon amrin guer wla avdin
4	wa'srin mawble yakiraza wsamin al kazpaza davnay nasha henon den bsevhon la saven dnekrvon lhen
5	wjulhon vadayhon avdin dnezjson lavnay nasha maften guer teflayhon wmawrjin tejlaza dmartutayhon
6	wrajmin rish smaje bajshamyaza wrish mawtve bajnushaza
7	washlama bshuke wadnehon mezkren men nasha rabi
8	aton den la tezkron rabi jad u guer rabjon aton den kuljon aje aton
9	wa'va la tekron ljon b'ara jad u guer avujon dvashmayya
10	wla tezkron mdabrane metul djad u mdabranjon mshija
11	haw den drab bjon nehwe ljon mshamshana
12	man guer danrim nafsheh nezmakaj wman dnamej nafsheh nettrim
13	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape d'ajlin ton bate d'armlaza belza dmawrjin ton slawazjon metul hana tkablon dina yatira
14	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape d'ajidin ton malkuza dashmayya kdam bnay nasha aton guer la a'lin ton wla'ylen da'lin la shavkin ton lmeal
15	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape dmezkarkin ton yama wyavsha dzebdon jad guiyyura wma dahwa avdin ton leh breh dguihana afa laykon
16	wa ljon nagude smayya d'amrin ton dman dyame bhaykla la wa medem man den dyame bdahva davhaykla ja'ev
17	sajle wasmayya mana guer rav dahva aw haykla dhu mkadesh leh ldahva
18	wman dyame bmadbja la wa medem man den dyame bkurbana dalel meneh ja'ev
19	sajle wawire mana rav kurbana aw madbja damkadesh lkurbana
20	man dyame hajil bmadbja yame beh wavjul ma d'iyz lel meneh
21	wman dyame bhaykla yame beh wavman damar beh
22	wman dyame bashmayya yame bjursyeh d'alaha wavman dyazev lel meneh
23	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape damasrin ton nana washveta wjamuna washvakton yakirazeh dnamusa dina wajnana whaymanuza halen den wale wa dzebdon whalen la teshbkon
24	nagude smayya damsallin bake wvalin gamle
25	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape damdaken ton bareh djasa wadsavura lgaw den mlen jtufya wawla
26	prishe wire dakaw lukdam gaweh djasa wadsavura dahwa af barhon dje
27	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape ddamen ton lkavre majlshe dmen lvar mezjsen shapire men lgaw den mlen garme dmize wjulah tanfuza
28	hajana af aton men lvar mezjsen ton lavnay nasha ayj sadike wmen lgaw mlen ton awla wmasav b'ape
29	wa ljon safre wafrishe nasbay b'ape dvanen ton kavre danviyye wamsabzin ton bez kvure dsadike
30	wa'mrin ton d'elu hwayn byawmay avahayn la hawen wayn lhon shawtafe badma danviyye
31	maden mashdin ton al nafshjon davnayya aton dhanon dkatelw lanviyye
32	wa'f aton malaw mshujza d'avahaykon
33	jwawaza yalda d'ajedne aykana terkon men dina dguihana
34	metul hana ha ena mshadar na lwazjon nviyye wjakime wsafre menhon katlin ton wsakpin ton wmenhon mnagdin ton bajnushazjon wzerdfon enon men mdina lamdina
35	aykana dni'ze laykon kuleh dma dsadike d'eze'shed al ara men dmeh dhavel sadika wadama ladmeh dasjarya bar brajya haw daktalton baynay haykla lmadbja
36	amin amar na ljon dni'zyan halen kulhen al sharbza hade
37	urishlem urishlem katlaz nviyye wragmaz la'ylen dashlijin lwazah kma savnin sviz d'ekanesh bnayky ayj djansha tarnagulta parugueh tjez guefeh wla svayton
38	ha meshtvek ljon baytjon jarba
39	amarna ljon guer dla tejsunany men hasha dama dzi'mron brij u d'aze bashmeh dmarya
