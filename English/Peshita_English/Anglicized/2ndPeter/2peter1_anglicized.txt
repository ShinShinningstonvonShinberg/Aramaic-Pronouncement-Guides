1	shemon petraws avda washleekha dyeshoo msheekha laylayn dalhaymanootha shawyath biyqara aman eshtweew bzadeeqootha dmaran wfarooqan yeshoo msheekha
2	taybootha washlama nesge lkhon bshoodaa dmaran yeshoo msheekha
3	aykh man dkhulhayn aylayn diythayhayn dkhayla alahayya lwath khayye wdhekhlath alaha yav byadh shoodaa dhaw daqra lan btheshbookhta deeleh wamyatrootha
4	dviydhayhayn shoodayye rawrve wyaqeere lkhon yav davyadh halayn tehon shawtafe dakhyana alahayya kadh arqeen ton men khvala dargheeghatha davalma
5	whee dayn hadhe kadh shqaltana kuleh maleen ton awsefw al haymanoothkhon myatrootha al dayn myatrootha eedhatha
6	al dayn eedhatha mkhamsnanootha al dayn mkhamsnanootha msaybranootha al dayn msaybranootha dekhlath alaha
7	al dayn dekhlath alaha rekhmath akhootha al dayn rekhmath akhootha khooba
8	halayn gayr kadh shkheekhan lkhon wyateeran la wa bateele afla dla pire mqeeman lkhon bshoodaeh dmaran yeshoo msheekha
9	haw gayr dla shkheekhan leh halayn samya eethawy dla khaze data dookayya dakhtahawy qadhmayye
10	wal ha yateeraeeth akhay eesafw davyadh vadhaykon tave qratkhon waghveethkhon mshararta tebdhon kadh gayr halayn avdeen ton la mthoom meshtareen ton
11	hakhana gayr ateeraeeth methyava lkhon maalta dmalkootha dalalam dmaran wfarooqan yeshoo msheekha
12	wal hadhe la mamen na men dmahedh na lkhon ameenaeeth al halayn kadh tav af yadheen ton wasmeekheen ton al shrara hana
13	dkhina dayn estavrath lee dakhma diythay bfaghra hana aeerkhon boohdana
14	kadh yadha na doondana dfaghry baghal hawe aykana daf maran yeshoo msheekha awdany
15	yasef na dayn daf ameenaeeth wa eeth lkhon daf men bathar mapqana deely oohdana dhalayn tehon avdeen
16	la wa gayr kadh bathar mathle daveedheen buwmanootha azeenan awdanakhon khayleh wmetheetheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha ela kadh khazayye wayn drabootha deeleh
17	kadh gayr nsav men alaha ava eeqara wtheshbookhta kadh qala etha leh daykh hana men teshbookhta payath braboothah dhanaw bery haw khabeeva haw dveh estveeth
18	af khnan leh lhana qala shmanan men shmayya detha leh kadh eethayn wayn ameh btoora qadeesha
19	wiyth lan dshareera af meltha danviyyootha ha dshapeer avdeen ton kadh bah khareen ton aykh dvashragha dmanhar bathra amoota dhama diymama nanhar wshemsha nedhnakh blebawathkhon
20	kadh hadhe looqdham yadheen ton dkhul nviyyootha shrayya dakhthava deelah la hawya
21	la wa gayr bsevyana dvarnasha ethath men mthoom nviyyootha ela kadh men rookha qadeesha methnaghdeen malelw qadeeshe bnaynasha dalaha
