1	hwaw dayn af nviyye dagale bama aykh daf bkhon nehon malfane dagale aylayn dmaleen heresees davdana wavmara dazvan enon kafreen kadh maytayn al nafshhon avdana msarhva
2	wsagiyye nizoon bathar tanfootha deelhon halayn dmetulathhon oorkha dashrara tethgadaf
3	wavaloovootha wavmele dvedhya nettagron bkhon halayn ddheenhon men qdheem la batel wavdanhon la naem
4	en gayr alaha al malakhe dakhtaw la khas ela bshishlatha damtana agen enon bthakhtayyatha washlem enon dnethnatron ldheena dshoonaqa
5	wal alma qadhmayya la khas ela lnookh dathmanya karooza dkhinootha ntar kadh tawfana al alma drasheee aytee
6	wkhadh lamdheenatha dasdhoom wadhamoora awqedh wvahfookhya khayyev enayn kadh takhweetha lrasheee datheedheen dnehon sam
7	af lloot zadeeqa dmethqapakh wa men hoopakha davtanpootha dhalayn dadhla namoosa eethayhon waw pasee
8	bakhzatha gayr wavshema kadh amar wa kina haw baynathhon yoom men yoom lnafsheh zadeeqta bavadhe dla namoos mshaneq wa
9	yadha oo marya dnefrooq men oolsana laylayn ddhakhleen leh lawale dayn lyawma ddheena kadh meshtanqeen netar
10	yateeraeeth dayn laylayn dvathar besra bregtha dtamootha azeen wal marootha mvasreen marakhe wamshaqle dmen teshbookhta la zeeeen kadh mghadfeen
11	ayka dmalakhe davkhayla wavooshna rawrveen menhon la maytayn layhon men marya deena dghoodafa
12	halayn dayn daykh khaywatha kharshatha waw bakhyana lkharba wlakhvala kadh baylayn dla yadheen mghadfeen bakhvala deelhon nethkhablon
13	kadh aylayn dhawe bhon awla aghra dawla haniyyootha khsheev lhon boosama dahwa biymama mkhatme wmalyay moome dkhadh methbasmeen banyakhathhon methpanqeen
14	kadh ayne eeth lhon dmalyan gawra wakhtahe dla mawpayn kadh mshadleen lnafshatha dla smeekhan wleba damdharash baloovootha eeth lhon bnayya dlawttha
15	dkhadh shvaqw oorkha treesta shghaw wezalw buwrkha dvelam bar boor haw daghra dawla akhev
16	maksanootha dayn dahwath leh lmethavranootheh athana dla qala davqala davnaynasha mallath klath lshatyootheh danviyya
17	halayn enon meene dla mayya nane dmen alala methradhpan halayn damtana dkheshookha nteer lhon
18	kadh gayr goonkhe dasreeqootha mmalleen mshadleen bargheeghatha tanfatha dvesra laylayn dvamle qaleel arqeen men aylayn davtayootha methhapkheen
19	wkhirootha lhon meshtawdayn kadh henon avde eethayhon dakhvala lhaw gayr medem dnash zkhe leh lhana af mshabadh
20	en gayr kadh raqw men tanpwatheh dalma bshoodaeh dmaran yeshoo msheekha wfarooqan bhayn bhalayn kadh metharzleen toov mezdkhayn hwath lah kharthhon dveesha men qadhmata
21	paqakh wa lhon gayr dla neshtawdon luwrkha dzadeeqootha aw dkhadh eshtawdaw lvathra nehpkhon men pooqdana qadeesha deshtlem lhon
22	gdhash lhon dayn halayn dmathla shareera dkhalba dahfakh al tyooveh wakhzeerta daskhath boorgala dasyana
