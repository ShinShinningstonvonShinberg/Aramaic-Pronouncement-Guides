1	bhanon dayn yawmatha kadh kensha sagiyya eeth wa wla eeth wa medem dnekhlon qra lthalmeedhawy wemar lhon
2	methrakham na al kensha hana dha tlatha yawmeen qaweew lwathy wlayt lhon mana nekhlon
3	wen hoo dshare na lhon kadh sameen lvatayhon apeen buwrkha nasha gayr menhon men rookhqa ateeeen
4	amreen leh talmeedhawy aymeka meshkakh nash harka bkhoorba dansaba lakhma lhalayn kulhon
5	wshael enon hoo kma lakhmeen eeth lkhon amreen leh shava
6	wafqadh lkhenshe dnestamkon al ara wansav hanon shava lakhmeen wvarekh waqsa wyav lthalmeedhawy danseemon wsamw lkhenshe
7	wiyth waw noone qaleel waf layhon barekh wemar danseemon enon
8	wekhalw wasvaw washqalw tawtare daqsayye shava espreedheen
9	eethayhon waw dayn nasha dekhalw aykh arba alfeen
10	washra enon wasleq mekhdha lasfeeta am talmeedhawy wetha lathra ddhalmanootha
11	wanfaqw preeshe wshareew lmeva ameh wshaleen waw leh atha men shmayya kadh mnasayn leh
12	wettanakh brookheh wemar mana baya atha sharbtha hadhe ameen amar na lkhon dla tetheehev lah atha lsharbtha hadhe
13	washvaq enon wasleq lasfeeta wezalw lhaw evra
14	wataw dnesvon lakhma wela khdha greesta layt wa amhon basfeeta
15	wfaqedh enon wemar lhon khzaw ezdarw men khmeera dafreeshe wmen khmeereh dherawdhes
16	wmethkhashveen waw khadh am khadh wamreen dlakhma layt lan
17	yeshoo dayn eedha wemar lhon mana ranayn ton dlakhma layt lkhon la dhama lhasha yadheen ton wla mestakleen ton dhakeel leba qashya eeth lkhon
18	wayne eeth lkhon wla khazayn ton wedhne eeth lkhon wla shameen ton wla ahdeen ton
19	kadh halayn khamsha lakhmeen qseeth lkhamsha alfeen kma qoofeeneen daqsayye kadh mlayn shqalton amreen leh tresar
20	amar lhon wkhadh shava larba alfeen kma espreedheen daqsayye kadh mlayn shqalton amreen shava
21	amar lhon aykaw la dhama lhasha mestakleen ton
22	wetha lvayth-sayyada wayteew leh samya wvaayn waw meneh dneqroov leh
23	wekhadh biydheh dsamya wapqeh lvar men qreetha wraq baynawy wsam eedheh wshaleh dmana khaze
24	khar wemar khaze na bnay nasha aykh eelane damhalkheen
25	toov sam eedheh al aynawy wathqen wkhaze wa kul medem naheeraeeth
26	wshadreh lvayteh wemar af la laqreetha teool wla timar lnash baqreetha
27	wanfaq yeshoo wthalmeedhawy lqoorya dqesariyya-dhfeeleepaws wamshael wa lthalmeedhawy buwrkha wamar lhon manoo amreen lay nasha diythay
28	henon dayn emarw dyookhanan mamdhana wakhrane diliyya wakhrane khadh men nviyye
29	amar lhon yeshoo aton dayn manoo amreen ton lay diythay na shemon wemar leh at oo msheekha breh dalaha khayya
30	wakha bhon dalnash la nimron lawy
31	wsharee wa lmalafoo enon datheedh oo breh dnasha dnekhash sagee wadhnestle men qasheeshe wmen rabay kahne wmen safre wnethqtel wlathlatha yawmeen nqoom
32	ween baghle meltha mmalel wa wdhavreh kifa wsharee lmekha beh
33	hoo dayn ethpnee wkhar bthalmeedhawy wakha bshemon wemar zel lakh lvestary satana dla rane at dalaha ela davnay nasha
34	waqra yeshoo lkhenshe am talmeedhawy wemar lhon man dsave dnithe bathary nekhpoor bnafsheh wneshqool zqeefeh wnithe bathary
35	kul man dsave gayr dnakhe nafsheh nawbdheeh wkhul dnawbedh nafsheh metulathy wmetul svarthy nakhayh
36	mana gayr nethadar barnasha en alma kuleh nithar wnafsheh nekhsar
37	aw mana netel barnasha takhloofa dnafsheh
38	kul gayr dnevhath bee wavmelay bsharbtha hadhe khatata wghayyartha waf breh dnasha nevhath beh ma dathe bshoovkha davoo am malakhawy qadeeshe
