1	wakhnashw lwatheh preeshe wsafre dethaw men oorishlem
2	wakhzaw nasheen men talmeedhawy dakhleen lakhma kadh la mshaghan eedhayhon wethadalw
3	kulhon gayr eehoodhayye wafreeshe en hoo davteelaeeth la msheegheen eedhayhon la laseen metul dakheedheen mashlmanootha dqasheeshe
4	wmen shooqa ela amdeen la laseen wsagiyyatha khranyatha eeth aylayn dqabelw dnetron mamoodhyatha dkhase wadhqeste wadhmanay nkhasha wadharsatha
5	wshaloo safre wafreeshe lmana talmeedhayk la mhalkheen aykh mashlmanootha dqasheeshe ela kadh la mshaghan eedhayhon akhleen lakhma
6	hoo dayn emar lhon shapeer ethnabee laykon eshaya nviyya nasbay bape aykh dakhtheev dama hana bsefwatheh oo myaqar lee lebhon dayn sagee rakheeq meny
7	wasreeqaeeth dakhleen lee kadh malfeen yoolpane dfooqdane davnay nasha
8	shvaqton gayr pooqdana dalaha wakheedheen ton mashlmanootha davnay nasha mamoodhyatha dkhase wadhqeste wsagiyyatha aylayn dalhalayn damyan
9	amar lhon shapeer talmeen ton pooqdana dalaha dathqeemon mashlmanoothkhon
10	mooshe gayr emar dyaqar lavookh wlemakh wman damsakhe lava wlema mawta nmooth
11	aton dayn amreen ton en nimar gavra lavoo aw lemeh qoorbany medem dmeny tithar
12	wla shavqeen ton leh dnebedh medem lavoo aw lemeh
13	wmaslayn ton meltha dalaha metul mashlmanootha dashlemton wadhdhamyan lhalayn sagiyyatha avdeen ton
14	waqra yeshoo lkhensha kuleh wemar lhon shoomoony kulkhon westalw
15	layt medem dalvar men barnasha hoo wael leh dmeshkakh msayyev leh ela medem dnafeq meneh haw oo msayyev lvar nasha
16	man diyth leh edhne dneshma neshma
17	kadh dayn al yeshoo lvayta men kensha shaloo talmeedhawy al mathla haw
18	amar lhon hakhana af aton atleen ton la yadheen ton dkhul medem dmen lvar ael lvar nasha la meshkakh msayyev leh
19	dla wa llebeh ael ela lkharseh wmeshtdhe bthadhkeetha damdhakya kulah mekhoolta
20	medem dayn dnafeq men barnasha haw oo msayyev lvar nasha
21	men lghaw gayr men leba davnay nasha nafqan makhshvatha beeshatha gawra zanyootha ganavootha qetla
22	aloovootha beeshootha nekhla sakhnootha ayna beeshta goodafa shavhranootha shatyootha
23	halayn kulhayn beeshatha men lghaw oo nafqan wamsayvan leh lvarnasha
24	men taman qam yeshoo wetha lathkhooma dsoor wadhsaydan wal lvayta khadh wla save wa dnash neda beh wla eshkakh dnetshe
25	mekhdha gayr shemath attha khdha metulatheh diyth wath lvarthah rookha tanftha wethath neflath qdham reghlawy
26	hee dayn attha eethayh wath khanftha men pooneeqi dsooriyya wvaya wath meneh dnapeq shidha men barthah
27	wemar lah yeshoo shvooqy looqdham dnesbon bnayya la wath gayr shapeera dnesav lakhma davnayya wnarme lkhalbe
28	hee dayn nath wamra leh een mary af kalbe men tkhayth pathoore akhleen partoothe davnayya
29	amar lah yeshoo zely metul hadhe meltha nfaq leh shidha men barthekhy
30	wezath lvaytah weshkkhath barthah kadh ramya barsa wnapeeq menah shidhah
31	toov nfaq yeshoo men tkhooma dsoor wadhsaydan wetha lyama daghleela bathkhooma desrath-mdheenatha
32	wayteew leh kharsha khadh piqa wvae waw meneh danseem lawy eedha
33	wnaghdeh men kensha balkhoodhawy warmee sevatheh bedhnawy wraq waqrev lleshaneh
34	wkhar bashmayya wettanakh wemar leh ethpakh
35	wvah bshatha ethpatakh edhnawy weshtaree esara dleshaneh wmalel psheeqaeeth
36	wzahar enon dalnash la nimron wakhma dhoo mzahar wa lhon henon yateer makhrzeen waw
37	wyateeraeeth metdamreen waw wamreen dkhul medem shapeer avedh lkharshe avedh dneshmon wadhla mmalleen danmallon
