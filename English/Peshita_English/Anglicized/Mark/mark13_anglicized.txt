1	wkhadh nfaq yeshoo men haykla emar leh khadh men talmeedhawy malfana ha khzee aylayn kife waylayn benyane
2	yeshoo dayn emar leh khaze at halayn benyane rawrve la meshtavqa harka kif al kif dla mestathra
3	wkhadh eethev yeshoo btoora dzayte looqval haykla shaloo kifa wyaqoov wyookhanan wandrewas balkhoodhayhon
4	emar lan emathy halayn nehwyan wmana atha ma dqareevan halayn kulhayn lmeshtalamoo
5	hoo dayn yeshoo sharee lmimar lhon khzaw dalma nash nataykhon
6	sagiyye gayr nithon bshemy wnimron dena na walsagiyye naton
7	ma dayn dashmaton qrave wteba dqirse la tedhkhlon theedh oo dnehwe ela la dhakeel khartha
8	nqoom gayr ama al ama wmalkoo al malkoo wnehon zawe bdhooka dooka wnehon kafne washghooshye halayn risha enayn dkhevle
9	khzaw dayn aton nafshkhon nashlmoonakhon gayr ldhayyane wvakhnooshathhon tethnagdhon waqdham malke whighmoone tqoomon metulathy lsahdoothhon
10	looqdham dayn theedha dthethkrez svarthy bkhulhon amme
11	ma damqarveen lkhon dayn dnashlmoonakhon la tqadmon tispon mana tmallon wla ternon ela medem dmetheehev lkhon bha shatha haw malelw la wa gayr aton mmalleen ela rookha dqoodhsha
12	nashlem dayn akha lakhoo lmawta wava lavreh wanqoomon bnayya al avahayhon wanmeethon enon
13	wthehon sneeeen men kulnash metul shemy man dayn dansaybar dhama lkhartha hoo nikhe
14	ma dayn dakhzayton atha tanftha dkhoorba ha dameera bdhaneeyel nviyya dqama ayka dla wale haw dqare nestakal hadayn aylayn dveehoodh enon nerqon ltoora
15	wman dvegara w la nekhooth wla neool lmeshqal medem men bayteh
16	wman davkhaqla w la nethhfekh lvestreh dneshqool lvasheh
17	wa dayn lvatnatha wlaylayn dmaynqan bhanon yawmatha
18	salaw dayn dla nehwe rooqeekhon bsathwa
19	nehwe gayr byawmatha hanon oolsana dla hwa akhwatheh men reesh breetha davra alaha dhama lhasha wla nehwe
20	weloo la marya dkharee yawmatha hanon la khayye wa kul bsar ela metul gvayya daghva karee yawmatha hanon
21	hadayn en nash nimar lkhon dha harka w msheekha wha hartaman la thaymnon
22	nqoomon gayr msheekhe ddhagalootha wanviyye dkhadavootha wnetlon athwatha wthedhmratha wnaton en meshkkha af laghvayya
23	aton dayn ezdarw ha qadmeth emreth lkhon kul medem
24	bhanon dayn yawmatha bathar oolsana haw shemsha nekhshakh wsahra la netel noohreh
25	wkhawkve neplon men shmayya wkhaylawatha dashmayya nettzeeon
26	whadayn nekhzoona lavreh dnasha kadh athe banane am khayla raba wam shoovkha
27	hadayn nshadar malakhawy wankhanesh laghvawy men arbathayhayn rookhe men rishah dara wadhama lrishah dashmayya
28	men tita dayn eelafw peletha dma drakh sawkayh wafraw tarfayh yadheen ton damta qayta
29	hakhana af aton ma dakhzayton halayn dhawyan daw dqareeva y al tara
30	ameen amar na lkhon dla tebar sharbtha hadhe dhama dhalayn kulhayn nehwyan
31	shmayya wara nebron wmelay la nebran
32	al dayn yawma haw wal shatha ha nash la yadha afla malakhe dashmayya wla bra ela en ava
33	khzaw etteerw wsalaw la gayr yadheen ton emathy oo zavna
34	aykh gavra w gayr dakhzaq washvaq bayteh wyav shooltana lavdawy walnash nash vadheh walthara paqedh dnehwe eer
35	etteerw hakheel dla yadheen ton emathy athe mareh dvayta bramsha aw bfelgeh dlilya aw bmaqra tarnaghla aw bsafra
36	dalma nithe men shelya wneshkakhkhon kadh damkeen ton
37	medem dalkhon dayn amar na lkhulkhon oo amar na hwayton eereen
