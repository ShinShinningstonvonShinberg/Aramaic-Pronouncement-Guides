1	mana hakheel amreenan al avraham risha davahatha deshkakh bavsar
2	eloo gayr avraham men vadhe ezdadaq wa eeth wa leh shoovhara ela la lwath alaha
3	mana gayr emar kthava dhaymen avraham lalaha wethkhashbath leh lzadeeqoo
4	lman dfalakh dayn la methkhshev leh aghreh aykh davtayboo ela aykh man dmettkheev leh
5	lhaw dayn dla plakh ela haymen balkhoodh bman damzadeq lkhatayye methkhashba leh haymanootheh lkhinoo
6	aykana daf daweedh emar al tooveh dghavra ayna dalaha khashev leh zadeeqootha dla vadhe kadh amar
7	dtoovayhon laylayn deshtveq lhon awlhon wethkaseew khtahayhon
8	wtoovawy lghavra dla nekhshoov leh alaha khteetheh
9	hana hakheel toova al gzoorta hoo aw al oorlootha amreenan gayr dethkhashbath lavraham haymanootheh lkhinoo
10	aykana hakheel ethkhashbath leh baghzoorta aw boorlootha la wa baghzoorta ela boorlootha
11	atha hoo gayr shaqlah laghzoorta wkhathma dkhinootha dhaymanootheh davoorlootha dnehwe ava lkhulhon aylayn damhaymneen men oorlootha dthethkhshev af lhon lkhinoo
12	wava laghzoorta la wa laylayn dmen gzoorta enon balkhoodh ela af laylayn dshalmeen leqvatha dhaymanootha doorlootha davon avraham
13	la wa gayr bnamoosa hwa moolkana lavraham walzareh dnehwe yarta lalma ela bkhinootha dhaymanootheh
14	eloo gayr halayn dmen namoosa hwaw yarte sreeqa wath haymanootha wamvatal wa moolkana
15	namoosa gayr mabdhana hoo drooghza kar dlayt gayr namoosa afla var namoosa
16	metul hana bhaymanootha davtaybootha nezdadaq wnehwe shareer moolkana lkhuleh zareh la layna dmen namoosa hoo balkhoodh ela af layna dmen haymanootha hoo davraham diythawy ava dkhulan
17	aykana dakhtheev dsamtakh ava lsoogha damme qdham alaha haw dhayment beh dmakhe meethe wqare laylayn dla eethayhon aykh eethayhon
18	wadhla savra lsavra haymen dnehwe ava lsoogha damme aykh dakhtheev dhakhana nehwe zarakh
19	wla ethkrah bhaymanootheh kadh methbaqe bfaghreh meetha dahwa bar ma shneen wavmarba meetha dsara
20	wavmoolkana dalaha la ethpalagh aykh khseer haymanootha ela ethkhayyal bhaymanootha wyav teshbookhta lalaha
21	washar dmedem damlakh leh alaha meshkakh lmeghmar
22	metul hana ethkhashbath leh lkhinoo
23	wla wa metulatheh balkhoodh ethkathbath hadhe dethkhashbath haymanootheh lkhinoo
24	ela af metulathan daflan theedh oo dnekhshoov aylayn dhaymenn bman daqeem lmaran yeshoo msheekha men bayth meethe
25	dhoo eshtlem metul khtahayn wqam metul danzadqan
