1	ela amar na dalma dakhqeh alaha lameh khas af ena gayr men eesrayyel na men zareh davraham men sharbtha dvenyameen
2	la dkhaq alaha lameh ayna dmen qdheem eedhee wa leh aw la yadheen ton bakhthava deliyya mana emar kadh qavel wa lalaha al eesrayyel wamar
3	mary lanviyyayk qatelw walmadhbkhayk sakhefw wena hoo balkhoodhay eshtakhreth wvaayn lnafshy
4	wethemar leh bghelyana dha shevqeth lnafshy shava alfeen gavreen aylayn dal boorkayhon la brekhw wla sghedhw lvala
5	hakhana af bhana zavna sharkana hoo eshtkhar baghveetha dtaybootha
6	en dayn btaybootha la wa men vadhe wen la taybootha la eethayh taybootha en dayn bavadhe la wa men taybootha wen la vadha la eethawy vadha
7	mana hakheel ha dvae wa eesrayyel la eshkakh gveetha dayn eshkkhath sharkhon dayn ethawarw blebhon
8	aykana dakhtheev dyav lhon alaha rookha mdharaneetha wayne dla nevkhron bhayn wedhne dla neshmon dhama lyawma dyawmana
9	wdhaweedh toov emar nehwe pathoorhon qdhamayhon lfakha wfooranhon lthooqaltha
10	nekhshkhan aynayhon dla nekhzon wkhashon bkhulzvan nehwe kfeef
11	amar na dayn dalma ettqelw aykh dneplon khas ela bthooqlathhon deelhon hwaw khayye lamme latnanhon
12	wen tooqlathhon hwath oothra lalma wkhayyavoothhon oothra lamme kma hakheel shoomlahon
13	lkhon dayn amar na lamme ena diythay shleekha damme ltheshmeshty mshabakh na
14	dalma aten lavnay besry wakhe nasheen menhon
15	en gayr mestalyanoothhon tarootha lalma hwath kma hakheel poonahon ela khayye dmen bayth meethe
16	wen dayn risheetha qadeesha af gveelta wen eqara qadeesh oo af sawke
17	wen men sawke ethpshakh wat dzayta at dvara ettemt bdhookyathhayn wahwayt shawtafa leqareh walshoomneh dzayta
18	la teshtavhar al sawke en dayn meshtavhar at la wa at shqeel leh leqara ela hoo eqara shqeel lakh
19	wakhvar timar dsawke dethpshakh dena bdhookyathhayn ettem
20	shapeer halayn metul dla haymen ethpshakh at dayn bhaymanootha qamt la tetreem breyanakh ela dkhal
21	en alaha gayr al sawke dmen kyanhayn la khas dalma afla layk nekhoos
22	khzee hakheel baseemootheh wqashyootheh dalaha al aylayn danfalw qashyootha layk dayn baseemootha en tqawe bah bvaseemootha wen la af at tethpshakh
23	whanon en la nqaon bkhaseerooth haymanoothhon af henon nettamon meshkakh gayr alaha dthoov natem enon
24	en gayr at dmen zayta at dvara haw dvakhyanakh ethpshakht wadhla bakhyanakh ettemt bzayta tava kma hakheel hanon en nettamon bzayta dakhyanhon
25	save na gayr dthedon akhay raza hana dla tehon khakeemeen breyan nafshkhon daweerooth leba men athar qaleel hwath liysrayyel dhama dneool moolayya damme
26	whadayn kuleh eesrayyel nikhe aykana dakhtheev dnithe men sehyon parooqa wnahpekh awla men yaqoov
27	whadayn tehwe lhon diyyathiqi ha dmen lwathy ma dshevqeth lhon khtahayhon
28	bewangeliyyawn dayn beldvave enon metulathkhon wavghavyootha khabeevayn enon metul avahatha
29	la gayr hafka alaha bmawhavtheh wavqeryaneh
30	aykana gayr daf aton la mettpeeseen wayton lalaha men qdheem whasha ethkhnenton metul la mettpeesanoothhon dhanon
31	hakhana af halayn la ettpeesw hasha lrakhme dalaykon daf layhon nehon rakhme
32	khvash gayr alaha lkhulnash bla mettpeesanootha dal kul nash nrakhem
33	aw oomqa doothra wkhekhmtha wmada dalaha dnash la mash deenawy oowrkhatheh la methaqvan
34	manoo gayr eedha reyaneh dmarya aw manoo hwa leh bel melka
35	wmanoo qadem yav leh wkhen nsav meneh
36	metul dkhul meneh wkhul beh wkhul biydheh dleh teshbkhan wvoorkan lalam almeen ameen
