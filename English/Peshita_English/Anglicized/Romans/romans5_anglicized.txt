1	metul dezdadaqn hakheel bhaymanootha nehwe lan shlama lwath alaha bmaran yeshoo msheekha
2	dveh ethqaravn bhaymanootha ltaybootha hadhe dvah qameenan wmeshtavhreenan bsavra dtheshbookhteh dalaha
3	wla hakhana balkhoodh ela af buwlsaneen meshtavhreenan dyadheenan duwlsana msaybranootha gamar ban
4	wamsaybranootha booqya wvooqya savra
5	savra dayn la mavheth metul dkhoobeh dalaha mshapa al lebawathan brookha dqoodhsha dethyahbath lan
6	en dayn msheekha metul kreehoothan bzavna hana khlaf rasheee meeth
7	lmakhsen gayr nash khlaf rasheee maeth khlaf tave gayr takh mamrakh nash lammath
8	harka mkhawe alaha khoobeh dalwathan den kadh khatayye eethayn wayn msheekha khlafayn meeth
9	kma hakheel yateeraeeth nezdadaq hasha badhmeh wveh nethpase men rooghza
10	en gayr kadh eethayn beldvave ethraee aman alaha bmawta davreh kma hakheel yateeraeeth btharootheh nikhe bkhayyawy
11	wla hakhana balkhoodh ela af neshtavhar balaha byadh maran yeshoo msheekha dveh oo hasha qabeln tarootha
12	aykana gayr davyadh khadh barnasha elath khteetha lalma wavyadh khteetha mawta whakhana bkhulhon bnay nasha var mawta bha dkhulhon khtaw
13	dhama gayr lnamoosa khteetha kadh eethayh wath balma la khsheeva wath khteetha metul dlayt wa namoosa
14	ela amlekh mawta men adham wadhama lmooshe af al aylayn dla khtaw badhmootha davar namooseh dadham haw diythawy dmootha dhaw datheedh
15	ela la wa aykh shooratha hakhana mawhavta en gayr metul shooratheh dkhadh sagiyye meethw kma hakheel yateeraeeth taybootheh dalaha wmawhavtheh metul khadh barnasha yeshoo msheekha bsagiyye tethyatar
16	wla aykh sakhlootha dkhadh hakhana mawhavta deena gayr dahwa men khadh lkhooyyava hwa mawhavta dayn men khtahe sagiyye hwath lkhinoo
17	en gayr metul sakhlootha dkhadh amlekh mawta yateeraeeth aylayn dansavw soogha dtaybootha wadhmawhavtha wadhkhinootha bkhayye namlkhon byadh khadh yeshoo msheekha
18	akhzna hakheel dmetul sakhlootha dkhadh hwa khooyyava lkhulhon bnaynasha hakhana metul kinootha dkhadh tehwe zakhootha lkhayye lkhulhon bnaynasha
19	aykana gayr dmetul la meshtamanootheh dkhadh barnasha khatayye sagiyye hwaw hakhana af metul meshtamanootheh dkhadh sagiyye kine hawayn
20	malana dayn dahwa lnamoosa dthesge khteetha wkhar dseghyath khteetha taman ethyatrath taybootha
21	daykh damlkhath khteetha bmawta hakhana tamlekh taybootha bkhinootha lkhayye dalalam byadh maran yeshoo msheekha
