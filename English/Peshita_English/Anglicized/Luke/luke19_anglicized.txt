1	wkhadh al yeshoo wavar biyreekhoo
2	gavra khadh dashmeh zakay ateera wa wrav makhse
3	wsave wa dnekhze lyeshoo dmanoo wla meshkakh wa men kensha metul davqawmtheh zoor wa zakay
4	warhet qadmeh lyeshoo wasleq leh lthita pakeehta dnekhzaywy metul dhakhwath theedh hwa dnebar
5	wkhadh etha lha dooktha yeshoo khza wemar leh estarv khooth zakay yawmana gayr wale davvaytakh ehwe
6	westarhav nkheth wqableh kadh khadhe
7	kadh khzaw dayn kulhon ratneen waw wamreen dalwath gavra khatayya al shra
8	qam dayn zakay wemar lyeshoo ha mary pelgooth nekhsay yahev na lmeskine walkhulnash medem dghelzeth khadh barba para na
9	amar leh yeshoo yawmana hwaw khayye lvayta hana metul daf hana breh oo davraham
10	etha gayr breh dnasha dneve wnakhe haw medem dabeedh wa
11	wkhadh shameen waw halayn awsef lmimar mathla metul dqareev wa luwrishlem wsavreen waw davha shatha theedha dthethgle malkootheh dalaha
12	wemar gavra khadh bar toohma raba ezal lathra rakheeqa dnesav leh malkootha wnehpookh
13	waqra esra avdawy wyav lhon esra mneen wemar lhon ettarw adh athe na
14	bnay mdheeteh dayn sanayn waw leh wshadarw eezgade bathreh wamreen la savayn nan dnamlekh layn hana
15	wkhadh nsav malkootha wahfakh emar dneqron leh hanon avdawy dyav lhon kespa dneda mana kul khadh khadh menhon ettagar
16	wetha qadhmayya wemar mary manyakh esra mneen awtar
17	amar leh eew avda tava davqaleel eshtkhakht mhayman tehwe shaleet al esra karkheen
18	wetha dathrayn wemar mary manyakh khamsha mneen vadh
19	emar af lhana af at tehwe shaleet al khamsha karkheen
20	wetha khrina wemar mary ha manyakh haw diyth wa lwathy kadh seem bsedoona
21	dekhleth gayr menakh dghavra at qashya wshaqel at medem dla samt wkhasedh at medem dla zrat
22	amar leh men poomakh edoonakh avda beesha yadha wayt lee dghavra na qashya wshaqel na medem dla sameth wkhasedh na medem dla zereth
23	lmana la yavt kespy al pathoora wena athe weeth tava leh am rebyatheh
24	walhanon dqameen qdhamawy emar savw meneh manya whavw lhaw diyth lwatheh esra mneen
25	amreen leh maran eeth lwatheh esra mneen
26	amar lhon amar na lkhon dalkhul man diyth leh netheehev leh wmen haw dlayt leh af haw diyth leh nethnsev meneh
27	bram lhanon beldvavay aylayn dla svaw damlekh layhon aytaw enon wqatelw enon qdhamay
28	wkhadh emar halayn yeshoo nfaq laqdhamawy dnizal luwrishlem
29	wkhadh matee lvayth-page wvayth-anya al gev toora dmethqre dvayth zayte shadar trayn men talmeedhawy
30	wemar lhon zelw laqreetha ha dalqoovlan wkhadh aleen ton lah ha meshkkheen ton eela daseer dnash memthoom la rkhev lawy shraw aytaoo
31	wen nash mshael lkhon lmana sharayn ton leh hakhana emarw leh lmaran methbe
32	wezalw hanon deshtadarw weshkakhw aykana demar lhon
33	wkhadh sharayn leh leela amreen lhon marawy mana sharayn ton eela haw
34	wemarw lhon dalmaran methbe
35	waytyoo lwath yeshoo warmeew al eela manayhon warkvoo lyeshoo lawy
36	kadh dayn azel parseen waw manayhon buwrkha
37	wkhadh qrev lmakhattha dtoora dvayth zayte shareew kuleh kensha dthalmeedhe khadhayn wamshabkheen lalaha bqala rama al kul khayle dakhzaw
38	wamreen waw breekh oo malka dathe bashmeh dmarya shlama bashmayya wshoovkha bamrawme
39	nasha dayn men preeshe men baynay kenshe amreen leh rabee kee bthalmeedhayk
40	amar lhon amar na lkhon den halayn neshtqon kife neqyan
41	wkhadh qrev wakhzah lamdheeta bkha layh
42	wemar eloo kay yadhaty aylayn diythayhayn dashlamekhy afen bhana yawmekhy hasha dayn ethkasee lhayn men aynayky
43	nithon lekhy dayn yawmatha dnekhdroonekhy beldvavayky wnelsoonekhy men kul dooka
44	wneskhfoonekhy wlavnayky bghawekhy wla neshbqon bekhy kif al kif khlaf dla eedhaty zavna dsooranekhy
45	wkhadh al lhaykla sharee lmapaqoo laylayn dzavneen beh wamzabneen
46	wemar lhon ktheev dvayty bayth slootha eethawy aton dayn vadtoona martha dlestayye
47	whoo malef wa kul yoom bhaykla rabay kahne dayn wsafre wqasheeshe dama baayn waw lmawbadhootheh
48	wla meshkkheen waw mana nebdhon leh kuleh gayr ama tle wa beh lmeshmeh
