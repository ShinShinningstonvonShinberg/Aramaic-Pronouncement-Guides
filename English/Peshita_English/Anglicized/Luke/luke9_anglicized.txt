3	wemar lhon medem la teshqlon luwrkha la shavta wla tarmala wla lakhma wla kespa wla tartayn kooteenyan nehwyan lkhon

5	walman dla mqableen lkhon ma dnafqeen ton men mdheeta ha af khela men reghlaykon pesw layhon lsahdootha

7	shma dayn herawdhes tetrarkha kulhayn dhawyan way biydheh wmetdamar wa metul damreen waw nasheen dyookhanan qam men bayth meethe

10	wkhadh hfakhw shleekhe eshtaeew lyeshoo kulmedem davadhw wadhvar enon balkhoodhayhon lathra khoorba dvayth-sayyada
11	kenshe dayn kadh eedhaw ezalw bathreh wqabel enon wammalel wa amhon al malkootha dalaha wlaylayn dasneeqeen waw al asyootha mase wa
12	kadh dayn shree yawma lmesla qrevw talmeedhawy wamreen leh shree lkhenshe dnizoon lqoorya dakhdharayn walkhafroone dneshron bhon wneshkkhon lhon saybarta metul dvathra khoorba eethayn
13	amar lhon yeshoo havw lhon aton lmekhal henon dayn amreen layt lan yateer men khamsha lakhmeen wathrayn nooneen ela en ezalnan wazvanan saybarta lhana kuleh ama
14	hawayn waw gayr aykh khamsha alfeen gavreen amar lhon yeshoo asmekhw enon smakhe khamsheen nasheen basmakha

18	wkhadh msale balkhoodhawy wthalmeedhawy ameh shael enon wemar manoo amreen lay kenshe diythay
19	naw wamreen leh dyookhanan mamdhana wakhrane diliyya khrane dayn danviyya khadh men nviyye qadhmayye qam
20	amar lhon aton dayn manoo amreen ton diythay na shemon wemar msheekheh dalaha

22	wemar lhon datheedh oo breh dnasha dsagiyyatha nekhash wadhnestle men qasheeshe wrabay kahne wsafre wneqtloona walyawma dathlatha nqoom

32	weeqarw waw lhon bshentha shemon whanon dameh walmakhsen etteerw wakhzaw shoovkheh walhanon trayn nasheen dqameen waw lwatheh

41	na dayn yeshoo wemar on sharbtha dla mhaymna wamaqalta dhama lemathy ehwe lwathkhon wesaybarkhon qareva lkha lavrakh
