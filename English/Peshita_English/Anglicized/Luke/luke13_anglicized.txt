1	bhaw dayn zavna ethaw nasheen emarw leh al gleelayye hanon dfeelataws khlat demhon am devkhayhon
2	wana yeshoo wemar lhon savreen ton dhalayn gleelayye khataeen waw yateer men kulhon gleelayye dhakhana hwa enon
3	la amar na lkhon dayn daf kulkhon ela tthoovon hakhana tivdon
4	aw hanon tmantasar danfal layhon maghdla bsheelookha wqatel enon savreen ton dkhataeen waw yateer men kulhon bnaynasha damreen buwrishlem
5	la amar na lkhon dayn dela tthoovon kulkhon akhwathhon tivdon
6	wemar mathla hana tita eeth wath lnash danseeva bkharmeh wetha ba bah pire wla eshkakh
7	wemar lfalakha ha tlath shneen athe na bae na pire bthita hadhe wla meshkakh na psooqayh lmana mvatla ara
8	amar leh palakha mary shvooqayh af hadhe shata adh eflkheeh wezableeh
9	wen evdath pire wela lmankhay tefsqeeh
10	kadh dayn malef yeshoo bshabtha bakhdha men knooshatha
11	eeth wath taman attha diyth wath lah rookha dkhoorhana shneen tmanaesre wakhfeefa hwath wla meshkkha wath dthethpshet laghmar
12	khzah dayn yeshoo waqrah wemar lah attha sharyaty men koorhanekhy
13	wsam eedheh layh wmekhdha ethpashtath wshabkhath lalaha
14	na dayn rab knooshta kadh methkhamath al dasee bshabtha yeshoo wemar lkhenshe shta enon yawmeen davhon wale lmeflakh bhon hwayton athayn methasayn wla byawma dshabtha
15	yeshoo dayn na wemar leh nasev bape khadh khadh menkhon bshabtha la share tawreh aw khmareh men oorya wazel mashqe
16	hadhe dayn dvartheh ee davraham wasrah akhelqarsa ha tmanaesre shneen la wale wa dtheshtre men hana asoorya byawma dshabtha
17	wkhadh halayn amar wa bahteen waw kulhon aylayn dqameen waw lqoovleh wkhuleh ama khadhe wa bkhulhayn tmeehatha dhawyan way biydheh
18	emar wa dayn yeshoo lmana damya malkootha dalaha walmana edameeh
19	damya lafredta dkhardla ha dansav gavra armyah bghantheh warvath wahwath eelana raba wfarakhtha dashmayya aqnath bsawkayh
20	toov emar yeshoo lmana edameeh lmalkootha dalaha
21	damya lakhmeera dnesbath attha temrath bqamkha dathlath saeen dhama dkhuleh khma
22	wradhe wa bqoorya wvamdheenatha kadh malef wazel luwrishlem
23	shaleh dayn nash den zooreen enon aylayn dkhaayn
24	yeshoo dayn emar lhon ethkashw lmeal bthara aleesa amar na lkhon gayr dsagiyye nevon lmeal wla neshkkhon
25	men shatha danqoom mare bayta wnekhoodh tara wthehon qameen lvar wnaqsheen bthara wathsharon lmimar maran maran pthakh lan wnene hoo wnimar amar na lkhon dla yadha na lkhon aymeka aton
26	wathsharon lmimar qdhamayk ekhaln weshteen wavshooqayn aleft
27	wnimar lkhon dla yadha na lkhon aymeka aton prooqw lkhon meny palkhay shooqra
28	taman nehwe bekhya wkhooraq shene kadh tekhzon lavraham wliyskhaq walyaqoov walkhulhon nviyye bmalkootha dalaha aton dayn tehon mapqeen lvar
29	wnithon men madhnkha wmen marva wmen taymna wmen garvya wnestamkon bmalkootha dalaha
30	wha eeth khrayye dnehon qadhmayye wiyth qadhmayye dnehon khrayye
31	beh bhaw yawma qrevw nasha men preeshe wamreen leh pooq zel lakh meka metul dherawdhes save lmeqtlakh
32	amar lhon yeshoo zelw emarw lthala hana dha mapeq na shidhe waswatha avedh na yawmana wamkhar walyawma dathlatha meshtamle na
33	bram wale lee dyawmana wamkhar esoor walyawma khrina izal metul dla meshkkha danviyya nivadh lvar men oorishlem
34	oorishlem oorishlem qatlath nviyye wraghmath laylayn dashleekheen lwathah kma zavneen sveeth lamkhanashoo bnayky aykh tarnaghoolta dkhansha parooghayh tkhayth gefayh wla svayton
35	ha meshtveq lkhon baytkhon kharba amar na lkhon gayr dla tekhzoonany dhama dthimron breekh oo dathe bashmeh dmarya
