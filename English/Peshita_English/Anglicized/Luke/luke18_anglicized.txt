1	emar lhon dayn af mathla davkhul edan nsalon wla teman lhon
2	dayyana khadh eeth wa bamdheeta khdha dmen alaha la dakhel wa wmen bnaynasha la methkakhadh wa
3	armalta dayn khdha eeth wath bamdheeta ha wathya wath lwatheh wamra tvaayny men bel deeny
4	wla save wa zavna sagiyya batharken dayn emar bnafsheh en men alaha la dakhel na wmen bnaynasha la methkakhadh na
5	afen metul dmaleya lee hadhe armalta ethbeeh dla bkhul edan tehwe athya mahra lee
6	wemar maran shmaw mana emar dayyana dawla
7	alaha dayn la yateeraeeth nebedh tvatha laghvawy dqarayn leh biymama wavlilya wmagar rookheh layhon
8	amar na lkhon dnebedh tvathhon baghal bram nithe breh dnasha wneshkakh kay haymanootha al ara
9	wemar wa mathla hana looqval nasha aylayn dathkheeleen al nafshhon diythayhon zadeeqe wvasayn al kulnash
10	trayn gavreen sleqw lhaykla lamsalayyoo khadh preesha wakhrina makhsa
11	whaw preesha qaem wa baynawy lnafsheh whalayn msale wa alaha mawde na lakh dla weeth aykh sharka dnasha khatoofe waloove wghayyare wla aykh hana makhsa
12	ela saem na trayn bshabtha wamasar na kul medem dqane na
13	haw dayn makhsa qaem wa men rookhqa wla save wa af la aynawy nreem lashmayya ela taref wa al khadhyeh wamar alaha khoonayny lkhatayya
14	amar na lkhon dankheth hana mzadaq lvayteh yateer men haw preesha kul nash gayr danreem nafsheh nethmakakh wkhul dnamekh nafsheh nettreem
15	qarevw waw leh dayn af yaloodhe dneqroov lhon wakhzaw enon talmeedhawy wakhaw bhon
16	hoo dayn yeshoo qra enon wemar lhon shvooqw tlayye athayn lwathy wla tekhlon enon ddhaylayn gayr daykh halayn enon deelhon hee malkootha dashmayya
17	ameen amar na lkhon dman dla nqabel malkootha dalaha aykh talya la neool lah
18	wshaleh khadh men rishane wemar leh malfana tava mana ebedh dirath khayye dalalam
19	amar leh yeshoo mana qare at lee tava layt tava ela en khadh alaha
20	pooqdane yadha at la teqtool wla tghoor wla teghnoov wla teshadh sahdootha dshooqra yaqar lavookh wlemakh
21	amar leh halayn kulhayn netreth enayn men talyoothy
22	kadh shma dayn halayn yeshoo emar leh khdha khaseera lakh zel zaben kul medem diyth lakh whav lmeskine wthehwe lakh seemtha bashmayya wtha bathary
23	hoo dayn kadh shma halayn keryath leh ateer wa gayr tav
24	wkhadh khza yeshoo dkheryath leh emar aykana atla laylayn diyth lhon nekhse dnelon lmalkootheh dalaha
25	dleela y lghamla dvakhroora damkhata neool aw ateera lmalkootheh dalaha
26	amreen leh aylayn dashmaw wmanoo meshkakh lmekha
27	yeshoo dayn emar aylayn dalwath bnay nasha la meshkkhan lwath alaha meshkkhan lmehwa
28	amar leh shemon kifa ha khnan shvaqn kul medem wethayn bathrakh
29	amar leh yeshoo ameen amar na lkhon dlayt nash dshaveq bate aw avahe aw akhe aw attha aw bnayya metul malkootha dalaha
30	wla nqabel bafe sagiyye bhana zavna wavalma dathe khayye dalalam
31	wadhvar yeshoo lathresarteh wemar lhon ha salqeenan luwrishlem wmeshtalman kulhayn dakhtheevan banviyye al breh dnasha
32	neshtlem gayr lamme wanvazkhon beh wnerqon bapawy
33	wannagdhoona wansaroona wneqtloona walyawma dathlatha nqoom
34	henon dayn khdha men halayn la estakalw ela mkhasya wath menhon meltha hadhe wla yadheen waw halayn dmethmallan amhon
35	wkhadh qareev liyreekhoo samya khadh yathev wa al yadh oorkha wkhadhar
36	washma qal kensha davar wamshael wa dmanoo hana
37	amreen leh yeshoo nasrayya avar
38	waqa wemar yeshoo breh ddhaweedh ethrakham lay
39	waylayn dazeen waw qdhamawy dyeshoo kaayn waw beh dneshtooq hoo dayn yateeraeeth qae wa breh ddhaweedh ethrakham lay
40	wqam yeshoo wafqadh dnaytoona lwatheh wkhadh qrev lwatheh shaleh
