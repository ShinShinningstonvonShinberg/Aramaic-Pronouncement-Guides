1	hwa dayn kadh knash lawy kensha lmeshma meltha dalaha whoo qaem wa al yadh yamtha dghenesar
2	khza sfeene tartayn dqaman al gev yamtha wsayyadhe dasleqw menhayn wamsheegheen mseedhathhon
3	wakhdha menhayn dshemon hwath kifa wasleq yeshoo eethev bah wemar dnedhbroonah qaleel men yavsha lmayya wyathev wa wmalef men sfeeta lkhensha
4	wkhadh shtheq men mamlleh emar lshemon dvarw loomqa warmaw mseedhtkhon lsayda
5	na shemon wemar leh rabee lilya kuleh liyn wmedem la ekhadhn al melthakh dayn rame na mseedhta
6	wkhadh hadhe vadhw khvashw noone sagiyye dtav wmestarya wath mseedhthon
7	warmazw lkhavrayhon dvasfeeta khritha dnithon nadron enon wkhadh ethaw mlaw enayn sfeene tartayhayn aykh dqareevan way lmetba
8	kadh khza dayn shemon kifa nfal qdham reghlawy dyeshoo wemar leh bae na menakh mary prooq lakh meny dghavra na khatayya
9	temha gayr akhdeh wa walkhulhon dameh al sayda haw dnoone dsadhw
10	hakhwath dayn af lyaqoov walyookhanan bnay zavdhay diythayhon waw shawtafe dshemon emar dayn yeshoo lshemon la tedhkhal men hasha bnay nasha tehwe saedh lkhayye
11	wqarevw enayn sfeene lara washvaqw kul medem wethaw bathreh
12	wkhadh hwa yeshoo bakhdha men mdheenatha etha gavra damle kuleh garva khza lyeshoo wanfal al apawy wvae wa meneh wemar leh mary en save at meshkakh at lamdhakayyoothy
13	wafshat eedheh yeshoo qrev leh wemar leh save na etdaka wvar shatheh ezal meneh garveh wetdakee
14	wfaqdheh dalnash la timar ela zel khawa nafshakh lkhahne wqarev khlaf tadhkeethakh aykana dfaqedh mooshe lsahdoothhon
15	wanfaq lawy teba yateeraeeth wmethkanash wa ama sagiyya lmeshma meneh walmethasayyoo men koorhanayhon
16	hoo dayn mshane wa ldhavra wamsale
17	wahwa bkhadh men yawmatha kadh malef wa yeshoo yathbeen waw preeshe wmalfay namoosa dethaw waw men kul qoorya daghleela wdheehoodh wdhuwrishlem wkhayla dmarya eethawy wa lmasayyoothhon
18	wnasha ayteew barsa gavra khadh msharya wvaayn waw dnalon nseemoona qdhamawy
19	wkhadh la eshkakhw daykana naloona metul soogha dama sleqw lhon legara wshaboo am arseh men tatleela lamsatha qdhamawy dyeshoo
20	kadh khza dayn yeshoo haymanoothhon emar lhaw msharya gavra shveeqeen lakh khtahayk
21	wshareew safre wafreeshe methkhashveen wamreen manoo hana dammalel goodafa manoo meshkakh lmeshbaq khtahe ela en alaha balkhoodh
22	yeshoo dayn eedha makhshvathhon wana wemar lhon mana methkhashveen ton blebkhon
23	aydha psheeqa lmimar dashveeqeen lakh khtahayk aw lmimar qoom halekh
24	dthedon dayn dshaleet oo breh dnasha bara dneshbooq khtahe amar lamsharya lakh amar na qoom shqool arsakh wzel lvaytakh
25	wmekhdha qam linayhon washqal arseh wezal lvayteh kadh mshabakh lalaha
26	wthemha ekhadh lkhulnash wamshabkheen waw lalaha wethmleew dekhltha wamreen dakhzayn yawmana tedhmratha
27	bathar halayn nfaq yeshoo wakhza makhsa dashmeh lewee dyathev bayth makhse wemar leh ta bathary
28	washvaq kul medem wqam ezal bathreh
29	wavadh leh lewee bvayteh qoobala raba wiyth wa kensha sagiyya dmakhse wdhakhrane dasmeekheen hwaw amhon
30	wratneen waw safre wafreeshe wamreen lthalmeedhawy lmana am makhse wkhatayye akhleen ton wshathayn
31	wana yeshoo wemar lhon la methbe asya lakhleeme ela laylayn dveesh beesh veedheen
32	la etheeth deqre lzadeeqe ela lkhatayye lathyavootha
33	henon dayn amreen leh lmana talmeedhawy dyookhanan sameen ameenaeeth wamsalayn af dafreeshe deelakh dayn akhleen wshathayn
34	hoo dayn emar lhon la meshkkheen ton lavnawy daghnoona kma dkhathna amhon dthebdhon dansoomon
35	nithon dayn yawmatha kadh nettreem khathna menhon hadayn nsoomon bhanon yawmatha
36	wemar lhon mathla dla nash qaedh oorqatha men mana khatha wrame al mana blayya dla lkhatha qaedh wlavlayya la shalma oorqatha dmen khatha
37	wla nash rame khamra khatha bzeqe blayyatha wen dayn la mvaza khamra khatha lzeqe whoo khamra metheshedh wzeqe avdan
38	ela khamra khatha bzeqe khatatha ramayn wathrayhon methnatreen
39	wla nash shathe khamra ateeqa wmekhdha bae khatha amar gayr ateeqa baseem
