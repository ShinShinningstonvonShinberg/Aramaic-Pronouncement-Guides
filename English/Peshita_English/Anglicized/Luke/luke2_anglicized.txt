1	hwa dayn byawmatha hanon wanfaq pooqdana men aghoostaws qesar dnethkthev kuleh ama duwkhdhaneh
2	hadhe makhtvanootha qadhmata hwath bhighmanootha dqooreenaws bsooriyya
3	wazel wa kulnash dnethkthev bamdheeteh
4	sleq wa dayn af yawsef men nasrath mdheeta daghleela leehoodh lamdheeteh ddhaweedh dmethqarya bayth-lkhem metul diythawy wa men bayteh wmen sharbtheh ddhaweedh
5	am maryam mkheerteh kadh batna dthaman nethkthev
6	wahwa dkhadh taman enon ethmaleew yawmathah dthiladh
7	wyeldath brah bookhra wakhrakhteh bazroore warmyatheh buwrya metul dlayt wa lhon dooktha ayka dashrayn waw
8	raawatha dayn eeth waw beh bathra dashrayn waw taman wnatreen matartha dlilya al maryathhon
9	wha malakha dalaha etha lwathhon wtheshbookhteh dmarya anhrath layhon wadhkhelw dekhltha rabtha
10	wemar lhon malakha la tedhkhlon ha gayr msabar na lkhon khadhootha rabtha dthehwe lkhuleh alma
11	etheeledh lkhon gayr yawmana parooqa diythawy marya msheekha bamdheeteh ddhaweedh
12	whadhe lkhon atha meshkkheen ton oola dakhreekh bazroore wseem buwrya
13	wmen shely ethkhzeew am malakha khaylawatha sagiyye dashmayya kadh mshabkheen lalaha wamreen
14	teshbookhta lalaha bamrawme wal ara shlama wsavra tava lavnay nasha
15	wahwa dkhadh ezalw men lwathhon malakhe lashmayya malelw raawatha khadh am khadh wamreen nerde dhama lvayth-lkhem wnekhze lmeltha hadhe dahwath aykh dmarya awda lan
16	wethaw msarhvaeeth weshkakhw lmaryam walyawsef waloola dseem buwrya
17	wkhadh khzaw awdaw lmeltha dethmallath amhon lawy al talya
18	wkhulhon dashmaw etdamarw al aylayn dethmalel lhon men raawatha
19	maryam dayn natra wath kulhayn mele halayn wamfakhma blebah
20	wahfakhw raawatha hanon kadh mshabkheen wamhalleen lalaha al kul dakhzaw washmaw aykana dethmalal amhon
21	wkhadh mlaw tmanya yawmeen dnethgzar talya ethqree shmeh yeshoo dethqree men malakha qdham dnethbten bkharsa
22	wkhadh ethmaleew yawmatha dthadhkeethhon aykh namoosa dmooshe asqoo luwrishlem danqeemoona qdham marya
23	aykh dakhtheev bnamoosa dmarya dkhul dekhra pathakh marba qadeesha dmarya nethqre
24	wadhnetlon devkhtha aykana dameer bnamoosa dmarya zawga dshoofneene aw trayn parooghe dyawna
25	gavra dayn khadh eeth wa buwrishlem shmeh hwa shemon wghavra hana kiyn wa wzadeeq wamsake wa lvooyyaeh diysrayyel wrookha dqoodhsha eeth wath lawy
26	wameer wa leh men rookha dqoodhsha dla nekhze mawta dhama dnekhze lamsheekheh dmarya
27	hoo hana etha wa brookha lhaykla wkhadh maleen leh avahawy lyeshoo talya dnebdhon khlafawy aykana dafqeedh bnamoosa
28	qableh al draawy wvarekh lalaha wemar
29	mekeel shara at leh lavdakh mary aykh melthakh bashlama
30	dha khzay aynay khnanakh
31	haw dtayyevt bparsoopa dkhulhayn emwatha
32	noohra lghelyana damme wshoovkha lamakh eesrayyel
33	yawsef dayn wemeh tameeheen waw al aylayn dmethmallan way lawy
34	wvarekh enon shemon wemar lmaryam emeh ha hana seem lmapoolta wlaqyama dsagiyye biysrayyel wlatha dkheryana
35	wavnafshekhy dayn deelekhy tebar roomkha aykh dnethgalyan makhshvatha dlebawatha dsagiyye
36	wkhana dayn nveetha bartheh dafnooeel men shavta dasheer af hee qasheeshath byawmathah wath washva shneen am balah khyath men bthooloothah
37	wahwath armalta aykh shneen tmaniyn warba wla parqa wath men haykla wavsawma wvaslootha palkha wath biymama wavlilya
38	waf hee qamath bah bshatha wawdyath lmarya wammalla wath lawy am kulnash damsake wa lfoorqanah duwrishlem
39	wkhadh shalemw kul medem aykh davnamoosa dmarya hfakhw laghleela lnasrath mdheethon
40	talya dayn rave wa wmethkhayyal brookha wmethmale khekhmtha wtaybootha dalaha eeth wath lawy
41	wnashawy bkhul shna azeen waw luwrishlem badhidha dfeskha
42	wkhadh hwa bar shneen tartaesre sleqw aykana damadheen waw ladhidha
43	wkhadh shlemw yawmatha hfakhw lhon yeshoo dayn talya pash leh buwrishlem wyawsef wemeh la eedhaw
44	savreen waw gayr dam bnay lweethhon oo wkhadh ethaw marde yawma khadh baoo lwath nashoothhon walwath man dyadha lhon
45	wla eshkkhoo wahfakhw lhon toov luwrishlem wvaayn waw leh
46	wmen bathar tlatha yawmeen eshkkhoo bhaykla kadh yathev mesath malfane wshama menhon wamshael lhon
47	wthameeheen hwaw kulhon aylayn dshameen waw leh bkhekhmtheh wavfethghamawy
48	wkhadh khzaoo tmahw wemrath leh emeh bery lmana vadt lan hakhana dha avookh wena btoorafa sagiyya baayn hwayn lakh
49	amar lhon mana baayn hwayton lee la yadheen ton dvayth avy wale lee dehwe
50	henon dayn la eshtawdaw lmeltha demar lhon
51	wankheth amhon wetha lnasrath wmeshtabadh wa lhon emeh dayn natra wath kulhayn mele blebah
52	yeshoo dayn rave wa bqawmtheh wavkhekhmtheh wavtaybootha lwath alaha wavnaynasha
