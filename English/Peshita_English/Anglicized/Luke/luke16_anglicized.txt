1	wemar mathla lwath talmeedhawy gavra khadh eeth wa ateera wiyth wa leh rabayta wethekhelw leh qarsawy dqenyaneh mfarakh
2	waqra mareh wemar leh manaw hana dshama na layk hav lee khooshbana drabath baytoothakh la gayr meshkakh at mekeel rabayta dthehwe lee
3	amar haw rabayta bnafsheh mana ebedh dmary shaqel lah meny rabath baytootha dekhpoor la meshkakh na walmekhdar baheth na
4	yedheth mana ebedh dma dnefqeth men rabath baytootha nqabloonany bvatayhon
5	waqra khadh khadh men khayyave dmareh wemar lqadhmayya kma khayyav at lmary
6	amar leh ma mathryan meshkha amar leh sav kthavakh wthev baghal kthoov khamsheen mathryan
7	wemar lakhrina wat mana khayyav at lmary amar leh ma kooreen khete amar leh qabel kthavakh wthev kthoov tmaniyn kooreen
8	wshabakh maran lrabayta dawla dkhakeemaeeth vadh bnawy gayr dalma hana khakeemeen enon men bnawy dnoohra bsharbathhon hadhe
9	waf ena amar na lkhon davedhw lkhon rakhme men mamoona hana dawla dma daghmar nqabloonakhon bamtalayhon dalalam
10	man davqaleel mhayman af bsagee mhayman oo wman davqaleel awal af bsagee awal oo
11	en hakheel bmamoona dawla mhaymne la hwayton shrara lkhon manoo mhaymen
12	wen badhla deelkhon la eshtkhakhton mhaymne deelkhon manoo netel lkhon
13	layt avda dmeshkakh lathrayn marawan lmeflakh aw gayr lkhadh nesne wlakhrina nerkham aw lkhadh nyaqar wlakhrina nshoot la meshkkheen ton lmeflakh lalaha walmamoona
14	preeshe dayn kadh shmaw halayn kulhayn metul drakhmeen waw kespa mmayqeen waw beh
15	yeshoo dayn emar lhon aton enon damzadqeen nafshhon qdham bnay nasha alaha dayn yadha lebawathkhon dmedem dram bayth bnaynasha qdham alaha ndheedh oo
16	namoosa wanviyye dhama lyookhanan men hadayn malkootha dalaha mestabra wkhul lah khaves dneool
17	psheeq oo dayn dashmayya wara nebron aw athootha khdha men namoosa tebar
18	kul man dshare attheh wnasev khritha gaar wkhul man dnasev shveeqta gaar
19	gavra dayn khadh ateera eeth wa wlavesh wa boosa wargwana wkhulyoom methbasmeen wa gayaeeth
20	wmeskina khadh eeth wa dashmeh laazar warme wa lwath tareh dhaw ateera kadh mmakhay bshookhne
21	wmethyaav wa dnemle karseh men partoothe dnafleen men pathooreh dhaw ateera ela af kalbe athayn waw mlakhkheen shookhnawy
22	hwa dayn wmeeth haw meskina wawbloo malakhe loobeh davraham af haw dayn ateera meeth wethqvar
23	wkhadh meshtanaq bashyool areem aynawy men rookhqa wakhza lavraham wallaazar boobeh
24	waqra bqala rama wemar avy avraham ethrakham lay wshadar llaazar dnesboo reesh seveh bmayya wanratev lee leshany dha meshtanaq na bshalheveetha hadhe
25	amar leh avraham bery etdar dqabelt tavathakh bkhayyayk wlaazar beeshatheh whasha ha mettneekh harka wat meshtanaq
26	wam halayn kulhayn hawtha rabtha seema baynayn walkhon daylayn dsavayn meka dnebron lwathkhon la neshkkhon wafla dmen taman nebron lwathan
27	amar leh madhayn bae na menakh avy dathshadreewy lvayth avy
28	khamsha gayr akheen eeth lee nizal nsahedh enon dla af henon nithon ldhooktha hadhe dthashneeqa
29	amar leh avraham eeth lhon mooshe wanviyye neshmon enon
30	hoo dayn emar leh la avy avraham ela en nash men meethe nizal lwathhon tabeen
31	amar leh avraham en lmooshe wlanviyye la shameen af la en nash men meethe nqoom mhaymneen leh
