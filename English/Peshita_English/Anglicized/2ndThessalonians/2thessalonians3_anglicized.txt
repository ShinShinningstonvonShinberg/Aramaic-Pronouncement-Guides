1	men hasha akhayn salaw layn dmeltheh dmaran tehwe rahta wmeshtabkha bkhul dooka aykh dalwathkhon
2	wadhnethpase men bnaynasha beeshe wanathe law gayr dkhulnash ee haymanootha
3	mhayman oo dayn marya dhoo nnatarkhon wanshawzevkhon men beesha
4	tkheeleenan dayn laykon bmaran dmedem damfaqdheenan lkhon vadton af avdeeton
5	wmaran nethroos lebawathkhon lkhoobeh dalaha wlamsabranootheh damsheekha
6	mfaqdheenan lkhon dayn akhay bashmeh dmaran yeshoo msheekha dahwayton pareeqeen men kul akha ayna dveesh beesh mhalekh wla aykh pooqdane dqabelw menan
7	aton gayr yadheen ton aykana wale lmetdamayyoo ban dla halekhn beesh beesh baynathkhon
8	afla lakhma dmagan ekhaln men nash menkhon ela bamla wavleootha blilya wviymama palkheen wayn dal nash menkhon la niqar
9	la wa metul dla shaleet lan ela davnafshan netel lkhon toofsa dvan tetdamon
10	af kadh lwathkhon wayn gayr hadhe mfaqdheen wayn lkhon dkhul dla save dneflookh afla nelas
11	shameenan gayr diyth bkhon nasha dveesh beesh mhalkheen wmedem la palkheen ela en sreeqatha
12	lhon dayn lhalayn mfaqdheenan wvaaynan menhon bmaran yeshoo msheekha davshelya nehon palkheen wakhleen lakhmhon
13	ton dayn akhay la teman lkhon lmebadh dshapeer
14	wen nash la meshtma lmelayn halayn dvegarta nethpresh lkhon hana wla hwayton methkhalteen ameh dnevhath
15	wla aykh beldvava tekhdooneh ela hwayton martayn leh aykh dlakha
16	hoo dayn mareh dashlama netel lkhon shlama bkhulzvan bkhulmedem maran am kulkhon
17	shlama bakhthavath eedhay ena pawlaws kethbeth diythawy atha davkhulhayn egrathy hakhana kathev na
18	taybootheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha am kulkhon ameen
