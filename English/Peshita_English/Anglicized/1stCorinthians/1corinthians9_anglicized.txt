1	lma la weeth bar khire aw la weeth shleekha aw lyeshoo msheekha maran la khzeeth aw la wayton vadhy bmary
2	wen lakhrane la weeth shleekha ela lkhon eethay wkhathma dashleekhoothy aton enon
3	wmapaq brookhy laylayn ddhaneen lee hanaw
4	lma la shaleet lan lmekhal walmeshta
5	walma la shaleet lan khatha attha lmakhrakhoo aman aykh sharka dashleekhe waykh akhawy dmaran waykh kifa
6	aw ena balkhoodh wvarnava layt lan shooltana dla neflookh
7	manoo dmaflakh bfalkhootha bnafqatha dnafsheh aw manoo dnasev karma wmen pirawy la akhel aw manoo drae na wmen khalva dmareetheh la akhel
8	dalma aykh barnasha halayn amar na ha af namoosa halayn emar
9	ktheev gayr beh bnamoosa dmooshe dla tevloom tawra dmadhrekh lma al tawre bteel leh lalaha
10	ela eedheea dmetulathan oo emar wmetulathan gayr ethkathbath metul dal savra hoo wale leh lkharoova dnekhroov wayna dmadhrekh al savra dalaltha
11	en khnan drookha zran bkhon raba y en khnan menkhon dfaghra nekhsoodh
12	wen lakhrane eeth lhon shooltana laykon la lan wale yateeraeeth ela la ethkhashakhn bshooltana hana ela kulmedem msaybreenan davmedem la nthakes svartheh damsheekha
13	la yadheen ton daylayn dvayth qoodhsha palkheen men bayth qoodhsha mestaybreen waylayn dalmadhbkha palkheen am madhbkha palgeen
14	hakhana af maran paqedh daylayn dasvartheh makhrzeen men svartheh nikhon
15	ena dayn la ethkhashkheth bakhdha men halayn wlaw metul hadhe kethbeth dhakhana nehwe lee paqakh lee gayr dammath emooth wla dnash shoovhary nsareq
16	af damsabar na gayr layt lee shoovhara qteera gayr seem lay wa lee dayn ela esabar
17	en gayr bsevyany hadhe saar na aghra eeth lee en dayn dla bsevyany rabath baytootha hoo mhayman na
18	ayna hoo hakheel aghry dkhadh msabar na dla nfaqtha ebdheeh lasvartheh damsheekha wla ethkhashakh bshooltana dyav lee bewangeliyyawn
19	kadh mkharar na gayr men kulhayn lkhulnash shabdheth nafshy dalsagiyye ithar
20	wahweeth am eehoodhayye aykh eehoodhayya dleehoodhayye ithar wam dathkhayth namoosa enon hweeth aykh dathkhayth namoosa dlaylayn dathkhayth namoosa enon ithar
21	wlaylayn dnamoosa layt lhon hweeth aykh dla namoos kadh la eethay lalaha dla namoos ela bnamooseh damsheekha daf lhanon dadhla namoos enon ithar
22	hweeth am kreehe aykh kreeha dlakhreehe ithar lkhulnash kul hweeth dalkhulnash akhe
23	hadhe dayn avedh na metul dehwe shawtafa lasvartha
24	la yadheen ton daylayn dvestadhiyyawn rahteen kulhon oo rahteen ela khadh oo nasev lah zakhootha hakhana hatw aykh dthadhrkhon
25	kul nash dayn daghoona avedh men kul medem akhedh reyaneh whalayn rahteen dnesvon kleela dmethkhabal khnan dayn dla methkhabal
26	ena hakheel hakhana rahet na la wa aykh dal medem dla eedhee whakhana methkatash na la aykh haw dlaar kathesh
27	ela paghry oo kavesh na wamshabedh na ddhalma na dlakhrane akhrzeth ena qnoomy estle lee
