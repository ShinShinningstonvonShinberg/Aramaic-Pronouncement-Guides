5	afen gayr eeth dmethqrayn alahe aw bashmayya aw bara aykana diyth alahe sagiyye wmarawatha sagiyye

7	ela la wa bkhulnash eedhatha eeth gayr nasha davthirthon dhama lhasha dal pthakhre aykh dadhveekha akhleen wmetul dakhreeha tirthon mettawsha
8	mekhoolta dayn la mqarva lan lalaha la gayr en akhleenan methyatreenan wla en la nekhool methbasreenan
