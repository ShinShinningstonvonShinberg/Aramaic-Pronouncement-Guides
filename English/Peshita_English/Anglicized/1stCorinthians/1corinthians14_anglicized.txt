1	hatw bathar khooba wtanw bmawhvatha drookha yateeraeeth dayn dthethnabon
2	man dammalel gayr bleshana la wa lavnaynasha mmalel ela lalaha la gayr nash shama medem dammalel ela brookh raza mmalel
3	ayna dayn dmethnabe lavnaynasha mmalel benyana wloobava wvooyyaa
4	dammalel bleshana nafsheh hoo bane wadhmethnabe ita bane
5	save na dayn dkhulkhon tmallon bleshane yateeraeeth dayn dthethnabon rav oo gayr ayna dmethnabe men haw dammalel bleshana en la mfasheq en dayn mfasheq ita bane
6	whasha akhay en ithe lwathkhon wemalel amkhon bleshane mana mawtar na lkhon ela en emalel amkhon aw bghelyana aw beedhatha aw banviyyootha aw byoolpana
7	af sevwatha gayr aylayn dlayt bhayn nafsha wyahban qala en aboova wen qeethara en poorshana la avdan bayth qeenta lakhvarthah aykana metheedha medem dmezdmar aw medem dmethnqesh
8	wen qarna teqre qala dla preesh manoo nettayyav laqrava
9	hakhana af aton en timron meltha bleshana wla tehwe mfashqa aykana metheedha medem damreen ton hawayton lkhon aykh haw dam aar mmalleen ton
10	ha gayr gense dleshane sagiyye eeth balma wlayt khadh menhon dla qala
11	wen la yadha na khayleh dqala hawe na lee barbrayya lhaw dammalel waf haw dammalel hawe leh lee barbrayya
12	hakhana af aton metul dtanane aton dmawhvatha drookha lvenyana dita baw dthethyatron
13	whaw dammalel bleshana nsale danfasheq
14	en hoo gayr dahweeth msale bleshana rookhy oo msalya maday dayn dla pireen oo
15	mana hakheel ebedh esale brookhy wesale af bmaday wezamar brookhy wezamar af bmaday
16	wen la en mvarekh at brookh haw dammale dooktheh dhedhyoota aykana nimar ameen al tawdeethakh deelakh metul dmana amar at la yadha
17	at gayr shapeer mvarekh at ela khavrakh la methbane
18	mawde na lalaha dyateer men kulkhon mmalel na bleshane
19	ela bita save na dkhamesh meleen bmaday emalel daf khrane alef yateer men reboo meleen bleshana
20	akhay la hwayton tlayn breyanaykon ela lveeshatha hwaw yaloodhe wavreyanaykon hwaw gmeereen
21	bnamoosa ktheev davmamlla nookhrayya wavleshana khrina emalel ameh am ama hana afla hakhana neshmoonany amar marya
22	madhayn leshane latha hoo seemeen la lamhaymne ela laylayn dla mhaymneen nviyyawatha dayn la wa laylayn dla mhaymneen ela laylayn damhaymneen
23	en hoo hakheel dthethkanash kulah ita wkhulhon bleshane nmallon wnelon hedhyoote aw aylayn dla mhaymneen la amreen dhalayn shnaw lhon
24	wen kulkhon tehon methnabayn wneool lwathkhon hedhyoota aw man dla mhaymen methbse men kulkhon wmethkawan men kulkhon
25	wkhasyatha dlebeh methgalyan whadayn nepel al apawy wnesgoodh lalaha wnimar shareeraeeth alaha eeth bkhon
26	amar na hakheel akhay demathy dmethkansheen ton layna menkhon diyth leh mazmoora nimar wayna diyth leh yoolpana wayna diyth leh gelyana wayna diyth leh leshana wayna diyth leh pooshaqa kulhayn lvenyana nehwyan
27	wen bleshana nash nmalel trayn nmallon wkhadh sagee tlatha wkhadh khadh nmallon wkhadh nfasheq
28	wen layt damfasheq neshtooq leh bita haw dammalel bleshana wvaynawy lnafsheh wlalaha nmalel
29	nviyye dayn trayn aw tlatha nmallon wsharka nfarshon
30	wen lakhrina nethgle kadh yathev qadhmayya neshtooq leh
31	meshkkheen ton gayr kulkhon dkhadh khadh tethnabon dkhulnash nilaf wkhulnash nethbayya
32	rookha gayr danviyye lanviyye meshtabdha
33	metul dalaha la wa dashghooshya ela dashlama aykh davkhulhayn idhatha dqadeeshe
34	neshaykon bita nehwyan shateeqan la gayr mapas lhayn danmallan ela dneshtabdhan aykana daf namoosa emar
35	wen medem savyan dnilpan bvatayhayn nshalan lvalayhayn behttha y gayr dneshe bita nmallan
36	aw dalma menkhon w nefqath meltheh dalaha aw lwathkhon w balkhoodh mtath
37	en nash dayn menkhon savar danviyya hoo aw drookha hoo neda halayn dkhathev na lkhon dfooqdane enon dmaran
38	en nash dayn la yadha la neda
39	tanw hakheel akhay lmethnabayyoo wlammalaloo bleshane la tekhlon
40	kulmedem dayn beskima wavteksa nehwe
