1	al aylayn dakhthavton lee dayn shapeer oo lghavra dlattha la nethqarav
2	ela metul zanyootha nash attheh nekhoodh wattha lvalah tekhoodh
3	gavra lattheh khooba dmettkheev nefroo hakhana af hee attha lvalah
4	attha la shaleeta al paghrah ela balah hakhana af gavra la shaleet al paghreh ela attheh
5	la hakheel teghlzon khadh lkhadh ela emathy dathraykon teshton bazvan dthethnon lsawma wlaslootha wthoov lah lasvootha tethpnon dla nnasaykhon satana metul regtha dfaghrkhon
6	hadhe dayn amar na aykh dlamkheele law men pooqdana
7	ena gayr save na dkhulhon bnaynasha akhwathy nehon bdhakhyootha ela kulnash mawhavta eeheeva leh men alaha eeth dhakhana wiyth dhakhana
8	amar na dayn laylayn dlayt lhon neshe wlarmlatha dfaqakh lhon en nqaon akhwathy
9	en la dayn msaybreen nezdawghon paqakh gayr lmesav attha tav men dalmiqadh bregtha
10	laylayn dayn diyth lhon neshe mfaqedh na la ena ela mary dattha men balah la tefroosh
11	wen tefroosh tqawe dla gavra aw lvalah tethrae wghavra lattheh la neshbooq
12	lsharka dayn amar na ena la mary en eeth akha diyth leh attha dla mhaymna whee savya dthemar ameh la neshbqeeh
13	wattha aydha diyth lah bala dla mhaymen whoo save dnemar amah la teshbooq balah
14	mqadash oo gayr gavra ayna dla mhaymen battha damhaymna wamqadsha y attha aydha dla mhaymna bghavra damhaymen wen la bnayhon tamiyn enon hasha dayn dkhayn enon
15	en dayn haw dla mhaymen paresh nefroosh la mshabadh akha aw khatha bhalayn lashlama hoo qran alaha
16	mana gayr yadha aty attha en lvalekhy takhayn aw at gavra yadha at en latthakh takhe
17	ela nash nash aykh daflagh leh marya wnash aykh daqra alaha hakhana nhalekh waf lkhulhayn idhatha hakhana mfaqedh na
18	en kadh gzeer nash ethqree la nehpookh leh loorlootha wen boorlootha ethqree la neghzoor
19	gzoorta gayr la wath medem af la oorlootha ela ntooratha dfooqdanawy dalaha
20	kulnash bqeryana dethqree beh nqawe
21	en avda ethqreet la nethbtel lakh ela afen meshkakh at lmethkhararoo gvee lakh dtheflookh
22	ayna gayr davda ethqree bmaran mkharra hoo dalaha hakhana af ayna dvar khire ethqree avda hoo damsheekha
23	badhmayya ezdventon la tehon avde davnaynasha
24	kulnash bmedem dethqree akhay beh nqawe lwath alaha
25	al bthoolootha dayn pooqdana men alaha la akheedh na melka dayn yahev na aykh gavra dethkhanneth men alaha dehwe mhayman
26	wsavar na dhadhe shapeera metul ananqi dzavna dfaqakh leh lvarnasha dhakhana nehwe
27	aseer at battha la teve shrayya shre at men attha la teve attha
28	wen tesav attha la khate at wen bthoolta tehwe lghavra la khatya oolsana dayn bafghar hawe laylayn dhakhana enon ena dayn laykon khaes na
29	whadhe amar na akhay dzavna mekeel ezdalhaz leh daylayn diyth lhon neshe nehon aykh dlayt lhon
30	waylayn dvakhayn aykh la bakhayn waylayn dkhadhayn aykh la khadhayn waylayn dzavneen aykh la mqadayn
31	waylayn dmethkhashkheen balma hana la lvar men zedhqa dakhshakhtha avar leh gayr eskimeh dalma hana
32	metul hana save na dadhla seftha tehon ayna gayr dlayt leh attha rane badhmareh daykana neshpar lmareh
33	wayna diyth leh attha yasef dalma daykana neshpar lattheh
34	poorshana dayn eeth af baynath attha lavthoolta aydha dalghavra la hwath ranya bmarah dthehwe qadeesha bfaghrah wavrookhah waydha diyth lah bala ranya dalma daykana teshpar lvalah
35	hadhe dayn loodhrankhon oo deelkhon amar na la wa makhnooqeetha rame na lkhon ela dthehon ameeneen lwath markhon beskima shapeera kadh la ranayn ton balma
36	en nash dayn savar dmethbazakh bavthoolteh davar zavnah wla yahbah lghavra wwalya dnetleeh aykh dsave nebedh la khate tezdawagh
37	ayna dayn dshareeraeeth psaq breyaneh wla alsa leh svootha wshaleet al sevyaneh whakhana dan blebeh dnetar bthoolteh shapeer avedh
38	wayna hakheel dyahev bthoolteh shapeer avedh wayna dla yahev bthoolteh yateeraeeth shapeer avedh
39	attha kma dkhay balah aseera y bnamoosa en dayn nedhmakh balah mkharra y dthehwe lman dsavya balkhoodh bmaran
40	toovayh dayn en hakhana tqawe aykh reyany deely savar na dayn af ena drookha dalaha eeth bee
