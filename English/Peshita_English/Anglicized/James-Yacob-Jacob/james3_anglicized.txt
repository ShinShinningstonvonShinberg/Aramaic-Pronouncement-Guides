1	la sagiyye malfane nehon bkhon akhay ela hwayton yadheen ddheena yateera khayyaveenan
2	sagiyyatha gayr meshtareenan kulan kul davmeltha la shara hana eethawy gavra gmeera dmeshkakh nshabedh af kuleh paghreh
3	ha gayr pghoodhe bfooma drakhsha ramaynan aykh dneshtabdhon lan wkhuleh gooshmhon mahpkheenan
4	af elfe asheenatha kadh dveeran lhayn rookhe qashyatha men qaysa zoora methnatfan lathar dkhaar sevyaneh dhaw damdhabar
5	hakhana af leshana hadama w zoora wmeshtale af noora zoorta ave sagiyye mawqdha
6	wleshana noora w walma dakhteetha aykh ava w whoo leshana kadh eethawy bhadamayn mkhatem leh lkhuleh paghran wmawqedh yoobale dsharvathan drahteen aykh geeghle wyaqedh af hoo bnoora
7	kulhon gayr kyane dkhaywatha wadhfarakhtha wrakhsha dyama wadhyavsha meshtabdheen lakhyana dnashootha
8	leshana dayn nash la eshkakh dnekhbsheewy beeshta hadhe dla mettakhsa mle oo sama dmawta
9	beh mvarkheenan lmarya wava wveh lateenan lavnaynasha dvadhmootha dalaha veedheen
10	wmeneh men pooma nafqan boorkatha wlawtatha la wale akhay dhalayn hakhana nestaran
11	dalma meshkkha dmen khadh mabooa nepqon mayya khlayya wmareere
12	aw dalma meshkkha tita akhay dzayte tebedh aw gfeta tine hakhana af la mayya maleekhe meshkkheen dnethavdon khlayya
13	manoo menkhon dkhakeem wardhe nkhawe vadhawy bhoopakhe shapeere bkhekhmtha makeekhta
14	en dayn khsama mareera eeth bkhon aw kheryana blebaykon la tethkhatron al qooshta watdhaglon
15	metul dhadhe khekhmtha men lel la nekhtath ela eethayh aranata men khooshave dnafsha wmen shidhe
16	ayka gayr diyth khsama wkheryana taman af dlookhya wkhulmedem dveesh
17	khekhmtha dayn dmen lel dakhya y wmalya shlama wmakeekha wmeshtamanya wmalya rakhme wfire tave wadhla palgootha y wvape la nasba
18	pire dayn dzadeeqootha bshayna mezdareen laylayn davdeen shlama
