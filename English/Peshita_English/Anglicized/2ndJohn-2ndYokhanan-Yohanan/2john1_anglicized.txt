1	qasheesha laghveetha qooriyya wlavnayh aylayn dena makhev na bashrara la wa dayn ena balkhoodhay ela kulhon aylayn dyadhoo lashrara
2	metul shrara ayna damqawe ban waman eethawy lalam
3	tehwe aman taybootha wrakhme washlama men alaha ava wmen maran yeshoo msheekha breh dava bashrara wavkhooba
4	khdheeth sagee deshkkheth men bnayky damhalkheen bashrara aykana dfooqdana nsavnan men ava
5	whasha mpees na lekhy qooriyya la wa aykh pooqdana khatha kathev na lekhy ela haw diyth wa lan men shoorayya dnakhev khadh lkhadh
6	whana eethawy khooba danhalekh aykh pooqdanawy hana pooqdana eethawy aykh ma dashmaton men shoorayya dveh hwayton mhalkheen
7	metul dsagiyye matyane nfaqw beh balma aylayn dla mawdayn dyeshoo msheekha etha bavsar hana eethawy matyana wanteekhreestaws
8	ezdarw bnafshkhon dla tawbdhon medem daflakhton ela aghra mshalmana tethparon
9	kul ayna davar wla mqawe byoolpaneh damsheekha alaha layt beh haw damqawe byoolpaneh hana wlava wlavra eeth leh
10	en nash athe lwathkhon whana yoolpana la mayte la tqabloona bvayta wakhdhee lakh la timron leh
11	haw gayr damar leh khdhee lakh mshawtaf oo lavadhawy beeshe
12	kadh sagiyyatha eeth wa lee lmekhtav lkhon la beeth davyadh karteesa wadhyootha ela msabar na dithe lwathkhon wfooma lwath pooma nmalel dkhadhootha deelan tehwe mshamlya
13	shaleen shlamekhy bnayya dkhathekhy gveetha ameen
