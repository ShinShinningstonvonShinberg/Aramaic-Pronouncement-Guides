1	bqadhmata dayn eeth wa bah pooqadhe dtheshmeshta wvayth qoodhsha almanayya
2	mashkna gayr qadhmayya dethvedh eeth wa beh mnarta wfathoora walkhem ape wmethqre wa bayth qoodhsha
3	mashkna dayn gawayya dalghaw men apay tara dathrayn methqre wa qdhoosh qoodhshe
4	wiyth wa beh bayth besme ddhahva wqivootha ddhiyyathiqi daqreema kulah bdhahva wiyth bah qesta ddhahva ha diyth wa bah manna wshavta dahron haw dafra wlookhe ddhiyyathiqi
5	walel menah kroove dshoovkha dmatleen al khoosayya la wa dayn zavna w dnimar al khdha khdha men halayn dhakhana mathqnan way
6	walmashkna barayya bkhulzvan aleen waw koomre wamshalmeen waw teshmeshthon
7	lmashkna dayn dalghaw meneh khdha w bshata balkhoodhawy ael wa rabkoomre badhma haw damqarev wa khlaf nafsheh wakhlaf sakhlootheh dama
8	bhadhe dayn mawda wath rookha dqoodhsha dla ethgalyath dhakeel oorkha dqadeeshe kma zavna diyth wa qyama lmashkna qadhmayya
9	wiythawy wa mathla hana lzavna haw dveh qoorbane wdhevkhe methqarveen waw aylayn dla meshkkheen waw lmeghmar tirteh dman damqarev lhon
10	ela bmekhla wavmashtya balkhoodh wavmamoodhyatha dazneen zneen diythayhayn pooqadhe dvesra dseemeen dhama lzavna dthoorasa
11	msheekha dayn detha hwa rabkoomre dtavatha dasar wal lmashkna raba wamshalmana dla veedh biydhayya wla wa men halayn beryatha
12	wla al badhma dsifrayye wadheghle ela badhma dnafsheh al khdha zvan lvayth maqdsha weshkakh poorqana dalalam
13	en gayr dma dsifrayye wadheghle wqetma daghelta methrses wa al aylayn dmettamiyn waw wamqadesh lhon ldhookayya dvesrhon
14	kma hakheel yateeraeeth dmeh damsheekha davrookha dalalam nafsheh qarev dla moom lalaha ndhake tirtan men vadhe meethe danshamesh lalaha khayya
15	metul hana hoo hwa mesayya ddhiyyathiqi khdhatha davmawteh hoo hwa poorqana laylayn davarw al diyyathiqi qadhmata dnesvon moolkana aylayn dethqreew lyartootha dalalam
16	ayka gayr diyth diyyathiqi mawta w mkhawya dhaw davdah
17	al meetha dayn balkhoodh meshtarra metul dakhma dkhay haw davdah layt bah khashkhoo
18	metul hana afla qadhmata dla dma eshtarrath
19	kadh ethpqedh gayr kuleh pooqdana men mooshe lama kuleh bnamoosa nsav wa mooshe dma daghelta wmayya bamra dazkhooreetha wzoopa wras al sefre wal ama kuleh
20	wemar lhon hana w dma ddhiyyathiqi ha dethpaqdath lkhon men alaha
21	af al mashkna wal kulhon mane dtheshmeshta meneh men dma ras
22	metul dkhulmedem badhma w metdake bnamoosa wadhla shoopa dma layt shoovqana
23	ananqi ee gayr dhalayn dadhmootha enayn dashmayyanyatha bhalayn metdakyan halayn dayn shmayyanyatha bdhevkhe damyatreen men halayn
24	la wa gayr lvayth maqdsha daveedh biydhayya al msheekha diythawy dmootha dhaw shareera ela leh lashmayya al dnethkhze qdham parsoopeh dalaha khlafayn
25	afla danqarev nafsheh zavnatha sagiyyatha aykh davedh wa rab koomre wael bkhul shna lvayth maqdsha badhma dla deeleh
26	wen la khayyav wa dzavnatha sagiyyatha nekhash men shoorayyeh dalma hasha dayn bkhartheh dalma khdha w zvan qarev nafsheh badhveekhootheh danvatleeh lakhteetha
27	waykana dseem lavnaynasha dakhdha zvan nmoothon wmen bathar mawthon deena
28	hakhana af msheekha khdha zvan ethqarav wvaqnoomeh dvakh khtahe dsagiyye dthartayn dayn zavneen dla khtaheen methkhze lkhayyayhon daylayn damsakayn leh
