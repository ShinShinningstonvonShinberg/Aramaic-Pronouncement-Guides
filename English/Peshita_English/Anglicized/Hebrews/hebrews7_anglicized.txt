1	hana dayn malkeezdheq eethawy mlekh shaleem koomra dalaha mrayma whoo areh lavraham kadh hfakh men kharba dmalke wvarkheh
2	wleh prash avraham masare men kulmedem diyth wa ameh methpashaq dayn shmeh malka dkhinootha wthoov mlekh shaleem diythawy malka dashlama
3	dla avoo wla emeh ethkthevw bsharvatha wla reesheetha dyawmawy wla shoolama dkhayyawy ela badhmootha davreh dalaha mqawya koomrootheh lalam
4	khzaw dayn kma rav hana davraham reesh avahatha leh yav masare drishyatha
5	aylayn gayr men bnay lewee damqableen waw koomrootha pooqdana eeth wa lhon dnamoosa dnesvon masare men ama henon men akhayhon kadh af henon men khaseh davraham nfaqw
6	hana dayn dla ktheev bsharvathhon masare shqal men avraham wvarkheh lhaw dqabel moolkana
7	dla kheryana dayn haw davseer methbarakh men haw damyatar meneh
8	wharka bnaynasha dmateen nasbeen masare lhal dayn haw dashedh lawy kthava dkhay oo
9	waykh nash nimar byadh avraham af lewee haw dmasare nasev wa af hoo ethasar
10	dhakeel gayr bkhaseh hwa davoo kadh areh lmalkeezdheq
11	eloo hakheel gmeerootha byadh koomrootha dlewayye eethayh wath dvah seem namoosa lama lmana methbe wa koomra khrina danqoom badhmootheh dmalkeezdheq emar dayn dvadhmootheh dahron nehwe
12	ela aykana dahwa shookhlafa bkhoomrootha hakhana hwa shookhlafa af bnamoosa
13	haw gayr dethemar lawy halayn men sharbtha w khritha etheeledh dnash memthoom menah la shamesh bmadhbkha
14	galya y gayr dmen eehoodha dnakh maran men sharbtha dla emar layh mooshe medem al koomrootha
15	wyateeraeeth toov eedheea bha demar dvadhmootheh dmalkeezdheq qaem koomra khrina
16	haw dla wa bnamoosa dfooqdane paghranayye hawe ela bkhayla dkhayye dla meshtrayn
17	mashedh gayr lawy dat oo koomra lalam badhmootheh dmalkeezdheq
18	shookhlafa dayn dahwa lfooqdana qadhmayya metul mkheelootheh wadhyoothran layt wa beh
19	medem gayr la gmar namoosa al dayn khlafawy savra damyatar meneh dveh methqarveenan lalaha
20	wsharreh lan bmawmatha
21	hanon gayr dla mawmatha hwaw koomre hana dayn bmawmatha aykh demar leh byadh daweedh deema marya wla ndhagel dat oo koomra lalam badhmootheh dmalkeezdheq
22	hana kuleh myatra diyyathiqi hadhe dahwa bah arava yeshoo
23	whanon hwaw koomre sagiyye metul dmateen waw wla meshtavqeen waw danqaon
24	hana dayn metul dalalam qayyam la avra koomrootheh
25	wmeshkakh lmakhayyoo lalam laylayn dmethqarveen biydheh lalaha khay oo gayr bkhulzvan wmaseq slootha khlafayhon
26	daykh hana gayr koomra af zadheq wa lan dakhya dla beeshoo wadhla toolsha dfareeq men khtahe wamrayyam lel men shmayya
27	wlayt leh oolsana kulyoom aykh rabay koomre dlooqdham khlaf khtahawy nqarev devkhe whadayn khlaf ama hadhe gayr avdah khdha zvan bnafsheh dqarev
28	namoosa gayr bnaynasha hoo kreehe mqeem koomre meltha dayn dmawmatha dahwath bathar namoosa bra gmeera lalam
