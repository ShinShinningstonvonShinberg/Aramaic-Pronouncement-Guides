1	reesha dayn dkhulhayn eeth lan rab koomre ayna deethev men yameena dkhoorsya drabootha bashmayya
2	wahwa mshamshana dvayth qoodhsha wadhmashkna dashrara haw daqva alaha wla barnasha
3	kul rab koomre gayr dqaem danqarev qoorbane wdhevkhe metul hana zadhqa wath af lhana dnehwe eeth leh medem danqarev
4	weloo bara wa afla koomra hawe wa metul diythayhon waw koomre damqarveen waw qoorbane aykh davnamoosa
5	hanon damshamsheen ladhmootha waltelanyatha dhalayn dvashmayya aykh dethemar lmooshe kadh avedh wa mashkna dakhzee wavedh kulmedem badhmootha ha dethkhazyath lakh btoora
6	hasha dayn teshmeshta damyatra men ha qabel yeshoo msheekha akhma damyatra af diyyatheeqi ha daveedh bah mesayya wavshoodayye damyatreen men dha ethyahbath
7	eloo gayr eethayh wath qadhmata dla reshyan layt wa athra lhadhe dthartayn
8	rashe lhon gayr wemar dha yawmatha athayn amar marya weghmoor al bayta dvayth eesrayyel wal bayta dvayth eehoodha diyyathiqi khdhatha
9	la aykh ha diyyathiqi dyehbeth lavahayhon byawma dekhdeth biydhhon wapqeth enon men ara dmesrayn metul dhenon la qaweew bdhiyyathiqi deely af ena bseeth bhon emar marya
10	hadhe dayn diyyathiqi detel lvayta dvayth eesrayyel bathar yawmatha hanon amar marya etleewy lnamoosy bmadayhon wal lebawathhon ekhtveewy wehwe lhon ena alaha whenon nehon lee ama
11	wla nalef nash lvar mdheeteh afla lakhoo wnimar ddha lmarya metul dkhulhon nedoonany men zoorhon wadhama lqasheeshhon
12	wekhase enon men awlhon wakhtahayhon toov la etdkhar lhon
13	bha demar khdhatha lqadhmata atqah wayna datheq wasev qareev oo lakhvala
