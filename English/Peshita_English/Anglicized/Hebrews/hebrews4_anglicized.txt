1	nedhkhal hakheel dalma kadh qayyam moolkana dmaalta dlanyakhteh neshtkhakh nash menkhon dfaesh men dalmeal
2	af khnan gayr estabarn aykh daf henon ela la awtrath enon lhanon meltha dashmaw metul dla mmazgha wath bhaymanootha lhanon dshamooh
3	aleenan dayn lanyakhta khnan aylayn dhaymenn aykana dayn emar aykh deemeeth brooghzy dla nelon lanyakhty dha vadhawy dalaha men shoorayyeh dalma hwaw
4	aykh demar al shabtha detneekh alaha byawma shveeayya men vadhawy kulhon
5	wharka toov emar dla nelon lanyakhty
6	metul hakheel diyth wa athra dnash nash neool leh whanon destabarw qadhmayye la alw badhla ettpeesw
7	toov yawma khrina saem men bathar zavna sagiyya aykana dmen lel ktheev demar daweedh dyawmana en bqaleh teshmon la tqashon lebawathkhon
8	eloo gayr yeshoo bar non aneekh wa enon la amar wa men batharken al yawma khrina
9	madhayn qayyam oo lmashbathoo lameh dalaha
10	ayna gayr dal lanyakhteh ettneekh af hoo men vadhawy aykh dalaha men deeleh
11	nethkhapat hakheel dneool lha nyakhta dla nepel badhmootha dhanon dla ettpeesw
12	khayya y gayr meltheh dalaha wkhul sara wkhareefa tav men safsira dathrayn poomayh wala dhama lfoorshana dnafsha wadhrookha wadhsharyatha wadhmookha wadhgharme wdhana makhshvatha wtharyatha dleba
13	wlayt breetha dtashya men qdhamayh ela kul medem artel waghle qdham aynawy dleh yahbeenan pethghama
14	metul diyth lan hakheel rab koomre raba yeshoo msheekha breh dalaha dasleq lashmayya nkhamsen bthawdeetheh
15	la gayr eeth lan rabkoomre dla mse dnekhash am kreehoothan ela damnasay bkhulmedem akhwathan star men khteetha
16	nethqarav hakheel baghle een lkhoorsya dtaybootheh dnesav rakhme wneshkakh taybootha loodhrana bzavna duwlsana
