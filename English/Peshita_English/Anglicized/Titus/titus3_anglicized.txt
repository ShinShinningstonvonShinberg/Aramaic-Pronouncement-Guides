2	wal nash la nghadfon wla nehon methkatsheen ela nehon makeekheen wavkhulmedem nkhaon baseemoothhon lwath kulhon bnaynasha
3	af khnan gayr men qdheem dla reyana wayn wadhla pyasa wtaayn wayn wamshabdheen wayn largheeghatha mshakhlfatha wavveeshootha wvakhsama methhapkheen wayn wasneeeen wayn af khadh lkhadh sanayn wayn
4	kadh dayn ethgalyath baseemootheh wamrakhmanootheh dalaha makhyanan
5	la bavadhe dzadeeqootha davadhn ela brakhmawy deeleh akhyan baskhatha dmawladha dmen dreesh wavkhoodatha drookha dqoodhsha
6	ha deshadh layn ateeraeeth byadh yeshoo msheekha makhyanan
7	davtaybootheh nezdadaq wnehwe yarte bsavra bkhayye dalalam
8	mhaymna y meltha wavhalayn save na daf at hwayt msharar lhon dnethbtel lhon lmeflakh vadhe tave aylayn dhaymenw balaha halayn enayn tavan wmawtran lavnaynasha
9	men batha dayn sakhltha wmen tashyatha dsharvatha wmen kheryane wmen takhtooshe dsafre eshtal yoothran gayr layt bhayn wasreeqan enayn
10	men gavra heresiyyawta men bathar khdha zvan wthartayn dmarte at leh eshtal
11	wahwayt yadha dman dhakhana w maqam oo wkhate whoo khayyev nafsheh
12	ma dshadreth lwathakh lartema aw ltookheeqaws nethbtel lakh dthithe lwathy lneeqapawlees taman gayr sameth breyany daste
13	al zina dayn safra wal apalaw nethbtel lakh dathlawe enon shapeer dmedem la nekhsar lhon
14	wnilpon af aylayn ddheelan enon dnehon palkheen vadhe tave bsevwatha dalsan dla nehon dla pire
15	shaleen bashlamakh kulhon aylayn damy enon shal bashlama dkhulhon aylayn drakhmeen lan bhaymanootha taybootha am kulkhon ameen
