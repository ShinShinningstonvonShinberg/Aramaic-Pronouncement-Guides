1	kadh dayn etheeledh yeshoo bvayth-lkhem deehoodha byawmay herawdhes malka ethaw mghooshe men madhnkha luwrishlem
2	wamreen aykaw malka deehoodhayye detheeledh khzayn gayr kawkveh bmadhnkha wethayn lmesgadh leh
3	shma dayn herawdhes malka wettzee wkhulah oorishlem ameh
4	wkhanesh kulhon rabay kahne wsafre dama wamshael wa lhon dayka metheeledh msheekha
5	henon dayn emarw bvayth-lkhem deehoodha hakhana gayr ktheev banviyya
6	af aty bayth-lkhem deehoodha la hwayty bseera bmalke deehoodha menekhy gayr nepooq malka dhoo neraywy lamy eesrayel
7	hadayn herawdhes matshyaeeth qra lamghooshe weelef menhon bayna zavna ethkhzee lhon kawkva
8	wshadar enon lvayth-lkhem wemar lhon zelw aqevw al talya khfeetaeeth wma deshkakhtoona taw khawaoony daf ena izal esgoodh leh
9	henon dayn kadh shmaw men malka ezalw wha kawkva haw dakhzaw bmadhnkha azel wa qdhamayhon dhama detha qam lel men ayka diythawy talya
10	kadh dayn khzaoo lkhawkva khdheew khadhootha rabtha dtav
11	walw lvayta wakhzaoo ltalya am maryam emeh wanfalw sghedhw leh wafthakhw seemathhon wqarevw leh qoorbane dahva wmoora walvoonta
12	wethkhzee lhon bkhelma dla nehpkhon lwath herawdhes wvuwrkha khritha ezalw lathrhon
13	kadh dayn ezalw ethkhzee malakha dmarya bkhelma lyawsef wemar leh qoom dvar ltalya wlemeh warooq lmesrayn wthaman hwee dhama damar na lakh theedh oo gayr herawdhes lmevyeh ltalya aykh dnawbdheewy
14	yawsef dayn qam shaqleh ltalya wlemeh blelya waraq lmesrayn
15	wahwa taman dhama lmawteh dherawdhes dnethmale medem dethemar men marya banviyya damar dmen mesrayn qreeth lvery
16	hadayn herawdhes kadh khza dethbazakh men mghooshe ethkhamath tav wshadar qatel tlayye kulhon dvayth-lkhem wadhkhulhon tkhoomayh men bar tartayn shneen walthakht aykh zavna daqev men mghooshe
17	hadayn ethmalee medem dethemar byadh eramya nviyya demar
18	qala eshtma bramtha bekhya welya sagiyya rakhayl bakhya al bnayh wla savya lmethbayyaoo metul dla eethayhon
19	kadh meeth dayn herawdhes malka ethkhzee malakha dmarya bkhelma lyawsef bmesrayn
20	wemar leh qoom dvar ltalya wlemeh wzel lara diysrayel meethw lhon gayr hanon dvaayn waw nafsheh dtalya
21	wyawsef qam dvar ltalya wlemeh wetha lara diysrayel
22	kadh dayn shma darkhelaaws hwa malka beehoodh khlaf herawdhes avoo dkhel dnizal lthaman wethkhzee leh bkhelma dnizal lathra daghleela
23	wetha mar bamdheeta dmethqarya nasrath aykh dnethmale medem dethemar banviyya dnasrayya nethqre
