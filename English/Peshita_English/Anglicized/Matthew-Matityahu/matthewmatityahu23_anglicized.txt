1	hadayn yeshoo malel am kenshe wam talmeedhawy
2	wemar lhon al koorsya dmooshe eethevw safre wafreeshe
3	kul medem hakheel dnimron lkhon dthetron tarw wavedhw aykh vadhayhon dayn la tebdhon amreen gayr wla avdeen
4	wasreen mawble yaqeeratha wsameen al kathpatha davnay nasha henon dayn bsevhon la savayn dneqrvon lhayn
5	wkhulhon vadhayhon avdeen dnethkhzon lavnay nasha maftayn gayr teflayhon wmawrkheen tekhlatha dmartootayhon
6	wrakhmeen rish smakhe bakhshamyatha wrish mawtve bakhnooshatha
7	washlama bshooqe wadhnehon methqrayn men nasha rabee
8	aton dayn la tethqron rabee khadh oo gayr rabkhon aton dayn kulkhon akhe aton
9	wava la teqron lkhon bara khadh oo gayr avookhon dvashmayya
10	wla tethqron mdhabrane metul dkhadh oo mdhabrankhon msheekha
11	haw dayn drab bkhon nehwe lkhon mshamshana
12	man gayr danreem nafsheh nethmakakh wman dnamekh nafsheh nettreem
13	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape dakhleen ton bate darmlatha beltha dmawrkheen ton slawathkhon metul hana tqablon deena yateera
14	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape dakheedheen ton malkootha dashmayya qdham bnay nasha aton gayr la aleen ton wlaylayn daleen la shavqeen ton lmeal
15	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape dmethkarkeen ton yama wyavsha dthebdhon khadh giyyoora wma dahwa avdeen ton leh breh dghihana afa laykon
16	wa lkhon naghoodhe smayya damreen ton dman dyame bhaykla la wa medem man dayn dyame bdhahva davhaykla khaev
17	sakhle wasmayya mana gayr rav dahva aw haykla dhoo mqadesh leh ldhahva
18	wman dyame bmadhbkha la wa medem man dayn dyame bqoorbana dalel meneh khaev
19	sakhle waweere mana rav qoorbana aw madhbkha damqadesh lqoorbana
20	man dyame hakheel bmadhbkha yame beh wavkhul ma diyth lel meneh
21	wman dyame bhaykla yame beh wavman damar beh
22	wman dyame bashmayya yame bkhoorsyeh dalaha wavman dyathev lel meneh
23	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape damasreen ton nana washveta wkhamoona washvaqton yaqeeratheh dnamoosa deena wakhnana whaymanootha halayn dayn wale wa dthebdhon whalayn la teshbqon
24	naghoodhe smayya damsalleen baqe wvaleen gamle
25	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape damdhakayn ton bareh dkhasa wadhzavoora lghaw dayn mlayn khtoofya wawla
26	preeshe weere dakaw looqdham gaweh dkhasa wadhzavoora dahwa af barhon dkhe
27	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape ddhamayn ton lqavre makhlshe dmen lvar methkhzayn shapeere men lghaw dayn mlayn garme dmeethe wkhulah tanfootha
28	hakhana af aton men lvar methkhzayn ton lavnay nasha aykh zadeeqe wmen lghaw mlayn ton awla wmasav bape
29	wa lkhon safre wafreeshe nasbay bape dvanayn ton qavre danviyye wamsabtheen ton bayth qvoore dzadeeqe
30	wamreen ton deloo hwayn byawmay avahayn la hawayn wayn lhon shawtafe badhma danviyye
31	madhayn mashdheen ton al nafshkhon davnayya aton dhanon dqatelw lanviyye
32	waf aton malaw mshookhtha davahaykon
33	khwawatha yalda dakhedhne aykana terqon men deena dghihana
34	metul hana ha ena mshadar na lwathkhon nviyye wkhakeeme wsafre menhon qatleen ton wzaqpeen ton wmenhon mnagdheen ton bakhnooshathkhon wtherdfon enon men mdheena lamdheena
35	aykana dnithe laykon kuleh dma dzadeeqe detheshedh al ara men dmeh dhavayl zadeeqa wadhama ladhmeh dazkharya bar brakhya haw daqtalton baynay haykla lmadhbkha
36	ameen amar na lkhon dnithyan halayn kulhayn al sharbtha hadhe
37	oorishlem oorishlem qatlath nviyye wraghmath laylayn dashleekheen lwathah kma zavneen sveeth dekanesh bnayky aykh dkhansha tarnaghoolta parooghayh tkhayth gefayh wla svayton
38	ha meshtveq lkhon baytkhon kharba
39	amarna lkhon gayr dla tekhzoonany men hasha dhama dthimron breekh oo dathe bashmeh dmarya
