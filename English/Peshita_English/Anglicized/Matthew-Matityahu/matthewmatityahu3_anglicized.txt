1	bhon dayn byawmatha hanon etha yookhanan mamdhana wmakhrez wa bkhoorba deehoodh
2	wamar toovw qerbath lah malkootha dashmayya
3	hanaw gayr haw dameer byadh eshaya nviyya qala dqare bkhoorba tayyevw oorkheh dmarya washwaw lashveelawy
4	hoo dayn yookhanan eethawy wa lvoosheh dsara dghamle wesar khase dmeshka al khasawy wmekhoolteh qamse wdhevsha dvara
5	hadayn nafqa wath lwatheh oorishlem wkhulah eehoodh wkhuleh athra dakhdharay yoordnan
6	wamdeen waw meneh byoordnan nahra kadh mawdayn bakhtahayhon
7	kadh khza dayn sagiyye men preeshe wmen zadooqayye dathayn lmemadh emar lhon yalda dakhedhne manoo khaweekhon lmeraq men rooghza dathe
8	vedhw hakheel pire dshawayn lathyavootha
9	wla tasbron wthimron bnafshkhon dava eeth lan avraham amar na lkhon gayr dmeshkakh alaha men halayn kife lamqamoo bnayya lavraham
10	ha dayn nargha seem al eqara diylane kul eelana hakheel dfire tave la avedh methpseq wnafel bnoora
11	ena mamedh na lkhon bmayya lathyavootha haw dayn dvathary athe khaseen oo meny haw dla shawe na msanawy lmeshqal hoo mamedh lkhon brookha dqoodhsha wavnoora
12	haw drafsha biydheh wamdhake edrawy wkhete kanesh lawsrawy wthevna mawqedh bnoora dla daka
13	hadayn etha yeshoo men gleela lyoordnan lwath yookhanan dnemadh meneh
14	hoo dayn yookhanan kale wa leh wamar ena sneeq na dmenakh ethmedh wat lwathy ethayt
15	hoo dayn yeshoo na wemar leh shvooq hasha hakhana gayr yae lan danmale kulah kinootha whadayn shavqeh
16	kadh madh dayn yeshoo mekhdha sleq men mayya wethpthakhw leh shmayya wakhza rookha dalaha dnakhta aykh yawna wethath lawy
17	wha qala men shmayya damar hanaw bery khabeeva dveh estveeth
