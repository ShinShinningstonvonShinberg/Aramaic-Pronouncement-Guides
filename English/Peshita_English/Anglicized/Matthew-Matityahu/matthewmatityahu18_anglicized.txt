1	bha shatha qrevw talmeedhe lwath yeshoo wamreen manoo kay rav bmalkootha dashmayya
2	waqra yeshoo talya waqeemeh baynathhon
3	wemar ameen amarna lkhon dela tethhafkon wthehon aykh tlayye la telon lmalkootha dashmayya
4	man hakheel dammakekh nafsheh aykh hana talya hoo nehwe rav bmalkootha dashmayya
5	wman danqabel aykh talya hana bshemy lee mqabel
6	wkhul dnakhshel lkhadh men halayn zoore damhaymneen bee paqakh wa leh dthehwe talya rakhya dakhmara bsawreh wamtaba boomqawy dyama
7	wa lalma men makhshoole ananqi gayr dnithon makhshoole wa dayn lghavra dviydheh nithon makhshoole
8	en dayn eedhakh aw reghlakh makhshla lakh psooqayh washdheeh menakh tav oo lakh dtheool lkhayye kadh khghees at aw kadh psheegh wla kadh eeth lakh tartayn eedheen aw tartayn reghleen tepel bnoora dalalam
9	wen hoo daynakh makhshla lakh khseeh washdheeh menakh tav oo lakh dvakhdha ayna teool lkhayye wla kadh eeth lakh tartayn ayneen tepel bghihana dnoora
10	khzaw la tevson al khadh men halayn zoore amar na lkhon gayr dmalakhayhon bashmayya bkhulzvan khazayn parsoopeh davy dvashmayya
11	etha gayr breh dnasha dnakhe medem dabeedh wa
12	mana methkhze lkhon en nehon lnash ma erbeen wnete khadh menhon la shaveq tesheen wthesha btoora wazel bae lhaw data
13	wen neshkkheh ameen amarna lkhon dkhadhe beh yateer men tesheen wthesha dla taw
14	hakhana layt sevyana qdham avookhon dvashmayya dnivadh khadh men zoore halayn
15	en dayn askel bakh akhookh zel akesa baynayk wleh balkhoodh en shamakh eethart akhookh
16	wela shamakh dvar amakh khadh aw trayn dal poom trayn aw tlatha sahdeen tqoom kul mela
17	en dayn af la lhanon neshma emar lita en dayn af la lita neshma nehwe lakh aykh makhsa waykh khanfa
18	wameen amar na lkhon dkhul ma dthesron bara nehwe aseer bashmayya wmedem dtheshron bara nehwe shre bashmayya
19	toov amarna lkhon den trayn menkhon neshton bara al kul svoo dneshelon nehwe lhon men lwath avy dvashmayya
20	ayka gayr dathrayn aw tlatha kneesheen bshemy taman ena baynathhon
21	hadayn qrev lwatheh kifa wemar mary kma zavneen en naskel bee akhy eshbooq leh dhama lashva zavneen
22	amar leh yeshoo la amar na lakh dhama lashva ela dhama lshaveen zavneen shva shva
23	metul hana etdamyath malkootha dashmayya lghavra malka dasva dnesav khooshbana men avdawy
24	wkhadh sharee lmesav qarevw leh khadh dkhayyav reboo kakreen
25	wkhadh layt leh lmefra pqadh mareh dnezdaban hoo wattheh wavnawy wkhul medem diyth leh wnefroo
26	wanfal haw avda sghedh leh wemar mary agar lay rookha wkhul medem para na lakh
27	wethrakham mareh davda haw washra wkhawbtheh shvaq leh
28	nfaq dayn avda haw weshkakh lkhadh men knawatheh dkhayyav wa leh deenare ma wakhdeh wkhaneq wa leh wemar leh hav lee medem dkhayyav at lee
29	wanfal haw knatheh al reghlawy ba meneh wemar leh agar lay rookha wfara na lakh
30	hoo dayn la sva ela ezal armyeh bayth aseere dhama dnetel leh ma dkhayyav leh
31	kadh khzaw dayn knawathhon medem dahwa keryath lhon tav wethaw awdaw lmarhon kul dahwa
32	hadayn qra mareh wemar leh avda beesha ha kulah khawbtha shevqeth lakh davayt meny
33	la wale wa lakh af at dathkhon lakhnathakh aykana dena khantakh
34	warghez mareh washlmeh lamnagdhane dhama dnefroo kul medem dkhayyav leh
35	hakhana nebedh lkhon avy dvashmayya ela teshbqon nash lakhoo men lebkhon sakhlootheh
