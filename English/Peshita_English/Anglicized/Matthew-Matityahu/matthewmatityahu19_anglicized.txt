1	wahwa dkhadh shalem yeshoo mele halayn shqal men gleela wetha lathkhooma deehoodh levra dyoordnan
2	wethaw bathreh kenshe sagiyye wasee enon taman
3	waqrevw lwatheh preeshe wamnasayn waw leh wamreen en shaleet lnash dneshre attheh bkhul ela
4	hoo dayn na wemar lhon la qrayton dhaw davadh men braysheeth dekhra wneqbtha vadh enon
5	wemar metul hana neshbooq gavra lavoo wlemeh wneqaf lattheh wnehon trayhon khadh bsar
6	madhayn la hwaw trayn ela khadh pghar medem hakheel dalaha zawegh barnasha la nfaresh
7	amreen leh lmana hakheel mooshe paqedh dnetel kthava dshoovqana wneshreeh
8	amar lhon mooshe looqval qashyooth lebkhon apes lkhon dtheshron neshaykon men braysheeth dayn la hwa hakhana
9	amar na lkhon dayn dman dshaveq attheh dla gawra wnasev khritha gaar wman dnasev shveeqta gaar
10	amreen leh talmeedhawy en hakhana eeth edhlayya baynay gavra lattha la paqakh lmesav attha
11	hoo dayn emar lhon la kulnash safeq lah lmeltha hadhe ela man deeheev leh
12	eeth gayr mhaymne dmen karsa demhon etheeledhw hakhana wiyth mhaymne dmen bnaynasha hwaw mhaymne wiyth mhaymne dhenon vadhw nafshhon mhaymne metul malkootha dashmayya man dmeshkakh dnespaq nespaq
13	hadayn qarevw leh tlayye danseem eedheh layhon wansale wakhaw bhon talmeedhawy
14	hoo dayn yeshoo emar lhon shvooqw tlayye athayn lwathy wla tekhlon enon ddhaylayn gayr daykh halayn enon eethayh malkootha dashmayya
15	wsam eedheh layhon wezal men taman
16	wetha khadh qrev wemar leh malfana tava mana dtav ebedh dnehon lee khayye dalalam
17	hoo dayn emar leh mana qare at lee tava layt tava ela en khadh alaha en dayn save at dtheool lkhayye tar pooqdane
18	amar leh aylayn hoo dayn yeshoo emar leh dla teqtool wla tghoor wla teghnoov wla teshadh sahdooth shooqra
19	wyaqar lavookh wlemakh wthakhev lqareevakh aykh nafshakh
20	amar leh haw layma halayn kulhayn netreth enayn men talyoothy mana khseer na
21	amar leh yeshoo en save at gmeera lmehwa zel zaben qenyanakh whav lmeskine wthehwe lakh seemtha bashmayya wtha bathary
22	shma dayn haw layma meltha hadhe wezal kadh karya leh eeth wa leh gayr qenyana sagiyya
23	yeshoo dayn emar lthalmeedhawy ameen amar na lkhon datla y lateera dneool lmalkooth shmayya
24	toov dayn amarna lkhon dadhleel oo lghamla lmeal bakhroora damkhata aw ateera dneool lmalkootha dalaha
25	talmeedhe dayn kadh shmaw taheereen waw tav wamreen manoo kay meshkakh dnikhe
26	khar bhon yeshoo wemar lhon lwath bnaynasha hadhe la meshkkha lwath alaha dayn kulmedem meshkkha
27	hadayn na kifa wemar leh ha enakhnan shvaqn kulmedem wethayn bathrakh mana kay nehwe lan
28	amar lhon yeshoo ameen amar na lkhon daton dethayton bathary balma khatha ma dyathev breh dnasha al tranaws dshoovkheh tetvon af aton al tresar koorsawan watdhoonon tresar shavte diysrayel
29	wkhulnash dshaveq bate aw akhe aw akhwatha aw ava aw ema aw attha aw bnayya aw qoorya metul shemy khadh bama nqabel wkhayye dalalam nirath
30	sagiyye dayn qadhmayye dnehon khrayye wakhrayye qadhmayye
