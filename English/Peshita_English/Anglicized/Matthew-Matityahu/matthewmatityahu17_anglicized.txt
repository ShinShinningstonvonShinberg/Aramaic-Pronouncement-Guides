1	wvathar shta yawmeen dvar yeshoo lkhifa walyaqoov walyookhanan akhoo waseq enon ltoora rama balkhoodhayhon
2	weshtakhlaf yeshoo qdhamayhon wanhar parsoopeh aykh shemsha nakhtawy dayn khwarw aykh noohra
3	wethkhzeew lhon mooshe weliyya kadh mmalleen ameh
4	na dayn kifa wemar lyeshoo mary shapeer oo lan dathnan nehwe wen save at nebedh tnan tlath matleen khdha lakh wakhdha lmooshe wakhdha leliyya
5	wadh hoo mmalel ha nana naheerta atlath layhon wqala hwa men nana damar hanaw bery khabeeva dveh estveeth leh shmaw
6	wkhadh shmaw talmeedhe nfalw al apayhon wadhkhelw tav
7	wethqarav lwathhon yeshoo waqrev lhon wemar qoomw la tedhkhlon
8	wareemw aynayhon walnash la khzaw ela en lyeshoo balkhoodhawy
9	wkhadh nakhteen men toora paqedh enon yeshoo wemar lhon leen nash la timron khezwa hana dhama danqoom breh dnasha men meethe
10	wshaloo talmeedhawy wamreen leh mana hakheel safre amreen deliyya wale dnithe looqdham
11	na yeshoo wemar eliyya athe looqdham dkhul medem nshalem
12	amar na lkhon dayn dha eliyya etha wla yadhoo wavadhw beh kul ma dasvaw hakhana af breh dnasha theedh dnekhash menhon
13	hadayn estakalw talmeedhe dal yookhanan mamdhana emar lhon
14	wkhadh ethaw lwath kensha qrev leh gavra wavrekh al boorkawy
15	wemar leh mary ethrakham lay bery diyth leh bar egara wveeshaeeth veedh kma gayr zavneen bnoora nafel wakhma zavneen bmayya
16	wqarevtheh lthalmeedhayk wla eshkakhw lmasayyootheh
17	na yeshoo wemar on sharbtha dla mhaymna wamaqalta dhama lemathy ehwe amkhon wadhama lemathy esaybarkhon ayta lee lkha
18	wakha beh yeshoo wanfaq meneh shidha wethasee talya men ha shatha
19	hadayn qrevw talmeedhe lwath yeshoo balkhoodhawy wemarw leh lmana khnan la eshkakhn lmasayyootheh
20	amar lhon yeshoo metul la haymanoothkhon ameen gayr amar na lkhon den tehwe bkhon haymanootha aykh predta dkhardla timron ltoora hana dshana meka wanshane wmedem la nekhsankhon
21	hana dayn gensa la nafeq ela bsawma wvaslootha
22	kadh methhapkheen dayn baghleela emar lhon yeshoo theedh oo breh dnasha dneshtlem biydhay bnay nasha
23	wneqtloona walyawma dathlatha nqoom wkheryath lhon tav
24	wkhadh ethaw lakhfarnakhoom qrevw hanon dnasbeen trayn trayn zoozeen dakhsef risha lwath kifa wemarw leh rabkhon la yahev trayn zoozawy
25	amar lhon een wkhadh al kifa lvayta qadmeh yeshoo wemar leh mana methkhze lakh shemon malkayh dara men man nasbeen makhsa wakhsef risha man bnayhon aw men nookhrayye
26	amar leh shemon men nookhrayye amar leh yeshoo madhayn bnay khire enon bnayya
27	dla dayn nakhshel enon zel lyama warma balooa wnoona qadhmayya dsaleq pthakh poomeh wtheshkakh estira ha sav whav khlafay wakhlafayk
