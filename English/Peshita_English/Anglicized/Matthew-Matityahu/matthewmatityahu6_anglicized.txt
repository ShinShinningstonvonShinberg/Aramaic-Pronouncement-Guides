1	khoorw dayn bzedhqathkhon dla tebdhoonah qdham bnay nasha aykh dthethkhzon lhon wela aghra layt lkhon lwath avookhon dvashmayya
2	emathy hakheel davedh at zedhqtha la teqre qarna qdhamayk aykh davdeen nasbay bape bakhnooshatha wavshooqe aykh dneshtabkhon men bnay nasha wameen amar na lkhon dqabelw aghrhon
3	at dayn ma davedh at zedhqtha la teda semalakh mana avda yameenakh
4	aykh dthehwe zedhqthakh bkhesya wavookh dkhaze bkhesya hoo nefrakh bghelya
5	wma damsale at la tehwe aykh nasbay bape drakhmeen lamqam bakhnooshatha wavzawyatha dshooqe lamsalayyoo dnethkhzon lavnay nasha wameen amar na lkhon dqabelw aghrhon
6	at dayn emathy damsale at ool lthawanakh wakhoodh tarakh wsala lavookh davkhesya wavookh dkhaze bkhesya nefrakh bghelya
7	wma damsalayn ton la hwayton mfaqqeen aykh khanfe savreen gayr davmamlla sagiyya meshtameen
8	la hakheel tedamon lhon avookhon gayr yadha mana methbe lkhon adhla tesheloona
9	hakhana hakheel salaw aton avon dvashmayya nethqadash shmakh
10	tithe malkoothakh nehwe sevyanakh aykana dvashmayya af bara
11	hav lan lakhma dsoonqanan yawmana
12	washvooq lan khawbayn aykana daf khnan shvaqn lkhayyavayn
13	wla talan lnesyoona ela pasan men beesha metul ddheelakh y malkootha wkhayla wtheshbookhta lalam almeen
14	en gayr teshbqon lavnaynasha sakhlwathhon neshbooq af lkhon avookhon dvashmayya
15	en dayn la teshbqon lavnaynasha afla avookhon shaveq lkhon sakhlwathkhon
16	emathy dayn dsameen ton la tehon kmeere aykh nasbay bape mkhableen gayr parsoopayhon aykh dnethkhzon lavnaynasha dsameen wameen amar na lkhon dqabelw aghrhon
17	at dayn ma dsaem at asheegh apayk wamshookh rishakh
18	aykh dla tethkhze lavnaynasha dsaem at ela lavookh davkhesya wavookh dkhaze bkhesya hoo nefrakh
19	la tseemon lkhon seematha bara athar dsasa wakhla mkhableen wayka dghanave palsheen wghanbeen
20	ela seemw lkhon seematha bashmayya ayka dla sasa wla akhla mkhableen wayka dghanave la palsheen wla ganbeen
21	ayka gayr diythayh seemathkhon taman w af lebkhon
22	shragha dfaghra eethayh ayna en aynakh hakheel tehwe psheeta af kuleh paghrakh naheer oo
23	en dayn aynakh tehwe beesha kuleh paghrakh kheshookha nehwe en hakheel noohra dvakh kheshookha hoo kheshookhakh kma nehwe
24	la nash meshkakh lathrayn marawan lmeflakh aw gayr lkhadh nesne wlakhrina nerkham aw lkhadh nyaqar wlakhrina nshoot la meshkkheen ton lalaha lmeflakh walmamoona
25	metul hana amar na lkhon la tispon lnafshkhon mana tekhlon wmana teshton wla lfaghrkhon mana telbshon la ha nafsha yateera men saybarta wfaghra men lvoosha
26	khoorw bfarakhtha dashmayya dla zareen wla khasdeen wla khamleen bawsre wavookhon dvashmayya mtharse lhon la ha aton myatreen ton menhon
27	manoo dayn menkhon kadh yasef meshkakh lmawsafoo al qawmtheh amtha khdha
28	wal lvoosha mana yaspeen ton ethbaqaw bshooshane ddhavra aykana ravyan dla layan wla azlan
29	amar na lkhon dayn dafla shlaymawn bkhuleh shoovkheh ethkasee aykh khdha menhayn
30	en dayn lameera dkhaqla dyawmana eethawy wamkhar nafel bthanoora alaha hakhana malbesh la sagee yateer lkhon zooray haymanootha
31	la hakheel tispon aw timron mana nekhool aw mana neshte aw mana nethkase
32	kulhayn gayr halayn amme w baayn lhayn avookhon dayn dvashmayya yadha daf lkhon methbayan halayn kulhayn
33	baw dayn looqdham malkootheh dalaha wzadeeqootheh wkhulhayn halayn mettawsfan lkhon
34	la hakheel tispon damkhar hoo gayr mkhar yasef deeleh safeq leh lyawma beeshteh
