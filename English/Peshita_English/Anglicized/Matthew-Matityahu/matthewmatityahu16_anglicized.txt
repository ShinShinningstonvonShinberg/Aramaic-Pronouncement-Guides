1	waqrevw preeshe wzadooqayye mnasayn leh wshaleen leh atha men shmayya dankhawe enon
2	hoo dayn na wemar lhon ma dahwa ramsha amreen ton sakhwa hoo semqath gayr shmayya
3	wavsafra amreen ton yawmana sathwa hoo semqath gayr shmayya kmeeraeeth nasbay bape parsoopa dashmayya yadheen ton dthevqon athwatha dzavna hana la yadheen ton dthefrshon
4	sharbtha beeshta wghayyartha atha baya watha la methyava lah ela atheh dyawnan nviyya washvaq enon wezal
5	wkhadh ethaw talmeedhawy levra taw dnesvon amhon lakhma
6	hoo dayn emar lhon khzaw ezdarw men khmeera dafreeshe wadhzadooqayye
7	henon dayn methraayn waw bnafshhon wamreen dlakhma la nsavw
8	yeshoo dayn eedha wemar lhon mana methkhashveen ton bnafshkhon zooray haymanootha dlakhma la shqalton
9	la dhama lhasha estakalton la ahdeen ton lhanon khamsha lakhmeen dkhamsha alfeen wakhma qoofeeneen shqalton
10	wla lhanon shava lakhmeen darba alfeen wakhma espreedheen shqalton
11	aykan la estakalton dla wa al lakhma emreth lkhon ela dthezdahron men khmeera dafreeshe wadhzadooqayye
12	hadayn estakalw dla emar dnezdahron men khmeera dlakhma ela men yoolpana dafreeshe wadhzadooqayye
13	kadh dayn etha yeshoo lathra dqesariyya-dhfeeleepaws mshael wa lthalmeedhawy wamar manoo amreen lay nasha diythay breh dnasha
14	henon dayn emarw eeth damreen yookhanan mamdhana khrane dayn eliyya wakhrane eramya aw khadh men nviyye
15	amar lhon aton dayn manoo amreen ton diythay
16	na shemon kifa wemar at oo msheekha breh dalaha khayya
17	na yeshoo wemar leh toovayk shemon breh-dyawna dvesra wadhma la gla lakh ela avy dvashmayya
18	af ena amar na lakh dat oo kifa wal hadhe kifa evnayh lity wthare dashyool la nekhsnoonah
19	lakh etel qleedhe dmalkootha dashmayya wkhul medem dthesoor bara nehwe aseer bashmayya wmedem dtheshre bara nehwe shre bashmayya
20	hadayn paqedh lthalmeedhawy dalnash la nimron dhooyyoo msheekha
21	wmen hadayn sharee yeshoo lamkhawayyoo lthalmeedhawy datheedh oo dnizal luwrishlem wsagee nekhash men qasheeshe wmen rabay kahne wsafre wnethqtel walyawma dathlatha nqoom
22	wdhavreh kifa wsharee lmekha beh wemar khas lakh mary dthehwe lakh hadhe
23	hoo dayn ethpnee wemar lkhifa zel lakh lvestary satana tooqalta at lee dla methrae at dalaha ela davnay nasha
24	hadayn emar yeshoo lthalmeedhawy man dsave dnithe bathary nekhpoor bnafsheh wneshqool zqeefeh wnithe bathary
25	man dsave gayr dnakhe nafsheh nawbdheeh wman dnawbedh nafsheh metulathy neshkkheeh
26	mana gayr methhane barnasha en kuleh alma neqne wnafsheh nekhsar aw mana netel barnasha takhloofa dnafsheh
27	theedh oo gayr breh dnasha dnithe btheshbookhta davoo am malakhawy qadeeshe whadayn nefroo lnash nash aykh vadhawy
28	ameen amar na lkhon diyth nasha dqameen tnan dla netmon mawta dhama dnekhzon lavreh dnasha dathe bmalkootheh
