1	hadayn yeshoo ethdvar men rookha dqoodhsha lmadhbra dnethnase men akhelqarsa
2	wsam arbeen yawmeen warbeen laylawan khrayyath dayn kfen
3	waqrev haw damnase wemar leh en breh at dalaha emar dhalayn kife nehwyan lakhma
4	hoo dayn na wemar ktheev dla wa blakhma balkhoodh khayye barnasha ela bkhul mela dnafqa men poomeh dalaha
5	hadayn davreh akhelqarsa lamdheenath qoodhsha waqeemeh al kenfa dhaykla
6	wemar leh en breh at dalaha shdhee nafshakh lthakht ktheev gayr dalmalakhawy nfaqedh layk wal eedhayhon neshqloonakh dla tetqel bkhifa reghlakh
7	amar leh yeshoo toov ktheev dla tnase lmarya alahakh
8	toov davreh akhelqarsa ltoora dtav ram wkhawyeh kulhayn malkwatha dalma wshoovkhhayn
9	wemar leh halayn kulhayn lakh etel en tepel tesgoodh lee
10	hadayn emar leh yeshoo zel lakh satana ktheev gayr dalmarya alahakh tesgoodh wleh balkhoodhawy teflookh
11	hadayn shavqeh akhelqarsa wha malakhe qrevw wamshamsheen waw leh
12	kadh shma dayn yeshoo dyookhanan eshtlem shanee leh laghleela
13	wshavqah lnasrath wetha mar bakhfarnakhoom al yadh yama bathkhooma dazvawlon wadhnaftalee
14	dnethmale medem dethemar byadh eshaya nviyya demar
15	ara dazvawlon ara dnaftalee oorkha dyama evrawy dyoordnan gleela damme
16	ama dyathev bkheshookha noohra raba khza waylayn dyathbeen bathra wavtelale dmawta noohra dnakh lhon
17	men hadayn sharee yeshoo lmakhrazoo walmimar toovw qerbath lah gayr malkootha dashmayya
18	wkhadh mhalekh al yadh yama daghleela khza trayn akheen shemon dethqree kifa wandrewas akhoo dramayn mseedhatha byama eethayhon waw gayr sayyadhe
19	wemar lhon yeshoo taw bathary webedhkhon dthehon sayyadhe davnay nasha
20	henon dayn mekhdha shvaqw mseedhathhon wezalw bathreh
21	wkhadh var men taman khza khrane akhe trayn yaqoov bar zavdhay wyookhanan akhoo belfa am zavdhay avoohon damthaqneen mseedhathhon waqra enon
22	henon dayn mekhdha shvaqw lelfa wlavoohon wezalw bathreh
23	wmethkrekh wa yeshoo bkhulah gleela wmalef wa bakhnooshathhon wmakhrez svartha dmalkootha wmase kul kiv wkhoorhan bama
24	weshtma tebeh bkhulah sooriyya wqarevw leh kulhon aylayn dveesh beesh veedheen bkhoorhane mshakhlfe waylayn daleeseen bthashneeqe wdhaywane wadhvar egare wamsharayya wasee enon
25	wezalw bathreh kenshe sagiyye men gleela wmen esrath-mdheenatha wmen oorishlem wmen eehoodh wmen evra dyoordnan
