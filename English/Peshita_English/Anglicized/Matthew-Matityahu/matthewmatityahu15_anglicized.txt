1	hadayn qrevw lwath yeshoo preeshe wsafre dmen oorishlem wamreen
2	lmana talmeedhayk avreen al mashlmanootha dqasheeshe wla msheegheen eedhayhon ma dakhleen lakhma
3	na yeshoo wemar lhon lmana af aton avreen ton al pooqdana dalaha metul mashlmanoothkhon
4	alaha gayr emar yaqar lavookh wlemakh wman damsakhe lavoo wlemeh mmath nmooth
5	aton dayn amreen ton kul man dnimar lava aw lema qoorbany medem dthethhne meny wla nyaqar lavoo aw lemeh
6	wvatelton meltha dalaha metul mashlmanootha deelkhon
7	nasbay bape shapeer ethnabee laykon eshaya wemar
8	ama hana bsefwatheh w myaqar lee lebhon dayn sagee rakheeq meny
9	wasreeqaeeth dakhleen lee kadh malfeen yoolpane dfooqdane davnaynasha
10	waqra lkhenshe wemar lhon shmaw westalw
11	la wa medem dael lfooma msayyev lvarnasha ela medem dnafeq men pooma haw oo msayyev lvarnasha
12	hadayn qrevw talmeedhawy wamreen leh yadha at dafreeshe dashmaw meltha hadhe ethkshelw
13	hoo dayn na wemar lhon kul nesbtha aydha dla nasbah avy dvashmayya tethqar
14	shvooqw lhon smayya enon naghoodhe dasmayya samya dayn lsamya en nedhbar trayhon bghoomasa nafleen
15	wana shemon kifa wemar leh mary pasheq lan mathla hana
16	hoo dayn emar lhon dhama lhasha af aton la mestakleen ton
17	la yadheen ton dmedem dael lfooma lkharsa hoo azel wmen taman bthadhkeetha meshtdhe lvar
18	medem dayn dmen pooma nafeq men leba nafeq whooyyoo msayyev leh lvarnasha
19	men leba hoo gayr nafqan makhshvatha beeshatha gawra qetla zanyootha ganavootha sahdooth shooqra goodafa
20	halayn enayn damsayvan lvarnasha en nash dayn nelas kadh la mshaghan eedhawy la mestayyav
21	wanfaq men taman yeshoo wetha lathkhooma dsoor wadhsaydan
22	wha attha knanata men tkhoome hanon nefqath kadh qaya wamra ethrakham lay mary breh ddhaweedh brathy beeshaeeth metdavra men shidha
23	hoo dayn la panyah pethghama waqrevw talmeedhawy baw meneh wamreen shreeh dqaya bathran
24	hoo dayn na wemar lhon la eshtadreth ela lwath erbe dataw men bayth eesrayyel
25	hee dayn ethath seghdath leh wemrath mary adarayny
26	emar lah la shapeer lmesav lakhma davnayya walmarmayyoo lkhalbe
27	hee dayn emrath een mary af kalbe akhleen men partoothe dnafleen men pathoore dmarayhon wkhaayn
28	hadayn emar lah yeshoo aw attha raba y haymanoothekhy nehwe lekhy aykh dsavya aty wethasyath barthah men ha shatha
29	wshanee men taman yeshoo wetha al gev yama daghleela wasleq ltoora weethev taman
30	waqrevw lwatheh kenshe sagiyye diyth waw amhon khgheere wasmayya wkharshe wafsheeghe wakhrane sagiyye warmeew enon lwath reghlawy dyeshoo wasee enon
31	aykh dnetdamron kenshe hanon dkhazayn kharshe dammalleen wafsheeghe dmethkhalmeen wakhgheere damhalkheen wasmayya dkhazayn wshabakhw lalaha deesrayyel
32	hoo dayn yeshoo qra lthalmeedhawy wemar lhon methrakham na al kensha hana dha tlatha yawmeen qaweew lwathy wlayt lhon ma dnekhlon wdheshre enon kadh sameen la save na dalma noofon buwrkha
33	amreen leh talmeedhawy aymeka lan bkhoorba lakhma dansaba kensha hana kuleh
34	amar lhon yeshoo kma lakhmeen eeth lkhon amreen leh shava wqaleel noone daqdqe
35	wafqadh lkhenshe dnestamkon al ara
36	washqal lhalayn shava lakhmeen walnoone wshabakh waqsa wyav lthalmeedhawy wthalmeedhe yavw lkhenshe
37	wekhalw kulhon wasvaw washqalw tawtare daqsayye mle shava espreedheen
38	hanon dayn dekhalw hawayn waw arba alfeen gavre star men neshe watlayye
39	wkhadh shra lkhenshe sleq lelfa wetha lathkhooma dmaghdoo
