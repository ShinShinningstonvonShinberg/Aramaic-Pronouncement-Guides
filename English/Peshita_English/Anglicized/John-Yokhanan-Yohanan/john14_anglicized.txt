1	la netdawadh lebkhon haymenw balaha wvee haymenw
2	sageeeen enon awane bayth avy wela amar weeth lkhon dazel na detayyev lkhon athra
3	wen izal etayyev lkhon athra toov ithe wedhbarkhon lwathy dayka dena eethay af aton tehon
4	wlayka dazel na yadheen ton oowrkha yadheen ton
5	amar leh tawma maran la yadheenan layka azel at waykana meshkkheen nan oorkha lmeda
6	amar leh yeshoo ena na oorkha washrara wkhayye la nash athe lwath avy ela en bee
7	eloo lee yadheen wayton af lavy yadheen wayton wmen hasha yadheen ton leh wakhzaytoona
8	amar leh feeleepaws maran khawan ava wkhadoo lan
9	amar leh yeshoo hana kuleh zavna amkhon na wla eedhatany peeleepe man dlee khaze khza lava waykana at amar at khawan ava
10	la mhaymen at dena bavy wavy bee mele aylayn dena mmalel na men nafshy la mmalel na avy dayn dvee amar hoo avedh vadhe halayn
11	haymenw dena bavy wavy bee wela afen metul vadhe haymenw
12	ameen ameen amar na lkhon dman damhaymen bee vadhe aylayn dena avedh na af hoo nebedh wadhyateereen men halayn nebedh dena lwath ava azel na
13	wmedem dtheshelon bshemy ebedh lkhon dneshtabakh ava bavreh
14	wen tesheloonany bshemy ena avedhna
15	en rakhmeen ton lee pooqdanay tarw
16	wena eve men avy wakhrina paraqlita netel lkhon dnehwe amkhon lalam
17	rookha dashrara haw dalma la meshkakh lamqabalootheh metul dla khza wla yadheh aton dayn yadheen ton leh dalwathkhon amar wavkhon oo
18	la shaveq na lkhon yathme athe na gayr lwathkhon qaleel khreen
19	walma la khaze lee aton dayn tekhzoonany dena khay af aton tikhon
20	bhaw yawma tedon dena bavy waton bee aton wena bkhon na
21	man diyth lwatheh pooqdanay wnatar lhon haw oo makhev lee haw dayn drakhem lee nethrkhem men avy wena erkhmeewy wekhawaywy nafshy
22	amar leh eehoodha la wa skharyoota mary manaw lan theedh at lamkhawayyoo nafshakh wla wa lalma
23	na yeshoo wemar leh man drakhem lee melathy natar wavy nerkhmeewy walwatheh athayn nan wawana lwatheh avdeen nan
24	haw dayn dla rakhem lee melathy la natar wmeltha hadhe dshameen ton la hwath deely ela dava dshadrany
25	halayn malleth amkhon kadh lwathkhon eethay
26	haw dayn paraqlita rookha dqoodhsha haw damshadar avy bshemy hoo nalefkhon kulmedem whoo nahedhkhon kul ma damar na lkhon
27	shlama shaveq na lkhon shlama deely yahev na lkhon la wa aykana dyahev alma ena yahev na lkhon la netdawadh lebkhon wla nedhkhal
28	shmaton dena emreth lkhon dazel na wathe na lwathkhon eloo rakhmeen wayton lee khadhayn wayton dazel na lwath avy davy rav oo meny
29	whasha ha emreth lkhon adhla nehwe dma dahwa thaymnon
30	mekeel la emalel amkhon sagiyyatha athe gayr arkhooneh dalma wvee layt leh medem
31	ela dneda alma drakhem na lavy waykana dfaqdany avy hakhwath avedh na qoomw nizal meka
