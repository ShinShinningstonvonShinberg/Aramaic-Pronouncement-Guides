1	eeth wa dayn khadh dakhreeh laazar men bayth-anya qreetha akhooh dmaryam wadhmarta
2	maryam dayn hadhe eethayh ha dmeshkhath bvesma reghlawy dyeshoo wshawyath bsarah akhooh wa dhadhe laazar dakhreeh wa
3	wshadarayn tartayhayn akhwatheh lwath yeshoo wamran maran ha haw drakhem at kreeh
4	yeshoo dayn emar hana koorhana la hwa dmawta ela khlaf teshbookhta dalaha dneshtabakh breh dalaha metulatheh
5	makhev wa dayn hoo yeshoo lmarta walmaryam wallaazar
6	wkhadh shma dakhreeh katar bdhooktha diythawy wa trayn yawmeen
7	wvatharken emar lthalmeedhawy taw nizal toov leehoodh
8	amreen leh talmeedhawy raban hasha eehoodhayye baayn waw lmergmakh wthoov azel at lthaman
9	amar lhon yeshoo la tartaesre shaeen eeth byawma wen nash mhalekh biymama la mettqel metul dkhaze noohreh dalma hana
10	en nash dayn blilya nhalekh mettqel metul dnaheera layt beh
11	halayn emar yeshoo wvatharken emar lhon laazar rakhman shkhev ela azel na daeereewy
12	amreen leh talmeedhawy maran en dmekh methkhlem
13	hoo dayn yeshoo emar al mawteh whenon svarw dal madhmkha hoo dshentha emar
14	hadayn emar lhon yeshoo psheeqaeeth laazar meeth leh
15	wkhadhe na dla hweeth taman metulathkhon dathhaymnon ela halekhw lthaman
16	amar tawma dmethemar tama lthalmeedhe khavrawy nizal af khnan nmooth ameh
17	wetha yeshoo lvayth-anya weshkakh darba leh yawmeen diythawy bvayth qvoora
18	eethayh wath dayn bayth-anya al gev oorishlem kadh pareeqa menah aykh estdhawatha khameshtasar
19	wsagiyye men eehoodhayye ateeeen waw lwath marta wmaryam dnemlon blebhayn metul akhoohayn
20	marta dayn kadh shemath dyeshoo etha nefqath luwreh maryam dayn bvayta yathba wath
21	wemrath marta lyeshoo mary eloo tnan wayt la maeth wa akhy
22	ela af hasha yadha na dakhma dtheshal lalaha yahev lakh
23	amar lah yeshoo qaem akhookhy
24	amra leh marta yadhana dqaem bnookhama byawma khrayya
25	amar lah yeshoo ena na nookhama wkhayye man damhaymen bee afen nmooth nikhe
26	wkhul dkhay wamhaymen bee lalam la nmooth mhaymnaty hadhe
27	amra leh een mary ena mhaymna na dat oo msheekha breh dalaha dathe lalma
28	wkhadh emrath halayn ezath qrath lmaryam khathah kasyaeeth wamra lah raban etha wqare lekhy
29	wmaryam kadh shemath qamath ghal wathya wath lwatheh
30	hoo dayn yeshoo la dhakeel etha wa laqreetha ela bah wa bha dooktha deratheh marta
31	af hanon dayn eehoodhayye diyth waw amah bvayta damvayeen waw lah dakhzaw lmaryam daghal qamath nefqath ezalw lhon bathrah svarw gayr dalqavra aza lmevka
32	hee dayn maryam kadh ethath ayka diythawy wa yeshoo wakhzatheh neflath al reghlawy wamra leh eloo tnan wayt mary la maeth wa akhy
33	yeshoo dayn kadh khzah dvakhya wleehoodhayye hanon dethaw amah dvakhayn ethazaz brookheh wazee nafsheh
34	wemar ayka samtoona wamreen leh maran ta khzee
35	wathyan way demawy dyeshoo
36	wamreen waw eehoodhayye khzaw kma rakhem wa leh
37	nasheen dayn menhon emarw la meshkakh wa hana dfatakh aynawy dhaw samya nebedh daf hana la nmooth
38	yeshoo dayn kadh methazaz baynawy wleh etha lvayth qvoora whoo bayth qvoora eethawy wa martha wkhifa seema wath al tareh
39	wemar yeshoo shqoolw kifa hadhe amra leh marta khatheh dhaw meetha mary men kadoo sree leh arba leh gayr yawmeen
40	amar lah yeshoo la emreth lekhy den thaymneen tekhzayn shoovkheh dalaha
41	washqalw kifa ha whoo yeshoo areem aynawy lel wemar ava mawde na lakh dashmatany
42	wena yadha na davkhulzvan shama at lee ela metul kensha hana dqaem amar na halayn danhaymnon dat shadartany
43	wkhadh emar halayn qa bqala rama laazar ta lvar
44	wanfaq haw meetha kadh aseeran eedhawy wreghlawy bfesqyatha wapawy aseeran bsoodhara amar lhon yeshoo shraoo washvooqw azel
45	wsagiyye men eehoodhayye dethaw lwath maryam kadh khzaw medem davadh yeshoo haymenw beh
46	wnasheen menhon ezalw lwath preeshe wemarw lhon medem davadh yeshoo
47	wethkanashw rabay kahne wafreeshe wamreen waw mana nebedh dhana gavra athwatha sagiyyatha avedh
48	wen shavqeen nan leh hakhana kulhon nasha mhaymneen beh wathayn roomayye shaqleen athran waman
49	khadh dayn menhon dashmeh qayyafa rab kahne wa dha shata wemar lhon aton la yadheen ton medem
50	wla methkhashveen ton dfaqakh lan dkhadh gavra nmooth khlaf ama wla kuleh ama nivadh
51	hadhe dayn men svooth nafsheh la emar ela metul drab kahne wa dha shata ethnabee datheedh wa yeshoo danmooth khlaf ama
52	wla balkhoodh khlaf ama ela daf bnayya dalaha damvadreen nkhanesh lakhdha
53	wmen haw yawma ethkhashavw waw dneqtloona
54	hoo dayn yeshoo la mhalekh wa galyaeeth bayth eehoodhayye ela ezal leh men taman lathra dqareev lkhoorba lkharkha dmethqre afraym wthaman methhapakh wa am talmeedhawy
55	qareev wa dayn peskha deehoodhayye wasleqw sagiyye men qoorya luwrishlem qdham adhidha dandhakon nafshhon
