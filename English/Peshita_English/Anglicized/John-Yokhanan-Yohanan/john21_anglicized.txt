3	amar lhon shemon kifa azel na esoodh noone amreen leh af khnan athaynan amakh wanfaqw wasleqw lasfeeta wavhaw lilya medem la sadhw

6	amar lhon armaw mseedhtkhon men gaba dyameena dasfeeta wmeshkkheen ton warmeew wla eshkakhw lmegdhah lamseedhta men soogha dnoone dekhdath

8	khrane dayn talmeedhe basfeeta ethaw la gayr rakheeqeen waw sagee men ara ela aykh mathayn ameen wnaghdeen waw lah lamseedhta ha dnoone
9	kadh dayn sleqw lara khzaw goomre kadh seeman wnoona kadh seem layhayn wlakhma

11	wasleq shemon kifa wnaghdah lamseedhta lara kadh malya noone rawrve ma wkhamsheen wathlatha wavhana kuleh yooqra la estaryath mseedhta ha
12	wemar lhon yeshoo taw eshtaraw nash dayn men talmeedhe la mamrakh wa danshaleewy dmanoo dyadheen waw dmaran oo
13	qrev dayn yeshoo washqal lakhma wnoone wyav lhon
14	hadhe dathlath zavneen ethkhzee yeshoo lthalmeedhawy kadh qam men bayth meethe
15	kadh dayn eshtareew emar yeshoo lshemon kifa shemon bar-yawna rakhem at lee yateer men halayn amar leh een mary at yadha at drakhem na lakh amar leh ree lee emray
16	amar leh toov dthartayn zavneen shemon bar-yawna rakhem at lee amar leh een mary at yadha at drakhem na lakh amar leh yeshoo ree lee erbay
17	amar leh dathlath zavneen shemon bar-yawna rakhem at lee wkheryath leh lkhifa demar leh dathlath zavneen drakhem at lee wemar leh mary kul medem at khakhem at at yadha at drakhem na lakh amar leh yeshoo ree lee nqawathy
18	ameen ameen amar na lakh dkhadh tle wayt at lnafshakh asar wayt khasayk wamhalekh wayt layka dsave at ma dayn dasevt tefshoot eedhayk wakhreen nesoor lakh khasayk wnawblakh layka dla save at
19	hadhe dayn emar dankhawe bayna mawta theedh danshabakh lalaha wkhadh emar halayn emar leh ta bathary
20	wethpnee shemon kifa wakhza lthalmeedha haw drakhem wa yeshoo dathe bathreh haw danfal wa bakhshameetha al khadhyeh dyeshoo wemar mary manoo mashlem lakh
21	lhana kadh khza kifa emar lyeshoo mary whana mana

23	wnefqath hadhe meltha bayth akhe dhaw talmeedha la maeth yeshoo dayn la wa dla maeth emar ela den save na danqawe hana dhama dathe na lakh ma lakh
