1	halayn malleth amkhon dla tethkashlon
2	napqoonakhon gayr men knooshathhon wthithe shatha dkhul dneqtoolkhon nasbar dqoorbana mqarev lalaha
3	whalayn nebdhon metul dla eedhaw wla lavy wla lee
4	halayn malleth amkhon dma detha edanhayn tehdon enayn dena emreth lkhon halayn dayn men qdheem la emreth lkhon damkhon weeth
5	hasha dayn azel na lwath man dshadrany wla nash menkhon mshael lee layka azel at
6	emreth lkhon gayr halayn wethath karyootha wamlath lebawathkhon
7	ela ena shrara amar na lkhon dfaqakh lkhon dena izal en gayr ena la azel na paraqlita la athe lwathkhon en dayn izal eshadreewy lwathkhon
8	wma detha hoo nakseewy lalma al khteetha wal zadeeqootha wal deena
9	al khteetha dla mhaymneen bee
10	al zadeeqootha dayn dalwath avy azel na wla toov khazayn ton lee
11	al deena dayn darkhoona dalma hana deen oo
12	toov sagee eeth lee lmimar lkhon ela la meshkkheen ton lmekhadh hasha
13	ma detha dayn rookha dashrara hoo ndhabarkhon bkhuleh shrara la gayr nmalel men reyan nafsheh ela kul dneshma haw nmalel watheedhatha nawdakhon
14	whoo nshabkhany metul dmen deely nesav wankhawaykhon
15	kul medem diyth lavy deely oo metul hana emreth lkhon dmen deely nesav wankhawaykhon
16	qaleel wla tekhzoonany wthoov qaleel wthekhzoonany dazel na lwath ava
17	wemarw waw talmeedhawy khadh lkhadh mana y hadhe demar lan dqaleel wla tekhzoonany wthoov qaleel wthekhzoonany wdhazel na lwath avy
18	wamreen waw mana y hadhe qaleel demar la yadheenan mana mmalel
19	yeshoo dayn eedha dvaayn waw lamshaalootheh wemar lhon al hadhe baayn ton am khdhadhe demreth lkhon dqaleel wla tekhzoonany wthoov qaleel wthekhzoonany
20	ameen ameen amar na lkhon dthevkon aton wthelon walma nekhde walkhon tekhre ela karyoothkhon lkhadhootha tehwe
21	attha ma dyalda karya lah damta yawma dmawladhah ma dyeldath dayn bra la ahda oolsanah metul khadhootha detheeledh barnasha balma
22	af aton hasha karya lkhon toov dayn ekhzaykhon wnekhde lebkhon wkhadhwathkhon la nash nasev menkhon
23	wavhaw yawma lee la teshelon medem ameen ameen amar na lkhon dkhul medem dtheshelon lavy bshemy netel lkhon
24	dhama lhasha la shelton medem bshemy shalw wthesvon dthehwe khadhwathkhon mshamlya
25	halayn bfelatha malleth amkhon athya dayn shatha emathy dla emalel amkhon bfelatha ela een baghle evadeq lkhon al ava
26	bhaw yawma dtheshelon bshemy wla amar na lkhon dena eve men ava laykon
27	hoo gayr ava rakhem lkhon daton rkhemtoonany whaymenton dena men lwath alaha nefqeth
28	nefqeth men lwath ava wetheeth lalma wthoov shaveq na lalma wazel na lee lwath ava
29	amreen leh talmeedhawy ha hasha galyaeeth mmalel at wfeletha wla khdha amar at
30	hasha yadheen nan dkhul medem yadha at wla sneeq at dnash nshalakh bhadhe mhaymneen nan dmen alaha nfaqt
31	amar lhon yeshoo haymenw
32	dha athya shatha whasha ethath dthethbadron nash lathreh wtheshbqoonany balkhoodhay wla hweeth balkhoodhay dava amy oo
33	halayn emreth lkhon dvee nehwe lkhon shlama balma hawe lkhon oolsana ela ethlabvw ena zkheetheh lalma
