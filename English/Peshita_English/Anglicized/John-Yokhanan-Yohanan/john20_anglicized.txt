1	bkhadh bshaba dayn ethath maryam maghdlata bsafra adh kheshookh lvayth qvoora wakhzath lkhifa dashqeela men qavra
2	wrehtath ethath lwath shemon kifa walwath haw talmeedha khrina drakhem wa yeshoo wamra lhon dshaqloo lmaran men haw bayth qvoora wla yadha na ayka samoo
3	wanfaq shemon whaw talmeedha khrina wathayn waw lvayth qvoora
4	wrahteen waw trayhon akhkhdha haw dayn talmeedha rhet qadmeh lshemon wetha qadhmayya lvayth qvoora
5	wadheeq khza ketane kadh seemeen meal dayn la al
6	etha dayn shemon bathreh wal lvayth qvoora wakhza ketane kadh seemeen
7	wsoodhara haw dakhzeeq wa brisheh la am ketane ela kadh kreekh wseem lastar bakhdha dooka
8	hadayn al af haw talmeedha detha qadhmayya lvayth qvoora wakhza whaymen
9	la gayr dhakeel yadheen waw men kthave datheedh wa lamqam men meethe
10	wezalw henon talmeedhe toov ldhookathhon
11	maryam dayn qama wath lwath qavra wvakhya wkhadh bakhya adheeqath bqavra
12	wakhzath trayn malakhe bkheware dyathbeen khadh men esadhawy wkhadh men reghlawy ayka dseem wa paghreh dyeshoo
13	wamreen lah attha mana bakhya aty amra lhon dshaqloo lmary wla yadha na ayka samoo
14	hadhe emrath wethpanyath lvestrah wakhzath lyeshoo dqaem wla yadha wath dyeshoo oo
15	amar lah yeshoo attha mana bakhya aty walman baya aty hee dayn sevrath dghanana hoo wamra leh mary en at shqalta emar lee ayka samta izal eshqleewy
16	amar lah yeshoo maryam wethpanyath wamra leh evraeeth raboolee dmethemar malfana
17	amar lah yeshoo la tethqarveen lee la gayr dhakeel selqeth lwath avy zely dayn lwath akhay wemary lhon saleq na lwath avy wavookhon walahy walahkhon
18	hadayn ethath maryam maghdlata wsabrath lthalmeedhe dakhzath lmaran wadhhalayn emar lah
19	kadh hwa dayn ramsha dyawma haw dkhadh bshaba wthare akheedheen waw dayka diythayhon waw talmeedhe metul dekhltha deehoodhayye etha yeshoo qam baynathhon wemar lhon shlama amkhon
20	hadhe emar wkhawee enon eedhawy wsetreh wakhdheew talmeedhe dakhzaw lmaran
21	emar lhon dayn toov yeshoo shlama amkhon aykana dshadrany avy af ena mshadar na lkhon
22	wkhadh emar halayn nfakh bhon wemar lhon qabelw rookha dqoodhsha
23	en teshbqon khtahe lnash neshtavqon leh wen tekhdon dnash akheedheen
24	tawma dayn khadh men tresarta haw dmethemar tama la wa taman wa amhon kadh etha yeshoo
25	wamreen leh talmeedhe khzayn lmaran hoo dayn emar lhon ela khaze na biydhawy dookyatha dsese wrame na bhayn sevathy wmawshet na eedhy bdhafneh la mhaymen na
26	wvathar tmanya yawmeen toov lghaw waw talmeedhe wthawma amhon wetha yeshoo kadh akheedheen tare qam bamsatha wemar lhon shlama amkhon
27	wemar lthawma ayta sevakh lharka wakhzee eedhay wayta eedhakh wawshet bghaby wla tehwe la mhaymna ela mhaymna
28	wana tawma wemar leh mary walahy
29	amar leh yeshoo hasha dakhzaytany hayment toovayhon laylayn dla khzaoony whaymenw
30	sagiyyatha dayn athwatha khranyatha vadh yeshoo qdham talmeedhawy aylayn dla ktheevan bakhthava hana
31	af halayn dayn dakhtheevan dathhaymnon dyeshoo hoo msheekha breh dalaha wma dhaymenton nehon lkhon bashmeh khayye dalalam
