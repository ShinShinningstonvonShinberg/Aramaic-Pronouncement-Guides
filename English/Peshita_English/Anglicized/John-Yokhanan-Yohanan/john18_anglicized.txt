1	halayn emar yeshoo wanfaq am talmeedhawy levra darghelta dqedhron athar diyth wath gantha ayka dal hoo wthalmeedhawy
2	yadha wa dayn af eehoodha mashlmana ldhooktha ha metul dsagee zavna kanesh wa taman yeshoo am talmeedhawy
3	hoo hakheel eehoodha dvar espeer wmen lwath rabay kahne wafreeshe dvar dakhshe wetha lthaman am naftire wlampeedhe wzayna
4	yeshoo dayn dyadha wa kul medem dathe lawy nfaq wemar lhon lman baayn ton
5	amreen leh lyeshoo nasrayya amar lhon yeshoo ena na qaem wa dayn af eehoodha mashlmana amhon
6	wkhadh emar lhon yeshoo dena na ezalw lvestarhon wanfalw al ara
7	toov shael enon yeshoo lman baayn ton henon dayn emarw lyeshoo nasrayya
8	amar lhon yeshoo emreth lkhon dena na wen lee baayn ton shvooqw lhalayn azeen
9	dtheshlam meltha demar daylayn dyavt lee la awbdheth menhon afla khadh
10	shemon dayn kifa eeth wa lawy safsira wshamtah wamkha lavdeh drab kahne wshaqlah edhneh dyameena shmeh dayn davda malekh
11	wemar yeshoo lkhifa seem safsira bkhelthah kasa dyav lee avy la eshtaywy
12	hadayn espeer wkiliyyarkhe wdhakhshe deehoodhayye akhdoo lyeshoo wasroo
13	waytyoo lwath khanan looqdham metul dakhmoo wa dqayyafa haw diythawy wa rab kahne dshata ha
14	eethawy wa dayn qayyafa haw damlakh leehoodhayye dfaqakh dkhadh gavra nmooth khlaf ama
15	shemon dayn kifa wkhadh men talmeedhe khrane athayn waw bathreh dyeshoo lhaw dayn talmeedha yadha wa leh rab kahne wal am yeshoo ldharta
16	shemon dayn qaem wa lvar lwath tara wanfaq haw talmeedha khrina dyadha wa leh rab kahne wemar lnatrath tara waleh lshemon
17	emrath dayn laymtha natrath tara lshemon lma af at men talmeedhawy at dhana gavra amar lah la
18	wqameen waw avde wdhakhshe wsameen waw noora dneshkhnon metul dqareesh wa qaem wa dayn af shemon amhon wshakhen
19	rab kahne dayn shaleh lyeshoo al talmeedhawy wal yoolpaneh
20	wemar leh yeshoo ena een baghle malleth am ama wavkhulzvan alfeth bakhnooshta wavhaykla ayka dkhulhon eehoodhayye methkansheen wmedem btooshya la malleth
21	mana mshael at lee shael lhanon dashmaw mana malleth amhon ha henon yadheen kul medem demreth
22	wkhadh halayn emar khadh men dakhshe dqaem wa mkha al pakeh lyeshoo wemar leh hakhana yahev at pethghama lrab kahne
23	na yeshoo wemar leh en beeshaeeth malleth ashedh al beeshta wen dayn shapeer lmana mkhaytany
24	khanan dayn shadar lyeshoo kadh aseer lwath qayyafa rab kahne
25	wshemon kifa qaem wa wshakhen wamreen leh lma af at khadh men talmeedhawy at whoo kfar wemar la weeth
26	amar leh khadh men avde drab kahne khyaneh dhaw dafsaq wa shemon edhneh la ena khzeethakh ameh bghantha
27	wthoov kfar shemon wvah bshatha qra tarnaghla
28	aytyoo dayn lyeshoo men lwath qayyafa lapretawreen wiythawy wa safra whenon la alw lapretawreen dla nethtawshon adh akhleen peskha
29	nfaq dayn peelataws lvar lwathhon wemar lhon mana mekhal-qarsa eeth lkhon al gavra hana
30	naw wamreen leh eloo la avedh beeshatha wa afla lakh mashlmeen wayn leh
31	amar lhon peelataws doovroo aton wdhoonoo aykh namooskhon amreen leh eehoodhayye la shaleet lan lmeqtal lnash
32	dtheshlam meltha demar yeshoo kadh mawda bayna mawta theedh danmooth
33	al dayn peelataws lapretawreen waqra lyeshoo wemar leh at oo malkhon deehoodhayye
34	amar leh yeshoo men nafshakh emart hadhe aw khrane emarw lakh lay
35	amar leh peelataws lma ena eehoodhayya na bnay amakh oo wrabay kahne ashlmookh lee mana vadt
36	amar leh yeshoo malkoothy deely la hwath men hana alma eloo men alma wath hana malkoothy methkatsheen waw mshamshanay dla eshtlem leehoodhayye hasha dayn malkoothy deely la wath meka
37	amar leh peelataws madhayn malka at amar leh yeshoo at emart dmalka na ena lhadhe eeleedh na walhadhe etheeth lalma dashedh al shrara kul man diythawy men shrara shama qaly
38	amar leh peelataws manaw shrara wkhadh emar hadhe nfaq leh toov lwath eehoodhayye wemar lhon ena afla khdha eltha meshkakh na beh
39	yadha dayn eeth lkhon dkhadh eshre lkhon bfeskha savayn ton hakheel eshre lkhon lhana malka deehoodhayye
40	waqaw kulhon wamreen la lhana ela lvar-aba eethawy wa dayn hana bar-aba gayyasa
