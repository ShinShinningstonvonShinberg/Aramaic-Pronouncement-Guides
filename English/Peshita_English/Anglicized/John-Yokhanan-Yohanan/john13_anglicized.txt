1	qdham dayn idha dfeskha yadha wa yeshoo damtath shatha danshane men hana alma lwath avoo wakhev ldheeleh davhana alma wadhama lkhartha akhev enon
2	wkhadh hwath khshameetha rme wa leh lsatana blebeh deehoodha bar shemon skharyoota dnashlmeewy
3	hoo dayn yeshoo metul dyadha wa dkhul medem yav ava biydhawy wadhmen alaha nfaq walwath alaha azel
4	qam men khshameetha wsam nakhtawy washqal sedoona mkha bkhasawy
5	warmee mayya bamshaghta wsharee lamshaghoo reghle dthalmeedhawy wamshawe wa bsedoona damkhe bkhasawy

8	amar leh shemon kifa lalam la msheegh at lee reghlay amar leh yeshoo en la msheegh na lakh layt lakh amy mnatha

10	amar leh yeshoo haw daskhe la sneeq ela reghlawy balkhoodh nsheegh kuleh gayr dkhe w af aton kulkhon dkhayya aton ela la kulkhon
11	yadha wa gayr yeshoo lhaw dmashlem leh metul hana emar dla wa kulkhon dkhayya aton
12	kadh dayn asheegh reghlayhon shqal nakhtawy westmekh wemar lhon yadheen ton mana evdeth lkhon
13	aton qarayn ton lee raban wmaran wshapeer amreen ton eethay gayr
14	en ena hakheel markhon wrabkhon asheegheth lkhon reghlaykon kma aton khayyaveen ton dathsheeghon reghle khadh dkhadh
15	hana gayr toofsa yehbeth lkhon daykana dena evdeth lkhon af aton tebdhon
16	ameen ameen amar na lkhon dlayt avda drab men mareh wla shleekha drab men man dshadreh
17	en halayn tedon toovane aton en tebdhon enayn
18	la wa al kulkhon amar na yadha na gayr laylayn daghveeth ela dakhthava neshlam dhaw dakhel amy lakhma areem lay eqveh
19	men hasha amar na lkhon men qdham dnehwe dma dahwa thaymnon dena na
20	ameen ameen amar na lkhon dman damqabel lman damshadar na lee mqabel wman dlee mqabel mqabel lman dshadrany
21	halayn emar yeshoo wethazaz brookheh washedh wemar ameen ameen amar na lkhon dkhadh menkhon nashlmany
22	kharw dayn talmeedhe khadh bkhadh metul dla yadheen waw dal manoo emar
23	eeth wa dayn men talmeedhawy khadh dasmeekh wa boobeh haw drakhem wa leh yeshoo
24	lhana rmaz shemon kifa danshaleewy dmanoo haw demar lawy
25	wanfal haw talmeedha al khadhyeh dyeshoo wemar leh mary manoo hana
26	na yeshoo wemar haw oo dsava na lakhma yahev na leh wasva yeshoo lakhma wyav leehoodha bar shemon skharyoota
27	wvathar lakhma hadayn ethalal beh satana wemar leh yeshoo medem davedh at vedh baghal
28	hadhe dayn la nash eedha men hanon smeekhe dal mana emar leh
29	nasheen gayr svarw metul daghloosqma sedhawy hwa deehoodha dmefqadh pqadh leh dnezben medem dmethbe ladhidha aw dnetel medem lmeskine
30	hoo dayn eehoodha nsav lakhma bar shatheh wanfaq leh lvar lilya wa dayn kadh nfaq
31	wemar yeshoo hasha eshtabakh breh dnasha walaha eshtabakh beh
32	wen alaha eshtabakh beh waf alaha mshabakh leh beh wmekhdha mshabakh leh
33	bnay qaleel khreen amkhon na wthevoonany waykana demreth leehoodhayye dlayka dena azel na aton la meshkkheen ton lmetha waf lkhon amar na hasha
