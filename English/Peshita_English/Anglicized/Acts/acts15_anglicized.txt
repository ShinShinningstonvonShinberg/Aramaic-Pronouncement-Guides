1	nkhethw waw dayn nasha men eehoodh wmalfeen waw lhon lakhe den hoo dla gazreen ton bayadha dnamoosa la meshkkheen ton lmekha
2	wahwa shghooshya sagiyya wavatha lpawlaws walvarnava amhon wahwath dnesqon pawlaws wvarnava wakhrane amhon lwath shleekhe wqasheeshe dvuwrishlem metul hadhe batha
3	wlawyath shadrath enon ita wradhayn waw bkhulah pooneeqi waf bayth shamrayye kadh meshtaayn waw al poonayya damme wavdeen waw khadhootha rabtha lkhulhon akhe
4	wkhadh ethaw luwrishlem ethqabalw men ita wmen shleekhe wmen qasheeshe weshtaeew lhon kul ma davadh amhon alaha
5	qamw waw dayn nasha aylayn dhaymenw waw men yoolpana dafreeshe wamreen dwale oo lkhon lmeghzar enon wathfaqdhon enon dnetron namoosa dmooshe
6	ethkanashw dayn shleekhe wqasheeshe dnekhzon al meltha hadhe
7	wkhadh hwath batha sageetha qam shemon wemar lhon gavre akheen aton yadheen ton dmen yawmatha qadhmayye men poomy deely gva alaha dneshmon amme meltha dasvartha wanhaymnon
8	walaha dyadha davlebawatha ashedh layhon wyav lhon rookha dqoodhsha aykh dlan
9	wmedem la prash baynayn walhon metul ddhakee bhaymanootha lebawathhon
10	whasha aton mana mnasayn ton lalaha aykh dathseemon neera al sawrayhon dthalmeedhe ayna dafla avahathan afla khnan eshkakhn lmetan
11	ela btaybootheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha mhaymneenan dnikhe akhwathhon
12	washtheqw kuleh kensha wshameen waw lpawlaws walvarnava dmeshtaayn waw kul ma davadh alaha biydhayhon athwatha waghvarwatha bamme
13	wvathar dashtheqw qam yaqoov wemar gavre akhayn shoomoony
14	shemon eshtaee lkhon aykana sharee alaha lmeghba men amme ama lashmeh
15	walhadhe shalman melayhon danviyye aykh ma dakhtheev
16	dmen bathar halayn ehpookh waqeem mashkneh ddhaweedh ayna danfal wevne medem danfal meneh waqeemeewy
17	aykh dnevon sharkhon davnaynasha lmarya wkhulhon amme aylayn dethqree shemy layhon amar marya davadh halayn kulhayn
18	eedheeeen men alam vadhawy dalaha
19	metul hadhe ena amar na dla nehon shakhqeen laylayn dmen amme methpnayn lwath alaha
20	ela neshtlakh lhon dnehon pareeqeen men tamootha dadhveekha wmen zanyootha wmen dakhneeqa wmen dma
21	mooshe gayr men dare qadhmayye bkhul mdheena eeth wa leh karooze bakhnooshatha davkhul shabeen qarayn leh
22	hadayn shleekhe wqasheeshe am kulah ita gvaw gavre menhon wshadarw lantiyyawkhee am pawlaws wvarnava leehoodha dmethqre bar-shaba walsheela gavre drishe waw bhon bakhe
23	wakhthavw egarta biydhayhon hakhana shleekhe wqasheeshe wakhe laylayn diyth bantiyyawkhee wavsooriyya wavqeeleeqiyya akhe dmen amme shlam
24	shmee lan dnasheen menan nfaqw wdhalakhkhon bmele wahpekhw nafshathkhon kadh amreen dthehon gazreen wnatreen namoosa aylayn dakhnan la paqedhn enon
25	metul hana ethkhashavn kulan kadh kneesheenan waghvayn gavre wshadarn lwathkhon am pawlaws wvarnava khabeevayn
26	nasha dashlemw nafshathhon khlaf shmeh dmaran yeshoo msheekha
27	wshadarn amhon leehoodha walsheela dhenon bmeltha nimron lkhon henayn halayn
28	hwa gayr sevyana lrookha dqoodhsha waf lan dla nettseem laykon yooqra yateera lvar men halayn dalsan
29	dthethrakhqon men dadhveekha wmen dma wmen khneeqa wmen zanyootha dkhadh tetron nafshkhon men halayn shapeer tehon hwaw shareereen bmaran
30	hanon dayn deshtlakhw ethaw lantiyyakhiyya wakhnashw kuleh ama wyavw egarta
31	wkhadh qraw khdheew wethbayyaw
32	wavmeltha ateerta khayyelw lakhe wqayyemw enon dvayth eehoodha wsheela metul daf henon nviyye waw
33	wkhadh hwaw taman zavna shraw enon akhe bashlama lwath shleekhe
34	bram hwa sevyaneh dsheela danqawe taman
35	pawlaws dayn wvarnava qaweew waw bantiyyawkhee wmalfeen waw wamsabreen am khrane sagiyye meltheh dalaha
36	wvathar yawmatha emar pawlaws lvarnava nethpne wnesoor lakhe davkhul mdheena dakhrezn bah meltha dalaha wnekhze mana veedheen
37	barnava dayn save wa dnedhbar lyookhanan haw dethkanee marqaws
38	pawlaws dayn la save wa dnedhbreh amhon metul dashvaq wa enon kadh henon bpamfooliyya wla ezal amhon
39	metul hana kheryana prashw khadh men khadh wvarnava dvar lmarqaws wardhaw byama wezalw lhon lqoopraws
40	pawlaws dayn gva leh lsheela wanfaq kadh maghel men akhe ltaybootha dalaha
41	wradhe wa bsooriyya wavqeeleeqiyya wamqayyem idhatha
