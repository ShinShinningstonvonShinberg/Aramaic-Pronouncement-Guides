1	shawal dayn dhakeel mle wa lookhama wkhemtha dqetla al talmeedhawy dmaran
2	washel leh egratha men rab kahne dnetel leh ldharmsooq lakhnooshatha den hoo dneshkakh dradhayn bhadhe oorkha gavre aw neshe nesoor nayte enon luwrishlem
3	wkhadh azel wa wsharee mmate ldharmsooq men tkhayth shelya azlegh wa lawy noohra men shmayya
4	wanfal al ara washma qala damar leh shawal shawal mana radhef at lee qshe w lakh lamvaatoo looqse
5	na hoo wemar man at mary wmaran emar ena na yeshoo nasrayya haw dat radhef at
6	ela qoom ool lamdheeta wthaman nethmalal amakh al ma dwale lakh lmebadh
7	wghavre dazeen waw ameh buwrkha qameen waw kadh tameeheen metul dqala balkhoodh shameen waw nash dayn la methkhze wa lhon
8	wqam shawal men ara wla methkhze wa leh medem kadh aynawy ptheekhan way wkhadh akheedheen biydhawy aloo ldharmsooq
9	wla methkhze wa leh tlatha yawmeen wla ekhal wla eshtee
10	eeth wa dayn bah bdharmsooq talmeedha khadh dashmeh hwa khananya wmarya emar leh bkhezwa khananya wemar ha ena mary
11	wmaran emar leh qoom zel lshooqa dmethqre treesa wavee bvayta deehoodha lshawal diythawy men tarsaws mdheeta ha gayr kadh hoo msale
12	khza bkhezwa lghavra dashmeh khananya dal wsam lawy eedha aykh dnethpatkhan aynawy
13	wemar khananya mary shemeth men sagiyye al gavra hana dakhma beeshatha asbel lqadeeshayk buwrishlem
14	wha af harka eeth leh shooltana men rabay kahne dnesoor lkhulhon aylayn dqarayn shmakh
15	wemar leh marya qoom zel metul dmana hoo lee gavya dneshqool shemy bamme wavmalke wvayth bnay eesrayyel
16	ena gayr ekhawaywy kma theedh lmekhash metul shemy
17	hadayn khananya ezal lvayta lwatheh wsam lawy eedha wemar leh shawal akhy maran yeshoo shadrany haw dethkhzee lakh buwrkha kadh athe at aykh dnethpatkhan aynayk wthethmle rookha dqoodhsha
18	wvar shatheh nfal men aynawy medem ddhame laqlafe wethpatakh aynawy wqam madh
19	wqabel saybarta wethkhayyal wahwa yawmatha lwath talmeedhe hanon diyth waw bdharmsooq
20	wvar shatheh makhrez wa bakhnooshatha deehoodhayye al yeshoo dhooyyoo breh dalaha
21	wthameeheen waw kulhon aylayn dshameen waw leh wamreen waw la wa hana haw dradhef wa lkhulhon aylayn dqarayn waw lashma hana buwrishlem af lharka layh al hadhe mshadar wa aykh dnesoor nawbel enon lrabay kahne
22	shawal dayn yateer methkhayyal wa wamzee wa lhon leehoodhayye hanon damreen waw bdharmsooq kadh mkhawe wa dhanaw msheekha
23	wkhadh sgheew leh taman yawmatha vadhw lawy nekhla eehoodhayye dneqtloona
24	ethbadaq leh dayn lshawal aparsna dvaayn waw lmebadh leh wnatreen waw tare damdheeta eemama wlilya dneqtloona
25	hadayn samoo talmeedhe bespreedha wshaboo men shoora blilya
26	wezal leh luwrishlem wsave wa lmethnaqafoo lthalmeedhe wkhulhon dakhleen waw meneh wla mhaymneen waw dthalmeedha hoo
27	barnava dayn akhdeh waytyeh lwath shleekhe weshtaee lhon aykana buwrkha khza lmarya wdhaykana malel ameh waykana bdharmsooq een baghle malel bashmeh dyeshoo
28	wael wa amhon wnafeq buwrishlem
29	wammalel wa bashmeh dyeshoo een baghle wdharesh wa am eehoodhayye aylayn dyadheen waw yawnaeeth henon dayn savayn waw lmeqtleh
30	wkhadh eedhaw akhe aytyoo blilya lqesariyya wmen taman shadroo ltarsaws
31	bram dayn ita davkhulah eehoodh wvaghleela wavshamreen eeth wa bah shlama kadh methbanya wath wradhya bdhekhlath alaha wavvooyyaa drookha dqoodhsha saghya wath
32	wahwa dkhadh methkrekh wa shemon bamdheenatha nkheth af lwath qadeeshe damreen bloodh mdheeta
33	weshkakh gavra khadh dashmeh aniyyas darme wa barsa wamsharay shneen tmane
34	wemar leh shemon aniyya mase lakh yeshoo msheekha qoom wshawa arsakh wvar shatheh qam
35	wakhzaoo kulhon damreen bloodh wavsarawna wethpneew lwath alaha
36	eeth wa dayn talmeedta khdha byawpi mdheeta dashmah hwa tveetha hadhe ateera wath bavadhe tave wavzedhqatha davda wath
37	ethkarhath dayn bhon bhanon yawmatha wmeethath waskhyooh wsamooh beleetha
38	washmaw waw talmeedhe dshemon bloodh oo mdheeta ha diythayh al gev yawpi wshadarw waw lwatheh gavre trayn dnevon meneh dla teman leh dnithe sedhayhon
39	wqam shemon ezal amhon wkhadh etha asqoo leleetha wakhnash qam lhayn khdharawy kulhayn armlatha kadh bakhyan wamkhawyan leh kooteenyatha wmartoote halayn dyahba wath lhayn tveetha kadh khayya
40	shemon dayn apeq lkhulhon nasha lvar waqedh al boorkawy wsalee wethpnee lwath shlada wemar tveetha qoomy hee dayn pethkhath aynayh wkhadh khzatheh lshemon yethbath
41	wawshet lah eedheh waqeemah waqra lqadeeshe wlarmlatha wyahbah lhon kadh khayya
42	wethyadhath hadhe bkhulah mdheeta wsagiyye haymenw bmaran
43	hwa dayn bah byawpi yawmatha la zooreen kadh shre wa bayth shemon boorsayya
