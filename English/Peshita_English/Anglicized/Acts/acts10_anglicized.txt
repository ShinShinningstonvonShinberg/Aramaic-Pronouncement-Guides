1	bqesariyya dayn eeth wa gavra khadh qentroona dashmeh wa qawrniliyyaws men speera ha dmethqarya eetaleeqi
2	wzadeeq wa wdhakhel wa men alaha hoo wvayteh kuleh wavedh wa zedhqatha sagiyyatha bama wavkhulzvan bae wa men alaha
3	hana khza malakha dalaha bkhezwa galyaeeth lapay tsha shaeen biymama dal lwatheh wemar leh qawrniliyye
4	whoo khar beh wadhkhel wemar mana mary wemar leh malakha slawathakh wzedhqathakh sleq ldhookhrana qdham alaha

8	weshtaee lhon kulmedem dakhza wshadar enon lyawpi

14	wshemon emar khas mary dmemthoom la ekhleth kul damsayyav watma
15	wthoov dthartayn zavneen qala hwa lwatheh aylayn dalaha dakee at la tsayyev

17	wkhadh metdamar shemon bnafsheh dmanaw khezwa dakhza mateew gavre hanon deshtadarw waw men qawrniliyyaws wshaelw al bayta dashre beh shemon wethaw wqamw al tara ddharta
18	wqarayn waw taman wamshaleen den shemon dmethqre kifa harka shre

20	qoom khooth wzel amhon kadh la methpalagh reyanakh metul dena hoo shadreth enon
21	hadayn nkheth shemon lwath gavre hanon wemar lhon ena hoo dvaayn ton aydha y eltha dmetulathah ethayton
22	amreen leh gavra khadh dashmeh qawrniliyyaws qentroona kina ddhakhel men alaha wmashedh lawy kuleh ama deehoodhayye ethemar leh bkhezwa men malakha qadeesha danshadar nalakh lvayteh wneshma meltha menakh
23	wael enon shemon wqabel enon kar dashre wa wqam lvathreh dyawma wanfaq ezal amhon wezalw ameh nash nash men akhe dyawpi
24	walyawma khrina al lqesariyya qawrniliyyaws dayn mqawe wa lhon kadh mkhansheen leh kulhon bnay toohmeh waf rakhme khabeeve diyth waw leh
25	wkhadh ael shemon areh qawrniliyyaws wanfal sghedh lreghlawy
26	wshemon aqeemeh wemar leh qoom lakh waf ena barnasha na
27	wkhadh mmalel ameh al weshkakh sagiyye dethaw waw lthaman
28	wemar lhon aton yadheen ton dla mapas lghavra eehoodhayya dneqaf lnasha nookhrayya dla wa bar sharbtheh wlee alaha khawyany dla imar al nash datma aw msayyav
29	metul hana theedhaeeth etheeth kadh shadarton bathary bram mshael na lkhon metul mana shadarton bathary
30	amar leh qawrniliyyaws arba yawmeen eeth dhama lhasha ha men dsaem na wvathsha shaeen kadh msale na bvayty qam gavra khadh qdhamay kadh lveesh kheware
31	wemar lee qawrniliyye eshtamath sloothakh walzedhqathakh dookhrana hwa qdham alaha
32	bram shadar lyawpi mdheeta wayta lshemon dmethqre kifa ha shre bvayteh dshemon boorsayya dal yadh yama whoo nithe nmalel amakh
33	wvar shatheh shadreth lwathakh wat shapeer vadt dethayt wha khnan kulan qdhamayk wsavaynan dneshma kul ma dethpqedh lakh men lwath alaha
