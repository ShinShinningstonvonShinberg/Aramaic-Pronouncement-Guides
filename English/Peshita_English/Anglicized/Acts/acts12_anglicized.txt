1	bhaw dayn zavna armee wa eedhayya al nasheen davita aykh dnavesh lhon herawdhes malka haw dmethkane wa aghripaws
2	waqtal bsaypa lyaqoov akhoo dyookhanan
3	wkhadh khza dshefrath hadhe leehoodhayye awsef wa lmekhadh af lshemon kifa wiythayhon waw yawmatha dfateere
4	wakhdeh warmyeh bayth aseere washlem leh shththasar stratawteen dnetrooneh dmen bathar peskha nashlmeewy lama deehoodhayye
5	wkhadh hoo shemon methntar wa bayth aseere slootha ameenta methqarva wath men ita khlafawy lalaha
6	wveh bhaw lilya dalsafra theedh wa dnashlmeewy kadh dmekh wa shemon bayth trayn estratiyyawteen waseer wa bthartayn shishlan wakhrane natreen waw tare dvayth aseere
7	malakha dmarya qam lel meneh wnoohra azlegh bkhuleh bayta wdhaqreh bghabeh waqeemeh wemar leh qoom ghal wanfal shishlatha men eedhawy
8	wemar leh malakha asoor khasayk wsan telarayk wavadh hakhana wthoov emar leh ethaf takhseethakh wtha bathary
9	wanfaq wazel wa bathreh kadh la yadha wa dshareera wath ha dhawya wath byadh malakha savar wa gayr dkhezwa khaze wa
10	wkhadh varw matartha qadhmata wadhthartayn ethaw dhama lthara dfarzla wethpthakh lhon men svooth nafsheh wkhadh nfaqw wavarw shooqa khadh praq men lwatheh malakha
11	hadayn eshtawda shemon wemar hasha yedheth bqooshta dmarya shadar malakheh wfaltany men eedheh dherawdhes malka wmen medem dmethkhashveen waw lay eehoodhayye
12	wkhadh estakal etha leh lvayta dmaryam emeh dyookhanan haw dethkanee marqaws metul dakhe sagiyye taman kneesheen waw wamsalayn
13	wanqash bthara ddharta wnefqath dthenaywy tleetha dashmah rawdhi
14	weshtawdath qaleh dshemon wavkhadhoothah la pethkhath leh tara ela hefkath brehta wamra lhon shemon ha qaem al tara ddharta
15	amreen lah mza zaty lekhy whee methkharya wath dhadhe hakhana y amreen lah kvar malakheh oo
16	wshemon naqesh wa bthara wanfaqw khzaoo wathmahw lhon
17	wamneef wa lhon eedheh aykh dneshtqon lhon wal weshtaee lhon aykana marya apqeh men bayth aseere wemar lhon eshtaaw halayn lyaqoov wlakhayn wanfaq ezal leh lathar khreen
18	wkhadh hwa safra hwa wa rawba sagiyya bayth estratiyyawte al shemon dmana hwa leh
19	herawdhes dayn kadh ba wla eshkkheh dan enon lnatoore wafqadh danmoothon wanfaq leh men eehoodh wiythawy wa bqesariyya
20	wmetul dargheez wa al soorayye wal saydanayye ethkanashw wethaw lwatheh akhkhdha wapeesw lavlastaws qaytoonqaneh dmalka wshelw meneh dnehwe lhon shayna metul dfoornasa dathrhon men malkootheh wa dherawdhes
21	byawma dayn eedheea lvesh wa herawdhes lvoosha dmalkootha weethev al beem wammalel wa am kensha
22	ama dayn kuleh qae wa wamar halayn bnath qale dalaha enayn wla way davnaynasha
23	wakhlaf dla yav teshbookhta lalaha bah bshatha mkha malakheh dmarya warfath bthawle wmeeth
24	wasvartha dalaha methkarza wath wravya
25	barnava dayn wshawal pnaw men oorishlem lantiyyawkhee men bathar dshalemw teshmeshthon wadhvarw amhon lyookhanan haw dethkanee marqaws
