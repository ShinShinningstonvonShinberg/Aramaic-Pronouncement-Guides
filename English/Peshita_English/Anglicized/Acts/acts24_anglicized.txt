1	wmen bathar yawmatha khamsha nkheth khananya rab kahne am qasheeshe wam tertulaws rhitra wawdaw lhighmawna al pawlaws
2	wkhadh ethqree aqef wa tertulaws mqatregh leh wamar bsoogha dshayna amreenan metulathakh wthaqnatha sagiyyatha way lama hana bashqal tanakh
3	wkhulan bkhul dook mqableenan tayboothakh naseekha peeliks
4	dla dayn naleykh bsagiyyatha bae na menakh dtheshma lmakeekhoothan bfaseeqatha
5	eshkakhn gayr lghavra hana diythawy mshakhtana wameer shghooshya lkhulhon eehoodhayye davkhulah ara risha w gayr dyoolpana dnasrayye
6	walhayklan sva lamsayyavoo wkhadh ekhadhna bayn dandhooneewy aykh davnamoosan
7	etha dayn loosiyyaws kiliyyarkha wvaqteera sagiyya men eedhayn adyeh wlakh shadreh
8	wafqadh laqtirghanawy dnithon lwathakh wmeshkakh at kadh mshael at leh dthilaf meneh al kulhayn halayn damqatrgheenan leh
9	areevw dayn lawy af henon eehoodhayye kadh amreen dhalayn hakhana enayn
10	warmaz highmoona lpawlaws danmalel wana pawlaws wemar men shnayya sagiyyatha yadha na diythayk dayyana dama hana wmetul hana khadhyaeeth nafeq na rookha al apay nafshy
11	kadh meshkakh at lmeda dlayt lee yateer men tresar yawmeen dselqeth luwrishlem lmesgadh
12	wla eshkkhoony dammalel na am nash bhaykla wafla kensha dkhanesh na la bakhnooshthon wla bamdheeta
13	wla dankhaon matya biydhayhon qdhamayk al medem dhasha mqatrgheen lee
14	bram dayn hadhe mawde na dveh bhana yoolpana damreen beh palakh na lalaha davahay kadh mhaymen na lkhulhayn dakhtheevan bnamoosa wvanviyye
15	wkhadh eeth lee savra al alaha ayna daf henon halayn msabreen datheedha dthehwe qyamta dmen bayth meethe dkhine wadhawale
16	metul hana af amel na dthirta dkheetha tehwe lee qdham alaha waqdham bnay nasha ameenaeeth
17	lashneen dayn sagiyyan etheeth lwath bnay ama deely detel zedhqtha weqarev qoorbana
18	weshkkhoony halayn bhaykla kadh mdhakay na la am kensha afla bashghooshya
19	ela en dashghashw nasheen eehoodhayye dethaw men asiyya aylayn dwale wa danqoomon amy qdhamayk wanqatrghon medem diyth lhon
20	aw henon halayn nimron mana eshkakhw bee sakhlootha kadh qameth qdham kenshhon
21	ela en hadhe khdha meltha daqeeth kadh qaem na baynathhon dal qyamta dmeethe metdheen na yawmana qdhamaykon
22	peeliks dayn metul dyadha wa lah luwrkha hadhe malyaeeth tahee enon kadh amar dma detha kiliyyarkha shama na baynathkhon
23	wafqadh lqentroona dnetreewy lpawlaws banyakha wadhla nash men yadhooawy nethkle dnehwe mshamesh leh
24	wmen bathar qaleel yawmatha shadar peeliks wdhoorsila attheh diythayh wath eehoodhata waqraw leh lpawlaws washmaw meneh al haymanootha damsheekha
25	wkhadh mmalel amhon al zadeeqootha wal qadeeshootha wal deena datheedh ethmlee dekhltha peeliks wemar dhasha zel wemathy dnehwe lee athra eshadar bathrakh
26	savar wa gayr dshookhda metheehev wa leh men pawlaws metul hadhe af ameenaeeth mshadar wa mayte leh wammalel ameh
27	wkhadh malee leh tartayn shneen khrina highmoona etha wa ldhooktheh dmethqre wa parqiyyaws-fistaws peeliks dayn aykh dnebedh taybootha beehoodhayye shavqeh lpawlaws kadh aseer
