1	wkhadh etha pistaws lqesariyya bathar tlatha yawmeen sleq luwrishlem
2	wawdoo rabay kahne wrishe deehoodhayye al pawlaws wvaayn waw meneh
3	kadh shaleen leh hadhe taybootha danshadar naytaywy luwrishlem kadh avdeen waw kmine buwrkha dneqtloona
4	wfistaws panee pethghama dpawlaws methntar bqesariyya wena msarhav na dekhzooq
5	aylayn hakheel diyth bkhon dmatya biydhayhon nekhthon aman wal kul sakhloo diyth beh bghavra nqatrghon
6	wkhadh hwa taman yawmatha tmanya aw esra nkheth leh lqesariyya walyawma khrina eethev al beem wafqadh dnayton lpawlaws
7	wkhadh etha khadhroo eehoodhayye dankhethw men oorishlem wrishe sagiyye waqshayya maytayn waw bathreh aylayn dla meshkkheen waw dankhaon
8	wkhadh pawlaws nafeq wa rookha dla askel medem la bnamoosa deehoodhayye wla bhaykla wla bqesar
9	pistaws dayn metul dsave wa dnarshe taybootha beehoodhayye emar leh lpawlaws save at dthesaq luwrishlem wthaman al halayn tetdheen qdhamay
10	na pawlaws wemar al beem dqesar qaem na harka hoo zadheq lee lmetdhanoo la medem khteeth leehoodhayye aykh daf at yadha at
11	wen sakhlootha veedha lee aw medem dshawe lmawta la meshtel na men mawta en dayn layt medem sedhay men halayn damqatrgheen lee la nash yahev lee lhon mawhvath bghenawy dqesar qare na
12	hadayn pistaws malel am bnay melkeh wemar bghen qesar qrayt lwath qesar azel at
13	wkhadh hwaw yawmatha nkheth aghripaws malka wverneeqi lqesariyya dneshelon shlameh dfistaws
14	wkhadh hwaw lwatheh yawmatha eshtaee pistaws lmalka deeneh dpawlaws kadh amar gavra khadh aseera eshtveq men eedhay peeliks
15	wkhadh hweeth buwrishlem awdaw lee lawy rabay kahne wqasheeshe deehoodhayye wavaw debedh lhon deena meneh
16	wemreth lhon dlayt yadha lroomayye dnetlon barnasha mawhvath lqetla dhama dnithe bel deeneh wnakseewy bapawy wnetheehev leh athra lmapaq brookha al ma dmethrshe
17	wkhadh etheeth lkha dla toohayya lyawma khrina yethbeth al beem wfeqdeth dnaytoona lee lghavra
18	wqamw ameh qtirghanawy wla eshkakhw medem reshyana beesha dankhaon lawy aykh ma dsavar weeth
19	zitime dayn medem medem al dekhlathhon eeth wa lhon lwatheh wal yeshoo nash dmeeth haw damar wa pawlaws dkhay oo
20	wmetul dla qaem weeth ena al batha dhalayn emreth weeth lpawlaws den bae at dthizal luwrishlem wthaman tetdheen al halayn
21	hoo dayn ba dnethntar ldheeneh dqesar wfeqdeth dnethntar dhama deshadreewy lwath qesar
22	wemar aghripaws save weeth deshmeewy lghavra hana wfistaws emar dlamkhar shama at leh
23	walyawma khrina etha aghripaws wverneeqi bzawkha sagiyya wal lvayth deena am kiliyyarkhe wrishe damdheeta wafqadh pistaws wetha pawlaws
24	wemar pistaws aghripaws malka wkhulkhon gavre diythaykon aman al hana gavra dkhazayn ton kuleh ama deehoodhayye qablany buwrishlem wharka kadh qaayn dla toov wale lhana dnikhe
25	ena dayn adhrkheth dmedem dshawe lmawta la seer leh wmetul dhoo ba dnethntar ldheeneh dqesar peqdeth dneshtadar
26	wla yadha na mana ekhtoov lawy lqesar metul hana sveeth lmaytayyootheh qdhamaykon wyateeraeeth qdhamayk malka aghripe dma deshtaal deeneh eshkakh mana ekhtoov
27	la gayr wale dkhadh mshadreenan gavra aseera dla nekhtoov sakhlootheh
