1	wafqadh lawy pistas dneshtadar lwath qesar liytaliyya washlmeh lpawlaws wlaseere khrane ameh lghavra khadh qentroona men espeer sevasti dashmeh hwa yooliyyaws
2	wkhadh hwath dnerde nkhethn lelfa diythayh wath men adhrmnutaws mdheeta waza wath lathra dasiyya wal wa aman lelfa aristarkhaws maqedhawniyya dmen thesalawneeqi mdheeta
3	walyawma khrina mateen lsaydan wethkhashakh qentroona bamrakhmanootha lwath pawlaws wapes leh dnizal lwath rakhmawy wnettneekh
4	wmen taman rdhayn wmetul drookhe saqoovlan way ethkrekhn al qoopraws
5	wavarn yama dqeeleeqiyya wadhpamfooliyya wmateen lmoora mdheeta dlooqiyya
6	weshkakh taman qentroona elfa men aleksandriyya daza wath liytaliyya wawtvan bah
7	wmetul dyaqeeraeeth radhya wath lyawmatha sagiyye lmakhsen mateen looqval qneedhaws gazarta wmetul dla shavqa wath rookha dnizal treesaeeth ethkrekhn al qriti looqval salmawni mdheeta
8	walmakhsen kadh radhaynan khdharayh mateen ldhooktha dmethqarya lmine shapeere wqareeva wath lah mdheeta dashmah lasea
9	wahwayn taman zavna sagiyya dhama davar af yawma dsawma deehoodhayye wahwa leh qenta dnerde nash byama wmalekh wa lhon pawlaws
10	wamar gavre khaze na dvuwlsana wavkhoosrana sagiyya hawya mardeethan law balkhoodh dmawblah delfan ela af dnafshathan deelan
11	qentroona dayn lqooverniti walmarah delfa shama wa yateer men melawy dpawlaws
12	wmetul dla ahen wa haw lmina lmastayyoo beh sathwa sagiyye menan savayn waw dnerde men taman wenhoo dmeshkkheen danmanon wnaston balmina khadh diythawy wa baqriti wmethqre wa pooniks wkhaar wa lthaymna
13	wkhadh neshbath rookha dthaymna wasvarw dammatayn aykh sevyanhon radhayn waw khdharay qriti
14	wmen bathar qaleel nfaq layn mashva dalala dmethqre toofaneeqaws ewraqleedhawn
15	wethkhatpath elfa wla eshkkhath dathqoom looqval rookha wyavn laydha dhee
16	wkhadh varn gazarta khdha dmethqarya qoodha lmakhsen eshkakhn ekhadhn lqarqoora
17	wkhadh shqalnah mkhayseen wayn wmathqneen lah lelfa wmetul ddhakhleen wayn ddhalma nepel bmakhatteh dyama akhethna larmenawn whakhwath radhayn wayn
18	wkhadh qam leh layn keemoona qashya lyawma khrina shdhayn maneen byama
19	walyawma dathlatha mane deelah delfa biydhayn shdhayn
20	wkhadh ekhadh leh sathwa yawmatha yateere wla shemsha methkhze wa wla sahra wla kawkve savra dkhayyayn kul kuleh ethpseq wa leh
21	wkhadh nash medem la mestaybar wa hadayn qam pawlaws baynathhon wemar eloo ettpeeston lee gavre la radhayn wayn men qriti wmethkhaskeen wayn men khoosrana wmen oolsana hana
22	whasha malekh na dthehon dla aqa nafsha gayr menkhon khdha la avda ela en elfa
23	ethkhzee lee gayr blilya hana malakheh dalaha haw ddheeleh na wleh palakh na
24	wemar lee la tedhkhal pawle theedh oo lakh lamqam qdham qesar wha yav lakh alaha mawhavta lkhul dradhayn amakh
25	metul hana ethlabavw gavre mhaymen na gayr balaha dhakhana hawe aykh ma dethmalal amy
26	bram lghazarta khdha eeth lan dneshtdhe
27	wmen bathar arbthasar yawmeen datayn wettarafn bhadhriyyaws yama bfelgeh dlilya svarw malakhe dlara methqarveen waw
28	warmeew ewqeenas weshkakhw qawmeen esreen wthoov qaleel rdhaw weshkakhw qawmeen khamshaesre
29	wkhadh dakheeleen wayn dalma neshtkhakh lan bdhookyatha diyth bhayn shooe armeew men kharthah delfa ewqeenas arba wamsalayn waw dnehwe yawma
30	malakhe dayn baw lmeraq menah men elfa wakhethw menah lqarqoora lyama beltha dnizoon bah wnesroonah lelfa bara
31	wkhadh khza pawlaws emar lqentroona wlestratiyyawte den halayn belfa la mkhatreen aton la meshkkheen ton dthikhon
32	hadayn psaqw estratiyyawte lkhavlayh dqarqoora men elfa wshavqooh taya
33	hoo dayn pawlaws dhama dahwa safra mpees wa lkhulhon danqablon saybarta kadh amar lhon yawmana ha arbthasar yawmeen men qenta medem la teem lkhon
34	metul hadhe bae na menkhon dathqablon mekhoolta lqooyyama dkhayyaykon mentha gayr men risha dkhadh menkhon la avda
35	wkhadh halayn emar nsav lakhma wshabakh lalaha qdham kulhon waqsa waqef lmekhal
36	wethbayyaw kulhon wqabelw toorsayya
37	eethayn wayn dayn belfa mathayn wshaveen wsheth nafshan
38	wkhadh svaw mekhoolta aqelw men elfa washqalw khete washdhaw byama
39	wkhadh hwa yawma sapane aydha y ara la eshtawdaw ela kharw al gev yavsha kenfa khdha dyama ayka dranayn waw den meshkkha nedhkhoonah lelfa
40	wafsaqw ewqeenas men elfa warpeew enayn byama washraw rakhbe dsawkane wathlaw armenawn zoora lrookha dnashba wradhayn waw lapay yavsha
41	wgheshath elfa bdhooktha drama baynath trayn oomqeen dyama wethkharyath bah wqam layh gabah qadhmayya wla mettzee wa gabah dayn khrayya eshtree men qteera dghalle
42	wasvaw waw estratiyyawte danqatlon enon laseere dla narmon sakhwa wnerqon lhon menhon
43	wqentroona kla enon men hadhe metul dsave wa dnakhe lpawlaws wlaylayn dmeshkkheen waw lmarmayyoo sakhwa pqadh lhon davqadhmayye neskhon wnebron lara
44	walsharka al dape wal qayse khrane delfa abarw enon whakhana kulhon eshtawzavw lara
