1	wghavra khadh dashmeh hwa khananya am attheh dashmah hwa shapeera zaben wa qreetheh
2	washqal men teemayh wtashee kadh rgheesha wath beh attheh waytee meneh men kespa wsam qdham reghlayhon dashleekhe
3	wemar leh shemon khananya manaw dhakhana mla satana lebakh datdhagel brookha dqoodhsha wattashe men kespa dadhmayh daqreetha
4	la wa deelakh hwath adhla tezdaban wmen dezdabnath toov at shaleet wayt al dmayh lmana samt blebakh dthebedh svootha hadhe la dagelt bavnay nasha ela balaha
5	wkhadh shma khananya halayn mele nfal wmeeth wahwath dekhltha rabtha bkhulhon halayn dashmaw
6	wqamw aylayn dalaymeen bhon wkhanshoo wapeqw qavroo
7	wmen bathar dahway tlath shaeen af attheh elath kadh la yadha wath mana hwa
8	amar lah shemon emar lee en bhalayn dmayya zabenton qreetha hee dayn emrath een bhalayn dmayya
9	amar lah shemon metul deshtweeton lamnasayyoo rookheh dmarya ha reghlayhon dqavoorawy dvalekhy bthara whenon napqoonekhy
10	wvah bshatha neflath qdham reghlayhon wmeethath walw layme hanon weshkkhooh kadh meetha waqfasw awbelw qavrooh al gev balah
11	wahwath dekhltha rabtha bkhulah ita wavkhulhon hanon dashmaw
12	whawyan way byadh shleekhe athwatha waghvarwatha sagiyyatha bama wkhulhon kneesheen waw akhkhdha bestwa dashlaymon
13	wmen nasha khrane nash la mamrakh wa dnethqarav lwathhon ela mawrev wa lhon ama
14	wyateer mettawsfan waw aylayn damhaymneen waw bmarya kensha dghavre wadhneshe
15	aykana davshooqe mapqeen waw lakhreehe kadh rmayn waw barsatha demathy dnehwe athe shemon afen telaneetheh tagen layhon
16	athayn waw dayn sagiyye lwathhon men mdheenatha khranyatha dakhdharay oorishlem kadh maytayn waw kreehe waylayn dhawyan way lhon rookhe tanfatha wmethkhalmeen waw kulhon
17	wethmlee wa khsama rab kahne wkhulhon dameh diythayhon waw men yoolpana dzadooqayye
18	warmeew eedhayya al shleekhe wekhadhw esarw enon bayth aseere
19	hadayn blilya malakha dmarya pthakh tara dvayth aseere wapeq enon wemar lhon
20	zelw qoomw bhaykla wmalelw lama kulhayn mele halayn dkhayye
21	wanfaqw edan shafra walw lhaykla wmalfeen waw rab kahne dayn waylayn dameh qraw lkhavrayhon walqasheeshe diysrayyel wshadarw lvayth aseere dnayton enon lashleekhe
22	wkhadh ezalw aylayn deshtadarw menhon la eshkakhw enon bayth aseere wahfakhw ethaw
23	amreen eshkakhn bayth aseere dakheedh zheeraeeth waf lnatoore dqameen al tare wafthakhn wnash la eshkakhn taman
24	wkhadh shmaw halayn mele rabay kahne warkhoone dhaykla taweereen waw layhon wmethkhashveen waw dmana y hadhe
25	wetha nash awda enon dhanon gavre dakhvashton bayth aseere ha qameen bhaykla wmalfeen lama
26	hadayn ezalw arkhoone am dakhshe dnayton enon la baqteera dakhleen waw gayr dalma nergoom enon ama
27	wkhadh ayteew enon aqeemw enon qdham kuleh kensha waqef wa rab kahne lmimar lhon
28	la wa mefqadh pqadhn wayn lkhon dalnash la talfon bashma hana aton dayn ha mlaytoonah luwrishlem men yoolpankhon wsavayn ton dthayton layn dmeh dghavra hana
29	na shemon am shleekhe wemar lhon lalaha wale lmettpasoo yateer men dlavnaynasha
30	alaha davahathan aqeem lyeshoo ayna daton qtalton kadh tlaytoona al qaysa
31	leh lhana aqeem alaha risha wmakhyana wareemeh byameeneh aykh dnetel tyabootha wshoovqan khtahe liysrayyel
32	wakhnan sahde nan dmele halayn wrookha dqoodhsha haw dyav alaha laylayn damhaymneen beh
33	wkhadh shmaw waw halayn mele methgawzleen waw brooghza wmethkhashveen waw lmeqtal enon
34	wqam wa khadh men preeshe dashmeh hwa gamaleeyel malef namoosa wamyaqar men kuleh ama wafqadh dnapqon enon lashleekhe lvar edana zoora
35	wemar lhon gavre bnay eesrayyel ezdarw bnafshkhon wakhzaw mana wale lkhon lmebadh al halayn nasha
36	men qdham gayr hana zavna qam wa tawdha wemar al nafsheh dmedem oo rav wezalw bathreh aykh arbama gavreen whoo ethqtel waylayn dazeen waw bathreh ethbadarw wahwaw aykh la medem
37	wqam men bathreh eehoodha gleelayya byawmatha dmethkathbeen waw nasha bakhsef risha wastee ama sagiyya bathreh whoo meeth wkhulhon aylayn dazeen waw bathreh ethbadarw
38	whasha amar na lkhon prooqw lkhon men halayn nasha washvooqw lhon den oo dmen bnaynasha eethayh hadhe makhshavta whana vadha meshtrayn wavreen
39	en dayn men alaha w la matya biydhaykon dathvatloona dalma teshtakhkhon lkhon dlooqval alaha qameen ton
40	wettpeesw leh waqraw enon lashleekhe wnagedhw enon wfaqedhw enon dla nehon mmalleen bashma dyeshoo washraw enon
41	wanfaqw men qdhamayhon kadh khadhayn dashwaw waw metul shma dnestaron
42	wla shalayn waw kulyoom lmalafoo bhaykla wavvayta wlamsabaroo al maran yeshoo msheekha
