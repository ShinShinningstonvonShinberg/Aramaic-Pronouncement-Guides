1	wemar aghripaws lpawlaws mapas lakh lammalaloo al apay nafshakh hadayn pawlaws pshat eedheh wnafeq wa rookha wamar
2	al kul ma dmethrshe na men eehoodhayye malka aghripe savar na al nafshy dtoovana na daqdhamayk yawmana nafeq na rookha
3	yateeraeeth dyadha na dampas at bkhulhon zitime wnamoose deehoodhayye metul hana bae na menakh davmagrath rookha teshmany
4	yadheen enon gayr af henon eehoodhayye en savayn dneshdhon doobaray dmen talyoothy dahwaw lee men shoorayya bamy wvuwrishlem
5	metul dmen nooghra mpaseen bee wyadheen davyoolpana rishayya dafreeshe kheeth
6	whasha al savra dshoodayya dahwa wa lavahathan men alaha qaem na wmetdheen na
7	dal hana savra tartaesre sharvan msabran danmanan baslawatha khfeetatha diymama wadhlilya walawy al hana savra methrshe na men eedhay eehoodhayye malka aghripe
8	mana daneen ton la wale danhaymen damqeem alaha meethe
9	ena gayr men qdheem sameth breyany dsaqoovle sagiyye esoor looqval shmeh dyeshoo nasrayya
10	hadhe devdeth af buwrishlem wqadeeshe sagiyye armeeth bayth aseere bshooltana dqableth men rabay kahne wkhadh methqatleen waw menhon eshtawtfeth laylayn dkhayyevw enon
11	wavkhul knoosha meshtanadh weeth bhon kadh ales weeth dnehon mghadfeen bashmeh dyeshoo wavrooghza sagiyya damle weeth layhon af lamdheenatha khranyatha nafeq weeth lmerdaf enon
12	wkhadh azel weeth metul hadhe ldharmsooq bshooltana wavmapsanootha drabay kahne
13	bfelgeh dyawma buwrkha khzeeth men shmayya aw malka dazlegh lay wal kul damy waw noohra damyatar men dshemsha
14	wanfaln kulan al ara wshemeth qala kadh amar lee evraeeth shawal shawal mana radhef at lee qshe oo lakh lamvaatoo looqse
15	wena emreth man at mary wmaran emar lee ena hoo yeshoo nasrayya dat radhef at
16	wemar lee qoom al reghlayk metul hadhe gayr ethkhzeeth lakh daqeemakh mshamshana wsahda dmedem dakhzaytany wdhatheedh at dthekhzayny
17	wepasaykh men ama deehoodhayye wmen amme khrane dalwathhon mshadar na lakh
18	dathfatakh aynayhon aykh dnehpkhon men kheshookha lnaheera wmen shooltaneh dsatana lwath alaha wanqablon shoovqan khtahe wfesa am qadeeshe bhaymanootha dvee
19	metul hadhe malka aghripe la qameth bkheryana looqval khezwa shmayyana
20	ela akhrzeth men lqoodhmeen lhanon davdharmsooq walhanon dvuwrishlem wdhavkhulhayn qoorya deehoodh waf lamme akhrzeth danthoovon wnethpnon lwath alaha wnebdhon vadhe dshawayn lathyavootha
21	wal apay halayn akhdoony eehoodhayye bhaykla wsavayn waw lmeqtlany
22	adrany dayn alaha dhama lyawma hana wha qaem na wamsahedh na lazoora walraba kadh medem lvar men mooshe wanviyye la amar na ela aylayn dhenon emarw datheedhan dnehwyan
23	dnekhash msheekha wnehwe risheetha daqyamta dmen bayth meethe wdhatheedh dnakhrez noohra lama walamme
24	wkhadh hakhana nafeq wa pawlaws rookha qa pistaws bqala rama shnayt lakh pawle sefra sagiyya avdakh dtheshne
25	amar pawlaws la shneeth naseekha pistaws ela melay shrara wthaqnootha mmalel na
26	waf malka aghripaws yateeraeeth yadha layhayn dhalayn wmetul hana een baghle mmalel na qdhamawy metul dakhdha men halayn mele la savar na dtayan leh dla wa btooshya seeran
27	mhaymen at malka aghripe lanviyye yadha na damhaymen at
28	amar leh aghripaws bqaleel medem mpees at lee dehwe kristyana
29	wpawlaws emar bae weeth men alaha bqaleel wavsagee la wa lakh balkhoodh ela af lkhulhon aylayn dshameen lee yawmana dnehon akhwathy lvar men asoore halayn
30	wqam leh malka whighmawna wverneeqi waylayn dyathbeen waw amhon
31	wkhadh praqw men taman mmalleen waw khadh am khadh wamreen dmedem dshawe lmawta aw lasoore la vadh gavra hana
32	wemar aghripaws lfistaws meshkakh wa gavra hana dneshtre eloo bghen qesar la qra
