4	wkhadh eshkakhn taman talmeedhe qaween lwathhon yawmatha shava whalayn amreen waw kulyoom lpawlaws brookh dla nizal luwrishlem
5	wmen bathar halayn yawmatha nfaqn dnizal buwrkha wamlawayn waw lan kulhon henon wneshayhon wavnayhon dhama lvar men mdheeta waqedhw al boorkayhon al yadh yama wsaleew

8	walyawma khrina nfaqn wethayn lqesariyya waln shrayn bvayteh dfeeleepaws msabrana ayna diythawy wa men shava

10	wkhadh eethayn taman yawmatha sagiyye nkheth wa men eehoodh nviyya khadh dashmeh hwa aghavaws
11	wal lwathan washqal eraqtha dkhasawy dpawlaws wesar reghle dnafsheh wiydhawy wemar hakhana amar rookha dqoodhsha dghavra marah deraqtha hadhe hakhana nesrooneh eehoodhayye buwrishlem wnashlmooneh biydhay amme
12	wkhadh halayn mele shman bayn meneh khnan wavnay athra dla nizal luwrishlem
13	hadayn na wemar pawlaws mana avdeen ton dvakhayn ton wshakhqeen ton leh lleby ena gayr la wa dethesar balkhoodh mtayyav na ela af demooth buwrishlem khlaf shmeh dmaran yeshoo
14	wkhadh la ettpees lan bheln lan wemarn dsevyaneh dmaran nehwe
15	wvathar yawmatha halayn ettayyavn wasleqn lan luwrishlem
16	wethaw waw aman nasha talmeedhe men qesariyya kadh dveereen amhon akha khadh men talmeedhe qadhmayye dashmeh hwa mnasawn wiythawy wa men qoopraws danqablan bvayteh
17	wkhadh ethayn luwrishlem qablon akhe khadhyaeeth
18	walyawma khrina aln am pawlaws lwath yaqoov kadh eeth wa lwatheh kulhon qasheeshe
19	wyavn lhon shlama wmeshtae wa lhon pawlaws bathar bathar kul ma davadh alaha bamme btheshmeshteh
20	wkhadh shmaw shabakhw lalaha wemarw leh khaze at akhon kma rebwan eeth beehoodh aylayn dhaymenw wkhulhon halayn tanane enon dnamoosa
21	ethemar lhon dayn layk dmalef at dnefrqon men mooshe kulhon eehoodhayye davamme kadh amar at dla nehon gazreen bnayhon wla bayadhe dnamoosa nehon mhalkheen
22	metul hakheel dmeshtama y lhon dethayt lkha
23	vedh medem damreenan lakh eeth lan gavre arba dandheer lhon dnetdakon
24	dvar enon wzel etdaka amhon wapeq layhon nafqatha aykh dneghron rishayhon wmethyadha lkhulnash dmedem dethemar layk dagal oo wat lnamoosa shalem at wnatar
25	al aylayn dayn dhaymenw men amme khnan kthavn dnehon natreen nafshhon men dadhveekha wmen zanyootha wmen khneeqa wmen dma
26	hadayn pawlaws dvar enon lghavre halayn lyawma khrina wetdakee amhon wal ezal lhaykla kadh mawda lhon moolayya dyawmatha dthadhkeetha dhama dethqarav qoorbana dnash nash menhon
27	wkhadh matee yawma dshava eehoodhayye dmen asiyya khzaoo bhaykla wghareew lawy ama kuleh warmeew lawy eedhayya
28	kadh mvagneen wamreen gavre bnay eesrayyel adarw hana w gavra dlooqval ama deelan malef bkhul dooka wlooqval namoosa wlooqval athra hana waf larmayye ael lhaykla wsayveh lathra hana qadeesha
29	qademw waw gayr khzaw ameh latrawfeemas efesayya bamdheeta wsavreen waw dam pawlaws al lhaykla
30	weshtaghshath kulah mdheeta wethkanashw kuleh ama wakhdoo lpawlaws wgharoo lvar men haykla wvar shatheh ettkhedhw tare
31	wkhadh bae wa kensha lmeqtleh eshtamath lkiliyyarkha despeer dkhulah mdheeta ettzeeath lah
32	wvar shatheh dvar lqentroona wlestratiyyawte sagiyye warhetw layhon wkhadh khzaw lkiliyyarkha wlestratiyyawte bhelw men dmakhayn waw leh lpawlaws
33	waqrev lwatheh kiliyyarkha wakhdeh wafqadh dnesrooneh bthartayn shishlan wamshael wa lawy dmanoo wmana vadh
34	wqaayn waw lawy nasha men akhlaws medem medem wmetul qathhon la meshkakh wa lmeda aydha y shareerta wafqadh wa dnawblooneh lmashreetha
