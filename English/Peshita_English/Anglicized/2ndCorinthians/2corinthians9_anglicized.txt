2	yadha na gayr tooyyaveh dreyankhon wmetul hana eshtavhreth bkhon lmaqedhawnayye dakhaiyya theedha y men eshtqadhy watnana deelkhon lsagiyye garegh
3	shadreth dayn lakhe dla nestaraq shoovharan deshtavharn bkhon al hadhe svootha daykana demreth tehon mtayveen
4	dalma nithon amy maqedhawnayye wneshkkhoonakhon kadh la theedheen ton wnevhath khnan dla nimar daton tevhthon bshoovhara haw deshtavharn
5	metul hana ethbtel lee deve men akhay halayn dnithon qdhamay lwathkhon wanatdhon boorktha ha dmen qdheem eshtmaton dthehwe mtayva hakhana aykh boorktha la wa aykh yanootha
6	hadhe dayn man dzara bkhawsana af bkhawsana khasedh wman dzara bvoorktha bvoorktha nekhsoodh
7	kul nash aykh diyth breyaneh la aykh dmen karyootha aw aykh dmen qteera lyahoova hoo gayr khadayya rakhem alaha
8	matya y dayn biydhay alaha kul tayboo danyatar bkhon davkhulzvan bkhulmedem haw ma dsafeq lkhon nehwe lkhon wthethyatron bkhul vadh tav

13	metul booqya gayr dtheshmeshta hadhe lalaha mshabkheen deshtabadton lthawdeetha dasvartheh damsheekha weshtawtafton bafsheetoothkhon amhon wam kulnash
