1	mawdeenan lkhon dayn akhayn taybootheh dalaha dethyahbath bidhatha dmaqedhawnayye
2	davvooqya sagiyya duwlsanhon yateerootha hwath lkhadhwathhon woomqa dmeskinoothhon ethyatar boothra dafsheetoothhon
3	sahedh na gayr daykh khaylhon wyateer men khaylhon bsevyan nafshhon
4	baw menan bvaootha sageetha dneshtawtfon btaybootha dtheshmeshta dqadeeshe
5	wla aykh ma dsavreen wayn ela nafshhon ashlemw looqdham lmaran waf lan bsevyaneh dalaha
6	dakhnan neve men titaws daykh dsharee hakhana nshalem bkhon af taybootha hadhe
7	ela aykana davkhulmedem methyatreen ton bhaymanootha wavmeltha wveedhatha wavkhul khfeetw wavkhooban dalwathkhon hakhana af bhadhe taybootha tethyatron
8	la wa mefqadh paqedh na lkhon ela bakhfeetootha dkhavraykon shrara dkhoobkhon mnase na
9	yadheen ton gayr taybootheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha dmetulathkhon ethmaskan kadh eethawy ateera daton bmeskinootheh tetron
10	memlakh dayn malekh na lkhon hadhe damadra lkhon metul dmen eshtqadhy shareeton law lmesba balkhoodh ela af lmebadh
11	hasha dayn shalemw bavadha haw medem dasvayton daykana dahwa lkhon soowakha lmesba hakhana bavadha tshalmon men ma diyth lkhon
12	en gayr sevyana eethawy aykh ma diyth leh hakhwath methqabal la wa aykh ma dlayt leh
13	la wa gayr dlakhrane tehwe rwakhta walkhon oolsana
14	ela bshawyootha hwaw bzavna hana dyateeroothkhon deelkhon tehwe lkhaseeroothhon dhalayn daf yateeroothhon dhanon tehwe lkhaseeroothkhon deelkhon dthehwe shawyootha
15	aykh dakhtheev dhaw dasgee shqal la ethyatar leh whaw dqaleel shqal la ethbasar leh
16	tayboo dayn lalaha dyahbah khfeetootha hadhe khlafaykon blebeh dtitaws
17	baoothan gayr qabel wmetul dtav bteel leh men sevyaneh nfaq sedhaykon
18	shadarn dayn ameh lakhon ayna dtheshbookhteh bewangeliyyawn bkhulhayn idhatha
19	hakhana daf meghba gve men idhatha dnepooq aman btaybootha hadhe dmeshtamsha menan lshoovkheh deeleh dalaha walloobavan deelan
20	qaneeteenan dayn bhadhe dla nash nseem ban mooma bhadhe rabootha dmeshtamsha menan
21	yaseefeenan gayr dshapeeratha la qdham alaha balkhoodh ela af qdham bnaynasha
22	shadarn dayn amhon af lakhon ayna dlan bqe bkhulzvan bsagiyyatha dakhfeeta hoo hasha dayn yateeraeeth khfeet bthookhlana sagiyya dalaykon
23	en hakheel titaws shawtafa hoo deely wamadrana bkhon wen akhayn khrane shleekhe enon didhatha dshoovkheh damsheekha
24	mekeel takhweetha dkhoobkhon wshoovharan davkhon bhon khawaw bparsoop idhatha kulhayn
