1	ena dayn pawlaws bae na menkhon bneekhootheh wavmakeekhootheh damsheekha dafen bapeen makeekh na lwathkhon ela kadh rakheeq na tkheel na laykon
2	bae na dayn menkhon dla ma detheeth etheles bthookhlana diyth lee dasheekh aykh dmethrae na al nasha aylayn dkhashbeen lan daykh dvavsar mhalkheenan
3	afen bavsar gayr mhalkheen nan ela la wa dvesra palkheenan
4	zayna gayr dfalkhoothan la wa dvesra ela dkhayla dalaha wveh kavsheenan khesne mreedhe
5	wsathreenan makhshvatha wkhul rawma dmettreem looqval eedhatheh dalaha wshavaynan kul taryan lmashmatheh damsheekha
6	wamtayveenan lmebadh tvatha men aylayn dla meshtameen ma dethmalyath meshtamanoothkhon
7	bparsoope khareen ton en nash tkheel al nafsheh ddhamsheekha hoo hana neda men nafsheh daykh dhoo damsheekha hakhana af khnan
8	en gayr af medem yateer eshtavhar al shooltana dyav lee maran la baheth na metul dalvenyana hoo deelkhon yav lan wla lsookhafkhon
9	mahme na dayn dla estvar aykh haw damdhakhaloo mdhakhel na lkhon begrathy
10	metul diyth nasha damreen degratha yaqeeran wkhaseenan methyeh dayn dghooshma kreeh wmeltheh sheeta
11	ela hadhe nethrae man dhakhana amar daykana diythayn bmeltha degartan kadh rakheeqeenan hakhana eethayn af ma dqareeveenan bavadha
12	la gayr mamrkheenan dnekhshoov aw danfakhem nafshan am hanon damshavhreen nafshhon ela metul dhenon bhon lhon mfakhmeen la mestakleen
13	khnan dayn la meshtavhreenan yateer men mshookhthan ela bamshookhtha dathkhooma daflagh lan alaha dnemte af dhama lwathkhon
14	la wa gayr aykh dla methmataynan lwathkhon mathkheenan nafshan dhama gayr lwathkhon mateen basvartheh damsheekha
15	wla meshtavhreenan lvar men mshookhthan bamla dakhrane ela eeth lan savra dkhadh ravya haymanoothkhon bkhon nethrawrav aykh mshookhthan wnethyatar
16	af lhal menkhon lamsabaroo la bamshookhtha dakhrane baylayn dmathqnan neshtavhar
17	haw dayn dmeshtavhar bmarya neshtavhar
18	la wa gayr man dnafsheh mshabakh haw oo bqe ela man dmarya nshabkheewy
