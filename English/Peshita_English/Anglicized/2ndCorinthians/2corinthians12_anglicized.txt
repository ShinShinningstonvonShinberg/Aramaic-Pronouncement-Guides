1	lmeshtavharoo wale ela la paqakh athe na lee gayr lkhezwane walghelyanawy dmaran
2	yadhana gavra bamsheekha men qdham arbaesre shneen en bafghar dayn wen dla pghar la yadha na alaha hoo yadha dethkhtef hoo hana dhama shmayya dathlatha
3	wyadha na leh lhana barnasha en bafghar dayn wen dla pghar la yadha na alaha hoo yadha
4	dethkhtef lfardaysa washma mele dla methmallan aylayn dla shaleet lvarnasha lammalaloo enayn
5	al hana eshtavhar al nafshy dayn la eshtavhar ela en bkhoorhane
6	en esbe gayr deshtavhar la hawe na shatya shrara hoo gayr amar na khaesna dayn dalma nash nethrae lay yateer men ma dkhaze lee wadhshama meny
7	wadhla etreem byateerootha dghelyane eshtlem lee shfayya lvesry malakheh dsatana dnehwe mqapakh lee dla etreem
8	wal hana tlath zavneen beeth men mary dnafreq meny
9	wemar lee safqa lakh tayboothy khayly gayr bakhreehootha hoo methgmar khadhyaeeth hakheel eshtavhar bkhoorhanay dnagen lay khayleh damsheekha
10	metul hana save na bkhoorhane bsara buwlsane bardhoofya bakhvooshya dal apay msheekha emathy dakhreeh na gayr hadayn oo khaylthan na
11	ha weeth khaseer reyana bshoovhary daton elastoonany khayyaveen wayton gayr daton tashdhon lay metul dla basreth medem men shleekhe aylayn dtav myatreen wafen la weeth medem
12	athwatha dashleekhe evdeth baynathkhon bkhul msaybranoo wvaghvarwatha wavthedhmratha wavkhayle
13	bmana gayr ethbasarton men idhatha khranyatha ela en bhadhe dena la yeqreth laykon shvooqw lee hadhe sakhlootha
14	ha hadhe dathlath ee zavneen damtayyav na dithe lwathkhon wla iqar laykon metul dla bae na deelkhon ela lkhon la gayr khayyaveen bnayya lamsam seematha lavahe ela avahe lavnayhon
15	ena dayn khadhyaeeth wnafqatha apeq waf qnoomy etel al apay nafshathkhon afen kadh yateeraeeth makhev na lkhon aton bseeraeeth makhveen ton lee
16	wakhvar ena la yeqreth laykon ela aykh gavra khara bnekhla gnavtkhon
17	lma byadh nash khreen dshadreth lwathkhon ethyaneth laykon
18	men titaws beeth wshadreth ameh lakhe lma medem ethyaan laykon titaws law bakhdha rookha halekhn wavhayn beqvatha
19	lma toov savreen ton dalkhon nafqeenan rookha qdham alaha bamsheekha mmalleenan wkhulhayn khabeevay metul benyana hoo deelkhon
20	dakhel na gayr dalma ithe lwathkhon wla eshkakhkhon aykh dsave na weshtkhakh lkhon af ena aykh ma dla savayton dalma kheryana wakhsama wkhemtha wesyana wmekhal-qarsa wretna wakhtheerootha washghooshya
21	dalma kadh ithe lwathkhon nmakkhany alahy wethevel al sagiyye aylayn dakhtaw wla tavw men tanfootha wmen zanyootha wmen pakhzootha davadhw
