1	aylayn diythayhon tkhayth neera davdootha lmarayhon bkhul eeqar nekhdhon dla nehwe shmeh dalaha wyoolpaneh methgadaf
2	aylayn dayn diyth lhon marayya mhaymne la nevson bhon al dakhayhon enon ela yateeraeeth nshamshon enon al damhaymne enon wkhabeeve halayn dmettneekheen btheshmeshthon halayn alef wavee menhon
3	en dayn eeth nash dmalef yoolpana khrina wla methqarav lmele khleematha dmaran yeshoo msheekha walyoolpana ddhekhlath alaha
4	hana metreem kadh medem la yadha ela kreeh badhrasha wvavatha dmele dmenhayn hawe khsama wkheryana wghoodafa wamsam breyana beesha
5	washkhaqa davnaynasha aylayn damkhabal reyanhon waghleezeen men qooshta wsavreen dtheghoorta y dekhlath alaha at dayn ethraq men halayn
6	teghoortan gayr deelan raba y diythayh dekhlath alaha bakhshakhtha dmesthan
7	medem gayr la aeln lalma weedheea dafla dnapeq meneh meshkkheenan
8	metul hana safqa lan mekhoolta wthakhseetha
9	aylayn dayn dsavayn lmetar nafleen bnesyoone wavfakhe wvargheeghatha sagiyyatha dsakhlan wamsagfan wamtaban lavnaynasha bakhvala wvavdana
10	eqara dayn dkhoolhayn beeshatha eethayh rekhmath kespa wiyth nasha dethraghraghw leh wmen haymanootha taw wnafshhon aelw ldhawane sagiyye
11	at dayn aw barnasha dalaha men halayn rooq what bathar zadeeqootha wvathar kinootha wvathar haymanootha wvathar khooba wvathar msaybranootha wvathar makeekhootha
12	wethkatash baghoona tava dhaymanootha wadhrekh khayye dalalam dalhon ethqreet wawdeeth tawdeetha tavtha qdham sahde sagiyye
13	msahedh na lakh qdham alaha haw dmakhe kul wyeshoo msheekha haw dashedh qdham pantiyyaws-peelataws sahdootha shapeerta
14	dthetreewy pooqdana dla toolsha wadhla mooma dhama lghelyaneh dmaran yeshoo msheekha
15	haw datheedh bzavneh nkhawaywy alaha mvarkha wkhaylthana balkhoodhawy malka dmalke wmara dmarawatha
16	haw dhoo balkhoodhawy la methkhabal wamar bnoohra dnash la meshkakh dnethqarav leh wnash men bnaynasha la khza wafla mse lmekhzyeh haw dleh eeqara wshooltana lalam almeen ameen
17	lateere dalma hana paqedh dla netreemon breyanayhon wla nettakhlon al oothra dlayt lawy tookhlana ela al alaha khayya haw dyav lan kul ateeraeeth lanyakhan
18	wnebdhon vadhe tave wnetron bsoorane shapeere wnehon dleeleen lmetal walmeshtawtafoo
19	wanseemon lnafshhon shethesta tavtha lmedem datheedh dnadhrkhon khayye shareera
20	aw teemathe ezdar bmedem dethgel lakh warooq men bnath qale sreeqatha wmen hfakhatha deedhatha dagaltha
21	aylayn gayr dmeshtawdayn lah taw lhon men haymanootha taybootha amakh ameen
