1	waton yadheen ton akhay dmalanan dalwathkhon la wa sreeqaeeth hwa
2	ela looqdham khashnan westaarnan aykh dyadheeton bfeeleepaws whadayn baghoona raba maleln amkhon bparesiyya dalahan svartheh damsheekha
3	booyyaan gayr la wa men tayootha afla men tanfootha afla bnekhla
4	ela aykh ma davqaynan lalaha dthethhayman svartheh hakhana mmalleenan la aykh dlavnay nasha neshpar ela lalaha haw dvaqe lebawathan
5	afla gayr memthoom ethkhashakhn bmamlla shadala aykh dyadheen ton wla beltha dyanootha alaha sahedh
6	wla bayn shoovkha men bnaynasha la menkhon wla men khrane kadh meshkkheen wayn yaqeere lmehwa aykh shleekhe damsheekha
7	ela hwayn baynathkhon makeekhe waykh mrabyaneetha damkhabva bnayh
8	hakhana af khnan mkhabveenan wamsawkheenan lmetal lkhon la balkhoodh svartheh dalaha ela af nafshan metul dkhabeevayn ton
9	ahdeen ton gayr akhayn dlaayn wayn wamleen bavadh eedhayn blilya wviymama dal khadh menkhon la niqar
10	aton sahdeen walaha aykana akhrezn lkhon svartheh dalaha dakhyaeeth wkhinaeeth wadhla reshyan hwayn lwath kulhon mhaymne
11	aykana dyadheen ton dmen khadh khadh menkhon aykh ava men bnawy baayn wayn wmalayn wayn blebkhon wamsahdheenan lkhon
12	dathhalkhon aykh dyae lalaha haw daqrakhon lmalkootheh walshoovkheh
13	metul hana af khnan mawdaynan ameenaeeth lalaha dmeltha dalaha dqabelton menan la wa aykh meltha davnaynasha qabeltoonah ela aykana diythayh shareeraeeth meltha dalaha whee bavadha mestara bkhon baylayn damhaymneen
14	aton dayn akhay etdameeton lidhatha dalaha dveehoodh halayn davyeshoo msheekha dhakhan saybarton af aton men bnay sharbathkhon aykana daf henon men eehoodhayye
15	hanon dalmaran yeshoo msheekha qtalw wlanviyye dmenhon wlan rdhafw wlalaha la shafreen waveedheen saqoovla lkhulhon bnay nasha
16	dkhalayn lan danmalel am amme dnikhon lamshalamoo khtahayhon bkhulzvan matee dayn layhon rooghza dhama lkhartha
17	khnan dayn akhayn hwayn yathme menkhon zavna dshatha bapayn wla bleban wyateeraeeth ethkhapatn lmekhza apaykon bkhooba sagiyya
18	wasvayn dnithe lwathkhon ena pawlaws khdha zvan wthartayn wawkhany satana
19	aynaw gayr savran wkhadhoothan wakhleela dshoovharan ela en aton qdham maran yeshoo bmetheetheh
20	aton enon gayr teshbookhtan wkhadhoothan
