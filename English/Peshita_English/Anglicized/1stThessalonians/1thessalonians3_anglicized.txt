1	wmetul dla khamsenn svayn lamfash bathinews balkhoodhayn
2	wanshadar lwathkhon lteematheaws akhon wamshamshana dalaha wamadranan basvartheh damsheekha danshararkhon wneve menkhon al haymanoothkhon
3	dla nash menkhon tethqata leh buwlsane halayn aton gayr yadheen ton dalhadhe oo seemeenan
4	af kadh lwathkhon hwayn gayr qademn emarn lkhon datheedheenan lmethelasoo aykana dyadheen ton dahwa
5	metul hana af ena la khamsneth dhama dshadreth deda haymanoothkhon ddhalma nnasaykhon mnasyana wnehwe amlan sreeqa
6	hasha dayn men detha lwathan teematheaws men sedhaykon wsavran al haymanoothkhon wal khoobkhon wdhiyth lwathkhon oohdanan tava bkhul edan waswayn ton lmekhzyan aykana daf khnan lkhon
7	metul hana ethbayyan bkhon akhayn al kulhayn aqathan oowlsanayn metul haymanoothkhon
8	whasha oo khaeenan en aton tethqaymon bmaran
9	aydha gayr tawdeetha meshkkheenan lmefra khlafaykon lalaha al kul khadhootha dkhadhaynan metulathkhon
10	ela daqdham alaha yateeraeeth nethkashaf blilya wviymama dnekhze apaykon wneghmoor ma dkhaseera haymanoothkhon
11	hoo dayn alaha avon wmaran yeshoo msheekha nethroos oorkhan lwathkhon
12	wnasge wanyatar khoobkhon dkhadh lwath khadh wdhalwath kulnash aykana dakhnan makhveenan lkhon
13	wanqayyem lebawathkhon dla reshyan bqadeeshootha qdham alaha avon bmetheetheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha dam kulhon qadeeshawy
