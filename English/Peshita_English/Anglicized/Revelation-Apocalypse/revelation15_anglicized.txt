1	wakhzeeth khritha atha bashmayya rabtha wathmeehta malakhe diyth layhon makhwatha shva khrayyatha davhayn eshtamlyath khemtheh dalaha
2	wakhzeeth aykh yama dazghoogheetha daftheekha bnoora waldhazkhaw men khayyootha wmen salmah wmen menyana dashmah dqameen lel men yama dazghoogheetha wiyth layhon qeetharawy dalaha
3	wamshabkheen teshbookhta dmooshe avdeh dalaha wtheshbookhta demra wamreen rawrveen wthameeheen vadhayk marya alaha akheedh kul kineen wshareereen vadhayk malka dalme
4	man la nedhkhal lakh marya wanshabakh lashmakh metul dat oo balkhoodh khasya metul dkhulhon amme nithon wnesgdhon qdhamayk metul dathrees at
5	wmen bathar halayn khzeeth wethpthakh haykla dmashkna dsahdootha bashmayya
6	wanfaqw shava malakheen men haykla hanon diyth layhon shva makhwan kadh lveesheen ketana dakhya wanheera waseereen al khadhyayhon esara ddhahva
7	wakhdha men arba khaywatha yehbath lshava malakheen shva zavooreen dmalyan khemtheh dalaha diythawy khayya lalam almeen ameen
8	wethmlee haykla men tnana dtheshbookhteh dalaha wmen khayleh wlayt damse wa lmeal lhaykla dhama dneshtamlyan shva makhwan dshava malakheen
