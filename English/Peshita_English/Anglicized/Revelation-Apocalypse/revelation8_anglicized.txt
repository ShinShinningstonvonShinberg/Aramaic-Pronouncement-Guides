1	wkhadh pthakh tava dshava hwa shethqa bashmayya aykh pelgooth shaa
2	wakhzeeth lshava malakheen aylayn daqdham alaha qameen waw detheehevw lhon shava sheefooreen
3	wakhrina malakha etha wqam al madhbkha wiyth leh peerma ddhahva wetheehev leh besme sagiyye dnetel baslawatha dkhulhon qadeeshe al madhbkha daqdham koorsya
4	wasleq etra dvesme baslawatha dqadeeshe men yadh malakha qdham alaha
5	wansav malakha lpeerma wamla men noora dal madhbkha warmee al ara wahwaw rame wqale wvarqe wnawda
6	wshava malakheen dalayhon shava sheefooreen tayyevw nafshhon lmazaqoo
7	whaw qadhmayya azeq wahwa bardha wnoora daftheekheen bmayya wethrmeew al ara wthoolthah dara eeqedh wthooltha diylane eeqedh wkhul esba dara eeqedh
8	wdhathrayn zaq wahwa aykh toora raba dyaqedh nfal byama wahwa tooltheh dyama dma
9	wmeeth tooltha dkhul beryatha davyama diyth beh nafsha wthooltha delfa ethkhabal
10	wdhathlatha zaq wanfal men shmayya kawkva raba dyaqedh aykh shalheveetha wanfal al tooltha dnahrawatha wal aynatha dmayya
11	washmeh dkhawkva methemar apseethna wahwa toolthhon dmayya aykh apsintheen wsoogha davnaynasha meethw metul dethmarmarw mayya
12	wdharba zaq wavla tooltheh dshemsha wthooltheh dsahra wthooltha dkhawkve wakhshekhw toolthhon wyawma la khawee tooltheh wlilya hakhwath
13	wshemeth lneshra khadh dfarakh bashmayya damar wa wa wa lamoorayh dara men qala dsheefoore dathlatha malakheen datheedheen lmazaqoo
