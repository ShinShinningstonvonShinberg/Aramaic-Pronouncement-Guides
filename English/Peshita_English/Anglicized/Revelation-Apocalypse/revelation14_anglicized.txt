3	wamshabkheen aykh teshbookhta khdhatha qdham koorsya waqdham arba khaywan waqdham qasheeshe wla nash ethmsee lmilfah ltheshbookhta ela en ma warbeen warba alfeen zveenay men ara
4	halayn enon aylayn dam neshe la ettawashw bthoole gayr eethayhon halayn dnaqpoo lemra kul kar dnizal halayn ezdvenw men nasha risheetha lalaha wlemra

6	wakhzeeth khrina malakha dfarakh mesath shmayya wiyth leh lawy svartha dalalam lamsabaroo al yathbay ara wal kul am wemwan wsharvan wleshan
7	lmimar bqala raba dkhalw men alaha whavw leh teshbookhta metul dethath shatha ddheeneh wasghoodhw ldhavadh shmayya wara wyama waynatha dmayya

9	wakhrina malakha dathlatha nqef lhon lmimar bqala raba ayna dasghedh lkhayyootha walsalmah washqal rooshmah bayth aynawy
10	af hoo neshte men khamra dkhemtheh dmarya damzeegh dla khelta bkhasa drooghzeh wneshtanaq bnoora wkhevreetha qdham malakhe qadeeshe waqdham emra
11	wathnana dthashneeqhon lalam almeen nesaq wlayt lhon nfisha eemama wlilya aylayn dsaghdeen lkhayyootha walsalmah walman dashqal rooshma dashmah

14	wha nana khewarta wal nana yathev dmootha dvarnasha wiyth leh al risheh kleela ddhahva wal eedheh magaltha khareefta

16	warmee haw dyathev al nana magaltheh al ara wethkhasdath ara
17	wakhrina malakha nfaq men haykla dvashmayya walawy eeth magaltha khareefta
18	wakhrina malakha nfaq men madhbkha diyth leh shooltana al noora waqa bqala raba ldhiyth leh magaltha khareefta shadar at magalthakh khareefta waqtoof lasghoole dkharmah dara metul darvay envawy
