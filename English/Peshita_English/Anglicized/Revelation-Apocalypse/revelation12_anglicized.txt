1	watha rabtha ethkhazyath bashmayya attha dateefa shemsha wsahra tkhayth reghlayh wakhleela dkhawkve tresar al rishah
2	wvatna wqaya wamkhablan af meshtanqa dthiladh
3	wethkhazyath atha khritha bashmayya wha taneena raba dnoora diyth leh shava risheen wasar qarnatha wal rishawy shava tagheen
4	wdhoonbeh garsha lthooltha dkhawkve dvashmayya warmee enon al ara wthaneena qaem wa qdham attha datheedha dthiladh dma dyeldath nekhleewy lavrah
5	wyeldath bra dekhra haw datheedh lmera lkhulhon amme bshavta dfarzla wethkhtef brah lwath alaha walwath koorsyeh
6	wattha erqath lkhoorba athar diyth wa lah taman dooktha damtayva men alaha dantharsoonah yawmeen alef wmathayn weshteen
7	wahwa qrava bashmayya meekhayel wmalakhawy maqrveen am taneena wthaneena wmalakhawy aqrevw
8	wla ethmseew wla athra eshtkhakh lhon bashmayya
9	wethrmee taneena raba haw khewya risha haw dmethqre akhelqarsa wsatana haw datee lkhulah ara wethrmee al ara wmalakhawy ameh ethrmeew
10	wshemeth qala raba men shmayya demar ha hwa shoozava wkhayla wmalkootha dalahan dethrmee masoora dakhayn haw dmasar wa lhon lilya wiymama qdham alahan
11	whanon zkhaw badhma demra wavyadh meltha dsahdootheh wla akhevw nafshhon dhama lmawta
12	metulhana shmayya ethpaskhw waylayn davhon shrayn wa lara walyama al dnakheth akhelqarsa lwathhon diyth leh khemta rabtha kadh yadha dqaleel zavna eeth leh
13	wkhadh khza taneena dethrmee al ara rdhaf lattha aydha dyeldath dekhra
14	wetheehev lattha trayn gepeen dneshra raba dthefrakhy lkhoorba ldhookthah lmettarsayyoo taman edan edaneen wfelgooth edan men qdham apawy dkhewya
15	warmee khewya men poomeh bathar attha mayya aykh nahra dashqeelath mayya nebdheeh
16	wadrath ara lattha wfethkhath ara poomah wavlatheh lnahra haw darmee taneena men poomeh
17	warghez taneena al attha wezal lmebadh qrava am sharka dzarah halayn dnatreen pooqdanawy dalaha wiyth lhon sahdootheh dyeshoo
