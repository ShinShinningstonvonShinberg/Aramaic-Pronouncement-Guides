1	wqameth al khala dyama wakhzeeth dsalqa khayyootha men yama diyth lah sar qarnan washva qarqfan wal qarnathah esra tagheen wal qarqfathah shma dghoodafa
2	wkhayyootha ha dakhzeeth dmootha wath dnemra wreghlayh aykh ddheba wfoomah aykh daryawatha wyav lah taneena khayleh wkhoorsyeh wshooltana raba
3	wakhdha men qarqfathah aykh peeta lmawta wamkhootha dmawtah ethasyath wetdamrath kulah ara bathar khayyootha
4	wasghedhw lthaneena dyav shooltana lkhayyootha wasghedhw lkhayyootha lmimar manoo ddhame lkhayyootha hadhe wmanoo meshkakh lmaqravoo amah
5	wetheehev lah pooma dammalel rawrvatha wghoodafa wetheehev lah shooltana lmebadh yarkhe arbeen wathrayn
6	wfethkhath poomah lamghadafoo qdham alaha dathghadefy bashma wavmashrya daylayn dashrayn bashmayya
7	wetheehev lah lmebadh qrava am qadeeshe walmezka enon wetheehev lah shooltana al kulhayn sharbatha wemwatha wleshane wamme
8	wnesgdhon lah kulhon amoorayh dara hanon aylayn dla ktheeveen bakhthava dkhayye haw demra qteela qdham tarmyatheh dalma
9	man diyth leh edhne neshma
10	man davshevya mawbel bshevya azel wayna davkharba qatel bkharba nethqtel harka y haymanootha wamsaybranootha dqadeeshe
11	wakhzeeth khayyootha khritha dsalqa men ara wiyth wa lah tartayn qarnan wdhamya lemra wammalla wath aykh taneena
12	wshooltana dkhayyootha qadhmata kuleh tebedh qdhamawy wthebedh lara wladhamreen bah wnesgdhon lkhayyootha qadhmata ha dethkhalmath mkhootha dmawtah
13	wthebedh athwatha rawrvatha aykana dnoora tebedh lmekhath men shmayya al ara qdham bnaynasha
14	wthate ladhamreen al ara byadh athwatha detheehev lah lmebadh qdham khayyootha lmimar ladhamreen al ara lmebadh salma lkhayyootha aydha diyth lah mkhootha dkharba wakhyath
15	wetheehev lah dthetel rookha lsalma dkhayyootha wthebedh dkhul dla nesgdhon leh lsalma dkhayyootha nethqatlon
16	wthebedh lkhulhon zoore wrawrve ateere wmeskine marayya wavde dnetheehev lhon rooshma al eedhayhon dyameena aw al bayth aynayhon
17	dla nash nezben aw nzaben toov ela ayna diyth lawy rooshma dashma dkhayyootha aw menyana dashmah
18	harka eethayh khekhmtha wdhiyth beh hawna nekhshveewy lmenyana dkhayyootha menyana w gayr dvarnasha shethma weshteen wsheth
