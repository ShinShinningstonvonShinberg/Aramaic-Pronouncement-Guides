1	wadhkhamsha zaq wakhzeeth kawkva danfal men shmayya al ara wetheehev leh qleedha dvirawy dathhooma
2	wasleq tnana men bire aykh tnana datoona raba dmeshtghar wakhshekh shemsha waar men tnana dvire
3	wmen tnana nfaqw qamse al ara wetheehev lhon shooltana diyth leqarve dara
4	wethemar lhon dla nahron lesbah dara walkhul yooraq afla liylane ela en lavnaynasha aylayn dlayt lhon khathma dalaha bayth aynayhon
5	wetheehev lhon dla neqtlon enon ela neshtanqon yarkhe khamsha wthashneeqhon aykh tashneeqa deqarva ma dnafla al nash
6	wavyawmatha hanon nevon bnaynasha lmawta wla neshkkhoona wnethraghrghon lammath wnerooq mawta menhon
7	wadhmootha dqamse aykh dmootha drakhsha damtayveen laqrava wal rishayhon aykh kleela dadhmootha ddhahva wapayhon aykh ape dnasha
8	wsara eeth lhon aykh sara dneshe wshenayhon aykh daryawatha
9	wiyth wa lhon sheryana aykh sheryana dfarzla wqala dghepayhon aykh qala dmarkvatha drakhsha sagiyye drahteen laqrava
10	wiyth lhon doonbyatha aykh dmootha deqarva wooqse dayn bdhoonbyathhon wshooltanhon lmaharoo lavnaynasha yarkhe khamsha
11	wiyth layhon malka malakheh dathhooma dashmeh evraeeth avadoo warmaeeth shma leh eeth share
12	wa khadh ezal ha toov athayn trayn wa
13	bathar halayn malakha deshta zaq wshemeth qala khadh men arba qarnatheh dmadhbkha ddhahva daqdham alaha
14	damar lmalakha shtheethayya diyth leh sheefoora shree larba malakhe daseereen al nahra raba prath
15	weshtreew arba malakheen aylayn damtayveen lshatha walyawma walyarkha walshata dneqtlon tooltha davnaynasha
16	wmenyana dkhaylawatha dfarashe tartayn reboo rebwan shemeth menyanhon
17	whakhana khzeeth rakhsha bkhezwa wladhyathbeen layhon eeth sheryana dnoora wqarkhedhna dkhevreetha wqarqfatha drakhshhon aykh qarqfatha daryawatha wmen poomhon nafqa noora wkhevreetha wathnana
18	wmen halayn tlath makhwan ethqatalw tooltha davnaynasha wmen noora wmen kevreetha wmen tnana dnafeq men poomhon
19	metul dshooltana drakhsha bfoomhon waf bdhoonbyathhon
20	wsharka davnaynasha dla ethqtalw bmakhwatha halayn wla tavw men vadh eedhayhon dla nesgdhon ldhaywe wlafthakhre ddhahva wadhsima wdhankhasha wadhqaysa wadhkhifa aylayn dla lmekhza wla lmeshma msayn aw lamhalakhoo
21	wla tavw men qetlayhon wmen kharshayhon wmen zanyoothhon
