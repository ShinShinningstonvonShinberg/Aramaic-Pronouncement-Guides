2	metul dshareereen wkhineen deenawy metul ddhan lzaneetha rabtha aydha dkhablath lara bzanyoothah wathva dma davdawy men eedhayh
3	dthartayn emarw halelooyya wathnanah sleq lalam almeen
4	wanfalw esreen warba qasheesheen warba khaywan wasghedhw lalahan dyathev al koorsya wamreen ameen halelooyya
5	wqala men koorsya damar shabakhw lalahan kulhon avdawy wdhakhlay shmeh kulhon zoore am rawrve
6	wshemeth qala aykh dkhenshe sagiyye waykh qala dmayya sagiyye waykh qala drame khaylthane damreen halelooyya metul damlekh marya alaha akheedh kul
7	khadhaynan wmethpaskheenan netel leh teshbookhta metul dethath meshtootheh demra wattheh tayvath nafshah

9	wemarw lee kthoov toovayhon laylayn dlakhshameetha dmeshtootheh demra eethayhon qrayya wemar lee halayn mele dshareeran dalaha eethayhayn
10	wnefleth qdham reghlawy wseghdeth leh wemar lee la knathakh eethay wdhakhayk halayn diyth lhon sahdootha dyeshoo lalaha sghoodh yateeraeeth sahdootha gayr dyeshoo eethayh rookha danviyyootha
11	wakhzeeth shmayya daftheekh wha soosya khewara wadhyathev lawy methqre mhaymna wshareera wavkhinootha daen wmaqrev
12	aynawy dayn aykh shalheveetha dnoora wal reesheh taghe sagiyye wiyth leh shma ktheeva ayna dla yadha ela en hoo
13	wamataf mana dazlee badhma wmethqre shmeh meltha dalaha
14	wkhaylawatha dashmayya naqeefeen waw leh al rakhsha kheware walveesheen boosa khewara wdhakhya
15	wmen poomhon nafqa kharba khareefta dvah neqtlon lamme whoo nere enon bshavta dfarzla whoo daesh masarta drooghzeh dalaha akheedh kul
16	wiyth leh al manawy al atmatheh shma ktheeva malka dmalke wmara dmarawatha
17	wakhzeeth khrina malakha dqaem bshemsha waqa bqala rama wemar lfarakhtha dfarkha mesath shmayya taw ethkashw lakhshameetha rabtha dalaha
18	dthekhlon besra dmalke wvesra drishay alfe wvesra dasheene wvesra drakhsha wdhaylayn dyathbeen layhon wvesra dkhire wadhavde wdhazoore wadhrawrve
19	wakhzeeth lkhayyootha walkhaylawathah wmalke dara walfalkhayhon damkhansheen lmebadh qrava am haw dyathev al soosya wam palkhawy
20	wettseedhath khayyootha wanviyya dagala ameh haw davadh athwatha qdhamayh davhayn atee laylayn dansavw rooshma dkhayyootha wlaylayn dasghedhw lsalmah wankhethw trayhon wethrmeew byamtha dnoora dyaqda wadhkhevreetha
