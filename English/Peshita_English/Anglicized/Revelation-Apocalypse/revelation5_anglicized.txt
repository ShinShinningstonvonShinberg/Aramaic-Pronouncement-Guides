1	wakhzeeth al yameeneh dhaw dyathev al koorsya kthava darsheem men lghaw wmen lvar watvee tave shava
2	wakhzeeth khrina malakha khaylthana dmakhrez bqala rama man shawe lmeftakh kthava walmeshra tavawy
3	wlayt dethmsee bashmayya wla bara wla dalthakht men ara lmeftakh lakhthava walmeshra tavawy walmekhzyeh
4	wvakhe weeth sagee metul dlayt deshtkhakh dshawe lmeftakh lakhthava walmeshra tavawy
5	wkhadh men qasheeshe emar lee la tevke ha zkha arya men shavta deehoodha eqara ddhaweedh neftakh kthava wtavawy
6	wakhzeeth bmesath koorsya wdharba khaywan wadhqasheeshe emra dqaem aykh nkheesa wiyth leh qarnatha shva wayne shva aylayn diythayhayn shva rookhe dalaha dmeshtadran lkhulah ara
7	wetha wansav kthava men eedheh dhaw dyathev al koorsya
8	wkhadh shaqleh lakhthava arba khaywan wesreen warba qasheesheen nfalw qdhamawy demra kadh eeth lkhulkhadh khadh menhon qeethara wzavoora ddhahva dmalya besme aylayn diythayhayn slawatha dqadeeshe
9	damshabkheen teshbookhta khdhatha wamreen shawayt oo lmesveewy lakhthava walmeshra tavawy al dethnkhest wazvantan badhmakh lalaha men kul sharbatha wamme wemwatha
10	wavadt enon lalahan malkootha wkhahne wmalke wnamlkhon al ara
11	wakhzeeth wshemeth aykh qala dmalakhe sagiyye khdharay koorsya wadhkhaywatha wadhqasheeshe wiythawy wa menyanhon reboo rebwan walef alfeen
12	wamreen bqala rama shawayt oo emra nkheesa lmesav khayla woothra wkhekhmtha wooshna wiyqara wtheshbookhta wvoorktha
13	wkhul breetha dvashmayya wvara wdhalthakht men ara wdhavyama eethayh wkhul davhon wshemeth damreen ladhyathev al koorsya wlemra dvoorktha wiyqara wtheshbookhta oowkhdana lalam almeen
14	warba khaywan damran ameen wqasheeshe nfalw wasghedhw
