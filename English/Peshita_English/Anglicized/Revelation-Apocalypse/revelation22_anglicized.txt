1	wkhawyany nahra dmayya khayye dakhya af naheera aykh gleedha wnafeq men koorsyeh dalaha wdhemra
2	wmesath shooqayh meka wmeka al nahra qaysa dkhayye davedh pire tresar wavkhul eerakh yahev pirawy wtarfawy lasyootha damme
3	wkhul kherma la nehwe taman wkhoorsyeh dalaha wdhemra bah nehwe wavdawy nshamshoona
4	wnekhzon apawy washmeh al bayth aynayhon
5	wlilya la nehwe taman wla nethbe lhon noohra washragha wnoohreh dshemsha metul dmarya alaha manhar lhon wmalkhon lalam almeen
6	wemar lee halayn mele mhaymnan wshareeran wmarya alaha drookhatha danviyye qadeeshe shadar lmalakheh lamkhawayyoo lavdawy ma deeheev lmehwa baghal
7	wha athe na baghal toovawy lman dnatar mele danviyyootha dakhthava hana
8	ena yookhanan dakhza washma halayn wkhadh khzeeth wshemeth nefleth lmesgadh qdham reghlawy dmalakha damkhawe lee halayn
9	wemar lee khzee la knathakh eethay wdhakhayk nviyye wdhaylayn dnatreen halayn mele dakhthava hana lalaha sghoodh
10	wemar lee la tekhtoom mele danviyyootha dakhthava hana zavna gayr qrev
11	wadhmawel toov nawel wadhsa toov nestasa wzadeeqa toov nebedh zadeeqootha wqadeesha toov nethqadash
12	ha athe na mekhdha waghry amy wetel lkhulnash aykh vadheh
13	ena alaf wena taw qadhmayya wakhrayya wshoorayya wshoolama
14	toovayhon ladhavdeen pooqdanawy nehwe shooltanhon al qaysa dkhayye wavthara nelon lamdheeta
15	wzanayye wqatoole wfalkhay pthakhre lvar wtame wkharashe wkhul khazayyay wavday dagalootha
16	ena yeshoo shadreth lmalakhy dansahedh bkhon halayn qdham idhatha ena na eqara wsharbtheh ddhaweedh wameh wkhawkav safra naheera
17	wrookha wkhaltha amreen ta wadhshama nimar ta wdhashe nithe wnesav mayya khayye magan
18	msahedh na lkhul dshama meltha danviyyootha dakhthava hana dayna danseem layhayn nseem lawy alaha makhwatha dakhtheevan bakhthava hana
19	wayna damvasar men mele dakhthava danviyyootha hadhe nvasar alaha mnatheh men qaysa dkhayye wmen mdheeta qadeeshta aylayn dakhtheevan bakhthava hana
20	amar kadh msahedh halayn een athe na baghal ta marya yeshoo
21	taybootheh dmaran yeshoo msheekha am kulhon qadeeshawy ameen
