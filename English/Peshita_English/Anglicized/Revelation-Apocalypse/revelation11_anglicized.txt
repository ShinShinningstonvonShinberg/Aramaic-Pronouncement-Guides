1	wetheehev lee qanya dmootha dshavta wqaem wa malakha wamar qoom wamshookh lhaykla dalaha walmadhbkha wlaylayn dsaghdeen beh
2	waldharta dalghaw men haykla apeq men lvar wla temshkheeh metul dethyahbath lamme wlamdheeta qadeeshta ndhooshon yarkhe arbeen wathrayn
3	wetel lathrayn sahday lmethnabayyoo yawmeen alef wmathayn weshteen kadh teefeen saqe
4	halayn enon trayn zayteen wthartayn mnaran daqdham mara dkhulah ara qameen
5	wman dvae dnahar enon nafqa noora men poomhon wakhla laveldvavayhon wlayna dsave dnahar enon hakhan eeheev lhon lmethqtaloo
6	whalayn eeth lhon shooltana dnekhdon lashmayya dla nekhooth metra byawmatha danviyyoothhon wiyth lhon shooltana dnahpkhon mayya ladhma wadhnemkhon lara bkhul makhwan kma dnesbon
7	wma dshamleew sahdoothhon khayyootha dsalqa men yama tebedh amhon qrava wthezke enon wtheqtool enon
8	washladayhon al shooqe damdheeta rabtha aydha dmethqarya rookhanaeeth sdhoom wmesrayn ayka dmarhon estlev
9	wkhazayn men emwatha wsharbatha wleshane wamme lashladayhon tlatha yawmeen wfelgeh wlashladayhon la neshbqon lmettsamoo bqavre
10	wamoorayh dara nekhdon layhon wnethpaskhon wmawhvatha nshadron lakhdhadhe metul trayn nvieen dshaneqw lamoorayh dara
11	wmen bathar tlatha yawmeen wfelgeh rookha khaytha men alaha elath bhon wqamw al reghlayhon wrookha dkhayye neflath layhon wdhekhltha rabtha hwath al aylayn dkhazayn lhon
12	washmaw qala raba men shmayya damar lhon saqw lkha wasleqw lashmayya banana wmasdheen bhon beldvavayhon
13	wavshatha ha hwa zawa raba wkhadh men esra damdheeta nfalw wethqatalw bzawa shmahe dghavre alfe shava wadhsharka hwaw bdhekhltha wyavon teshbookhta lalaha dvashmayya
14	ha trayn wa ezalw wha wa dathlatha athe mekhdha
15	wmalakha dshava zaq wahwaw qale rawrve bashmayya damreen hwath malkootheh dalma dalahan wdhamsheekheh wamlekh lalam almeen
16	wesreen warba qasheeshe aylayn daqdham alaha yathbeen al koorsawathhon nfalw al apayhon wasghedhw lalaha
17	lmimar mawdaynan lakh marya alaha akheedh kul diythawy wiythawy wa dansavt bkhaylakh raba wamlekht
18	wamme rghezw wetha rooghzakh wzavna dmeethe dnetdheenon wthetel aghra lavdayk nviyye walqadeeshe waldhakhlay shmakh lazoore am rawrve wathkhabel laylayn dkhabelw lara
19	wethpthakh haykla bashmayya wethkhazyath qeevootha ddhiyyathiqi deeleh bhaykla wahwaw barqe wrame wqale wnawda wvardha raba
